# Fabricly

Fabricly is an online marketplace that takes the risk out of sourcing apparel manufacturers and materials.

It is used by a growing number of brands, independent designers and retailers to research manufacturers and quickly bid out sourcing jobs to a wide-reaching network.

Specialties

Apparel sourcing, clothing manufacturing, sourcing marketplace
