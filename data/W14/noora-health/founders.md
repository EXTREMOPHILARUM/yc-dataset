# Founders

## Edith Elliott
**Founder/CEO**

Edith is committed to helping patients and their loved ones prepare for the demands of caregiving. Her work focuses on the intersection of research, policy, and design in global health systems. Edith is an Ashoka, Mulago, DRK, and Echoing Green fellow. She received her MA from Stanford University and is an associate faculty member at Ariadne Labs at the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health. Edith was a 2022 TED speaker, Audacious Project winner, and Skoll Foundation awardee.
