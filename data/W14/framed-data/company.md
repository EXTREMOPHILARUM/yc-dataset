# Framed Data

> Predicted churn for enterprises. (Acquired by Block)

Predictive analytics for user churn. We use machine learning to help you identify when, and why, your users are leaving.

We're building data science tools and predictive analytics solutions for businesses who want value without headcount. Our platform will also include ways for people to collaborate on datasets and analyses. Data science is a high demand field and want to democratize it for all companies to use.

Framed Data was acquired by Block in 2016.
