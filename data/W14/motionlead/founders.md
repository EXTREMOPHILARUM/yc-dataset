# Founders

## Alexei Chemenda
**Founder**

Alexei Chemenda is the CRO and Managing Director, Americas for Adikteev. He joined Adikteev in 2015 when the latter acquired Motionlead, a company he co-founded in Paris. MotionLead was the first French company to ever be accepted in YCombinator. Alexei is a software engineer who now focuses on the business side of the company. 

Since 2015, Alexei actively invests in early stage tech companies (40+ investments to date). 
Alexei has been selected in Forbes 30 under 30 in 2019
