# Dating Ring

> In person matchmaking meets group dating. We do the work. You go on…

We're a Y Combinator W'14 class. We use matchmakers to set up group dates. What Uber has done for transportation - we're doing for dating. Members can book high quality dates on their schedule with the click of a button. Dating Ring is live in NYC and SF and will be launching next in Boston and LA.
