# Shoobs

> We help you find and book tickets to great entertainment events that…

Shoobs offers event organisers with a convenient and powerful platform to sell tickets to their events. A couple of years ago we got thinking that selling tickets should be QUICK & EASY!
So, we decided to create Shoobs to help event organisers sell out their events efficiently!
Shoobs is the leading destination to discover and book great urban culture events in the UK,
such as Hip Hop concerts and RnB club nights.
