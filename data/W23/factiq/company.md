# FactIQ

> Data Explorer for the US Economy

FactIQ helps economic analysts find and visualize the right data, across 7.4M+ official time series. 

It interprets users' questions, identifies the most relevant datasets, analyzes them and reports back with interactive charts and tables. Every answer is fully transparent: users can inspect the underlying sources and methods, so they can verify and trust every insight.
