# Founders

## Ash Rai
**Founder**

Ash is the Co-Founder and CEO of Dataglade. Before Dataglade, Ash founded a hedge fund from his freshman dorm room at Vanderbilt and grew it to $1 million AUM before turning 20. He was the country’s youngest-ever registered investment advisor. While running the fund, Ash was cut off from his university’s Bloomberg Terminal for overuse, so he built his own solution. Ash graduated early from Vanderbilt University with degrees in Economics, History, and Classics.
