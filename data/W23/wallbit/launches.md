# Launches

## Wallbit: US banking for global remote workers

**Wallbit: The All-in-One Neobank for Global Remote Workers**

We’re excited to introduce you to the new version of Wallbit: your US banking app from anywhere in the world—without ever stepping foot in the US.

### **FOR INDIVIDUALS:**

* **US Checking Account**: Open a fully functional US checking account 100% remotely. No need for costly travel or lengthy paperwork.
* **US Debit Card**: Spend globally with a US debit card linked directly to your Wallbit checking account.
* **Investment Accounts**: Access US stocks and ETFs with ease, giving users the ability to invest and grow their wealth.
* **Cash Management**: Earn competitive yield on your cash with low-risk options designed for stability and growth.
* **Instant Withdrawals to Latin America**: Send money back home in seconds, saving up to 5% and many days compared to traditional remittance methods.

### **For Businesses:**

Our new **Business Portal** allows companies to easily pay to all their contractors without incurring in swift bank transfers or paying high fees to other intermediaries. With no cost, businesses can now "bankarize" all their contractors, open US bank accounts to all of them and manage their payroll seamlessly in Wallbit or their own business banking (aka Mercury, Brex, etc.)

## Wallbit: Neobank in the US for global remote workers

**TL;DR:** Wallbit is a Neobank for global remote workers. We give non-US residents a bank account in the US where they can receive their income, and withdraw in crypto and local currencies around the world.

Hey everyone 👋, we are happy to introduce Wallbit!

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=69714&key=user_uploads/1021346/74112f3e-dbd6-4d80-b025-6b4e6c217aac)

**🙃 The Problem:**

As an international worker, you have to figure out ways to receive payments and withdraw money locally. Some banks in Latin America don’t even support US dollars, and the **US financial products are not so accessible if you don’t live there!**

**🌎 The Solution:**

**⚡ One-Stop Neobank for all the needs of remote workers ⚡**

Wallbit not only helps you receive payments, but also helps you get immediate liquidity. **Imagine receiving an ACH/Wire bank transfer from your client/employer in your Wallbit account, and then having those funds available in your bank account in your country in just 5 minutes…** sounds good, right? That’s exactly what we do.

Now, take a look to the rest of our features:

1. Receive ACH/Wire and Swift bank transfers ✅
2. Receive payments from payroll platforms like Deel, OnTop ✅
3. Convert and withdraw in crypto or local currencies ✅
4. Get access to a virtual US debit card ✅
5. Invest in the stock market and US treasuries ✅

Yep, all that, and it’s live already, no waitlists! Sign up in [app.wallbit.io](http://app.wallbit.io) and get access to your US bank account for free.

### **💪 Why us?**

We are a team of software engineers from Argentina who have faced this problem for years while working remotely for US companies. We’re combining all that understanding of the problem with our tech expertise to fill the banking gap between the US and the rest of the world.
