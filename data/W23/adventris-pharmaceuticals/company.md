# Adventris Pharmaceuticals

> Adventris makes cancer vaccines

Adventris makes cancer vaccines. Our platform solves the problem of targeting poorly immunogenic oncogenes. Our first "off-the-shelf" vaccine targets KRAS, the most common cancer oncogene. In the long run, we envision a world where every adult receives our pan-cancer vaccines annually – preventing the majority of cancer deaths.

If you are interested in learning more about our approach to treat and prevent cancer, please reach out to us at contact@adventris.com
