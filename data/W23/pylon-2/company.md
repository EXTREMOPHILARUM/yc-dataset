# Pylon

> The only customer support platform built for B2B.

Pylon is the modern alternative to Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk, and Salesforce Service Cloud. We help B2B companies track customer support issues across common B2B channels including shared Slack channels (Slack Connect), Microsoft Teams, email, in-app chat, ticket forms, Slack community, and more.

Hundreds of fast-growing YC companies of all sizes like Vellum, Hightouch, and Deel are customers.

Learn more at usepylon.com
