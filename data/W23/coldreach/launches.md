## ✍️ Coldreach: Automate sales email personalization for 10x conversions

**Tl;dr**

Sales reps can teach AI how to personalize sales emails **the way they want** in 5 minutes, and our AI writes and sends personalized emails for every prospect on autopilot, resulting in both higher conversion and much more emails sent per day. 

Hi! We are Shen and Hankel - the team behind Coldreach.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=70656&key=user_uploads/984968/3a9fe46c-225e-4641-8432-0df8f1287d73)

### **👪 Who is it for**

Is your sales team:

\- spending a lot of time writing sales emails? 

\- running templated email sequences that few people respond to? 

\- struggling to train good SDRs?

\- finding it hard to write follow-up emails after meetings?

Then Coldreach is for you. 

### **🧨 The Problem**

Templated emails get no attention and land in spam, because so many people are spamming. 

Personalization pays off, but it takes a lot of training to do it well, and takes >5 mins to research and write every email. 

# 💡 Our solution

**AI-powered templates** - instead of only replacing names on emails, Coldreach researches the prospect and fill in personalized information intelligently!

For example:

Import lead list from your favorite engagement platform or CRM easily, and start **sending personalized emails to everyone automatically** using your AI-powered templates.
