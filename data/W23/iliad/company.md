# Iliad

> AI-accelerated procedural material and texture tools.

Iliad builds procedural material tools for production studios. Its node-based material engine, Aqueduct, includes an AI assistant that helps artists build and edit procedural graphs. Iliad also offers diffusion tools for texture generation, PBR maps, and reference-to-texture conversion.
