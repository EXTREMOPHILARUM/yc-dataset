# Launches

## JustPaid.ai - Automate accounts receivable with the most powerful platform

[JustPaid.ai](http://JustPaid.ai) is reimagining B2B accounts receivable workflows, stepping away from rigid legacy CPQs, and entering a new era of finance efficiency, connecting CRO and CFO in one place.

Within seconds, the platform converts fully executed complex sales contracts into dynamic revenue records (customer names, billing parameters, customer notes) - where the journey of each invoice is timely generated, payments are reconciled automatically, and human-like collections workflows guide the cash gently into your bank account.

Here’s a preview: <https://youtu.be/baNem5HVYQQ>

JustPaid.ai excels in managing complexity and effortlessly handling consumption, tiered plans, renewals, price discounts, and custom SLAs.

Integrations to support your workflows with:

* API or CSV usage data upload 
* eSignature (Dropbox, PandaDoc, DocuSign, Box)
* CRM (SalesForce, HubSpot, [Close.io](http://Close.io), PipeDrive, Attio)
* ERP (Intuit, NetSuite, Xero, Sage)
* Sales Tax (Avalara, TaxJar)
* Payments Checkout (Bank payments and Credit Card)

## JustPaid.io (Loopfour) - Spend confidently with smart financial tools

### TL;DR

[JustPaid.io](http://JustPaid.io) is the AI-powered controller you wish you had earlier, so you will never have to worry about overpaying bills again.

### Problem

Companies' current financial processes are disconnected from what you _agreed_ to pay vs. what you were _actually_ charged, even when your vendor makes a billing error. This is what we’re here to resolve.

On average, United States businesses lose over $300,000 per year alone from invoice fraud totaling $12B. Don’t be a statistic. 

With expanding teams, a lot of vendor workflows are stuck in Slack and inboxes.  We are automating workflows to successfully manage vendors, including contracts, projected spend, W9/W8BEn collection, payment approval and remittance. 

### Solution

Saving thousands of dollars in real-time overview of your usage and billing, [JustPaid.io](http://JustPaid.io) provides a centralized experience to automate and manage vendor relationships, overspending, and more to ensure no matter the stage of your organization, you are always in control.

We collect data from your contracts all in one dashboard and compare it to both your historical as well as ongoing spend. We work with your existing bill payment solution or you can leverage our multi-approval workflow.
