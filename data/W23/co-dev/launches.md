# Launches

## co.dev: Turn your ideas into full-stack apps

[co.dev](https://co.dev) is an AI-powered platform lets you create scalable, modern full-stack apps using natural language, all while keeping full ownership of your code.

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V7NWWoa0kHY>

# **Problem**

Building full-stack applications is prohibitively complex, time-consuming, and costly for non-technical users. While current no-code platforms offer a partial solution, they come with serious drawbacks:

* Vendor Lock-In: They use they own stack, so you are tied to their platform.
* Scaling Challenges: Outgrowing these tools often means starting over.
* Limited Customization: Advanced features require a steep learning curve or outside help.

# **Solution**

[co.dev](https://co.dev) bridges the gap by combining the simplicity of no-code platforms with the flexibility and scalability of professional development tools. Using natural language, [co.dev](https://co.dev) enables users to build full-stack apps effortlessly through a chat-based interface.

The platform provides:

* Access to the Codebase: Users can download and own the generated code, eliminating vendor lock.
* Modern Tech Stack: Apps are built using Next.js and Supabase, providing scalability, speed, and reliability.
* Built-In Modules: Common features like authentication, database integration (via Prisma), and more, ready to use out of the box.
* AI Code Management: AI handles coding, bug fixes, database updates, and deployments, while you just provide requirements using natural language.
* Customization: Advanced users can continue app development outside the platform if desired.

This solution saves users time, reduces costs, and lowers the technical barriers to building fully functional, scalable applications.

# **Uses Cases**

1. **Startup MVP Development:** Entrepreneurs can quickly create and iterate on minimum viable products for their startup ideas, allowing for rapid testing and validation of concepts.
2. **Non-Technical App Development:** Anyone can describe their app vision in plain text, and [co.dev](https://co.dev) transforms it into a functional app. Whether you're a small business owner or a creative individual, [co.dev](https://co.dev) makes app creation accessible to all.
