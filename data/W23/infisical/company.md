# Infisical

> Unified platform for secrets, certs, and privileged access management

Infisical is the leading open-source platform to manage secrets, certificates, configs, and access across your team and infrastructure.

Infisical stores your secrets and injects them back into your development workflow, CI/CD pipelines, and cloud infrastructure. It comes with secrets versioning, point-in-time recovery, audit logging, automatic secret rotation capabilities, and more.
