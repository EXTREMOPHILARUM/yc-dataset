# Invopop

> We help global software companies comply with local tax reporting 

Invopop helps global companies like SumUp, Fever, and DeepL manage electronic invoicing across more than 25 countries through a single API.

In the next five years, over 40 countries—including all EU member states—will require businesses to issue invoices electronically and report them in real-time to tax authorities. Instead of building and maintaining numerous local integrations, global companies can integrate just once with Invopop, streamlining compliance and saving time.
