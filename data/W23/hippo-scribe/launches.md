## Hippo Scribe - Free AI scribe for PT/OT/SLP

**Who We Serve**

Meet Marissa, a pediatric speech-language pathologist (SLP) who helps kids with various disorders. She sees eight children daily, keeping her energy high to keep them engaged. After work, she spends an hour writing down exactly what happened in each session. She's tired, it's hard to remember, and she's getting burnt out.

Now meet John, a physical therapist who sees 8-13 patients a day. He helps adults relieve pain, athletes get back to sports, and the elderly avoid falls. He loves helping people, but after work, he has to recall what happened and spend 1-2 hours writing it all down. He's skipping family dinners and slowly burning out.

**The Problem**

Hundreds of thousands of clinicians like Marissa and John are suffering from documentation burnout. They love helping patients, but it's overwhelming to remember everything and constantly write it down.

**The Solution**

Our AI Scribe listens to patient visits and generates compliant clinical documentation in the clinician's preferred format within 20 seconds after the visit. With us, clinicians save hours every week, don't have to type during patient visits, and can give patients 100% of their attention.
