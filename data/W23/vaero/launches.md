# Launches

## Vaero - AI for Patent Lawyers

**Tl;dr: Vaero is AI software to help patent lawyers work 5x faster. It drafts an entire patent application with AI based on invention information. The software includes a complete platform for patent lawyers to use AI for reviewing patent applications, responding to office actions, and enhancing invention disclosures.**

**🔬 The Problem:**

Patent lawyers spend hours drafting patent applications, which can be dozens and even hundreds of pages long. Patent applications include lots of detailed technical description about inventions in a formalistic way that is slow and laborious to write. Many patent lawyers find writing these technical descriptions to be their least favorite part of the job given that it can be tedious.

Moreover, the time-consuming nature of patent application drafting is also a major business problem for patent attorneys. Budgets per patent application have been on a flat or declining trend over the last two decades due to increased client demands. As a result, patent attorneys find themselves having to write patent applications with a smaller and smaller budget, which has led to increased time pressure. In some firms, patent application drafting has become an unprofitable loss leader that only serves to retain clients for more profitable legal services.

In addition to drafting patent applications, patent attorneys also deal with large volumes of documents in the other parts of their job. They often have to analyze long patent documents; they have to respond to patent application rejections from the patent office, which can be dozens of pages long; and they have to work with varying levels of invention information, which range from a few sentences to entire manuscripts.

**🪄 The Solution:**

Vaero is AI software built specifically for patent lawyers to speed up their patent drafting and other daily tasks:

* Vaero drafts an entire patent application using AI based on the same inputs that patent lawyers use
* Patent applications are written to the highest legal standards, based on our CEO’s ten years as a patent lawyer including at one of the biggest U.S. law firms
* Automatically review existing patent applications to assist with legal analysis
* Enhance invention disclosures from inventors
