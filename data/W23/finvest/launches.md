### **Problem**

In an era where you can instantly access virtually anything from your phone, the tools for building real wealth remain frustratingly out of reach for most people. This isn't by chance - it's the result of systemic barriers that have persisted even as technology has transformed nearly every other aspect of our lives. Three critical obstacles stand between everyday investors and their financial goals:

* **Cost Barrier:** While 70% of millionaires work with financial advisors, only 37% of the general population has access to professional guidance. With advisors charging between $2,000-$7,500 annually or up to 1% of assets, financial advice remains out of reach for most people.
* **Quality Barrier:** People are flooded with generic financial content and advice that doesn’t reflect their needs. Without guidance tailored to their portfolio and goals, identifying what actually matters is very difficult. The result is sub-optimal decisions and results.
* **Access & Knowledge Barrier:** Modern investors juggle multiple platforms - trading apps, news websites, and spreadsheets – just to understand their complete financial picture. The financial world's complexity adds another layer of difficulty, requiring people to study and understand market mechanics and investment concepts before making any decision. This combination makes it difficult for the public to invest with confidence.
