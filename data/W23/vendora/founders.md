# Founders

## Sunny Singh
**Founder**

Sunny Singh grew up in supermarkets - his family owns and operates a mid-sized chain in the US. He has carried a passion for food and retail throughout his career. Prior to founding Vendora, Sunny was the Head of US Strategy & Expansion for Uber Grocery/Cornershop. He joined Cornershop from Amazon, where he spent years across their grocery businesses, speciality fulfillment, and search science and AI teams. Sunny graduated from Columbia University and is a proud native New Yorker.
