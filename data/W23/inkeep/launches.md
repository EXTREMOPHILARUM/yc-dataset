Hi everyone, we’re Nick and Robert - co-founders of [Inkeep](https://inkeep.com/).

As founders, we know that fast, quality support helps great products stand out.

Inkeep accelerates every step of support with:

🔍 An AI assistant for end-users

🎧 A copilot for support teams

📚 Tools to fix content gaps

Inkeep works with any docs or support platform; see our [showcase](https://inkeep.com/showcase?example=anthropic&tab=aiForCustomers) for examples.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=85650&key=user_uploads/1161446/2fab07e8-72a9-4e56-9fb9-e14f38f411c6)

### Problem: today’s tools focus too much on automation

Users of modern products expect two things:

(1) great self-serve experiences

(2) fast human help when they need it

AI support solutions often forget about this and end up feeling like a barrier to getting “real” help. 

### Solution - AI that builds trust

Our approach is simple: build practical tools for every step of the support process with the end-user as the #1 priority.

**Assistant for users**

Inkeep taps into your docs, help center, and other content to provide direct answers to user questions. Our assistant cites its sources, **admits when it doesn’t know**, and hands off to your support channels.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=85650&key=user_uploads/1161446/66cc6667-df67-4209-baa3-6ea0e6b38fd3)

**Copilot for support teams**

When your support team needs to step in, our CX copilot:

✍️ Drafts detailed answers

🔗 Links to relevant docs and past tickets

⚡ Summarizes long tickets 

🔔 Suggests to-dos and next steps

[Demo:](https://youtu.be/4_yEpMkdua4)

**Tools to fix content gaps**

The #1 way to improve support over time is to improve your content (or product).\
Inkeep helps close that loop with:

1. [AI reports](https://docs.inkeep.com/analytics/reports) that pinpoint and prioritize gaps in your content.
2. A [Ticket to FAQ](https://go.inkeep.com/ticket-into-faq) tool to create new content from closed tickets.

## Inkeep - Conversational search for your developer product 💬🔍

> tl;dr: [Inkeep](https://www.inkeep.com/?utm_campaign=yc) provides conversational search for developer-focused companies. We use LLMs to understand a company’s docs, GitHub issues, Stack Overflow questions, and more to answer developer questions and increase customer activation.

With years of experience building developer-focused products, we are excited to build Inkeep.

### **Problem:** Any blocker or unanswered question is a drop-off point

Developers evaluate a product by how easy it is to get started and use it for their use case. Optimizing this experience is the #1 goal for many developer-focused companies, but it’s hard to do well because:

* Knowledge about the product is scattered across docs, GitHub issues, Stack Overflow, forums, private communities, and more.
* Answering technical issues and questions at scale is hard.
* Developers often prefer to self-troubleshoot.

### **Solution:** Conversational search powered by all your existing content

[Inkeep](https://www.inkeep.com/?utm_campaign=yc) automatically indexes all of a developer tool company’s content with zero effort required. We use fine-tuned LLMs that fully understand your product to power search and chat experiences tailored for developers.

Inkeep is embeddable as a:

* Search bar for your docs
* Chat widget for your app or landing page
* Slack or Discord bot for your community

Your developers can search or ask anything and get answers immediately. And you’ll be able to get insights on what they're getting stuck on.
