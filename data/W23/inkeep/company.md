# Inkeep

> Build AI Agent teammates and automations in code or no-code

Inkeep is the only agent platform designed to help both developers and non-developers ship Agent-powered assistants and automations.

Companies like Anthropic, Midjourney, Clay, PostHog, and Postman deploy Inkeep Agents at scale to help with everything from support to sales.

Highlights:
- A No-Code Builder and Developer Framework with full 2-way sync
- Unified search and RAG for knowledge bases, docs, and company data
- MCPs and integrations with your apps, APIs, and software
- Intelligent insights and monitoring over how Agents perform

With Inkeep Agents, enterprises and high-growth teams empower their employees with AI teammates that help get work done and automate repeatable tasks.

Backed by Khosla Ventures, Y-Combinator, Great Point Ventures and leading CEOs and founders.
