## Hindsight – Competitive Intelligence That’s Actually Intelligent

Deals are more competitive than ever, and sales teams don’t feel prepared to win. Product marketing teams constantly get hit with questions like:

* “How do we beat \[competitor\]?”
* “Do we have a battlecard for this?”
* “What’s our answer to \[new competitor launch\]?”

To find answers, teams have to dig through call recordings, piece together competitor research, and hope they’re making the right call.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=88481&key=user_uploads/1213493/b87ca67c-de33-4a14-9dfa-28abc23ce023)

### **The Problem**

Competitive intelligence today is **broken**:

* **Prioritizing junk data** – Teams spend tons of money on web monitoring tools that flood them with alerts that don't actually matter.
* **Ignoring the best source of intelligence—actual buyers** – Most marketing teams review **<5%** of calls and talk to **<1%** of lost prospects. That’s not enough to know why deals are really won or lost.
* **Outdated content** – PMMs spend weeks building battlecards that go stale before they’re used. Sales stops trusting them, and adoption plummets.

### **How Hindsight Fixes This**

<https://youtu.be/vqNqL9ZylVU>

Unlike other CI tools, Hindsight prioritizes competitive insights directly from your buyers AND helps you collect it.

* **Win-Loss at Scale** – AI analyzes 100% of your CRM deals, calls, and emails to uncover decision drivers, identify competitors, and clip key quotes from deals.
* **Automated Buyer Feedback** – Hindsight runs unbiased debriefs with won and lost prospects to uncover how they really compare you vs. competitors—giving you the data sales teams actually need.
* **Real-Time Competitive Intelligence** – Hindsight monitors the web and your other sales tools daily to build and maintain battlecards automatically.
* **Just-in-Time Sales Enablement** – Insights and battlecards are delivered directly in Slack or email so reps always have the right info before their calls. Automatically updated as new insights roll in.
* And a lot more!
