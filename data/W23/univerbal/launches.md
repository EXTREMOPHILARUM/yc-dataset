## Univerbal - AI Language Learning App

## **TL;DR: Language learning that’s as unique as you are.**

If you’ve ever learnt a language, you’ve probably spent hours memorizing vocabulary or doing infinite grammar exercises only to then realize you can’t really… speak the language.

The [Univerbal](https://univerbal.app/) app helps you get confident to speak by practicing with real-life, open conversations with an AI conversation partner that adapts to your answers and your level. Most importantly, never judges you!

Your personal AI Tutor creates a language study plan combining both the language skills you’d like to work towards, based on CEFR language education standards and the conversation topics you’re interested in. **_No two study plans are the same because no two language learning journeys are the same._**

## **🤔 The problem**

People learn a language to be able to speak it, but they end up memorizing vocabulary and doing boring grammar exercises and in the process, forget or they get too shy to actually practice speaking

Practicing with native speakers can feel like a burden, language tutors can soon get expensive and using some language apps can only get you so far.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=85531&key=user_uploads/566264/8a04773c-911c-4f8d-bbdd-392e51ee0f0b)

## **💡 The solution**

We’re based in Switzerland: the country with 4 official languages🇨🇭 so it was only natural to try to solve this problem by building an AI-powered language learning app to help everyone increase their confidence to speak a new language.

Univerbal was born out of our own frustration as language learners (initially Philipp’s as he was learning Greek to connect to his Cypriot family heritage). It was obvious that the only way to get better was to speak, but it became both difficult to find the time for tutor sessions and very expensive 💸

Univerbal gives everyone access to a conversation partner and language tutor that’s customized to their own needs.

## **👉 How does it work?**

With the Univerbal app, each user gets a personal AI Language Tutor that creates a study plan based on the language goals they wants to work towards **and** the topics they’re interested in talking about.

Developed in collaboration with the Language Department at the University of Zürich, this ensures that everyone gets the language training based on CEFR language education standards, that’s also personalized to their own needs, skills and interests!

No two study plans are the same.

Users can practice with real-life conversations like ordering coffee or going for a job interview, get help from their AI Language Tutor in their own language and practice with short vocabulary exercises.

## Quazel - Talk your way to fluency

David, Sam, and I (Philipp) have been hard at work to build [Quazel](https://quazel.com), a language learning app that enables users to have natural and unscripted conversations with an AI tutor.

[🌐Try it (no account needed)](https://quazel.com/try) | 🍎Get the [iOS app](https://apps.apple.com/qa/app/quazel/id6443595452?platform=iphone) | 🤖 Android app is pending review.

**Problem**\
Right now, conversational practice gets neglected, and boring grammar and vocabulary are emphasized too much! Additionally, tailored tutoring is too expensive, cumbersome to schedule and beginners are often self-conscious about the way they talk.

_Background_

I faced the problem of exact issue a while ago when I started to learn Greek and thought to myself there must be a better way! To be honest, I really want Quazel for myself but seeing that other people really like it is the cherry on top 😄.

\
**Solution**\
With Quazel learners are thrown into real-world scenarios where they actually need to talk. Speak into the mic & listen to AI responses. Features include:

* Scene Builder - Create your own scenes & get an AI-generated image of your conversation partner!
* Grammar Analysis
* Built-in translator
* …much more
