### **Instant ⚡ Objection Handling with Grounded AI response**

How many times have we seen leads dying off because we couldn’t reply to them in time. _A recent HBS study says any response after 15 min of receiving a query reduces the chances of conversion by 80%._ Hence, we introduce Instant Objection Handling.

_Here’s how it works:_

While setting up the sequence, you provide AI SDR (a.k.a. Alisha) with all and any of your product/sales info. Supported format of files includes **PDF, Websites, CSV, Plain text, Docs.**

Now, lets say your prospect asks a question “How much does your pricing look like?”, “Can you share some case studies relevant to our industry”. Our AI looks into all the documents and provides an answer. In case it does not find the answer, it does not hallucinate, instead alerts the user.

### **Calendar Coordination without Calendly**

Sending over Calendly links and asking your prospect to book is never considered a sales best practice. First of all, it shifts the burden of work to the prospect (they have to click through, check your availability, and book). And naturally many people won't take the effort, so conversion suffers.\
\
Plus, in many cultures and settings, it's actually considered rude. It's like saying "have your people call my people". \
\
So here we come. Alisha AI SDR is integrated with your calendar and goes on to suggest vacant slots to the prospects. Based on the response of the prospect, Alisha AI SDR coordinates like a human and books a meeting accordingly.

### **Even more control in Composing Messages**

We found out that AI is not really great at composing Sales emails. However, it is great at collating information from multiple sources and plugging them appropriately in a specific format.

Alisha can now plug in inline images, links seamlessly in the email body.
