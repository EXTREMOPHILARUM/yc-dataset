# The problem

It’s currently hard to make long-form movies using AI tools.

Filmmakers need **one place** where the world, the shots, the voices, and the music stay in sync.

# Our solution

A tool that keeps context on a film and lets you generate **inside** it.

* **Script breakdown:** characters, scenes, dialogue.
* **Character design:** bring your art or generate; lock a base look + costumes.
* **Voices:** record or clone. One default voice per character.
* **Shot list:** type, lens, angle, move.
* **OST:** generate cues per scene; loopable; stems when supported.
* **Final movie:** assemble into a timed cut with dialogue + music. Export MP4

## Rubbrband - Generate images in your own style, repeatably

Hi everyone! We’re the team behind [Rubbrband](https://rubbrband.com/). We’re building a highly controllable way to generate images.

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kbkMARYxdBI>

**Problem:**

Generating images in your own unique style is difficult with the current AI tools. Creatives want specific controls over aspects of an image, like the color palette, as well as control over which model they’re using to generate images.

Additionally, consistently reproducing that style is difficult because AI models don’t have the ability to remember past interactions and preferences. This makes it hard for artists and designers to achieve their desired results repeatedly.

**Solution:**

**Rubbrband Workflows** allow creatives to design steps for generating images repeatably.

By setting up a workflow, you can define a sequence of steps that produce consistent results. This ensures that every image generated follows your specific style and preferences, eliminating the variability often encountered with other AI tools.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=81865&key=user_uploads/270510/aa30399b-e4ee-4f76-b0db-35c223885eb4)

Rubbrband offers the following features:

* 🎨 **Highly controllable**: Enjoy detailed control over color palettes, reference images, and other controls.
* 🖼️ **Access to thousands of models**: Utilize any CivitAI checkpoint or LORA for your image generation needs.
* 💡 **Smart Prompting**: Get suggestions for image styles in your prompts to enhance your creative process.
* 🧩 **Extensive Node Library**: Access over 20 nodes, including SD, ControlNet, Masking nodes, GPT-4, and more.
* 🛠️ **Simplified Playground Interface**: Use workflows in an intuitive, easy-to-navigate Playground page.
* 🌐 **Community Sharing**: Share your workflows and discover others’ creations on the Community page.

## Rubbrband: Train open-source ML models in a line of code

### **What is Rubbrband?**

Rubbrband is a CLI tool that enables developers to rapidly experiment with and train the latest open-source ML models. For example, Rubbrband allows you to fine-tune your own version of Dreambooth’s Stable Diffusion in just 1 line of code:

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=69806&key=user_uploads/270510/85f6ebcc-2d0f-49ce-adb9-e2dbb7c23253)

### **Who are we?**

We’re college roommates who met at orientation at UC Berkeley. While the state-of-the-art looked magical, it always seemed to be out of reach.

### **The problem**

There’s a deluge of breakthroughs in Machine Learning every week.

However, it’s extremely challenging for developers to leverage these open-source works to solve their own problems. _This is primarily due to the fact that researchers and ML developers optimize for development velocity, and not for reproducibility or extendibility._

As a result, the entire Machine Learning space seems to be held together with duct tape, as people throw together undocumented scripts to start training runs, which always seem to break one week later.

### **The solution**

With Rubbrband, **we want to bring a world-class experience to training ML models**. By creating a simple Python library that abstracts away training environments (CUDA drivers, pip dependencies, and C libraries), and exposing a few commands of interest, _developers will be able to rapidly experiment with the bleeding edge in Machine Learning_. By doing so, developers will be able to solve hard problems that otherwise may have remained unsolved.
