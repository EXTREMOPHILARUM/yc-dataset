### **The Problem**

**Private equity runs on relationships—but firms can't see their own network.**

Every PE firm has thousands of professional connections across partners, principals, alumni, operating advisors, and portfolio executives. When a deal team wants to reach a specific CEO or find companies matching their thesis, all that relationship capital is invisible.

The result:

* **"Do we know anyone at this company?"** - Asked dozens of times a week, answered by guessing
* **"Who's our best path to this founder?"** - Hours of LinkedIn searching for each target
* **"What companies fit our thesis?"** - Days of manual market mapping
* **Cold outreach to warm targets** - Missing relationships that could have opened doors

Firms pay millions for databases of company information, but the most valuable data—their own relationships—sits unused.

### **Our Solution**

Verata combines relationship intelligence with private company data in one platform built specifically for PE.

**Relationship Path Mapping**

See how your firm connects to any target company or executive. We identify high-quality, verified professional relationships—not LinkedIn's low-quality connections—and show you the warm paths that turn cold outreach into warm introductions.
