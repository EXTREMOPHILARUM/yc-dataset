# Launches

## Plutis: On-demand, modern-day mental healthcare

**TLDR:** We are building a mental health platform that adapts to your unique journey. Engage with specialized providers, access AI-based coaching tailored to your needs, and discover services to boost your mental well-being. **Download our** [**iOS app**](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/plutis/id6450721690) on the App Store and give it a try!

### **Problem**

According to the CDC, [more than 1 in 5 US adults live with a mental illness](https://www.cdc.gov/mentalhealth/learn/index.htm) (over 50 million Americans), more than half of whom do not receive treatment. Furthermore, [there are an estimated 350 individuals for every one mental health provider](https://mhanational.org/issues/state-mental-health-america) in the U.S.

**Currently, patients struggle with:**

1. Lack of personalization and options.
2. Constant discovery/search process to find the right services for them.
3. High costs/lack of insurance coverage for services.
4. Lack of cost-effective and continuous support in between sessions.

**Therapists and mental health service providers are looking for ways to:**

1. Gain access to a larger clientele (for therapy and coaching).
2. Drive up patient engagement and see more patients that are suited for their unique practice/specialty.
3. See clients / operate in more states than those they are currently licensed in.
4. Get credentialed with major payors to see a wider array of clients.
5. Off-load complex billing and reimbursement systems that enable them to see more patients without the overhead/headache.
6. Leverage online platforms while still maintaining the ability to set their own prices and schedules.

### **Introducing Plutis**

We are creating a more modern mental health care platform. Our platform allows patients to receive personalized care and gain access to a variety of services to improve their mental health, ranging from live sessions with service providers to AI-based coaching and feedback. We also help therapists, coaches, and other mental health service providers expand their practice and see more engaged clients and patients, on their own terms.

Our initial platform features are as follows:

**For Patients**

1. AI-based journaling with tailored feedback, advice, and guidance based on your unique goals.
2. Monthly goal-setting and constant check-ins/recommendations to keep you on track.
3. On-demand AI coaching for issues you may face.
4. Easy access to service providers (therapists, coaches, etc.) via scheduling or on-demand availability.
5. Access to wellness circles (peer support as well as facilitated support groups for almost any situation).
6. Access to curated content from communities centered around challenges you may be facing or certain life events.

**For Therapists**

1. Expanding your reach with patients and clients to help you grow your practice.
2. Services to reduce workload while also creating a more engaged client base.
3. Credentialing with major payors and multi-state licensing.
4. Ability to set your own prices and schedule.
5. Streamlined billing and claims management to ensure payouts without the overhead/complexity.
