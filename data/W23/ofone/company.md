# OfOne

> AI-powered order taker for restaurant drive-thrus

OfOne is building software to automate order taking at fast-food drive-thrus. Using conversational AI, we help restaurant chains operate more efficiently and level-up their customer experience.

OfOne can answer questions, intelligently upsell - like suggesting ice cream on a hot day - and always says please and thank you.

There are 200,000 drive-thrus in the US and they are the industry's largest order channel.

Our mission is to deliver human centric experiences that help restaurant operators prosper, deliver delight for their customers, and empower their workers.
