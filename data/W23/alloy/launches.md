# **The story behind Alloy**

Hey! I’m [Simon Kubica](https://simonkubica.com), CEO of Alloy. I co-founded the company with [Christian Iacullo](https://christianiacullo.com) after our time working at Canva and Atlassian. We founded the company with one mission: give product leaders new superpowers.

Initially, we focused on the workflow of planning and prioritization. But with AI, we started to notice PMs could finally _do_ the work, rather than just plan it. We completely re-oriented the company to build a dedicated tool for this new PM workflow.

The problem is that even with new AI app builder tools, PMs are struggling with off-brand prototypes. Using chat to prototype is powerful, but the outputs didn’t look anything like their existing app.

We felt that your prototypes should look like your actual product.

So starting today, they actually can.
