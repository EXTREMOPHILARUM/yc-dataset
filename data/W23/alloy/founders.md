# Founders

## Simon Kubica
**Founder**

Simon Kubica is an Australian entrepreneur and the co-founder and CEO of Alloy, one of the few Australian companies ever accepted into Y Combinator. He has featured in Forbes and Business Insider, and was previously a product leader at Atlassian, working on Jira, Forge, and new products and platforms.

## Christian Iacullo
**Founder**

Christian Iacullo is an Australian software engineer who is the co-founder and CTO of Index. Before founding Index, Christian was a software engineer at Canva, where he worked on their Editor, Developer Platform, Education and Collaboration products. Christian began his career at Atlassian, where he worked on Confluence.
