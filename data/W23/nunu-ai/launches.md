# Launches

## 🎮nunu.ai - AI agents to play and test games

**TLDR**: [nunu.ai](http://nunu.ai/) is building the first multimodal agents to test and play games. We infuse AI agents into the game development process, starting at QA all the way to player simulation.

**Trailer**: [ ](https://youtu.be/ZezZjkuY75M)

### The Problem

In the gaming industry, quality assurance is a critical yet often labor-intensive process. One among many. Studios invest countless hours in testing games for bugs, balancing gameplay, and ensuring a smooth player experience. The repetitive and manual nature of these tasks not only strains resources but can also lead to missed opportunities for innovation and improvement. Releasing unfished or undertested games is one of the largest pain points in the industry.

### The Solution

At [nunu.ai](http://nunu.ai/), we've developed multimodal AI agents that revolutionize the way game studios approach these challenges. Our AI agents are designed to automate and enhance various aspects of game development, starting with quality assurance. These agents are capable of playing games, reasoning, and adapting, all while providing valuable insights and feedback. With minimal adjustments, our AI can adapt to any game, offering interpretability and interactability unprecedented in the industry.

### Why Now

In short: Large Multimodal Models. Here is why:

1. Generalizability: Unlike other systems, our AI requires minimal retraining and can adapt to any game instantly.
2. Interpretability: Our agents can explain their actions and decisions, similar to interacting with a human player. They're capable of generating detailed reports on their observations.
3. Interactability: Studios can interact with our AI in real-time, altering tasks, and engaging in dialogue to gain deeper insights.
