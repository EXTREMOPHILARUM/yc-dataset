# FlexDesk

> AI Agents for corporate real estate teams

Real estate rent is often the largest non-payroll expense for companies. FlexDesk replaces fragmented, manual back-office work with intelligent agents that execute the workflows required to manage and optimize portfolios.

Internal real estate teams at enterprises like Reddit and Confluent use FlexDesk in the same way that Travel teams use Navan and Procurement teams use Zip.
