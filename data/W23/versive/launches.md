## Versive - AI-powered synthesis for user interviews

We’re [Eric](https://www.linkedin.com/in/erictli/) and [David](https://www.linkedin.com/in/correa-david/), the founders of [Versive](https://www.getversive.com/).

**tl;dr:** Versive now helps you turn user interviews and customer calls into ready-to-share reports. Upload your recordings to get shareable research reports with key quotes and actionable takeaways. Plus, use AI chat to dive deeper and get answers to specific questions.

# **Problem**

Talking to users helps companies make better decisions, but the process of conducting and analyzing user research is slow and manual.

Imagine you’re a researcher who just interviewed 10 users. To share your findings, you need to watch each recording, summarize the conversations, extract key themes and quotes, consolidate your notes into a single report, and then package everything into a polished document. It’ll take at least a week before you can share actionable insights with your team, and you’ll have spent hours that could have been used to drive actions or launch new studies.

# **Solution**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=83020&key=user_uploads/322456/bf2ff14a-662f-4762-be93-3ec32360a76b)

Versive’s mission is to help companies make faster, customer-informed decisions by making it easier to conduct high-quality user research. Our [AI-moderated interviews](https://www.ycombinator.com/launches/K81-versive-ai-powered-surveys-that-collect-interview-quality-insights) have already helped leading brands gather in-depth feedback from thousands of users. However, we believe that human-moderated interviews are still key for capturing nuanced feedback and building customer empathy.

That’s why we’re launching a new product that enables research teams to instantly synthesize interview recordings into shareable reports and summaries.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=83020&key=user_uploads/322456/4bd673aa-28a0-439e-a631-23f32c25dc7f)

# **How it works**

1. **Customize your reports:** Tailor the structure of your reports to fit the needs of your study, no coding or prompting expertise required.
2. **Upload interviews:** Import audio/video recordings or VTT transcripts.
3. **Get instant summaries:** You’ll get consistent, shareable summaries for each interview, complete with quotes and actionable takeaways.
4. **Get an aggregate report:** We’ll also generate a report for your entire study, with citations that link back to your transcripts.
5. **Chat with your results:** Have more questions? Chat with your study data and get immediate, citation-backed answers.

## Versive - AI-powered surveys that collect interview-quality insights

**tl;dr:** _Versive makes gathering user insights and customer feedback easier. Our AI-powered surveys ask intelligent follow-up questions to give you interview-quality insights in a fraction of the time._

Hi everyone, we’re David and Eric, the founders of Versive.

## **The problem**

Most companies don't speak to their users enough, especially as they scale. As a result, it's difficult to make informed product and marketing decisions.

In Eric’s past life as a product manager and designer, he conducted dozens of user studies at startups and public companies. Each time, it took weeks of scheduling, interviewing, and synthesizing to get from questions to answers. As a result, his teams collected feedback and validated ideas far less than they would have liked.

Existing tools don't solve this problem well. Interviews and focus groups are time-consuming and expensive to set up. Surveys only yield surface-level insights and are cumbersome to build. Even at companies with dedicated research teams, few requests get approved, leaving most PMs, designers, and marketers on their own.

## **The solution**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=77377&key=user_uploads/322456/1c77cc3e-958b-4f93-9803-ec92b67eadfe)

Versive enables teams to collect interview-level insights at the scale and speed of a survey. We support voice interviews, dozens of languages, concept and design testing, structured questions, branding customization, and more.

1. Build your survey in Versive, or ask our AI assistant to generate one in seconds.
2. Our AI moderator administers your survey and asks intelligent follow-up questions to dig deeper into user responses.
3. We'll auto-generate summaries and insights, plus give you transcripts for each response.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=77377&key=user_uploads/322456/96a56e72-eb80-41d8-a926-8474268d77ce)

## **Who is this for?**

Whether you’re creating B2B software or selling consumer products, we can help.

* **Product and design teams:** you'll validate designs, roadmaps, and requirements in hours instead of weeks.
* **Marketing and customer teams:** you'll conduct market research and collect customer feedback more efficiently and with higher quality.
* **Research teams:** you'll be able to offload simple requests so you can focus on high-value studies.
