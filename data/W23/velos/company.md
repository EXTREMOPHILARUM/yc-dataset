# Velos

> Platform to automate manual back-office work

Velos helps non-technical operations teams automate complex, manual back-office tasks with AI workers instead of overseas teams.

Unlike traditional robotic process automation (RPA) platforms like UiPath, Velos automations use machine learning to reliably handle ambiguities in their tasks, eliminating the need for an army of maintenance engineers and consultants to build and maintain your automations.

We're automating the repetitive work people hate to do.
