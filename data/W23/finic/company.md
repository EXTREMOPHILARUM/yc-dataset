# Finic

> Detect and investigate financial crimes with AI

Finic helps fintechs and banks automate the detection and investigation of financial crimes. 

We automate human jobs-to-be-done for:
- Fraud operations teams
- AML/BSA teams
- Compliance teams
- Credit risk teams

While our approach is agentic, we use AI only where it's better than the alternative. Our vertical focus allows us to be more accurate and efficient than horizontal agent companies on the same task.
