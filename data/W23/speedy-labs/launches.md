## Speedy - Democratizing marketing to SMBs

**TL;DR**\
Speedy gets traffic for SMBs at affordable prices through content creation that is 10x faster using Generative AI. We create a custom AI model for each business, that helps you curate hyper personalized content like blogs, socials and get organic traffic on your site.

### **Problem**

* SMBs cannot afford the high fees of digital marketing agencies that go into thousands of dollars per month.
* Due to a limited marketing budget, they often lose traffic to more prominent companies.
* They lack the time and resources to create and run content marketing campaigns.

### **Solution**

Using Generative AI, Speedy creates SEO-optimized blogs and social media content, which SMBs can use to do effective marketing at a price that works for them.

**How does it work?**

1. Once you onboard to our monthly/annual plan, we conduct a branding workshop to feed into our models to help bring your brand’s voice to the content.
2. For a monthly subscription, you get 4 blogs and 16 social media posts that are SEO-optimized.
3. For an annual subscription, you get monthly benefits + a monthly newsletter + an annual content strategy.
