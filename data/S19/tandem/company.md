# Tandem

> Tandem is a virtual office for remote and distributed teams.

Tandem is a digital office for remote teams. You can see who's online, what app they're working in, and talk/screenshare/video in one click. 

We designed Tandem by deconstructing the affordances and benefits of a physical space, and transcribing them to a virtual space.
