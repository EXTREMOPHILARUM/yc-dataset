# Flux

> Flux is building the payment network for alternative payments in…

Flux is building the payment network for alternative payments, with a focus on bringing acceptance of all BNPL options to on-site merchants so they can max their client's buying power.

Flux provides an instant-issuing and payment acceptance solution for consumer credit products, and a payment terminal for alternative credit (non-card) for retailers.
