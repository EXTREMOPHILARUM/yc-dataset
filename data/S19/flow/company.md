# Flow

> Improve your browsing experience by staying organized and intentful

Flow is building products to improve your browsing experience. The web browser is an indispensable part of our daily lives - we use it for work, shopping, entertainment, research, sharing memes, procrastinating, connecting with friends and family. Yet it remains tough to be organized and intentful when using it. We’re building products to dramatically improve your browsing experience, and Flow is our foray into intentful browsing.
