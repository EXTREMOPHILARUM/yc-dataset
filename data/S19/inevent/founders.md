## Pedro Góes
**Founder**

Pedro Góes is currently the CEO of InEvent. Previously Pedro started a company in college where he had more than 10,000 monthly active paying users. He is a 40 under 40 founder by Association Magazine, a UK Event Tech Award Winner and a Gold Crocodile Award Winner.
