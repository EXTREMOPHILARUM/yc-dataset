# Wingman

> Wingman helps inside sales reps with coaching while they're on a call.

Wingman is a software for sales teams that listens to sales calls and automatically surfaces suggestions to sales reps in real-time. The sales calls are also transcribed and analysed for postmortems, reviews and coaching.
