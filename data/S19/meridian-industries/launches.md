# Launches

## Index - A next generation business intelligence tool

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=85313&key=user_uploads/284902/bf90810a-8f45-48eb-86c6-e6eb013c5c2a)

We're excited to launch [Index](https://index.app/), a next-generation business intelligence platform that makes data analytics delightful and accessible. After years of wrestling with clunky BI tools, we built what we wished existed - a beautiful, fast, and intuitive way to explore your business data.

## **What is Index?**

Index is a modern business intelligence tool that helps teams make sense of their data without the complexity. We've focused on creating an experience that's both powerful for analysts and approachable for business users.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=85313&key=user_uploads/284902/1e8e5a1e-f907-4c3a-ab96-a3b0892dde87)

**Key Features:**

* Lightning-fast query performance and interactions
* Intuitive interface with keyboard shortcuts & command bar
* Text-to-SQL capabilities powered by AI
* Real-time collaboration with multiplayer presence
* Works offline - continue analyzing when connectivity drops
* Dark & light mode support

## **Why We Built Index**

Traditional BI tools are:

* Slow and cumbersome to use
* Require deep technical knowledge
* Expensive with complicated pricing
* Poor user experience

We believe teams deserve better. Index brings the polish and performance you expect from modern software to the data analytics space.

## **How We're Different**

* **Pricing:** While traditional BI tools require expensive upfront contracts, Index is free to start with simple per-seat pricing as you grow.
* **Setup:** Index gets you analyzing data in minutes, not the weeks or months needed with traditional tools.
* **Technical Knowledge:** Traditional tools require deep technical expertise. Index is built for everyone - technical knowledge optional.
* **Performance:** Say goodbye to slow load times. Index delivers instant results, while traditional tools keep you waiting.
* **User Experience:** Unlike the complex, dated interfaces of traditional BI, Index brings you a modern, intuitive experience.

## **Pricing**

Index is **free to start** - no trials, no commitments. Pay only when you scale, with simple per-seat pricing.
