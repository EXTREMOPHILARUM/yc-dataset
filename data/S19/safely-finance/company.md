# Safely Finance

> BNPL for moving and renting.

Safely Finance (YCS19) is a pay-over-time solution for moving and renting. We remove price as a barrier to leasing for renters. We increase NOI and decrease bad debt for properties. Safely Finance embeds directly in moving and renting experiences with easy-to-integrate options via widget, API, or property management solution.
