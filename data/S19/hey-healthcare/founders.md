# Founders

## Andrew Coyle
**Founder**

Andrew Coyle is a Y Combinator alum, a former co-founder of Hey Healthcare (YC S19), and Flexport's founding designer. He also worked as an interaction designer at Google and Intuit and was the director of design at Flexport. He holds two patents for supply chain management and freight service selection. He is currently the head of design at Distro (YC S24), an enterprise sales AI company.
