# Heru

> Tax Automation Infrastructure

Heru is the tech-based tax solution that automates tax filing procedures for consumers and Enterprises in Mexico. Our technology automatically, files taxes, deducts expenses, issues invoices and collects payments all from one app. Easy, transparent and automated.

14 million digital workers and SMEs in Mexico face constant stress derived from their lack of knowledge on tax related issues. By providing these users a suite of tax based solutions, we will allow them to further progress in their financial health.
