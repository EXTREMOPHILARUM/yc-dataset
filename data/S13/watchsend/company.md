# Watchsend

> Videos of your iOS apps

Watchsend (YC S13) makes actionable software analytics that tells you where problems are and how to fix them by recording an entire app's state to extract behaviors, actionable analytics and visualize ordinary metrics in the context of the application's appearance, all in the familiar form of video.
