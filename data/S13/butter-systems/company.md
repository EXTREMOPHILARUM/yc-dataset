# Butter Systems

> Replacing the paper restaurant menu with an interactive experience at…

Butter Systems lets customers at restaurants order food/drink on demand at the table, using a dedicated tablet or their own mobile device. The product  spurs more impulse orders and enables every transaction to happen, whether the server is available or not. Mobile payment also allows for a seamless customer experience and faster table turn.
