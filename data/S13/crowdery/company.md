# Crowdery

> [Now Clara, YC S14- claralabs.com]

Crowdery is an online platform, allowing its users to vote on their favorite product design out of a set. It then builds its users a personalized marketplace of their top picks, and top products are opened for them to pre-order at a discount.

Crowdery was launched in 2013 by Maran Nelson and Aditya Viswanathan. It is based in Mountain View, C.A.
