# Senic

> Simple Controls for Sonos, Philips Hue and other Smart Home products

Our mission is to empower every family member and business to set the right ambience of sound and light for every moment. 

Our company offers simple wireless switches and remotes for products like Sonos or Philips Hue, software and simplify the installation of Smart Home systems and add-on solutions to create a great smart home experience for consumers, prosumers and professionals.
