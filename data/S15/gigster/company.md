# Gigster

> Build and manage distributed teams

Gigster is democratizing access to software by enabling organizations harness the best teams regardless of where they are in the world.

Businesses with access to custom software have thousands of times more leverage than those without. We believe that by democratizing access as well as the ability to build great software we can increase the pace and efficiency of human advancement. 

Core to our approach is building the best place to work on the internet. We are building the largest network of exceptional engineers, designers, and product managers and making this network available to businesses. We are also using software and insights from thousands of projects to improve the odds of successful outcomes. 

Gigster is backed by Andreessen Horowitz, Redpoint, Marc Benioff, Adam D'Angelo, Michael Jordan and others.  Gigster has served well over 1000 customers including multiple F100 companies spanning a range of industries.
