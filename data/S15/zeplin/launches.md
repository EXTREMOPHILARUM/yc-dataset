# What is Omlet for VS Code?

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=88516&key=user_uploads/15544/f090d5bb-ec09-4c5b-ac34-4e4dc441e638)

Omlet for VS Code helps you **analyze how and where your React components (and their props) are used right inside your editor** — helping you confidently update, clean up, and maintain your component library.

Here’s a demo I just recorded: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BsZMMcHG3Ks>

If you want to learn more, read our full update here:

🔗 [Introducing Omlet for VS Code](https://omlet.dev/blog/omlet-for-vscode/)
