# CareLedger

> CareLedger helps companies save money on healthcare expenses by…

CareLedger helps employers find the best priced quality healthcare for their employees in their area. By providing pricing data on local providers and motivating employees to save, CareLedger helps employers get healthcare spend under control and reduced by more than 10%. They provide insight into who is a high-performing, high-value provider, and then motivate employees to spend less.
