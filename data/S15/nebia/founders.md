# Founders

## Philip Winter
**Founder/CEO**

Co-founded Nebia in 2013 (S15). Designed water atomizing showers that provided a steam shower experience, with a powerful spray, while saving over 50% of the water/energy used by traditional showers. Pre-sold over $10m on Kickstarter through six campaigns. Sold the company in 2022 to Brondell.
