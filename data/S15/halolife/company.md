# Halolife

> Halolife is an online platform to find and plan a burial or cremation…

Founded in May 2015 and based in San Francisco, California, Halolife is an online platform that provides step-by-step guidance to plan and find a burial or cremation service that meets your needs, budget, and location. Halolife is the only trusted online end-of-life marketplace that offers complete transparency of business reviews and prices of funeral and cremation providers.

Whether you are a family that needs immediate assistance or planning for the future, our team of caring experts will help you every step of the way. We eliminate the need to shop around by matching you with the right funeral provider. Currently, Halolife is operating in the state California with plans to expand to throughout the United States in the near future.
