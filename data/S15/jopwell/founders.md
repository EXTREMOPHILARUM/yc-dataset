## Porter Braswell
**Founder/CEO**

Porter Braswell is the co-founder and Executive Chairman of Jopwell (YC S'15). Porter is also the founder of 2045 Studio, launching Spring 2023. 

Porter's work centers around diversity in the workplace and supporting corporations in building inclusive cultures where communities of color can thrive.
