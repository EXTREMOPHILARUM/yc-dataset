# GiveCampus

> The fundraising platform for schools.

GiveCampus is the world's #1 fundraising platform for non-profit educational institutions. Trusted by millions of donors and more than 1,500 colleges, universities, and K-12 schools, our mission is to advance the quality, the affordability, and the accessibility of education. Since 2015, we have facilitated more than $7 billion in charitable giving.
