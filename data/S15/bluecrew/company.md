# Bluecrew

> Bluecrew is a web based staffing agency.

BlueCrew is an on-demand, data-driven staffing platform for blue collar jobs in Los Angeles, San Francisco Bay Area, Sacramento, and Reno. Bluecrew is a seed-stage, YC backed company creating software to automate outdated, and inefficient traditional temp agencies. We are the best platform to quickly fill positions with high-quality workers.

Bluecrew is an ideal staffing partner for companies looking for temporary, seasonal, or temp to hire workers for jobs including event staff, warehouse staff, drivers, forklift operators, data entry personnel, movers, and customer service reps. By innovating on screening, matching, dispatching, and time tracking technology, BlueCrew eliminates the major pain points of other alternatives.
