# Click & Grow

> Click & Grow makes indoor urban gardens and farms that take complete…

Click & Grow is an innovative, international company that’s changing the way people think about plant cultivation and food. Our unique line of Smart Gardens and Wall Farms uses automatically controlled technology and specially developed Smart Soil to keep oxygen, water, and nutritional ingredients at optimal levels so plants thrive naturally. This helps anyone effortlessly grow plants with complete success and no need for GMOs, pesticides, fungicides, insecticides, plant hormones, or any other suspicious substances.

Venture-backed, and a favorite of Wired, TechCrunch, Business Insider and more, Click & Grow boasts a forward-thinking team, based in the San Francisco Bay Area, and Tallinn and Tartu, Estonia. Our current products are the first steps towards a greater revolution in urban farming, gardening, and beyond.
