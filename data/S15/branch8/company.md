# Branch8

> Branch8 helps sellers to list and manage products on e-commerce…

Branch8 offers e-commerce sellers access to a single platform that lets them list their products across a wide swath of e-commerce sites. Updating and delisting items are similarly simple. Plus, the company also offers logistics and order management – the idea is that it can be a one-stop shop for e-commerce sellers, helping them to earn more money while saving them valuable time.

Branch8 is created by a team of sellers and engineers. They live and breathe e-commerce. In the past, They were a top seller selling across Lazada, Qoo10, Rakuten, Amazon and Ebay. Their team consists of individuals from Lazada, Rocket Internet, Facebook and Microsoft.
