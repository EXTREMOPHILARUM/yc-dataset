# Fetch

> Marketplace for self-service truck rental.

Fetch is a marketplace where you can rent a nearby truck instantly using just your phone. Instead of waiting in line at a traditional rental company, you can instantly book a truck in your neighborhood and unlock it with your smart phone - all at a lower price and without the hassle.
