# Founders

## Pushkar Mukewar
**Founder/CEO**

Pushkar Mukewar is the Founder & CEO of Drip, a digital trade finance and commerce platform he started in 2016 with the mission of making global trade simple and accessible for small businesses. Drip builds modern, AI-native infrastructure that streamlines credit assessment and automates end-to-end trade workflows for exporters and importers worldwide.

Under Pushkar’s leadership, Drip has facilitated over $8 billion in cross-border trade, serving 11,000+ sellers and buyers across 60+ countries.
