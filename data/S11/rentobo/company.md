# Rentobo

> The easiest way to find a tenant.

Rentobo is a tool that makes it…

Rentobo is a new tool built for landlords, managers, and agents of all sizes to simplify the process of finding and signing a new tenant for your rental property.

Enter your listing details once at Rentobo and we will help you promote your property across major rental search sites.

Rentobo organizes rental applications, credit scores, and social networks into an easy to use tenant profile - everything you need to make a decision.

Tenants use Rentobo to sign their lease and submit their deposit electronically. Combined with online applications you can stop pushing paper.

For more information, please visit us at: http://www.rentobo.com
