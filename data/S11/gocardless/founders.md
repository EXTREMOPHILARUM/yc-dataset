# Founders

## Hiroki Takeuchi
**Founder/CEO**

Hiroki is co-founder and CEO of GoCardless, a global leader in direct bank payment solutions. Prior to founding GoCardless, he worked as a Business Analyst at McKinsey & Co before succumbing to the entrepreneurial bug and enrolling in the Y Combinator program. He studied Mathematics at Oxford University where he served as Vice President of Oxford Entrepreneurs.
