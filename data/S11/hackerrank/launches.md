# Launches

## Chakra: AI interviewer that finally works

I’m Vivek, Co-founder and CEO at HackerRank (YC S11). We’re launching a new product - Chakra: an AI Interviewer that actually helps hiring teams screen candidates faster with evidence-backed interview reports.

**The problem we see**

We’re seeing recruiters and hiring managers are dealing with:

* Too many applicants and too much scheduling overhead for early rounds.
* Lower signal-to-noise because many applications are polished by AI or outright fabricated .
* Rising interview integrity issues — including tools marketed to “cheat” during interviews.

Teams spend tons of valuable human time on the wrong candidates - and hiring managers don’t trust early-round signals.

**What Is Chakra**

Chakra is an AI Interviewer by HackerRank that runs structured, voice/video screening interviews based on your rubric and delivers decision-ready reports backed by evidence.

Demo video - <https://youtu.be/6v5h-7Q4O2c>  

**How it works**

1. Configure the role, competencies, and rubric in a chat-based setup.
2. Invite candidates easily via email, a simple link, or directly through your ATS
3. Chakra conducts a voice/video based interview on your behalf, intelligently adapting to get the signals you need.
4. You get an instant report with an actionable recommendation, skill-level feedback aligned with your rubric, transcript references, and full session replay.

**What makes it different**

There are a lot of “AI interviewers” right now, but most fall into one of two buckets:

1. Niche / single-skill evaluators, or
2. Single-modal experiences that don’t generate robust, trustworthy signal

Chakra is designed to be multi-modal (voice, video, IDE, whiteboard etc.) and versatile across roles, while still producing grounded, auditable evidence in the report.
