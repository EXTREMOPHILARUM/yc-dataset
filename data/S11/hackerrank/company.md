# HackerRank

> Change the world to value skills over pedigree

At HackerRank, our mission is to change the world to value skills over pedigree.

Thousands of companies use our AI-powered platform to hire next-gen developers based on what they can do - not just what’s on their resume. From hiring to upskilling, we help teams identify and grow technical talent at every stage of the developer journey.

Over 26 million developers have joined the HackerRank Community to learn and certify their skills, practice interviewing, and discover relevant jobs.
