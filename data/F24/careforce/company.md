# Careforce

> AI Workers for Healthcare Orgs to Find, Call & Schedule More Patients.

Careforce builds AI Workers for healthcare orgs to take on coordination burden from staff and reduce time of care by autonomously identifying and outreaching patients (through phone, text, email, & mail) to schedule visits, book screenings, complete pre-post, and onboard. 

These agents require no integration, can navigate all tools, speak 29 languages, come with 50+ outreach scripts and have a remarkable 80% conversation-to-booking closing rate.
