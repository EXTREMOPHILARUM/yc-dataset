# Founders

## Charlene Wang
**Founder**

Charlene is the CEO and co-founder of Ember. Previously a product manager on Healthcare AI at Google, she collaborated with Elevance Health to develop remote patient monitoring solutions. Charlene led the launch of sleep-sensing technology on Google Nest’s smart displays, improving the sleep quality of millions of users. Her research has been featured in Radiology and NEJM Catalyst. She holds a degree in Applied Mathematics, Computer Science, and Economics from Brown University.

## Warren Wang
**Founder**

Warren is the co-founder of Ember. In the past, he did explainable AI research at MIT CSAIL and medical AI research with NVIDIA at hospitals in Taiwan. Warren holds a BS in EECS/Physics and MEng in EECS from MIT. He was a gold medalist at the 50th International Physics Olympiad.
