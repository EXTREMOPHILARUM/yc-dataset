# Matcha

> The best way to hire clinical talent.

We're building Matcha, the faster, smarter way to hire clinical talent—helping hospitals find active, qualified candidates at a fraction of the time and cost of job boards or headhunters.

Matcha engages candidates at scale, matching them to your organization by qualifications and cultural fit. Recruiters save time, hospitals save money, and candidates get a better experience—a radically better hiring model for everyone involved.
