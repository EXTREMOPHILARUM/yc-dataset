### **The Due-Diligence Dilemma**

Private equity funds are struggling to find and win the right deals because due diligence is time-consuming and labor-intensive, leading to:

1. Only assessing \~20% of deals due to limited resources
2. Wasting time on the wrong deals and missing out on the right deals
3. Missing critical details leads to losing millions or sometimes billions
4. Failing to discover the hidden insights that help win deals and generate alpha
