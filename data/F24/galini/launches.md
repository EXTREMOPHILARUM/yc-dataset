# ❌ The Problem:

Compliance and trust are major barriers to scaling AI, with [one in two executives fearing reputational damage.](https://www.complianceweek.com/surveys-and-benchmarking/deloitte-survey-ai-adoption-raising-concerns-about-trust-reputational-damage-ethics/35411.article)

**Ignoring the problem does not make it go away:** A staggering [44% of organizations](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/quantumblack/our-insights/the-state-of-ai) have reported negative consequences from using AI chatbots and assistants.

**The cost of non-compliance is rising:** Fines of [7% annual revenues (or 35M EUR)](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/article/99/#:~:text=Non%2Dcompliance%20with%20the%20prohibition,financial%20year%2C%20whichever%20is%20higher.) for non-compliance with EU AI Act; [similar regulations are fast approaching in the US.](https://www.cnet.com/tech/services-and-software/biden-administration-wants-ai-guardrails-heres-its-call-to-action/)

**Companies are not ready:** Only 6% feel ready to accommodate these changes, while [80% are committing](https://www.accenture.com/us-en/insights/artificial-intelligence/ai-compliance-competitive-advantage) 10%+ of their AI budgets to compliance.

# ✅ Use cases:

Most customer- or employee-facing AI applications require guardrails:

* **Search:** Block non-compliant queries and prevent exposing sensitive or proprietary information to users.
* **Chatbots:** Ensure responses are accurate and compliant with company policies.
* **Assistants:** Stop misleading advice in violation with company policies that could cause negative PR or legal risks.
* **Agents**: Control scope and prevent unauthorized actions or transactions.
* **Recommendation engines**: Filter outputs to avoid biased, irrelevant or offensive suggestions that damage brand trust.
* **Decisioning systems**: Ensure automated decisions (e.g., loan approvals, hiring) meet regulatory standards and prevent discriminatory outcomes.

No enterprise-grade guardrail solutions exist today. Companies **incur $1-10M+ in costs annually** **and 6-12 months in delays** building guardrails in-house.
