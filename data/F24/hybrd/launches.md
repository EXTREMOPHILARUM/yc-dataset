**Ok.. I want to be strong and fast, but what are you actually building?**

We break down the silos between your cardio, strength and wearable data to give you a complete view of your performance.

Then we use that data to personalize training plans from world-class coaches to you. We’re ex-WHOOP, ex-US national rowing team, triathletes / ultramarathoners ourselves and are building the training platform that we wish had existed for us.\
\
**Why do I need a separate app for this?**

Every workout you do is a puzzle piece in a broader picture of performance. But fitness tracking data is siloed across multiple domains. You might track your running data on a Garmin, your sleep/recovery on WHOOP and your strength training in a spreadsheet.

It kinda works if you’re _just_ a runner or _just_ a lifter.

But more and more people are becoming “hybrid” athletes who focus on strength _and_ cardio, so they’re left stitching together multiple data sources to see the full picture.

Do it wrong and you risk “cardio killing your gains”

**What are you guys doing about it?**

First, HYBRD integrates with all leading wearables and data sources. We pull in as much historical data as we can. We pull in your workouts and deduplicate them.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95448&key=user_uploads/1315661/ea007cfa-7a37-4fe1-ac3a-cf3766489e8f)

Then, we make it really easy to track your weightlifting, CrossFit, HYROX - things that aren’t well suited for tracking on a wearable.

You can use text to input your workout to our AI logger - or, simply upload a picture of your training journal, a whiteboard from a HYROX gym, or a screenshot from an app you already use.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95448&key=user_uploads/1315661/d7b6ae82-a69f-44ef-85de-eafad05467e6)

Based on that data, we provide a holistic view of your strength and cardio abilities, normalized for age/gender/weight and give you a single score (analogous to a chess Elo score) that you can use to track your progress.

Cycling, running, rowing, lifting data can all be interpreted and used to model your physiology.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95448&key=user_uploads/1315661/40a31bf9-1e3d-4cf3-98e1-f8b3ea2cd8a4)

That gives us all the information we need to know to personalize a training plan for you.

For example - lets say you want to be more like Alex Viada, the guy I mentioned above. You might not deadlift 700lb+ and be training 15+ hours a week (yet?), so we can scale down the fundamentals of his training plan to whatever is right for you:

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95448&key=user_uploads/1315661/316c1a00-295f-4498-a9a7-52a70ed43a06)

These training plans have been developed by likely the most reputable coaching organization in hybrid athletics - Complete Human Performance (shout out to Alex as well as Trevor Tartaglia and Ariana Scalfo!)

And once you have that workout plan, we can push it directly to your Garmin (Coros + Apple Watch coming soon), so you can easily run through the workout on the go.

You’ll be motivated to keep making progress by pushing up your Training Score, which is the first metric to encapsulate both strength and cardio training load, developed by us and Anna Cruse, the Director of Applied Health and Performance Science at the University of Utah.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95448&key=user_uploads/1315661/c04f9964-96e3-457c-a1e5-b03c8fd2fc8c)

We’ve already had hundreds of thousands of workouts get processed when HYBRD was a tracking-only app, so we’re really pumped about taking things to the next level by adding in adaptive programming.

We’re going to keep adding more plan types (HYROX, triathlon coming soon) as well as more personalization levers (probably sleep + recovery etc.)

## HYBRD: Strava for elite athletes

**TL;DR**

HYBRD is Strava for elite athletes who do more than just cardio. We’re ex-WHOOP, ex-US national rowing team, triathletes/ultramarathoners, and we are building the app that we wish existed for us.

**Problem**

Athletes who train **both strength and cardio** lack a unified way to track and visualize their training. They track cardio with apps like Garmin, Coros, WHOOP, or Strava and log strength workouts with others like Hevy, Strong, Stronglifts, or even good ol’ pen and paper.

This fragmented tracking makes it difficult to train effectively and impossible to compete with friends who train in different disciplines.

**Solution**

First, HYBRD integrates with **all leading wearables** to pull in your runs, rides, and more.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=85200&key=user_uploads/1315661/3c50b8d2-8148-4906-b8a5-05904686ec4a)

Then, we make it really easy to track your weightlifting.

You can input your workout to our AI logger using text— or simply **upload a picture of your training journal**, a whiteboard from a CrossFit gym, or a screenshot from an app you already use.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=85200&key=user_uploads/1315661/7c27a8dd-957c-4b2e-a295-2142cde54835)

Based on that data, we provide a holistic view of your strength and cardio abilities, normalized for age/gender/weight, and give you a **single score** (analogous to a chess Elo score) that you can compare with your training buddies.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=85200&key=user_uploads/1315661/073cb4f1-96da-4693-8d15-b482766761a5)
