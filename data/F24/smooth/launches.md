## Smooth - Fast, simple, reliable AI browser agent

Hey everyone! We're Antonio and Luca, the founders of Smooth - the fastest and most reliable browser agent yet.

**TL;DR:** Smooth is an AI browser agent that can perform actions autonomously on the web using mouse and keyboard. It’s the most reliable browser agent yet, while also being 5 times faster, and 7 times cheaper than any other browser agent on the market.

👉🏼 [Try it now free](https://app.smooth.sh)

**The problem**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=94829&key=user_uploads/846180/9b08b367-2751-4fb4-89cd-7ae50362a4c1)

Although browser agents are becoming a reality, we found that existing solutions are:

1. **Too expensive** Usually require large models - can easily end up costing a few dollars even for simple tasks.
2. **Too slow** Can take way too long to complete - e.g. 4 min to book a flight.
3. **Unreliable** Even when provided with detailed instructions, they can struggle to complete the task.

LLMs may be exceptional at advanced math, but they still struggle to book a flight.

**Our solution**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=94829&key=user_uploads/846180/5c0da911-8b6c-419c-9018-e638bf6e64d3)

We found that if you combine small and efficient models with a carefully curated context design, you can get **higher reliability** while also getting **human-speed navigation** and a **huge cost reduction**!

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=94829&key=user_uploads/846180/1f5cc0da-bcfb-48a1-8972-8a4f23790d2c)

We packaged Smooth in a **super-easy-to-use API** with instant browser spin-up, custom proxies, persistent sessions, and auto-CAPTCHA solvers. Our goal is to give you this infrastructure so that you can focus on what's important: building great apps for your users.

Smooth is infinitely scalable, production ready, and secure. You can get started for free and run browser tasks in just 4 lines of code.
