# Launches

## fixa - fix AI voice agents faster

👋 hey everyone - we’re oliver and jonathan from [fixa.dev](http://fixa.dev) 

# **tl;dr**

we find bugs in your voice agents - (including hallucinations, interruptions, transcription errors, and sentiment errors), so you don’t have to manually call your agents or listen to conversation recordings ever again. watch our launch video [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Cnhf3Vs5Dcw)

# **❌ the problem**

finding bugs in voice agents is a giant hassle

traditionally, voice AI developers

* hire teams of offshore 🌊 reviewers to read transcripts and listen in on calls
* often don’t find bugs until a customer complains 😡
* require the entire eng team to take 30 minutes out of their workday to call their agent prior to pushing changes 😪

# **✅ the solution**

fixa finds bugs automatically in tests and production, providing developers with precise and actionable insights when things go south. 

we pinpoint

* LLM hallucinations 🤯
* interruptions 🗣️
* transcription errors 📝
* sentiment errors (i.e. rude agent) 🤬

and propose changes to your prompt(s) accordingly.

we spent the past year developing voice agents at [play.ai](http://play.ai) where we experienced the pain of manual bug hunting firsthand and we’re building the solution we wish we had. If you’re building in voice AI - we’d love to hear from you! 

thanks ✌️
