# Diffusion Studio

> Professional-grade video editing, in your browser.

Diffusion Studio Pro is a browser based non-linear editor (NLE) that uses WebCodecs and WebGPU to deliver smooth, pro level performance. It brings the modern UX of tools like Figma and Framer to video editing, making it easy for anyone to create professional content.
