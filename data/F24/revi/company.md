# Revi

> AI-enabled M&A deal origination

Revi helps Investment Banks, PE firms, and Corporate Development teams source new deals.

We do this by using agentic workflows to replicate their end-to-end deal origination process, entirely removing the trade-off between precision and scalability.

We released the first version of Revi in September 2023 and now have companies like KPMG and platforms backed by KKR and Nordic Capital using our product.
