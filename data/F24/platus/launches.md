## 📬 **About Beglaubigt’s Address + Digital Mailroom**

In Germany, every founder needs a **_ladungsfähige Geschäftsadresse_** - a legally binding business address required for:

* Trade registrations
* Incorporations (UG/GmbH)
* Tax office communication
* Courts, authorities, and official notices
* Banking, payroll, insurance, vendors

Traditionally, this process is painfully analog: physical letters, misplaced notices, fake register scams, long delays, and hours lost walking to actual mailboxes.

**We turned the entire process into a digital, automated, AI-interpreted system.**

### **What you get**

* 🏢 **A legally compliant business address** (Hamburg, Berlin, Munich)
* 📨 **A fully digital mailbox** — every letter scanned and instantly available
* 🤖 **AI that reads, classifies & interprets every notice**
* 🔀 **Auto-routing to the right team member**
* 🚨 **Real-time fraud & fake-register detection**
* 📦 **Automatic physical forwarding when needed**
* 🔒 **Secure storage, searchable archive, full audit trail**
