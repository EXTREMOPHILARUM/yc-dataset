# Crouton

> The living map of your organization

Crouton is the living map of your organization.

It’s for founders and operators at growing companies who need to understand how people, teams, roles, and ownership actually fit together.

Today they piece this together from org charts, Notion docs, spreadsheets, Slack, and memory and then redo it every time something changes.
