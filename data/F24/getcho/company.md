# Getcho

> The reliability platform for last-mile delivery

Getcho is a local delivery platform for high-value goods. 

We are building a high-reliability delivery network on top of high-volume, unreliable fleets, just like how TCP builds reliable end-to-end networking on top of an unreliable base network (IP).
