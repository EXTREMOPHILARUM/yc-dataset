## **Problem**

The web has _everything_ about people and companies - funding posts, blogs, GitHub, research papers, podcasts, job changes, pricing pages, news - but it’s:

* scattered across **billions of pages**
* written for **humans**
* hard for AI to **reliably connect** into one coherent view

So agents either:

1. scrape brittle HTML and break constantly, or
2. use stale databases and miss what changed this week.

## **Solution**

We spent **2 years** building **verified People + Company APIs**. That gave us high-confidence entity profiles.

Now we’re indexing the **rest of the web** and building a proprietary indexing algorithm that:

1. finds every relevant document about a person/company
2. **maps it to the right entity**
3. returns it as **structured data** that agents can use immediately

That’s the foundation of [**Crustdata Web Search API**](https://crustdata.com/apis/websearch).

Think: **a search engine for agents**, where the output isn’t “10 blue links,” but **entity-linked, structured, fresh context from the web**.

## Crustdata: Live Company and People Data via APIs

**TL;DR**

We’re building Crustdata which provides live company and people data via APIs and webhooks. We let you watch, enrich, or search for any person or company on LinkedIn - and get updates in real-time.

**🚩 Problem**

Getting fresh, reliable, accurate B2B data at scale is hard.

In 2024, data needs to be live and pinpoint accurate, but most data platforms source from unreliable, poorly maintained 3rd party sources. And the data isn’t updated frequently — monthly, if you’re lucky.

Any company trying to add an intelligence layer to their system or process needs data.

Problem: it’s an incredible technology challenge to do that at scale.

**✅ Solution**

We have developed technology that allows us to index and pipe in live data from almost any data source. Currently, we do this at scale from sources including LinkedIn, the SEC, funding announcements and milestones, employee ratings, product reviews, web traffic -  and deliver this data instantly to our customers.

Our technology also enables you to ‘watch’ companies and people on a live basis. Get monthly, weekly, daily, hourly, or instant updates when a predefined event happens.

Events like:

\- Company grows or declines in headcount

\- Company grows in headcount by certain role

\- Company grows in certain headcount in specific regions

\- Company hires for specific technologies or titles in specific regions

\- Company lists a job posting for a certain title or technology

\- Company announces or closes funding round

\- Company gets a new product review

\- Person changes jobs

\- Person gets a promotion

\- Person adds a new skill

\- Company or Person makes a LinkedIn post
