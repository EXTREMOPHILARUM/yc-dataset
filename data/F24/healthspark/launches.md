# Launches

## HealthSpark - Empowering physical therapists to go independent

[HealthSpark](https://www.joinhealthspark.com/) empowers physical therapists (PTs) to start, run, and grow their own independent practices. Our AI-native “business-in-a-box” takes care of the administrative work—insurance billing, patient onboarding, documentation, and scheduling—so PTs can focus on patient care, not paperwork.

# The Problem

In a traditional clinic, office managers and billing staff handle everything outside of direct care: credentialing with insurers, scheduling, patient intake, claims, and compliance. For PTs who want to go independent, all of that work falls on their shoulders. It’s time-consuming, complex, and costly, which discourages many from ever starting a successful practice.

# Our Solution

HealthSpark gives PTs the administrative infrastructure of a full clinic without the overhead of hiring staff. Our AI-enabled back office handles the core workflows of running a practice—insurance credentialing, scheduling, patient intake, documentation, and revenue cycle management—so providers can focus entirely on care.

PTs run their practice on our custom EHR, designed with smart task reminders and equipped with an AI scribe to keep documentation and compliance on track. They also join our multi-state provider network, making it simple to go in-network with major insurers.

Behind the scenes, HealthSpark deploys AI agents to handle the manual workflows that normally require a team of back-office staff. Our agents process unstructured paperwork like referrals, insurance cards, and faxed records, navigate insurance portals to verify benefits, and coordinate with other providers and health systems over phone and fax.
