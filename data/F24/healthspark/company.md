# HealthSpark

> Empowering Physical Therapists to Go Independent

HealthSpark empowers physical therapists (PTs) to start, run, and grow their own independent practices. Our AI-native “business-in-a-box” takes care of the administrative work—credentialing, insurance billing, patient onboarding, documentation, and scheduling—so PTs can focus on patient care, not paperwork.
