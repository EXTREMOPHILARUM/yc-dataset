## PermitPortal | AI Operating System for Pre-construction

<https://youtu.be/lOYsVPDtKUA>

**TL;DR**

Over $1 trillion worth of real estate projects are initiated in the U.S. every year, and they all have one thing in common — they get delayed in the pre-construction stage. We experienced this firsthand while developing $500 million worth of clean energy projects in Europe. It took us 2 years to develop the first project when it should have taken only 6 months.

We started PermitPortal to build tools that automate the manual tasks that took up most of our time: finding, evaluating, and closing new sites, understanding local guidelines, and monitoring permit applications.

**How we address this**

Our goal is to build a platform that helps developers navigate and accelerate all stages of the pre-construction process. At the core of the solution is a custom model trained for each city we’re operating in. We’re using data such as local and national regulations, historical planning applications and hundreds of hours of recordings from planning commission meetings to help real estate companies be more efficient in the key stages of development:

1. Find the right sites faster
2. Assess 100x more sites than they do today and secure them
3. Understand local development sentiment
4. Navigate the local regulations and entitlements processes
5. Find the right local partners to work with
6. Prepare and draft the right documents to avoid pushback from authorities

We are building custom integrations and answering specific questions for each developer we are working with.
