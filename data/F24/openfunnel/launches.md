### **ENTER OPENFUNNEL:**

OpenFunnel is your search engine for inferred pain: you describe the activities that reveal potential pain-points, we find the companies with those pain points, identify the right people, and produce outreach-ready messaging.

**Find companies WHERE Activity happening = \[abc\] AND TimeRange = \[m..n months\]**

* Companies implementing usage-based billing in the past 3-6 months - then monitor daily for new ones

Live search agents continuously monitor time-based activities and changes across your ICP that matter to your GTM.
