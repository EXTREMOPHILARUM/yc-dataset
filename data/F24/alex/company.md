# Alex

> The Cursor for Xcode

Tired of switching tabs to cursor every time you're building your iOS app in Xcode?

Worry not!

Alex is a sidebar app that works directly with Xcode, and has the same Cmd+L, Cmd+K, and Apply Code commands as Cursor. And, it's super fast.

Check it out: https://alexcodes.app
