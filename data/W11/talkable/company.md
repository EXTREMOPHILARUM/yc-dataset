# Talkable

> Online store referrals.

Talkable (formerly Curebit) helps online stores increase revenue through referrals by turning existing customers into marketers.

When customers check out from a Talkable-enabled store, they are presented personalized deals that they can gift to their friends by posting to Facebook or forwarding a link. The deals give both the the original customer and their referred friends a rebate on their purchase at this store.
