# Instapainting

> Custom-made artwork from artists e-commerce marketplace.

Instapainting turns any photo (from your phone, Facebook, Instagram, DSLR, the internet) into a hand-painted oil painting on real canvas, for $45 (for 10"x10"), and delivered in less than two weeks.

The company is backed by Y Combinator and Start Fund (SV Angel and Yuri Milner).

Customers include founder and CEO of Pebble Technology, Eric Migicovsky, and founder and CEO of Apportable, Inc., Collin Jackson.
