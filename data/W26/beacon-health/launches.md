## 👹 **The Problem**

Primary care is being pushed into value-based care without the [bandwidth](https://youtu.be/Qy3eV6o55oE) to support it.

Most practices are juggling:

* More than a dozen quality measures
* Risk adjustment requirements
* After-hours patient needs
* Fragmented EHRs and disconnected vendor tools
* everything else…

The result is predictable: Physician frustration and money left on the table in their value based contracts.

Value-based care doesn’t fail because clinicians don’t care.

It fails because the economics and operations are broken.

---

## 🧩 **Our Solution**

Beacon Health builds **AI agents for primary care**.

Our Al agents handle workflows directly in the EHR. We own them from end to end - starting by putting their **value based care on autopilot**.

Today, Beacon helps practices:

* **Close quality gaps** by identifying patients who need action and executing outreach or teeing up a lab order automatically
* **Capture risk adjustment codes accurately** by surfacing missed documentation and coding opportunities
* **Support patients after hours** with triage and follow-up that doesn’t burn out staff
