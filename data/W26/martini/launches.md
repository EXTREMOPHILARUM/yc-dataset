# **🍸 Introduction**

We’re [Koh](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kohterai/) and [Long](https://www.linkedin.com/in/longh/) from Martini.

Generative AI video still feels like the wild west.

Brave members of the creative community are embracing this new medium.\
\
But making a great AI video still feels like fighting the tool rather than using it. Most AI tools are built around prompting and seeds—not the way filmmakers actually think.

Koh is a [professional cinematographer](https://www.quinzaine-cineastes.fr/en/film/grand-bouquet). He knows filmmakers live and breathe on film sets – a space for intentionality, collaboration, and control. Not endless prompts. So we built Martini to give filmmakers an environment to direct and shoot their generative AI films.

We shared Martini with a few filmmaker friends. Then more. Today over [200 films](https://www.madewithmartini.com/) have been made with Martini. They’ve aired as TV Commercials, been displayed as artwork, and have gone viral online.

Our users are filmmakers, artists, serious production teams, and anyone daring enough to take on the challenge of creating AI video.

# **🍸 Martini is the go-to platform for AI video production**

**Professional Control**\
Get the exact shot you want, not just the one the model hands you. Step into virtual worlds and compose shots using camera position, lenses, and movement — not just prompts and frames.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97320&key=user_uploads/2701033/41b4eb79-44ac-41fc-948f-dcde5b23ad60)

_Step into any image or video to reframe and reshoot_

**Streamlined Production Pipeline**\
Teams are forced to MacGyver their workflows across a mess of tools. Martini brings image, video, and world models directly into the editing process.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97320&key=user_uploads/2701033/3791bdb8-2631-4b1e-8ecf-e0165913cf3f)

_Built in timeline for creating rough assemblies, exportable as XML_

**Production as a Team Sport**\
Video production is collaborative. Different professionals bring different expertise. Martini supports real-time collaboration—sharing prompts, edits, and feedback—so teams can explore ideas together the way they do in tools like **Figma, but for generative film**.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97320&key=user_uploads/2701033/f2e831c6-9f6a-4336-9df0-2b6799d6210a)
