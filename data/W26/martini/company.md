# Martini

> Collaborative AI-native filmmaking for professionals

Martini is a collaborative, AI-native platform for professional filmmaking in the era of generative media.
By integrating the best generative models into professional workflows, we enable world-class creatives to harness generative AI as a legitimate medium for film.
