**Problem** 

AI has maxed out the electrical grid. Average data center time to power is five years and growing. **$3 Trillion in data center projects are slated for construction by 2030, yet half may never be built due to power constraints.** Energy prices for everyone, everywhere, are creeping up. **The AI bottleneck isn’t chips - it’s power.**

**Solution** 

Voxel Energy is building data centers with on-site solar energy generation, distribution, and storage for **24/7 power in months, not years**.  

* **No Grid Connection Wait** because Voxel doesn’t need agreements with utilities.
* **Solar at Scale** - we’re deploying hundreds of megawatts to power GPUs.
* **24/7 Battery Storage** powers compute when the sun is down.
* **Backup Generation** ensures redundant operation 365 days a year. 
* **Stable Power** with the rapid-response needed to handle dynamic training loads.
* **Inherent Redundancy** from Voxel’s proprietary DC power distribution system.

**We’ve Already Started**

Our systems prototype is live, operational, and **already running off-grid compute.** Hundreds (Update: Thousands) of acres are already under contract.  And we’re already amassing batteries.
