# Launches

## Carrot Labs: Custom models tuned to your tasks that never stop improving

**TL;DR:** Carrot Labs helps you build models for your agents that are faster and better, forever. Use our platform to build proprietary models using open source that progressively improve.

[**Our Launch Video**](https://youtu.be/Qiw-HG14AVE)

## **The Problems:**

Frontier models from Anthropic, OpenAI and Google are constantly pushing the boundary of intelligence further, but great quality comes with high latency and cost. Smaller models are fast, but can't get the job done right.

So you start modifying system prompts. It takes you far enough, but at some point you hit a ceiling. And every time a new model comes out, your prompts don't work the same way anymore! (just February 2026 alone, Opus 4.6 and Sonnet 4.6 from Anthropic, Gemini 3.1 Pro from Google, GPT-5.3-Codex from Open AI, Qwen 3.5 from Alibaba came out to name a few.)

## **Solution:**

We build you a proprietary model tuned to your tasks so you always have access to the most performant model for your agent.

Our platform records your agent’s activity and tunes an optimized model from an open source base model. Once it's ready, swap out the frontier model for yours. That's it. As new production data flows in, we continuously evaluate and retrain your model to boost performance. What you get is a model specialized to your tasks that outperforms frontier.

## **Why this matters to your startup:**

In the short run, you need your agent to be faster and more accurate, and prompt tuning will only get you so far. We are the fastest way to get there.

In the long run, every company building agents is using the same frontier models. Your competitor has the same intelligence you do. The thing that differentiates you is the knowledge you distill into your own model. Your model is your moat.
