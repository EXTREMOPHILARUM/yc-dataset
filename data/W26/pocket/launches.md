## **TL;DR**

Pocket is a small device that takes notes for all your conversations and meetings. Later, Pocket sends you transcripts, summaries and pulls out your to-dos.\

Pocket is taking off. In the last 5 months, we’ve delivered over 30,000 units. **We have a $27M annualized revenue run rate, growing 50% month over month.**

**Hey, we're Akshay and Gabriel.**

We kept noticing the same thing: every important conversation in our lives happened in person - investor meetings, doctor visits, client calls, team standups. And every single note-taking tool on the planet only worked over Zoom.

For in-person conversations, the best technology available was a pen.

**So we built Pocket.**

Pocket is a small device. **Three mics. One button.**

You press it. You have your conversation. **A summary is waiting for you in the Pocket app.**

The full transcript too. Pocket tacks who said what. You get automatically-generated summaries. And those things you promised? **We capture all your todos for you.**

With Pocket, **you don't type. You don't take notes. You can just talk and be present.**\
\
**It also does something no app can:**

Snap Pocket to the back of your phone. **Its contact mic can capture and summarize both sides of a phone call. No speakerphone required.**

Your doctor calls with test results? Someone rattles off an interesting quote? Your lawyer explains something complicated and fast? **It's all in Pocket. Word for word.**
