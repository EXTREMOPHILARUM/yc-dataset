# Sentrial

> Production Monitoring for AI Agents

Sentrial is the monitoring platform for AI products in production. 

When your AI product/agent loops indefinitely, hallucinates, or frustrates users, Sentrial detects it in real-time, diagnoses the root cause, and recommends specific fixes. We provide the monitoring, evaluation, and debugging infrastructure that traditional tools weren't built for, so teams iterate based on production reality, not just offline evals.
