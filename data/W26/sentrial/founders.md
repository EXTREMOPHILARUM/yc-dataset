# Founders

## Neel Sharma
**Founder/CEO**

Neel is the co-founder of Sentrial - production monitoring for AI products. 

He studied Computer Science at UC Berkeley and worked on agentic optimization at Sense. He saw firsthand that traditional evaluation frameworks break down once agents hit production. That insight led to the creation of Sentrial, a platform built to monitor, evaluate, and improve agent performance in real-world, production environments.
