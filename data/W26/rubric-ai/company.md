# Rubric AI

> Post-training research and product lab

Rubric AI is a post-training research lab (backed by Y Combinator) building the human and computational layer that turns expert judgment into training signals for frontier models and agents. We believe that shaping the world's most consequential AI systems requires both purpose-built training environments and exceptional judgment.
