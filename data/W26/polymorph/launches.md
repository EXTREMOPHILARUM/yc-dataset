## Polymorph: Personalize your experience for each user

Hi! 👋 We’re **Andrew, David, and Manas** - cofounders of [Polymorph](http://usepolymorph.com)

## tl;dr

Polymorph is a custom user growth platform for consumer and self-serve teams. We build living user profiles that learn from real product usage to send personalized messages at the time and channel that works. 

<https://youtu.be/N5mvl4jXIMM>

**Worldview:** users are not segments, events, or dashboard lines. Each user is their own person, and they deserve a dedicated experience.

Polymorph is that layer: **per user growth at scale.**

## problem

Most teams end up with:

* endless dashboards
* dozens of segments
* tons of brittle marketing automations

…and still miss the moment when a user is confused, high-intent, or about to churn.

## solution

**Polymorph runs inference over billions of signals and takes customized actions per user _at the right time_.**

It connects to your current stack (ex: **PostHog, Datadog, LogRocket**, plus CRM/support/warehouse) and combines:

* product logs + interactions
* funnels/cohorts/experiments
* feedback + support signals

Then it produces and executes the next best action:

* automated comms to your customers (push/email/Slack/CRM tasks)
* sales handoffs and insights when relevant
* alert humans instantly on Slack/Telegram during high intent user sessions
* experiments based on what’s actually happening

**Example:** “The Quiet One”

A user ignores announcements, unsubscribes from updates, and reads API docs deeply. Polymorph recognizes a self-serve power user, reduces outbound noise, and shows only contextual help on the exact page they’re reading.
