# Launches

## AutoSitu: AI-native Developmen Plan Review for Faster Approvals

**TL;DR:** Hi YC! We’re Lin and George, founders of AutoSitu. We’re building software that automatically reviews site and building plan sets in minutes and surfaces what a city is likely to flag before you submit.

**🧱 Problem**\
In real estate, _time kills deals_ — and permitting/review is one of the biggest sources of delay and variance.\
Most projects go through **2–3 resubmittal cycles**, adding **3–9 months**.

* Developers: **$50k–$250k+** in carrying costs while projects sit in review
* Architecture teams: **80–200 hours** of unplanned work per resubmittal\
  → at **\~$130/hr**, that’s **$10k–$25k+** of margin erased on fixed-fee work 🐌

**⚡ Solution**\
AutoSitu is an AI-powered development plan review engine that flags the issues that typically surface later — **before submission**, when fixes are cheap.

Teams use AutoSitu to:

* reduce city comments **50–70%**
* cut resubmittals from **2–3 → 0–1**
* shorten approvals by **3–6 months+**

**💰 Impact**\
That’s **$100k–$400k** in avoided carrying costs for developers and **$10k–$25k** in preserved fee margin per project for architecture teams.

👋 **Backstory**\
Lin worked across **20+ U.S. public and private projects** in planning and architecture, sitting on **both sides of the table**, and repeatedly hearing the same question from teams and clients: _why does review take so long when the issues are so predictable?_

George spent years building automation systems in robotics and AI, turning processes that took **weeks of manual effort** into scalable pipelines, cutting costs from **$50k to $2k** while keeping humans in the loop.

AutoSitu started from a shared realization: permit review isn’t slow because people aren’t trying, it’s slow because the same checks are repeated manually, at scale, with no early signal.
