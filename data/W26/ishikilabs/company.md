# Fern (Formerly Ishiki Labs)

> Realtime AI meeting assistant starting with sales

Fern is a real time AI that runs sales playbooks live in a sales call so any person can function as a top sales person. During the call, Fern will listen to the conversation and provide realtime coaching and help the sales person with questions about product, competitors and discovery. It’s like having a senior AE on every call giving suggestions in the background.
