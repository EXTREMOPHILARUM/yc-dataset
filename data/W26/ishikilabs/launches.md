## Fern – Realtime Socially Aware AI for Meetings

**TL;DR**\
[Fern](https://www.ishikilabs.ai/download) is a realtime AI that joins your meetings as either an invisible copilot or an active participant– your choice! It realtime proactively coaches you through every conversation so you always show up like a top 1% professional.

Hey everyone, we're Amit and Robert, cofounders of Ishiki Labs.

**The Problem**

_“AI meeting assistant” has become synonymous with a note-taker waiting to summarize at the end. That's not a meeting assistant. That's a fancier transcript._ 

Building a realtime AI meeting copilot is hard in two distinct ways.

The invisible assistant must understand the live meeting context at blazing speed and surface the right thing at the right moment – questions to ask, things you're missing, who you're talking to. Timing is everything. Current AI is purely reactive. Making AI an active participant in discussion is even harder. AI has no social awareness – every pause becomes a prompt, every gap an invitation to speak.

AI that is socially aware and proactive is an unsolved problem. That's what we're building.

The cost of this gap is real. Cofounders, AEs, recruiters, investors – in discovery calls, sales meetings, investor conversations. The moments that matter most, and AI is absent from all of them.

**How Fern Solves it**

[Fern](https://www.ishikilabs.ai/download) joins your meetings as a true participant or an invisible copilot – your choice. It has two modes:

**Full Presence**: Fern joins visibly, listens, and speaks up when invited. It offers realtime opinions, deep research, and can complete tasks like filing notes or sending emails on the spot. Built for internal product, engineering, and strategy meetings. Watch the demo- <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vRJfZDUHvSY>

**Shadow Mode**: Silent and invisible to everyone else. You get a private realtime feed: live summaries, questions to ask, things you're missing, context on who you're talking to. A sharp colleague whispering in your ear – with fully customizable insights. Built for client calls, investor meetings, and any conversation where you want invisible support.

Both modes include a post-meeting summary, transcript, and full insights.
