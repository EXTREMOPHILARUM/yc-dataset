[Q2Q Launch Video](https://youtu.be/am_2jrJbvWQ)

**The Problem**

Proprietary sourcing is extremely manual for deal teams. Firms typically hire a mini army of juniors to do it for them, and the quality of output is inconsistent.

* It takes a ton of time to hire and train analysts to run sourcing
* Sourcing is a repetitive loop… research the company, write a custom email, then cold reach out to them
* The entire industry relies on the same databases, so owners get flooded with the same generic outreach

**Q2Q’s Solution**

We’re your team of AI analysts that gets you first calls booked with companies you want to buy. We handle:

1. **Sourcing:** Tell us about your thesis. We’ll go find and qualify companies that match your buying criteria. 
2. **Outreach:** We pull owner contact info and research each company across the web and their socials to customize each outreach message.
3. **AI Phone Agent:** Our AI agent can directly dial business owners and ask for their availability and contact info for a first meeting.
4. **Bookings:** As owners respond, we book the intro calls for you. You can see meetings populate on your calendar with notes prepared by our team.
