# Q2Q

> We help private equity teams find acquisition targets faster

Q2Q is the AI-native outbound engine for deal sourcing. Tell us the types of companies you want to buy, and we'll get qualified meetings booked for you. 

You can think of Q2Q as your personal team of AI interns. You give us your buying criteria, and we'll go out and find companies, write personalized emails, and get first calls booked with business owners for you. As the human in the loop, you'll approve messages before we send. Q2Q handles the sourcing work so you can focus on evaluating great deals.
