# **❌** The Problem

The finance & accounting industry is broken - so **we’re building Balance to fix it.**

Companies spend over $680B each year on accounting services. Yet +80% of firms still rely on manual, spreadsheet-based reconciliation processes, and small business owners spend 20+ hours every week managing their books, from invoicing and expenses to reporting and taxes.

**As former CFOs and business owners, we’ve lived the problem for years:**

* Paying thousands per month for late, inaccurate books
* Scrambling to fix errors before board meetings
* Waiting weeks for sloppy month-end closes
* Chasing receipts to stay compliant

\
**Why hasn’t this been solved?**

Because bookkeeping isn’t just rules - it’s judgment. And traditional firms aren’t built to solve this with AI. But companies needed this _yesterday;_ Despite only incorporating in January, we are already active across multiple industries helping some of the most forward-looking and modern companies in everything from tech, ecommerce, florists, to digital consultants, close their books **faster**, **cheaper** and more **accurately**.

# [**👷**](https://emojipedia.org/construction-worker) Our Solution

We’re building the accounting firm we always wished existed; combining AI systems with human experts to deliver **clean books, real-time insights, and instant answers.**

**Our AI agents ingest your business context — revenue, payroll, banking, expenses, ledger, and automate the full back-office finance stack.**

Our agents categorize transactions, reconcile accounts, and surface anomalies in real time, with a human in the loop for review and accuracy.

Once the books are clean, Balance becomes the easiest way to actually _understand_ them.

Founders can dive into costs, audit subscriptions, break down revenue, and validate taxes; all from where they work (Slack, WhatsApp, email), without waiting on PDFs or parsing spreadsheets. It’s your outsourced finance function. Accurate books, responsive, and always up-to-date.
