### TL;DR

Voice AI can handle the conversation. It just can’t complete the transaction.

Maven lets voice agents collect payments during live calls with one API call.

---

### The Problem

Voice agents are being deployed across:

* Home services
* Healthcare
* Insurance
* Sales
* Collections
* And more

They can qualify leads, book appointments, and negotiate pricing.

But when a customer says, “I’m ready to pay,” everything breaks.

Adding payments today means:

* Building PCI-compliant infrastructure
* Handling sensitive card data
* Managing failed payments mid-call
* Supporting multiple gateways like Stripe, Authorize.net, and Adyen

---

### Our Solution

With a single API call, we take over secure card collection, processing, retry logic, and PCI compliance.

Card data flows directly through Maven’s PCI-compliant infrastructure, never your servers.

As voice AI becomes a large revenue channel, it needs real payment infrastructure. Maven completes the loop.
