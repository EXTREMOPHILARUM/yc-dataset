## **❌ The Problem**

Every month a drug is delayed from reaching the market costs pharma companies \~$45M in lost revenue and more importantly, delays treatment for thousands of patients who need it.

Yet regulatory teams are stuck in a painful, months-long process to create FDA submissions like CTDs, CSRs, INDs, and BLAs:

**1. Information overload** 📚 These submissions require synthesizing thousands of pages of clinical data, prior submissions, and regulatory guidance which is an overwhelming task even for experienced teams.

**2. Document hunting & version chaos** 🔄 Teams waste hours digging through SharePoint folders, juggling multiple document versions, and chasing colleagues for the right files.

**3. Endless follow-ups** 📧 Regulatory leads spend their days sending reminder emails, tracking down missing inputs, and managing status updates across teams.

**4. Manual drafting bottlenecks** ⏳ Even with all the information gathered, the first draft alone takes weeks of painstaking work by highly specialized (and expensive) regulatory writers.

---

## **🔧 Our Solution**

Ritivel deploys AI agents that transform how regulatory teams work:

**Draft the most common document types like CTDs, CSRs, INDs, and BLAs in minutes** ✍️ Our agents generate comprehensive first drafts directly in Microsoft Word, following FDA formatting requirements and pulling from your existing clinical data and prior submissions. Every data point includes citations linked back to the original source, so reviewers can verify with one click.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97327&key=user_uploads/939300/4d8b39f8-8230-4dcd-b654-c51b595d124e)

**Intelligent document retrieval** 🔗 Connected to SharePoint and Veeva, our agents automatically source the right documents from across your organization: no more digging through folders or chasing down files.

**Automated follow-ups** 📧 Integrated with Outlook, our agents handle reminder emails and follow-ups automatically, so you're not stuck chasing colleagues for missing documents.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97327&key=user_uploads/939300/aa5a53c8-8d14-4c11-a20c-fecd59724cae)

**Accelerated project management** 🚀 By automating the document gathering and drafting loop, teams can focus on review and refinement rather than administrative overhead.
