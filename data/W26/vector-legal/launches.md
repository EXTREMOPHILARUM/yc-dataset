# Launches

## Launch: Vector Legal™ - AI-native law firm + software for Startups

#### Startups know the stakes - it’s move fast or die. Your lawyers should operate at the same pace, without costing an arm and a leg.

Vector Legal combines experienced startup legal professionals + proprietary AI to help you close your deals, quickly, at startup-friendly prices. They serve clients from Seed through Series B, handling financings, IP protection, customer contracts, hiring, founder transitions, and more. 

In addition: their VectorOS platform helps founders:

* manage equity and dilution,
* draft documents,
* review contracts with AI,
* maintain investor-ready data rooms, and
* ask questions across their legal docs.

The team brings deep experience in the space, having previously worked at Fenwick, YC Legal, and Ironclad.
