## Moda: Monitoring and Reliability for Agents.

We’re Mohammad and Pranav (Moda, W26). **We built monitoring & reliability for AI agents.**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=98435&key=user_uploads/2168306/84330bae-242e-447d-a656-800061f4b1ac)

**In production, AI agents fail silently all the time:** tool calls that error or time out, agents that claim they “did it” without actually doing it, prompt injections that cause data leakage, and long customer chats that hide the real issue. Most teams only find out after users complain, churn, or something breaks.

<https://youtu.be/aKPDhL_AE4E>

### **The problem:**

When an agent fails, the “bug” is rarely one line of code. It might be a flaky tool, a prompt regression, a bad retrieval result, or a jailbreak attempt.

But most teams still debug agents by skimming transcripts, grepping logs, and hoping they notice the real issue. Previous tools were built for clicks, not for long, messy conversations with skills, MCP’s and tools.

As agents get more autonomous, this gets worse. Small changes in prompts, tools, or integrations can cause big reliability and security problems at scale.

### **What we built:**

Moda is monitoring & reliability for AI agents. It turns conversations and tool traces into low-noise, actionable signals.

**Trace-first drilldowns:** click from a metric or alert straight into the exact messages and tool calls that caused it.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=98435&key=user_uploads/2168306/6e062a13-340e-452f-9676-2bdd790d94e4)

**Conversation segmentation:** automatically splits long chats into topic segments so you can debug the right unit of work.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=98435&key=user_uploads/2168306/90151d61-e51b-45da-ac29-72af5c8a31d6)

**Failure detection:** flags tool-call errors, timeouts, and “claimed it worked” runs.
