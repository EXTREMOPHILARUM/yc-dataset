# Launches

## Terranox AI: AI-powered uranium discovery

**TL;DR:** Terranox AI ([terranox.ai](http://terranox.ai)) is the first vertically integrated AI-powered uranium discovery company. We discover economic uranium deposits in North America using AI trained on 70+ years of exploration outcomes. Our mission is to find the fuel we need to power the next century with nuclear energy.

<https://youtu.be/2R8vyyOSH0A>

⚡️ **The Nuclear Renaissance:**

Nuclear is experiencing a global resurgence as the only scalable clean baseload power option that can meet humanity's exploding energy needs:

* **AI datacenter electricity demand** will more than double by 2030
* Microsoft, Google, Amazon, and Meta have collectively **committed billions to nuclear power**
* 30+ countries pledged to **more than triple global nuclear capacity by 2050**
* In 2025, the U.S. government signed executive orders to **quadruple nuclear capacity**, with $80B+ in commitments across reactors and fuel supply chains

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=98155&key=user_uploads/898324/f98faf46-f9ae-4a9b-b299-2fbbd1361db4)

⛽️ **The Fuel Crisis:**

But this nuclear future has one critical dependency: **uranium fuel.**

* Global uranium production needs to **4x by 2050**, but we already have a **supply deficit today** with depleting stockpiles
* The world’s largest mines will **approach end-of-life** simultaneously starting in the mid 2030s
* New mines take **10-15 years from discovery to production**

**The West needs new economic discoveries in stable jurisdictions to ensure energy security.**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=98155&key=user_uploads/854295/8b02b13f-eeba-4933-a331-5a6556f57c7a)

🆘 **The Bottleneck: Exploration is Broken**

Exploration still works like in the 1960s: slow, fragmented, guided by intuition and outsourced workflows. **Hit rates are &lt;1%**, driving extremely high costs and decade-long timelines. Meanwhile, 70+ years of exploration data sits trapped in thousands of legacy PDFs and handdrawn maps, unused.

**We're not finding deposits fast enough to power the future.**

⚒️ **What We Are Building:**

**Terranox AI is the first vertically integrated AI-powered uranium discovery company.**

We find uranium deposits in North America using AI trained on decades of geoscience data and exploration outcomes.

**Our system does three things:**

1. **Multi-modal geoscience intelligence:** Extracts and processes geoscience data from decades of fragmented sources, building a unified exploration context base
2. **Prospectivity mapping for discovery:** Identifies high-potential areas humans would miss using uranium-specific AI models trained on decades of exploration outcomes
3. **Sequential decision intelligence:** Determines the optimal next action to maximize information gain per dollar spent, learning from every result in real time

**The result:** A compounding learning flywheel. Every drill hole (hit or miss) makes our predictions better across all projects. The more we explore, the smarter we get.

We're launching our own exploration projects in North America to ensure the nuclear renaissance has the fuel it needs.
