# Tensol

> AI Employees for your Business, Powered by OpenClaw

Tensol lets you deploy and manage AI employees for your team - built on OpenClaw. Each AI employee runs 24/7 in its own isolated VM, has full context of your company, and can monitor Slack, track Sentry errors, update your CRM, and more. We handle the infrastructure, security, and deployment.

Tensol also lets you create specialized AI employees for different jobs - engineering, sales, support, in a couple of minutes. You connect the tools your team already uses (Slack, GitHub, Sentry, HubSpot, Linear, Gmail, and more) with one-click integrations. Every action is logged with a full audit trail, credentials are injected at network-level, and each AI employee gets its own dedicated identity and sandboxed environment, with enterprise-grade security built-in.
