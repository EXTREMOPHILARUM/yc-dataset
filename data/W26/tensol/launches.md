## Tensol: AI Employees for Your Company, Built on OpenClaw

**Pratik** and **Oliviero** here - Co-Founders at [**Tensol**](https://www.tensol.ai/) (YC W26).

**Tensol deploys AI employees for your company, built on OpenClaw.** We give you the full power of OpenClaw deployed as AI employees, running 24/7 in secure, isolated sandboxes that connect to your tools (Slack, HubSpot, GitHub, Linear, and more) and have full context about your organization.

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-glTlPXgdnI>

**❌ The problem**

Most agents today require you to prompt them, and have limited memory & context capabilities. OpenClaw is an autonomous agent that acts proactively, runs 24/7 and remembers everything about your organization and can use a computer like a human, but deploying it is a nightmare: managing access to credentials, your file system, adding integrations, and time to set it all up on your personal computer.

🐂 **The solution**

Tensol lets you deploy AI Employees for different teams in your company in secure, isolated sandboxes. Here are some of the most common use cases of our customers:

* **Customer Support Employee**: It has access to Slack, GitHub, CRM, it proactively responds to all customer queries, pulls context from past conversations, and notifies an account executive or an engineer in case of a bug. Up and running in 5 minutes.
* **Engineering Employee**: Monitors your Sentry logs, CI/CD pipelines on GitHub, and Linear around the clock. If it sees repeated errors, it doesn't just send you an alert — it reads the stack trace, traces the root cause, spins up Claude Code, writes a fix, and drafts a PR for you to review. Fixed before your team wakes up.
* **SDR Employee**: It enriches inbound leads the moment they come in, updates your CRM automatically, drafts personalized follow-ups, and flags high-intent prospects in Slack so you can close faster. It can post content on your behalf or for your company on Linkedin, X or Moltbook!

Agents can work together and the more you use them the more context they will have about your organization.
