**Why This Matters**

Companies spend $1T on ads every year. $100B is wasted on ineffective ads due to non-compliance with brand guidelines, regulations, and platform best practices. So millions of lawyers, marketers, and compliance officers manually review every piece of content.

This process is slow, expensive, and about to break - AI tools are letting teams create 100x more content and human reviewers won’t be able to keep up.

**Our solution**

Veriad automates the entire process at scale. Our agent ingests brand policies, industry guidelines, and precedents, then analyzes every piece of content automatically - providing instant feedback to marketers. Veriad integrates directly into your marketing stack, to ensure that brand equity is protected across all surface areas.
