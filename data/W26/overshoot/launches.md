## Overshoot - AI Vision in Real-time

Overshoot is the fastest API for real-time vision. Literally faster than humans (&lt;200ms).

Try the playground: <https://overshoot.ai>

1000+ developers are using it today to ship video agents in gaming, robotics and security.

Real-time vision changes what AI can do in the physical world. However, existing platforms make it painful for developers to build these applications. Slow inference. Limited model availability. Break at scale.

Overshoot solves this.

Image and Video are fundamentally different from text. By focusing on them end-to-end, from codecs and streaming to inference engines, we’re pushing the AI stack forward for real-time vision.

Prior to Overshoot, Zakaria and Younes built low-latency high-throughput systems at Uber, Meta and Intel.
