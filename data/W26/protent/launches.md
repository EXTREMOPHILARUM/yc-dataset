# Launches

## Protent: Real-time video intelligence for public safety

**TL;DR:**

Public safety agencies are drowning in video they can't watch. From body cams to fixed infrastructure, the footage is there, but the live intelligence is missing. We’re launching Protent to transform public safety from reactive to proactive.

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iMFWR5_3vmg>

**Problem:**

Real-Time Crime Centers (RTCCs) and police departments rely on human operators physically staring at walls of monitors to assess threats from body cams, drones, and city street cameras. With a single operator responsible for watching hundreds of live screens simultaneously, brandished weapons, fights, or even medical emergencies are often missed. While video footage is useful in 65% of criminal investigations, fewer than 1 in 200 incidents are detected by operators in real time.\
\
The future of physical security is hurtling towards a massive scaling bottleneck: the number of surveillance streams is increasing exponentially, but the human capacity to monitor them is static. Because of this limitation, security teams are drowning in unwatched video, and critical moments are missed in real time. Today, cameras are retroactive tools used to see what went wrong. They should be proactive measures used to save lives.

### **Solution:**

Protent bridges this gap by watching alongside your operators, applying human-level insight to every single camera simultaneously. We're already working with police departments in Atlanta, Chicago, and St. Louis metro areas to deploy our technology today. We’ve built a real-time situational dashboard that monitors all active feeds and automatically "pops" the most critical streams to the front the moment volatility is detected. By using real-time video analysis to highlight threats instantly, we turn overwhelming video data into an infinitely scalable, proactive security team.

Beyond automated triage, Protent transforms how operators interact with live environments through natural language query. Instead of scrubbing through hours of footage after the fact, security teams can instantly search all active feeds for a "person in a red hoodie with a backpack" or a "child in a blue striped sweater." We are turning raw video into real-time, actionable intelligence to stop emergencies before they escalate and save lives.
