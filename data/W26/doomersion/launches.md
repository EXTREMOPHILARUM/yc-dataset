## Doomersion - Learn Languages by Doomscrolling

Doomersion lets you learn languages by doomscrolling in them! It shows you short videos in your target language that are exactly matched to your level, and then as you scroll they get progressively harder.

<https://youtube.com/shorts/x22sJ7nHVHw>

There’s no language learning app that’s fun enough to spend hours a day on while still being educational. Gen Z doomscrolls an average of 2.5-3 hours a day. As it turns out, the optimal strategy for language learning is immersion with spaced repetition, and by baking immersion into the doomscrolling algorithm, Doomersion makes it fun to learn languages.\
