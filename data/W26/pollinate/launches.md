# Launches

## 🦋 Pollinate – Agents that automate data entry into your ERP built in days not quarters.

**❌ Problem: Supply chain teams waste 40% of their time on manual work that their ERP should handle.**

Off-the-shelf tools force you to change your workflows. Custom development takes 6-12 months. So your operations team ends up running critical processes in spreadsheets - invoice reconciliation, vendor management, just-in-time production scheduling.

**✅ Solution: Pollinate builds the exact tools you need, automatically.**

Our platform connects to your ERP and enriches the data. We then leverage this data to generate custom applications and agents that are bespoke to your workflows. What used to take quarters of dev time now deploys in days.

**How it works:**

1. Connect your existing ERP & data sources.
2. We automatically map your data structure and identify operational patterns
3. We ship custom tools for invoice processing, production scheduling, vendor management
4. Your team works in tools built for _your_ processes, not generic ones

We're already processing $100k+ in purchasing volume through automated workflows our customers couldn't build themselves.

Our customers use our agents to automate:

1. Order processing from email, text or phone straight into their ERP systems. 
2. Three way invoice reconciliation for suppliers who need to match purchase orders and receipts to thousands of vendor invoices.
3. Just in time production for hardware teams managing assembly of their product.
