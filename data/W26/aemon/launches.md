## 😖 The Problem

Global R&D spend is $3.1 trillion a year. And every R&D problem has the same shape: you know what success looks like, but you don't know how to get there.

Today, that means hiring R&D engineers to read and align papers, pick an approach, implement it, test it, and iterate — by hand. It's slow, it's expensive, and no matter how good your team is, they can only explore a tiny fraction of the solution space. That means the best solution is almost certainly out there, untested, because no human team has the bandwidth to find it.

This isn't a tooling problem. It's a search problem — and humans are the bottleneck.

## 🤓 Our Solution

Aemon does what a world-class R&D engineer does — but at a scale no human team can match.

* **Research synthesis** — Aemon reads state-of-the-art papers to understand what's been tried, what works, and where the frontier is.
* **Autonomous evolution** — It generates and evolves thousands of approaches against your eval, exploring far more of the solution space than any human team could.
* **Expert steerable** — Your team stays in control. A simple direction is all it takes to guide Aemon — constraining the solution space, prioritizing certain approaches, or steering away from dead ends. R&D gets faster and more precise, and Aemon fits into your existing R&D workflow.
* **Beyond state-of-the-art results** — Aemon doesn't just match the best known solution. It looks for better ones — and finds them.

The result: solutions that are validated against your eval, benchmarked against the public state of the art. Your experts stay in the loop — Aemon just gives them superhuman reach. <https://youtu.be/YOswpdEcFUM>
