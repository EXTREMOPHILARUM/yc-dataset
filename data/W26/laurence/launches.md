## Laurence - Automated Amazon Advertising

Hey YC! 👋

We’re [Matt](https://bookface.ycombinator.com/user/1795983) and [Leo](https://bookface.ycombinator.com/user/3241658) building [Laurence](https://www.trylaurence.com/): automated Amazon pay-per-click (PPC) performance marketing using reinforcement learning and transformer models.

<https://youtu.be/EAiSUSByF6k>

**TL;DR**

* **Most brands lose money** advertising on Amazon after margin
  * Agencies outsource **simplistic copy-paste playbooks** to offshore workers
  * Existing software are rudimentary rules engines that break with any bad variance
* We drive performance using **reinforcement learning** and training our own conversion-rate-aware transformer models
* Our quant system is automating **$10 million in live ad spend**, increasing advertising profits by 15-20% while growing total revenue.
