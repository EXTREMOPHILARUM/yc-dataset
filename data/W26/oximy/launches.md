The biggest AI risk is unintentional data leaks.

Employees paste customer info, source code, financial data, and internal strategies into AI tools every day. Most companies have no visibility into this until something goes wrong.

Oximy monitors data flows to AI tools in real time. You can set policies, get alerts, and block sensitive data from reaching unauthorized tools.

_Example:_ A healthcare company set up Oximy and, within 48 hours, flagged an employee uploading patient records to an unapproved AI transcription tool. They were able to intervene immediately and avoid a potential HIPAA violation.
