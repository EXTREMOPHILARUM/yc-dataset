## Prana - An AI Doctor that Monitors You 24/7

Hey YC! We’re **Meer**, **Vishvam**, and **Sanjit** — the founders of **Prana**. ([pranadoc.com](http://pranadoc.com))

TL;DR:\
Prana is the "Always-On" AI Doctor. We replace the reactive annual physical with continuous, proactive monitoring. We connect to your medical records and wearables to detect "clinical drift" (like rising blood pressure or glucose) weeks before it becomes an emergency.

**The Problem**\
Primary care today is episodic and reactive: you only see a doctor when you are already sick. Real health issues don’t happen overnight; they happen in the "drift" between visits. Because data is siloed (your Oura ring doesn’t talk to your Epic chart), doctors miss the full context of your health, often leading to late detection of metabolic disease and chronic illness.

**The Solution**\
Prana acts as a 24/7 intelligent layer on top of your health data.

* **Unified Profile:** We aggregate your history (EHRs via Fasten) and real-time biometrics (Wearables via Junction) into a single clinical timeline.
* **Continuous Monitoring:** Instead of waiting for you to complain, our AI acts as a background monitor, analyzing your data for anomalies and drift.
* **Doctor-in-the-Loop:** When risks are detected, the system escalates to our physician network for immediate intervention—turning passive data into active care.
