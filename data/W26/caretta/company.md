# Caretta

> Realtime AI for Sales Calls

Caretta is a Sales Intelligence for revenue teams. It joins reps in live calls, helping them handle information requests, questions, and objections in real time. Caretta lives in Slack to join the team conversation.

Caretta gets smarter automatically: every call expands its knowledge base and objection library, turning conversations into compounding organizational advantage.
