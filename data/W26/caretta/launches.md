### **Problem**

Sales reps (BDRs and AEs) are often responsible for deeply technical products (and sometimes, many of them!). This makes everyone’s life hard:

* Deals slow down when reps can't answer questions
* Reps miss upsell opportunities due to lack of knowledge
* Engineers get looped in prematurely and often

This stalls deals, and makes everyone inefficient. There must be a better way.

### **How Caretta Solves It**

Caretta is a realtime AI that enables AEs and BDRs to handle even deeply technical questions on live calls. It learns from everything: your documents, website, documentation, blogs, internal playbooks and any other scattered information to create your organisational intelligence. 

In-call, Caretta listens in. If it detects that a rep needs any help, it brings up the answers to the questions a customer is asking – so they can answer any question confidently on the spot.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97398&key=user_uploads/2329217/1aaca260-ce32-4e9e-891c-0c24600917ac)

After the call, Caretta joins your team in Slack, where the team can ask questions about meeting notes, do GTM analysis, track their to-dos, or draft a follow-up email. 

The best part? 

Caretta **maintains and expands its knowledge** by listening to your team in Slack and your top-performing reps in their calls.
