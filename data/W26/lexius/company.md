# Lexius

> AI Security Guards

Lexius turns any retail security camera into an AI security guard that detects shoplifting, identifies repeated and organized crime, and automates case generation with video evidence.

Lexius is the AI layer for existing security cameras. No rip and replace. No new hardware. No expensive upgrades.

Think of it as a security guard that never misses a moment.
