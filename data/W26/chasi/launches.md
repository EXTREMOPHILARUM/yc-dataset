**⚙️Problem**

One-third of the cost to build, move, and maintain the physical world goes to equipment (forklifts, cranes, excavators, etc), with trillions of dollars flowing through 100K+ dealers, distributors, and rental companies in the US alone. And that share is growing every year as machines get more capable and autonomous.

There are three core problems in the industry  

* **$300B value lost in idle equipment** - Fleet utilization sits below 60%. One missed request or scheduling breakdown can leave a $200K asset earning nothing for weeks, wiping out months of margin.
* **Software that creates work instead of eliminating it** - Dealers spend millions on DMS, ERP, and CRM systems that don't talk to each other. 50% of staff spend 2+ hours every day on phone calls, emails, and data entry. 
* **Tribal knowledge is the operating system** - Customer history, availability, who to call - it all lives in a few people's heads or offline notes. When they're off the clock, on vacation, or retired, the business stalls. New hires take months to ramp up, and after-hours demand goes unanswered.

**🤖 Solution**

Chasi plugs AI agents into a dealer's existing stack to work around the clock, maximizing revenue and margins:

* **24/7 AI Workforce** - Chasi handles requests across sales, rentals, and service around the clock. Checks availability, generates quotes, and automates renewals, service follow-ups, and dispatch, so teams can focus on relationships, not busywork.
* **Revenue Intelligence** - Chasi pulls context from existing systems to build a complete picture of every asset, customer, and branch. Recommends what to add, remove, or reposition across locations to maximize utilization and profitability.
* **Proactive Market Intelligence** - Chasi also tracks which customers are spending more vs. less, scans permits and public filings, and pulls in hundreds of public data sources to surface opportunities before competitors do.
