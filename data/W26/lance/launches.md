### **The Problem**

Hotels still run on phone calls for everything.

Guests call for towels, late checkout, maintenance, parking, and directions. Each call kicks off a manual workflow: someone answers, gathers details, routes the task, tracks it down, and follows up.

That model breaks when teams are lean. With **65% of hotels reporting staffing shortages**, simple requests require too many people and too many handoffs.

The result is constant interruptions, slow fulfillment, missed revenue, and burned-out staff. During busy periods or after hours, hotels miss **up to 40% of incoming calls**, not because demand is too high, but because the operation doesn’t scale.

## **Our Solution**

**Receptionist AI Agent:** (Call, SMS, Email, Hotel Messaging Platforms) answers guests 24/7 and completes requests end-to-end

* answers questions instantly and handles service requests
* gathers the right details, routes work to the right team, escalates when needed
* supports multiple languages so every guest gets help fast
* takes action through **vision based computer-use agents** when workflows require navigating through older systems like on-prem PMS or CRS

**Sales AI Agent:** (Call, SMS, Email) turns every inbound inquiry into a qualified revenue opportunity, so your sales team only talks to guests who are ready to book.

* responds to inbound sales inquiries and RFPs automatically
* qualifies leads and gathers booking or event details
* follows up instantly so hotels never lose deals to slow response
* drafts pricing, availability, and sales responses using hotel data
* unibox keeps a full record across email and messaging

**Lance Pad:** a tablet-first operations hub where service teams coordinate work and stay connected in real time across the property.

* replaces walkie talkies with real-time team communication across the property
* track open guest requests and service tasks live in one shared system
* manage escalations and see resolution outcomes instantly
* stays synced with Lance from request → action → completion
