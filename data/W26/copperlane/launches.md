# Launches

## Copperlane - AI Native Mortgage Loan Origination

Hey! We’re [Athan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/athanzhang/) and [Brianna](https://www.linkedin.com/in/brianna-lin/), the cofounders of [Copperlane](https://www.copperlane.ai/) (YC W26).

Copperlane helps **mortgage lenders originate loans faster and eliminate intake errors**. Our AI agent Penny proactively closes loans by optimizing rate pricing, directing borrowers, and verifying docs. Loan officers receive complete files and can focus on bringing in new deals.

[Copperlane Launch Video](https://youtu.be/-7Z8CI0O-GE)

**The Problem**

It costs lenders $11,800 to originate a single mortgage. Most of that money is burned during intake:

* Loan officer labor
* Lost loans from confusing applications, back-and-forth, and bad rates
* Costly mistakes from poorly structured files

All of these issues share the same root cause: people miss things. Borrowers upload the wrong documents, and lenders have to chase down borrowers to clarify details. This happens on almost every loan, every day, across 8 million loans a year.

**Our Solution**

Copperlane is an **AI-native loan origination system** powered by our agent, Penny, who behaves like a real loan officer by:

1. Pulling borrower documents, reading them, and checking borrower loan eligibility
2. Answering borrower questions live and helping them complete the loan application correctly
3. Flagging missing documents or conflicting information and following up with borrowers
4. Generating Letters of Explanation and building context around the applicant's financial profile

Penny delivers complete, organized files to Loan Officers, so they can spend more time closing and bringing in business.
