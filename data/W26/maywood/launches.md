# **The M&A Process Doesn’t Scale** 

There was $5 trillion of M&A in 2025, yet M&A workflow spans dozens of fragmented systems. Deal teams spend an extraordinary amount of time reconciling updates across platforms, copying figures from client materials, and reviewing large decks line by line to ensure accuracy and consistency. As teams increase transaction volume, they are forced to trade off speed with quality. The more deals a team runs, the harder it becomes to maintain a consistently high standard of execution. 

Execution, in practice, does not scale cleanly. We see this across middle-market and large investment banks. Teams rely on increasingly manual oversight to keep processes on track, with senior bankers spending time reviewing mechanics rather than focusing on judgment, positioning, and client advice. 

We believe the best deal teams should win based on the insight and guidance they deliver to clients, not on their ability to manage PowerPoint and internal coordination at scale. 

 

# **Maywood Expedites Execution and Elevates Quality**

Maywood is building an AI execution layer for investment banking. Our goal extends much further than producing decks at the push of a button and instead centers around unlocking deal teams to focus on their relationships with clients and maximizing outcomes. 

### **1. We do the Foundational Analysis** 

Maywood provides a thorough research process covering the company, industry, deal dynamics, and more. Instead of analysts and associates spending days pulling comparables, industry context, and buyer rationales or managing directors (MDs) prepping endlessly for their next call with the management team, our system handles the entire process natively. This enables Maywood to optimally position each company being sold, in some cases replacing an MD’s original thesis altogether. 

### **2. Our Materials Can be Sent Directly to Clients** 

Maywood analyzes past confidential information memorandums (CIMs) and pitches to extrapolate firm-specific language and formatting standards. We run models that determine what has led to success in the past and emulate that in current deals, all while being in an isolated system. Some MDs forward materials directly generated on Maywood, while others use it to draft first-pass materials and iterate quicker using natural language to make slide mutations. 

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97535&key=user_uploads/2969341/86433f7d-2dfb-4678-a235-18b429c7690a)

Maywood also transforms the due diligence portion of the process, where buyers ask countless questions about the transaction. Maywood surfaces answers immediately and flags missing information along with key risks to ensure deal teams are fully equipped.  

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97535&key=user_uploads/2969341/9910819f-a237-47f5-8465-6421fad16989)

### **3. We Deliver Everything via a Single Platform** 

Everything is connected. The analysis feeds into decks and the due diligence module. Data updates propagate throughout the entire system automatically. Every feature works as a function of this continuously updating data layer, from first document uploaded to close.
