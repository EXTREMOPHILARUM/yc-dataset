## Asimov 🤖: On demand human intelligence for robots

Hi YC! 👋

We're Anshul and Lyem, co-founders of Asimov. We're building the data infrastructure to enable the GPT-3 moment for humanoid robots.

**TL;DR**

Language models trained on trillions of words. Image models trained on billions of photos. Robots are starting from scratch. The bottleneck is diverse, real-world human movement data. We're building the infrastructure to collect and own it at scale.

**🎥 Launch Video**

<https://youtu.be/p5Dh80QW3mc>

**🚨 The Problem**

Humanoid robots need to learn from human movement in diverse environments, but the data to teach them doesn't exist at scale. Existing datasets are narrow, captured largely in controlled factory settings, repeating the same tasks in the same environments. What's missing is diversity: thousands of different people, in thousands of different spaces, doing the full range of things humans actually do.

The market for robotics training data will dwarf what was spent on language model data, and right now nobody owns the supply chain.

**🚀 Our Solution**

Asimov empowers individuals worldwide to earn a living by recording what they already do: cooking, cleaning, working, living. That means data from thousands of different people, homes, and workplaces: the task and environment diversity that factory-only datasets simply can't replicate.

We've built the full stack to make it happen: proprietary hardware, annotation tooling, and a vertically integrated pipeline that makes our data higher quality and cheaper to produce than anything on the market. The largest robotics labs in the world are already our customers.
