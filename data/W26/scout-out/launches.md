# **❌ The Problem**

Residential contractors are running complex businesses on a patchwork of tools that were never built for construction.

* **Project management is fragmented.** Plans live in email. Estimates live in spreadsheets. Photos live on phones. Proposals get copy-pasted from old Word docs. Nothing talks to anything else.
* **Estimating is slow and error-prone.** Reading through plans, measuring takeoffs, pricing labor and materials, then assembling a professional proposal — this process takes hours and still produces mistakes. Contractors either underbid to win, or overbid to be safe, and lose anyway.
* **AI tools exist but aren't built for construction.** Generic project management software ignores the realities of field work — plan sets, per-page takeoffs, scope changes mid-build, subcontractor coordination.

  Contractors end up duct-taping tools together and paying the price in margin and stress.

**The result:** Jobs are won with incomplete bids, managed with ad-hoc systems, and closed with manual paperwork that eats hours contractors don't have.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97506&key=user_uploads/2106935/0cdecb42-5772-4e1d-81d1-1d7fb87db86d)

# **🙌 Our Solution: One Platform for the Entire Job**

Scout Out is a single platform that handles the full lifecycle of a construction job — from first estimate to final invoice — with AI doing the heavy lifting.

### Job & Customer Hub 🏗️

Everything lives in one place, organized around the job:

* Customer records and full contact history
* All uploads in one place: plans, photos, permits, contracts, and insurance
* Status tracking across every deliverable and milestone
* Every conversation, change order, and document tied to the right job

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97506&key=user_uploads/2106935/ffbb3afc-3a3e-425d-800a-23c7fc95413a)

### Instant AI-Powered Bidding ⚡

When a contractor finds a project they want, ScoutOut generates a **clean, editable quote in seconds**.

* Upload plans, photos, or a short description
* AI produces a structured estimate covering scope, labor, and materials
* Contractors can review, adjust, and send a professional bid immediately

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97506&key=user_uploads/2106935/cf8c8b3c-0744-4b8b-b092-16e0952bf102)

### Built for Real Contractor Workflows 🔧

ScoutOut is designed for how contractors actually work:

* No long setup or training
* Quotes are editable and contractor-owned
* Built to be used between site visits, calls, and active builds
