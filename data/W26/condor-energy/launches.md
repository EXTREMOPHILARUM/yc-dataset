❌ **The problem**

We realized that as the grid moves towards renewables, large energy consumers like data centers would need better tools for their electricity management. We interviewed 200+ companies spending €Ms/y on power, and we realized this was a consistent problem.

* Electricity costs are no longer “price \* volume”: they depend on location, time of day, peaks, imbalance, regulation…
* Large energy consumers must leverage highly complex options and invest $Ms : on-site generation and storage, long-term contracts, RECs, demand-response programs…
* Yet, they still manage power on incorrect spreadsheets, manual forecasting, wrong data and simplistic simulations.

And these errors lead to misinformed Capex investments, delayed interconnection, higher energy costs and unstable grids.

**🧠 The solution**\
Today, Condor works with large companies in Europe, and we’re launching in the US. They currently use our software for their:

* Hedging: a large European industrial simulates and tracks its hedge portfolios to secure long-term energy costs.
* Budgeting: a major retail chain forecasts PPAs and Spot market exposure over all of its stores, and then verifies its invoice.
* Investment: a leading data center operator with complex procurement optimizes its energy spending by simulating on-site & off-site investments.

\
Here’s what Condor does for them:

* Monitor and forecast their day-to-day energy needs and costs, on each facility and in total.
* Improve their energy procurement and plan their investments based on accurate data, simulations and forecasts.
* Plan their operations and steer their behind-the-meter assets directly via our platform.

Because we centralize and curate all their energy data (consumption, market, contracts, regulation…), our customers use Condor to make better decisions - saving millions and de-risking their operations.
