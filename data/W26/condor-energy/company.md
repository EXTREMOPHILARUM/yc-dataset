# Condor Energy

> Energy OS enabling reliable, cheap and sustainable electricity supply.

Condor helps large electricity consumers - C&I and data centers - cut electricity costs by helping the grid.
We are former electricity traders and energy systems experts building our AI-powered software. Our clients use Condor to control their electricity costs, to steer their consumption based on reliable forecasts and to conduct profitable investments.
