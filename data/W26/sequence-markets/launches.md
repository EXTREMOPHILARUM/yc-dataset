❌ **The problem**

Execution is fragmented across 20+ exchanges \~ Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, Uniswap. Liquidity, fees, and latency vary by venue, so teams are forced into manual venue-by-venue workflows.

That means:

* Hopping between exchanges to find the best price
* Stitching order books in spreadsheets or scripts
* Zero visibility into execution quality
* Managing risk separately across each venue

Result? Inconsistent fills and millions in hidden slippage. Even the biggest funds trade like it's 2017.

🐂 **The solution**

**What Sequence enables:**

With Sequence, you'll save millions by sourcing liquidity across CEX and DEX in one workflow \~ no more hidden slippage.

You'll make millions because our proprietary ultra-low-latency infrastructure \~ colocated servers, hardware-accelerated networking, compiled-code speed \~ enables high-frequency trading strategies that weren't possible before.

What Sequence enables:

* **Source liquidity** across CEX and DEX venues in one order
* **Apply risk controls** before and during execution
* **Track execution analytics** to improve fill quality over time
* **Run via terminal + API + SDK/MCP** \~ for humans or algos

The only thing stopping you is fragmented liquidity. We fix that.

We're building toward one-account, best-path execution across all crypto and tokenized rails.
