# Sequence Markets

> Low latency infrastructure for fragmented crypto markets

Sequence Markets helps crypto traders save millions in hidden slippage by sourcing liquidity across CEX and DEX venues in one workflow.

Digital asset liquidity is fragmented across hundreds of venues. Teams are forced into manual venue-by-venue workflows, stitching order books in spreadsheets, with zero visibility into execution quality. The result? Inconsistent fills and millions in hidden slippage.

We built ultra-low-latency execution infrastructure (colocated servers, hardware accelerated networking, compiled code speed) with sub 12 microsecond internal latency (faster than NYSE). Our stack enables strategies that weren't possible before.

Save millions by eliminating hidden slippage. Make billions by trading faster and smarter
