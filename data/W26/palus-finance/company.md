# Palus Finance

> Better yields on idle cash for startups and SMBs

Palus Finance gives startups and SMBs access to institutional-grade bond portfolios, earning 1–1.5% more than money market funds with comparable safety and near-immediate liquidity.

After a fundraise, startups park 18–24 months of cash in a money market fund and draw it down gradually, paying for same-day liquidity on cash they won't touch for months. Every existing treasury product does the same thing: sweep into an MMF and charge a fee. 

Palus replaces that with a bond portfolio optimized for startup treasuries, targeting 4.5–5% vs. ~3.5% from money market. On a $10M allocation, that's an extra $100–150K per year in runway. Fortune 500 treasury teams use these kinds of portfolios all the time, but they've never been easily accessible to startups until now.

We're the only startup treasury product with a differentiated investment strategy. Everyone else is just a wrapper on a money market fund. 

Instead of replacing your bank, we plug into it via Plaid, so founders can spend five minutes optimizing their treasury strategy with Palus and get back to building their company.
