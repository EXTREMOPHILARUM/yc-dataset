# 👩🏻‍💻 **How does it work?**

**1. Forward to Arcline:** A contract lands in your inbox full of redlines. Or you need something drafted from scratch. No matter what it is, forward it to Arcline [here](https://start.arcline-ai.com/), with a description on what you’re trying to get done. 

**2. Upfront flat fee:** Before our lawyers start working, you’ll know exactly what it costs. No surprise bills, no opaque “we’ll see how long it takes”-disclaimers. 

**3. AI does the first 80%**: Our AI drafts the first pass, pulls relevant templates, playbooks, precedent data, industry data, your attachments and customer data. 

**4. A top startup lawyer reviews 20%:** An elite startup lawyer reviews, revises, answers your questions and applies judgment to your situation. This is the part you’re paying for. 

**5. Same-day turnaround:** Because the heavy lift is already done, we can often turn work same-day. 

**Slack-integration**: We can also plug into Slack and operate like your fractional general counsel - on call whenever you need us.
