### **❌ The Problem**

Immigration law is broken - especially for startups

1. **Slow** - Traditional law firms take 6–8 weeks just to prepare a petition - burning runway and blocking hires
2. **Expensive** - Lawyers charge $10k-35k per visa, pricing out early-stage companies
3. **Black Box** - Founders & managers often have zero visibility into case progress until it's too late
4. **Unpredictable** - Inconsistent quality means RFEs, delays, and denials
5. **High Stakes -** A single denial can force a founder to leave the country or lose a critical engineer

Startups trying to make a critical hire simply can’t afford the cost, time, and uncertainty of traditional immigration firms.
