**❌ The problem**

Public companies are required to prove their financial reporting and underlying processes - from payments to system access - work as expected. Today, that work is **highly manual and costly**:

* **Weeks spent scoping what to audit**, assessing risk across financial statements, sub-accounts, and business units
* Meeting with stakeholders, then spending **5-15 hours to turn notes into a flowchart** visualizing each process
* Digging through **spreadsheets, PDFs, screenshots, and emails** to find relevant evidence
* **Thousands of hours/year repeating the same checks**: sifting through 200-page documents for signatures, matching orders to invoices, reviewing system access for hundreds of employees, etc

And it costs **$200–$800/hour** to outsource this work to outside accounting/consulting firms, adding up to **$300K–$1M+ per year** in annual spend.

🐂 **The solution**

What Oxus enables:

* **Accelerate audit scoping**, automatically surfacing higher-risk areas using both financial metrics and qualitative factors
* **Within minutes**, **generate and update process flowcharts** immediately after meetings
* **Simplify evidence review**, so teams can locate and interpret information across disparate files and systems
* **Eliminate repetitive manual checks**, handling large volumes of comparisons and reviews at once and producing reviewer-ready documentation with labeled, traceable evidence

With Oxus, teams save **millions** in outsourcing fees and get audits done **10x faster.**
