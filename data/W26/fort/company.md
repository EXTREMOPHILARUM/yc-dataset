# Fort

> Strength Tracking Wearable

Fort is a wearable that tracks strength training for people who care about longevity.

Other wearables make you manually log every rep, set, and exercise, then provide unhelpful feedback or underestimate strength-related fatigue. We use heart rate and motion data to track your strength workouts for you, then provide thoughtful, actionable feedback that helps you achieve your goals.
