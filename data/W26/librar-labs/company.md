# Librar Labs

> The data and intelligence layer of literature

Librar Labs is the data and intelligence layer of literature.

We're working to solve the biggest bottleneck for the AI singularity: the world is full of massive amounts of unstructured knowledge that is hard for computers and AI to navigate. 

Our first commercial product is for the literature industry with a focus on school libraries. It captures and analyze unstructured (and pre-Librar unattainable) data using it to more than double reading rates at schools. Fastest growing library tech company ever backed by founders and operators of OpenAI, Depict, google maps, kahoot etc.
