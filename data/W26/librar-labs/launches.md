Announcing Librar Labs - AI for librarians

Librarians spend countless hours on tedious work that they don’t like, but still have to do. Today, Librar is used in nearly 300 schools, helping librarians cut down on inventory, reporting, and acquisitions work so they can do what they are best at and enjoy the most: engaging with the community.

One of the most liked features is how the librarian with a photo of the shelf, Librar identify the items and can take inventory, analyze circulation or even just set up the library.

One of the Librar founders used to work as a librarian and saw the day-to-day challenges firsthand. Since managing a library is very challenging, we decided to build Librar to make it easier for everyone.\
