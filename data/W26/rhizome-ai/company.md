# Rhizome AI

> Always know what the FDA thinks

$8 trillion dollars of market cap needs to know what the FDA thinks. Rhizome AI makes that happen. 

Users ask a question and get an answer backed by up to 1,000 documents, where each statement has a citation. The data is pulled from terabytes of authoritative regulatory & clinical databases. 

Chetan started his career as a research engineer, but quickly realized he enjoyed building more than research. He joined EvolutionaryScale as their sole founding product engineer, launching their developer-scientist platform and scaling to tens of thousands of users and billions of API calls. He was also #16 at Instabase helping banks with document & imaging processing, closing $7m as the technical lead. 

Being so plugged into the AI world, he saw lots of AI drug discovery companies, but very few AI companies focused on bringing those drugs to market. Talking to people, it quickly became clear that a big, expensive barrier was understanding FDA thinking.
