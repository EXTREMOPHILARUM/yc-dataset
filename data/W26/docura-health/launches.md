## Docura Health - AI-Native Med-Legal Firm to automate tedious workers’ comp workflows for doctors

**Problem:**

When a worker gets injured on the job, they see their primary doctor for treatment. The doctor treats them through a series of appointments until they reach maximum medical improvement - the point where no more treatment will improve their condition.

If the insurance company denies the claim, that's when the bottleneck kicks in. Both sides get attorneys, and they send all the medical records to a Qualified Medical Evaluator (QME) for an independent assessment.

The QME has to manually review these records. Some cases have thousands of pages, while others have tens of thousands. Even straightforward cases take 6-8 hours. Complex cases can take multiple days.

There are also agencies that take 40% of the revenue per case to do some of this administrative work for them.

Either way, doctors are missing out. They're either losing significant revenue to agencies, or they're spending so much time on paperwork that they can't handle more cases.

**Solution:**

Docura Health automates the entire workflow:

Upload medical record PDFs and case documents → we generate fully compliant record reviews and reports in 30 minutes

Automate patient history collection calls that admin staff normally handle before evaluations

Doctors handle significantly more cases while keeping far more revenue per case.
