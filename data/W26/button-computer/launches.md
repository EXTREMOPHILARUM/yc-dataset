# **TL;DR**

Today we’re launching a small hardware device: a button that you clip to your shirt. You press it to talk to AI, and it responds immediately.

It's like an iPod Shuffle for AI!

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=98398&key=user_uploads/1273646/22d485b8-deff-468f-bd83-70b758432e34)

# **What makes Button unique**

Button really is the first piece of hardware designed exclusively to give you instant access to voice AI. It has dual microphones and a built-in speaker.

Unlike other AI pins and pendants, Button doesn't listen to your private conversations. It can only hear you when you press the button.

And it’s fast. _Really fast!_
