### **Problem**

Missing the right patent, or getting the analysis wrong, can cost billions. That’s why patent practitioners spend a large share of their time reading technical documents and building arguments. LLMs can generate text, but patent work has non-negotiables that most tools still don’t meet.

* **Research is manual:** practitioners spend hours searching and analyzing to find the few patents that matter
* **Tools produce static reports:** most solutions generate a final document or flat lists instead of interactive, argument-led analysis that adapts
* **Context gets lost:** teams lack flexible end-to-end tools, resulting in a fragmented and clunky software stack
* **AI isn’t trusted without evidence:** in patent work, outputs must be traceable to specific references with clear rationale

# **Solution**

We've built Cursor for patent practitioners, using agentic AI to improve how high-stakes patent work is done end-to-end. Our agentic approach works alongside you, iterating on analysis rather than producing one-time reports. Specialized agents with deep domain and skills expertise handle complex patent analysis while building context from your documents and prior work. This enables deeper and more granular analysis that was not previously technically feasible.
