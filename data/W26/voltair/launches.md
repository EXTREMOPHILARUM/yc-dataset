## Voltair: Drones that Charge on Power Lines

<https://youtu.be/ZSnkcbXWf7Q> 

**Tl;dr -** We build drones that charge directly on transmission lines. Our drones can remain deployed indefinitely, positioned where utility companies need them most, and ready to inspect power lines at a moment’s notice. 

**Background**

The American power grid faces unprecedented challenges. Extreme weather is more frequent, more destructive, and less predictable. From 2000 to 2023, 80% of all major outages were caused by extreme weather events. At the same time, power utilities are expected to deliver higher reliability, faster restoration, and stronger wildfire and safety outcomes. Utility investments in distribution grid hardening nearly tripled from 2003 to 2023.

Utilities cannot manage what they cannot see. Today, critical information on the physical grid arrives at the speed of a person reaching the problem. Linemen need high fidelity information faster to prioritize and act decisively.

**Public Utilities**

Our immediate focus is providing utilities with rapid infrastructure assessments across large areas following storms, power outages, and fires.

At scale, our technology enables a step change in how utilities operate. A linemen or engineer can request imagery of a specific line span or structure and receive it immediately. A downed pole in a high-risk residential area can be located and assessed quickly. Voltair can support routine, day-to-day operational visibility as well as urgent, high-consequence events.
