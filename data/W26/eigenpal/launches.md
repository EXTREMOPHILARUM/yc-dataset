**What We Learned**

Working on back office document automation, we kept hearing the same questions:

* _"How do I know this workflow will actually work before I bet my ops on it?"_
* _"I aim to have 100 workflows. How will I monitor them all?"_
* _"Can this run in our cloud? Data can't leave our infrastructure."_
* _"Our files have contracts, amendments, and extensions that reference each other. Can you piece that together?"_

Traditional automation tools were built for deterministic processes and they're not designed for AI. AI is a game changer for document processing, but businesses still can't trust it at scale. Why? Evals and monitoring aren't built in. And most new platforms can't run on-prem.

**The Solution: A Platform Where You Can Trust AI Workflows**

EigenPal is built around one principle: like any employees you entrust work to, you need to trust your workflows before you deploy them and monitor them after.

* **Agentic workflow builder.** Our AI workflow builder let’s you create workflows with easy in your natural language. It can even iterate on a workflow on a test dataset to tune accuracy.
* **Eval-first.** Before any workflow goes live, test it against your historical documents. See exactly what accuracy you're getting. Deploy only when you're confident. **Set up a feedback loop in production** so workflows are adjusted with feedback.
* **Document-centric.** Upload files directly, no public URLs needed. Built-in storage plus a powerful template filler to generate polished output docs from your extracted data.
* **Configure everything.** Choose your OCR/VLM, LLM, configure every step of the pipeline separately. Access built-in tracing for workflows in our app or export via OpenTelemetry.
* **Monitor hundreds of workflows at once.** One dashboard showing costs, compliance logs, and failures across every workflow. Know immediately when something drifts.
* **Collaborate with your team.** Org workspaces let you share workflows, templates, and results with colleagues. Build once, use across your team.
* **On-prem or cloud.** Runs in your infrastructure with your LLMs, or use our hosted version. Your choice.
