### What we are building

Unlike other GEO tools, **sitefire actually tells you how to improve your AI visibility**. sitefire analyzes AI answers (query fan-out, sourced pages, cited pages) to identify which content works. Based on this, sitefire tells you which third-party sites to reach out to and which UGC content (Youtube, Reddit, etc.) to engage with.

Second, sitefire synthesizes the **top-cited web pages** and **writes brand-aware, AI-optimized blog posts for you** **- directly delivered to your CMS**. You just publish, improve your AI visibility, and start winning leads.
