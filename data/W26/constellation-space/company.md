# Constellation Space

> AI for space mission assurance.

Constellation Space is building the autonomous infrastructure layer for the next generation of space networking. As satellite constellations rapidly scale from hundreds of nodes to tens of thousands, traditional ground-based orchestration fundamentally breaks down. ConstellationOS bridges this gap by providing zero-intervention network handoffs, predictive thermal load-balancing, and sub-second optical routing.

Our founding team spent years building and operating satellite systems at SpaceX, Blue Origin, and NASA. We started Constellation to solve the problem we kept running into: reactive monitoring doesn't scale.

We are building the sub-second routing protocols and hyperscale load-balancing infrastructure that will manage the future of off-world telecommunications and compute.
