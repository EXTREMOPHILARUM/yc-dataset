### 🐌 The aerospace supply chain is holding OEMs back

Every drone, submarine, and satellite needs a custom battery pack. But as demand grows into the tens of thousands per year, the supply chain is a generation behind:

* **6-12 months** to design and qualify custom builds.
* **6-12 months** to ramp to high volume production.
* **At least 5x** the cost of vertically integrating the product itself.

Forced vertical integration results in slower programs, higher costs, and significant delivery risk.

### 🛠️ Kyten builds aerospace battery packs fast and at high volume

We spent a combined eight years at **Starlink** and **Shield AI** driving down lead times and increasing production capacity of aerospace-grade battery packs. Kyten builds on this experience to deliver high volumes in weeks, not 12+ months:

* **1 week** to design via adaptive architecture.
* **1 week** to qualify via automated in-house testing.
* **1 week** to volume production via software-defined manufacturing.
* **1,000s of units/year** per product.
