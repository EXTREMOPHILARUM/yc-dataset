# Launches

## DroneTector: high-resolution radars to detect small drones

**TL;DR:** Drone threats have evolved. Detection technology hasn’t and makes lots of mistakes. We solve this with a suite of sensors, including our millimeter-wave radar, purpose-built for the hardest targets. If you’re responsible for countering drone threats, get in touch at [founders@dronetector.com](mailto:founders@dronetector.com).

**The problem**

Small drones are now a security problem. They’re cheap, widely available, and increasingly capable. As a result, they’re showing up in places they shouldn’t – from airports and critical infrastructure to prisons and defense environments.

Detecting them reliably, however, is still a hard problem.

Most drone detection systems weren’t designed for small, low-cost drones. False positives are common, and real threats are missed.

That’s why we’re building [@DroneTector](https://bookface.ycombinator.com/company/31144).

**Our solution** 

DroneTector provides millimeter-wave radar engineered to detect small drones that others miss. Integrated with our camera and acoustic arrays, we deliver reliable detection against the most challenging targets.

See our technology in action: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=50xAb06cyq4>

Our sensors can operate as a standalone solution or as part of a distributed detection network. They’re highly scalable, adaptable to complex environments, and able to detect drones that other solutions simply can’t.
