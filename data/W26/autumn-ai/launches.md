## Autumn AI: Stalk your prospects at scale.

Hi, we’re Vishnu and Shiv, and we’re super excited to introduce Autumn! 

**Tl;dr.** GTM teams waste hours manually tracking prospects across the internet. Autumn gives you a live feed of your entire target universe, so you're always first to know and first to act.

<https://youtu.be/v37JMhX8GVc>

**The problem.** Your best prospects are already telling you when and how to sell to them through posts, tweets, commits, blogs. These signals are valuable but decay fast. To catch signals in real time, you'd need to monitor dozens of sources per person, synthesize unstructured content across platforms, and surface intent the moment it appears. This is tedious for one prospect; imagine trying a few thousand.

**The solution.** Autumn tracks your prospects across the internet, at scale. Define your ICP and the signals that matter, and we deliver a condensed, real-time feed filtered by intents. Connect to email or Slack and get notified the moment a target prospect's behavior matches. Here are some workflows that Autumn is optimized for:

* **Incorporation prospecting:** Surface new startup founders as soon as they incorporate their companies and track early signals around banking, payroll, ML infra.
* **Enterprise account monitoring:** Build org trees and track entire departments within target accounts to see buying signals emerge across the org. 
* **Conference prep:** Monitor attendee lists ahead of events and prioritize outreach to the people already showing interest in what you sell.
