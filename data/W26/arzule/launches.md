## ❌ The problem

B2B Partnerships (e.g., distribution channels, technology integrations, etc.) drive a huge portion of SaaS revenue, but most teams still run them on intuition and manual systems of record.

Today, partnerships look like:

* Discovering good partners _after_ the opportunity window has passed
* Spending time on low-impact partners because there’s no prioritization
* Managing everything in spreadsheets or basic PRMs that go stale quickly
* Waiting 12–18 months to prove value while the rest of the business runs on monthly targets
* Having no clear way to connect partnership activity to actual revenue

The result is a lot of effort, a few wins, and no repeatable system.

## 🚀 The solution

Arzule connects to your CRM and works 24/7 to solve this.

**What Arzule enables:**

* Identify which partnerships are most likely to drive revenue based on your product, ecosystem, and timing
* Surface _why a partnership makes sense now_ (not just who to reach out to)
* Build deep partner profiles using ecosystem and proprietary data
* Guide partner activation, co-selling, and ongoing collaboration
* Track how partnership activity connects directly to revenue outcomes

With Arzule, teams move from managing partnerships manually to **scaling them like a predictable revenue channel**. 

We already work with companies collectively driving over $400M in partnership-driven revenue.
