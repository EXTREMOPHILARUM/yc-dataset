# Arzule

> ROI attribution to scale ecosystem driven growth

Arzule is an AI-native ecosystem revenue platform for B2B SaaS teams, starting with partnerships.

Arzule uses a quantitative approach to estimate ecosystem revenue impact by combining CRM deal outcomes with ecosystem signals such as shared customers, integrations, partner referrals, and co-selling activity. Using probabilistic regression models and influence chains across the ecosystem graph, Arzule estimates how much each partner or integration increases the likelihood of pipeline creation and deal conversion. This allows teams to prioritize the partnerships most likely to generate measurable revenue.

Beyond discovery, Arzule structures and manages the entire partnership lifecycle from identifying the right partners, to prioritizing based on revenue potential, to activating and scaling those relationships.

Using always-on AI agents and proprietary data, Arzule builds dynamic profiles of companies and predicts which opportunities will drive measurable revenue, when to engage, and how to structure each relationship for maximum impact.

It also optimizes execution through automated outreach, partner coordination, and performance tracking, ensuring every partnership and ecosystem motion is tied to clear ROI.
