## Proximitty - Autonomous loan servicing and collections

Hi everyone! We’re Wye Yew and Zi, co-founders of Proximitty. 

**TL;DR**: We help banks, fintechs, and credit unions use no‑code AI agents to automate complex servicing and collections workflows, so they can reduce manual work, operate more efficiently, and increase recovery rates. 

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JsiiDQqGPn0>

**Our ask**: If you are leading Operations, Finance, Collections, or Servicing at banks or fintechs and looking to modernise your servicing / collection operations, we’d love to help. Just reach out at [founders@proximitty.ai](mailto:founders@proximitty.ai).

**Problem**: 

Servicing and collections are heavily manual today in banks and fintechs - and the cost of servicing and collecting a delinquent loan has risen drastically in the last 5 years, constraining a bank’s ability to originate more loans.

Collections are often outsourced to collection agencies due to capacity constraints, but success rates have been historically low, and consumer complaints on collection practices cost &gt;$500M/year in fines.

Most voice agents are point solutions that lack crucial context during collection calls and aren’t effective if skip tracing isn’t done properly.

\
**Solution**:

Proximitty is a unified data and agent platform for loan servicing and collections. Banks and fintechs use our no-code builder to deploy AI agents across their collection and servicing workflows - no engineering resources or APIs needed.

The platform is built for complex workflows like:

* Negotiating hardship plans over the phone and updating backend systems in real-time
* Monitoring complex compliance requirements and performing QA across 100+ jurisdictions
* Increasing right-party contact rates by automating deep-skip tracing
* Parsing borrower financials from emails and spreading them across credit models for credit monitoring, and auto-following up to collect missing information

…and 100+ more custom SOPs!

Our agents self-improve based on data - and manage to increase collection velocity and collection rates on your existing tech, without the need for overhauling your tech or SOPs.
