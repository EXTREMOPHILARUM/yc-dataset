# Shofo

> The World's Largest Video Library

We're building the world's largest library of videos by combining public videos collected from the open web and private videos aggregated from thousands of different sources. Both feed into a single, continuously updating index of billions of videos that we clean, search, and label to build custom datasets for AI teams. If a lab needs 100K hours of cooking videos featuring hand-object interactions with no scene changes, we query our index, extract the matching subset, and deliver a clean, structured dataset in days.
