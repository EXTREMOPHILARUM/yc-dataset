## Shofo - Common Crawl for Video

### **TL;DR**

Shofo is building the largest index of videos.

[**https://youtu.be/J8wFAjQun8Y**](https://youtu.be/J8wFAjQun8Y)

### **❌ The Problem**

AI labs require massive, high-quality video data to train multimodal models, but clean, custom video datasets cost millions and take months to collect.

### **✅ Our Solution**

We're building the world's largest library of videos by combining public videos collected from the open web and private videos aggregated from thousands of different sources. Both feed into a single, continuously updating index of billions of videos that we clean, search, and label to build custom datasets for AI teams.

For example, if a lab needs 100K hours of cooking videos featuring hand-object interactions with no scene changes, we query our index, extract the matching subset, and deliver a clean, structured dataset in days.
