## REV1 - Automating engineering drawings for Mechanical Engineers

**TL;DR:** REV1 turns 3D CAD models into manufacturing-ready 2D drawings in minutes instead of hours.

[**https://youtu.be/rlGxyHiPOW4**](https://youtu.be/rlGxyHiPOW4)

**The Problem:**

Every manufactured part – whether it's for aerospace, robotics, medical devices, or consumer electronics – needs a 2D engineering drawing that defines the manufacturing specs to ensure the part functions as designed. Mechanical engineers spend **over 25% of their time** creating these drawings manually. It's tedious, error-prone, and unavoidable. It's also deeply technical – the standard that governs tolerancing is **over 300 pages of rules**.

The real cost isn't just time – it's innovation. Engineers should be designing the next iteration and solving hard problems, not manually specifying tolerances.

**Our Solution:**

REV1 automates drawing creation. Start with a 3D CAD file, specify your design intent, and we generate a fully annotated drawing with proper GD&T. Review and edit in our native interface before sending to manufacturers. All that while staying synced with your source files in your PLM system.
