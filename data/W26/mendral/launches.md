### The problem

Dev teams waste thousands of engineering hours chasing broken builds, flaky tests, and fragile pipelines. Most tooling is still pre-AI. It creates alerts and dashboards, but developers still do the work. Now, with coding agents accelerating code production, CI load and failure modes are only getting worse.

Teams should not need dedicated platform-engineering headcount just to keep delivery from breaking.

### What Mendral does

Mendral **observes → diagnoses → acts → learns** across your delivery pipeline.

Today, Mendral can:

* Diagnose CI failures (what changed, what failed, why)
* Detect and mitigate flaky tests
* Identify slow builds/runs and propose fixes
* Help with code reviews tied to delivery reliability

Over time, Mendral becomes a self-improving delivery system for **performance, quality, security, and compliance**, without needing dedicated DevOps/platform headcount for smaller teams.
