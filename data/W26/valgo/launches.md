# Launches

## Valgo — Risk quantification for insurance of physical AI

_TL;DR — Valgo is the risk quantification layer for insurance of physical AI. Since historical claims data doesn’t exist for autonomy, we turn simulations into loss estimates that insurers need to price coverage._

<https://youtu.be/td2uzvldFPY>

Insurers face challenges pricing physical AI systems because the historical data simply doesn't exist. Your car insurance draws from over 30 billion claims records, but autonomous trucks and robots have nearly zero. Valgo is the risk quantification platform that closes that gap. We are building probabilistic models of routes, tasks, and environments from the bottom up. We output simulated loss estimates that insurers need to price coverage. **Valgo is the foundational layer that gives insurance providers confidence to properly price autonomy risk.**
