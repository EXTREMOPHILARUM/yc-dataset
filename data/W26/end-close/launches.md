## End Close: Solving payments reconciliation with AI Agents

**TLDR**

End Close is building automatic reconciliation for payments companies, replacing manual ops work with AI agents that automatically investigate and resolve every exception.

Our team has 6 years of experience building systems to reconcile a trillion dollars annually.

<https://youtu.be/5xral3GHBVs>

**The Problem**

Every day, ops teams at payments companies spend hours manually investigating exceptions - transactions that didn't match, funds that can't be accounted for, discrepancies across banks, processors, and ledgers. Legacy reconciliation tools weren't built for this. They were designed for month-end close, not real-time fintech infrastructure. And the cost of getting it wrong is real: lost money, unreliable financials, and compliance exposure.

**The Solution**

End Close continuously ingests records from your banks, processors, and data warehouse - so exceptions are caught as soon as they arise, not weeks later during month-end close.

And unlike most reconciliation tools that only tell you something is wrong, our AI agent handles exceptions end-to-end before they have time to compound - pulling context from connected systems, finding the root cause, and resolving them without looping in your team. Hours of manual investigation become minutes of autonomous work, with full transparency into every decision.
