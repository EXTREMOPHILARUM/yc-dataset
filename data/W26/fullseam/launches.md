# 👫 Our story

We’re the same team that founded TaxProper, our first YC-backed company. At TaxProper, we paid more than a $1 billion a year in property tax payments for our customers until we were successfully acquired by Opendoor. 

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97900&key=user_uploads/135383/6d1853f9-5cc5-4804-9644-e5ab59b94d90)

We started FullSeam because we lived this pain ourselves.

At TaxProper, we were growing quickly — but our accounting team was drowning in invoicing, reconciliations, customer questions, and exception handling. The software we used kept records, but it didn’t do the work. Humans still had to chase emails, upload documents, correct entries, and follow up until payment landed.

We realized accounting teams don’t need another dashboard — **they need teammates**.

# How it works

FullSeam's AI agents plug into your ERP, billing system, and spreadsheets and take over repetitive, error-prone accounting and finance work – without changing how your team operates.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97900&key=user_uploads/135383/6dfee408-d7bd-4a26-b717-df5d1e9d9061)

For accounts receivable teams, FullSeam:

* Follows up with customers until payment is received
* Produces requested documentation
* Enters invoices and updates directly into customer AP portals

For accounts payable teams, FullSeam:

* Ingests invoices from email, portals, and PDFs\
  Matches invoices to POs, contracts, and proof of service
* Follows up with vendors when information is missing
* Categorizes expenses, pushing clean entries into your GL
