# Launches

## Traverse - Data Research for the Non-Verifiable

Hello. We are[ Lance Yan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/lance-yan/) and [Zachary Yu](https://www.linkedin.com/in/zacharyyu/), the founders of [**Traverse**](https://traverse.so).

**TLDR:** Traverse is a data research lab focused on the hardest unsolved problem in frontier model training: **ambiguous, taste-dependent work**. 

We capture real human behavior in highly controlled settings and turn judgment, taste, and long-horizon reasoning into high-fidelity environments for frontier models.

<https://youtu.be/CsDg8PYfg64>

**The Problem**

Reinforcement learning has already helped frontier models achieve superhuman ability in math and coding since these domains have very clean and deterministic feedback loops. 

However, the next frontier domains (law, healthcare, sales, writing, etc) aren’t as black and white. Current approaches fail to accurately represent subjective scenarios, despite these domains being where most of the economic value is.

**The Solution**

We are building data factories to create high fidelity real life work environments at scale.

Traverse captures what people actually think while doing their work; we are pioneering a new category of training data that is yet to exist.\
