## **The Problem**

Dental practices leak revenue at the front desk.

Calls come in while teams are busy or after hours. Try calling a dentist in the evening or on the weekend and it will usually go straight to voicemail.

Patients do not wait. They move on.

This becomes even more painful with paid acquisition. Clinics invest heavily in ads, but often follow up hours or days later. By then, the patient is gone.

What’s left:

* Missed opportunities
* Underutilized provider calendars
* High CAC with poor conversion

This bottleneck has existed for decades and worsens as clinics scale.

## **What We Built**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97818&key=user_uploads/2594817/80f45110-8b4d-47e2-8824-15d30704d9b2)

Patientdesk is an AI booking system built specifically for dental clinics.

Instead of solving one piece of the workflow, we run **patient intake end to end**, without increasing staff workload or forcing clinics to change how they operate.\
\
**Unlike generic booking tools, Patientdesk is patient-aware.**

It understands who is calling, their history with the clinic, and what type of appointment they are asking about, so conversations are handled with the right context instead of starting from zero every time.

Patientdesk covers:

* Incoming patient calls
* Intake and qualification
* Appointment booking
* **Insurance verification in real time during the call**
* Payments and deposits

All within a single system.

We are the first system that verifies insurance live during the call within a couple of seconds. This lets clinics confirm coverage and pricing immediately, instead of booking blindly or calling patients back later, which is where many high-value cases drop off.\
\
Patients get answers immediately instead of waiting for callbacks or manual checks.

## **How It Works**

Patientdesk integrates directly with major clinic management systems across the US, Australia, and the UK.

We have **active partnerships with three major practice management systems**, allowing us to book, update, and verify information directly inside clinic systems.

When something needs to be booked, checked, or updated, it happens automatically inside the clinic’s existing system.

If a patient asks about coverage, **insurance is verified on the spot**, typically within seconds.\
No follow-up calls. No dropped patients.

After the visit, Patientdesk continues to work:

* We follow up with patients
* When a payment is due, we handle it automatically

The front desk is no longer a bottleneck and becomes a revenue engine.
