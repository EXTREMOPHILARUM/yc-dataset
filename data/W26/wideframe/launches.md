**Our Story**

Zack has been an engineering leader for 20+ years. Daniel managed $1B+ in ads for tech companies. We are ex-coworkers and have been close friends for 15 years.

**Making pasta in Rome between Claude Code sessions.**

Throughout 2025, we developed an ever-increasing obsession with coding agents. 

By late 2025, we committed to bringing the power of coding agents to Daniel’s growth world in the form of agentic video editing. Our original YC pitch was “Claude Code for Video Editing”. 

In our first 75 days since launch, we’ve onboarded more than 50 brands and agencies. Many of them make tens or hundreds of videos per week for ads or organic social. What we discovered while building for them: **75% of their video work happens outside of the NLE.**

Our clients prefer to finish their videos in Adobe Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve. They don’t want an “agentic NLE”. And they don’t spend a lot of time using the NLE in the first place. 

They spend 4-5x more time each week doing research, writing briefs, searching for videos, scrubbing _within_ videos, organizing sequences of a-roll and b-roll, producing rough drafts, getting feedback from their team and/or clients, and now generating content to augment their footage as well.

We’re delivering real value within these workflows and our ambition is to become the default workspace where people and agents can collaborate to get the most out of their video.

**How Wideframe Works**

**Wideframe is an AI agent that speeds up the 75% of video work that happens outside the Non-Linear Editor. It helps teams save time and do more with their videos.**

Users link their video libraries to Wideframe and then work with our context-aware agent to tackle search, editing, and generative tasks. Our high-performance app lives on the machine and works seamlessly across the filesystem and can even natively handle Adobe Premiere Pro (.prproj) files in and out.

Wideframe is a coding style agent that lives on your machine with built-in tools and skills for frame-accurate video understanding, agentic search, sequencing, storytelling, research, and context-aware image, video, and audio generation.
