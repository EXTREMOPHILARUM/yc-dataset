Enterprise teams are stuck **manually reading, copy-pasting and tab-switching** for hours every week. RamAIn kills the busywork so your team can get back to the real work.

RamAIn agents simulate mouse and keyboard clicks to use your team’s computers just like a human to move data between your **legacy systems, desktop applications and web portals.** It dominates complex data entry and extraction tasks through UI-interaction on which traditional RPA breaks.

RamAIn is **self-healing** and great at handling unexpected pop-ups or UI changes by using AI to solve for deviations it hasn’t seen before. It takes just a **few days to train and deploy** across your team’s applications and maintains a full audit-trail of decisions.

Unlike current computer-use AI agents that have to think about every action while executing, **RamAIn learns UI-policies and interface structures** from your key interfaces that allows it to make decisions 10x faster while being super reliable.
