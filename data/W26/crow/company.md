# Crow

> Let users control your app through chat

Crow lets users control your app through chat. Connect Crow's AI agent to your product, and users can type what they want instead of clicking through menus.

Crow connects to your APIs and data sources, navigates your UI, and executes real actions. Set guardrails, define workflows, track every interaction, and deploy in under a week—no rebuilding required.
