Every company is affected by regulation. Most don't find out until it's too late.

Your options today: 

* Pay $30K/month for policy consultants or lobbyists to stop threats
* Get blindsided

Even the companies that pay still get burned. We watched one of our bills die in Nevada because a last-minute committee change happened and the consultants we were paying missed it.

Public policy teams spend most of their time on research when they should be spending it on the relationships that actually move legislation.

**What Fed10 does**

Our AI agents handle the research. Every regulation, every jurisdiction, monitored in real time. When something threatens your business, we flag it, draft the fix, and pair you with the right person to get it handled.

One user had a bill that would have cost them $10M/yr in compliance. We caught it, drafted the amendment, and delivered a full advocacy strategy. That took seconds, not the weeks a consultant would need.
