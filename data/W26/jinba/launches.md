## Jinba - Workflow Builder for Enterprise AI Automation

### **TL;DR**

Jinba lets enterprises build AI workflows just by chatting. Unlike traditional no-code tools, you don't need to struggle with a seemingly friendly drag-and-drop interface. Think Lovable meets n8n, but built for the enterprise. We now serve 40,000 users across Fortune Global 500 companies in banking, insurance, and other highly regulated industries.

### <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RBi9OtRGDpk>

**Why this matters**

Enterprises want AI automation but can't make it happen. They spend months building workflows that never make it to production. Internal engineering resources are stretched thin, consultants are expensive, and when something finally gets built, it stalls at deployment because it can't meet security, compliance, or scalability requirements.

Jinba fixes this. Business users go from idea to production-ready AI workflows just by chatting, with enterprise-grade security and compliance built in from day one.
