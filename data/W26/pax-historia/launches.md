# Launches

## Pax Historia - User & AI powered gaming platform

Hey! This is **Eli** and **Ryan**, cofounders at **Pax Historia** ([paxhistoria.co](http://paxhistoria.co)). 

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97520&key=user_uploads/1382898/f7de4ff4-c0d2-4a10-9785-d2ca30e30949)

Pax Historia is a map-based gaming platform which combines the creative freedom of sandbox games with the depth of grand strategy. Players imagine scenarios (like ‘What if the USSR hadn’t broken apart?’ or ‘What if a zombie outbreak started in 2019?’ or ‘What if the USA took over Greenland?’) and create maps and presets which are then published to the community and become playable by anyone.

Already, we have **35k daily users**. Over the last week, in total, we’ve processed **100+ billion tokens**. Our **passionate community has published over 4k presets** with 50+ plays each.  

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97520&key=user_uploads/1382898/099acb66-577a-4be7-973c-109c16087c69)

Creators create, edit, and publish custom maps and worlds. Users then play as a country or group in the world. They can make actions, start diplomatic chats, and jump forward in time. As the game progresses, the map shows any changes from player actions. 

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97520&key=user_uploads/1382898/062a68cf-5178-40bf-888d-e31b681431c2)

By using AI to power the responses of other countries/actors to in-game actions, **we are able to move beyond constraining, pre-designed narrative paths**. Players are truly able to do whatever they can imagine. 

Check out our [Pax Historia Launch Video](https://youtu.be/-RXSxMVLcL4) for a closer look.
