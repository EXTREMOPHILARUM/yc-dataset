**THE PROBLEM** ❌

Operations work today is fragmented across dozens of tools. Updating CRMs after calls, generating reports, triaging customer issues, and coordinating workflows all require constant context switching and manual effort.

Automating these processes is even harder. Existing tools are brittle, require engineering time, or live outside where teams actually work. As a result, most ops teams still rely on manual processes, even for repetitive, predictable tasks.

This slows teams down and makes it harder to scale operations without scaling headcount.

**OUR SOLUTION** 👷

Demo: <https://youtu.be/BIvOZCGgRSA>

Bubble Lab lets you deploy Pearl, your ops super-employee, directly into Slack in under a minute.

We’re already powering 4,000+ operators and fast-moving teams.

Pearl connects securely to your tools— Notion, Jira, HubSpot, Stripe, Google Workspace, and more—and can take action on your behalf. Teams use Pearl to:

* Update HubSpot automatically after sales calls
* Create Jira tickets from user feedback
* Generate daily pipeline and revenue reports
* Track and follow up on open invoices
* Run operational workflows triggered directly from Slack

When something needs to be repeated, Pearl turns it into a reliable workflow your team can run anytime.

Instead of switching between tools or building custom automation infrastructure, teams can run and manage operations entirely from Slack.
