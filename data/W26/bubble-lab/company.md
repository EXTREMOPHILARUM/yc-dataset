# Bubble Lab

> Your ops super-employee in Slack

Bubble Lab (YC W’26) lets you deploy Pearl, your ops super-employee, directly into Slack.

Pearl connects to your tools like Notion, Jira, HubSpot, Stripe, and Google Workspace, and can take action on your behalf—updating systems, running reports, and executing operational workflows automatically.

Operations teams use Bubble Lab to eliminate manual work, reduce context switching, and scale their operations without scaling headcount.

Already trusted by thousands of operators across BizOps, RevOps, and fast-growing teams.
