# Launches

## Sila: Agentic Workspace Messaging

**_Sila is an agentic workplace messaging & collaboration platform where teams and AI work as one._**\
\
Every great company in history was built on one thing: communication. Sila is an agentic workspace messaging & collaboration platform for teams and companies. Our mission is to lead human-AI communication as the standard for work.

# Problem

The communication tools we use today (Slack, Microsoft Teams, etc) were built over a decade ago for a world that no longer exists. Full of "fake work" – endless messages, notifications, context switching, AI slapped on, and pings that feel productive but never move things forward. \
\
Nearly 60% of knowledge workers are overwhelmed by their communication tools, and AI is making this worse as work speeds up and the volume of communication explodes. The gap between how teams talk and how work actually gets done has never been wider.

# Solution

We rebuilt messaging from the ground up for human-AI collaboration.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=98386&key=user_uploads/2559833/147e96a6-3968-49c7-baee-173a44c2b72d)

On Sila, custom Claude agents live in your team's channels – talking, listening, and executing alongside naturally. They share updates in real time and automatically evolve based on your workspace conversations, tools, and apps.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=98386&key=user_uploads/2559833/62b4297e-d404-468b-88e9-11aec0b6c6ba)

Every AI session within your workspace is shared across your team in a Google Docs-like experience, so anyone can start, delegate, and collaborate together in real time.
