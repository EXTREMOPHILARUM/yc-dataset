## Robbie Bourke
**Founder**

Robbie is the co-founder of Zymbly. 

At Zymbly, he is building AI Agents to automate admin and troubleshooting for maintenance technicians.

Robbie has spent 25 years in aviation as an executive, operator, engineering, investor and advisor. He is a qualified Aeronautical Engineer who led design teams at Airbus, was Head of Aircraft Maintenance at Virgin Atlantic, and a Partner in Oliver Wyman Aviation & Aerospace practice.
