# Travo

> We use AI to collect and analyze real estate data

We are building the most accurate and comprehensive real estate data set for RV parks and similar niche asset classes.

Travo is the one stop shop for anyone working in real estate to search any property or parcel of land and instantly access rental comps, ownership, zoning and financials. With our data, we build the best informed AI applications for real estate private equity firms, developers, and brokers.
