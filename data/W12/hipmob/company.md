# Hipmob

> Customer communication for iOS and Android developers - an in-app…

Hipmob brings easy to use live chat, helpdesk, feedback and customer engagement tools to mobile and tablet businesses. Whether you're a mobile-native startup, a small business going mobile, or a large public company with a growing mobile business, we give you the tools to increase sales, transform customer support, and make your customers happy. 

With real time contextual information about your customers including device, app, OS, geographic info and more, you'll be able to make take actions that improve your bottomline. Our partnerships and integrations (see more at www.hipmob.com/documentation) mean that we'll fit right into your team's habits.

AND, we're insanely responsive to customer feedback - email us with questions at ayo@hipmob.com!
