# FamilyLeaf

> Help families stay in touch

FamilyLeaf is a private place to share your life with your family.

You create a private page where your family can easily share photos, share life updates, store contact information, and post messages.

Our mission is to bring families around the world closer together. Family is the most irreplaceable but overlooked social network; we want to help you share the little things that build a lifetime of love!
