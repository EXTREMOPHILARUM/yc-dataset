# Tsenta

> AI-Native, On-Device Job Automation

Tsenta is a desktop app that matches you with jobs, optimizes your profile & applies on your behalf. It runs on your device, so you have granular control over every application being submitted in real-time.

We built Tsenta after applying to over 3,000 jobs manually, so that nobody else would have to.

Check us out at https://tsenta.com :)
