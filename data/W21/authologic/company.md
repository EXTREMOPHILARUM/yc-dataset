# Authologic

> Stripe for online identity verification

Authologic is an orchestration and aggregation layer for local KYC & AML methods. We allow businesses to check the identity of their clients online using video/biometrics, banking data, and government issued digital IDs. Our API  allows a flexible definition of verification scenarios based on the risk profile of the client or risk involved in the given process.
