# Parsagon

> AI to track and analyze public policy

Parsagon is an AI that can collect and analyze publications from any government body in any jurisdiction. It currently parses and analyzes legislation, press releases, speeches, meeting minutes, executive orders, and much more in 64 countries and counting.

Fortune/Global 500 companies, policy firms, and other organizations are using Parsagon to uncover policy insights in hundreds of jurisdictions that they would otherwise miss. Customers use us to:

- Analyze changes in US state education policy
- Generate reports on UK financial regulations
- Monitor vaccine policies across the EU
- And much more!
