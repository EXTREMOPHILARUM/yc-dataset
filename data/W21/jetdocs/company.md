# Jetdocs

> Collaborative ticketing that integrates into Teams and Slack

Jetdocs is a collaborative ticketing solution that is integrated into Microsoft Teams and Slack. We allow teams to create no-code workflows that digitize your processes and connect your teams.

We empower IT and Ops teams with the interface, as well as the ability to build out workflows to handle internal tickets, requests and approvals. We eliminate the problems of bloated email chains, group inboxes and long messaging threads.
