# Founders

## Omar Ekram
**Founder**

Omar Ekram is the Founder of Dayra. Ekram was a Managing Partner at Tanmiya Capital Ventures, a private equity firm focused on the Egyptian SME market
Ekram was an IBanker with EFG Hermes where he advised on notable transactions with values in excess of USD 1 bn
Ekram earned a BA in Economics and Finance, with minors in Computer Science and Mathematics, from the American University in Cairo and Columbia University in the City of New York; he is also a Chartered Financial Analyst
