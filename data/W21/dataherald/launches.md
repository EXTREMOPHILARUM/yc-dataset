## Dataherald - NL-to-SQL API for developers

**TL;DR**

Dataherald enables you to embed Natural Language to SQL (“NL-to-SQL”) into your product. The NL-to-SQL API, built with an LLM (such as GPT), allows users to conduct no-code data analytics, pull reports, and conduct complex data queries using just natural language.

Hi, we’re [Amir](https://www.linkedin.com/in/amir-zohrenejad-72a517184/) and [Anuj](https://www.linkedin.com/in/anujsaigal/) from [Dataherald](https://www.dataherald.com/contact). There have been countless game-changing applications of AI, but one area that has remained uncharted is NL-to-SQL. But fear not - we’re changing that. We’ve built a cutting-edge NL-to-SQL engine, meticulously crafted to be the fastest and most accurate in the market, delivering an unmatched developer experience!

**The Problem**

Developers struggle to build NL-to-SQL into products because LLMs do not work out-of-the-box. LLMs do not:

1. Utilize metadata and business definitions since they are not already stored in the relational database
2. Conduct **complex** SQL queries, such as self joins, multiple sub-queries, or rarer operations like windowing, partitioning, lags, temporal calculations, etc.
3. Create NL-to-SQL training dataset(s) for fine-tuned models
4. Assess the accuracy of AI-generated SQL

**The Solution**

Dataherald’s API solves these challenges by allowing developers to:

* Add business context from various sources (data dictionaries, DBT, Confluence docs, Google docs, etc.)

* Identify relevant data tables
* Fine-tune NL-to-SQL LLMs **(including GPT-4!)** specific to their data
* Assess confidence levels in AI-generated SQL

All this happens with a few simple API calls.

## 🚀 Dataherald - Query your structured database in natural language

### The TL;DR

Rather than waiting hours or days, business users get answers to complex data questions in seconds – all in plain English. Dataherald’s AI-powered natural language to SQL agent gives you answers, fast. It’s like having a data analyst on standby 24/7.

👋 Howdy! - we’re [Amir](https://www.linkedin.com/in/amir-zohrenejad-72a517184/) and [Anuj](https://www.linkedin.com/in/anujsaigal/); we’re building [Dataherald](https://dataherald.com/). We’re on a mission to enable anyone to get answers to their data questions immediately. No longer do you have to click through dozens of BI dashboards or “wait on the data team” to get the answers you need.

### The Problem

With an ever-increasing volume of data, businesses have invested heavily in BI tools with the aim of providing answers to data questions. These tools often fail to handle complex data queries, leading to a backlog of **ad hoc questions** for the data team, inevitably creating a bottleneck.

**The Data BI “Doom Loop” —** Dashboards inevitably fall short in answering certain data questions, leaving people waiting.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=73999&key=user_uploads/307611/9c21d382-337a-4bcf-9d06-27be4cd90a5f)

### The Solution

Business users want answers to their data questions in the most natural way possible - by simply posing a question in plain language and receiving a response in the same natural language format.

Dataherald cuts through the noise, providing quick answers to complex data questions, all in plain English. Users Slack their queries to our bot and receive instant responses.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=73999&key=user_uploads/307611/9daf452e-4d9e-44a3-b41b-6594bea0391d)

Our natural language-to-SQL agent, supported by an LLM, comprehends questions, identifies relevant data tables, writes SQL queries, and assesses confidence levels in responses. If confidence is low, a member of your data team can review and modify the SQL. Best of all, Dataherald's algorithm continuously improves, delivering more accurate insights over time.
