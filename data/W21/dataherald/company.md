# Dataherald

> Embed NL-to-SQL into your Product 📈

Dataherald enables you to embed Natural Language to SQL (“NL-to-SQL” or "Text-to-SQL") into your product. The NL-to-SQL API, built with an LLM, allows your users to conduct no-code data analytics, pull reports, get answers to data questions, and conduct complex data queries using just natural language.
