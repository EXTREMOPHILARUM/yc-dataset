# Taste

> Online marketplace for dinners shipped from great restaurants

Taste is an online marketplace where you can order ready-to-eat dinners from great restaurants, shipped nationwide. The next time you want to have a nice dinner at home, instead of cooking for hours or getting low quality take out, you can order a nice multi-course dinner from Taste to enjoy with friends or loved ones.
