[**Rethoric**](https://rethoric.com/) is not an AI tool that writes content for you. **World-class human writers and editors create all the content** with your own voice based on your insights, lessons, experiences, and stories, which we extract on interview calls.

* Step 1: **Join an Interview Call Twice a Month,** during which we extract your expertise into optimized social-media-ready content.
* Step 2: **Approve Twice a Month** - We send you 2-week content batches for review with unlimited revisions.
* Step 3: **Engage Online** - We coach you to reply and start engaging in conversations in your comments.
