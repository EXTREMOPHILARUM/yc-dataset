# Segments.ai

> Build better computer vision models by building better datasets

[Segments.ai](http://segments.ai/) is helping robotics and automotive companies label their multi-sensor data for AI training and validation. Our platform enables customers to efficiently annotate their point cloud and image data, accelerating their path to autonomy.

We're a fast-growing, remote-first YC startup with a lean team and healthy runway. [Segments.ai](http://segments.ai/) is used by large organizations as well as innovative startups building the next generation of autonomous drones, delivery robots, self-driving cars, and more.
