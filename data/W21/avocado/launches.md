## **🧠 The problem: running a restaurant is overwhelming**

Independent restaurant owners juggle dozens of tools, dozens of vendors, and constant interruptions - all while trying to serve food and keep customers happy. Managing the POS, updating menus, pulling sales reports, figuring out Instagram posts, dealing with changing prices… it’s a full-time job _on top of_ their actual full-time job.

But the real bottleneck? **Information is trapped in systems that don’t talk to each other**, and most POS systems are clunky, hard to use, or just dumb.

---

## **🍳 The solution: an AI agent that _knows_ your restaurant**

Avocado is a POS system designed from the ground up to work with an AI assistant. Our agent understands your menu, your modifiers, your sales data, your customers, your staff - and it can take action across the system in real time.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=91825&key=user_uploads/258127/4f4d0889-0077-48bd-a427-72d160f30492)

You can ask it:

* “Add a matcha latte to the menu for $5.50”
* “What’s my best-selling item on weekends?”
* “Update all egg-based items to reflect the new cost of eggs”
* “Create a social media post featuring today’s specials”
* “Tell me which servers had the highest tips last week”

…and it just **does it**. Right away. No UI to navigate, no learning curve, no training required.

💡 This is more than a chatbot. This is a **restaurant-native AI agent** with full control over the POS, KDS, kiosk, and online ordering systems - like having a superpowered GM in your pocket.

**👩‍🍳 Why we built Avocado**

We’ve worked with restaurants for years and have seen the same story over and over: people want to focus on hospitality and food - not software. But they were stuck with outdated POS systems or janky integrations between too many tools.

So we built Avocado to be the _last_ system a restaurant needs - tightly integrated hardware + software, a beautiful POS UI, and now, a deeply capable AI agent.\
(Also, we were frustrated that nobody else had done it right. So we did.)
