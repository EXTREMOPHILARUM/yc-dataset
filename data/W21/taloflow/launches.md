## Taloflow 2.0 - Evaluate tech vendors 10x faster

# **Say goodbye to soul-sucking tech vendor evaluations.**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=81316&key=user_uploads/429966/3d1eaab4-19dd-4a32-8d03-d24ef138f7c4)

### **Why**

We built [Taloflow](https://www.taloflow.ai) to solve technology and software vendor selection–CRM, ERP, HRIS, Internal Developer Portals, Object Storage, API Gateways, etc. Having been deeply involved in the tech scene, I saw firsthand how a wrong or slow decision could make or break any project or company. The endless options, opaque pricing, ambiguous features, and subjective and biased reviews make evaluating vendors challenging.

**“Wait, but isn’t this solved?”** you may ask. Nope. Gartner (if you can afford the subscription), G2, and the like offer mostly generic insights. You’re on your own to determine exactly the solution that’s most relevant to you.

### **What**

Since our last launch, we’ve done extensive research into the needs of enterprise customers. This new platform is designed to be collaborative from the ground up, delivers clear, data-driven recommendations tailored to specific use cases, and comes with all the CYA you could imagine. We’ve also leaned in on AI to allow our analysts to assess, curate, and refresh vendor data much faster and in more categories for our customers.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=81316&key=user_uploads/429966/fb671e0a-75df-4a8f-ae65-89332a95374c)

**Key Features**

1. Use case questionnaire to pinpoint your exact needs.
2. AI and expert-assessed feature ratings for solutions.
3. Real-time feedback based on your changing inputs.
4. Over 100,000 AI and expert-curated data points.
5. Real-time collaboration (Notion or Google Doc-like UX) and audit logs.
6. Tailored reports based on detailed user inputs and needs.
