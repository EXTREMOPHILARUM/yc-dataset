# Gigs Live

> Video publishing and monetization platform for creators and artists

We are the Substack for millions of artists, musicians, indie producers, extreme sports athletes and video production companies. With us, creators have the freedom to choose flexible monetization plans such as PPV, micro-subscriptions, merch drops and many more.   Creators choose us because our platform is available in 4K on Apple TV, Roku, Amazon Fire, Gaming consoles and more. Creators also get audience analytics and marketing to retain and acquire their audience. So take care of marketing, distribution and monetization for creators so they can focus on their craft and we take care of the rest.
