# Spokn

> AI-powered video platform for Internal Comms and HR teams 

Spokn makes it easy for People and Comms teams to make videos starring their own employees and leaders - no editing skills or special equipment necessary.  In minutes, you can create videos that feel as engaging as TikTok, and share them anywhere: Slack/Teams, your LMS, intranet, careers page, or job offer emails.  

Companies like InterContinental Hotels, Coinbase, Twilio, Opendoor, Udemy, SiriusXM, Gong and more use Spokn to create engaging videos for their learning & development, internal comms, recognition, DEI and employer branding.
