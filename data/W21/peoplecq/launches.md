# Launches

## peopleCQ - Build your hiring infrastructure in minutes

Hey there,

I'm thrilled to introduce [peopleCQ](https://www.peoplecq.com/)! \
\
We recognized how Gen-AI could help with every facet of the hiring process, so we built a solution to do just that. We also understand how painful it was for us to set up our original hiring infrastructure in the early days. Our product is designed to streamline and accelerate every aspect of recruitment.

Watch our [YouTube Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xlZh6QjPRhY)

### With peopleCQ, you can:

* Launch your own hiring portal and post job openings in seconds.
* Generate job descriptions from minimal input using AI and customize them to fit your needs.
* Automatically analyze every aspect of incoming resumes with our co-pilot AI.
* Get tailored interview questions based on the candidate’s resume and the job description. peopleCQ also highlights candidate strengths and weaknesses to help you probe deeper during interviews.
* Compare candidates based on job-relevant attributes.
* Use generative AI to search through received applications.
* Tailor your recruitment process to meet your specific needs via a configurable pipeline

We believe peopleCQ can empower both companies and candidates by enhancing how you evaluate each candidate.

Would love to hear your feedback. Thanks!
