# Launches

## Friz - AI social media manager

Friz is an AI social media manager that helps DTC brands automate brainstorming, creating, editing and scheduling content and ads.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=77520&key=user_uploads/576527/c61426a8-205d-4ef8-a6da-32408fd9b912)

👨‍💻 Receive contextual posts and ads for your target audience and industry

💡 Go from prompt to post with visuals and captions in your brand style

🚀 Manage your entire calendar across various social media accounts from 1 place

Our mission is to save your marketing teams time and money while growing conversions and revenue ([www.usefriz.com](http://www.usefriz.com)).\
Support us on Product Hunt: <https://www.producthunt.com/posts/friz-2>
