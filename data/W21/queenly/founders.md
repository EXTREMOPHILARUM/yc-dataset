# Founders

## Trisha Bantigue
**Founder**

Trisha Bantigue is the CEO and co-founder of Queenly, the leading online marketplace for the formalwear industry. She was recently featured on the Forbes 30 Under 30 2022 list and she was the cover for the Art & Style category. Prior to founding Queenly, she has worked at some of the biggest tech companies like Google, Facebook and Uber. She found her initial inspiration for starting Queenly from seeing so many women struggle to find and afford their dream dress and experiencing it herself.
