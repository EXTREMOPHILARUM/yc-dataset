# Reshape Biotech

> Robots that automate the everyday tasks of microbiologists.

Reshape Biotech builds lab robots that automate the everyday tasks of microbiologists, from filling plates to analyzing images.
Other companies try to sell “one lab robot that does everything”.  But those robots become so complicated that they sit unused on the shelf.  We build one robot for each function, which makes them easy to use.
