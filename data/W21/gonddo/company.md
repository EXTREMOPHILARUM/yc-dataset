# Gonddo

> Wholesale marketplace for independent retailers buy & manage inventory

Gonddo is a B2B wholesale marketplace that digitalizes inventories purchases for independent retailers and brings technology to modernise distribution and trade credit for SMBs in Brazil. With gonddo, retailers can find fresh products, discover new brands and easily connect with wholesalers
