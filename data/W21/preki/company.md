# Preki

> Shopify for LatAm

Preki creates easy to use e-commerce solutions for local businesses in LatAm, you can think of us as Shopify for LatAm.

Setting up an online business in LatAm is expensive, hard to learn and time consuming.

With us, all businesses have to do is follow an onboarding process and add products - no code or design involved. This process takes less than 30 seconds and users can start selling immediately.
