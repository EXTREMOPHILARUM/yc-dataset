# Quicknode

> Tools & infra to build and scale high-quality blockchain applications.

Quicknode is an enterprise-grade blockchain development platform that helps teams build and scale high-performance onchain applications. Developers get fast and reliable access to over 80 chains, real-time and historical data, automation tools, and a unified suite of APIs. From startups to global enterprises, teams choose Quicknode for consistent performance, predictable pricing, and a platform built for growth.
