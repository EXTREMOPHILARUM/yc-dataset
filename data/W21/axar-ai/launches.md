# **🤯 The Problem:** Execs work 14+ hours a day, but 70% of it is spent in reactive _manager mode_.

Founders and execs are often trapped in reactive mode without a clear view of where their time really goes. Between firefighting urgent issues and back-to-back meetings, strategic focus slips. With packed schedules, they’re stuck operating in reactive _manager mode_, leaving no space for proactive leadership and pursuing bold bets. As strategic priorities get sidelined, it becomes harder to focus on what really drives the company forward.

The result? A leadership team too busy being busy, running on autopilot with little time left for high-impact work—or even for themselves.

# **🧠 Our Solution:** Automated time audits that keep executives in _founder mode_.

Tackle AI turns your calendar into a strategic asset by automatically tracking and analyzing your activities. It delivers actionable insights into where your time and energy go, keeping your day-to-day in sync with big-picture goals.
