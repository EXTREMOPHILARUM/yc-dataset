## Odin - Attack surface monitoring and internet scanning for everyone

We're excited to share that we launched [Odin](https://getodin.com), our new attack surface monitoring capability!🎉 It's available to try out now at [**getodin.com**](http://getodin.com).

**About**: With its ability to scan the entire IPv4 space, Odin delivers unprecedented visibility into your external attack surface. 🕵️‍♂️

**Key benefits:**

✔️ Catalogs over 100+ internet-facing services to reveal your complete digital footprint

✔️ Builds a comprehensive inventory of hosts, domains, subdomains, certificates, images and more

✔️ Enables easy discovery of unknown assets, misconfigurations, risks

✔️ Provides continuous monitoring for any changes

✔️ Identifies vulnerabilities before attackers can exploit them ⚠️

By leveraging Odin's unmatched coverage, organizations gain total visibility and control over their attack surface. 💪
