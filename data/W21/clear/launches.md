# Launches

## Clear: Track and Share your Skincare

🚀 **We just launched Clear Premium: the smarter way to track your skincare, discover what actually works, and feel confident in your skin.**

👋 Hi everyone, I’m Ahana, founder of [Clear](https://getclearapp.com/), a skincare app helping tens of thousands of people take control of their skin through tracking, education, and community. We’re YC W21, and today, we’re launching something big: **Clear Premium**.

### **💡 The Problem**

Skincare is broken. People spend hundreds on products, but don’t know what’s working, what’s causing breakouts, or how to personalise their routine. Skin is complex, and most advice is vague, biased, or not designed for people of colour.

As someone who struggled with acne for years, I found myself wishing there was a way to _track_ my skin, get _honest_ recommendations, and see _real_ results over time.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=91922&key=user_uploads/543078/1890c5c9-36d4-4580-af64-7fdee16b3144)

### **🧪 What We’ve Built**

**Clear** is the app I wish I had when I was struggling.

With Clear, you can:

* Log your routine + products
* Take progress photos
* Get ingredient breakdowns
* Learn from a diverse, supportive skincare community

And now, with **Clear Premium**, we’re giving people even more power:

🔓 **Premium Features:**

* Track unlimited progress photos
* Smart routine analytics: see what’s really helping (or hurting) your skin
* Compare products and ingredients side-by-side
* Scan products for instant info
* Export your routine and skin journal
* More customisation, better insights, and no guesswork

### **🛠️ Why We Built It**

For the past 3 years, we’ve grown Clear with very little marketing - just word-of-mouth from a passionate, underserved community. We’ve been used by 50K+ people across 100+ countries, featured on the App Store, and recently partnered with top dermatologists to help people get clinical guidance too.

This launch is a huge milestone for us. It’s our next chapter - one that gives our community more tools, more control, and a way to stop wasting time and money on trial and error.
