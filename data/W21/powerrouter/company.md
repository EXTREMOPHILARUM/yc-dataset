# PowerRouter

> PowerRouter helps sales teams with lead routing in Salesforce

PowerRouter helps sales teams assign leads and contacts to the right reps, instantly within Salesforce.  We provide a powerful automation engine with a drag and drop canvas to create routing rules, visually.  With actionable metrics to increase speed-to-lead and account based sales support, we make it very simple to stay on top of every lead in your Salesforce CRM.
