# Launches

## AI Blaze - Your personal AI co-pilot

Install AI Blaze [here](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/ai-blaze-instantly-use-ai/cebmnlammjhancocbbnfcglifgdpfejc). It’s free!

Here are a few things you can do with AI Blaze (very partial list:)

1. Quickly write an excellent cover letter for a specific role.
2. Turn your messy notes into a professional-looking and actionable summary.
3. Summarize the key points in an article.
4. Write witty comments on social threads.
5. Translate your email to any language.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=83737&key=user_uploads/116722/c2c9846e-f5d2-431f-820c-c8e45efed8ea)

AI Blaze allows you to:

1. Create personal AI prompt templates and use them anywhere.
2. Automatically include the full content of the page in your prompt.
3. Write new text or improve the written text.
