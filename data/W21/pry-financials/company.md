# Pry Financials

> Finance for Founders

Pry makes it simple for companies to manage their budget, hiring plan, financial models, and cash runway.

Today, most people plan for their business once a quarter. It's done in Excel - buggy, boring, confusing, and really time-expensive. A lot of people have tried to solve this and only found moderate success or failed.

We're the team to do it right.
