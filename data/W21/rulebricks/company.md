# Rulebricks

> Add powerful decision-making capabilities to any automated workflow.

Many workflow builders and automation tools have support for adding logic to your processes, but get complicated quick– to the point where process owners are unable to edit them directly and require specialist services.

Rulebricks separates decision-making logic from your workflows, giving your team a clear visual, spreadsheet-like interface to manage the changing rules that drive your business, and use those rules as API endpoints, visual forms, and in automation tools like Zapier.
