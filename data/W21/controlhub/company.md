# ControlHub

> Purchasing Software | Procure-to-Pay SaaS 

ControlHub automates the Procurement and Accounts Payable process of mid-size companies with intuitive purchasing software and AP solutions.

Key features: Purchase Requests generation, Purchasing Approvals, Purchase Orders (PO), Bill processing and payments, sync with ERPs such as Quickbooks and NetSuite.
