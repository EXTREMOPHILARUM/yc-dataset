# Launcher Labs

> Maker of consumer crypto apps: Launchcaster, Frameboard, Maindrop

Launcher Labs made consumer crypto apps, including Launchcaster (acq by Orange DAO), a leading community for launching and discovering crypto projects; Frameboard, a visual curation app and protocol; Launcher Actions, a smart contract mini-app platform; and Maindrop, a Venmo for NFTs.
