# Founders

## Patricia Bubner
**Founder**

Patricia Bubner, PhD, is the CEO and founder of Orbillion Bio, a food tech startup pioneering scalable bioprocesses for cultivated meat. With a PhD in Biotechnology, she has led Orbillion from inception to acquisition and has raised over $15M. Her career spans biopharma (Boehringer Ingelheim), battery tech (Sila Nanotechnologies), and global entrepreneurship, fostering strategic partnerships across industries.
