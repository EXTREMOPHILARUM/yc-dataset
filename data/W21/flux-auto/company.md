# Flux Auto

> General Autonomy for Controlled Spaces

Flux Auto is building autonomous mobility technology to fully automate the movement and operation of vehicles in controlled spaces. Using Flux's solutions customers can reduce their dependency on human drivers, while also increasing productivity and reducing operating costs. 

Flux is currently deploying in warehouses, farms and mines.
