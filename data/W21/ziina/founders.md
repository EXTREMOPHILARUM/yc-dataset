## Andrew Gold
**Founder**

Andrew began his career as an engineer on the iOS Safari team at Apple. He left Apple to join an early stage startup, Distributed Systems, which was later acquired by Coinbase. At Coinbase, Andrew helped build the Coinbase Wallet app, a product which enables users all over the world to store their own crypto and access the decentralized web. Inspired by the mission of expanding economic freedom, Andrew relocated from San Francisco to Dubai to found Ziina.
