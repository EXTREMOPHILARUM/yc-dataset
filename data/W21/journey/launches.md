# **What is it, specifically**

Journeys are webpages that have the structure of slides:

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=67033&key=user_uploads/19524/dd50c772-2e01-42cd-8bfa-7359735401d0)

On the left you have the steps of your pitch, and then each step can be all sorts of content: Slides, Videos, Onboarding Checklists, Calendars, or [anything else](https://www.journey.io/integrations).
