# Launches

## Moonvalley.ai - Generate cinematic videos with AI

**TL;DR**

Hey YC - thrilled to introduce you to Moonvalley ([moonvalley.ai](https://moonvalley.ai/)), the most powerful text-to-video AI model ever built.

**What is it?**

Y’all remember the holodeck in Star Trek? We’re on a mission to build it.

If you’re familiar with Stable Diffusion, Dall-E or Midjourney, we’re the video counterpunch to them. We’re proud to unveil the fruit of many months of blood, sweat, tears and GPUs - the most powerful AI video generation engine ever built.

With Moonvalley, you can:

* Generate HD, 16:9 cinematic film quality footage from text prompts
* Choose from several different styles, from hyperrealism, to anime, to fantasy, & more
* Prompt engineer and compose clips to your liking

In Beta, Moonvalley is completely free, so hop on over to our Discord and join us: <https://discord.gg/moonvalley>

**Our Journey**

You may know us from [draft.co](http://draft.co/). We’ve spent years in generative AI for content creation (primarily text and graphics).

Once we saw text-to-video progress in the market wasn’t happening nearly fast enough, we threw all of our time and resources into building a world class AI team and going after the problem ourselves.
