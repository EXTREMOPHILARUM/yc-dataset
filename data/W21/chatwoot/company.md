# Chatwoot

> The modern, open source, self-hosted AI customer support platform

Chatwoot is an AI-powered customer support platform that enables businesses to deliver exceptional support experiences. Built as a modern, open-source, and self-hosted alternative to tools like Intercom and Zendesk, Chatwoot brings all your customer conversations into one place. You can connect email, website live chat, social media accounts, WhatsApp, and more, making it easy to manage every interaction from a single, unified platform.
