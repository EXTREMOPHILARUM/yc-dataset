## Chris Tara-Browne
**Founder**

Chris grew up in Sebastopol, CA, and graduated from San Francisco State University in 1992.  After 6 years of managing music artists in SF, he moved to Novosibirsk, Siberia on a Baha’i community-building project.  He was a co-founder and CEO of Traveler’s Coffee, establishing over 100 coffeehouses in six countries. Currently, he's President and co-founder of Unfurl (San Mateo, CA) and the vice-chairman of the board of directors for Bauman College & Bauman Wellness (Berkeley, CA).
