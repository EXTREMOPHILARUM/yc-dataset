# Founders

## Jessica Jackley
**Founder**

Entrepreneur and investor best known as a cofounder of Kiva, >$1.5B in loans since its founding. Founder and CEO of Alltruists, providing at-home volunteer activities for families through a monthly subscription. Cofounder and Managing Director of Untapped Capital, instructor of entrepreneurship at USC. Stanford MBA, Bucknell BA, Harvard Kennedy School. Author of CLAY WATER BRICK. Based in LA with husband Reza Aslan and their four awesome kids.
