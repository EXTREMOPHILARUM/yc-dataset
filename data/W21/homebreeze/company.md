# HomeBreeze

> The fastest and easiest way to make home upgrades

HomeBreeze offers the fastest and easiest way to make home upgrades. Our AI system remotely scopes projects based on photos, video, answers to questions, and other data. We send those scopes to our network of contractors and get homeowners quotes within minutes, rather than weeks. We started with energy-efficient upgrades (e.g. heat pumps), as our first stop toward modernizing home management.

As a pioneer in bringing AI to home services, we served thousands of customers across California and delivered an experience that both homeowners and contractors love (our reviews average 4.97 stars!).
