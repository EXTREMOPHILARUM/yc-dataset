# Launches

## Tyran: AI Health Agents for your app

Hey 👋 We are building [Tyran](https://tyran.ai), the AI Health Agents.

**tl;dr:** Build **AI health agents** on top of any data — wearables, labs, sleep, HRV. Detect jet lag, catch illness before symptoms, spot sleep apnea, flag overtraining — and plug it all into your app in minutes, without a line of code.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VXDRR_qU250

## **What you can use Tyran AI for**

Some early examples

* Detect jet lag
* Detect signs of potential asthma (NOT medical diagnosis, obviously!)
* Detect the upcoming menstrual cycle
* Understand recovery
* Run multiple experiments: test different workout timings or rest protocols per user segment.

## **How it works**

1. Sign up, connect health data sources via plug-and-play auth widget.
2. Pick an agent template (or prompt the agent).
3. Define schedule + outcomes (alerts, notifications, adjusted plans).
4. Get validated outputs (typed JSON), export via webhooks, or integrate directly into your product.

## **What’s new**

* 🚀 **Fast feature build** - From idea to working health insights in **minutes**
* 🔗 **Connect any health source** - 500+ metrics from wearables, labs, nutrition, sleep, HRV, etc.
* 📚 **Templates & agent types** - Prebuilt agents for recovery optimization, risk detection, women’s health, root-cause analysis, experimental health logic.
* ⚙️ **Custom logic + actions** - Go beyond dashboards: trigger alerts, notifications, update workout plans, or send recommendations programmatically.
* 🛠 **Full agent lifecycle tools** - scheduling, versioning, monitoring; typed output (JSON) so downstream systems / UIs stay reliable.
* **📝 Memory -** Agents remembering information and improving over time

## Health Scores for developers

[Health Scores](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=frTfSO39e10)

We are launching today Health Scores: The easiest way for developers to integrate and display scores such as Sleep, Stress, Heart Health, Training Load and many more.

This provides a seamless way to show critical data points such as Sleep, Stress, Heart Health, Training Load, and much more to end users. 

**Launching Today: The Sleep Score**

The first available feature, the Sleep Score, is now live. 

This score analyzes various aspects of a user’s sleep, including REM, Deep, Light, and Awake stages, along with HRV, RHR, respiration, and body temperature, to provide a comprehensive sleep quality assessment.

**Customizable Scoring Model**

As a Developer, you can easily customise this and create proprietary models using a simple drag-and-drop interface, assigning different weights to various metrics to tailor the scores according to specific needs.

### The problem:

Connecting to wearables such as Garmin, Fitbit, Oura, Wahoo, etc is a very long process. You need to deal with partnerships and poorly written docs and create an internal DevOps team.

### What we do

[Terra](https://tryterra.co/) is an **API** that makes it easy for apps to connect to all wearables.

We already have all the [integrations](https://tryterra.co/integrations) you need, such as Apple, Garmin, Peloton, Zwift, Freestyle Libre, Samsung, and the list goes on.

One integration is all that matters 😀
