# Outfit

> DIY renovations in a box. Materials, tools, and instructions delivered

We sell DIY home renovations in a box.

We format our architect-designed templates to a customer's space. We then buy all the materials needed on their behalf, and ship it to their door for them to build.

The experience is accompanied by an app that guides from start-to-completion of the project.
