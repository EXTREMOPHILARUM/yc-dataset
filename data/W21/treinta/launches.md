# **The Problem**

Building software today is **still not user friendly for non-tech people**. That includes non-technical entrepreneurs and small business owners. At Treinta, with **7M+ SMBs across the US and Latin America**, we see this every day.

Even with AI builders, you end up having to integrate tools such as Supabase, or Auth0, and debugging the codebase.

Non-tech entrepreneurs, SMB owners, and independent creators **get stuck before deployment.**

---

# **The Solution**

**idealane turns prompts into full products**; made for non-technical entrepreneurs and small business owners.

You describe what you want, idealane builds it, deploys it, and lets you edit it visually.

No dependencies. Just you and your idea without getting stuck.

---

# **Key Features**

* Full-stack generation: UI, backend, DB, auth, etc.
* 1-credit = 1-prompt pricing. Simple and predictable.
* Conversational editing: change your app by talking to it.
* Consumery UX: designed for clarity and friendliness.
* Automatic hosting & deployment: one click to publish.
