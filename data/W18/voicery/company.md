# Voicery

> Automated voice acting and emotive speech synthesis with deep neural…

Voicery synthesizes the most realistic human voices using deep neural networks. Prior to starting Voicery, Andrew, one of the founders, led the speech synthesis research team at Baidu Research. By synthesizing speech nearly indistinguishable from human, Voicery enable new media applications, such as auto-generated audiobooks, podcasts, television dubs, and voice overs.
