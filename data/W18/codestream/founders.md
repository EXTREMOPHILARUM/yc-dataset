# Founders

## Peter Pezaris
**Founder/CEO**

Peter Pezaris is Vortex's CEO. He served as New Relic’s Chief Strategy and Design Officer. Pezaris was the Founder & CEO of CodeStream, a developer productivity tool. Pezaris was CEO of Glip, a team collaboration platform acquired by RingCentral in 2015, and Multiply.com, a social commerce platform acquired by Naspers in 2010. He founded Commissioner.com, a fantasy sports company acquired by CBS in 1999. Pezaris holds BS degrees in Computer Science and Applied Math from CMU.
