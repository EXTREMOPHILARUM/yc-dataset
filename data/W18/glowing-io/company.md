# Glowing.io

> Unified, enterprise messaging platform for leading brands of the world

Glowing enables leading global brands and their customers  to text message with each other via existing popular mobile messaging platforms. Our solution allows for brands to appeal to millennials (and other digital travelers) as well as to the “silent guests” who prefer messaging as their primary communication channel.
