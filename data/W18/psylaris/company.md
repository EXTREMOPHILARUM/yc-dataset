# Psylaris

> Psychotherapy through autonomous and personalized VR-sessions.

We empower mental healthcare clients with virtual reality sessions that are additive to traditional therapy. Clients regain their self-reliance the moment they are given the chance to contribute to their treatment. Therapists can focus on the aspects of treatment that e-health is not equiped to deal with. Treatment efficiency goes up - costs go down: 
E-health the way it was meant to be.
