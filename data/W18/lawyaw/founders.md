# Founders

## Tucker Cottingham
**Founder/CEO**

Tucker is the founder and CEO of Lawyaw. He is passionate about using technology to increase access to legal services and has worked extensively on criminal justice reform issues. He currently serves on the board of the American Bar Association's Legal Technology Resource Center and prior to Lawyaw, built a successful law firm in San Francisco.
