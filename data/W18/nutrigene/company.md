# Nutrigene

> Personalized liquid supplements based your health data

Our mission is to optimize health and prevent nutrition-related disease, which affects over half of the world’s population. Current supplement solutions are ineffective and inconvenient. Dried pills lack potency while liquid vitamins quickly degrade - neither form gives your body specifically what it needs to perform. 

Nutrigene helps you optimize your health - by sending you pure, personalized liquid supplements. Pick a goal, fill out our metabolic assessment, and we will optimize your supplements based on scientific research to create unique formulations are 10x more effective than generic off the shelf pill-based formulations.
