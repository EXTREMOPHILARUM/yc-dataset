# Precious

> AI to curate your baby photos

Precious is the iPhone app that tells the story of your children growing up.

Using Artificial Intelligence, the app automatically finds the best baby pics on your phone, and then creates deliciously cute photo stories for you.

Our vision is to help all families capture the joy of childhood. To do this, we are building the platform for preserving, enjoying & sharing your family's memories.
