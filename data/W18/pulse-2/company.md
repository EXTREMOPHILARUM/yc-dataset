# Pulse

> We're crowdsourced Gartner so IT executives can learn directly from…

Companies like Salesforce, Microsoft, Okta and Dialpad already rely on Pulse to turbocharge their strategy, content and marketing teams on a daily basis and as we build out our products, we are excited about what the future holds.
