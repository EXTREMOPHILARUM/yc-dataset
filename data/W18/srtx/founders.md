# Founders

## Katherine Homuth
**Founder/CEO**

Katherine Homuth is a serial entrepreneur and executive known for leading high-growth companies at the intersection of software, manufacturing, and consumer innovation. She is the Founder of Oomira, building the memory infrastructure layer for AI. Previously, she founded and scaled SRTX, makers of Sheertex ‘unbreakable’ tights, raising nearly $300M and securing global retail partnerships.
