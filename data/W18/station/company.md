# Station

> Your team’s automated knowledge base

Station is the central interface for your team’s knowledge. Thanks to our browser extension teams easily visualize and switch across all apps, documents, notes, messages, and more.

Connect any app to Station, customize your interface and watch how Station creates links between files and highlights the information you need when you need it.
It’s a one click install.
