# Aspire

> All-in-one Finance OS for businesses

Aspire is the all-in-one finance operating system for businesses.

We help companies pay, manage, and earn smarter - by doing more than a bank, bookkeeper, or rewards program could ever do alone.

Our mission is to empower the next generation of entrepreneurs with the financial tools they need to realise their company’s full potential.

We believe that if we can empower business owners to be successful, they will go on to make a meaningful impact to the communities we are all part of.
