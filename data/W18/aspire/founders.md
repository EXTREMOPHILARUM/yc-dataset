# Founders

## Andrea Baronchelli
**Founder/CEO**

Andrea Baronchelli is the Co-founder and CEO of Aspire, the finance stack for global founders. Under his leadership, Aspire grew into a global company serving 50,000+ businesses worldwide.

Before launching Aspire, Andrea was in Lazada’s founding team (exit to Alibaba Group), where he led growth, operations and product across multiple markets, including roles as EVP, CMO, CEO. That experience shaped his conviction that modern founders deserve a financial platform as global as their ambitions.
