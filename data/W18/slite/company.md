# Slite

> Team documentation removing the pain of maintaining and finding info

Slite is an AI-powered knowledge base making it easy to create, manage, and access company information. 

Teams can quickly find what they need by simply asking questions. 
Slite makes it easy to keep your company's knowledge current. 

Over 1,500 companies and 50 000 users leverage Slite as their trusted source of knowledge.
