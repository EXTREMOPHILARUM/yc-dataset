# OurMenu

OurMenu provides an online food ordering service. The company replaces ordering from a waiter with ordering from a website.

OurMenu wants to let its users to order from phone instead of talking to a waiter, without downloading an app. Users can scan a QR code on the table, choose their food, order and pay, and then the food is brought tothem. OurMenu plans to charge restaurants $300 per month for the tech, and will aggregate the menu choice data to sell back to them.
