# Founders

## Andrew Peek
**Founder/CEO**

An entrepreneur and angel investor, Andrew is the founder of Agora Intelligence, makers of Tilt: a way for anyone to build their own index of stocks. Prior to Tilt, he co-founded Delphia: an investment collective where people pooled their data to access to quantitative investment strategies. Delphia built and sold a systematic (US equities) hedge fund and spun out three separate startups, one of which was Tilt. Prior to Delphia, Andrew sold a SaaS startup to Shopify.

## Cliff van der Linden
**Founder/CEO**

Cliff is a computational social science professor and serial entrepreneur, having founded and led numerous companies including non-profit organizations and venture-backed technology start-ups. He is founder and CEO of Vox Pop Labs, a social enterprise that empowers informed decisions through innovative digital products and advanced analytics.
