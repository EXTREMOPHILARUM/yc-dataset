# miru

> Experience any piece of furniture in AR

Miru is an augmented reality app that shows you what any piece of furniture will look like in your home. We use a computer vision pipeline to create an augmented reality experience for furniture from any retailer's website so you can see what the coffee table you love will look like in your home.
