# AviaryAI

> Outbound AI voice agents for the financial services industry

AviaryAI is the industry's first outbound AI service team for banks, credit unions, and insurance companies. In a landscape where only 18% of financial service providers make proactive outbound calls and rely heavily on emails, AviaryAI offers a groundbreaking solution. Powered by a private LLM, our suite of products helps institutions leverage Generative AI while adhering to stringent regulatory and compliance standards.
