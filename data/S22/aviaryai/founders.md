## Blesson Abraham
**Founder**

Blesson Abraham is a two-time founder and is the CEO of Cambio. He started his career at a retail bank as a leadership development associate and went on to lead marketing, sales and helped start their analytics team.  In 2014, he co-founded SavvyIntel, a SaaS analytics solution for banks and credit unions. In 2017, SavvyIntel was acquired by CUNA Mutual Group.
