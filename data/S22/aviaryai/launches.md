# Launches

## AviaryAI - Outbound voice agents for the financial services industry

**TLDR**\
[AviaryAI](https://www.helloaviary.ai) is the industry's first outbound AI service team for banks, credit unions, and insurance companies. In a landscape where only 18% of financial service providers make proactive outbound calls and rely heavily on emails, AviaryAI offers a groundbreaking solution. Powered by a private LLM, our suite of products helps institutions leverage Generative AI while adhering to stringent regulatory and compliance standards.

**The problem**

* **Limited Outbound Calls:** Only 18% of banks, credit unions, and insurance companies make outbound calls, missing crucial engagement opportunities throughout the customer lifecycle.
* **Inefficiency and Missed Opportunities:** Internal employees, often untrained or unmotivated, handle outbound calls which leads to incomplete interactions and lost revenue.
* **Challenges with GenAI Adoption:** Financial institutions hesitate to use general-purpose models like ChatGPT due to their lack of transparency and regulatory compliance concerns.
* **Complex Interactions:** Automating post-call workflows (e.g., opening new accounts, activating cards) is challenging due to the need for multiple systems to work together seamlessly.

**Our Impact**

* **Proven Success Rate:** 26% of our AI-driven calls have resulted in successful customer interactions, significantly boosting engagement and revenue for our clients.
* **High Interaction Rate:** 72% of all answered calls have an interaction with our voice agents beyond a simple “hello”.
