## Ciro - A Prospecting Autopilot for Sales 🚀

**TL;DR:** Ciro’s new Prospecting Autopilot uses AI to build the perfect prospect list — so you don’t have to.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90130&key=user_uploads/503100/6de1c5c1-df62-43ec-8866-ae15b658be32)

After much tinkering, we built the sales tool we always wanted — an AI agent that can build your lead list for you.

### **Here’s how it works:**

* **First, Ciro scans your website** to learn about your Ideal Customer Profile and the products you sell
* **Then, it agentically tests multiple search strategies** (combinations of job titles, geography / headcount filters, industries, vector vs. keyword search over the universe of companies) to determine the best way to surface prospects within a universe of 500m+ profiles
* **Next, it performs the best-performing search**, reviews each profile, and scores them individually to ensure fit with your Ideal Customer Profile
* **Finally, it enriches contacts with verified emails & mobile numbers** from 15+ sources and dedupes/pushes relevant contacts to your CRM in one click

### **Why we built Autopilot**

* Founders & sales reps need a repeatable way to find decision-makers, without taking hours every day
* Contractors and virtual assistants cost $$ and can’t keep pace with shifting ICPs
* Users told us they wanted one button that _thinks_ through the search—and then just does it
* 👉 Our vision is friction-free prospecting from “Who should I target?” to “Meeting booked” — and we think Ciro Autopilot is a big step toward that future

## Ciro - 10x Your Prospecting on LinkedIn Sales Navigator

**TL;DR** — LinkedIn has the best data for B2B sales but **zero automation**. Founders and sales reps waste TONS of time manually searching, qualifying, and engaging leads on the platform.

Ciro makes prospecting on LinkedIn 10x easier:

* **Ciro Autopilot** scans profiles (in Sales Navigator), qualifies them against your ideal customer profile, and builds a ready-to-contact list in minutes
* **Ciro Enrich** instantly reveals phone numbers and emails on profiles — whether you’re on a free or paid LinkedIn account (from our network of 13 high-quality data providers)

[Here’s a video](https://youtu.be/z0dYm46MfsM) of me using Ciro & watching Netflix with Ciro’s CEO, Richard.

### **The Problem:**

1. **A huge time commitment.** Sales reps (and Founders!) lose hours each week sifting through Sales Navigator searches, poking around profiles for the right fit
2. **Hard to filter out unqualified leads.** A great title alone doesn’t always mean they’re a great prospect — context matters, and double checking is a chore
3. **Zero contact info.** You’ll find the perfect lead, then pay for yet another tool just to get their email or phone
4. **Expensive integrations + data providers.** Connecting to LinkedIn so you can push to CRM? That’s $1600 annually, per seat. A license to Zoominfo? $10k a year. It adds up!

### **Why We Built Ciro**

Like many YC companies, Ciro’s customers operate on lean budgets but expect high quality output from their sales teams. The only way to bridge that gap is to arm them with great tools. We saw a few common problems:

* **Bottlenecks** **form** when companies rely on humans to parse information — revenue growth is gated on executing manual, repetitive tasks
* **Many products do too little** (simple browser extensions) or **require** **complicated orchestration** that takes forever to set up
* **Finding great contact data** is expensive and usually requires several additional subscriptions

## Ciro 🚀 The GTM Platform for companies selling to SMB and Local businesses

### **What’s the Problem?**

If you run a business selling to an SMB vertical, you’ve probably found it **impossible to** **find rich data on the prospects in your market…** and that existing data solutions are rife with missing records, infrequent updates, and incorrect contact information.

### **What’s the Solution?**

Ciro handles prospect list building so you don’t have to.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=64232&key=user_uploads/928465/3ca9d271-95f9-45da-80ce-a085a8052544)

Our solution combines hundreds of vertical-specific web crawlers that run every day, plus a data ingestion engine that joins dozens of public and proprietary data sources. Through these tools we provide our customers with a complete prospect database, plus vertical-specific “signals” to focus their outreach.

Examples of these “signals” include:

* Changes to a prospect’s **tech stack** (e.g. a doctor’s office uninstalls ZocDoc)
* **New company formation** (e.g. a newly-registered construction license)
* **Ownership changes** (e.g. change-of-control updates to company reporting docs)
