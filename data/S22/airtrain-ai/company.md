# Airtrain AI

> No-code data curation for LLM fine-tuning and evaluation.

Airtrain AI is a no-code data platform for Large Language Models.

Proprietary AI models such as GPT-4 are very powerful but also very costly, slow, unreliable, and unsecured.

As businesses look to scale their AI prototypes into production-grade products, they struggle with large AI bills, slow APIs and large failure rates.
On the other hand, smaller language models have been proven to be able to perform on-part with large ones with fine-tuned on high-quality datasets.

Airtrain AI lets AI practitioners explore alternatives to proprietary models, build up training datasets, evaluate, fine-tune, and serve a large selection of open-source LLMs.
