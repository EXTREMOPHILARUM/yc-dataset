# Peoplebox.ai

> AI talent platform to hire, manage, and grow your people 

Peoplebox.ai is an AI-native talent platform used by 500+ companies to hire, manage, and grow their people.

At the center is Nova, the world’s most human-like AI teammate, for all talent conversations. Nova runs two-way voice/video pre-screen calls, first-round interviews (with coding round and advance proctoring), check-ins, and assessments,  all with your company’s context built in.

The talent management platform brings together OKRs/goals, performance reviews, career growth, calibration, and compensation into one unified system, helping companies align, assess, and develop talent faster.
