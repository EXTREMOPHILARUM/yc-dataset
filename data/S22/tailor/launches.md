### **❌ The Problem: Retail ops software is stuck in the 90s**

Legacy ERP software (think Netsuite and SAP) is rigid, monolithic, and difficult to implement and customize. This hits **retail operations** especially hard because of the industry’s unique operational makeup of supply chains, online marketplaces, and varying sales channels. 

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=91217&key=user_uploads/965414/c28e51ee-4b3f-4f22-80b3-8ac011720893)

Retail operators basically must choose between:

* **Legacy ERP**: Bulky, monolithic systems that take several months or years to implement.
* **Point solutions that don’t scale**: Tools for inventory, order management, or light manufacturing can solve short-term pain but aren’t API-first and break easily ops need to scale.
* **Spreadsheets or homegrown**: Even fast-growing brands run core ops on Google Sheets, Airtable, or in-house tools held together by duct tape.

 

### **✅ The Solution: Tailor Omakase**

**Tailor Omakase** is a curated, customizable ERP lite built for fast-growing retail brands. It helps operations teams manage purchasing, inventory, and orders across DTC, wholesale, and 3PL — without needing a full-time ERP admin, multi-year contracts, or expensive agency support.

**HubSpot** filled a gap for SMBs who couldn’t compete with **marketing** from enterprise giants using enterprise tools and budgets. **Tailor Omakase** does the same for **retail and ecommerce brands** when it comes to **operations**.

### **🔧 What’s under the hood**

Tailor Omakase runs on the same powerful [platform](https://www.tailor.tech/platform) we use to build internal tools and custom workflows for global enterprises.

* **Instant CRUD APIs**: Generate GraphQL/REST APIs from schema definitions in seconds, complete with validation, filtering, pagination, and relationships.
* **Composable backend**: Launch headless apps with custom logic, workflows, and event triggers without writing boilerplate code.
* **50+ pre-built integrations**: Seamlessly connect to Shopify, QuickBooks, 3PLs, and more using Tailor’s API-first architecture.
* **Automation-ready**: Use Pipelines, Functions, and Event Triggers to automate business logic and cross-system workflows.
* **State machine support**: Handle complex approval flows and record-level transitions using schema-defined states.
* **AI-native tools**: Enable vector search, parse documents with OCR, and integrate LLMs from OpenAI, Anthropic, or open-source models like Llama.
* **_Coming soon —_** **_Fabrix_**: Auto-generate UI components from GraphQL schemas using Tailor’s open-source framework, Fabrix
