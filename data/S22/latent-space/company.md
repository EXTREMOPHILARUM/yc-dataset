# Latent Space

> Applied AI for The Emerging Economies

Latent Space develops AI applications specifically designed to address the unique challenges and opportunities in emerging economies. Our products are Halo AI (an AI universal concierge for SEA enterprises), GoVisa (an AI-enabled online visa and permit application), and Supa (a hyperlocal knowledge AI for 280M+ Indonesians).
