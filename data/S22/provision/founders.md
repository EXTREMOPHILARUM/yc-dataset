## Luigi La Corte
**Founder**

Luigi is the co-founder and CEO of Provision. Before co-founding Provision, he worked in the construction industry for seven years between Arup (a multi-disciplinary engineering design firm) as a civil engineer and Plenary (infrastructure developer) as a Director. He managed $3B+ of large infrastructure projects with some of the most sophisticated stakeholders across the value chain (owners, GCs, sub-contractors, facility managers).
