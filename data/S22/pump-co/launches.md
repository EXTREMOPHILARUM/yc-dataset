**How does Pump work?**

AWS incentivizes long term commitments, 1 year to 3 year with high discounts. Not only does this require careful planning & thorough future forecasting, but small startups are often not able to commit for such long periods. That’s where Pump comes in, we bring companies together under one umbrella - as long as there’s other companies within the same region and using the same instance as you, Pump rotates these commitments across Company A to B and so on; thus shrinking a 3 year commitment to just about a month; maximum savings and minimal risk 😀

![](https://assets-global.website-files.com/634b9f9236d9ffdc11dc0eb6/65041ea8580984bf8c7e4065_pump-schema.webp)

**What’s new with this launch?**

Previously, we could work with companies around the world with the exception of India (irony much?). For legal and tax reasons, AWS has set up a completely different entity for the country and to work with Indian customers, we ourselves had to setup a subsidiary in India. Our second highest traffic & signups always came from India since our launch in April, and given this strong pull we decided to go down this path even as a young company.

We are beyond PUMPED to go live in India and save Indian customers up to 60% on AWS costs!

**Why this launch is important?**

While adapting our product for India wasn’t that much work, we went through a grueling 4 months of copious paperwork, legalese and tests of patience.

There was just so much monumental effort that went in that we essentially had to package this as a launch :’)
