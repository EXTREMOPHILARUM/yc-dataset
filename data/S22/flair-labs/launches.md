## Flair Labs: Digital Concierge for Real Estate and Lending

**tl;dr**: Real estate and mortgage brokerages lose billions in commissions yearly in missed opportunities with prospective home buyers and sellers. Flair lets any team deploy their own fleet of digital assistants - tailored to real estate workflows - that allows brokerages to multiply their active conversations.

### **❌ The Problem**

**Real estate and lending teams lack resources to properly manage client communications.**

Every day brokerages leave millions of dollars on the table. Why?

* Communication in real estate quickly becomes overwhelming, especially when there aren’t enough hands to stay on top of every client
* They, therefore, miss over half their phone calls and follow-ups
* Today - 300,000+ loan officers and 1.6m+ realtors spend hours on the phone getting applicants to complete applications or gathering additional information before they churn to competitors.
* 85% of the time when a call is missed, clients don’t call back; they often go with another realtor

**Existing alternatives are either expensive or inefficient.** Even small to midsize brokerages can make and receive hundreds, if not thousands, of calls per day

* Receptionists (both virtual & non-virtual) are costly, not 24/7 and operational on the weekend, require months of onboarding, and have high variance in terms of performance
* Call centers are costly (few dollars / minute), inconsistent, and don’t integrate with business systems (e.g., CRMs)

### **✨ Our Solution**

[Flair Labs](https://flairlabs.ai) allows brokerages to set up their own **digital concierge** that automates client communication and relationship nurturing

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=86082&key=user_uploads/986377/994c9276-0ffe-44b0-b080-5416a5dc294b)

**Speed-to-lead**

* 24/7 availability - automate follow-ups from inbound inquiries to nail down appointment times and collect necessary information
* Pick up and reengage clients that dropped off online forms or applications
* Multi-channel sequences through call, sms and email ensure to reach clients where they prefer most

**Lead reactivation**

* Booking confirmations to minimize cancellations / no-shows
* Follow-ups with previous leads to gather updated information and changes in timelines

**Live transfer & callback scheduling**

* Warm transfer hot leads directly to realtors or mortgage brokers
* Connect with calendar and CRM to set up times to call back
