## ⚓ Manatee - Interactive demos for your SaaS product

TLDR: **We help you create and manage interactive demos for your SaaS product that anonymize data, auto-update, and track interaction.**

Hey everyone! [@Bryan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bryan-houlton/) and [@Ryan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ryansdowning/) here, excited to share what we have been working on at [@Manatee](https://www.trymanatee.com/).

# 🌟 Our North Star

Prospects leaving your demo should know exactly how your product helps them, and be excited to buy.

### 😠 What’s the Problem?

**Product demos should be personalized to each prospect** - and not just data. Often times the demo flow itself isn’t applicable to the user. Here’s how some of our users used to remedy this:

* Pester your engineers to make a demo deployment (which then has to be maintained)
* Photoshop/Figma to edit static images of the product Walk prospect through a flow and hope it’s useful for them

If you do any of these things, we could be a fit for you!

### 🔨 The Solution

* **Full fledged sandbox**. Don’t constrain your prospect to a flow, give them access to play with your full platform (without actually having access)
* Customize these demo environments for each prospect
* Send the link to the prospect to walk through the demo with them on call or asynchronously
* Leave Figma-esque comments around the demo to ask questions
* Replay the prospects interaction with the demo to see what they care about in your product
* Embed the demo on your site with an email collection form to generate inbound leads

We put the demos in the hands of the sales team entirely, meaning **every part of the demo process can be done without code**.

### 🤯 Why can’t we just use Walnut/Reprise?

Walnut and Reprise are great products, but have limited use cases. Without a sandbox, you can’t be sure that the prospect is looking specifically for the feature highlighted.

Also, we auto-update your demo to be in sync with frontend changes!

## 💻☁PreStacks: Create Cloud Infrastructure in Seconds

### **_TL;DR - Quickly configure a templated cloud stack and deploy to your accounts._**

Hey everyone, we’re [Bryan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bryan-houlton/) and [Ryan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ryansdowning/) and we’re building [PreStacks](https://www.prestacks.com/). We’re on a mission to reduce the time you spend deploying and managing your cloud.

### **😠 What’s the Problem?**

**Cloud engineering takes time away from building a solid product.** It’s a necessary evil, but let us deal with it.

* Deploying servers, cluster, databases, and configuring pipelines, APIs, and auto-deployments shouldn’t be this hard.
* Let’s face it. We all have our go-to stack that we deploy in the same way, every time. Why not automate it?

### **🔨 Our Solution**

PreStacks makes building proper cloud infrastructure an easy choice.

* **We provision your infrastructure from the ground up**: GitLab/GitHub, Docker, PostgreSQL, AWS. All connected with PreStacks.
* **You own the source.** We don’t lock you into a custom cloud or language. We deploy to all of your accounts, so if you need to make changes, you can.
* **Ongoing monitoring** of your cloud resources.

### **🔄 How It Works**

1. Link your GitLab/GitHub and AWS accounts.
2. Configure your Stack options (domain name, database size, staging environments, etc).
3. Watch as a repository gets created in your GitLab/GitHub, resources are provisioned in your AWS, and your first pipeline auto-deployment is triggered.
4. Within minutes, see **Django’s** `The install work successfully!` pop up at your domain.
5. Push a change and watch it deploy!
