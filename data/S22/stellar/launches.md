## Stellar 📈 - AI-powered OKRs

**TL;DR** - Implementing an OKR process that works for your team can feel impossible. [Stellar](https://www.stellarapp.io) drives alignment with an AI-powered OKR platform that simplifies writing and tracking goals.

Hi everyone - we’re Angela, Quan and Elise! Stellar is launching an AI product to tackle the biggest obstacles startups face when implementing OKRs.

### **Problem**

* **Writing cascading goals is hard -** after defining topline lagging metrics (revenue, churn, LTV, etc), each team member might not know how to write SMART goals that ladder up to them.
* **Operational best practices are a mystery -** it’s hard to know where to start when creating a goal management process.
* **A hero is required to drive accountability** - someone needs to step up to implement and enforce the process throughout the quarter, in addition to their full time job.

Introducing Stellar AI – a copilot for every employee that simplifies writing SMART, cascading goals and derisks them throughout the quarter.

### **How it works**

Answer 4 simple questions and Stellar spins up a customized OKR workspace. An integrated experience uses GenAI to write your unique inspiring top-line goals and cascading domain-specific goals at all levels.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=72653&key=user_uploads/997645/6097185e-9f26-4846-aaea-cdea8c355d6f)

Once goals are written, Stellar automates a recurring check-in process for your team throughout the quarter and year. These updates create an agenda for business reviews.

Stellar’s smart notification system drives accountability, resulting in an average of 85% of all goals being updated weekly. Additionally, If someone is stuck on a goal, Stellar AI recommends domain-specific next steps to help unblock them.

### **The problem with OKRs**

Setting business goals is easy, but iterative execution is hard.

Too many companies set OKRs, forget them in an isolated document and then realize they haven’t made sufficient progress. When OKRs aren’t reassessed openly on a recurring basis, you miss the opportunity to motivate your team, collaborate and unblock progress together.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=65966&key=user_uploads/997645/ed098364-c2e7-449f-9436-f637d0041fc2)

The designated responsible person (usually a founder or Chief of Staff) spends hours a week running this process, when they could be working on strategic work.

### **Achieve your goals with confidence**

If you’re ready to embed OKRs and agile collaboration in your organization, Stellar will help you:

1. Stay focused and organized by linking your goals in an OKR tree
2. Raise and remove blockers quickly through effective, asynchronous check-ins
3. Save time with scheduled notifications and automatic reminders about missing updates
