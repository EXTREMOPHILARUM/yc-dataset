# Dittofeed

> Open-source customer engagement & messaging automation

Dittofeed is an open-source alternative to platforms like Klaviyo and Customer.io. Self-host us for free, or use our low-cost cloud offering to automate messages across email, SMS, mobile push, WhatsApp, and more. 

We also offer solutions for embedding Dittofeed in your own SaaS! Instantly provide your customers with automated messaging tools.
