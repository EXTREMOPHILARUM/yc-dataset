## Versa: Smart B2B receipts

**tl;dr:** B2B companies use [Versa](https://versa.org/) to automatically sync sales receipts into your customers’ expense management system. For free. Happier corporate customers == more corporate business for your startup.

### The problem

Big companies need receipts for everything they buy (every SaaS bill, every hotel folio, every Uber, every coffee). Because:

* The IRS requires receipts for every transaction over $75.
* They need the itemized details to enforce their expense policies.
* In Europe, companies can reclaim 20% of their spend (VAT)... but only if they have receipts.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=82606&key=user_uploads/8725/eda8dbb3-503a-46c5-bda3-b8c4b5689350)

But despite this need, no system exists to aggregate this data. Finance teams rely on employees to photograph paper receipts, or forward emailed receipts to their expense management / AP systems. This creates a bunch of employee busywork, and compliance is still far below 100%. 

Great tools like Brex streamline every other part of expense management, but not receipts.

### Beautiful receipts, synced directly into your business customers’ expense management system. For free.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=82606&key=user_uploads/8725/316a25c6-32cd-4bf9-b597-be017d34a872)

We built Versa to eliminate this manual process. Your encrypted receipt data is passed directly to your buyers. 

We currently have several customers (including many YC companies) using it.

## Bend - A corporate card built for climate-friendly businesses ($100 to try it)

TL;DR - Just by shifting some of your company spending to Bend, you’ll be helping to reduce climate-changing emissions, thanks to the carbon removal rewards you’ll automatically earn, and our embedded climate analytics.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=71112&key=user_uploads/8725/dd74d8ed-b20a-4977-bd01-bdf196ce6ceb)

### **How it works:**

* Bend is a modern card and cash account, combined with free carbon accounting and climate rewards
* Bend works similarly to how you’d use a business bank account. Send and receive ACH and wires, earn 4% yield on deposits, issue physical and virtual cards with budgets, etc.
* Carbon accounting typically runs companies $40k/year and up. We make money off the card interchange (same as any bank), enabling us to offer the carbon accounting for free, and pass through carbon removal rewards.
* The way we actually do the carbon accounting is laid out here — we always return a confidence score with every CO2e assessment: <https://usebend.com/how-it-works>
* We have awesome customers like Harmonic AI (<https://bend.green/harmonic>) and Fathom (<https://bend.green/fathom>). If you choose, you can create a report of your climate action through Bend like these ones, which you can share with customers, employees, investors, etc.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=71112&key=user_uploads/8725/b8c15c5e-5e61-4646-b46d-b6480ff4ece1)

### **Why tracking and reducing climate-changing emissions is worthwhile:**

* It’s good for the planet (and free!)
* It will help you sell your products to B2C customers who care about the climate, and B2B customers who have set net zero goals (64% of global market cap companies now report on climate data, and are increasingly asking their suppliers to do the same)

## Bend ⚡ 1-click Carbon Accounting

At Bend, we help companies develop an **affordable**, **automated**, and **credible** path to net zero using your spend data. And we help fintech companies **embed** sustainability into b2b tools.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=64027&key=user_uploads/8725/de981465-da6b-4888-b0cd-3df01f8cdce6)

Thomas and I found our way to this project via our background in fintech. Prior to Bend, we worked together at Abacus (W14), a real time expense management company that I cofounded in 2014, serving small businesses. At Abacus, we noticed a pattern: finance teams are increasingly paying attention to the climate impact of their purchase decisions, from travel policies, to cloud hosting, and beyond. And better tooling and data is needed to drive pragmatic, effective climate action. 

### **From zero to net zero in 5 minutes**

Here’s how Bend works:

* Bend converts spend data into CO2e data.
* For example: imagine a $1,000 bill from Atlassian. Bend aggregates corporate emissions disclosures; we know that for every $1,000 spent with Atlassian, there’s a 30.2 kg footprint.
* For long-tail merchants, we have merchant category fallbacks, so you’re always guaranteed 100% coverage (e.g. for Starbucks, we use the Starbucks factor, for a mom-and-pop coffee shop, we use a benchmark ‘coffee shop’ factor).
* Our methodology is developed and vetted by climate science PhDs (degrees from MIT, Columbia, etc.), and our team has years of fintech experience (Abacus, Emburse, Google, Venmo).
* Once armed with a complete picture of your emissions, you can choose a course of action — you might consider switching to lower-carbon-intensity vendors, offsetting your remaining emissions, and then **closing sales as a sustainable business** (we can help!).

### **Why now**

* Companies (e.g. Salesforce, Apple, Walmart) now require their suppliers to measure emissions and set reductions targets. Bend can help you sell to these customers.
* The SEC is expected to roll out new climate data disclosure requirements this year, applying to public companies, with downstream impacts.
* Your company has a surprising amount of leverage to accelerate decarbonization. It’s a great thing to be a part of, and has positive brand benefits for your startup.
