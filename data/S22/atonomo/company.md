# Atonomo

> Autonomous conversion rate optimization for startups

Atonomo is an AI team member which continuously ingests product analytics to provide a once-weekly conversion rate optimization you can ship or A/B test, prioritized by ROI (+KPIs). Atonomo submits a PR or gives you a Claude Code/Cursor prompt.
