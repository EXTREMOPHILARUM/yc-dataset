# Why do we need plant-based seafood?

1. Bottom trawling creates **more GHG emissions than the entire global aviation sector.**
2. Almost 80% of the world's marine **fish stocks are now fully exploited** (FAO). 
3. **93% of CO2 is stored in marine vegetation** and every 6 days an area in the size of Germany is destroyed by fishing trawlers.
4. Nearly all **seafood contain mercury** or in aquaculture antibiotics
