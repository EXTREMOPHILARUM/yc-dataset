# REVER

> Changing the way people buy by giving superpowers to shoppers

We receive any product in 24h anywhere on the planet, but... Why can't we also return & get refunded just as quickly?

REVER is a game-changing shopping experience that makes eCommerce returns possible in 2 clicks and refunds in 24h.

The REVER team is shaping the future of eCommerce bringing frictionless, superfast and sustainable returns to everyone, anytime, anywhere. Because returning should be as easy as buying. 

REVER's ambition is to dominate ALL eCommerce post-sale services by creating the world's first global platform for online returns.

REVER is currently handling +100M€ in yearly returns, with more than 50M€ in revenues
