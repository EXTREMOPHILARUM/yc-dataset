### Here’s How it Works

### Your Ideal Customer Profile

CustomerOS automatically generates your ICP based on your website, content, and existing customers.  Then we score every lead  based on how well they fit your ICP.

Only high-fit leads make it into your pipeline so you don’t waste time on prospects who are never going to convert.  Everything on your screen is a qualified lead we actually WANT to close.
