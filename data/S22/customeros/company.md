# CustomerOS

> Uncover high-fit leads already silently engaging with your brand

CustomerOS uncovers high-fit leads who are engaging directly with your brand but not yet in your sales funnel. By lead scoring your pipeline against your ICP we build data-driven profiles of your best customers and provide a real-time ICP-fit indicator so your team doesn't waste time on low-value/high-cost leads. This ensures you're spending your CAC acquiring customers who are primed to renew year after year and expand as they grow.

The Top 10% of SaaS companies generate 87% of all market returns. CustomerOS gives you the data and tooling to compete with the top 10%.
