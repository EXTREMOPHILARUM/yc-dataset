## Spread - simple email sending for marketing, sales, product, and transactional emails

We’re [Relate](https://www.relate.so/). Our customers kept asking for a simple email tool that works with our CRM.

So we built it. They loved it. Then we realized everyone wants simpler email sending. That’s why we made it a standalone product — Spread.

**Spread.** **We're making email simple and easy.**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=88529&key=user_uploads/380923/68398109-cf5f-4980-bea5-1eb8c83b6d13)

**tl;dr**

* **Spread is an email sending platform that combines sales emails (cold), marketing, product, and transactional email together.**
* Give it a try: [**www.spread.so**](http://www.spread.so)

Email is still the best way to reach customers. Unlike social media, emails wait in your customer's inbox until they're read. \*\*Write something worth reading, and engagement follows.\*\*\
\
**But sending emails is still painful.** Writing a simple oneoff campaign is too complicated. We were tired of using multiple tools for marketing, product, transactional and sales emails.

Worse, managing contacts across tools is chaos - endless CSV exports/imports between marketing, sales, product, and transactional emails.

Spread makes it easy by:

* Notion-like email editor (markdown supported!)
* Easy integration with emails
* Automatic 1:1 email sequencing
* Triggered emails based on contact adds
* Forms, Docs, Calendar to capture leads

## Relate: Modern Sales CRM for B2B Startups

Hey YC!

After nearly two years of building in private, we are finally opening our doors to build Relate with a broader public user community. **Today, we're launching **[**Relate**](https://www.relate.so/)** — a modern sales CRM software built for B2B startups.**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=76188&key=user_uploads/380923/6293f5c2-08fd-4db5-9062-f5e8b3a9bdce)

### **Why are we building a new CRM platform?**

It started with scratching our own itch. Before building Relate, we spent our [first four pivots](https://www.relate.so/blog/six-applications-y-combinator/) working on various B2B products. We needed a way to track our early sales and customer development activities, so we picked a CRM available on the market then — and we quickly found we were limited with old processes.

Our sales motion, as a result, was very slow. We jumped around different CRM solutions, but none worked for us. Adding customer profiles was cumbersome and felt manual. Updating deals daily was taking longer than the selling itself.

Why isn't there a simple and easy-to-use CRM that just works out of the box?

For chat, we love using Slack. And for issue tracking, we love using Linear. But what about sales? Why isn't there a CRM that gives you similar excitement and joy as Slack and Linear?

Frustrated by failing to find one, we built Relate to solve our problem.
