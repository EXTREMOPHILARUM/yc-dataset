# Launches

## ⏩ Blitz - Build internal apps with zero code

We make it easy to structure and automate very manual processes - such as onboarding a new partner or resolving customer tickets. 

Hi everyone!

We’re Clement and Loic, the co-founders of [Blitz,](https://blitznocode.com/) and we are super excited to announce the release of [our closed beta](https://www.blitznocode.com/signup)!

### **The problem**

Building internal tools is broken. Many no-code tools have tried to address that issue, enabling developers to code faster or helping end users build web applications on top of Google Sheets or Airtable.

But let’s face it: they don’t work, and the result is always the same: operations teams go back to spreadsheets.

### **Our solution**

Blitz is a no-code platform to build internal apps and workflows on top of a flexible database—so that:

1. **developers** can focus on developing user-facing features, and don’t have to build and maintain back-office applications anymore
2. **product managers** have now the capabilities to build internal apps with no tech resources
3. **operations teams** never have to do the same manual task over and over again

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=64760&key=user_uploads/813313/a34fc140-8bf9-48d3-8fef-fab0051bbad1)

**Blitz relies on 3 main pillars:**

1. **a reactive database**—design your data model and integrate your business
2. **an interface designer**—create internal apps using pre-built templates and reusable components
3. **a workflow builder**—set up backend or frontend triggers with conditions to automate actions
