# Polymath Robotics

> General Autonomy for Industrial Vehicles

Polymath is building a general autonomy stack for cautious vehicles.  Our software allows any industrial vehicle - whether it's a tractor in a field or a bulldozer in a mine, drive itself.  We bundle together AI, ML, Controls, ROS, Safety and best-in-class deployment practices to enable our customers to tell automated vehicles to do via a REST API.

We're on more robots than we have engineers, are seeing our revenue (and robotic fleet) grow rapidly, and are looking for folks who want to help automate the world.
