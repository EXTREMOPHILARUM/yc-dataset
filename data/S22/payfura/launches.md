# Launches

## Rampay - Fiat<>Crypto on/off ramp payment gateway for web3 applications.

Hi All, we are [Gajendra](https://www.linkedin.com/in/gajendra-khatri-9955a14a/) & [Sagar](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sagar-agarwal-625317a1/), we are super excited to introduce [Rampay](https://rampay.io/)!

### **🚀TL;DR**

[Rampay](https://rampay.io) allows web3 applications like crypto exchanges, wallets, NFT marketplaces etc. to enable their users to convert fiat to crypto and vice-versa using local payment methods of a region.

The compliance requirements regarding KYC and AML are handled within the solution and the integration is available over API and widget.

### **❓Problem**

* Web3/Blockchain applications need a seamless bridge between fiat and crypto to onboard users globally.
* Lack of buying/selling crypto with local payment methods of a region.
* High platform fees and price spread in the existing solutions.

### **🎯Solution**

At Rampay we are building a next generation payment infrastructure to on/off ramp crypto with fiat, so our partners can grow business across different geographies in a compliant way by just integrating with us. Key features are:

* **Currencies supported**: 150+ fiat currencies, 100+ crypto currencies.
* **Regions supported**: US, Europe, LATAM, SEA, Africa.
* **Payment methods**: Credit/Debit cards, SEPA, Apple pay, Google pay, E-wallets, Bank transfers and many more.
* **Partner Dashboard:** A powerful dashboard for partners to get detailed insights and analytics for their orders
* Handle KYC and AML compliances of local regions.
* API and IFrame integration.

Both the founders have around 7 years of experience in the software engineering field, we have worked with small startups to big MNCs, we have been involved in the blockchain space for 4 years now.

After having worked with web3 companies, we understood that allowing users to convert their fiat to crypto is a big pain point hindering growth of these companies and solving this requires a lot to be done on the compliance/regulatory side.

Also, we believe onboarding next billion users to web3 could not be achieved without there being a platform which allows seamless  conversion of fiat to crypto and vice versa with local payment methods of a region.
