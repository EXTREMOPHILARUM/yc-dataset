Bitstack is the easiest way for people in Europe to get their first crypto wallet. Users can get started by turning their spare change into bitcoin and then use the app to buy, sell, earn and spend new digital currencies like bitcoin.

Simply download the app, securely link your bank account and you’re all set. Bitstack will automatically round up your everyday purchases to the nearest euro and invest the spare change in Bitcoin.

There is no need to determine the right time to buy and no investment knowledge required.

### **Why should you save in Bitcoin?**

Today, 2 out of 3 people are worried about not achieving their financial goals, whether that’s buying a house, saving for retirement, a vacation, or their children’s future.

Unfortunately, most people continue saving in low-interest savings accounts that are consistently outpaced over the years by rising inflation.

But Bitcoin fixes this. Bitcoin is the first open, global and permissionless monetary network with a fixed supply of 21 million bitcoins, ever, and protected by a distributed network of users all around the world.

**So we built Bitstack to finally make it simple, fun and accessible for everyone to save and invest in Bitcoin.**
