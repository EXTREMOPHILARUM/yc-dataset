# Slingshot

> Analyze medical records in seconds

Slingshot is AI  that analyzes medical records to determine if the diagnosis is supported by the clinical evidence.

The company is founded by Pranov Duggasani, a former Google and Microsoft AI engineer, and Zoe Holderness, a former Lyft and Tesla engineer. They started working on Slingshot after facing their own frustrations with inflated and erroneous medical bills with the goal to save patients more money with less work.
