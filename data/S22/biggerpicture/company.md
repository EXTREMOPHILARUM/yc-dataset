# BiggerPicture

> Scheduling automation platform between trucking and warehousing 

BiggerPicture is a B2B SaaS solution that automates the scheduling of pick-up and delivery times for trucking companies and warehouses. 75% of shipments are still scheduled over phone calls and emails.
