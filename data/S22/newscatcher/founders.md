# Founders

## Artem Bugara
**Founder**

Artem Bugara is the co-founder, CEO of NewsCatcher. Artem works with data and risk teams to help them integrate actionable signals from online news. Previously he ran a team of data engineers for one of the largest underwriting companies within the aviation, energy, and space insurance sectors.

He earned a degree in Econometrics from Toulouse School of Economics.

**Founder**
