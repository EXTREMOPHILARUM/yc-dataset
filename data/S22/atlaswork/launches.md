## Atlas: AI Operating System for Enterprise Real Estate

Hey everyone 👋 We’re Shaan & Avi, the founders of Atlas.\
\
TL;DR: Real estate teams manage billions in assets using the same manual spreadsheets they used in 1990. Atlas is an AI analyst that sits on top of your existing systems to instantly ingest messy data and complex PDFs, allowing you to scale your portfolio without scaling your back-office.

### The Problem:

The biggest barrier to scaling a real estate portfolio long-term isn't acquiring properties; it's the operational nightmare that comes immediately after, caused by a data overload.

Whether you are underwriting a new acquisition or actively managing 5,000 units, the bottleneck is always manual data extraction and comparison. \
\
Highly paid asset managers spend weeks cross-referencing messy rent rolls, property management exports, and 100-page loan agreements just to figure out what's happening.

Because this data lives in silos, firms constantly miss revenue opportunities, risk breaching loan covenants, and spend months just trying to build an annual budget.

### In real estate, data goes into spreadsheets to die.

### The Solution:

Atlas acts as an AI Analyst that sits directly on top of your existing systems (like Yardi, Entrata, or Appfolio).

### 1. Ingest the Mess:

Add your data, including messy CSV exports, dense loan agreements, and insurance policies, directly into Atlas. We offer multiple options,s including automated pulls, so complex integrations or multi-month onboarding aren’t necessary.

### 2. Instant Audits & Reconciliation:

Instead of your team spending weeks cross-referencing PDFs, Atlas instantly audits deals for risk, flags compliance issues, and reconciles your operations directly against the source documents.

### 3. Scale Your AUM:

Go from question to answer in seconds. You get the operational capacity of a massive analyst team, instantly.
