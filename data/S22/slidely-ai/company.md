# Slidely AI

> AI for professional PowerPoint presentations, available in PowerPoint

Create new slides or upgrade existing ones with AI that works natively inside PowerPoint and stays true to your company’s branding, templates, and standards.

How Slidely AI is different -

1. Works directly in PowerPoint
Native PPTX support — no HTML-to-PPT hacks, exports, or broken layouts.

2. Improves slides you already have
Because it understands real PowerPoint files, Slidely AI can rewrite, redesign, and tighten existing decks — not just generate new ones.

3. Agentic, consulting-grade output
Unlike other tools which can only use simple templates, Slidely AI agent puts each shape individually on slides to express your complex data.

4. Truly on-brand, every time
Learns from your past decks to follow your colors, fonts, layouts, and slide patterns — so every slide looks like it came from you, not a generic AI tool.
