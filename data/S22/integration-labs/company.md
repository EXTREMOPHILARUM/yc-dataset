# Integration Labs

> The Unified API for Business Financial Data 

Integration Labs makes it easy to interact with your business customers' financial data.

Leading SaaS, payments, and financial service companies use a single API to seamlessly retrieve and sync data across their customers' accounting, commerce, and payments platforms.

With Integration Labs, B2B companies integrate once to connect to 30+ accounting, payments and e-commerce integrations and build innovative business tools for invoice financing, digital lending, expense management, automated AP/AR reconciliation, and more.
