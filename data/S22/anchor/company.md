# Anchor

> Embedded finance platform for African businesses

Anchor provides APIs, dashboard and tools that help developers easily embed and build banking products. This includes APIs for creating bank accounts, funds transfers, savings products, issuing cards and offering loans.

We also provide regulatory coverage and continuous compliance support to our partners.
