# Slauth.io

> The IAM Policy Copilot

Slauth.io generates and rightsizes IAM policies in order to save engineering time and secure the organization. We use PR's to suggest what good functional and secure IAM policies should look like and integrate with IaC (Terraform)  to have a seamless end-to-end IAM policy creation and deployment. Save hundreds of DevOps engineering time each year and increase your security to scale faster.
