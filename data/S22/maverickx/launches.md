Hey YC — we’re **Eric and Jesse**, co-founders of **MaverickX**.

MaverickX makes next-generation chemicals that break rock to unlock valuable natural resources — oil and critical metals.

We started in mining. Now we’ve expanded into oil & gas, launching our PetroX™ product line with multiple major operators already under contract.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95571&key=user_uploads/89898/23215d7c-5b15-4672-9408-dadbc92688ad)

**🔥 The Problem**

American energy production has a math problem:

* Only 20% of the oil in unconventional shale wells is recovered – 80% of the oil is still underground
* Operating costs are projected to rise 40% over the next decade, heading toward $95/BBL

And nearly 10% of U.S. oilfield deaths each year are linked to exposure to toxic chemicals still used in daily operations

It’s one of the biggest “old-tech” sectors left untouched by modern science.

**⚡ What We Built**

MaverickX creates next-gen chemistry that actually removes iron, breaks scale and paraffin, liberates metals and more oil from shale. We reduce operating costs and keep production flowing.

With PetroX, operators are seeing:

* dramatically cleaner systems
* lower operating cost
* reduced downtime
* safer field operations
* and the ability to liberate **50%+ more hydrocarbons** in certain shale environments

Our chemistry increases shale porosity by up to 40x, allowing oil to move freely where it previously couldn’t. We aren’t just swapping chemicals — we’re rewriting the physics of the reservoir.

**⛽ Why Oil & Gas?**

Oil is the backbone of our modern economy and demand is only projected to increase.

MaverickX started in mining, building better chemistries to extract metals.\
Then the Department of Energy contracted us for a wild project:

_“Turn old oil wells into mines. Extract the metals trapped inside.”_ We did it.\
\
And in that process, we discovered something bigger: Our chemistry didn’t just free metals, it freed hydrocarbons — a lot of them. The moment we saw that, we knew we had to expand into oil & gas. Today we operate in South Texas with production-scale manufacturing and contracts for PetroX™ across multiple operators.
