# Launches

## ✈️ Velontra - Hypersonic space plane that can take-off from any location and weather

Velontra is building a hypersonic space plane that can takeoff from anywhere in any weather.

Check us out at <https://velontra.com/>

**Problem**

1\.     Conventional Rocket-based launch is expensive (>$5000 per kg)

2\.     Rocket launch cancellations cost satellite companies millions

3\.     Launch lead times are greater than 1 year

4\.     Satellite companies do not get launched into their desired orbits

**Solution**

Velontra is building a hypersonic space plane that can takeoff from anywhere in any weather.  This horizontal takeoff increases launch reliability and enables customers to directly access any orbit.  Our air-breathing propulsion system is up to 6 times more fuel efficient than any rocket, and uses oxygen from the air which creates thousands of pounds of additional payload capacity.

**Revenue Generating Products**

Here are the steps of our tech development path.

1\.     Propulsion System

2\.     Supersonic UAV

3\.     Hypersonic UAV

4\.     Space Plane for Low Earth Orbit

**How It Works**

The hypersonic space plane will work by taking off from any runway with a jet propulsion system.  Next, it will climb to over 100,000’ and Mach 5.  At this point, it will launch a 2nd stage rocket directly into the desired Low Earth Orbit.
