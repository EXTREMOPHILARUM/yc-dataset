# Launches

## Delegasi - The first automated accounting service for Small Businesses in Indonesia

👋 Hi we are Okky and Ans, founders of Delegasi.

We assist small business owners in putting together their financial statements and analysis automatically. To generate the financial statements, business owners can simply connect their POS and upload their receipts.

—

## ❓ The Problem

Accounting is always a hassle for small business owners. As they need to handle lots of things (marketing, operations, purchasing, etc.), finding time for accounting is too hard. Not to mention, they need to learn accounting to do it properly.

Many accounting software have claimed to help make the process faster and easier, yet many business owners we spoke with stop using them in the first 1-2 months, even after paying a one year subscription fee. Turns out, these software are still too complicated for users without accounting knowledge.

## ✅ **Our Solution**

If you are a restaurant owner, you can simply:

1. Connect your POS and food delivery service data;
2. Upload your receipts, bank statements, and stock opname data; and
3. Your financial statements and analysis are ready

With Delegasi, no manual input nor accounting knowledge are needed to generate your financial report. Additionally, you will get financial analysis that is catered to restaurants and cafes’ owners.

### 🗒️ **Founders Backstory**

We have known each other for more than twelve years since college. In early 2021 we started to try finding solution for restaurant and cafe owners around staff hiring problems. Turned out, every businesses that we pitched always mentioned accounting as one of their most painful problems. Thus, we took major pivot and tried to find solution on this one. We helped our friends’ businesses and crafted our first products. After experimenting for a few months, we received positive feedback and realized that this could become huge 📈. That was how we started Delegasi 🚀
