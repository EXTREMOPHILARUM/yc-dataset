# Delegasi

> Automated accounting service for small businesses in Indonesia

Financial statements are definitely a hassle for business owners. Many accounting software are available, yet not everyone has the capability to operate them.

Amidst this problem, Delegasi dares to provide a solution. As the first virtual financial manager in Indonesia, we assist SMB owners in putting together their financial statements and business analysis. By simply uploading receipts and bank statements, owners will receive their financial statements and business analyses with guaranteed accuracy, allowing them to make well informed financial decisions.
