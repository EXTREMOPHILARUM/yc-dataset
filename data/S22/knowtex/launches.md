## Knowtex: Transparent, Explainable AI for Smarter Healthcare

[Knowtex](http://www.knowtex.ai/) is a voice AI platform built to empower healthcare organizations, turning real-time doctor-patient conversations into accurate clinical notes, ICD-10 and E&M codes tailored by medical specialty, including an admin dashboard for operations to save hours on admin work and billing.

**Bringing Visibility to AI in Medicine**

AI has the power to transform healthcare, but [black box decision-making](https://www.ama-assn.org/practice-management/digital/succeed-health-care-ai-get-rid-black-box) has no place in patient care. Knowtex is changing the game by making AI measurable and explainable, ensuring clinicians and healthcare systems can understand and trust every AI-driven recommendation.

**What This Means for Healthcare Teams**

With the Model Evaluation Platform, Knowtex can now:

* Evaluate AI performance in real-world clinical workflows for accuracy and reliability
* Enhance traceability by surfacing insights into model predictions and decisions
* Strengthen accountability with structured benchmarking and continuous monitoring

Knowtex’s benchmarks are built on insights from providers across multiple disciplines and focus on areas that matter most to them, such as:

* **Style** – How well does the AI-generated note match the clinician’s voice? Over time, the model learns and adapts to improve precision.
* **Note Length** – Does the AI capture the necessary details while avoiding unnecessary verbosity?
* **Summarization Faithfulness** – How accurately does the summarized note convey the entirety of the clinical encounter?
* **Structure** – Does the AI-generated note align with the expected template and clinical formatting?

## Knowtex: Ambient Billing for Clinicians 🗣️💸

**Introducing Ambient Billing at Knowtex**

At Knowtex, we’re excited to launch a new way to capture every billing opportunity with zero added effort. Knowtex is built to empower healthcare clinics and systems, turning real-time doctor-patient conversations into accurate ICD-10, CPT, and HCC codes. Our ambient, AI-driven billing solution helps clinicians avoid leaving revenue on the table—all while keeping them focused on patient care, not paperwork.

Medical coding is complex, and the rules and codes are constantly changing. Missed or inaccurate coding can dramatically impact a health system’s bottom line. That’s where Knowtex comes in. **Knowtex is a clinician’s partner in revenue:** capturing the full range of codes a visit is eligible for and keeping your documentation up-to-date with the latest standards. The result is an optimized, accurate billing workflow that catches every code, every time.

**Unlike other solutions that simply analyze documentation to generate codes, we’ve spent the last two years developing Knowtex into a deeply integrated, personalized coding model based on the visit conversation that is tailored to clinicians and organizations. We create custom models trained on each clinic’s historic coding data so Knowtex tailors its suggestions to the unique needs of every clinician. This level of personalization allows Knowtex not only to generate accurate codes but also to prescriptively suggest coding updates that are finely tuned to the specific workflows and nuances of the practice.** 

Knowtex is a truly adaptive partner in coding at the point of care. It captures information correctly upfront and continuously improves and aligns with each clinic’s standards for maximized revenue and streamlined workflows.

## Customer Results

One health system integrated Knowtex and observed significant improvement in revenue retention. With Knowtex identifying and correcting undercoded visits, they successfully capture approximately **$92,800 per month** in previously lost revenue.

One of our oncology practices with 50+ providers saw substantial improvement in billing outcomes with Knowtex. Before implementing Knowtex, their daily billing average was $7,101.60. Following integration, the mean daily billing rose to $7,893.70, an increase of almost **$800/provider/day**.

## Features

**🩺 Streamlined intelligent Code Selection**

Our interface is designed for simplicity and speed and makes it easy for clinicians to confirm suggested codes instantly. As a clinician speaks with a patient, Knowtex listens, prompting relevant codes based on the conversation. For E&M codes, it analyzes encounter elements like time, medical decision-making complexity, risk, and data, guiding clinicians to the correct documentation with zero interruptions.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcd1urRt3iovIjG_hdQrkbwJ0U0xMAQcrWNBMJvXj06EkCgVa7VeHot38yjpzGpMDDBPR0lRG_9jLd5bd2tIp2dB-GRbjIs8t723zatJ3OfZ3R8oPR2QKz7NPr7Jh59qYpFC11K?key=PrSNha-uknkZVns6FaLUE8yc)

In addition to live prompting, Knowtex provides targeted suggestions to help clinicians optimize coding accuracy. When assessing complexity, risk, or data documentation, Knowtex offers text snippets to update E&M codes based on the conversation info and historic data. These snippets guide providers by suggesting personalized updates that increase documentation complexity where applicable.

For example, with MDM:

\- Complexity: Knowtex can prompt providers to consider documenting any updates on the patient’s status under treatment with a pre-created snippet like “Patient is improving on current chemotherapy treatment but not yet at goal.”

**🔍 Accurate & Personalized Diagnosis Capture and Search**

Imagine a clinician is treating anemia. Most physicians would code the patient with D50.9 (Iron deficiency anemia, unspecified). While this can be accepted by insurance and reimbursed, there can be more precise codes that would be a better choice based on the patient’s symptomatology and history. 

Knowtex can capture and synthesize conversation and EHR data in real time as the clinician talks through symptoms and treatment with the patient to suggest precise ICD-10 codes like D63.1 (Anemia in chronic kidney disease). 

We also provide intelligent search functionality for clinicians that surfaces the best and most detailed ICD-10 code based on the conversation detail and EHR information.

**tldr;** Knowtex automatically creates visit notes with billing codes from doctor-patient conversations. We built our ML solution in under two months and are working with healthcare organizations to save doctors time and prevent revenue leakage from improper documentation and coding. Our ask is for help connecting with private clinics (with 8+ doctors) in any specialty, digital healthcare companies with providers, and PE firms with medical roll-ups.

### **The Problem:**

Doctors are spending more time on taking notes than seeing patients, and aren’t taught how to code their notes optimally for proper reimbursement. This leads to physician burnout and revenue leakage for healthcare organizations (up to $125 billion a year in the US).

### **Our Solution:**

Knowtex creates visit notes with the proper codes for reimbursement automatically from doctor-patient conversation.

**Wait, doctors code?**

We’re not talking about software coding, but medical coding, which means assigning the right diagnostic (ICD-10) and procedural codes (CPT) to a note to allow a claim to be created and submitted to insurers for appropriate payment. Correct coding is also critical for demographic assessments and studies of disease prevalence, treatment outcomes and accountability-based reimbursement systems.

With Knowtex, we can cut the time doctors currently spend on admin work in half, and allow them to focus on what they love: patient care.
