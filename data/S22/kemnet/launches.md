**About Us**

I am [Morẹ́nikẹ́ Ẹniọlá Ọláòṣebìkan](https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/kemnet) and I am thrilled to share **KemNet** with you. 😇

KemNet supplies pharmacies with premium reformulations of popular drugs that are more useful for specific groups of patients. For example, we supply reformulated products for erectile dysfunction that work up to 8 times faster than alternatives 🎉. In addition to sexual function, other reformulated products for novel drug delivery are supplied on KemNet including medications for beauty, hair growth, pain, weight loss and women’s health.

We are a Business to Business - “Amazon” for customized medications.

At KemNet we think that empathy is great for patient care so we source and supply customized medications accordingly. Patients adhere to medications that are formulated to work faster, and are enjoyable to use, with reduced side effects. It turns out that empathizing with patients by design is also really good business, because our pharmacy customers’ bottom-line grow as well. I stumbled into these insights around empathy by medication formulation design, as a multi-pharmacy owner.
