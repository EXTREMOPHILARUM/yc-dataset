# Sonnet

> Meeting notes and CRM, automated

Sonnet is an end-to-end meeting assistant, combining a human-quality notetaker with an auto-updating CRM.

Our AI notetaker uses the most powerful AI models to transcribe, summarize, and generate notes for your meeting recordings. With Sonnet, collaboration and recollection is both faster and better. 

Sonnet CRM updates itself automatically using conversation data, so you'll never have to wrangle with CRM fields again. Get up to speed on the context of an entire relationship, in seconds.
