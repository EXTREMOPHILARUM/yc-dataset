# Launches

## SigmaMind AI - Automate customer support with AI 🚀

Hi, we are [Ashish](https://www.linkedin.com/in/agarwalashish101/) and [Pratik](http://www.linkedin.com/in/mundrapratik90/), founders of [**SigmaMind AI**](https://sigmamind.ai/?utm_source=yc&utm_medium=web) - an AI platform to automate customer support for brands and call-centers.

We came to work on this idea when we were ordering food from a food delivery service and had to wait almost 15 minutes to talk to an agent - leaving us frustrated. After talking to E-commerce companies, we realized the problems they faced and decided to find a solution.

**Problem:** E-commerce brands are overwhelmed by the increasing volume of customer inquiries, leading to slow response times, frustrated customers, high support costs, and lost sales.

* Limited support 🚫: Current support models lack 24/7 availability, causing missed inquiries and lost opportunities.
* Delayed responses 🕐: Long wait times and inaccurate information frustrate customers and damage brand perception.
* Inconsistent communication 😕 : New agent training leads to conflicting information and inconsistent brand messaging.
* Inefficiency and High Cost: Agents waste time on basic questions, hindering their ability to solve complex issues and prevent problems.

**Solution:** SigmaMind AI seamlessly integrates with your Shopify store and Gorgias to automatically resolve customer tickets, freeing up your human agents for more complex issues. Our powerful AI can:

* Respond to frequently asked questions about products, orders, shipping, and returns.
* Suggest personalized product recommendations based on individual customer preferences.
* Create subscriptions on Recharge, cancel orders on Shopify, and tag tickets appropriately.
* Close tickets automatically once the issue has been resolved.
* Generate draft responses for human agents, saving them valuable time.
* Learn from your Shopify store, past tickets, FAQs, and other data sources to ensure accurate and personalized support.

**How does it work?**

[Check out this short demo video](https://youtu.be/6RgqHcCJ5Ew?si=PuxtgQyPoGlIcsLm)

SigmaMind AI works within helpdesk tools like Zendesk/Gorgias and needs to be linked to your Shopify store. It has 2 modes - Autopilot and Co-pilot.

* In Autopilot mode, it will automatically send responses to customer tickets, take any action like creating a subscription on Recharge or cancel an order on Shopify, tag the ticket appropriately, and close it. You can set up this custom workflow on the SigmaMind AI dashboard.

  ![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=76739&key=user_uploads/1016312/baebbd2a-79a9-440e-b611-993d3d991f01)

  ![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=76739&key=user_uploads/1016312/4b56ceae-2b59-4be4-b472-7cd3685374c5)

* In Co-pilot mode, AI will suggest response drafts for any incoming ticket which can be used by the support agent to send a response.

  ![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=76739&key=user_uploads/1016312/e2159a15-3bf2-4e93-a0c6-a313a26d82a5)


You can set up the AI brand voice and tone on the SigmaMind AI dashboard to customize the AI for your brand. This enables you you create a truly personalized AI for your brand.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=76739&key=user_uploads/1016312/a3932108-9752-4e73-89be-e49bf4b2ec85)

It tackles frequently asked questions about products, orders, shipping, and returns, freeing up your team for more complex issues.
