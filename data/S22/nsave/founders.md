## Amer Baroudi
**Founder**

Syrian-born entrepreneur and Rhodes Scholar, Amer holds two degrees from the University of Oxford and is a governance, policy, and finance expert. He co-founded award-winning international companies, built properties in Europe, served as the founding president of the Oxford Syria Society, and advised clients ranging from government authorities to a Premier League FC. He is a tireless optimist, an expert problem solver, and, secretly, a music composer.

Amer is co-founder & CEO at nsave.
