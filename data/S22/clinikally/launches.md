## 🤕 The Problem

**The global challenge:** 3 billion people face skin issues, yet 90% can't access dermatologists due to cost and availability barriers.

**India's crisis:**

* 1 [dermatologist](https://clinikally.com/products/video-consult-with-a-skin-hair-specialist?utm_source=yc&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=290525+yc_launch_post) per 100,000 people
* Consultations often require long journeys + weeks of waiting + month's skincare budget in fees
* 70% of Indians live with untreated chronic skin concerns

## 💡 Our Solution

Open Clara's web app, grant camera access to our [AI face scanner](https://clara.clinikally.com/?utm_source=yc&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=290525+yc_launch_post), and take a front-facing photo. Within seconds, receive a plain-language, dermatologist-vetted report covering 14 clinical parameters: acne severity, hydration levels, pigmentation patterns, wrinkle mapping, inflammation hotspots, under-eye health, texture analysis, elasticity readings, and more—metrics typically reserved for expensive clinical imaging.

**What follows:**

* Personalized treatment plans backed by Clinikally's widely popular [skin and haircare marketplace](https://clinikally.com/?utm_source=yc&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=290525+yc_launch_post)
* Ongoing tracking system for monitoring skin progress over time

## 💡 How Clara Works

1. **Capture**: Use our guided photo process with any device
2. **Analyze**: Clara evaluates your skin based on age, gender, and skin type (Dry, Oily, Combination, Sensitive)
3. **Report**: Get detailed skin health breakdown with visual representations and actionable insights
4. **Personalize**: Receive curated product suggestions and complete [skincare routine](https://clara.clinikally.com/?utm_source=yc&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=290525+yc_launch_post)
5. **Track**: Monitor progress with periodic analysis and improvement tips

## 📜 The Science

Clara’s [AI skin analysis](https://clara.clinikally.com/?utm_source=yc&utm_medium=blog&utm_campaign=290525+yc_launch_post) runs entirely on-device for complete privacy. Our deep-facial model identifies 209 landmarks in real-time—even on budget phones in poor lighting. A CNN backbone with frequency-localization layers segments skin, hair, and lips, while transformers capture long-range texture details across all Fitzpatrick skin tones. The result: private inference delivering a comprehensive 14-parameter skin analysis in under five seconds—no images ever leave your device.

* Receive doorstep delivery of a personalized treatment plan

**⚡️ The Problem & Our Mission**

300 million Indians face some skin/hair issue, and there is only 1 dermatologist available for every 200,000. While large numbers of patients often visit major dermatology clinics in cities like Delhi, Mumbai and Bengaluru from far and wide, it becomes cost-prohibitive and inconvenient to consistently follow up in-person.

Moreover, the acceleration of digital health driven by COVID lockdowns has further demonstrated to physicians and patients alike that a growing number of derma conditions can be treated through a combination of telehealth and a preset number of dermatologist-recommended products.

We are democratizing dermatology in India like never before, condition by condition, and shipping solutions that actually work.
