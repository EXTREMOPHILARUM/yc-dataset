# Founders

## Matthew Iommi
**Founder**

Matthew Iommi is the Co-founder and CEO of Fetii, a fast growing rideshare platform that aims to decongest cities and provide a superior transportation experience. Fetii utilizes a network of 15-passenger vans to move groups and businesses together, removing unnecessary vehicles off of the road and reducing congestion. As of 2022, Fetii has transported over 617,000 passengers and completed over 49,000 trips. Matthew earned an Economics and Business degree from Texas A&M University.
