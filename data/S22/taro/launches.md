# Launches

## 🧋 Taro - Faster Career Growth for Software Engineers

**tl;dr**: Your career growth shouldn’t depend on your ability to find a good mentor or manager. Taro brings together top SWEs with proven insights to scale your career. **Check out our** [**website**](https://bit.ly/taro_app_web) **and** [**YouTube channel**](https://bit.ly/rahul_yt)**.**

Taro helps full-time engineers get onboarded and promoted faster. We do this through Q&A and case studies from tech veterans, organized by company and level.

### **⛔️ The Problem**

As a software engineer, you already know how to code. But **software engineering is so much more than coding**.

Over the last 2 years, Alex and I built a community of 50K software engineers on [YouTube](https://youtube.com/rpandey1234), [Slack,](https://join.slack.com/t/techcareergrowth/shared_invite/zt-1ctxuq8h2-b3nnkQG0MtXyBpvrEFpgqQ) and [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/71614174/admin/). We talked to hundreds of engineers and found that their career bottleneck was not their coding ability, but how effectively they managed projects, understood the context, and communicated blockers.

[📱 Alex](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alexander-chiou/) has mentored dozens of engineers to Senior+ levels across his time at Meta and Robinhood.

What separates an average engineer from a great engineer is the ability to independently create business impact, and that doesn’t get taught in a CS degree or a bootcamp. Taro fills the gap.

### **💡 Our Solution**

1. A Q&A database from real engineers, with answers from industry experts. **All questions are tagged by company + level, which makes it easy to find relevant content.** [Here’s a sample](https://app.jointaro.com/question/mvMQj8228kuUe38BvdT9).
2. Case studies from eng leads + managers at top tech companies.
3. A community of software engineers at top companies who share insights + advice.
