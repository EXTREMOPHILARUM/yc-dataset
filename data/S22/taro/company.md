# Taro

> Help high-growth tech companies hire strong engineers

Taro is a community of 150k+ software engineers looking to grow their careers. High-growth tech companies leverage the Taro platform to hire engineers who can grow the company. 

Taro's education platform offers online courses and discussions with leaders at top tech companies. These resources attract many top engineers seeking their next career move.

Taro supports the hiring process at startups through sourcing and feedback on engineers. If you're looking to expand your engineering team quickly, without compromising on quality, book time here: https://www.jointaro.com/enterprise/
