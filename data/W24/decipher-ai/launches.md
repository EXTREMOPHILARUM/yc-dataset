## **Why we built Decipher**

Shipping speed is up, but visibility into _real_ user experience hasn’t kept pace. Teams still burn days—and sometimes miss problems entirely:

* Users hitting confusing UI and running into bugs in core workflows
* Figuring out what exactly people are doing that lead to app errors and how many people are affected
* Scrubbing thousands of sessions to learn why an important new feature isn’t used

The root cause is signal overload—billions of events, little prioritization. We flipped that on its head: let AI watch the firehose, surface what truly matters to adoption, satisfaction and revenue, and automate the busywork after discovery.

---

## **How it works**

* **Discover:** Real‑time monitoring catches hiccups, drop‑offs, errors—or any custom condition you set, no redeploy needed.
* **Investigate:** Ask in plain English and get AI summaries, session clips, and console logs instantly.
* **Act:** One‑click Linear ticket or PR draft with all the context so fixes land before metrics suffer.

A feedback loop on every action continuously teaches the model, dialing down noise while accuracy climbs.
