# Meticulate

> Making it easy for B2B sales teams to send stellar outbound

Meticulate is an AI-native sales platform. We make it easy for sales teams to send stellar outbound messaging at scale. Instead of using boilerplate campaigns, Meticulate codifies your rules and strategies (e.g. “CFOs want to hear an ROI calc upfront”) and combines them with deep research to send custom sequences to every lead. We’re serving leading GTM teams today (incl OpenAI, Noda, ThreeKit), and are backed by leading AI investors: Y Combinator, Link, Cervin Ventures.
