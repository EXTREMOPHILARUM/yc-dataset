## Topo - AI sales agent custom trained for each company to reach the best leads

TL;DR - [Topo](https://www.topo.io/) is an AI sales agent trained specifically for each company. The agent is trained to handle all outbound tasks automatically: finding the best leads in the industry-specific channels and then automatically contacting prospects with a personalized approach.

### **The problem 🤯**

B2B lead generation is broken.

It's difficult to generate outbound pipeline as we did in the past years: mass mailing over the TAM based on demographics criteria.

It's especially very human-intensive for a founder, a full cycle sales team, or even an SDR dedicated to the task.

Finally, the cost of implementing a high-quality outbound strategy has completely exploded.

### **Our Solution 🥳**

AI allows us to automate these tasks by doing like a top performer.

**What makes a top performer?**

* They know the product, the industry, the pain points, and the company's value proposition perfectly.
* They will find the right leads, ready to buy, based on buying signals in the industry-specific channels or on the company's website visitors.

_For example, the buying signals for a dev tool will likely happen more on GitHub, whereas for an HRIS, it will happen on LinkedIn or job boards._

That's what we do: **Create the smartest AI sales agent on the market - custom-trained for each company!**

### **How it works? ✨**

1. Train your agent to become an expert on your business thanks to various knowledge sources of your company (Knowledge base, CRM, Call transcripts…).
2. The agent will then scan the lead sources specific to your industry and start to suggest enriched leads with a personalized approach.
3. In co-pilot mode, you will be able to validate the leads and approaches to train the agent and then switch to autopilot once satisfied with the lead quality.
4. The agent will book meetings in your calendar.

## Topo.io - Create centralized deal hubs to accelerate your closing

**TL;DR** [Topo.io](http://Topo.io) is a Buyer Engagement Platform that helps B2B sellers better engage their buyers to accelerate closing through new ways of collaboration. It pushes the most relevant content to buyers to animate sales cycles, encouraging buy-in while alleviating the sales rep from endless admin and follow-ups.

—

Hello YC 👋

We are thrilled to share [Topo.io](http://Topo.io) with you - a Buyer Engagement Platform that helps B2B sellers better engage buyers to accelerate closing.

🤯 **The Problem**

**The B2B selling process is broken.**

85% of B2B buying decisions happen outside of conversations with the sales rep.

This leaves Sales teams facing:

* longer sales cycles
* lack of momentum
* increasing number of stakeholders
* and difficulty in scaling a proven sales process.

Having a background in B2B sales, we experienced these problems first-hand. Unable to find a tool that could solve these problems, we decided to build one ourselves 💪

⚡️ **The Solution**

Topo is a Buyer Engagement Platform that allows sales reps to keep all of their interactions in one place, facilitating collaboration, increasing engagement, and creating sales motions at scale. It includes the following key features:

* **Digital Sales Room** - Establish a deal hub that sellers can use to engage buyers, pushing them the most relevant content to animate down times during a deal.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=76388&key=user_uploads/1148042/94b18c3c-3319-4ce1-812f-bbf8f8aa0fed)

* **Automations** - Set up workflows to automate the rhythm of your follow-ups: content sharing, follow-up on low-engagement stakeholders, receive action suggestions in Slack…
* **Engagement & Collaboration** - Make the sales cycle more self-service with our mutual action plan, engage all internal and external stakeholders, and enable sales to anticipate what’s coming next in their deal.
* **Analytics** - Observe when, how, and how long your customers/prospects are interacting with your content to better prioritize opportunities via deal scoring.
* **Integrations** - Create a workspace from your CRM (Salesforce & HubSpot), keep your data up-to-date with two-way sync, be informed about the latest buyer activity via Slack, and enrich your workspace with content from your existing tools (Gong, Loom, Google Slide, Youtube, etc.).

🔥**Our customers shortened their sales cycles by 30% just two quarters after implementing Topo**🔥
