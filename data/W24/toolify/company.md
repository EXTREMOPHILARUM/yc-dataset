# Toolify

> Build internal tools with AI

Toolify is dev-first internal tooling. At some point, companies using low-code builders hit a brick wall, and are bogged down by their limitations rather quickly.

We offer:
- A starter kit with sensible defaults,
- A VSCode extension which has AI that creates the internal tool for you, end to end, and,
- A web app where you can deploy your tools.

This gives you the best of both worlds -- everything you need to build tools just as fast as low-code, and an escape hatch when you need it.
