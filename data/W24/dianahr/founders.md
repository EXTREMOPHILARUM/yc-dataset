# Founders

## Upeka Bee
**Founder**

Upeka is the co-founder and CEO of DianaHR. Before DianaHR, she was at Gusto where she was the Head of Engineering of their HR Platform. Her team built the feature set that put Gusto on the map as an HR provider, beyond payroll and benefits. Prior to that she worked at salesforce and several startups. She and her co-founder, Tim Kral, met at Carnegie Mellon, and this is the third time they are working together.
