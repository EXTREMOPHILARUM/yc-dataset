# Latted

> A Unified Editor For AI Video

Latted is an all-in one tool to generate and edit professional AI video projects.

All professional AI video projects take more than a single generation. You need to juggle between hundreds of images and videos and use multiple tools to edit your project.

With Latted, you can generate a 10-minute long video from a single prompt or generate and edit your project from scratch.

Thousands of creators use Latted for marketing videos, content creation and educational content. Create your first project today at latted.com!
