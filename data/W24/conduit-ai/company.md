# Conduit

> Conversational AI agents for Customer Service.

Conduit is the AI agent platform that drives conversational outcomes across support and sales.

Customer conversations are the backbone of every business. Consumer businesses spend nearly a third of their working hours calling and texting customers. Conduit turns those conversations into an engine for conversions, revenue, and cost savings. 

We enable operators across real estate, financials, and service-based businesses, to build and deploy AI agents that perform better than your best employee. Our modular platform enables automation for the most complex, high-volume conversational workflows.
