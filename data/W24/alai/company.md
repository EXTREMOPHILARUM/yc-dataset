# Alai

> Create high quality presentations quickly using AI

Alai makes it easy and quick to create high quality presentations with AI. Our users bring in their context and get a great first draft with 4 options for each slide in minutes. Alai has a responsive canvas made up of high-level build blocks which makes it easy for both humans and AI to iterate together to get to a polished presentation.
