# Founders

## Aman Gour
**Founder**

Aman Gour is the Co-founder/CEO of FurtherAI. 

Previously he co-founded, TurboHire - a platform for recruitment automation, where he was responsible for scaling up the revenue to over $1m in ARR. He has approximately 10 years of experience in developing workflow automation solutions for various industries.

Before that, he worked as a Product Manager at Microsoft after obtaining a degree in computer science from IIT Bombay (India)

## Sashank Gondala
**CTO/Co-founder**

Sashank Gondala is the co-founder and CTO of FurtherAI. Previously, he is a former language modeling scientist at Apple, where he built and scaled language models for Siri. 

Sashank is a Computer Science graduate from IIT Bombay and later did his Masters in Computer Science (Machine Learning) from Georgia Tech where he conducted research in multimodal AI alongside industry-leading scientists.
