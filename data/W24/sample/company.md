# Sample

> Flexible systems for clinical reasoning.

Sample transforms healthcare operations from a burden into a competitive advantage. 

Our users build copilots in Sample to support processes like patient intake and reimbursement. These copilots don't just speed up work – they capture valuable ground-truth data that make our models smarter over time.

We work with leading national providers, already have data from 76% of US oncology clinics, and our network is rapidly expanding across the nation.
