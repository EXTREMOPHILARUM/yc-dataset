# **The Problem: Excel is Powerful but Painful**

Excel remains the universal language of business despite being:

* **Time-consuming**: Building models takes hours of manual work
* **Error-prone**: Manual data entry and complex formulas lead to mistakes
* **Intimidating**: Mastering Excel formulas takes years

Professionals spend countless hours battling spreadsheets instead of focusing on decision-making.

# **Our Solution: Your Personal Excel Intern**

Carousel is an AI assistant that builds spreadsheets & models in minutes, not hours. 

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8_MWw1lMrzQ

We've built four core features:

**🤖 Carousel Chat** - Build complete multi-tab models from scratch with conversational prompts. No Excel expertise required. [Models that took hours to spin up now take minutes.](https://youtu.be/dQJbqbEJIiY)

**⚡ Quick Fix** - Select any range, hit Ctrl+Enter, and describe what you want. [Format tables, add analytics, or restructure data instantly.](https://youtu.be/BeXluYKKArM)

**🔍 Model Walkthrough** - Understand complex formulas in plain English. [Model Walkthrough rewrites formulas by swapping cell references like S&U_Sheet!K7 with Transaction Closing Costs (4%).](https://youtu.be/sG3s4Bz1sM0)

**📄 File Context** - Stop doing manual data entry. [Carousel pulls information from your documents and puts them into your Excel file live, complete with automatic source citations.](https://youtu.be/jDx9lNbgLMk)

Unlike your last intern, Carousel is competent, doesn’t complain when asked to fix spreadsheet formatting, and doesn’t need a return offer.

Carousel is what Microsoft’s Copilot for Excel promised to be.

## 📊 👀 Carousel: Track changes for PowerPoint & Excel

Hi! We’re [Jude](https://linkedin.com/in/jude-rizzo-aa71aa207) & [Daniel](https://linkedin.com/in/daniel-wolf-6242431b1), and we’re the co-creators of [Carousel](https://www.carouseltech.io/), a change-tracking tool for PowerPoint & Excel.

 

**Investment bankers & consultants don’t use PowerPoint like you do:**

For most investment banking or consulting projects, the final deliverable is a PowerPoint deck.

 

To make sure the final draft of that deck is perfect, it goes through multiple rounds of review, feedback, & edits.

 

This means that many bankers and consultants spend hours squinting at different decks to see if the footnote on pg. 57 was updated correctly or if all the companies in a table on pg. 34 have uniform EBITDA adjustments.

 

Small details matter a lot, so teams often have to check over hundred-page decks from scratch. This review process is tedious & prone to mistakes.

 

**Track deck & model changes with one click:**

 

Carousel is a PowerPoint & Excel add-in that captures all the changes made to your team’s materials from shell to client PDF.

 

**Carousel tells you exactly WHO changed WHAT and WHEN.** With one click, the add-in highlights everything that changed between any two versions of the model or deck (in your current window, without having to open another file).
