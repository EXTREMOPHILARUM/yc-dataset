# Carousel Technologies

> AI Assistant for Excel

Carousel is an AI assistant for Excel that builds spreadsheets & models in minutes, not hours.

Carousel can:

- Create & edit Excel models of any complexity, including but not limited to: multi-statement financial models with several tabs, FP&A templates based on raw revenue and cost data, scientific data analyses, and more

- Take in data from PDFs, CSVs, presentations, and images, and build the data into your Excel directly

- Walk you through complex spreadsheet architecture and each cell's precedents and dependents

- Format your models tastefully & professionally

- Build chart dashboards to visualize your data

It's more useful than the last intern you hired.
