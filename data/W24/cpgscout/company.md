# Scout

> AI insights for consumer products

Scout is an AI insights tool for consumer products companies to increase topline revenue. It ingests data from retailer portals and syndicated sources (such as SPINS, NielsenIQ), and tell sales teams exactly where they can grow. Brands use Scout to figure out which accounts to expand, which SKUs to prioritize, and which promotions drove incremental sales.
