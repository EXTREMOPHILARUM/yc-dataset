# Dragoneye

> Custom video detection models in less than 5 minutes

Dragoneye helps devs build their custom video detection models in less than 5 minutes. These models can then be used to power apps and features that use images and videos. No more arduous process of annotating any training data or doing any machine learning work themselves.

Try out our demo today at https://playground.dragoneye.ai/ !
