![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95768&key=user_uploads/818332/51fa21e6-bc13-41ad-8f9e-03947fd50b7a)

# **The Problem:**

Video detection models are incredibly useful. Analyzing security camera footage, ensuring PPE compliance, monitoring production lines. 

But it’s always been hard to build video detection models. Thousands of hours of videos that need to be annotated. You train the model only to find that it doesn’t perform as well as you need it. Really cool ideas that never get to prototype, never mind released in production. Crucial features that take tens of thousands of $$ to trial and months to implement. 

# **Our Solution:**

Our new Playground allows you to build and deploy production-ready video detection models in less than 5 minutes, enabled by our powerful zero-shot models.

To get started:

1. Upload a video
2. Describe the categories of items you want to detect in your video
3. The model streams the predictions and you can preview it on your video
4. Iterate on your categories till you’re set. 

Once you’re happy with your model, you can save it out and it’s live! To make it easier to integrate with your apps, we have libraries for Python and Node.js, with more languages to come.

Check out the Playground at https://playground.dragoneye.ai/ to get started. You can follow our step by step guide below as well!

https://youtu.be/2rhKBO4N7B0

## ❓The problem

Getting data from images today is hard. LMMs allow you to get started quickly but have capability limitations and are slow. On the other hand, building your own vision recognition models requires a ton of time and a ton of expertise.

I learned this firsthand when I tried to build my own fashion outfit**\-**matching app a few years ago. What started as coding a fun consumer app was quickly taken over by the challenges of building custom image models - annotating data, model evaluation, training, deployment, etc. Before long, I realized that I was basically building a computer vision startup - literally!

## ✨ Our solution

Dragoneye enables developers to build and use cutting-edge CV models simply by specifying the model spec.

**Specification → Working Model Automagically:** After specifying the model, Dragoneye’s systems scurries away to find the best implementation strategy and then builds it for you!

**Continuous Improvement:** In the background, Dragoneye constantly keeps the models and tech at the cutting edge so you keep getting the best performance without any work on your end at all.

**Grows with your need:** As your needs grow, we grow with you. Simply update your model specification, and Dragoneye will figure out how to make it work best.
