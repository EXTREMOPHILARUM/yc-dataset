# Circleback

> AI-powered meeting notes and automations

Circleback helps teams get the most out of every conversation with best-in-class AI-powered meeting notes, action items, automations, and search.

For example, Circleback can automatically identify feature requests that come up in product demo calls and create Linear tasks for each of them, or update your CRM with customer details after a sales call.

Before Circleback, Ali led the first integration of LLMs into the Stripe support product and Kevin worked on predictive analytics at Tableau as a senior software engineer.
