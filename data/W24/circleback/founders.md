# Founders

## Ali Haghani
**Founder**

Ali is the co-founder and CEO of Circleback.

Prior to Circleback, Ali was an engineer at Stripe where he led the first integration of LLMs into support experiences, reducing operating costs by over $2M annually. Before that, he worked on ads and experimentation at Twitter, building a system that enabled A/B testing ads campaigns on the platform.
