# Launches

## Terrakotta: Add voice as a channel to grow your business 🗣️

TL;DR: [Terrakotta](https://www.terrakotta.ai/) is a web-phone that uses personalized AI voicemails to double the number of contacts you can engage over the phone. Voicemails left using Terrakotta are personalized to every contact and generate more callbacks than generic scripts.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=79253&key=user_uploads/1032568/e407487e-18cb-4c20-b6ba-0c2257e42252)

👋Hey Everyone! We’re Kus, Vincent, and Gabe (from left to right) and we are the founders of Terrakotta. Terrakotta lets users leverage their unique voice (yes, their literal voice) to grow their business. We’re calling it ‘voice nurture’.

**Problem**

Voicemail is a great growth channel to generate interest and callbacks from leads, but historically it hasn’t scaled well. Leaving voicemails during call sessions is: 

* Time-consuming
* Repetitive
* Tiring

 

**Solution: ☎️⚡️Here’s what a call session looks like in Terrakotta⚡️☎️**

With Terrakotta users can clone their voice, load up a contact list, and set a dynamic AI voicemail script that will be personalized for unanswered calls. From there, users can flip on the auto-dial feature and zoom through their call list, reaching up to 100 contacts/hour.
