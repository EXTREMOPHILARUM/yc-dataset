# Fluently

> Your personal English tutor

Imagine a human-like English tutor, but 20x cheaper and available 24/7 - that's Fluently!

Fluently made for people who already know the basics of English but want to feel truly confident in real conversations: whether that’s in an interview, a business meeting, or chatting with friends.

Most English learning apps are built for beginners and don’t help you improve speaking skills.

With Fluently, you get:
- Unlimited real-world speaking practice
- Personalized learning plan
- Free English level assessment and progress tracking
- Real progress in English speaking skills

We’re non-native founders who know the stress of speaking English in important moments. With deep AI expertise, we built Fluently to solve it.
