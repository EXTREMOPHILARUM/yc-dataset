## InspectMind AI – The AI Checker Agent for Construction

## We’re launching an **AI agent for Construction Drawings Review**. It automatically scans drawings, specs and building codes to catch issues _before_ they turn into rework, delays, lawsuits, and margin erosion.

### **The Problem**

Every construction project is slowed down by drawings mistakes:

* Building code violations
* Dimensions that don’t match between structural, architectural, civil, MEP drawings
* Materials mismatch for doors, windows, cabinets etc.
* Ownership/responsibility confusion

These inconsistencies cause rework, schedule delays, and frustrated clients. They happen on **every project**, and can cost **6-7 figures per job**. 

Humans aren’t the best to check hundreds or thousands of pages.

### **Our Solution**

An AI agent analyzes all project documents, architectural / structural / civil / MEP drawings, project specifications, and building codes, and flag issues like dimension mismatches, materials mismatches, ownership/responsibility confusion, missing info, ADA/code red flags, and uncoordinated addenda.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95694&key=user_uploads/1753502/f4847fca-af87-4839-91bf-d3059e73674d)

It’s simple: you upload your project documents in PDF. DONE. AI will prepare the issues list that is easy to review in our web app.

No workflow change. No training. Just fewer errors and better margins.

We’re already running our agent on Tenant Improvement, K-12, civic, industrial, healthcare, affordable housing, and residential projects.

It recently saved a GC $2M in rework for a $100M project due to a material mismatch.
