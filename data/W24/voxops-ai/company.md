# VoxOps AI

> Voice AI for Automotive

Garages, dealers and lenders receive and make a huge number of calls across the customer journey, covering many uses cases. Customers don't want to be kept waiting and want to be proactively informed with reminders and updates by their service providers. 

VoxOps automates up to 80% of their inbound and outbound calls, whilst automating downstream workflows.
