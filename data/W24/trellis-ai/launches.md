# Launches

## 🌊 Trellis AI: Automate your Pre-sevice workflow⚡️

### **TL;DR**

Trellis helps healthcare providers treat more patients faster—while eliminating pre-service paperwork. We automate document intake, prior authorizations, and appeals at scale to streamline operations and accelerate care.

❌ **Problem — Administrative costs are delaying care and draining revenue**

Over 20% of U.S. healthcare spending goes toward administrative costs, delaying care, reducing revenue, and driving staff burnout—while leaving providers with less visibility into patient care than ever before.

Prior authorizations, appeals, and pre-service documentation are some of the biggest bottlenecks to timely treatment. Manual processing is slow, error-prone, and costly—leading to delayed treatments, increased denial rates, and frustrated patients.

🎉 **Solution**

Our AI agent is trained on millions of clinical data points and converts messy, unstructured documents into clean, structured data directly in your EHR—at scale.

We handle the heavy lifting:

* **Automating document intake** from faxes, PDFs, and scanned forms
* **Processing prior authorizations and appeals** with high accuracy
* **Integrating directly into EHR workflows** so staff can focus on care, not paperwork

Trellis in action: Processing hundreds of pages per patient case to identify missing information, complete submissions, and accelerate treatment approvals.

🏃🏽‍♂️ **Leading healthcare organizations use Trellis to:**

* **Reduce time to treatment by over 90%**—ensuring patients start therapy faster
* **Improve prior authorization approval and reimbursement rates**—reducing revenue leakage
* **Leverage structured data for better clinical decision-making**—enhancing drug program performance and patient outcomes
