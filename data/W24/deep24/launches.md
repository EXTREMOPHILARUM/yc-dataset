**❌ The Problem**

Ambitious students are underserved. A hungry high schooler is capable of so much more than APs and creating fake non-profits to get into a college.\
\
But the guidance is often nothing or directly negative. Schools and counseling departments are already overwhelmed, so they have to focus their advice on the low-performing students. What is possible for a high schooler has also changed so much that a lot of previously sound advice has aged out. \
\
College remains the primary goal for ambitious high schoolers. And the best way to get in is to be shockingly impressive, which also helps later in life, too. But access to guidance on how to make yourself stand out to colleges is expensive (private college consultants) or not well packaged.\
\
[**👷**](https://emojipedia.org/construction-worker) **Our Solution**\
We’ve created an AI advisor that helps students become extraordinary.

1. Firstly, we simulate the process that elite colleges use to evaluate profiles to give students an idea of what their odds are (Naviance but better).
2. Secondly, we give recommendations that students can iterate on. This pulls from a database of 3000+ opportunities, including opportunities exclusive to our platform, like high school-specific internships with venture-backed startups.
3. Finally, we have a 24/7 AI coach that calls users to check in with them and help them plan and reflect.

AI will make the top performers produce a larger and larger proportion of human work. We will help create this next generation of top performers from the most ambitious students of today.\
