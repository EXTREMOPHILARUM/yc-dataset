# Founders

## Rachel Wang
**Founder**

Rachel Wang is the co-founder and CEO of Storio, which she started with Xinyue Liang in 2023. Before founding HeartByte, Rachel was the Chief Data Officer at Wish, a cross border e-commerce marketplace. Rachel graduated from the Columbia University where she received a Master of Data Science and Peking University where she received a Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering. Why does she work on HeartByte? She thinks the world can be a bit boring and needs more imagination.
