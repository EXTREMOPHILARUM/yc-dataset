# Superagent

> Red teaming for AI agents

Superagent helps developers make their AI apps safe.

We offer:
- Red team testing — specialized attack agents probe your production AI for data leaks, harmful outputs, and unwanted actions
- Structured reports with findings, evidence, and remediation guidance
- A Safety Page to prove it to your customers — use it in sales, procurement reviews, and security questionnaires

Built for companies selling AI into enterprises and regulated industries.
