# InQuery

> Intelligent Medical Record Reviews

InQuery is a fully-automated medical record review platform that indexes, labels, and summarizes patient medical records so that physicians and attorneys can understand patient journeys at a glance. 

Our proprietary technology reasons about each record to surface key insights that could make or break your medical legal case.
