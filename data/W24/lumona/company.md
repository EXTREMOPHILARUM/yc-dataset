# Lumona

> AI-enabled search engine featuring insights from social media.

Lumona is an AI-enabled search engine featuring insights from social media. When you’re looking to buy a new phone, instead of adding reddit to the end of your search and opening tabs of threads that take forever to get through, you can search on Lumona to get all of that processed for you on one page so you can make the best decision while saving time.
