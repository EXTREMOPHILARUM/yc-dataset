**The problem: the death loop of customer service**

We’ve all been there: you have a simple problem that you take to customer support, but you’re forced into a never-ending cycle of being passed between different agents and explaining your situation for the (n + 1)th time.

**1. The burden of effort:** If a customer is working harder than the company to solve a problem, the system has failed.

**2. Fragmentation:** You start a chat on your phone, move to your desktop, and have to explain your account number and issue for the third time.

**3. Dumb automation:** You talk with a chatbot that acts as a gatekeeper, not a helper.

**4. Costly, slow onboarding:** Onboarding a new customer support agent brings an expensive and time-consuming burden.

Traditional BPOs (Business Process Outsourcing) try to solve this through headcount in low-cost regions, and rely on manual processes and legacy CRMs that are slow to adapt and prone to error. This approach does not work.

### **The solution: [14.ai](http://14.ai) is the AI-native customer service agency**

We are a full-service agency built from the ground up with AI as the core engine.

#### **1. High-touch concierge**

Excellent service guides everything we do. After our customers hand over their existing integrations, we tell them to stop answering tickets; now, it’s our problem. This level of service extends to our customer’s customers. We offer the best customer service in the world, and we are always working to stay that way.

#### **2. Low constant onboarding**

[14.ai](http://14.ai) goes live within hours. We take away the burden of onboarding new customer support agents and begin answering your tickets immediately. We get our customers to inbox zero on day zero.

#### **3. Agentic resolution**

Most bots just provide “how-to” links. [14.ai](http://14.ai) actually does the work. We plug in to your company’s most complex processes, autonomously verifying purchases, generating shipping labels, and triggering refunds in a single, seamless flow.

#### **4. Outcomes, not tools**

Our AI engineers operate our own highly tailored software for automation. Customers only see the parts they actually need to see: reports, CX metrics, resolved conversations, VIP or high-touch integrations, and the like.

#### **5. The human-AI feedback loop**

Our human team is supercharged, not replaced. We also service our customers around the clock, 24/7.

Our full-time AI engineers are based in San Francisco and are responsible for answering every support ticket, automated or not. Every time we encounter an edge case, that intervention is fed back into our agents - they become smarter every day.

## Markprompt – AI for customer support

Hi YC!

We’re [Marie](https://twitter.com/marieschneegans) and [Michael](https://twitter.com/michaelfester) from [Markprompt.](https://markprompt.com)

We are building AI infrastructure to power customer support at scale. We are live with companies like AngelList, Mindbody, and Plotly.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=76470&key=user_uploads/72181/65f672d8-ad2b-4f01-843a-75653e4010ab)

**Problem**

* **Scaling customer support with headcount is expensive:** Human resources account for 90%+ of customer support expenses.
* **Customer support agents burn out:** Support tasks incur large amounts of manual, repetitive, and ungratifying work that humans don't need to be doing, causing burnout, turnover, and reduced quality.
* **Customer support is more than a chatbot on a website:** In an API-driven world, customer support tools need to evolve with the company’s tech stack. Existing AI-powered solutions are not delivering on the promise, resulting in sub-par customer experience and low NPS and CSAT scores.
* **Company resources are siloed across systems:** As companies grow, so does their expert knowledge. It is hard to access at scale, and keeping employees up-to-date is nearly impossible.

**Solution**

* **Comprehensive data indexing:** Markprompt addresses the root of the information access problem by indexing all available knowledge within a company, setting the foundation for a robust system of record to feed the AI. This includes:
  * Salesforce Knowledge databases
  * Salesforce and Zendesk tickets
  * Notion wikis
  * GitHub repositories, discussions, and issues
  * Company websites, docs, guides, blogs
* **An API-first approach:** Markprompt is built from the ground up with an API-first approach. Every feature, from indexing and processing of data to generating drafts, triaging tickets, creating chat replies, or triggering automations, can be achieved via our APIs. Markprompt also provides SDKs (such as React hooks and components) and no-code tools to enable integrations at all touchpoints in the customer journey.
* **Deep integrations:** Markprompt works well within existing support platforms, such as Zendesk and Salesforce. And with our APIs, it is possible to build custom integrations to accommodate any setup.

**How we help**

* **Efficient ticket deflection:** Deploy AI-driven chatbots and ticket deflection systems that efficiently handle routine queries, allowing support teams to focus on more complex cases.
* **Accelerated case resolution:** Use automated drafts to streamline case resolution, ensuring faster response times based on the up-to-date expert knowledge.
* **Actionable AI agents:** Enable AI agents to perform user actions, such as processing refunds or canceling a subscription, with high accuracy.
