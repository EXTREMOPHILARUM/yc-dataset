# Quivr

> AI-powered auto-resolutions and reply suggestions for customer…

Quivr is an AI-powered customer support tool that helps teams draft responses, automate replies, and resolve tickets directly inside platforms like Zendesk or other helpdesk solutions.

The platform augments agent productivity by reducing repetitive work and accelerating resolution time.
