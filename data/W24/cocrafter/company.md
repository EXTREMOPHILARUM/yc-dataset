# CoCrafter

> B2B construction marketplace to match contractors and subcontractors.

CoCrafter is a construction marketplace to match contractors and subcontractors. Already used by over 3000 companies in the strong German SMB sector, CoCrafter helps contractors to find and manage local as well as foreign subcontractors.
