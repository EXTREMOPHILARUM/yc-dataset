# Clarum

> Investment Due Diligence & Research, Accelerated by AI.

Clarum is an investment due diligence and research platform designed for private equity investors. It connects directly to a firm’s data sources, structures key financial metrics and deal terms, and performs analysis with AI across Excel, Word, and PowerPoint—mirroring the approach of an associate conducting due diligence.

Clarum is trusted by private equity firms, family offices, venture capital groups, and consulting teams. We automate major parts of the diligence workflow, enabling faster insights and better risk coverage.
