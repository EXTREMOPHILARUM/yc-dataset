# Yenmo

> Secured consumer lending in India

Yenmo offers a better way to access cash for India’s 65 million investors who either resort to personal loans at over 18% interest or have to liquidate their investments when in need of money. With Yenmo, they can pledge their investments digitally and instantly get a loan at a fixed 10.5% interest rate.

Our Insight - In India, over 30% of personal loan borrowers have active investments and are paying double the interest rate they qualify for with us.
