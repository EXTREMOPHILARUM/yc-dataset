# Veles

> The Sales Calculator

Are you trying to run a hybrid sales motion, but Stripe is limiting your ability to bill your custom contracts?

Most of us start on Stripe because it’s the default for self-serve. But as soon as you move into Founder-Led Sales, you hit a wall. When an enterprise customer asks for a ramp-up period, tiered usage, or a custom quarterly billing schedule, Stripe’s standard checkout breaks.

You end up with "Billing Debt" - a mess of manual invoices, spreadsheets to track renewals, and "hacked" subscriptions that will eventually require a painful migration.

We built Veles to be the sales-led billing layer for Stripe. It’s the flexible "Deal Desk" that lets you sell exactly how your customer wants to buy, without the manual overhead.

The Problems We Solve (So You Don't Have To):
✅ Complex Pricing & Logic: Whether it’s usage-based, flat-fee hybrids, or "draw-down" credits, if you can dream up the pricing logic, Veles can bill it through your Stripe account.

✅ Custom Deal Structures: Close deals with custom ramp-ups or non-standard terms without manually editing Stripe objects.

✅ Deal Desk & Approvals: Set guardrails so your first sales hires can’t go rogue on pricing. Build a repeatable approval workflow for every contract.

✅ Presentation Flexibility: Your quotes should look as professional as your product. Veles generates quotes as Web Portals, Slides, Docs, or PDFs—all while staying perfectly synced to your backend.
