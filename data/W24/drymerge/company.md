# DryMerge

> AI that updates your CRM for you

We create AI agents that constantly monitor emails, calendar, calls, or wherever else companies have relationship data and organize it into CRM records as meticulously as a great rep would.

We build these agents on top of the DryMerge platform, a tool to build AI agents that integrate with business apps and work in the background.
