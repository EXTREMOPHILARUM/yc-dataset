# Launches

## Voicepanel: User research on autopilot

**TL;DR: Voicepanel is an AI agent that automates user research end-to-end.** Given a simple prompt, Voicepanel conducts conversations with users and shares the learnings in Slack. Our platform enables product teams to continuously gather feedback, discovery usability issues, and test new product concepts without the overhead of traditional research methods.

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-dYtHN1TwuE>

### The problem

As shipping new products becomes easier, collecting quality user feedback is becoming a bottleneck to crafting successful products.

For decades companies have used basic surveys to keep a pulse on their users, but people are tired of them. The best product teams talk to their users regularly, but scheduling interviews for users to weigh in every product decision is an impossible task.

### Our solution

Voicepanel delivers detailed feedback from users over chat, voice, video, or screen share on a continuous or on-demand basis. No survey coding or interview scheduling is required: product teams tell Voicepanel their learning objectives and get back detailed responses & reports in Slack.

### How does it work?

Voicepanel requires setting up one or more intercepts:

1. **Product embed** (currently web apps only; native mobile apps coming soon)
2. **Link sharing** (e.g. by email when user reaches a key milestone)
3. **Research panel** (based on unlimited custom screening & demographic criteria)

From there, product teams simply state their learning objectives. Voicepanel automates the process of recruiting respondents, screening for quality, conducting conversations, distributing incentives, and sharing the responses in real-time.

### **Who is it for?**

Product teams that lack dedicated resources for research.

### **Why do this?**

**Demand for consumer insights is higher than ever, but businesses lack a scalable solution to get answers.**

Traditional surveys can be helpful but provide only surface-level insights. Qualitative research methods (e.g. interviews & focus groups) provide deeper insights but typically involve hiring expensive research agencies or time-consuming manual processes. We think businesses shouldn’t have to compromise: they should be able to gather qualitative insights at the same speed, scale & rigor as a survey.

### **How does it work?**

Voicepanel uses a combination of LLMs, transcription, and voice synthesis to deliver a seamless experience for respondents. We’ve worked hard to ensure that we consistently deliver high-quality insights to our customers by leveraging AI at every step of the process:

* Recruiting & screening respondents based on quality
* Probing respondents deeply while keeping interviews on track
* Synthesizing hours of conversations into themes (illustrated below)

### **Who is it for?**

* **Product managers** who seek rapid user feedback to improve their work
