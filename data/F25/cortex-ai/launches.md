# Launches

## 🦾 Cortex AI — Large-Scale Real-World Data for General-Purpose Robotics

We’re building the world’s most diverse _real-world, real-workplace_ egocentric + robot dataset — where the **real physical world itself** becomes the next training and eval set for embodied AI.\
\
🌍 **The Problem**\
Most robotics datasets today are captured in lab environments or simulated settings — far removed from the **real physical world** where robots actually need to operate.\
\
These datasets helped build the foundation models we have today, but the **next phase** of embodied AI needs to learn directly from the real world — from the noise, diversity, and complexity of real workplaces.\
\
🤖 **The Solution**\
\
Frontier labs use **Cortex AI** through 3 stages:\
1️⃣ **Get real-world egocentric data** → for world model + robotics foundation model pretraining\
2️⃣ **Collect real robot data** in real workplaces\
3️⃣ **Roll out policies** across real-world environments → the best evals happen in the real world, with our operators providing human-in-the-loop support to capture recovery data\
