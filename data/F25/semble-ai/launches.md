## Semble AI: Automate Building-System Design

Semble AI automates building-system design for construction companies.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F-0XdMA9azY

It used to take billions of dollars and thousands of hours of manual drafting, building code lookups, and back-and-forth with permitting authorities. With Semble AI, it takes 5 minutes.

Instantly generate code-compliant device layouts, bills of materials, project cost estimates, and proposals. No spreadsheets, no painstaking pdf editing, no hidden costs. Just ready-to-submit design specs.

**🔥** Semble AI is the new standard for building-system design. **🔥**
