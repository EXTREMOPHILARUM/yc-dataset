# Thesis

> An AI lab at your fingertips.

Our aim is to help uncover the next AlphaFold or build the next Transformer, faster, more systematically, and at scale. We treat discovery not as a matter of luck, but as an engineering challenge to be solved.

Thesis lets anyone build powerful machine learning models for anything they can imagine. The mission is to democratize invention by putting machine intelligence in everyone’s hands.

Every major breakthrough in the next decade, in science, industry, or AI, will be built on systems like Thesis. 

We intend to define that category.
