# Launches

## Lexi: AI Associates for Law Firms & Legal Teams

Hi everyone! We’re [Harshit](https://www.linkedin.com/in/harshitgarg03/) and [Kiran](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kiranmohanb/), and we’re building Lexi - AI Associates for law firms & legal teams

### **TL;DR**

Law firms & legal departments spend countless hours drafting, reviewing, organizing, and managing documents.

Unlike other LegalTech tools, Lexi integrates directly into your workflow, acting as an AI associate that learns your team’s standards, tone, and processes to handle your workflows end to end.

**Lexi saves lawyers 10+ hours of manual work every single week.**

[Lexi Launch Video](https://youtu.be/3LSrawsmCfI)

### 🔴 **The Problem**

Lawyers are drowning in low-leverage work.

70% of a lawyer's day is spent on tasks that don't require legal expertise: summarizing documents, drafting routine correspondence, building chronologies, organizing files.

Existing tools don't solve this—they're point solutions that force lawyers to juggle between multiple platforms and subscriptions, slowing them down instead of speeding them up.

The result? Clients wait for updates, strategy gets postponed, and burnout rises. Even top-tier firms struggle to scale without adding headcount.

---

### 🟢 **The Solution**

Lexi doesn't just "help" with legal work—it does the work. From intake to delivery, Lexi acts as a trained associate that learns your firm's style, standards, and processes.

**What used to take 8 hours now takes 20 minutes.**

**Document Intelligence**\
Transform scattered emails, PDFs, and transcripts into organized, searchable case files. Analyze thousands of documents and extract key information with reliable accuracy.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95269&key=user_uploads/2650346/de522fac-c1ec-4375-a762-74e096a803f6)

**Drafting**\
Generate polished first drafts of pleadings, contracts, and letters in your firm's voice. Lexi learns your writing style and standards to produce documents that need minimal editing.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95269&key=user_uploads/2650346/f2be1518-b09f-4069-8f0f-7d2f4b693fdc)

**Contract Review**\
Review agreements against your firm's playbooks and flag risky clauses automatically. Lexi learns from your past reviews to improve recommendations and match your standards over time.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95269&key=user_uploads/2650346/c5c1a104-ed80-4e30-8a1d-1111270646a7)

**Chronology Builder**\
Build comprehensive timelines from thousands of documents. Extract dates, facts, conflicts, and citations instantly—perfect for depositions or trial prep.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95269&key=user_uploads/2650346/6b475d7c-a3f7-45c6-90d3-35fe6c15124f)

The best part: Lexi **Integrates seamlessly** with the tools you use daily: Microsoft Word, Outlook, Google Docs, iManage, and more. No new systems to learn. No workflow disruption.

**The impact?** Firms using Lexi take on **25% more cases without new hires**—reclaiming 10+ hours per lawyer per week.
