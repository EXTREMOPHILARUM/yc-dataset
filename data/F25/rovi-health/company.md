# Rovi Health

> AI healthcare concierge for employees

We deliver high-quality, cost-effective healthcare to employees through text. Employers cut 10–20% of healthcare spend. Employees get concierge care that improves outcomes.

Our AI agents analyze clinical data to identify care needs. We treat common conditions through our integrated virtual clinic. For more complex care, our agents find the best in-network providers using millions of price and quality data points, and handle end-to-end appointment booking.
