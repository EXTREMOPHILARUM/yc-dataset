# Launches

## 🎥 Aspect: AI platform for enterprise media content

Hey! We’re Edison, Gurish, and Bright - founders of [Aspect](https://aspect.inc) 🎥 💥

### **TL;DR**

We’ve spent **10,000+ hours** with in-house content teams as the founding members of a media asset management company, learning every broken part of their workflow. Post-production supervisors and assistants still have to organize folders, check revisions, comb through old content, and update data across project management tools, not to mention constant upkeep with clients. These are just a few of the manual tasks keeping creatives from doing what they love.

**Aspect takes care of everything and more.**

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1EwHnYKPWBo

### What Aspect Does:

* Ingests media from traditional post-production storage apps and NAS storage
* Crawls every frame of video to create a humanlike understanding of whole libraries
* Connects to other business tools to keep data in sync without human intervention
* Searches for second-accurate clips, assembles rough cuts, reviews footage, creates folder trees - anything a post-production expert can do, Aspect can do

Aspect is the single source of truth from ingest, to rough cut, and final delivery, with agents you can send off to do the mundane work you hate.

### Aspect is the Future of media Production:

Previous waves of media-tech only focused on simplifying media retrieval with brittle tagging and non-actionable semantic search. The reality is that searching for files is just a fraction of the time wasted by post-production teams. Aspect is built on agents with humanlike **visual-temporal understanding**, so they _go beyond_ _just search_ - they work with video like any post-production teammate and can scale infinitely.
