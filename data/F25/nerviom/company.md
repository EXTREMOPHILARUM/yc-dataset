# Nerviom

> AI Autopilot for Infrastructure Permitting

Infrastructure projects like data centers, factories, and energy facilities require navigating hundreds of permits and regulatory approvals before construction can begin. 

Today this process is handled through consultants, spreadsheets, and thousands of pages of documentation, adding years to project timelines and millions in consulting costs.

We are building an AI autopilot to automate infrastructure permitting and regulatory compliance. 

Our goal is to significantly reduce the time and cost required to get infrastructure projects approved and built.
