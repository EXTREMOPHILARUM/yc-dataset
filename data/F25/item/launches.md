# Launches

## item: The AI-Native CRM that works for you

**Hey there 👋 we’re Akshay and Andrés, and we’re building _item._**

Launch video: https://youtu.be/x8Z5QZNjdWw

\
**In a nutshell:**

_item_ is the first agentic CRM, a system that does the work. A system that understands your business the way your best employee does, and works for you 24/7.

If you’re building the future and still running on a 10-year-old CRM, let us show you what it feels like to run autonomously → [item.app](http://item.app)

**Meet Item**

_item_ connects to all your customer tools, gathers and enriches context automatically, and becomes your company’s **source of truth** — one place where your team and your AI agents share the full picture to act autonomously.

At the center is the _Assistant_ — your sidekick for daily work. It can send follow-ups, research leads, or add deals with a simple prompt. No complex UI, drop-downs or fields, simply ask.

For repeatable work, you build _Agents_ that you can train like new hires, simply by writing documents that describe your processes. item Agents run business processes across sales, support, and account management autonomously.

\
**The Status Quo**

AI has changed nearly every part of work. Yet the system at the center, the CRM, hasn’t evolved. It’s manual, data incomplete, and still takes trained experts to setup and automate.

We believe a CRM should know everything about your company and be able to do human-level work on your behalf, just like your best employee would.

**How companies use item Agents today to run autonomously:**

* Outbound Sales: finding and contacting qualified leads on email and LinkedIn
* Inbound Management: qualifying and responding to leads that sign up to a waitlist or a self-serve product
* Growing Existing Accounts: tracking customer data and automatically sending personalized upsell sequences based on usage
* Keeping Pipeline Warm: setting up item to auto-update deals from emails and meetings
