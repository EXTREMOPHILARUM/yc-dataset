# ❌ The Problem

Most growing enterprises still process invoices manually—hiring AP clerks to comb through inboxes, type up documents, and chase approvals. As volume grows, they scramble to add more headcount or outsource to expensive temps.

This creates three major pains:

1. **Cash flow blindness** – No visibility until month-end close
2. **Unscalable manual work** – More invoices means more clerks (highest-turnover role in finance)
3. **Vendor relationship damage** – Late payments and errors erode trust

We talked to 23 CFOs last month—nearly all said their AP process couldn't scale.

# 👷 Our Solution

Mod AI deploys AI agents that handle your accounts payable process end-to-end—from the moment an invoice arrives to the moment it's paid and synced to your books. Companies simply connect their systems and instantly get:

* **Complete invoice capture** 📥**:** Auto-pull from email, supplier portals, EDI, physical mail
* **Intelligent processing** 🧠**:** 97%+ accurate GL coding, 2-way/3-way matching, fraud detection
* **Seamless approval & payment** ✅**:** Custom workflows, one-click approvals, direct sync to accounting software

**Result:** Real-time spend visibility, 10x invoice volume without hiring, and stronger vendor relationships.
