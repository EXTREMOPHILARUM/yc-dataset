# Karumi

> The Waymo of Personalized demos

Karumi is an AI agent that joins a live video call with your prospects to deliver personalized product demos, 24/7, in any language.

It bridges the gap between your landing page and your sales team by engaging visitors at the peak of their interest, turning curiosity into conversion. For those not ready to talk to sales but eager to explore, Karumi offers the next generation of interactive, self-guided demos.
