# Iris

> The Digital Extension of Yourself

Iris observes how you handle your day - how you reply, decide, prioritize - and prepares the next set of actions. Our key insight: if you model real behavior, you can replicate judgment, not just tasks using personal agents and knowledge graphs. Iris knows what needs your attention across your accounts, pre-fills the actions, and you can simply swipe right to hand it off to your agent.

We started with connecting calendars and emails. We launched four weeks ago. Over 1,200 people have downloaded Iris, 168 use it every day, and they’ve taken more than 36,000 actions so far growing 48% week over week.

In the future, everyone will have a personal proxy running their digital life with near perfect accuracy. Iris is the first real version of that future.
