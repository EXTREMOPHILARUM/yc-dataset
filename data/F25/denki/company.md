# Denki

> The full-stack AI financial audit firm

Automate internal auditing (planning, testing, documenting) to comply better with financial regulations.

Denki performs audit work with 99% software, 1% services:

✔️ Works on existing systems (e.g. Auditboard, Workiva, ERPs)
✔️ Automates Control processes (e.g. collects evidence, runs walkthrough interviews, tests)
✔️ Records every step for traceability (workpapers w/ full audit trace)

Focused on SOX programs and financial regulations (SEC Rules etc.)
