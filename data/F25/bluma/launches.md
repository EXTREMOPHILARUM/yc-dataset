**Problem**

If you’re building in consumer, you know that it is super hard to get eyes on your product.

In a post-AI world, attention is the most valuable commodity. Social media is only growing faster and faster, and we're still early.

Short form has a high barrier of entry due to cost (UGC costs $35/video + $1 CPM, editors are $50/video) and operations as shown below:

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95662&key=user_uploads/2724981/ade8a7fd-4114-4164-ae5c-f0b389922684)

**Solution**

Instead of manually assembling a video in CapCut, Bluma works like this:

1. Create template format by chatting with AI
2. Generate videos on autopilot
3. Push traffic to product/service

Our biggest client runs 8 templates across 16 accounts, posting 2x daily. Bluma is growing 100% w/w and went from 0 to 28k MRR in 4 weeks.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95662&key=user_uploads/2724981/c5fd4e08-2eb3-4c12-80ea-bcf74db92c92)

Using templates, we can replicate proven formats and A/B test scripts for you cheaper, faster and more scalable than human creators (UGC, editors).\
