## Spatial AI: Large datasets for robot foundation models

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95567&key=user_uploads/2367289/61a41a40-748f-4ef4-8196-4b5ca76ef86d)

**Spatial AI** ([**https://spatial-ai.com/**](https://spatial-ai.com/)) is building large datasets for robot foundation models.

**Open Source**: [**https://huggingface.co/datasets/spatial-ai/sea-small**](https://huggingface.co/datasets/spatial-ai/sea-small)

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95567&key=user_uploads/2367289/5313b99e-aa0b-46d4-b4d4-cd9983bedecd)

**The Problem:**

The biggest obstacle to bringing robots into everyday life is the software. We need far more capable models to control general-purpose robots effectively and enable them to carry out tasks in the physical world.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95567&key=user_uploads/2367289/7a226006-47e9-4c89-bcd2-b09ce89cb241)

**The Solution:**

To train capable models, we need vast amounts of high-quality data. Our first dataset SEA (Spatial Everyday Activities) is the largest well-curated egocentric dataset of people performing tasks.
