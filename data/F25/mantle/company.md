# Mantle

> Build internal agents to automate back office work with one prompt

Mantle connects to all your tools: your CRM, Mail, Calendar, Payments, Product Analytics, and more. With just one prompt, you can create agents that handle back office work for you.

These agents can automate reports  and email them to you, drafts emails right in your email client, generates pre-meeting research directly in your calendar event, and much more. They run quietly in the background, react to changes in real time, follow a schedule or work when you chat with them. 

You didn’t start up to stay stuck in admin. Turn your company AI-native with Mantle.
