# Zavo

> AI Point of Sale for Restaurants 

Zavo is the first AI-point of sale platform for restaurants.

We unify payments, point of sale, and AI agents into one seamless platform, allowing business to accept payments, manage operations and automate manual processes with Ai. 

From reservations, to payments to point of sale, we have built the full restaurant tech stack in-house with AI automation across all products.

We started Zavo in May 2025, currently over 400 businesses of different sizes use Zavo to accept payments and manage operations
