**Why It Matters**

Perimenopause is when women begin to experience hormonal changes and symptoms of menopause such as brain fog, mood shifts, and hot flashes, while still having their period. It can start in the **mid-30s** and last **10+ years**.

Despite impacting **30M+ women in the U.S. alone**, perimenopause remains one of the most **overlooked stages in women’s health**. The science is complex, the data is sparse, and existing solutions are either **t**oo generic or too narrow. Women are left navigating life-altering symptoms with little guidance and few tools.

Perimenopause care today is where fertility care was a decade ago: under-researched, under-funded, and urgently in need of innovation. Play Health is changing that.
