# Launches

## Nori: Personal health coach in your pocket

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=94783&key=user_uploads/156882/2070df36-afa6-4a71-87e7-1398758caed3)

**I wrecked my body** over six years building my last startup, then spent six months obsessively fixing it… juggling five different apps, spreadsheets, counting every calorie.

Two weeks into my new company, I could feel it happening again. Staying healthy was becoming a second full-time job. 

**That’s why we built Nori.**

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kUbvmqdwqSA

\
**What it is:** All your health data in one place—Apple Health, Oura, Whoop, Peloton, nutrition apps, Garmin, MyChart, blood tests from Function Health, Superpower—all feeding your personal health AI.

**Who it’s for:** People who want weight loss, better energy, or faster recovery—starting with iOS users

**Real examples from my own data:**

* I got stronger when I added rest days instead of grinding 6-7 workouts weekly
* Magnesium at bedtime increased my REM sleep by 25%
* When I miss a workout, Nori tells me exactly how to adjust
