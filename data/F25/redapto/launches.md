# **The Problem:**

* 33% of customers churn after one bad support experience
* Most QA teams review less than 5% of conversations, missing SOP gaps, and hallucinations
* Product velocity slows down because reps are not trained fast enough and knowledge bases fall behind
* Outdated sources lead to bad answers that frustrate customers and erode trust

# **The Solution:**

Redapto runs checks on 100% of your interactions across chat, voice, and email allowing you to:

* Detect quality issues, hallucinations, and knowledge gaps in real time
* Include custom evals tailored to your brand tone, policies, compliance rules, and business goals
* Automatically design training materials, update SOPs, and knowledge bases, keeping your team on the same page
* Run agent simulations to test teams and humans before launch
