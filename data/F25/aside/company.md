# Aside

> Live answer copilot for enterprise tech sales

Aside listens to your sales calls and surfaces real-time answers pulled from your docs, HubSpot, or past calls. It's like pressing 𝚌𝚝𝚛𝚕+𝚟 on your company’s knowledge into every rep – so anyone can sound like your best rep.

- 🔎 Shows answers from your docs, Slack, and past winning calls
- 📝 Live coaching that catches what reps miss: Aside spots what reps skip during calls and guides every rep to apply your playbook - BANT, SPICED, or your own - not after, live.
- 💬 Preload your cheatsheets as Live Cards: Turn your playbooks, FAQs, and notes into instant, in-call cards.
- 🎓 Coaching feedback, remembered by AI: Aside reviews every call, gives feedback, and turns it into live suggestions for your next one - so you never repeat the same mistake.
