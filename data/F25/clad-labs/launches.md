**The problem:**

AI coding creates a time span isn't long enough to do something new, and it's not short enough to be entirely negligible. Developers are spending time off the IDE to fill the inference time gap (usually brain-rotting on their phones)

* **1-5 minute wasted downtime between prompts**
* **People often forget to come back to their code**
* **Context-switching builds mental fatigue → decreased efficiency**

**CHAD:**

Chad aims to manage your AI coding inference time effectively by integrating the modern workflow into a single IDE. Specifically, you can now ⬇️

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=94891&key=user_uploads/2989207/01f277eb-4914-40e1-85ec-5874461efd1e)

* **shit-post on X**
* **scroll Instagram/Tiktok**
* **shoot Stake craps**
* **swipe Tinder, etc.**

directly from your coding agent orchestration workflow.

Chad ends your brainrot session when it's time to get back to work. No more losing track of time.

Login with existing Claude Code and integrate your brainrot workflow.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=94891&key=user_uploads/2989207/de016cec-c3a1-4feb-b363-69c412a15cb5)

In our early beta users, we've seen an average of 15 minutes saved per hour vibe coding when users switch over to Chad. (not a research study - a survey and anecdotal results from watching beta users code on their setup vs with Chad).
