# Founders

## Jonathan Miranda
**Founder**

Jonathan is co-founder and CEO of Compyle.

He studied Computer Science and Economics at Johns Hopkins University, where he led multiple AI research projects for the CS department and developed snake robots. He also founded Hopkins Deliveries, a campus food delivery service that built cost-effective tele-operated delivery robots. 

Before Compyle, Jonathan was founding engineer #4 at Hadrius (YC W23), where he built core AI systems powering compliance at thousands of businesses today.
