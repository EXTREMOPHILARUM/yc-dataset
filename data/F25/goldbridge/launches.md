**THE PROBLEM**

Over **$1 trillion** **of rent** flows through landlords’ bank accounts every year, and nearly 25% of it sits idle in reserves and deposits. In other words, **real estate banking is fundamentally broken**:

* Traditional banks don’t understand real estate cashflows
* Property management systems don’t handle money movement, treasury, or yield
* Operators juggle multiple bank accounts, spreadsheets, and manual reconciliations
* Billions are lost every year in missed yield, overpayments, and operational inefficiencies

This is one of the largest underserved financial categories in the country.
