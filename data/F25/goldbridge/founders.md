# Founders

## Alvin Salehi
**Founder**

Alvin Salehi is a 2x YC founder and CEO of Goldbridge, the all-in-one financial platform for real estate owners. He's also the co-founder of Shef, a Series B marketplace that enables anyone to cook and sell food from home. Salehi previously served as Senior Technology Advisor at the White House, where he authored the country’s first federal open source software policy and spearheaded its regulatory enforcement. Salehi also co-founded Code.gov and is a Venture Partner at NextGen VP.

## Greg Rami
**Founder**
