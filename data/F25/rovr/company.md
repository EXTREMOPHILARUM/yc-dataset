# Rovr

> AI FDE Engine that runs your engineering workflows

Rovr streamlines engineering operations by automating intake, triage, routing, and cross-team coordination. It replaces internal forms, manual Slack triage, and scattered docs with an AI front desk that understands requests, assigns owners, updates context, and posts status changes directly into Slack. Rovr’s built-in ops CRM gives teams a single system of record for eng requests — from tooling access to platform incidents to dev-infra workflows — reducing operational drag across the entire org.
