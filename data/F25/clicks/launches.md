# Launches

## Clicks: Back-office automation for recruiting and exec search firms

**TL;DR**

Clicks builds AI agents that automate back-office work for recruiting and exec search firms—saving customers 60% on costs while reducing time-to-close per search.

Our agents click and type in your existing software (Bullhorn, Salesforce, custom systems) just like a human would, but faster and error-free. No coding, no integrations, no IT involvement.

---

**The Problem**

Recruiters waste 30-40% of their time on soul-crushing admin work:

* 20 minutes updating the CRM after every call
* 1-3 hours generating assessment reports
* Hours researching candidates across LinkedIn, company websites, and internal databases

---

**How Clicks Works**

1. **Show us your workflow** – 30-60 minute screen share
2. **We automate it** – Built in under a week by our team
3. **Delegate via email or Slack** – Send tasks to Clicks like you'd delegate to an employee
4. **Get results back** – Pay only for completed tasks

Clicks works with everything: modern software, legacy systems, custom-built tools.

**Our Story**

Dominik and Oli met 8 years ago while studying at ETH. Both started their careers in consulting. While Dominik consulted globally leading enterprises at BCG, Oli was building 15+ custom AI automations at his first startup. This is where Oli had a key realization: Instead of spending months to building a custom automations and IT integrations, it should only take a couple of hours to building an automation on top of the tools that humans already use. 

That's how Clicks was born. The most intuitive and reliable way to build automations on top of your existing tools.
