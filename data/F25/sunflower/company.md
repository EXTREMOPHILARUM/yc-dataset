# Sunflower

> Building the Sobriety Platform for the future of Superintelligence. 🌻

Sunflower is on a mission to create one trillion days sober. Our product is both a consumer app (think a "Duolingo for addiction") but also with a tele-therapy clinic inside. 

Consumer App: 
- We built the #1 AI Sponsor for any kind of addiction
- Beautiful visual progression tracking to power the core "task to be done" of addiction
- A twitter style social media for addicts 
- DIY learn content (think Masterclass for addiction) for all major addiction types 
- CBT based journaling exercises 

Tele-Therapy: 
- Think a "Early Medical Peter Attia" quality of clinic, but completely focused on addiction 
- Starting with talk therapy in CA & TX we're rolling out to all 50 states + MAT 
- Bringing together the worlds best doctors to create the most effective protocol for addiction 

Sunflower went from 0 - 100k MAU in less than 6 months, and now we're continuing the traction to create the #1 product for addiction for the 1.2B humans on this planet that struggle with this fundamental human problem.
