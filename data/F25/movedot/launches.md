# ❌ The Problem

Every engineer says the same thing:

> “Too much data, not enough time.”

Data collection has grown 10x, but the **same human brain** still has to make sense of it. Engineers spend days digging through spreadsheets and KPIs to find what failed. Or more realistically, they give up, leaving most data unused.

# ⚙️ The Solution

We built **MOVEdot**, an AI agent platform that allows you to use 10x more data to make decisions. It connects to data, video, and test standards, automating reports or answering questions like:

> _When did temperature spikes cause mechanical failures, and what were early signs?_

Agents process the data and produce interactive dashboards and mind maps.

# 🌍 The Vision

Physical-world testing is a **$250B market** across development, durability, and validation. Our goal: give every engineering team **thousands of AI teammates** that helps them use **100%** of their data, increasing performance and shortening time to market. From racing to robotics. From proving grounds to production lines.
