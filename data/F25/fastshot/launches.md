### The Problem

The world runs on mobile. Yet over half of businesses still don’t have a mobile app.

Why? Because building one is still seen as a luxury — expensive, complex, and slow.

Today, users have three options:

1. Hire a Mobile Development Agency\
   Agencies handle everything: from design to app store launch.\
   But at a steep cost: $60K–$120K for an MVP, taking 3–6 months before the first users see it.
2. Build an In-House Team\
   Hiring a developer costs about $130K/year, not including design, backend, and QA.\
   Expect similar timelines: 3–6 months to launch.
3. Use traditional No-Code Builders (Adalo, Bubble, etc.)\
   These platforms promise simplicity - but fail to deliver real scalability, design quality, and functionality.
