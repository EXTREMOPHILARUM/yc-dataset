## AnswerThis: The AI Research Assistant

Hello YC! We’re [**Ayush Garg**](https://linkedin.com/in/ayush4921) and [**Ryan Mccarroll**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ryan-mccarroll-/) from [**AnswerThis**](https://answerthis.io/)**.**

**TL;DR**

AnswerThis analyzes all the relevant research in your field to answer complex scientific questions with line-by-line citations. Over 250,000 researchers have saved months on their literature reviews using AnswerThis, making it one of the fastest-growing apps in the research space.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nKTS2QWnjqw

**Problem**

Over 5 million research papers are published every year. Researchers spend months crawling through academic databases to conduct their literature reviews. By the time they finish, new literature has often been published.

When they finally have their literature collected, they must read dense papers while taking detailed notes. If they want to explore a new aspect of the topic, they have to repeat the process, making it difficult to advance science quickly.

Once they have their notes, synthesizing the findings into a detailed literature review requires precision, highly accurate citations, and concise but informative scientific writing. 

This entire process is extremely time consuming, and takes away from actual experimentation. 

**Solution**

Enter your question, select your filters, and AnswerThis searches multiple academic databases to answer it with exact citations to every relevant paper. The literature review that took months, now takes minutes.

You can save the sources, analyze them, and write with our AI assistant on a single platform while collaborating with your team. 

We also integrate with tools researchers already use, like Zotero and Mendeley, and we offer advanced exports that correctly carry over citations and equations.\
