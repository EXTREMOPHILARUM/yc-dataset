### **The problem**

Work today happens across a maze of apps. Slack for messaging. Linear for tickets. Gmail for communication. Calendar for scheduling. Every task requires jumping between tabs and tools.

Even with AI everywhere, we are still using the same old interfaces. Voice assistants like Siri or Alexa can answer trivia but cannot do real work. They do not understand what is on your screen, what app you are in, or what you mean when you say “follow up on this.”

The result is constant context switching, lost flow, and hours wasted on work that should happen automatically.

### **What we are building**

Caddy lives on your desktop and lets you operate your computer with your voice. It has two simple modes.

**Action mode:** You speak your intent, and Caddy takes action.

Ask Caddy to create a ticket in Linear, send a message in Slack, or schedule a meeting in Calendar. Caddy sees what is on your screen and connects it to your apps, so you can stay focused on the work that matters.

**Dictation mode:** Never type again

Write emails, respond to messages, or leave comments anywhere text input exists. It is a direct replacement for typing that works globally across your computer.

Together, these two modes let you automate work and control your computer completely with your voice.
