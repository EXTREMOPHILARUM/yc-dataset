# **The Problem**

Filmmakers, musicians, and creators spend **weeks to months scripting, shooting, and editing** or pay **$100,000+** to agencies to get something that feels right.\
\
Even with new AI tools, you’re still wrestling with prompting, uncanny performances, and inconsistent characters.

# **The Solution**

Koyal lets you speak your story once, and watch it come to life.\
We capture the **emotional tone** in your voice or script and translate it into expressive, cinematic performances with consistent characters, settings and storylines.

You can **storyboard, reshoot, and refine** each scene naturally, like directing your own crew: no complex prompts, no model wrangling, no editors needed.\
\
Just your voice, your script, and your vision.

# **How Koyal Works**

[Demo Video](https://youtu.be/6Slhq8fbQ1c?si=9OaAU2xVKzhEbre8)

* **Upload your script or record your voiceover**: Koyal understands the emotion, pacing, and intent behind every line.
* **Design your characters**: Create an AI character or safely place yourself in the film using **CHARCHA: our Character Captcha protocol** that ensures your likeness is used **with verified consent.**

  ![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=94569&key=user_uploads/2383496/0f411243-c4be-4b15-af57-b5ae3f957e2d)

* **Craft your world:** Create or find real-life locations to shoot your movie in, add assets and choose art styles (animated, realistic, etc.)
* **Auto-generate cinematic scenes**: complete with consistent characters, environments, and shot logic.
* **Storyboard and edit visually**: adjust lighting, camera angle, or emotion directly with simple directions like “make it more hopeful” or “change the setting to night.”
* **Export your finished film**: ready to share across YouTube, socials, or investor decks.
