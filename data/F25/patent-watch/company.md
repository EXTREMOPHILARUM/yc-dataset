# Patent Watch

> AI for detecting patent infringements

We automate patent infringement detection. Our software interprets patents, identifies competing products, and generates claim charts showing who’s infringing and how.

Teams use us to:

- Find licensing and litigation targets
- Send cease-and-desist letters
- Find counter litigation
- Sell IP in wind-downs
- Accelerate M&A diligence

We also run AI invalidity and prior-art searches to help your patents hold up in court.

Companies spend up to $50k to file and maintain a single patent over its lifetime, but once it's granted, they let it collect dust. With Patent Watch, you can start turning your patent portfolio into revenue in 10 minutes.
