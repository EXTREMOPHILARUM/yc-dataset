# Launches

## Patent Watch - AI for patent infringements 📜

[**Patent Watch Launch Video**](https://youtu.be/9rLBsb_cX6E)

**Problem**

My co-founder, [**Andreas Stroe**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/andreasstroe) (also my brother), was doing research at Philips. He applied for 4 patents while he was there. After spending way too much time talking to the IP team, he realized that they hold 50,000 patents with a team of \~10 people to defend them.

A little background: a **patent costs** around **$50k** to obtain and maintain. $17k is non-negotiable, paid directly in fees. That means their **portfolio costs $850M** to **$2.5B**. The best part is that they, and pretty much every company in the world, rely on Googling competitors and sending salespeople to trade shows to hear about new products that could be infringing. This whole **portfolio** is **making** little to **no revenue.**

I guess you can see why we started building this right away.

**Solution**

Super simplified to keep it easy to understand:

✅ We let companies upload their patents and get a list of claim charts showing what **products are infringing and how**, in 20 minutes instead of months.

✅ When companies get sued, they can **use us to find counter litigation**, since the person suing them is most likely infringing on some of the patents they hold too.

✅ The most common way to get these cases thrown out of court is by considering a patent invalid. That means there is some proof, like other patents, research papers, or products that existed before your patent was filed. We find all these and **confirm your patents will hold up in court.**

There is more, but you can consider this our “core”.

**Proof**

As you can imagine, pretty much everyone we work with has us under NDA. What I can tell you is that we work with private inventors up to Fortune and Global 500 companies.

**Why now?**

Of course, AI …

But also, the US Patent Office is tightening the rules for challenging patents in its review court. The director is being pickier about which cases get heard, and some evidence no longer counts.

🚨 It’s going to be a lot harder to attack a patent and a lot easier for owners to defend one🚨
