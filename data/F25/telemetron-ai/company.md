# Telemetron

> Support and Operations Platform for Hardware Companies

Telemetron is an AI-powered support platform tailored for hardware products (medical devices, consumer electronics, industrial hardware, robotics, and more). 

Our platform unifies ticketing, device diagnostics, order tracking, and product documentation into one intelligent system. Our AI agents connect directly to devices, APIs, and backend systems to deliver faster resolutions, smarter routing, and better support experiences.
