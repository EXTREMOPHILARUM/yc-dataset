## Fernstone: AI-powered insurance brokerage for businesses

**The Problem:** 

Every business buys insurance at some point. 

But, the process of finding coverage remains frustratingly analog. Insurance brokers call, email, and even fax carriers for quotes. That back and forth is both manual and slow. 

One customer told us he waited 6 weeks for his broker to find him construction insurance. In that time, he lost a major deal. 

**The Solution:** 

https://youtu.be/34X7QjVKpZ8?si=8bzilqt7uaReGO-m

We are building the fastest and most effective insurance brokerage in the world. We combine AI with licensed brokers-in-the-loop to review and bind coverage. 

Our AI agent automates the manual back and forth, tracking email threads, and instantly following up. 

We find insurance for complex risk faster, and instantly issue documents like COIs.
