## Logical: The Proactive Desktop Copilot

Hi everyone,

We’re [Sam](http://linkedin.com/in/samurdhilbk) and [Anushka](http://linkedin.com/in/anushkaidamekorala/), co-founders of [Logical](https://trylogical.ai).

**TL;DR:**

**Logical is a copilot that lives on your desktop and proactively assists with things like drafting emails, meeting insights and tracking to-do items.**

Instead of waiting for you to prompt, Logical suggests context-aware actions in many common apps and services. It’s like Clippy, but actually good.

https://youtu.be/XfyPZb14LZM?si=aaJMF37-ipwkJBDV

### Problem

Prompt-first AI is fundamentally flawed. We waste hours every week writing lengthy prompts, context-switching and copy-pasting to get a chatbot to understand what we're doing. The context surrounding our work (emails, meeting notes, knowledge bases etc.) lives in siloed services that don't talk to each other. A truly helpful AI must live where we work: on the desktop.

### Solution

Logical lives on your desktop, observing your work to build an understanding of your intent and the context that surrounds it. As you switch between apps and tabs, Logical transfers that context with you.

It proactively suggests actions based on what you're doing—whether you're in Gmail, Slack, or Excel. This turns Logical into an all-in-one assistant: it's your email drafter, to-do manager, AI note-taker, and chatbot, all combined.
