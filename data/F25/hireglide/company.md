# HireGlide

> The AI-native job board

HireGlide is an AI job board where companies post roles and candidates either apply directly or take a generalized assessment to be matched to opportunities. We screen everyone and pass companies the candidates who fit, and anyone who doesn't fit one role can be redirected to others where they're a better fit.
