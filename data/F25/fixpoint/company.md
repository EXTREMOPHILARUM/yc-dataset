# Fixpoint

> Uber for AI trainers and human-in-the-loop supervisors

Fixpoint is Uber for human data annotation. We’re building the network of experts and the platform to automate sourcing, vetting, and deploying them on AI training projects.

It used to be a manual process to staff people on AI training data or eval projects - but Fixpoint leverages AI to staff people and create data better, cheaper, and faster. This includes a network of pre-vetted experts, AI-automated vetting, and annotator management + payroll.
