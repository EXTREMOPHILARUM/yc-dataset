# Launches

## Fixpoint - Uber for AI trainers and human-in-the-loop supervisors

Hello, world! We’re [Jakub](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jakub-c-14016a3b/) and [Dylan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dbmikus/), the co-founders of [Fixpoint](https://fixpoint.co/).

**Fixpoint helps AI training data companies hire the expert humans that create AI training data**.

AI needs human experts to teach it new skills and benchmark its performance: PhDs for science, lawyers for contract review, etc. But finding and vetting these experts is time-consuming, and without properly vetting experts, you’re paying for bad data!

Fixpoint automates this process, connecting companies with verified experts at the push of a button, so you can focus on creating quality AI training data instead of recruiting.

[Launch Video](https://youtu.be/9TCgpe0tT3w)

**We have two products:**

1. **Worker Vetting API** - Plug Fixpoint’s API into your onboarding workflow and vet the credentials and backgrounds of workers. **Catch 10x the worker fraud!**
2. **White-Glove Expert Staffing** - When you need annotators fast, Fixpoint can source and hire a team of qualified experts. **Staff a team of 100s in just a couple weeks.**

**Our track record:**

* We’ve caught 10x the fraudulent applicants for top AI data companies
* We’re partnered with data companies to staff teams for the top AI labs
* We are GDPR ready and SOC 2 certification is underway

Ultimately, we're building the platform for "human data ops". Just as chip designers use TSMC for chip manufacturing, AI companies can use Fixpoint for the human expertise and tools needed to create specialized training data and evals.
