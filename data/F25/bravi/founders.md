# Founders

## Anas Bouassami
**Co-Founder & CEO**

Co-founder & CEO of Bravi (YC F25), the AI communication platform for home service companies. 
Alongside Bravi, he also runs Volet Dumont, an online rolling shutter business that serves as a live testing ground for Bravi’s technology and customer workflows.
He previously co-founded Snipfeed in 2017, where he scaled the company to 20+ employees before its acquisition in 2024. 
Anas holds a Master Degree from UC Berkeley and a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from EPFL.
