# Launches

## Bravi - AI voice and chat assistants for home service companies

👋 We’re [Pierre-Habté](https://www.linkedin.com/in/pierre-habte-nouvellon/) & [Anas](https://www.linkedin.com/in/anas-bouassami/) from [Bravi (F25)](https://bravi.app/) and we are bulding AI voice and chat assistants for home service companies.

youtube.com/watch?v=Zl3jtIK_yco&feature=youtu.be

**TL;DR:** Bravi builds **AI voice & chat assistants** for home service installers (shutters, solar, HVAC, carpentry) and manufacturers. We help them capture every lead, follow up instantly, and close more jobs.

### **Problem**

**Missed revenue**

Most installers and manufacturers selling directly to consumers lose **30–40% of potential revenue** because they miss calls, don’t answer emails, and fail to respond to chats in time. Every unanswered lead is lost business.

**Broken communication workflows**

Sales, admin, and field teams rely on **manual callbacks, scattered inboxes, and slow CRMs**, which makes it impossible to respond instantly or follow up consistently. That lag costs them real money.

**Tools not made for them**

Generic chatbots and call centers don’t understand **home service workflows** — quotes, site visits, or product specs — leaving customers frustrated and companies unable to scale efficiently.

### **Solution**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=94602&key=user_uploads/231836/5c45980d-e8ee-4351-bb93-f7f115717a92)

Bravi fixes this with AI agents that:

* **Answer calls & chats** 24/7
* **Pre-qualify leads** (project type, urgency, location)
* **Book** **callbacks** for the team
* **Create tasks & reminders** for team members to ensure follow-through
* **Generate** and **Send** **quotes** automatically

This isn’t theory. We (Anas & Pierre) met at UC Berkeley in 2017. After co-founding and scaling our first startup to 20+ employees (acquired in 2024), we wanted to build again, this time in a space we knew personally.

That’s how **Bravi** was born.
