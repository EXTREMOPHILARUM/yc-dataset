# **The Problem**

Users encounter friction at every stage of their journey:

* **Prospects** want to see your product now, not wait days for a scheduled demo
* **New users** struggle to find value and churn before they experience what makes your product great
* **Existing customers** get blocked by questions that could be answered instantly

The root issue: help shouldn't require scheduling, digging through documentation, or waiting for business hours. The best time for guidance is always right now, while someone actively needs it.

# **Our Solution**

Primer agents become experts on your product. They explain features, handle objections, answer questions, and adapt in real time. Like your best team member – from sales to support – but always on, in any language.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95354&key=user_uploads/734292/28a6b627-3f63-4633-b478-bb9db369d523)

This isn't just task automation. It rewires your funnel so users see the product, learn how to use it, and grasp the value immediately.

# **How Primer Works**

Creating a Primer agent takes just minutes. Upload a sample demo video and add context about your product. Primer learns your pitch – it studies how your best salespeople demo and adapts their approach. It can fill the shoes of sales, ops, product, eng, and other stakeholders you would usually loop in with prospects.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95354&key=user_uploads/734292/2bbebbf3-ee56-4bb4-8366-ad5c43cfd6a7)

You stay in control: Set objectives, qualification criteria, and even promotions or closing tactics.

Primer passively multiplies the number of demos you give, and helps users learn how to make the most of your product.

We’ve already seen Primer increase landing page demos **by 10x**.
