# Founders

## Sky Yang
**Founder**

Sky Yang is the Co-founder & CEO of Imagine AI.

Before Imagine AI, Sky was the youngest-ever elected Student Body President at UC San Diego, where he led 32,000 students and successfully raised $150M for university infrastructure improvement. He also founded the nonprofit Break the Outbreak, scaling it to a 400-person organization operating in 53 cities/18 States. His expertise lies in building communities and mobilizing resources to solve critical bottlenecks— now he help B2B companies scale.
