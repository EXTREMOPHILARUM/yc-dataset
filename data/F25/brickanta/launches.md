## Brickanta 🧱 the construction estimator's best friend 👷‍♂️🏗️

👋 We’re [Brickanta](https://brickanta.com/) (F25), building _cursor for construction estimation & project management._

**TL;DR**: Our AI platform analyzes construction estimates and project details to automate admin work, reduce errors, and accelerate delivery. 👉 _We’d appreciate your social media hype support and any potential intros, as we look to make more connections in the construction and real estate industry._

**Why this matters**\
Construction projects run on mountains of paperwork, drawings, and codes. Miss one item, and budgets blow up — every year, it costs the small European country of Sweden over $11 billion (according to the government itself) and the global industry _hundreds of billions_ every year. Estimators and PMs are the heroes who keep projects on track, but today they’re buried in manual work.

**How Brickanta helps**\
Our AI reads and organizes project docs, flags missing items & compliance risks, and supports cost analysis, tendering, and planning. It’s trained on standardized building protocols (starting with the Swedish versions of the Eurocodes, soon more countries) and integrates the systems that construction already runs on, like MS SharePoint, Outlook, and Teams — helping teams work faster, catch risks earlier, and protect margins.
