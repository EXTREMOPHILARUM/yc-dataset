**❌ The Problem**

AI has entered engineering at scale. There are now **hundreds of tools** across the engineering lifecycle - from **Cursor, Claude, Codex for coding** to **Greptile, CodeRabbit for review**, and more emerging every week.

But the reality?

* Traditional developer metrics like PR counts and number of code reviews don’t tell the story anymore.
* Engineering orgs are becoming **AI–human hybrid systems**, yet leaders **can’t measure impact, quality, or ROI**.
* Even worse - if teams aren’t aligned to org OKRs, none of these tools matter. Misalignment between goals and execution kills impact.

At companies like Meta, Google, and Microsoft, **30%+ of code is now AI-generated**, yet few organizations have visibility into which systems are actually driving outcomes.

**🤖 Our Solution: Nivara Catalyst - The AI Engineering Intelligence Platform**

**Catalyst** provides visibility into how **humans and AI are building together.** It helps engineering leaders understand:

* Which systems and models drive the most ROI 💰
* How AI adoption affects throughput, quality, and velocity 📈
* Where organizational bottlenecks exist ⚙️

Catalyst learns your product and engineering signals - from incidents and bug rates to resolution times, and ties them back to your **organization OKRs**. 

It helps you:

* Identify **AI champions** and benchmark best practices across the industry 🏆
* Understand **total AI spend** and **map it to business outcomes**
* Spot misalignment before it slows your org down

As AI adoption grows, **Catalyst becomes your intelligence layer** - the system of record for how your organization performs in the AI era.
