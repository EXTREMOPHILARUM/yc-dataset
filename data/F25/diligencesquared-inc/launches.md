**❌ The Problem**

Private equity funds spend **&gt;$10B annually** on commercial due diligence. A standard engagement:

* **Costs $500K-$1M** per project
* **Takes 2-4 weeks** to deliver
* **Relies on junior Associates** often only 1-2 years out of university
* **Provides zero transparency** – no way to trace PowerPoint conclusions back to underlying evidence

The result: Funds delay diligence until the last minute because it's too expensive to do early or skip it entirely on smaller deals. In increasingly competitive capital markets, this creates a real disadvantage.

**✅ Our Solution**

Our AI agents autonomously conduct expert interviews and synthesize insights with **every claim linked directly to source transcripts**. The advantages are significant:

* **Speed & Scale**: Parallel process interviews in local language and instantly synthesize insights with AI.
* **Transparency and depth**: Trace any insight back to its underlying source, such as exact quote in interview transcripts. No black box, no "trust me" slides.
* **Cost**: Fraction of traditional fees—making diligence economical for deals that couldn't justify it before.

As one Managing Director at a PE fund told us: _"80% of the insight at 5% of the cost. It's a no-brainer for early-stage Commercial Due Diligence."_
