# Launches

## Claybird: The Full-Stack AI Ad Company

Hey everyone, we’re Abdullah and Saad - cofounders of [Claybird](http://claybird.com/) 👋

**TL;DR**

**Claybird** is an **AI ad agency** that delivers **studio-quality creative in under 72 hours**, at a fraction of the cost. Our AI agents generate reference images, modify the camera angle with single-image NeRFs and fine-tuned image-editing models, and animate them into polished, on-brand videos that actually perform.

In our first month, we’ve signed **$67k** from brands like **Coca-Cola, Eight Sleep, Delve, and Mercor**.

**_The Problem_**

Ad production hasn’t kept up with the pace of marketing.

Making a video ad still means coordinating actors, crews, agencies, revisions, delays, and bottlenecks. Creative gets filtered through layers of people, and by the time the ad ships, the opportunity window is already closed.

Worse: high production costs make it **nearly impossible** for marketers to create **personalized creatives** across **multiple audience segments** — even though those personalized ads consistently outperform generic ones.

**_Our Solution_**

Claybird is an **AI-powered ad agency** that makes **personalization** actually possible.

We jam with marketers on creative direction, generate each shot using Veo, Sora, and other top video models, and edit everything end-to-end with our agentic pipeline. But unlike typical AI content, Claybird’s agents understand quality — they know which camera angles, shot types, lighting, and model choices are needed to nail a scene with cinematic consistency.\
\
And because we can deliver cost-effective creative in record time, **marketers can dynamically adjust re-marketing campaigns**. When consumer tastes and market conditions change, brands can use Claybird to roll out targeted creative in days instead of months.

The result:\
**Picture-perfect, coherent, high-performing ads — delivered in under 72 hours.**

Traditional production limits marketers to 3–5 audience segments. Claybird customers like Delve can expand from **2 to 12+ targeted cohorts in their first week**, unlocking truly personalized ads that outperform generic creative.
