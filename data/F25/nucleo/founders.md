# Founders

## Angelica Iacovelli
**Founder**

Angelica Iacovelli is the CEO and co-founder of Nucleo. 

Previously, she was a researcher at Stanford University, where she worked on cardiovascular modeling, simulating blood flow dynamics using GNNs, LSTMs, and Transformers. The models are now part of NVIDIA’s scientific libraries. 

She holds BS+MS in Mathematical Engineering from Politecnico di Milano and completed a research exchange at IIT Madras. In 2025, she was recognized as one of the 11 most influential Italians under 35 in software.
