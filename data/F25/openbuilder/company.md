# OpenBuilder

> The vibe coding platform where builders actually finish.

OpenBuilder is the vibe coding platform that helps non-technical builders take their ideas to production. Instead of paying for credits and getting stuck. OpenBuilder offers unlimited build credits, gets you unstuck and ready for production. It's also an open platform where you have access to all of your code from day one.
