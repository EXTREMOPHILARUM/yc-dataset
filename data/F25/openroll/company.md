# Openroll

> AI-Native Workday

Openroll is the AI-native operating system for compensation.

Openroll gives companies a single system where compensation finally happens. Offers are sent instantly, budgets stay aligned automatically, promotions stay consistent, and salary decisions are made with confidence. Instead of fragmented tools, spreadsheets, and manual approvals, compensation runs end-to-end in one intelligent system used across the entire organization.
