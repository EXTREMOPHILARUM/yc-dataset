**Problem**

Imagine playing chess without seeing the board.

That’s how companies set pay today. Salaries decide who joins, who stays, and who leaves – yet leaders still move blindly.

In fast-moving markets, the stakes are extreme. Miss the mark and:

• The candidate you need takes a competing offer.

• A top performer walks when they discover they’re underpaid.

• Replacements cost months of time and resources.

Despite salaries being the largest spend in business, companies still rely on industry averages, gut feeling, and overpriced consultants. By the time they get an answer, it’s already too late.

The consequences of guessing on pay are brutal: lost candidates, frustrated teams, and six-figure mistakes. And even the biggest, most sophisticated companies still make them.

**What We’ve Built**

Openroll is an agentic AI platform that brings real transparency into compensation for the first time:

* **See competitor pay, company by company:** Know exactly what your peers pay their employees. No averages. No black boxes. Just transparent data.
* **AI-guided pay decisions:** From setting starting salaries to balancing cash vs. equity, agents guide leaders through the toughest calls.
* **Speed is survival:** Weeks of manual work and spreadsheets collapse into seconds – giving you the edge to win and keep the people who matter most.
