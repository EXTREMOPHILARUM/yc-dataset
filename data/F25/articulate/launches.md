# Launches

## Articulate: The Construction Painkiller

**TL;DR:** Articulate is the AI platform that helps **construction companies, architects, and civil engineers** catch design errors and avoid costly rework before construction begins. We automatically detect clashes, callouts, and discrepancies, and generate RFIs and save construction companies hundreds of hours and millions of dollars.

**Launch Video: [https://youtu.be/sen1fR6e7n4](https://youtu.be/sen1fR6e7n4)**

**❌ The Problem:**

* Drawings and specs are often incomplete or inconsistent, leading to design clashes and costing companies **hundreds of millions of dollars**
* Mechanical, structural, and architectural plans don’t align, forcing long coordination cycles and rework
* Incorrect blueprints and outdated revisions cause crews to build from the wrong designs, especially in fast-paced solar installations
* Project managers and superintendents lose weeks chasing updates, drafting RFIs, responding to messages, and writing documentation across tools and inboxes

**✅ The Solution:**

Articulate is the construction and solar painkiller.

* **Detect design discrepancies automatically:** AI identifies clashes across MEP, structural, architectural, and solar layouts before they reach the field.
* **Automate crew check-ins and procurement:** Proactive communication via text, email, and voice agents improves team efficiency
* **Instantly generate RFIs and submittals:** Proactive document and drawing auditing allows project managers and installers to stay focused on building.
* **Integrated and proactive:** Articulate connects with Procore, Revit, Autodesk, Outlook, Gmail, Google Drive, SharePoint, Bluebeam, and more
