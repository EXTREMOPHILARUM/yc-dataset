### **Problem**

Deal teams spend a surprisingly large amount of their time on repetitive processes:\
reviewing CIMs, rebuilding models, creating tear sheets and IC decks, updating CRMs and trackers, and piecing together context fragmented across file drives.

### **What We Do**

Zarna connects our **cohort of AI associates** to your firm’s history. _Past deals, the CRM, shared drives, board decks, third-party databases, etc._

Once connected, they execute core work end-to-end:

* Generate tear sheets, IOI materials, and IC drafts in the firm’s format
* Convert CIM financials into diligence model structures
* Complete all data entry & enrichment into [CRM](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8daMRb9IPxU)s
* Sync operator notes & portfolio KPI trackers over time
* Analyze CIMs, teasers, and market reports to surface what matters

**The goal is simple:**

With the summaries, models, and trackers handled, investors can spend their time forming deeper views on companies and markets — building conviction faster on the right deals.

---

### **Why Zarna**

Every firm has its **own way** of evaluating businesses, management, and risk.\
So **generic copilots fail**—they don’t understand the firm’s history, style, or decision-making patterns.

The hard part is **institutional memory and reasoning**, not text generation.

We embed forward-deployed engineers with deal teams to tune how the agents:

* interpret financial signals
* format materials
* map market structures
* reflect the firm’s voice and conviction process

This turns what used to live in **individuals who could walk out the door** into something **searchable, reusable, and cumulative across the firm.**

The result is not just “faster work.”\
It is **firms that start each deal loaded with context instead of from zero.**
