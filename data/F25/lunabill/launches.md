## LunaBill - AI Voice Callers for Healthcare Billing Teams

Hello YC! We’re Suhail Parry, David Day, and Yash Raj from LunaBill.

_TL;DR:_ We built AI voice agents that automate insurance calls for healthcare billing teams. Fine-tuned on a dataset of over 1.2M call transcripts, our voice agents can handle insurance calls regarding claim-status inquiries, A/R recovery, and appeals follow-up.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95408&key=user_uploads/2978935/704621ef-8701-4468-9167-c84f44c67f0a)

### **🚨 The Problem**

Every week, U.S. healthcare billing teams make tens of millions of calls to insurance companies to get claims paid.\
Each one averages **25 minutes**. That’s how hospitals unlock part of the **$260B in denied or delayed payments** every year.

But there’s a bottleneck:

* Reps can only handle a few calls per hour.
* Staffing is expensive and hard to scale.
* Delays cost millions in unrecovered revenue.

Meanwhile, every new hospital system or insurer integration compounds the complexity.

### **💡 The Solution**

LunaBill’s **AI voice callers** automate those phone conversations end-to-end.

Our voice agents:

* Handle **claim status checks, appeals follow-ups, and reprocessing calls** automatically.
* **Connect to any billing software** and dial claims that need to be worked.
* Run up to **100 concurrent agents** per client, like having a 100-person call center on demand.
* Deliver post-call summaries with the to-do steps, integrated directly into workflows.

They’re **fine-tuned on 1.2M+ real call transcripts**, understand payer logic, and sound completely natural.\
🎧 [Listen](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1jxi7GRUV4h1YDX8XkEYQdFzepboOQLq8/view?usp=sharing) to a real AI insurance call, see if you can guess who’s human.

### **⚡ Our Progress**

To date, LunaBill has automated **60,000+ insurance calls**, saving billing teams over **20,000 hours** and recovering **$30M+** in payments that would otherwise be lost.

Billers now can complete 300+ claim follow-ups per day — up from about 25/day before LunaBill, a \~12x productivity improvement.

LunaBill has partnered with **top healthcare systems** and **three of the biggest enterprise billing companies** across the country.
