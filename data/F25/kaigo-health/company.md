# Kaigo Health

> AI outpatient care workforce

Kaigo Health provides an AI-powered outpatient care management platform that automates and scales remote care management for Medicare patients including post-discharge followups, ongoing check-ins, and monitoring. Using phone calls as an age-friendly interface, we aggregate patient responses, voice biomarkers, and remote patient monitoring data for powerful preventative and proactive healthcare.
