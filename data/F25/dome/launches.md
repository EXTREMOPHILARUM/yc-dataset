**Prediction Markets**\
Prediction markets are a new financial instrument that combines news, polls, and expert opinions into a single value that represents the market’s view of an event’s odds. In the past few years, platforms like Polymarket and Kalshi have processed tens of billions in volume, giving real-time insight into collective public belief.

# Problem

_Fragmented_**\
The same markets are scattered across multiple platforms**. Fragmented formats and liquidity make them difficult to analyze or trade efficiently.

_Siloed Data_**\
Accessing reliable data is a challenge.** Every platform exposes different pieces of information, in different formats, leaving traders without a unified historical view.

_Infrastructure_\
**Primitive tools build primitive products.** Developers do not have the adequate infrastructure to grow this ecosystem. 

# Solution

**One Market View**\
Dome unifies prediction markets across platforms into a single, consistent interface.

* Matches **identical markets** across multiple platforms
* Streams **real-time data** through one API 
* **Integrate once** to access every platform

**Complete Data**\
Dome delivers both **real-time** and **historical data** across every platform, giving traders and developers the insight to analyze past performance and react **instantly** to shifting markets. 

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=95017&key=user_uploads/2118203/e56f0e0a-6179-4dd6-8d84-a46a94f9736a)

**Empowered Builders**\
Dome gives developers the tools to build exponentially on top of prediction markets.

* **Multi-language SDKs** for effortless development
* **Real-time** streaming architecture for live insights and updates
* Unified **on-chain** and off-chain data pipeline for consistent accuracy
