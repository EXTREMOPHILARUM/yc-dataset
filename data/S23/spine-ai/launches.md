# The problem

From having built over 200 copilots for companies and having processed more than 100k user queries here are some of our key learnings:

* Analytics and business intelligence are critical to running any organization. They allow leaders to understand what’s working, what’s not, and where they can find new opportunities.
* Most existing BI tools are inflexible and technical. To find answers to novel questions requires knowledge of the data model, or a BI specific query language.
* In practice, it's not always obvious what you'll need to know to respond to an evolving situation, or what new questions your data will raise. Static reports and dashboards create dead ends. You can get stuck waiting for someone from the data team to be available, or having to act on best guesses.
* And it's not just internal decision makers who face these challenges. Your customers can only interact with their application data through:
  * static dashboards that require engineering teams to build,
  * custom reports your support teams and analysts put together, and/or
  * data exports that require expertise to dissect
* While the extraction of information from unstructured sources has evolved, handling structured data remains a challenge:
  * Beyond accurately deciphering user intent, structured data often comes with messy table schemas and unwritten business context, for which there are no established methods of embedding in the models.
  * Simply having models generate complex SQL and/or code to process the data makes the execution less transparent, flexible and reliable.
  * Isolating data across multiple tenants and user access controls remains a barrier in making this a customer facing feature.

## The solution

We build custom AI analysts that allow any user to ask plain language questions about their data, and get reliable answers.
