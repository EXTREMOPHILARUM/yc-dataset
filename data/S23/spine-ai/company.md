# Spine AI

> Manage a swarm of AI agents to get real work done

Spine AI is a visual workspace where a single prompt spins up a swarm of AI agents that plan, delegate, and execute complex tasks together — with humans fully in the loop.

Instead of chatting back and forth with one model, Spine breaks work into visible steps on a shared canvas. Agents collaborate in parallel, ask clarifying questions, use connected tools, and show their reasoning and intermediate outputs as they work.

Every agent’s work stays inspectable, auditable, and editable — reducing hallucinations and giving you real control over the outcome.

Spine connects to leading AI models and your existing tools, but the real power comes from orchestration: you act as the team lead, Spine runs the swarm.

Chat is great for answers. Spine is built for execution.
