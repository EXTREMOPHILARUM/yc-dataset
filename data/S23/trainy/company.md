# Trainy

> Infrastructure for managing GPU clusters for training/serving.

Goodbye Slurm, Hello Konduktor.

Trainy Konduktor is a software platform for AI teams to schedule workloads with priority, control resource allocation, and improve GPU reliability. With Konduktor, teams submit jobs to a healthy pool of GPUs, assign job priority with a simple user interface, and never worry about hardware faults again.
