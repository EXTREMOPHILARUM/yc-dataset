# **Improving the client-auditor relationship, one request at a time**

The toughest part of an audit isn’t issuing the opinion—it’s getting the right documents on time. Existing solutions still confuse clients, require significant training, and lead to re-work that strains relationships. Agentive set out to change this by making document requests seamless and user-friendly.

# **The current frustration**

Requesting documents (often called a PBC list – “Provided By Client” list) can be a nightmare:

1. **Confusing User Interface:**
   * Clients often need training just to see what’s outstanding and upload a document.
2. **Unorganized Sample Requests:**
   * For example, a client might receive a request with an Excel file listing 25 expenses, each expense needing multiple pieces of evidence (like invoices and bank statements).
   * They must gather and upload all evidence into an unorganized folder. This disorganization leads to many mistakes and re-work that can be prevented.
   * Auditors often take days—or even weeks—to review documents provided by the client; they must match each file to its correct item in the sample. Any missing or incorrect evidence requires re-work from the client.
3. **Constant Follow-Ups & Strained Relationships:**
   * Every missing document adds friction, but the longer it takes for clients to learn about mistakes, the more pain it causes.
   * Hours are lost chasing missing documents, leaving clients feeling nagged and auditors drained.

This **painful status quo** has been accepted as “just part of auditing” for far too long. Clients dread it, and firms worry that each request for info might annoy their client. The result? A process that should be straightforward often drags on with frustration on both sides.

# **The purpose-built solution**

What if providing audit evidence required zero training—and even felt satisfying? Enter Agentive’s new request flow, purpose-built to eliminate friction in auditing.

1. **Request with Samples**
   * **Native Itemized Requests:** Instead of downloading an Excel file to view the items in the sample, Agentive natively integrates the sample table into the request.
   * **Precise Uploads:** Clients attach specific evidence at the intersection of each sample row and document request column—eliminating guesswork and simplifying review.

2. **Engagement Board View**
   * **Instant Visibility:** Both auditors and clients see requests by status and category at a glance with drag and drop updates.
   * **Satisfaction:** Clients enjoy checking off completed items, ensuring everyone knows what’s done and what’s outstanding.
3. **Auditor Review Improvements**
   * **Flexible Upload**: Whether clients upload files one-by-one or in bulk, AI matches each document to the correct sample item—even in multi-page scans.
   * **Instant Citations**: Every value is automatically located within uploaded documents, with one-click navigation to its exact location.
   * **90% Faster Review**: AI-powered matching, detail testing, and analysis, let auditors quickly spot missing or insufficient documentation, slashing follow-up time with client.

Imagine your next audit kickoff: your client opens Agentive and says, **“Oh, this is nice!”** Instead of groans and procrastination, they begin right away—confident about what you need. Each step is transparent and even rewarding as they see their progress. What once felt like pulling teeth becomes a smooth collaboration.

Audit staff spend 40% of their time searching, extracting, and manipulating data during audit procedures. And another 20% is spent preparing audit documentation. While it’s gotten easier to work with evidence provided by clients over the years thanks to OCR and other semi-automation solutions, it’s still painful. The desire of audit staff to be involved in audit strategy is often in conflict with the amount of manual work that needs to be done at firms. **What can you use Copilot for?**

1. **Extracting Text/Tables**: Extract text and data from documents like PDFs which can then be analyzed or moved to spreadsheets or workpapers for further processing.
2. **Compliance Checking**: Analyze the content of documents to check compliance with various regulations, standards, or policies, and provide reports detailing any areas of non-compliance.
3. **Content Search and Retrieval**: Enables auditors to conduct advanced content searches within documents, including searching for specific phrases, keywords, or patterns and retrieving relevant sections.
4. **Natural Language Querying**: Simplify data retrieval by extracting information from spreadsheets using natural language queries without complex formulas.
5. **Reconciliations**: Validate data within documents by cross-referencing it with other sources, such as spreadsheets, and highlight any discrepancies that require further investigation.
6. **Natural Language Summarization:** Automatically generate concise written summaries of financial data in spreadsheets, helping auditors quickly comprehend key insights without digging through raw data.
