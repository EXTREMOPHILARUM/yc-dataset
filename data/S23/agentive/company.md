# Agentive

> AI Workspace for Audit & Advisory Firms

Agentive is an AI workspace for financial and technology audit firms that leverages context-aware AI agents to perform testing procedures from natural language instructions—just like an audit associate.

By combining critical workflows such as audit planning, client document requests, and evidence testing into one seamless platform, Agentive streamlines audit processes, reduces inefficiencies, and frees teams to focus on high-impact areas.

We're backed by Y Combinator, Foundation Capital, Dell Technologies Capital, Liquid 2 Ventures, Ritual Capital, UpHonest Capital, and other fantastic investors from the public accounting profession.
