# Docsum

> AI contract repository

Docsum is a contract intelligence platform designed for legal and finance teams. Using AI, Docsum extracts critical data like renewal dates, obligations, and more into a searchable database. The platform is fully customizable with features like custom field reporting, alerts, and playbook-powered redlining. Getting started is simple—upload contracts or connect Docsum to tools like Google Drive, DocuSign, and more.
