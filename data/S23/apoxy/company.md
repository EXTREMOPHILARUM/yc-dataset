# Apoxy

> Simpler and cheaper multi-cloud and cross-platform deployments.

Apoxy is the programmable edge platform that simplifies connectivity, security, and custom logic deployment for distributed applications. Our platform combines secure site-to-site networking with a high-performance runtime for custom TypeScript and Go filters, allowing developers to deploy business logic at the edge without complex infrastructure. Whether you need instant HTTP/TCP/UDP connectivity, advanced access controls, or custom data processing, Apoxy meets you where you are - from Kubernetes clusters to simple processes - with powerful observability and sub-second troubleshooting built in.
