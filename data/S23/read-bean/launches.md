# Launches

## 🛍 Glaze - What would Barbie and Ken wear to an investor meeting?

### **TL;DR**

[Glaze](https://useglaze.com) is the easiest and most fun shopping experience powered by AI. Instead of needing to know exactly what you want when shopping, you can find relevant and cool items from queries like “[simple and elegant dinner party outfit](https://www.useglaze.com/search?query=simple%20and%20elegant%20dinner%20party%20outfit)” and “[what would Barbie and Ken wear to an investor meeting](https://www.useglaze.com/search?query=what%20would%20barbie%20and%20ken%20wear%20to%20an%20investor%20meeting)”. We are 3 MIT founders who love fashion as much as we love coding. 

### **The Problem**

Who knew spending money on clothes could be so full of friction and painful —

you are looking for a pair of linen pants, 

you go on google shopping or an aggregator like Nordstrom, 

you get literally 1000s of listings fed to you, 

you go down the listings, 95% of which are pure noise and only 5% are actually interesting, 

you couldn’t quite decide on anything, yet wasted 2 painful hours in the process.

# **Our Team**

We became best friends while studying computer science at MIT. We’ve had many adventures together but our favorite was running campus-wide clothing pop-ups our senior year. We sold over 1000 pieces of clothes in <24 hrs and built a clothing reselling app for students.

Now wanting to use AI to shape the future of online shopping, we’re building [Glaze](https://www.useglaze.com/) together.
