# Founders

## Natalia Khosla, MD
**Founder**

Natalia is CEO and co-founder of Simbie AI. Before this she completed medical training at the University of Chicago, was a Fulbright Scholar at the LSE where she got her master's in health policy, and was an undergrad at Yale. Prior to Simbie she built DesiSpace, a social network for South Asian immigrants' mental health, and WeDesi, an artists' community building platform. Outside of Simbie, she is a dancer, writer, and does stand-up comedy.

## Rachel O’Driscoll
**Founder**
