# Problem: 30% of patients get lost in follow up and don’t make it to their next appointment

Needing to schedule diagnostic tests like bloodwork or imaging, getting a new med or diagnosis, or general confusion about how to accomplish next steps stop patients from getting there. This loses practices 10% of revenue. Medical groups and hospitals throw expensive people at the problem—sinking 20% of revenue on human staff to chase patients down manually.

# Solution: EHR-integrated Clinical AI agents that see patient questions/barriers through to resolution

Beyond reactively answering phones: Simbie AI proactively reach patients at vulnerable touchpoints and get them back on track, answering their responses and scheduling outreach (call & text) sequentially until loops are closed.

## Simbie AI - Clinically-intelligent AI Voice Agents for Healthcare

Hi! We’re Natalia Perina, Rachel O’Driscoll, and Natalia Khosla MD and we’re the founders of Simbie AI.

**TL;DR**

[Simbie AI](https://www.simbie.ai) is an AI voice agents platform for medical practices. Our agents answer practice phone calls, call patients and outside providers for practices, and integrate fully with the EHR.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=73955&key=user_uploads/617488/e6eeeb33-fdb3-49f9-b10e-c05f7d3b8ea2)

**The Problem: Doctors are overburdened with tasks that take them from their key work: medical decision making.**

During medical training, Natalia Khosla identified firsthand the tasks in the overstretched medical workflow that were perfect cases for AI automation - not only administrative, but a step further: medical, clinical, and practice-of-medicine-changing. We apply these insights.

**Our Solution: EHR-Integrated, Clinically Intelligent AI Voice Agents**

Our agents work directly in practices’ EHRs, and are clinically trained to handle all tasks, administrative and beyond. They answer practice phone calls, complete relevant tasks in the EHR, call patients, and document appropriately.
