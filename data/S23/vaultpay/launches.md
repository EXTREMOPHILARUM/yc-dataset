# Launches

## VaultPay: Digital banking for Central Africa

VaultPay is a digital banking platform bringing Visa cards and modern financial services to the Democratic Republic of Congo, a country of 120 million people where 85% remain unbanked, and 90% of transactions happen in US dollars.

**The Problem**

The DRC has been overlooked by the fintech industry. While mobile money dominates most of Africa with razor-thin margins, the DRC's dollarized economy means our users transact at an average of $40/month, nearly 3x Nigeria's $15 average. Higher transaction values mean healthy unit economics from day one.

**What We Built**

Think Apple Wallet meets CashApp. We offer virtual and physical Visa cards, USD and CDF accounts, and we're rolling out an agency banking network that transforms local businesses into cash-in/cash-out points, following the Square model for Africa.

**Traction**

We launched in Q4 2025. In four months, with zero marketing spend:

* 17,000+ users signed up
* $20K MRR
* 80% are accessing formal financial services for the first time

**Why Now**

We're the first startup to secure a full payment license from the Central Bank of Congo, rather than operating under another bank's license, as most African fintechs do. This opened doors: Visa invested in our launch and is finalizing a deal recognizing us as their distribution partner for DRC expansion. Ecobank, one of Africa's largest banks, chose us to power its card experience, integrating its CashXpress program ($15M in monthly cash-in volume) into our platform.
