# Launches

## Decoda Health: the operating system for elective care

Clinics that rely on point solutions and disconnected tools create missed opportunities, fragmented patient experiences, and endless admin work.

**Decoda Health** **is the operating system for modern elective-care clinics.** Our AI agents answer calls, take notes, keep calendars full, and handle patient follow-ups - all on top of a unified platform for scheduling, payments, memberships, prescriptions, labs, and analytics.

We’re live in **150+ clinics**, processing **$100M+** annually, saving **$7M** in admin time, and generating **$20M+** in new revenue for our customers. We have grown more than ten-fold in the last 6 months.

https://youtu.be/Ala-OpQMy4M

### Problem

Every clinic has knowledge - how patients move through the workflow, which tasks matter most, what providers need, what's already been done, what's still pending.

But that knowledge lives in **ten different tools** and the minds of staff who come and go.

So every day feels like starting from scratch:

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=96026&key=user_uploads/1279796/be2f277d-ce92-4e4d-91f7-bcca78422717)

This isn't a workflow issue. **It's a missing foundation.**

### **Solution**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=96026&key=user_uploads/1279796/65d5dc7a-69ef-430c-a89f-5d4376294f1a)

First, we built the **full suite of modules every clinic needs**: scheduling, intake & consent forms, unified communications, charting & documentation, inventory & checkout, patient messaging, and reporting & analytics.

Then we layered on intelligent agents that carry deep context and perform tasks like **answering calls, taking notes, and keeping calendars full.**

Because these agents and modules live inside the same platform, every workflow becomes connected and actionable.

The more a clinic uses Decoda, the **smarter** and **more streamlined** it becomes. This creates a **compounding advantage:** each clinic builds more longitudinal data, each agent becomes smarter with greater context, and each workflow grows increasingly deterministic.

Over time, the system becomes not just a tool, but a **trusted co-pilot for running the clinic**.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=96026&key=user_uploads/1279796/ee1b49ba-10ab-4e46-9e86-f9d152c5c10f)

### Why Now

**AI gives tiny teams massive leverage.** As a team of &lt;10 people, we’ve already out-shipped incumbents with 500-person orgs. This moment, and our focus on clinic workflows, creates a rare window to build a category-defining platform.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=96026&key=user_uploads/1279796/f3e45a34-b6a8-4599-89bc-0606823b3237)

### Starting with elective care

More people want to live longer, happier, and healthier and are willing to spend on caring for themselves.

We are starting with a focus on **wellness, longevity, anti-aging, medical aesthetics, dermatology,** and **cosmetic surgery practices.**

This segment of care is growing fast and remains underserved by incumbent solutions.
