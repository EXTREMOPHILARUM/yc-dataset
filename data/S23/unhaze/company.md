# Unhaze

> The right leads at the right time for B2B sales

Unhaze is a revenue optimization tool for mid-market B2B teams.

Unhaze finds the leads that sales teams are most likely to close right now. We do this by analyzing previous deals and continuously monitoring for similar traits among all our collected company data.

By focusing on the right leads (at the right time!), we can almost double win rates.
