# CandorIQ

> Headcount & Compensation Management

CandorIQ is software to manage Headcount and Compensation planning. Employees are +70% of all OPEX and yet still managed in sheets & email. We manage all people spend in 1 platform, integrated into your Finance & HR stack.

Product capabilities include:
- Headcount & backfill planning
- Headcount budget scenarios across departments
- Merit cycle planning
- Analytics including employee churn, hiring win/loss, cash burn
