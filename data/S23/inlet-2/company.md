# Inlet

> AI timekeeping for legal professionals.

Attorneys at small- and medium-sized law firms juggle dozens of matters, context-switching throughout the day – and often failing to record their billable time for every task they worked on.

Inlet records these tasks automatically – eliminating painful admin time and increasing their revenue.
