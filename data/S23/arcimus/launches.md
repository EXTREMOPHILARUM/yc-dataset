### **📑 Problem:**

Workers' compensation insurance pricing depends on a business’s total payroll and its employees' duties (a roofer is at higher risk of injury than a Software Engineer). However, these factors change as companies hire/fire and change salaries.

Every year, insurance companies contract auditing firms or individual premium auditors to hand review their customers’ information. They report changes in total payroll and employee duties, enabling insurance carriers to assign them a new premium.

This manual process can take auditors 1-3 hours per customer annually and is prone to human error. Insurance carriers are at a high risk of failing state compliance tests and getting fined due to human errors.

### **🤖 Our Solution:**

We use a combination of LLMs and human judgment to read a customer’s documents and compile their information into the same final report created by premium auditors. We can do this within minutes, at half the cost, and with higher accuracy than any singular auditor.

AI and humans together led to unmatched performance and dramatically less cost, better than any auditor alone.
