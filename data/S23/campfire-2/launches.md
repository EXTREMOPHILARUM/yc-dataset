## Campfire ⛺ 🔥 : Revenue management for Series A+ tech companies

**TL;DR**

Most Series A+ tech companies still manage revenue recognition, attribution (expansion, churn, etc.), and KPIs in spreadsheets. Campfire simplifies the entire process by ingesting your revenue data (customer contracts, Stripe data, etc.) through AI to automate your revenue recognition, invoice management and KPI reporting for any business model (subscription, usage, marketplace, etc.). We’re currently performing the data migration (at no cost!) for new customers through the end of August. Check us out at [meetcampfire.com](http://meetcampfire.com)!

**How we know this is a problem**\
As an exec at Invoice2go (90 employees, Series C), one of the largest pain points [John](https://bookface.ycombinator.com/user/815940) encountered was that the company had outgrown QuickBooks, but the migration to NetSuite didn’t make sense due to pricing, resourcing and the required migration effort. After selling the company to and leading partnerships at [Bill.com](http://Bill.com), John noticed significant innovation happening around core accounting (Brex, Zip, Rippling, etc), but accounting software itself like NetSuite hadn’t been refreshed in 20+ years.

At the same time, [Fernando](https://bookface.ycombinator.com/user/847698) led back-end engineering at Trovata, which is treasury software for customers like Square and Etsy. He realized there was an opportunity to bring enterprise-grade automation to mid-size companies.

Bringing our experiences together, we know that we can make a difference in an area that hasn’t seen improvements in decades. 

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=73767&key=user_uploads/815940/eab61627-c57c-4806-a75c-2dbc58c577db)

**Our Solution**\
To fix this inefficiency, we’ve launched _Revenue Management_ as our first product. We’re migrating accounting and finance teams out of spreadsheets and into automated, AI-powered software that ingests customer contracts and computes revenue recognition and KPIs (ARR, churn, etc.) to improve accuracy, saving both time and money. Early customers are saving 90% of the time it takes to manage revenue, allowing their accounting/finance teams to focus on high-value, impactful work instead of performing manual tasks in spreadsheets. Your investor/Board updates just got a lot better, and your accounting teams just got a lot more efficient. Our product is fully operational for any business model and referenceable with happy campers (pun intended!) like Jenny Jao, Head of Finance at Sprig (Series B) who said:

> Our time-to-value with Campfire was really fast. We're moving our revenue and invoice management out of spreadsheets and into Campfire to automate everything. It's a big win for us.
