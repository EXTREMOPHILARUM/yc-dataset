# Prosper

> AI Phone Agents for Healthcare Operations

Prosper builds AI phone agents for healthcare providers. Our agents automate manual, time-intensive workflows like patient scheduling, balance reminders, claims status checks, benefit verification, and prior authorizations. We integrate with 80+ EHRs, go live in 3 weeks, and offer a no-code interface for customization.

Today, Prosper handles thousands of daily calls for dozens of providers, reducing costs by 50% and tripling productivity. We’re building the AI-first operating system for healthcare to eliminate the $200B lost to administrative overhead every year.
