# **Problem**

Healthcare organizations face growing backlogs and [**fines of up to $55M**](https://www.dhcs.ca.gov/formsandpubs/publications/oc/Documents/DMHCDHCS-JointPressRelease-LACare-Accusation-030422.pdf). It's getting harder to stay compliant as **requirements increase 30% every year** - many of which are frustratingly vague, leaving staff overworked.

Staff spend hours sifting through 100,000 pages of policies and regulations, finding specific references on how to stay compliant. But as their tasks grow in volume and complexity, **Excel and PDF search just don’t cut it anymore**.

# **Solution**

Readily makes **compliance straightforward** for healthcare organizations.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=83298&key=user_uploads/318129/784c1efe-98f4-4579-ab1d-57a5f6055010)

We connect their policies with regulations and **surface pertinent information** as their staff conduct research, analyze gaps, or prepare for audits.

> _It used to take me 10 hours to review an All Plan Letter, and a junior staff twice as long. This saved me hours!” - Manager, Regulatory Affairs_

For any new requirement, we identify which departments, policies, and procedures are impacted and **what actions are needed to stay compliant**. This saves them hours of work and helps prevent costly violations.
