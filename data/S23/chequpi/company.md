# CheqUPI

> India's first UPI wallet for foreign nationals and NRIs

Cheq is a modern UPI payments app for NRIs and international citizens visiting India. 
Using Cheq, you can pay digitally at over 55mn+ merchants across India. 
We help tourists avoid ATMs and minimize their foreign exchange costs while in India.
