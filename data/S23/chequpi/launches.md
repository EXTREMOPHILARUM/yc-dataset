# Launches

## Cheq UPI: Digital payments for NRIs and foreigners in India

Hi everyone!

Cheq is a UPI payment wallet for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and foreign citizens visiting India.

Using Cheq, you can purchase almost any goods/services across India 🇮🇳. \
Just scan the merchant QR code / choose the pay by UPI id option to make the purchase. We accept all international debit/credit cards and support international phone numbers.

### Foreigners Struggle to make payments in India

Most foreigners visiting India will invariably face one of the below problems👇

1. Indian merchant not accepting your international credit card
2. Indian merchants not tendering exact loose change (you’ll collect lots of candies!)
3. Unavailability of ATMs/money changers at remote locations

### Indian Expats struggle to make payments in India

1. Enabling UPI on an international number doesn’t work.
2. Linking NRE/NRO accounts to UPI does not work
3. NRE/NRO accounts are linked to the customer’s (often deactivated) Indian phone number.

More than **10mn+** **international citizens** visiting India each year regularly **face issues in making payments**!

### Cheq UPI lets foreigners pay digitally in India 🇮🇳

1. Cheq provides NRIs/Tourists with a digital UPI wallet that can be used to purchase goods/services across 55mn+ merchants across India
2. Users can add money to their Cheq wallet anytime, anywhere via their international debit/credit card 💳
3. Purchasing INR through Cheq is much cheaper than exchanging currency at the airport. We strive to serve you for less.
4. Having Cheq, lets you travel throughout India cashlessly. Your bank card details remain safe from ATM fraud and clone attacks.
