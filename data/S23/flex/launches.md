**Why Flex Market is Unique**

For products like supplements, fitness equipment or other “dual use” items, consumers are required to have a doctor's note, or Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN), to qualify to spend their HSA/FSA funds. 

Flex is the only place where consumers can both shop for traditional healthcare products (think what you might expect to find at a CVS) as well as those items that require a Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN). For those that require the latter, consumers can initiate a two minute telehealth consultation immediately, qualify, and receive their LMNs later that day.

**Quick HSA/FSA Background**

Health Savings Accounts and Flexible Spending Accounts are tax advantaged accounts that are designated to be spent on healthcare related purchases. Historically these funds have only been able to be spent on trips to the doctor’s office or buying items at CVS. 

FSAs are use-it-or-lose-it, meaning that at the end of the year any funds that aren't spent are forfeited. On average, individuals lose about $450 per year, resulting in nearly $10B of lost benefits.

## 💪 Flex: Stripe for HSA/FSA payments

**TL;DR** - Flex is Stripe for HSA/FSAs\*. We enable businesses to unlock the $120B per year sitting in HSA/FSA accounts. To learn more or start accepting HSA/FSA payments, email [partners@withflex.com](mailto:partners@withflex.com).

**PROBLEM**

Today, HSA/FSAs can only be spent at medical institutions or large retailers that have specific inventory management systems in place to comply with IRS regulations.

As a result many consumer wellness, fitness, nutrition, and even e-commerce companies selling health related products are not able to accept HSA/FSAs. 

These businesses are missing out on 60M American consumers who are looking to spend $120B per year from their HSA/FSA accounts on health related products and services.

**SOLUTION**

Flex is a payment platform for businesses to accept HSA/FSA cards. 

We provide a prebuilt, hosted checkout page or UI building blocks to integrate HSA/FSA payments directly into your existing payments flow. 

We know how critical your checkout flow is so Flex pages are thoughtfully designed to optimize conversion.

**HOW IT WORKS**

For [pre-approved items by the IRS](https://www.irs.gov/publications/p502#en_US_2022_pu): Flex checks your inventory against the most up to date IRS approved products list and it processes the FSA or HSA payment, and keeps the necessary records in the case of an audit.

For non-pre-approved items: Flex’s asynchronous telehealth team assesses medical necessity and can issue the required paperwork to make your product or service HSA/FSA eligible for the customer — all in a streamlined checkout flow.
