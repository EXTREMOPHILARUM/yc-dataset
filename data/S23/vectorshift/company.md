# VectorShift

> No-Code Generative AI Automations Platform

VectorShift is an AI automations platform. Teams leverage AI through VectorShift’s no-code or SDK interfaces to search through knowledge bases, generate documents, and deploy chatbots and assistants.

The no-code platform gives end-users an interface to access pre-built workflows for end use cases (e.g., responding to RFPs, writing proposals, writing account plans) while letting them customize and build new ones with drag and drop components.
