## VectorShift: No-Code AI Automations Platform

[VectorShift](http://www.vectorshift.ai/) is a no-code AI automations platform. Teams leverage AI through VectorShift’s no-code interface to search through knowledge bases, automate workflows, and deploy AI chatbots and assistants without needing to code or having knowledge about AI. 

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t18ySXNDYvs>

**VectorShift Platform**

Through drag-and-drop components, VectorShift’s no-code builder allows you to: 

* Use a flexible “visual programming language” to prototype, build, deploy, and manage LLM apps.
* Allow AI workflows to leverage a knowledge base with your data (e.g., files, documents, website). Knowledge bases can live-sync with your data wherever it sits (e.g., Google Drive, OneDirve, Notion, etc.).
* Define triggers that automatically run workflows (e.g., Slack message / Email) and allow workflows to perform actions (e.g., write a notion document).
* Place interfaces on workflows that you build (e.g., chat, forms, search engine, bulk runs, voice, etc.).
* Maintain enterprise-grade security - VectorShift is SOC2 Type 2, GDPR, and HIPPA compliant.

 

**Enterprise Search Product Launch**

Today, VectorShift launches its unified Enterprise search product. Connect to all your team’s tools (e.g., Google Drive, Notion, Slack) for a single place to securely search/chat with your documents. 

With VectorShift, you can:

1. Choose your preferred LLM model provider or leverage your own.
2. Live-sync with your data wherever it sits. VectorShift will automatically transform, optimize, and enhance your data based on source and type (e.g., PDFs, tabular data, images, etc.) to significantly improve retrieval accuracy.
3. Granularly site all answers generated by AI with 1) document source/type, 2) chunks used, and 3) page numbers/sheet names. 
4. Utilize advanced retrieval methods - e.g., re-ranking, hybrid search, advanced data extraction/processing models, metadata filtering, and query optimization. 
5. Leverage enterprise-grade permissioning to deploy the AI search engine directly to your organization.

# **Python SDK Launch**

In building our initial product line, users requested a way to interface with our platform through code, so we’ve built a Python SDK for working with our various product lines. Now, you can access functionality present in our no-code platform directly through your IDE. Using the SDK, you can:

* Fetch, edit, save, and run your pipelines and chatbots. Run your AI workflows and use your preferred version-control system to work with your VectorShift-managed AI pipelines in code.
* Load and query your vector stores, and add new indices to index your data.
* Work with any connected integrations, like Notion, OneDrive, Slack, etc.

Any changes you save through the SDK will automatically update in the no-code platform so you can enjoy complete interoperability between the two interfaces. You can view the documentation for our SDK here: <https://docs.vectorshift.ai/vectorshift/developers/sdk/getting-started>

## VectorShift: No-code LLM workflow builder

[VectorShift](http://www.vectorshift.ai/) is a no-code platform that lets teams quickly create AI workflows. Enterprises use us to build internal tools to generate marketing copy, proposals, and RFP responses. Startup teams use us to automate workflows such as writing personalized emails and deploying chatbots.

[ ](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tKhVKJGOqDM&ab_channel=AlbertMao)

**Introduction**

This is [Alex Leonardi](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alexanderleonardi/) and [Albert Mao](https://www.linkedin.com/in/albert-mao-8b263aa2/) - we studied CS and statistics together at Harvard and previously, we grew a business >200% Y/Y to 7 figures as CEO and CRO. During that time, we realized that our sales reps were spending more than half their time 1) writing proposals, 2) writing personalized outreach emails, and 3) researching prospects. If we could automate these workflows, we would be able to reach more leads and grow faster!

However, there was no way to build these custom generative AI workflows at scale, meaning that we had to build all of our tooling from scratch. That's why we built VectorShift - it is what we wished we had when running our previous business.

**VectorShift Platform**

Our platform’s core features include:

* **Pipeline Builder:** Connect different nodes like LLMs, data loaders, vector databases, and more.
* **File Handling:** Pass files as inputs and save them as outputs while using pipeline nodes to parse their meaning.
* **Deployment Acceleration:** A marketplace of pre-built workflows, easily customizable for your needs.
* **Reusable Components:** Use previous pipelines as nodes in new ones, allowing quick reuse of existing functionality.
* **Chatbot Creation:** Convert any pipeline into a Chatbot with just a few clicks.
* **API Integration:** Run pipelines and chatbots easily with API calls.
* **Public Marketplace:** Run pipelines that other users have already refined, and publish your own pipelines for others to use.

Our pipeline builder allows the user to quickly connect different components needed in a generative AI workflow. For example, sales reps use VectorShift to replicate the logic they use to write account proposals and reclaim hours of time every day.
