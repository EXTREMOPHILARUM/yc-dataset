## Catamaran: Intelligent Catastrophe Risk Analysis and Underwriting 🌊

Hey YC! We’re the CatX team, and we are excited to announce the launch of [Catamaran 2.0](https://trycatamaran.com/)—the smartest way to analyze natural catastrophe risks for any property, location, or business.

🔍 **Problem**

Organizations today struggle with outdated, slow, and expensive processes for assessing natural catastrophe risks. Traditional tools lack comprehensive insights, require significant manual effort, and leave critical gaps in understanding how disasters like wildfires, hurricanes, earthquakes, and severe weather could impact their assets and portfolios.

💡 **Our Solution**

Catamaran makes catastrophe risk analysis effortless and powerful. Just upload your property details or any relevant documents, and Catamaran instantly generates comprehensive risk insights using extensive historical data across all major catastrophe perils—including wildfires, hurricanes, earthquakes, typhoons, weather, hail and more.

## CatX - the digital marketplace for insurance risk

CatX is a digital marketplace connecting insurance carriers with institutional investors to trade and transfer insurance risk efficiently. We help to make insurance risk models understandable and accessible for asset managers so they can unlock new sources of return. In addition, we streamline and digitalize the entire risk transfer process, so insurers can get faster, cheaper access to capital from our partner funds.

**❌ The Problems:**

1. **Rising hedging and funding costs for insurers,** US property rates are up 265% over a six-year period
2. **Highly inefficient risk transfer markets,** with average cost ratios of 40% remaining largely unchanged despite recent technological advancements
3. **Slow and expensive set-up and structuring processes** when issuing new insurance-linked products, involving multiple parties
4. **Little secondary market liquidity for insurance-linked securities** due to their complex economics and limited availability of risk models
5. **Poor analytics or data-drive insights** for understanding novel areas of risks

**💡Our Solution**

CatX addresses these challenges by simplifying the market and providing access to world’s most advanced modeling technologies directly within our platform. In addition, we streamline the origination process, including legal setup and pricing, and offer an electronic secondary trading venue coupled with sophisticated analytics.

CatX helps:

* **Insurers** to access capital at **better rates** through direct capital markets access,
* **Investors** to unlock new investment opportunities in an uncorrelated asset class,
* **Protection buyers** find more efficient structures to offload risks, reducing the protection gap for novel and emerging risks.
