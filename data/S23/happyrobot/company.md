# HappyRobot

> Intelligence that runs your operations. 

HappyRobot is the AI-native operating system for the real economy, closing the circuit between intelligence and action. By combining real-time truth, specialized AI workers, and an orchestrating intelligence, HappyRobot manages complex, mission-critical work with true autonomy. Acting as a central brain, the OS compounds intelligence over time, optimizes decisions at every level, and continuously improves as it operates. Starting with supply chain and industrial-scale operations, HappyRobot enables enterprises to run with greater resilience and speed, while humans focus on strategy, creativity, and values.
