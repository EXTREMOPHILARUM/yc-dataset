# Inari

> Your junior AI product manager

Inari automatically surfaces actionable insights and product opportunities from your customer feedback, CRM, and backlog. 

Instead of sifting through 1000’s of sales calls, support tickets, and customer feedbacks manually, Inari automates the process of extracting useful quotes, surfacing customer insights and product opportunities, then linking insights and issues with your CRM and backlog so your team can spend less time analyzing and more time building products that customers love.
