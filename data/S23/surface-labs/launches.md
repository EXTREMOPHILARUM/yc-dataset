# **The Problem.**

**Over 40% of ad spend is wasted.** Businesses spend money on ads to drive traffic to websites/ PPC pages, but businesses struggle to convert visitors into leads if the page fails to convey value and collect lead data. This results in **leads leaving to visit competitors' websites** and **wasted ad spend.**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=81386&key=user_uploads/1179226/f96972a0-9b96-420e-afcb-eb33ed6f4957)

Even if a lead does initially convert on the site, it’s not enough. Businesses still need to

* **filter out unqualified leads/ fake form fills**
* **decrease speed-to-lead**
* **repeatedly** **engage the lead**

This problem is most severe for businesses in **competitive ad spaces** with commoditized offerings (e.g. services, lending, higher education, home services, etc).

As a result, many businesses end up **buying low-quality leads in bulk** from lead gen services or just **spray-and-pray** cold outbound campaigns.
