# Letterbook

> AI-native customer support platform

Letterbook is the AI-native alternative to Zendesk, Freshdesk, Zohodesk, and Intercom. We help companies manage and resolve customer support tickets across email, in-app feedback, and website forms. Our AI will triage your tickets, write replies, and build your knowledge base.
