# Launches

## 🤵‍♂️ Martin - Your personal voice AI

**TLDR:** Martin is like Jarvis in an app. Through voice conversations, Martin builds a personal relationship with you. He can find information, brainstorm ideas, or just chat with you about your week! You can also ask Martin to send you notes or set reminders in any conversation.
