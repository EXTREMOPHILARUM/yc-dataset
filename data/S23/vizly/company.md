# Vizly

> Data to insights in seconds

Vizly helps you get the most out of your data by turning it into actionable insights in seconds. Whether you're a researcher, professor, data analyst, or business leader, Vizly's AI capabilities enable you to visualize, analyze, and interpret your data effortlessly.

For example, Vizly can automatically generate interactive visualizations, perform detailed statistical analyses, and offer predictive insights, making your data analysis workflow seamless.

Before Vizly, Ali built out people-based AI recommendations at Apple and Sami built mission-critical data visualization systems at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory.
