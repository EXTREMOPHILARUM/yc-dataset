# Repaint

> Build a website by chatting with AI

Repaint lets you build professional websites by chatting with AI. It interviews you and searches information to plan your website. Then it builds it. You can edit anything through AI chat, and directly publish to a custom domain. It lets non-coders build sites without learning coding or a heavy design tool. Our vision is to make a new type of software creation that's accessible to the masses.
