# Flint

> Personalized learning built for schools

Flint is the leading personalized learning platform proven to increase student engagement, performance, and collaboration. With AI-native student analytics, instant feedback, and interactive activities, Flint is there for teachers who want to provide individualized attention, students who want a 24/7 tutor that's perfectly attuned to their needs, and admins who want proper AI guardrails and responsible AI use in their school community.
