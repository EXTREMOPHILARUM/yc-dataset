# **The Problems**

There are \~450,000 law firms in the US, many of which are _boutique_ law firms that focus on one practice area, like personal injury or workers’ compensation.

* **Client Intake Bottleneck**: The key bottleneck is their client intake process - efficiently identifying suitable clients and onboarding them with contracts, insurance, documents, etc.
* **Importance of Empathy:** Lawyers are here to help people. Nobody wants a robot to represent them. Empathy and human connection are crucial to signing and keeping a client.
* **Antiquated Tech, Low-Risk Appetite:** Law firms are built on outdated technology, which translates to outdated workflows. Many are hesitant to change systems because of the importance of the human side of law.

# **AI + Human = Better Advocacy**

Hidden Hand provides a hybrid solution that combines the best parts of both automated intake and human-led intake to increase speed without sacrificing human connection.

* **Offload easy tasks to AI:** We build customized messaging chatbots and beautiful intake forms for tasks that don’t require human involvement. Don’t worry, we always give clients the ability to talk to a real human. _Less labor, more time for what matters._

  ![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=73645&key=user_uploads/396461/f19dc744-87be-413e-9ca2-ee5c807ce40e)

* **Automated communication**: Hidden Hand can automatically follow up with clients to get any missing information via text or email. Messages are automatically connected to any case management software, and everything is kept in one place.

  ![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=73645&key=user_uploads/396461/1e026afd-2d30-4f12-8a1f-e94e9eb55ee1)

* **Automatic Data Entry:** Instead of manually entering information into case management software, we can extract data or documents from conversations, and automatically enter it, saving hours for paralegals. Most importantly, _we maintain the security and privacy of these conversations under attorney-client privilege_ in accordance with the American Bar Association (ABA) guidelines.
