## Twine 🪡 AI text conversations for SMBs

**tl;dr**: We make sure small businesses never miss out on potential customers because of missed calls. We use conversational AI to engage customers over text, find out what they need, and book them for a service.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=74112&key=user_uploads/406208/e4650e95-528b-4a3f-97ed-babcf0965a2f)

We’re Divyesh, Omar, and Anand, and we’re building [Twine](https://www.usetwine.com/).

# **Problem: Missed Calls = Missed Business** 💸

Small businesses are missing out on thousands of dollars because they can’t immediately pick up the phone. Customers often work with the company that responds to them the fastest. To solve the problem of missed calls, businesses employ expensive solutions, such as a receptionist or an outsourced answering service.

# **Solution: Missed Calls** ☎️ **→ Immediate AI Text Message Conversations** 💬

If a business misses a call, Twine texts the customer to find out why they were calling and can get them on their calendar. We integrate with existing VOIP and cell phone services with minimal setup time.
