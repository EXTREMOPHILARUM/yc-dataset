# dili

> Automating PWA Compliance and Diligence

Dili (YC S23) is building the most reliable AI automation for compliance in real-world industries. 
Across energy, infrastructure, datacenters, advanced manufacturing, and construction, Dili has run prevailing wage, apprenticeship, & other labor compliance to help customers secure over $1 billion in federal funding.

Dili is building the AI system of record to help physical industries track compliance, analyze labor and spend, run payroll, and more.
