> Speed up materials innovation with Artificial Intelligence

To date, manufacturing companies rely on trial-and-error and manual approaches to develop new materials. This is a tedious and time-consuming process. Yet, carbon neutrality requirements urge manufacturers to adopt greener and lighter materials to decarbonize the industry.
