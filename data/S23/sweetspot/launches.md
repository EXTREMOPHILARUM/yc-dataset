# **Sweetspot AI Form Fill — Autonomous form completion for GovCon teams**

Leverage AI to finish RFIs, PPQs, security questionnaires, vendor packets, and teaming agreements, so your capture & proposal teams can focus on winning.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92424&key=user_uploads/1284150/387d941f-5586-48ac-93c4-72297f4ba53e)

### **TL;DR**

Sweetspot’s AI Form Fill is the first and only autonomous form-fill agent built for government contracting teams. Think of it as Cursor for forms: it finds every field (even unlabeled ones!) across _any_ document, then fills them with your validated company data and works with you to fill in the remaining gaps.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92424&key=user_uploads/1284150/3bbc2cef-b307-4a0c-8e52-f871c64ace8a)

### **The Problem**

GovCon BD teams spend shocking amounts of time re-typing boilerplate data into forms that all look different but ask for the same information:

* 15-page RFIs that hide critical fields in footers
* PPQs and security questionnaires where one missed checkbox triggers re-work
* Vendor onboarding packets and teaming agreements that clog pipelines just when deadlines tighten

Manual entry steals hours from color reviews, strategy sessions, and pricing. Worse, each agency’s format changes just enough that no template sticks, and existing PDF tools don’t understand compliance nuance.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92424&key=user_uploads/1284150/d0b6afd9-19d3-46cc-92b6-aac5b9cc04ed)

### **Our solution**

**Sweetspot Form Fill** replaces the copy-paste slog with an AI copilot purpose-built for the public sector:

1. **Autonomous field detection**\
   Our Form Fill agents scan the entire form, including headers, tables, annexes, and odd footnotes, to surface every required field, labeled or not.
2. **Live, accurate population**\
   We pull from your company data library and reflect all changes in realtime in the browser. No context switching or separate PDF tools needed.
3. **Ask-once memory**\
   If a field is unclear, Form Fill prompts you, stores the answer, and re-uses it automatically on future submissions. This means zero hallucinations and a full audit trail.
4. **Built-in signatures & edits**\
   Add e-signatures, tweak language, and manually add or adjust any field you need. One tab from first click to final PDF.
5. **Rapid-fire templates**\
   For truly repeatable workflows such as recurring state and local government bids, save a template and cut completion time to seconds.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92424&key=user_uploads/1284150/f84d23f3-5679-4ee6-9f78-72d8a78e06bf)

### **How It Works Under the Hood**

* **Semantic parsing engine** reads form structure, not just keywords, so Section M or SF33 blocks are never missed. It even works on poorly structured PDFs that don’t use annotations or native fields.
* **Domain-trained models** tuned on hundreds of thousands of GovCon documents ensure terminology accuracy (CAGE codes, NAICS, JCP, export controls, etc.).
* **DoD-Compliant architecture** means all of your data, answers, and content are stored securely.

### **Traction & Early Results**

* **12–18 hours saved per team per week** during beta across mid-market federal and SLED contractors.
* **3× faster package turnaround**, giving users extra days for color reviews, price sharpening, and all the other important tasks that go into winning their next contract.
* **Zero compliance rejections** to date; all of our users have been extremely happy with the results and have adopted Sweetspot Form Fill directly into their every day business processes.

### 

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=82512&key=user_uploads/1284150/4183ece5-910d-4a17-a284-f916476a30e1)

### **TL;DR**

[Sweetspot](https://www.sweetspot.so/) has added **200,000+** state, local, and education (SLED) contracts that span **17,000+ agencies** across **47 states**.

### **The Problem**

Currently, SLED contract opportunities are spread out across thousands of different city, state, municipality, and county websites. Although some tools have made progress in aggregating this data, the bid-matching process remains suboptimal. These tools often rely solely on the brief descriptions provided, rather than analyzing the actual files and documents uploaded by the government.

The problem is that these descriptions are frequently missing important details or are too vague about the contract's specific requirements. As a result, small businesses are forced to manually review each opportunity's documentation to determine if it's a good fit for them. After conducting hundreds of market research calls, we reached the conclusion that this process is too time-consuming and inefficient.

Here’s an example of a contract from Maryland asking for a Java Engineer with little to no information in the description. Since it doesn’t even mention the word software once, it is nearly impossible to discover this opportunity easily:

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=82512&key=user_uploads/1284150/53e49f39-3da3-4c53-b01f-a79a2ee451b4)

### **Our Solution**

[Sweetspot](https://www.sweetspot.so/) has leveraged AI to track and aggregate opportunity data across hundreds of disparate SLED data sources, with our coverage growing daily.

Our AI doesn’t simply match keywords. It performs deep semantic analysis on over 20 billion tokens from every opportunity’s associated files and documents.

Using this information, it then generates a detail-rich description based on these files, and highlights exactly where its relevant to your search query along with providing you the file matches.

For example, here’s the same Maryland contract that was previously shown. You can clearly see how our AI was able to enrich this search result and present it as a viable opportunity to go after:

## Sweetspot - AI for government contracting

### **TL;DR**

[Sweetspot](https://www.sweetspot.so/) is an end-to-end AI-powered solution that helps users find, manage, and respond to government opportunities.

### **The Problem**

Navigating the complex landscape of government contracting by relying on existing tools is difficult. Both public and private sector solutions have disorganized user interfaces and lag in adopting technological advancements, contributing to numerous inefficiencies.

Beyond user experience issues, there's a significant gap in the holistic support these tools provide. Many focus exclusively on specific parts of the contracting journey — such as search functionalities, proposal writing, or business intelligence — without addressing the need for a comprehensive solution. This siloed approach forces businesses to stitch together a patchwork of tools to navigate the federal contracting space, inadvertently increasing complexity and costs.

### **Our Solution**

Sweetspot is an all-in-one platform that streamlines the entire government contracting process. Our solution brings together discovery, management, compliance, and proposal drafting — eliminating the need for multiple disjointed government contracting softwares. To achieve this, we’ve released four new products.

#### **1\. AI-Powered Search Engine**

Sweetspot leverages AI-powered search to ensure that users can discover opportunities that align with their capabilities and interests, even if the exact keywords do not match. This approach significantly reduces the time and effort required to find suitable government opportunities.

#### **2\. Opportunity Chat**

Opportunity Chat uses AI to interpret and answer questions directly from the content of solicitation documents. This allows users to gain insights into critical aspects of solicitations, such as evaluation criteria, pricing tables, and wage rates — facilitating faster and more informed bid/no-bid decisions.

Pursuit Management facilitates seamless collaboration within teams working on contract proposals. The tool allows users to create and assign tasks, comment on progress, and ensures that no internal and external deadlines unexpectedly creep up.

#### **4\. Proposal Copilot**

Sweetspot's Proposal Copilot leverages AI to generate compliance matrices and draft competitive proposal responses by indexing user-uploaded information. This tool helps users stay compliant with requirements and reduces the time and effort traditionally required in proposal writing.
