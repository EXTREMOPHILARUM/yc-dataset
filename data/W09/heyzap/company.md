# Heyzap

> Heyzap (acquired by Fyber for $45m in 2016) is one of the largest…

Heyzap is a premier mobile ad network that helps millions of users discover apps they love. Heyzap’s SDK for Android and iOS apps allows both well-established app producers and innovative indie developers to monetize and bring their apps to a wide audience. It currently provides advertising, publishing, mediation, and cross-promotion services to mobile developers worldwide.

Heyzap is based in San Francisco and was founded in 2009 by Jude Gomila and Immad Akhund.
