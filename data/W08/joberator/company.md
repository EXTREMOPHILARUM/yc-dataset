# Joberator

Joberator was formed to tackle head-on the growing challenges and connection-gap issues in the STEM employment and recruitment marketplace. Our goal is to help the right talent and right employers connect together through accelerated, streamlined and STEM-specialized search / matching tools and features.

With a Centralized end-to-end platform where Graduates and Professionals can showcase their skills, plus access a unique connection-pipeline that empowers job seekers at all levels and in every Science, Technology, Engineering and Math fields - to 'Pinpoint' the right businesses who have the highest STEM employment and hiring demand for their skills, thus creating improved and increased hiring opportunities.

Employers can post jobs with fully integrated social recruitment features plus extended reach job distribution channels, and search profiles of top-tier talent members, connect privately, arrange interviews and hire directly on the Joberator platform.

Both Job seekers and Employers can create a Free account.
