# 🚨 **The Problem**

Manufacturing is facing a critical inflection point.

**Labor crisis:** Labor accounts for nearly half of global GDP ($40+ trillion), but over 70% of factories face severe labor shortages.

**Flexibility gap:** Traditional robots require week-long reprogramming by engineers or specialists every time things change.

**Untapped automation potential:** Hundreds of billions of dollars in manufacturing tasks, especially those with high variation, remain out of reach for automation because robots can’t adapt fast enough.

This mismatch, between inflexible robots and ever-shifting production needs, leaves a massive automation gap across global manufacturing. We simply need robots to be more **versatile**. It wasn’t quite possible — **until now**.

# **🧠 How Mbodi AI Helps**

Mbodi is an embodied AI platform that makes robots learn like humans, with natural language. So anyone can teach robots new skills by talking to them and execute the learned skills reliably in production, in minutes.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90633&key=user_uploads/1111983/cd7897e7-4fc9-4137-8c3d-2b6fe0b487bb)

We’ve built a cloud-to-edge system that takes your instructions, understands the environment, makes a plan, and turns it into precise, safe robot actions. All within a second. Our universal agent framework can run across any robotic hardware, coordinating perception, reasoning, planning, and control in real time.

* 🕐 No week-long reprograms
* 👷‍♂️ No on-site engineers
* 🗣️ Just say what you want done
* 🏭 Run reliably in production

When one robot learns something, the entire fleet does. Individual skills can be reused, repurposed, and adapted instantly — across machines, factories, and tasks.
