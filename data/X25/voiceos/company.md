# VoiceOS

> The Siri for Productivity

VoiceOS turns voice into a seamless, universal interface to 10x productivity — a true voice operating system for the next era of computing.

It works across any app, letting users speak naturally to dictate, edit, and write text instantly. Whether writing an email, revising a document, or grasping complex information, VoiceOS transforms voice into the most powerful productivity tool, bridging the gap between thought and expression.
