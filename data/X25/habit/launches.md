# Launches

## HABIT: Neighborhood robotics — starting with car details.

**TL;DR** - HABIT is building a **robotic car detail**. Convenience of a tunnel wash + premium services of a human detailer. 

**Watch our launch video to learn more!**

https://youtu.be/gOQL87xoUxk

**Americans spend $20B a year on car washes**

* A single operator with locations in just 21 states earned $1B last year

**But car details suck**

* Automatic washes scratch paint and don’t offer services like ceramic coating ($1,500+)
* That’s why Americans spend $4.2B a year on manual details and hand washes
* Human detailers are slow (hour-long wait), expensive ($50+), and inconsistent

**So HABIT is building the first robotic car detail**

* Throughput and convenience of an automatic wash
* Results and premium price point of a human — starting with exterior details

**Car detailing is our first vertical**

* Small businesses across America don’t want to buy, maintain, or equip a humanoid fleet
* HABIT centers will act as your neighborhood’s humanoid fleet manager

**Our ask:** Car wash operators have been lining up for our system. So if you know a car wash operator (or are one), hit us up! [founders@habitrobotics.com](mailto:founders@habitrobotics.com)
