**Hello! We are Acolite - we help insurance agencies spend more time with clients by building AI teammates for operational work.** \

### **The problem**

Agencies spend more time running operations than advising clients.

Teams are buried under manual work like **certificate requests,** **updating the AMS**, and **carrier submissions** all the while client expectations rise and the talent pipeline collapses.

* **73%** of account managers nearing retirement are **60+**
* Hiring takes **4–5 months**
* Agencies spend **$65B** annually on BPOs, yet only get \~15% cost savings

It’s time to add _AI teammates_ that can actually work inside your operations, not just automate around them.

### **What Acolite does today**

Agencies use Acolite just like they use virtual assistants but faster, scalable, and always available.

Forward an email or delegate a task from your AMS, and Acolite handles:

* **Certificate generation**: reads policy data, verifies coverage, and generates COIs from any AMS
* **Carrier submissions**: pre-fills ACORDs, normalizes data, validates apps - cutting hours of work into minutes of review
* **SOV processing**: cleans & standardizes multi-property PDFs and Excel files
* **Updating the AMS**: extract data from carrier websites to update policy information during renewals and changes

Our AI runs in a secure browser environment with humans-in-the-loop.
