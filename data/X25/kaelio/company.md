# Kaelio

> AI that operates your business

Kaelio connects your CRM, analytics, billing, support, and ops tools. It monitors what's happening across your stack, flags what needs attention, recommends the next step, and executes with one command.

Teams today switch between five dashboards just to figure out what's going on. They catch problems after they've escalated, spend 45 minutes prepping for meetings, and still miss the follow-up.

Kaelio fixes that. It watches everything, tells your team what matters, and acts on it. Revenue, ops, marketing, finance, customer success. One agent that works across all of them.
