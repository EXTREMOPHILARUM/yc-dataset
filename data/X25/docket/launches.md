# Launches

## Docket – AI agents for QA testing

**TL;DR**

**Docket lets you write tests in plain English. Then we keep those tests in sync with how users actually interact with your product — no more flaky selectors or stale test scripts.**

---

### **The Problem**

E2E tests are a _pain_ to write — and an even bigger pain to maintain.

* Writing Cypress or Playwright tests is time-consuming and brittle
* Even no-code tools require manually defining flows or relying on flaky CSS selectors
* Every UI change risks breaking your suite, forcing teams to either spend hours fixing tests or just give up and test manually

Most teams hit the same tradeoff: **move fast and break things** — or slow down to ensure quality.

### **Our Solution**

Docket lets you:

* Write tests in plain English ("Log in, Search for Y Combinator t shirt, Add 2 shirts to cart")
* Skip the brittle selectors — we use multimodal agents that interact with the page like humans
* Automatically keep tests up to date with real user session data
