## Opusense – AI-Powered Field Reports for Site Inspectors

# **TL;DR**

Opusense turns field notes, voice memos, and photos into fully formatted inspection reports **automatically**. Used by engineers and inspectors in construction, infrastructure, and consulting to save time each day, reduce errors, and keep branding consistent. It’s like having a reporting assistant in your pocket!

# **❌ The Problem**

Most field teams still rely on writing notes by hand and typing them into Word templates hours or even days after leaving the site. It’s slow, error-prone, and easy to fall behind. For firms managing dozens of active sites, the reporting backlog is the most painful part of the job. No engineer enjoys writing the same reports over and over. It’s an overlooked market, but consultants are on every construction site, and inspections in the US alone make up a $30B industry.

# **✅ Our Solution**

**Opusense is your AI-powered reporting assistant.**

📱 On-site just open our mobile app and speak or type notes, and snap photos

🧠 Our AI turns raw inputs into structured reports tailored to your firm’s format and style

📄 Instantly export as Word or PDF — zero formatting needed

It works offline on any phone or tablet  — no laptop, no end-of-day chaos.

_Field oversight for managers back at the office? Don’t worry_ —  _we’ve got a solution for that!_

Our users also have access to a web interface which shows real-time reports as they are generated.
