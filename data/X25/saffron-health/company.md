# Saffron Health

> AI-powered care coordination for primary care

Saffron Health is building AI agents that automate specialist referrals for primary care. 

Primary care staff spend hours each day on tedious coordination work: searching for in-network specialists, filling out authorization forms, and chasing down medical records.

Saffron's agents find available in-network providers, complete prior authorizations, and help patients book appointments, which lowers administrative costs and improves access to care.
