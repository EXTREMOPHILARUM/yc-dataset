**Founder**

Tugce is the co-founder of Eloquent AI and a serial entrepreneur. Previously, she founded Streetbees, scaling it into a 200-person global AI company with $80M in VC funding and multi-million dollar enterprise deals with Fortune 500 clients.

## Aldo Lipani
**Founder**

Aldo is the Co-founder at Eloquent AI, and a Professor of Machine Learning at UCL. His research focuses on LLMs, simulation-based evaluation, and cost-effective training methods like instruction modeling. He holds a PhD from TU Wien and has worked with NIST, Microsoft Research, NII Tokyo, and Nvidia. Aldo has also advised startups and enterprises on building reliable, real-world AI systems.
