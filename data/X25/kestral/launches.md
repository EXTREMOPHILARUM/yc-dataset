# 💭 📖 **The Solution**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90532&key=user_uploads/12690/7fd4ffb6-0954-4fb8-b661-3063088bae79)

We instantly bridge this divide between customer-facing roles and product development by:

1\. Extracting ALL actionable tasks from a variety of sources (transcripts, Slack/Discord messages, GitHub issues…)

2\. Consolidating duplicate asks and prioritizing them by theme

3\. (optionally syncing to project management platforms like Linear/Notion until we displace them…)

4\. Closing the loop with them, so you go from feature launch to immediate customer engagement & guaranteed impact

**We’re building teams of AI agents that:**

🤖 - understand the context of roles, projects, companies, and customers

🤖 - rank work on various heuristics like opportunity size, effort, and team goals

🤖 - make direct connections between customer asks and R&D’s work

🤖 - personally follow up with users or revitalize the pipeline to guarantee a positive business impact of work

Our technology can also easily transition to save orgs countless hours by eliminating work about work, like updating the state of tasks from meeting transcripts/messages and giving status updates while reprioritizing.
