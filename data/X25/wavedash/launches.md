# Launches

## Wavedash — zero-install gameplay

**TL;DR: Wavedash uses cutting edge browser technology to let gamers play the latest releases with no downloads or installation.**

https://youtu.be/J_33b9malzc

## The Problem

Large downloads and lengthy installs create friction between your game and potential players

## Our Solution

Wavedash uses cutting edge browser technologies (like WebAssembly and WebGPU) to allow players to hop into your game instantly — with no downloads and no installations.
