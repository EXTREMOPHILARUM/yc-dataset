## Kirana AI: Full-Stack AI Store Manager

Prateek and I are building a new full-stack manager for grocery stores. Growing up, in the summers I worked at my family's grocery store, and saw first hand the challenges my father went through on a day to day basis. My family's store was started over 100 years ago, and with this industry experience, Prateek and I are able to offer new insights.\
[Overview](https://youtu.be/VEAZGZZ4_RU) 

\
**The Problem: Operating an independent grocery store means surviving on razor-thin margins.**

* 3 % of sales vanish due to shrinkage.
* Out of stocks wipe out 4% more in lost sales
* Slip-and-fall hazards lead to liability risks.
* These slim margins prevent grocers from hiring back office staff.

**What We Built-**

* 24/7 Theft Detection – Real-time alerts when someone crosses the “no-go” zones behind registers or swipes merchandise.
* Safety Monitoring – Automatic slip-and-fall hazard spotting so you can fix risks before they become claims.
* Shelf & Stock Insights – Instant out-of-stock notifications to keep product levels optimized.
* Pricing Research - Soon we will introduce an agentic workflow to manage one of grocers' most tedious tasks, looking up competitors prices.

We chose to work in this space to help local grocers thrive in the AI age and be on the forefront of innovation. Our cameras are just the first step- by combining POS, ordering, and camera data, we can automate every back-office process in the store, allowing them to achieve their optimal margins.\
