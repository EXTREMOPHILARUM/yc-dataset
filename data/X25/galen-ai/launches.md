**💥 The Problem**

For 4+ years now, I’ve struggled to navigate several chronic low back conditions. I often waited weeks for responses from my care team, received little guidance about new diagnoses, and felt overwhelmed trying to manage overlapping conditions and medications.

I found myself turning to ChatGPT. A lot. And quickly, Priyanka and I realized my experience is not unique…it’s the norm. Our healthcare system is overloaded and fragmented, doctors are pressed for time, and patients are expected navigate the majority of it alone.

Increasingly, **patients are trusting LLMs with their health questions**, but these tools weren’t built to understand a person’s full clinical history, wearable data, and evolving health context, so they fall short of delivering truly personalized insights.

**🧠 Our Solution**

Meet Galen AI — our AI-powered solution to make high-quality, personal medical support accessible to everyone. No more screenshotting your records and uploading them to ChatGPT. No more WebMD rabbit holes about your symptoms. No more waiting months for your next doctor’s appointment.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90700&key=user_uploads/1833511/b89751e0-80e4-479d-aa23-bf7209579b13)

**All Your Medical Data in One Place**

Galen securely integrates with **Apple HealthKit** to connect directly to your medical records, allowing you to unify health data from all your providers and institutions in one place.

It also syncs with your **Apple Watch** to bring in daily fitness and activity data, with support for **20+ additional wearables** coming soon.

As you share new symptoms and health updates, Galen **keeps track** and **continuously learns** about your evolving needs, providing long-term support.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90700&key=user_uploads/1833511/cc5d7b50-ea97-4b63-926e-92bb25049ead)

**Talk to Your Health Data**

Once your records are connected, you can:

* Receive clear, **comprehensive summaries** of your medical history
* Ask questions about and **uncover patterns** in your health data
* **Understand new symptoms** in the context of your existing conditions
* **Receive tailored insights** about medication side effects or interactions, comorbidities, and treatment impacts
