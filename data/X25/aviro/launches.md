## Why This Matters

Agents are now being tasked with longer, higher-stakes enterprise processes, where mistakes can compound quickly, and reliability erodes rapidly. Today's agents:

* **Don't carry forward what worked**
* **Lose context across tools and handoffs**
* **Repeat the same errors** (wasting time and compute)
* **Deliver inconsistent results** teams can’t trust

They fail not because models aren't smart enough, but because agents can't learn or adapt to enterprise workflows.

## What Makes Aviro Different

Our intelligence layer, Cortex, solves this by providing task-time intelligence that evolves with your enterprise. Cortex:

* **Learns From Every Attempt -** extracts reusable lessons from long, complex runs that help future ones stay on track
* **Context-Aware Retrieval -** surfaces the right lessons at the right moments, without slowing or overloading the agent
* **Workflow-Aware Guidance -** anchors to your **existing SOPs** while evolving them as better approaches emerge
* **RL-Powered -** uses reinforcement learning to optimize guidance and retrieval over time
* **Seamless Enterprise Integration -** works alongside your current agents, models, and tools with **lightweight wiring**

With Cortex, agents identify bottlenecks early, apply proven solutions, and adapt established workflows to new scenarios without context overload. Even as complexity increases, they maintain focus and improve with each use. Our deep research agent, powered by Cortex, **outperformed OpenAI's by 70% on enterprise search tasks and topped Microsoft's Deep Research benchmark.**
