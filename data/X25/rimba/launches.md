## **❌ The Problem**

## Compliance today is manual, error-prone, and incredibly expensive. From low carbon fuel reports, like

**LCFS, RFS,** to air permits, from tax credit filings to prevailing wage documentation, and more.\
A look at the numbers:

* $23B+ is spent annually on energy compliance in the U.S.
* $2B+ in annual fines
* Not to mention the missed incentive and tax credit revenue. Plus 1,000s of hours lost spent on copy-pasting values across spreadsheets, data cleaning, and filings.

And yet—there are **no modern tools** built for how energy producers actually operate: across spreadsheets, legacy software, and passing data back-and-forth.

## **💡 Our Solution:**

## **One Platform for Energy & Industrial Compliance Automation**

## **Rimba** is building the compliance operating system for the industrial sector.

1. We monitor and extract operational data from **any source**—ERP Data, meter data, PDF—and turn it into **clean, structured, and report-ready data**.
2. Verify data and reports for regulations, such as:

* LCFS / RFS / Air Permits
* Prevailing Wage
* Sustainability Certification

1. Catch issues early and avoid mistakes that lead to costly penalties
