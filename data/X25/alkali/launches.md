## Alkali – The AI Copilot for Chemical Process Design

**TL;DR:** Scaling a chemical process from lab-scale result to commercial plant can take years and hundreds of millions of dollars. Alkali’s Engineering Copilot (ProcessMate) uses AI to catch process design errors and automate equipment sourcing – keeping your projects on time and on budget.

[Demo!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JRsBGalf-7k)

**The Problem:**

As chemical engineers, we’ve seen how design errors and procurement delays derail project schedules and budgets:

* **Engineering Rework:** Projects often undergo 10+ design iterations due to incorrect equipment selection, overlooked safety requirements, or careless design errors.
* **Procurement Delays:** Complex part sourcing, manual quote/response workflows, and relentless vendor follow-up (chasing confirmations, shipments, exceptions) can delay projects by months.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90604&key=user_uploads/2744343/38799444-2d92-431e-9e5c-807e9fe1bbbb)

**Our Solution:**

[Alkali Engineering](https://alkali.engineering/)’s ProcessMate platform streamlines error checking and procurement. Free engineers to do creative work, get projects built on time!

1. **AI-Driven Design Checks** – Like an engineering spell-check for your schematics, AI identifies design errors before it’s too late.
2. **Smart Equipment Selection** – Cross reference your engineering designs and vendor catalogues to help you get the right parts, faster.
3. **Version Control & Chat Interface** – Interpretable version control for engineering schematics. Version control is no longer just for software engineers!
