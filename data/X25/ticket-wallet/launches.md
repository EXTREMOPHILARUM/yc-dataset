## Ticket Wallet - AI-Powered Ticket Resale

🎟️ **TL;DR**\
**Selling tickets sucks…** We’re building Ticket Wallet - an **AI-powered resale platform** **for ticket brokers.** We automate pricing, listing, and distribution across all major marketplaces. 

We do everything you just press sell.\

🌐 **The Origin Story**\
Ryan and I (Truman) met on the Princeton Men’s Soccer team and have been best friends for nearly 8 years. He studied computer science with a focus on AI/ML, and I studied econ. Ryan could build, I could sell - we just needed something to work on.

We finally got the chance our senior year when we tried selling our own season tickets.

It was terrible. The process was manual, time-consuming, and completely outdated. That pain quickly turned into an obsession. 

💥 **The Problem We’re Solving**\
It's really difficult to sell tickets, especially if you have a lot of them. Brokers are the **power sellers** of the industry, often moving **$10M+** in ticket volume annually. Yet they are stuck using spreadsheets, updating prices manually, and listing tickets on one site at a time. You can think of it like day trading on the stock market 10+ years ago. Even crazier, the biggest brokers are hiring pricing teams in the Philippines…

It’s 2025. We have self-driving cars but ticket sellers are still updating rows in Excel and refreshing StubHub?

🔨 **What We’re Building**\
Ticket Wallet is the first **AI-powered selling system for ticket brokers.** We price, list, and manage their tickets across all the major marketplaces (Ticketmaster, StubHub, SeatGeek, Vivid Seats, Gametime, Tickpick, AXS, etc...) Sellers **just press “list”** and we handle the rest.

Since launching, we’ve onboarded over **$3M in ticket inventory**, **100+ paying sellers**, and we’re **starting to work with pro sports teams**. We charge 10% of every ticket sold.

Ticket brokers using Ticket Wallet have seen a **27% increase in revenue**  per ticket sold. We run their entire business 

The market for ticket brokers in the US is **$26.3B.**

**🔑 Key Features:**

* AI pricing that updates 24/7
* Listings pushed everywhere automatically 
* Central dashboard to manage inventory 
* Analytics to track progress and sales
* Zero manual work
* Website and mobile application

It’s like giving sellers superpowers - or at least their weekends back.
