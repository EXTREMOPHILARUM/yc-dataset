# Ticket Wallet

> AI-optimized ticket resale

Ticket Wallet is an AI-powered resale platform for season ticket holders and brokers.
We automate pricing, listing, and distribution across all major marketplaces.

Today, most tickets are still sold manually: spreadsheets, guessing prices, and listing on one site at a time. The biggest brokers outsource pricing to teams in the Philippines… 

We built Ticket Wallet because we lived this. Truman has been flipping tickets for the last 5 years. Ryan built pricing software after seeing the mess first-hand. We’ve turned that pain into a product.

Since launch, we’ve onboarded $3M+ in ticket inventory, signed up 100+ paying sellers, and started working with pro teams. We charge 10% per ticket sold. This is our wedge into the $26B resale market.

Other platforms cost you money. We make you more of it.
