# Launches

## Kanava - Your First AI Sales Hire for Wholesale Distributors

**Industrial Sales teams are underwater. Here’s why - and what we’re doing to fix it.**

Hi everyone! We’re [Smit](https://www.linkedin.com/in/smitdagli/) and [Vik](https://www.linkedin.com/in/vikhyath-m-a00357128/) - the founders of [Kanava AI](https://kanava.ai/)

**Problem and Opportunity:**

The wholesale distribution industry is massive: _$9T+ in annual revenue. 410,000+ businesses. 6 million+ employees._

And yet — sales teams are drowning.

* **A third of the workforce is over 55**, and replacements aren’t coming fast enough.

  (“Most of my sales reps are **retiring in the next 2-3 years**, I have **NO idea** what I’ll do after” - a medium sized electrical goods distributor from Indiana we met)
* **Reps spend less than 30% of their time selling**. The rest? Answering repetitive calls (“What’s the price of this SKU?” “Which warehouse has it?”), digging through systems, and manual follow-ups.
* **Over 25% of inbound calls never reach a human** — lost revenue, lost trust, lost time.

This isn’t just inefficiency. It’s operational drag at scale — and it's only getting worse.

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s1NVVxRnLL8](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s1NVVxRnLL8%5C%5C)

**How’re we solving it:**

We’re building Kanava AI, a voice-based AI agent designed for wholesale distributors.

It handles repetitive tasks like **answering stock, price,** and **order status calls** using live **ERP** data and logging **field notes hands-free**, drafting follow-ups, updating the **CRM** all _automatically_.

It’s like a junior rep-in-training. It starts with the basics, but improves quickly by learning from the company’s best performers. Over time, Kanava takes on more:

•⁠ **⁠Managing consultative sales calls** \
•⁠ ⁠**Identifying upsell opportunities** in real time \
•⁠ ⁠**Surfacing coaching insights** from rep conversations \
•⁠ ⁠**Re-engaging dormant accounts** with timely, data-backed outreach

And by the time these reps are ready to retire, Kanava is **ready to fill the void they leave.**

Most AI tools in this space fail because they try to do too much, too early, and distributors are famously late adopters. We’re taking a more deliberate path: start with low-leverage work, prove value, and scale responsibility as the system earns it.
