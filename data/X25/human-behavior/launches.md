**❌ The Problem**

Two options to understand how your product is used:

1. Product analytics (like Mixpanel or Amplitude): Great for aggregating data but miss user context.
2. Session replays capture absolutely everything, but there's no good way to aggregate them to learn anything useful.

"How many users abandon my product after a long LLM generation time? How long do enterprise vs free users spend on feature X?" These questions become difficult to answer with traditional methods.

**💡Our Solution**

We built Human Behavior to give you the best of both worlds using vision AI that actually watches your users. No more choosing between high-level analytics or drowning in session videos!

Without AI, thousands of session videos can be overwhelming. Now you'll never have to set manual feature tracking again – retroactively explore feature usage patterns from all your past users without wasting engineering time and effort.\
