# Launches

## Code Four – Bringing Our Cops Home

#### **TL;DR**

We auto-generate reports, verify narratives, and surface evidence instantly — cutting desk time by 60% and putting officers where communities need them most.

https://youtu.be/5K72MBx20fg

#### **👮‍♂️ The Problem**

* **40% of a typical shift** vanishes to transcribing body-cam footage, filling forms, and locating clips across siloed systems.
* Manual redaction and video scrubbing **delay** investigations, **strain** budgets, and **erode** public trust.
* Departments **juggle 8–10 incompatible** software tools that weren’t built for today’s fast-paced operations

#### **🚓 Our Solution – the Intelligence Layer for Digital Evidence**

1. **Real-time transcription & summarization** — every body-cam or interview video becomes searchable text + key-frame timelined.
2. **One-click redaction** — faces, plates, minors’ voices, and PII are masked with CJIS-compliant logs.
3. **Context-aware search** — ask “show me every time suspect #42 handled a weapon” and jump straight to the frames.
4. **Autofilled narrative reports** that match your RMS schema and chain-of-custody rules.
5. **Plug-and-play integrations** with leading body-cam vendors, evidence lockers, and CAD/RMS APIs—no rip-and-replace.
