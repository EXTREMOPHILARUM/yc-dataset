# **The Mortgage Problem**

A huge number of buyers still do not shop their mortgage, even though doing so can save serious money. The main reason is not laziness. The process is overwhelming.\
**High friction** \
Comparing lenders usually means re-entering the same information, re-uploading the same documents, and navigating multiple portals with different requirements and timelines.

**Lack of know-how** \
Most people do not know what they can negotiate or how to compare offers. Terms like points, credits, and relationship pricing add confusion. Switching later can feel risky if it might slow down or jeopardize the deal.

**Spam overload** \
Trying to shop for a mortgage can quickly turn into a flood of calls and emails.

# **What Ralo Does**

Ralo reimagines how mortgages work.

**One form. One Workflow.** \
You fill out one application. We keep everything organized and guide you from pre-approval through closing.

**Lowest Rates From the Start** \
Yes, we can negotiate. More importantly, we streamline the process and remove middlemen so buyers can access great rates with low fees upfront, without endless back-and-forth.

**Zero Spam**\
We protect buyer data and only share what is necessary, so getting a mortgage does not become an inbox nightmare.

**Proof in the Rates**\
We publish our rates here: [ralo.com/rates](https://ralo.com/rates). On average, Ralo’s rates are about 0.50 percent to 0.75 percent below the market average, often with lower fees too
