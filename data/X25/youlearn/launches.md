YouLearn is an AI tutor personalized to each student. In the last **eight months**, we’ve grown from **$1K to $70K MRR**, with **150,000 monthly active users**, by providing concise notes, an AI tutor you can talk to, and personalized tests based on your learning materials.

# **📍 How we started**

We met two weeks into college and started hacking on projects in our dorm. By sophomore year, we were fed up with watching **long, outdated lecture videos** and reading **dense textbooks**, only to forget a few days later. Generic AI tools gave **quick answers**, but those shortcuts left us with only **surface-level understanding**. We kept **cramming before exams**, forgetting most of it soon after, and repeating the same cycle.

# **💻 What we built**

Out of that frustration, we built **YouLearn**. Upload any learning material, and YouLearn turns it into **clear notes**, an **AI tutor** that walks you through the topic, and **adaptive quizzes** to help you remember. Learning is now **faster, easier, and more memorable.**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90256&key=user_uploads/1378551/166ca331-2283-486c-a7a4-074ae4ea4c86)

# **🧑‍🎓 Why students choose YouLearn**

* **Designed by students, for students:** As recent undergrads, we lived the stress of college life, so we built YouLearn’s AI tutor and quizzes to solve these challenges.
* **Everything in one place:** One upload instantly delivers notes, an AI tutor, and quizzes. No more bouncing between ChatGPT, Quizlet, Notion, etc.
* **Made for exam success:** Take practice tests with full answer breakdowns, track your progress, and pinpoint your weak spots (e.g, “Review Page 42, paragraph 1”).

# **📚 Building a better way to study**

Today, over **150,000 students use YouLearn each month** to study smarter, with more than **2.2 million+ learning materials uploaded.** One med student told us he didn’t think AI could do this until he started **acing exams with perfect scores** across multiple courses. His friend jumped from **60% to 90%** using YouLearn, and now **four of his classmates have upgraded**. As he puts it:

> I got better grades than people that went to class. Because literally for me, it's my tutor.

This is just the start. We believe **every student will have a personal AI tutor**, and we’re building the one they’ll never want to miss a class on.
