# Launches

## Zero – AI Native Email

**TL;DR:** Zero is an open source AI-native email client that organizes your inbox, summarizes threads, writes replies, and lets you chat with your email, so you can stay effortlessly on top of work.

**What is Zero and how does it work?**

Zero makes email work for you, not the other way around (checkout our launch video below!).

[Launch Video](https://youtu.be/3Hy3jEQQVFQ)

Zero uses AI to:

* **Prioritize** emails and surface what matters most
* **Summarize** long email threads into quick takeaways
* **Draft** and **Reply** to emails automatically
* **Organize** your inbox into smart categories, let you chat with your inbox, understand projects, and plan your day.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90228&key=user_uploads/2556219/4f2601b3-7b85-4447-8b75-9f78c3d6c314)

**Problem**\
Traditional email clients are outdated and passive. They overload you with notifications, bury important messages, and force you to manually manage threads, tasks, and follow-ups. Meanwhile, most “AI” email tools are just bolt-on features, slow, clunky, and unaware of the full context of your inbox.

**Our Solution**\
Zero is built from the ground up as an **AI-native email platform**. That means every part of your experience, from prioritization to replies, is powered by a deep understanding of your inbox and your workflow. Zero doesn’t just help you **handle** email, it helps you get **ahead** of it.

* AI that thinks like a human assistant, not a search algorithm
* Real-time summaries and smart organization that adapts to your day
* A seamless way to interact with your email through natural conversation
* Fully open source for complete transparency

**Try Zero**

Instead of drowning in an endless stream of emails, Zero gives you a live, intelligent inbox you can actually control.
