# Launches

## Beluga Labs – Rippling for Content Creators

**TL;DR:** Beluga Labs is the business back-office for content creators. Today, we automate their taxes and finances. Soon, we'll be the indispensable OS creators rely on to run their entire business: from brand deals and revenue diversification to payroll and compliance.

https://youtu.be/5lkW26dIKiM

**Problem:**

Content creators manage million-dollar businesses with spreadsheets and fragmented tools, losing hours to admin work. The chaos of taxes, bookkeeping, and revenue tracking steals time from creativity, stunts growth, and prevents them from becoming truly professional businesses.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=91201&key=user_uploads/2204818/f812c3e4-92ab-40ef-9dda-a172fe1ca143)

**Solution:**

Beluga Labs gives creators a single platform to effortlessly manage their entire financial life:

* **Today:** We automatically handle bookkeeping, expense tagging, income tracking, and tax filings, saving creators thousands in deductions and countless hours.
* **Next:** We're launching a CRM to streamline brand deals and revenue stream integrations, enabling creators to easily diversify their income.
* **Future:** Beluga Labs evolves into a full financial operating system, automating S-Corp formation, payroll, compliance, and even monetizing unauthorized content.

Our software helps creators professionalize, scale sustainably, and retain control over their success.

Jack and I previously built products at Meta, HubSpot, and Bubble, with a deep background in AI and creator monetization. We've experienced firsthand how inefficient financial tools hold creators back. Beluga Labs solves that problem, empowering creators to succeed at every stage of their journey.
