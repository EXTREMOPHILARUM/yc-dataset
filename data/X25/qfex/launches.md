Hello World! We’re [Annanay](https://www.linkedin.com/in/annanay-kapila/) and [Joshua](https://www.linkedin.com/in/joshua-wharton/), and we’re building [QFEX](http://www.qfex.com/): a global, 24/7 stock exchange.

#### **What We’ve Built**

Trillions of dollars change hands every day in the global financial markets, but the pace of innovation is excruciatingly slow. As of 2025, you still can’t:

* Trade directly on an exchange **without a broker** (unless you’re as big as Jane Street).
* **Trade 24/7**.
* Get **&gt;20x leverage** or avoid margin fees.
* Trade leveraged US stocks without **worrying about options**.

We’re changing this.

We’ve built an exchange that provides all of the above, packaged into a sleek, **direct-to-market** interface with one-of-a-kind purely **volume-based fees**. Bye-bye Robinhood selling your orders for Citadel to front-run. Adios to the Interactive Brokers GUI. Hello to markets as they _should_ **be**.

#### **PS - For the Crypto/ Finance Bros**

Taking a leaf out of the crypto world, [QFEX](http://www.qfex.com/) is listing Perpetual Futures in traditional assets, making it the first exchange to do so. Futures haven’t changed since the first standardized contract was made in 1865 to help farmers hedge their corn harvests. Now S&P500 futures trade $500 billion daily, but it still expires in time for the harvest. Why?

[QFEX](http://www.qfex.com/) will save institutional traders billions of dollars in rolling costs, operational headache, and deeper liquidity from an unfragmented market - just like perps did for crypto.
