# Aravolta

> Software to monitor, control, and optimize data centers.

Aravolta provides a single pane of glass software for data centers to increase uptime, reduce costs, and improve performance.

A single utility node onboards every asset within an hour and detects hardware failures, hotspots, and other issues.

It also generates reports on important metrics like Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE), energy metering, resource utilization, and much more.
