# Launches

## Photonium: AI-Accelerated Optical Design Automation ⚛️

Hey YC!

We’re [Jennifer](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jennifer-y-song) and [Adam](https://www.linkedin.com/in/adam-mhatre-5b8aaa230/) - cofounders of Photonium.

**TL;DR**

Photonium automates optical system design with AI, replacing costly manual iteration with end-to-end system optimization.

**🚨 The Problem**

Optical systems are mission-critical across many high-growth sectors — including quantum technology, biomedical imaging, LiDAR for defense and autonomous vehicles, and metrology for semiconductor fabrication.

But designing them is a nightmare:

* Companies pour millions into teams of 20+ optical engineers
* Projects take months or years to go from R&D to production
* Tools are fragmented and outdated
* Optimization is manual and often based on trial-and-error

Engineers spend countless hours on repetitive workflows — constrained by tools that haven’t changed in decades.

**⚙️ Our Solution**

Photonium is building the electronic design automation (semiconductor EDA) for optics. We work with complex optical setups across industries and handle the entire design process — from system generation and optimization to simulation, tolerancing, and sourcing.

Photonium enables companies to:

* Generate and optimize designs from product specs
* Predict tolerance impacts without slow simulations
* Recommend data-driven alignment and manufacturing tweaks
* Optimize for cost, size, and performance trade-offs
* Seamlessly integrate with existing CAD workflows and optics simulation tools
* Automate the full design-to-sourcing pipeline

⚛️ **Why This Matters**

Optical systems are foundational to cutting-edge technologies, from quantum computing to semiconductor fabrication. Yet, outdated tools and fragmented workflows slow innovation and inflate costs. We hope to eliminate these bottlenecks and accelerate innovation across industries.
