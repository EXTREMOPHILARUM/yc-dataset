# Launches

## 📦 chrt – Logistics Platform for Time-Critical Shipping

Hi everyone! We are [Kyle](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kyle-reagan-surfs/) and [Aaron](https://www.linkedin.com/in/aaroncarver/), co-founders of [chrt.com](https://chrt.com). We’re helping businesses deliver and track their most critical same-day shipments.

https://youtu.be/EJfOqCY06rw

**🎯 Who is it for?**

* Industrial manufacturers and healthcare companies that need white-glove, same- or next-day delivery with live tracking — for shipments such as life‑saving therapeutics or mission‑critical production parts
* Courier companies and freight forwarders that want to provide their customers with end-to-end tracking and custom workflows

**⛔️ Problem:**

* Businesses shipping critical items depend on outdated freight forwarders
* Two-thirds of these forwarders’ staff are call-center agents coordinating shipments across thousands of professional courier companies
* Call center agents rely on phone and email to reach couriers
* With no real-time tracking, shipment status is unknown, causing frequent failures and delays
* Your life-or-death shipment? Tracked worse than a pizza

**✅ Solution:**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90745&key=user_uploads/1381778/dc006314-7ec3-4274-afff-89ca44a92b31)

chrt is the all-in-one platform for time-critical logistics

* 📦 Businesses place urgent shipments on [**chrt.com**](http://chrt.com)
* 🚛 chrt matches a vetted provider for pickup and delivery
* 📱 Couriers execute the job entirely through chrt’s mobile app
* 📍 Real-time tracking and custom workflows provide complete shipment visibility
* ⚡ Automation slashes overhead, delivering faster, cheaper, and more reliable service
