1. Wasted engineering time
2. Delayed releases
3. Bugs reaching real users

These intensify as companies scale, with some dedicating entire teams to QA or accepting that their users will find the bugs for them. But with Propolis, this is finally changing:

* **Precise bug reporting** 🐛: Detailed reports delivered directly to Slack, Linear, Github, or wherever with videos, traces, and reproduction steps.
