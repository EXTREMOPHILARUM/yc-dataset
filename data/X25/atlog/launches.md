## Atlog: AI Employee for Furniture Stores

**The Problem**:

Furniture stores, specifically rent-to-own furniture stores, write their own contracts regarding rental agreements. This gives them a unique advantage: they can rent to anyone, regardless of credit score.\
\
However, this market has one major flaw: **Customers are always late with payments.** The stores know about this, and it is just a fact of the business model, but these constant late payments cause a few key operational issues:

1. **Staff Overhead**: Stores have to have multiple people on staff dedicated to calling people about their late payments.
2. **Poor Call Execution**: Those employees who have to call late payments often miss their call quota, may not talk professionally to the customers, and forget to do compliance checks like mentioning that they are calling on a recorded line. This can mean 5 figures of lost revenue per store per week.
3. **Lost Inventory:** Stores lose \~2% of their inventory each year to customers who stop paying. This is mid 5-figures in lost inventory each year per store.

 

**The Solution:** 

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DVhRkppy8hQ

**AI Voice Agent that calls, negotiates, and gets payments from customers.** It will always try to work out _something_ with the customer so they aren’t sent to collections, their credit isn’t hurt, and the store keeps its reputation intact.

**Key Features:**

* The agent speaks in **English** and **Spanish**
* **Optimizes call time** and day to improve conversion
* **Finds updated contact info** (customers often change numbers or addresses)
* **Collects payments** directly over the phone
