# Launches

## Lumari - AI Digital Workers for Supply Chain

Hey everyone! We’re [Sam](https://www.linkedin.com/in/samarthlamba/) and [Eshani](https://www.linkedin.com/in/eshanimehta/), the co-founders of Lumari 🔥

**TL;DR:** Lumari streamlines supply chain operations with **fully integrated AI digital workers** that adapt to each team’s specific processes and systems.

### **The Problem ❌**

* Teams are stuck in **firefighting mode** and buried in manual work without any time left for strategic planning
* Missed dates, delays, and **lack of visibility** result in costly expediting, production delay costs, and deductions
* Existing systems force you into **one-size-fits-none** flows that never match your actual process

Teams need a solution that enables them to move from reactive firefighting to proactive risk management.

### **Lumari’s Solution ✨**

Lumari brings autonomous agents to supply chain and acts as a **force multiplier** for the team.

* Lumari **automates** the manual parts of any procurement process from RFQs to order management
* Teams are always in **control** with configurable guardrails and approval layers for every action
* We **connect** the agents with deep integrations to your existing tools (ERP, Slack, Excel, email, etc)
* **Customize** your agent behavior and configuration in plain English

Teams use Lumari to improve and scale their operations across sourcing, purchasing, and procurement. We partner closely with every customer to adapt our agents to their specific processes and systems.
