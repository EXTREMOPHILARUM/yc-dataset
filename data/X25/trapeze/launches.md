## **Problem**

61% of patients miss out on essential appointments each year due to scheduling hassles, costing our healthcare system $280B annually. 

We’re starting by tackling where 88% of patient appointments are scheduled and managed today: the phone.

Doctors have a hair-on-fire problem managing their call centers and front desks. They’re expensive to run and have high turnover rates, which leads to long hold times and high abandonment rates — and ultimately lost patients. For example, our customers have the call center as a multi-million dollar annual expense, and are seeing 20%+ abandonment rates.

## **Solution**

To solve this, we’re building AI voice agents that can be deployed to act as a doctor’s best scheduler with:

* Our scheduling rules engine structuring doctor-specific rules across .pdfs, EHRs, paper, tribal knowledge
* AI voice agents that handle intake, insurance verification, and slotting integrated with the EHR and telephony system
* Admin portal and dashboard that provides visibility into patient call resolution gaps
