**🧠** **The Enterprise GTM Problem**

If you’re selling into a niche or regulated market ( health systems, manufacturing, fintech, govtech, and so on), you already know: generic data platforms don’t work.

Here’s why:

**💡 “Intent signals” are too broad**

Most tools will tell you someone visited a pricing page. That’s not useful when you’re selling a $250K solution into a regional hospital network or a logistics hub in the Midwest.

**🧱 The nuance is everything**

Enterprise sellers ask real questions:

Did that manufacturing facility just expand its fleet?

Does it have two or three parking bays?

Did that nursing home file a new Medicare cost report?

Is the new VP of Ops a better entry point than the outgoing admin director?

These are the signals that drive pipeline and predict fit, and no off-the-shelf platform can give you that.

**🔍 Static filters can’t model your ICP**

Try finding “companies that just adopted Drift, expanded their marketing team by 30%, and hired a new CRO in the past 90 days” in traditional tools. Impossible. Our agents live in that world

**✨ The Sixtyfour Advantage**

We don’t sell contact lists. We build intelligence infrastructure for your GTM engine.

**📍 Custom TAMs, always current**

**📡 Proprietary real-time signals**
