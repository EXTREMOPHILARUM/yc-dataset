Government work is critical for our country, but across federal, state, and local agencies, it’s being slowed to a crawl by outdated software, siloed systems, and manual processes. ### **❓FAQ**

**Who is Caucus for?**\
Government employees: starting with congressional offices and soon expanding across agencies, departments, and local governments.

**What does it do?**\
Caucus embeds AI agents into every government workflow.

**How is this different from existing government software?**\
Caucus _isn’t_ software in the traditional sense. It’s the connective layer across all software, a general purpose agent that works with the tools you already use, at the moment you need them, without disrupting your workflow. Think: Cursor, but for the government.

**What does the Congressional product do?**\
The Congressional product is an AI platform purpose built for House and Senate offices. It automates constituent communication and casework through website chatbots and phone based voice agents that answer policy questions, collect casework details (including signed privacy forms), and route structured summaries to staff. We are cutting down the time it takes to help constituents from over a week to minutes. A secure dashboard surfaces real-time trends and constituent sentiment. By handling repetitive tasks instantly and accurately, Caucus empowers staffers to focus on complex casework and legislative priorities, without requiring any changes to their existing workflows.

**Can Caucus help with federal, state, and local governments?**\
Absolutely. While we are starting with congressional offices, Caucus is built to scale across agencies, federal, state, and local governments. Whether it's a city council or a federal agency, we’re making it easier for government employees at all levels to serve our country more effectively.
