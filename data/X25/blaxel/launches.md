# Launches

## Blaxel — AWS for AI agents

**tl;dr**: Yesterday’s cloud can’t power tomorrow’s AI. Blaxel is a cloud infrastructure built for AI agents. Our computing platform gives AI Builders tools, infra, and developer experience optimized to build and deploy agentic AI — with a twist: your agents can also take the wheel.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aISExQg14qg

### Problem

Our team of 6 co-founders previously built and sold a data platform to OVHcloud, which eventually became their entire Analytics product suite. What we saw during that time is that **the current world’s infrastructure** **wasn't built for the AI revolution that's coming**.

Traditional clouds were built almost 20 years ago. Their architecture and service sprawl weren't designed for agentic workloads. Nor was the UX thought for the developers who build them.

Not only that, but the economics don't make sense anymore. As AI agents become more autonomous, they're racking up huge bills from LLM calls, serverless tasks, and network bandwidth.

In the next decade, we're looking at billions or even trillons of AI agents coming online, each needing computing power, memory, and connectivity in every direction. The world’s current infrastructure isn't just unprepared, it's completely inadequate.

### Solution **💡**

We're creating **a new kind of infrastructure**. One that's not only built to make agents, but also to be operated by them.

Blaxel is a complete computing platform with all services designed and optimized for both AI Agents and their Developers — from the hardware to the user experience.

We offer two levels of APIs to give you full flexibility:

**1-** Our **high-level platform** lets developers to fast-track deploying agents without reinventing the wheel:

* Agent deployments as serverless autoscalable APIs — fully framework-agnostic
* MCP servers hosting
* AI gateway — with features like token rate limiting or model routing and fallbacks
* Built-in observability
* GitHub synchronization

**2-** Our flexible **low-level API** gives both builders and agents access to our core compute infrastructure to run agentic workloads fast and reliably:

* Sandboxes with &lt;25ms boot time — designed for agents to execute code or commands
* Batch processing jobs
* Headless browser VMs _(coming soon!)_
* Programmable AI gateway _(coming soon!)_
