# Launches

## Capacitive:  AI Agent Building for Knowledge Workers

**TL;DR:**

Capacitive lets anyone build agents that work on their behalf. Connect your platforms to us once, “chat” freely with your data, and automate workflows using only your words.

[Launch Video](https://youtu.be/7d0EwiiHirQ)

**🔋 Capacitive, the solution:** 

Work happens in what can feel like a million places — Slack, Confluence, Google Drive, HubSpot, Jira… you name it. Capacitive is a delightfully taut web platform that allows you to run work across all of those platforms and hundreds more, all from one place.

Connect your platforms to us once and “chat” with your data freely… we make asking one-off questions like “what’s my monthly burn?” or performing tasks like “find all clients in my pipeline who haven’t created an account and follow up with them” brilliantly simple. And building lightweight agents for repetitive tasks happens with just a few sentences.

With granular tool filtering, robust role-based access controls, and airtight security permissioning, Capacitive allows knowledge workers and companies alike to turbocharge their operations using AI worry-free.
