## QualGent: AI Quality Assurance agent for mobile software testing

Today, we’re launching QualGent, an AI Quality Assurance agent for mobile that helps companies catch more bugs and ship much faster without hiring more human QA testers. One customer hit **#3 in the App Store’s Education category** by improving their app quality and release process with QualGent.

https://youtu.be/k1eSm6QvO9c

**🐛 The Problem: Mobile QA is a Bottleneck**

Shipping a great mobile app is hard. Even top teams struggle with flaky end-to-end tests, missed edge cases, and regressions that slip past CI.

Script-based automation tools like Playwright, Selenium, Appium or Espresso break often. Visual diff tools miss functional bugs. And in-house human QA teams can’t scale with today’s release cycles.

CTOs, VPs, Directors of Eng, and QA leads at fast-growing companies are asking:

> Why does our test suite say green when our users say broken?

**🛠️ Why We Built This: From frustrated mobile engineers to reinventing QA**

We’ve been mobile engineers at Google who shipped products like Android XR, Quick Share, Stadia, and foldable phones. We’ve seen firsthand how slow, flaky QA can derail a great product. We built QualGent so teams can catch real bugs faster, ship with confidence, and stop wasting time maintaining fragile tests.

We're grateful to be part of the YC community and excited to bring modern AI to a painful, expensive problem that slows down almost every mobile team we’ve met.

**⚙️ The Solution: QualGent - Your AI QA Copilot**

QualGent uses computer-use LLM agents and vision models to mimic a real human tester:

1. You describe a test in natural language (“Sign up, go to settings, change theme”)
2. Our AI agent runs that test on real iOS and Android devices in the cloud
3. It taps, swipes, scrolls, types, speaks, listens - just like a human
4. And gives you video, logs, and test reports for every step

No more rigid scripting. No more emulator-only limits. No more test maintenance hell.

Our AI agent self-heals, handles dynamic pop-up content, navigates unexpected UI changes, and catches bugs like a pro.

Our vision is to close the loop on software creation. As AI enables engineers to code 100x faster, QualGent eliminates the next bottleneck—software verification—unlocking truly scalable, end-to-end product-driven software development.
