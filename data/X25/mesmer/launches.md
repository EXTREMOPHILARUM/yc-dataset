# **Enter Mesmer**

**Mesmer gives founders and CTOs full context on their org without drowning in meetings or chasing people for updates**. It watches the work itself — code, specs, designs, meetings — to build a live, detailed picture of what’s going on. Our customers can keep their teams fast, flat, and focused as they scale — and free from busywork.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90395&key=user_uploads/178/c0f0220d-62be-4e38-9246-ecd3c2493a79)

We are starting with **an AI chief of staff for product development orgs**. Every week, it delivers a report showing what actually happened across all in-flight projects: what moved forward, what shipped, and what’s stuck. Founders and CTOs can then ask questions and get in-depth answers based on their team’s actual code, specs, and designs, like:

* Why is this project slipping?
* Who’s the best engineer to unblock it?
* What architecture did we use in this repo?
* How does the business logic work for this categorization engine?
* What changed in the roadmap last week?

**We’re already live with 10+ companies worth over $3B** and have reviewed over 70M lines of code along the way. We’d love to help you, too.
