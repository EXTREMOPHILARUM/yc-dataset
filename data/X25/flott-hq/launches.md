## Flott HQ - AI-native Operating System for Fleet Management

# **TL;DR**

Flott helps transport and logistics companies run leaner, smarter, and **more profitable operations.**

**Flott unifies everything transport and logistics teams need in a single, AI-powered control center** — connecting operational data (planning, gps, fuel, etc.) and financial data (revenue, customers, orders) in one place to deliver true end-to-end visibility. Flott’s AI agents act as an extension of fleet managers, automating repetitive tasks and optimizing workflows. This enables companies to focus on what truly matters: growing the business.

https://youtu.be/P6UUBwV7kVk

**🚨 The Problem**

**Fleet operations today are burdened by complexity, silos, and inefficiencies.**

Most teams rely on a patchwork of disconnected tools, leading to:

* Business owners **lacking clear end‑to‑end visibility**
* **Manual workflows** and duplicated effort
* **Endless back‑and‑forth** between dispatch, drivers, and admin
* Delayed invoices, lost payments, and shrinking margins

As operations grow, so do the headaches — making it harder to scale without losing control. Many companies end up losing money without even realizing it, buried under operational inefficiencies and lack of visibility.

**✅ The Solution**

Flott unifies everything transport and logistics teams need into one AI‑powered control center.

📆 **Planning**: AI-assisted planning and dispatch

📍 **Live Tracking**: track your asset position but also resource consumption like fuel, mileage, driving hours…

💸 **Invoicing & Payments**: Fast, automated billing and payment tracking — Dynamic and customizable pricing strategies

👥 **Collaboration**: Collaborate within your team to get things done faster with the help of our **AI Agents**

📊 **Insights**: Powerful analytics to reduce costs and increase efficiency — Track the KPIs that matter in real-time and get in-depth AI-based insights from operations to finance

**Traction:** We are already at $200k ARR, growing 45% month over month, all of it through word of mouth.
