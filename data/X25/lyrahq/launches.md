## Lyra: The final meeting platform

Hey YC community 👋

We’re excited to share Lyra, the final meeting platform.

AI notetakers clutter meetings. Multiple tabs separate knowledge.

Lyra brings meetings and work together in one clean workspace.

You can work on docs with AI and your team live, no screenshare required. Create follow-ups and action items as you talk. Ask your meeting questions after the call. Access auto-transcribed calls and instant summaries. Everything stays in one place with full context across every meeting.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=94508&key=user_uploads/1617789/f9014ef2-a26a-4734-bfef-3348b05afca0)

Lyra isn’t another notetaker. It’s a meeting platform with AI built in. You can host, record, transcribe, and collaborate all in one tab. The AI sits alongside your team to help you work during and after the call, instead of adding another window or attendee.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=94508&key=user_uploads/1617789/9a37d1c6-2b08-45dd-a76e-b4556a8615da)

Each meeting becomes a shared workspace where conversations, notes, and tasks connect automatically. The AI captures what matters, answers your questions, and keeps knowledge flowing between teammates. Nothing gets lost after the call.
