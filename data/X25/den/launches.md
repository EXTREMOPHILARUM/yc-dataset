# Launches

## Den: Cursor for knowledge workers

### **TL;dr**

Den is Cursor for knowledge workers.

We make it 10x easier to build AI agents within a Slack/Notion-like platform.

https://youtu.be/OBpZ4RaV_9Y

### **The Problem**

AI agents are great, but impossible for most people to use.

At our previous startup, engineers would ship features during their lunch break using Cursor, while the rest of the team drowned under repetitive tasks.

We believe everyone, not just engineers, deserves 10x leverage on their time.

### **Our Solution**

Den is where you and your team create and collaborate with AI agents. It unifies your entire productivity stack behind one natural language interface, allowing you to create AI agents that work with your team in realtime, and can keep working while you sleep.

We want to deploy trillions of AI agents into the mainstream workforce and unlock the most productive era in human history.
