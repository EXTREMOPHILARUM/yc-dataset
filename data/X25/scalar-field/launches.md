### **The Problem We're Solving**

Why does anyone log in to a trading terminal like Bloomberg or FactSet?

Because — whether consciously or subconsciously — there’s a **hypothesis in their mind**. Something they're trying to validate as they jump across **charts, news, fundamentals, and macros**.

But here’s the issue: **Terminals today are built like giant dashboards**.\
What traders and analysts actually want is to **test and validate ideas**, not just observe data. So we asked ourselves — why not **rebuild the terminal from scratch** to do exactly that?

---

### **What We’re Building**

At **Scalar Field**, we’re reimagining the trading terminal from the ground up using **intelligent agents**. We started with retail traders as a way to build credibility with institutions—and already have around 800 paying traders, over 34,000 signups, and **$74,000** in monthly revenue.

Our goal is simple:\
**Let users test any market hypothesis instantly — and intelligently.**

We’re building a terminal that can:

→ **Run compute-heavy backtests** across multiple datasets (fundamentals, prices, macro, alternative data)\
→ **Spin up agents** that reorganize dashboards based on new signals or events\
→ **React to live market changes** and even **initiate trades** when conditions match your criteria\
→ **Persist memory across sessions** — so your research trail is never lost\
→ **Chain multi-hop workflows:** e.g., _If ETF flows spike and earnings surprises drop, highlight affected sectors and show next trade ideas_

It’s not just data access — it’s **agent-driven insight and action**.
