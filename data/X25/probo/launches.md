## **What’s broken**

Compliance.

As an auditor, I asked companies for the same checklists and sent the same doc requests, over and over. Founders wasted time filling in boxes without thinking.

Looking back, it was mindless busywork that frustrated everyone.

## **What we are building**

Probo acts like your compliance **team.** You tell us how you work, and Probo does the rest:

1. Set the _right_ requirements - not overkill
2. Handle docs, risks, controls, evidence
3. Run the audit
4. Keep everything up to date afterward

Founders shouldn’t lose weeks to this stuff. **Even one day is too much.**

## **What’s next**

Three things:

* **Open source**: Commoditize existing tools 
* **AI powered:** Agents to continuously monitor and maintain things up to date
* **No third-party trust:** proofs are end-to-end verifiable
