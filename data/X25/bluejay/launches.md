# Launches

## Bluejay - Quality Assurance for AI Voice Agents

**TL;DR:** Bluejay **learns** about your agent, **mimics** your customers, and **simulates** every customer interaction **_BEFORE_** you release your product. With Bluejay, **simulate 1 month of customer interaction in 5 minutes.**

If you are building a voice agent, let us[ run a sanity check](https://cal.com/rohanv) for you.

**Mission:** enable developers to trust their AI voice agents.

**Problem:** We were **sick and tired** of manually call-testing our voice agent 50 times before every release. Turns out, **so was everyone else** building voice agents. 

**Solution:** So, we built a platform that **does 100x the testing we could ever do, in minutes.** From there: fix the bug laundry list, re-run Bluejay’s test suite, and push to prod[.](https://youtu.be/bpWu_0TWIP4)

**Think about it:** SaaS has robust, feature-rich E2E testing platforms, CI/CD, and regression testing. _Why don’t AI voice agents have the same?_

For us, building Bluejay wasn’t a “let’s see if this works” idea. 

It was a no-brainer. 

**Key Features:**

* **Mimic: Hyper-realistic Customer Simulations:** Generate a custom workforce of **digital humans** to test every conversational turn of your voice agent. Our digital humans speak/have varied languages, accents, background noise, tone, delivery, emotion, and vocal punctuation. They’re better at being customers than your real customers…
* **Skywatch: Production Observability:** Another no brainer. Why stop at testing your agents pre-production? We tell you what went wrong with production calls, and how to fix it.
* **Research-backed Evaluation:** Bluejay learns about your agent’s goals, and tells you how close it got to achieving them. And of course, the standard stuff: latency, redundancy, hallucination, tool calls, interruptions, sentiment… anything worth knowing, really.

Voice agents **_need_** automated, scalable testing. And we won’t stop at voice.
