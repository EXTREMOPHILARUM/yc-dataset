## Auctor: AI agents that streamline enterprise software implementations.

Hey YC! 👋

We're Xinan, Will, Matt, and Sky—co-founders of Auctor. We’re building an AI-native platform that streamlines enterprise software implementations.

**TL;DR:**\
Enterprise software implementations (Oracle, SAP, Salesforce, etc.) are messy and expensive, plagued by shifting requirements, misaligned teams, and obsolete documentation. Auctor's AI agents solve this by automatically capturing requirements, generating artifacts (like SoWs, proposals, architecture docs, user stories, and more), and seamlessly syncing everything across your tools, eliminating costly delays and change orders.

🎬 **Launch Video:** https://youtu.be/A-T2gwZCOMM

## **🚨 The Problem**

Every implementation team—whether at software vendors or tech consultancies—knows this pain:

* Fuzzy, incomplete requirements at kickoff
* Tribal knowledge buried in notes and scattered docs
* Endless manual edits across SOWs, user stories, test plans, and more
* Costly rework, misalignment, and change orders

Despite billions invested annually, over 70% of enterprise implementations still experience significant delays, overruns, and failures.

## **🚀 Our Solution**

After over 300 customer calls, we've spent the last several months building what we think every implementation team needs:\
🧠 AI agents that join your discovery/refinement calls,

✍️ capture every requirement,

📄 generate artifacts—from SOWs & design docs to user stories & configs,

🔁 and keep it all in sync every time something changes.

**It’s like having a sales engineer, solutions architect, and a delivery lead...all working 24/7, automatically.**
