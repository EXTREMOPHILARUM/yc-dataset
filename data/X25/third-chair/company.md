# Third Chair

> Automating complex legal workflows with human lawyers in the loop.

Third Chair automates complex legal workflows at scale, supported by an on-demand network of top lawyers to step in when you need them.

We’ve started with revenue-generating workflows like IP enforcement, giving media companies visibility into who’s using their content and helping them turn unauthorized ads into a predictable income stream.

 The biggest media and entertainment companies in the world rely on Third Chair to enforce their intellectual property, unlock licensing revenue, and accelerate deal flow.
