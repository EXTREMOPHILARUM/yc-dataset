# Launches

## Third Chair: Agents for in-house legal teams

# TL;DR

Third Chair is building agents for in-house legal teams. We’ve started in media/entertainment, where our agents can identify IP infringements, collect evidence, and help recover millions of dollars in damages at scale. 

# 🛑 Problem

In-house legal teams are constantly underwater with a million tasks on their desk. Many of these are **repetitive**, **manual**, and **mind-numbing**: 

* Our customer at a music distributor spent hours tracking down brand contacts, then weeks following up with different emails just to reach the right department.
* Our customer at a major record label spent weeks reviewing thousands of social media ads and manually logging results in a spreadsheet.
* Our customer at a music publisher spent a majority of his time on email follow-ups for unpaid invoices, unsigned contracts, and unanswered emails.
* Our customer at a music rights investment firm didn’t have time to draft individual demand letters for thousands of infringements they wanted to pursue.

When you're buried in a million tasks, it can be hard to prioritize what matters most to your business's bottom line. At worst, you can miss opportunities that could turn the legal department from a cost center into a **profit driver**.

# ✅ Solution

That’s why we’re building Third Chair for in-house legal teams, starting in media/entertainment. Our customers delegate end-to-end workflows to Third Chair so they can operate at scale: 

* Our browser agent detects uses of your copyrights, trademarks, or name-image-likeness across the web.
* Our outreach agent finds the right business contact, verifies deliverability, and sends follow-ups.
* Our contracts agent drafts demand letters for human review, incorporating the context gathered in previous steps.

> This is definitely the best product I’ve seen in this category. Every other vendor just sends us CSV data files from outsourced data labelers. - _Top 3 Music Company_
