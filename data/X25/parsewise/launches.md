**❌ The Problem**

Business experts waste hours each week on **manual, repetitive data tasks**. Extracting and validating information from complex documents is slow and error-prone. As a result, analytics, benchmarking, and reporting **processes are brittle and expensive**.

**Current AI solutions** like ChatGPT are black boxes that generate **unreliable outputs**. Intelligent document processing tools require technical expertise, resulting in limited productivity gains.

**The data monkey era is over!** We believe that business experts have struggled with complex documents long enough. We 10x their productivity while improving the quality of their outputs.

**✅ The Parsewise Solution**

Parsewise extracts trusted information from complex documents, supported by these core features:

* **End-to-end Traceability**: We trace every single output (text, number, date, etc.) to its exact source and highlight it with bounding boxes. _No more black boxes._
* **Granular Human Control**: Business experts directly review and modify data extraction logic and consistency check strategies. _No more re-prompting._
* **Exhaustive Processing**: Our agents exhaustively parse data, proactively flagging issues. _No more second-guessing._
