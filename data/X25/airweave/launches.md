# ❌ **The Problem**

* **Agents struggle with vague or informal requests** like _”Resolve that one Asana ticket about auth configs”_ or _“cancel Alex’s Stripe payment for Taylor Swift tickets”_
* **Typically, then agents hallucinate when they can't find data,** or rely on inefficient chains of function calls
* **MCP encourages agent-app interactions, but MCP servers alone don’t make workspaces semantically searchable**

# ✅ **Our Solution: Airweave**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90465&key=user_uploads/1896666/c686c20f-62ac-454c-8d3b-d43ce9c857c4)

* **Airweave connects to productivity tools, databases, or document stores makes their contents searchable for agents**
* **Bottom line:** Your agent gets a simple search endpoint. Your users get reliable, personalized experiences

# **Features**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90465&key=user_uploads/1896666/d8e186b6-cc60-4b97-802b-f3ef29cf2a4a)

* **Open-Source**
* **Simple yet powerful search interface** via REST or MCP
* **MCP-compatible:** Turn any app into a semantically searchable MCP server
* [**Python & TypeScript SDKs**](https://docs.airweave.ai/sdks)
* **Connect to your agent to your favorite apps** like Google Drive, Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar, HubSpot, Notion, Jira, Slack, Dropbox, Stripe, Github, etc.
* [**White-Labeled Multi-Tenancy**](https://docs.airweave.ai/white-labeling) to provide OAuth2-based integration across user accounts with secure boundaries
* **File Handling:** Supported formats: DOCX, PDF, PPTX, XLSX, HTML, TXT, PNG, JPEG

# **Examples of How Customers Use Airweave:**

* Legal AI assistants searching Google Drive/OneDrive to give accurate Perplexity-like answers
* Agents for engineering managers scanning GitHub, Notion, and Jira to understand codebases and draft design docs
* Compliance agents verifying marketing materials against current Dropbox financial data in regulated industries
