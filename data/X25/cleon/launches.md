## Cleon: AI Agents for Software Implementations

Hi everyone! We’re Ricardo, Rohan and Alexandros, and we are building Cleon — an end-to-end AI software implementation platform.

**TL;DR**

Cleon captures knowledge during the planning and discovery phase, automates the generation of key artifacts and documentation, and feeds that context to teams of AI agents for complex data migrations - pulling in a human when needed.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BGwga9vq9TM

**The Problem:** Software implementations are a resource drain and revenue bottleneck for SaaS companies and system integrators:

* Misalignments between stakeholders delay project kick-off
* Shifting requirements force implementation teams to constantly re-write SOWs and BRDs
* Bespoke, manual data transformation is time-consuming and error-prone

**Our Solution:** An AI-native platform that proactively guides you through implementations, from signature to go-live:

* **Implementation Source of Truth:** Cleon captures information from customer calls, emails and internal communication channels to track evolving requirements
* **AI-Generated Documentation:** Our agents generate SOWs, BRDs and PRDs on your behalf with full knowledge about each customer
* **Auditable, AI-Powered Data Transformation:** Cleon’s AI agents index complex datasets and transform them to your schema with a clear audit trail, so that you can trust the outputs
* **Human Approval - Only When Needed:** Each implementation is unique, so we use human-in-the-loop workflows to handle edge cases

**Why are we building this?**

We’ve witnessed first-hand how long implementation cycles can delay ROI for customers.

At Palantir, Ricardo saw enterprise implementations that took anywhere from months to years.

Rohan previously worked at a fintech startup with 3-9 month customer implementation timelines. He also spent time building AI agents for SAP custom code modernization.
