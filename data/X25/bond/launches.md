⚠️ **The Problem**

* **Data fog:** At 30 employees, you juggle 5+ tools that don’t talk to each other; by 100, it’s 20 tools…eventually, visibility vanishes as you scale.
* **Hunting answers:** Every question spawns meetings, email chains, or Slack searches. 10 people pause work just to pull reports.
* **Missed moments:** No real-time insight means lost chances to unblock projects or close deals…you’re stuck in reactive fire drills.

\
**🛠️ Meet BOND: Your AI Chief of Staff**

* **Ask Donna Anything**: A chatbot that has the answers to all of your questions. Your single source of truth across every tool in your stack.
* **Presidential Brief**: Each morning BOND sends a one-page snapshot via Slack or email: what moved, who’s blocked, and your top 3 priorities. No more all-hands, just the intel you need to start your day.
* **Live Dashboard**: Central view of KPIs, project statuses (with owners), and team capacity.
* **Pattern Radar**: Automated alerts on stalled projects, team overload, churn spikes, and outliers.
