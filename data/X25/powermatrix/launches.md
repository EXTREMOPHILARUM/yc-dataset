## PowerMatrix: Efficient and Compact Power Supply for AI Hardware

**TL;DR**

PowerMatrix delivers highly efficient and compact power supplies for cutting-edge applications—from GPUs and data centers to drones, EVs, and aerospace. Based on proprietary technologies, our power supply systems can reduce energy loss by up to 50%. We’ve built working prototypes and are piloting with world-leading partners.

# **The Problem**

AI computing heavily relies on data centers where every watt of electricity is processed and converted by multiple stages of power supplies—from the power grid to processors.

Currently power supply solutions face significant challenges:

* **Inefficiency:** Data centers consume 5% - 10% of global electricity, while 20% of that (100TWh) is wasted on power supplies.
* **Size Constraints:** Power supply is given very limited space in server trays and on GPU board surface but required to provide much higher power.
* **Performance Gaps:** It is required to provide a voltage conversion ratio over 100 and provide 1A/ns dynamics for robust operation of future advanced AI computing processors.

# Our Solution

Based on proprietary technologies, we provide the reverlutionary **single-stage vertical power delivery solution:**

* **Single Stage:** directly convert high voltage to &lt;1V low voltage, eliminating inefficiencies multiple stage power conversion chains.

* **Vertical Power Delivery:** minimize power transfer loop, locating directly underneath computing chips.

Results vs. incumbents:

* **80%** smaller form factor
* **50%** less energy wasted
* **10×** faster dynamic response

Deploying our modules can save customers **$10 B** annually, cut **30 TWh** of electricity waste, and avoid **100 Mt** of CO₂ emissions each year.
