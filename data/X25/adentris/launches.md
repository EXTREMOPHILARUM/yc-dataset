**Problem:**

One small documentation mistake — like recording a medication time incorrectly — can destroy a family’s finances.

These errors are often made by exhausted clinicians after 12-hour shifts and are caught by automated insurance systems that instantly deny the claim.

**Hospitals don’t discover the problem until it's too late** — after the claim is denied, the audit triggered, or the reputation damaged.

Documentation errors are the invisible threat in modern healthcare. They’re not caused by negligence, but by broken systems: cluttered interfaces, overworked staff, and legacy EHRs that make it easy to click the wrong time, forget a discharge note, or miss a required signature.

**And it’s not just claims** — these small errors lead to failed audits, regulatory fines, delayed discharges, and increased burnout.

Hospitals end up with compliance teams that are reactive — chasing mistakes days or weeks after they happened, often without resolution. The cost isn’t just money. It’s morale, trust, and patient safety.

**Solution:**

Adentris is an AI compliance autopilot that prevents small documentation errors from becoming costly disasters in real time.

We integrate directly with hospital EHRs, monitor clinical activity in real time, and use AI agents to detect and fix issues — like missed discharge notes or wrong medication times — before they trigger claim denials or audits.

When a problem is found, our AI opens a quick conversation with the responsible staff member to confirm and correct it immediately.

**We don’t just alert**. We resolve — and help hospitals run smoother, reduce pressure on teams, and recover revenue that would otherwise be lost.
