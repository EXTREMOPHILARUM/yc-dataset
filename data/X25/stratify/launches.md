# **🤦‍♂️ The User Research Problem**

**Here's the brutal truth**: 90% of product failures happen because teams don't truly understand their users. You can’t really blame them because traditional user research **is broken**:

1. **💸 Focus groups are expensive:** $15K+ for 8 people in a room for 2 hours. Half of them drop out last minute, and you're left wondering if these insights represent your actual users.
2. **📅 Manual interviews take weeks:** Booking user interviews feels like herding cats. Calendars stay empty for weeks while your launch deadline approaches. And big ideas _cannot_ wait.
3. **📝 Surveys are unresponsive and impersonal:** Generic forms with dropdown menus can't capture the _why_ behind user behavior. You get some data, but absolutely no story.

**The result?** Teams ship based on assumptions, not insights. Features flop. Users churn. Millions of dollars wasted.

# **✨ Introducing Stratify**

What if you could ask "What do users think of our new product?" and get answers from hundreds of real users in under a few hours? That's exactly what Stratify does.

1. **🚀 Define your Research Questions:**

   Simply describe what you want to learn about your product or upload product materials: _"How do millennials feel about our pricing?"_ Our vetted AI researcher transforms your query into a comprehensive study framework in seconds.
2. **🎯 Smart Participant Matching:**

   Set demographic and behavioral parameters or simply specify a query like "U.S., Gen Z, Online Shopper, Foodie". Our AI automatically finds the perfect participants from our network of millions of real users or directly within your own company’s customers.
3. **🎥 Uncover Real Insights:**

   Watch our AI agents conduct hundreds of interviews simultaneously, asking follow-up questions that dynamically adapt to each participant’s unique responses. Uncover the _why_ behind user behaviors that surveys and focus groups miss completely.
4. **🧠 Instant Analysis:**

   Transform hours of interview content into actionable intelligence instantly. Search conversations semantically, generate reports and slides presentations, or simply chat with your data to find exactly what you need to know.
