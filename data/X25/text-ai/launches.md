Hey all 👋

**🔄 The Rapid Evolution**

You may remember [**text.ai**](http://text.ai), our AI that lives inside SMS, iMessage, WhatsApp, and Telegram chats. Tens of thousands of users and over 10M+ messages across 150+ countries in a rapid few months showed us something we didn't expect:

People didn't just want answers. We realized they wanted a social friend who understands and acts. Together.

Not a sterile bot. Someone with personality and character. Someone who understood their unique group, who's vegan, who hates horror movies, who always wants ramen.

They wanted a premium chat experience that was inherently social.

Why?

We distilled it down to one thing.

There is a place where we feel our happiest. All of us.

The group.

Humans are born into them. And thrive in them. But today, there are very few products to support them.

Places where groups can live. But not thrive.

And so, friends drift apart. Plans go unplanned. The chat fades.

So, we started building Alfi, our human-first, AI native approach to messaging.

**💙 Meet Alfi**

Alfi is your new social friend. The one who remembers everything, plans effortlessly, and keeps the crew together. Alfi also has a proprietary framework that knows when to talk (and when not to). We taught Alfi… social skills! No commands required.

It doesn’t simply process but understands context, group dynamics, and whether you’re actually asking for help or just chatting with friends.

Here's what that looks like:

* **Book restaurants together**, with Yelp built in(exclusive partnership), directly in the chat. No more "where should we go?" spirals. Reserve instantly.
* **Create images _with_ your friends**: drop your crew into funny hats, wild scenes, anything you can dream up. Multiplayer image gen, first of its kind.
* **Social calendar**: upcoming hangs, all in one place. Everyone is on the same page. No more managing separate cals.
* **Group Wrapped**: who's the chattiest? Top inside jokes? Now you'll know. Something we all have wanted for the longest time!
* **Reminders & Memory**: Alfi remembers your spots, your preferences, and nudges you to actually stay connected.
* **Customize your Alfi**: Customize how Alfi responds, creates, and helps to fit in with your group’s style. Custom AI for each chat.

Of course, you have your solo Alfi too! The group chat is simply sacred.

We're in the middle of a quiet shift. Gen Z is leaving broadcast social media. They're retreating to group chats, smaller, more intentional and personal spaces where they're honestly themselves.

But group chats are also where plans go to die. Someone has to be "the planner" or nothing happens.

**We believe the group chat is the real social network.** Where the funniest moments and real conversations happen in real time. With Alfi, we hope to bring you together more expressively, a lot more often.

This technology will bring humanity closer than it's ever been before. Not replace connection, deepen it. That's the future we're building toward.

**🤔 Why did we build this?**

Ever tried explaining to your mom how to download and use a new AI app? Yeah... that's why we built [text.ai](http://text.ai).

I built the first version just for fun, a personal travel companion for when I was hunting for hidden veggie restaurants on my first solo trip to Spain & Portugal (I'm vegetarian) and had no internet. It was ridiculously simple but solved a real problem for me.

Then something unexpected happened. Friends started adding our AI to their group chats. They used it to plan Vegas trips, settle restaurant debates, and even as a digital wingman for date nights.

The magic? No one had to download a thing. AI just showed up where they already were. In their messages.

Now we're obsessed with one mission: making AI accessible to everyone.
