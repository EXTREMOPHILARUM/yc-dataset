## LineWise - AI Copilot for Manufacturers

### **TL;DR**

LineWise is building an AI assistant for manufacturing teams to get their production lines back up and running - fast.

We connect siloed factory data (PLCs, maintenance logs, manuals) to triage faults, guide technicians to fixes, and capture every resolution for smarter troubleshooting in the future.

### **THE PROBLEM**

When a line goes down, it can take **hours** to identify the problem. Engineers and technicians chase PLC logs, flip through outdated manuals, and rely on the one person who “just knows.” While production stalls, tribal knowledge disappears, and the same issues repeat.

### **THE SOLUTION**

LineWise centralizes critical knowledge by integrating siloed data from IoT sensors, maintenance records, and machine manuals. It ranks the most likely causes and guides the team to a fix in just **minutes**. But the best part? It remembers every issue for next time and advocates for preventative actions.

---

### **How LineWise Works**

🔧 Upload an error code image or describe the issue in a message.

🤖 The AI provides a refocused problem statement for confirmation.

📊 The AI dynamically ranks possible hypotheses of causes by probability.

🧠 A human validates each hypothesis to identify the true cause.

🛠️ Resolve the issue by following a step-by-step guide.

🔍 Conduct further investigation to uncover the root cause.

📚 Enrich the knowledge base with insights from the issue and resolution.
