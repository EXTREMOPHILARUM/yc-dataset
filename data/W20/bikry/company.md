# Bikry

> Bikry: Helping SMB's in India go online 

Bikry App is a no-code platform that helps stores and services go online in India. We build them an online catalogue integrated with digital payments and pan-India delivery logistics within 5 minutes. Stores share their website and Android app link via Whats App and other social media channels to generate orders.
