# Blok

> A platform for fitness instructors to build their business and…

Fitness professionals have large, loyal followings, but don’t have the tools to build scalable businesses. Blok makes it easy for fitness professionals to teach live classes, sell content, get paid for differentiated services, partner with brands, reward their community, and build a unique brand online."Fitness entrepreneurs" will continue to unbundle from brands and will be armed with Blok's digital tools to monetize their audiences directly.
