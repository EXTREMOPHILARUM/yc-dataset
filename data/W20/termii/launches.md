# Launches

## Sotel: Network-Intelligent eSIM for global connectivity

Hi Everyone! I’m Emmanuel, co-founder of [Termii](https://termii.com) (YC W20), and we’re launching [Sotel](https://sotel.com), a network-intelligent eSIM app that gives you seamless mobile connectivity in 180+ countries — no SIM cards, no hidden fees.

📌 **TL;DR**\
[Sotel](https://sotel.com) is the easiest way to get mobile data globally. We intelligently and automatically analyze carrier coverage, switching you to the best local network in real-time based on your location, usage pattern, and performance needs.

**The Problem:**\
Millions of people — especially remote teams, travellers, and startup nomads — suffer from:

* Expensive roaming charges
* Poor network performance when travelling
* Fragmented eSIM platforms with no real-time optimization
* No visibility into where their connection is weakest (until it fails)

**Our Solution:**\
🔁 **Dynamic Network Optimization**: We evaluate local carriers in real-time and connect you to the one with the best speed, latency, and coverage — using network and signal intelligence.\
📱 **Global eSIM Access**: Download once, connect anywhere. We offer affordable plans across 180+ countries.\
📊 **Smart Usage Dashboard**: Track data usage, get alerts and optimize performance before a video call drops.\
🛡️ **Privacy-Focused**: No personal ID is needed for 95% of destinations.

**Why We’re Building This**\
At Termii, we power critical communication APIs used by 20,000+ businesses processing 2B+ transactions annually. As we expanded globally, we ran into the same issue our users faced — unpredictable and expensive connectivity. Sotel is our solution, built from the ground up for modern, mobile-first users.

**Who It’s For**

* International teams and remote workers
* Frequent travellers, digital nomads
* Businesses that want to keep their global staff always connected
* Developers integrating global IoT/M2M connectivity

**Traction So Far:**

* Pilots running across 4 continents
* Partnered with major telecom companies in Africa, US & Europe
