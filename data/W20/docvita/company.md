# DocVita

> Telehealth provider for India.

We make it easy for anyone in India to access healthcare over Zoom, Meet, or WhatsApp. We provide care in 11 local languages.

For every appointment, we provide a concierge who manages all logistics for the patient & handles the back-office work for doctors. With this, we have cracked the code to make telehealth work in India.
