# FUTURA

> Digitizing dentistry in LatAm

Futura is the first digital dental laboratory in LATAM. We provide intraoral digital scanners to dentists in exchange for a minimum monthly spend on lab services, enabling a fully digital workflow for crowns, bridges, and other prosthetics. We provide dentists with a platform through which they can manage their patients, cases, and in future payments
