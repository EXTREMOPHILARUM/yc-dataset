# Recall.ai

> The API to get recordings, transcripts, and metadata from meetings

Recall.ai is the API to get recordings, transcripts, and metadata from meetings. We offer several form factors to capture this data: 
1. Meetings bots
2. A desktop recording SDK
3. A mobile recording SDK

We integrate with meeting platforms like Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and more, as well as in-person meetings.

Over 3000 companies rely on our API today to process millions of hours of meeting data. We provide stable infrastructure that typically takes engineering teams 6+ months of time to build, and help our customers get up and running in days instead of months.
