# Metaplane

> Data observability for modern data teams

Metaplane ensures everyone trusts the data that powers your business. Data teams at Bose, Ramp, and Klaviyo use our data observability platform to prevent and detect data issues — before the CEO pings them about weird revenue numbers.

We do this with ML-based anomaly detection, end-to-end column-level lineage, and tools to help prevent incidents before they occur. You can monitor your entire data stack within 30 minutes.

The company is backed by Khosla Ventures, Y Combinator, and the founders of Okta, HubSpot, and Vercel.
