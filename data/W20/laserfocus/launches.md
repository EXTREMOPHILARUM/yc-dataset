## ⭐Laserfocus - Implement streamlined Salesforce processes for agile GTM teams

Okay, maybe it's our german OCD, but we can't stand it if products are not organized, fast and efficient.

But why do sales organizations let a clunky Salesforce setup slow them down?

#### **😐 The problem for GTM teams**

Companies that scale their GTM rely on Salesforce as the source of truth for all customer data. And they mostly rely on their Sales team to capture that data. But processes and data requirements change fast and different stakeholders just add more data fields into their CRM until it becomes too cluttered to be usable.

We found that the average Salesforce instance has 757 fields to maintain and update. It's a nightmare.

Your GTM team probably suffers from:

* loss of sales productivity due to time spent updating instead of selling
* messy and duplicate data that needs constant manual clean up so you can run reports

#### **🧿 Introducing Laserfocus - Implement streamlined Salesforce processes for agile GTM teams**
