## Handoff - AI Agent for Residential Contractors

### **The Problem**

There are 1.2M+ residential contractors (remodelers, home builders, etc.) in the US. They are severely understaffed and overworked:

* 40% of their time goes to paperwork instead of building.
* They rely on pen & paper or outdated software.
* The US needs 4.7 million new homes (and contractors need to keep up with that demand).

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jd85wN1hhBo

### **The Solution: Handoff AI Agent**

Handoff is the AI agent that helps owners of residential construction businesses automate their back office with AI.

Here are some of the things Handoff can do:

* Generate accurate construction estimates from short project descriptions and files (blueprints, photos, videos).
* Automatically create material shopping lists (with links to purchase).
* Send invoices, accept payments, and issue consumer loans.
* Build project schedules, subcontractor bid invites, daily logs, and more.

Handoff takes on the roles of:

1. Cost estimator
2. Project coordinator
3. Admin assistant

This reduces the operational burden of scaling a construction business by $500k+/year, at a price point of just $149/month.

### **The Secret Sauce**

How did we build Handoff? In short, we became obsessed with creating the world’s most accurate API for construction costs and a knowledge base of construction logic.

We work directly with top suppliers like Home Depot and Lowe’s to integrate their catalogs into Handoff, giving our customers real-time pricing and inventory.

We’ve also trained Handoff on 10,000+ construction cost estimates we completed by hand while running a manual cost estimating service in our early days.

## 🏗️ 1build - Plaid for construction cost data

**TL;DR: 1build is launching the API for construction pricing data. Developers can use our API to access up-to-date information on construction costs for workflows like construction estimating, procurement, and loan/insurance underwriting. The API can be either queried with GraphQL or easily integrated into any application with one line of HTML.**

﻿📚﻿ **Backstory**

As wild as it sounds, the construction industry still uses actual books that are published once a year to track construction market pricing. I previously ran the data science team at CloudKitchens where I needed an API for tracking live construction pricing data--but instead of an API I had to deal with these cost books.

![](https://t4207102.p.clickup-attachments.com/t4207102/24e83b10-e8f3-4b04-b043-a050c01c2eb1/image.png)

﻿📊﻿ **Problem**

Construction costs have been extremely volatile over the last several years. For example, lumber prices skyrocketed 400% and then crashed in 2021. At the same time, the construction tech space is booming ($5B+ is invested annually), but it still lacks its own "Plaid" for mission-critical market pricing data.

﻿🛠️﻿ **Our solution**

1build has an easy-to-use API as well as a JavaScript embed widget that gives you access to live pricing data on 68 million construction materials, labor, and equipment anywhere in the US.

﻿🚀﻿ **How it works**

You can either use our GraphQL API ([docs](https://developer.1build.com/1build-api-reference)) or JavaScript embed widget ([docs](https://developer.1build.com/1build-widget-reference/)) to integrate construction pricing data into your application. Sign up [here](https://app2.1build.com/public/register?signupType=SOFTWARE_DEVELOPER) to get your API key.

Here are just some of the use cases for our data:

* Your contractor sent you an estimate and you want to validate their quote vs market rates.
* You're serving bank underwriters who need to check whether their client's construction loan application has an accurate budget.
* You're working with insurance brokers who need to approve a quote for home repairs.
