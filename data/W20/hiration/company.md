# Hiration

> We help job seekers quickly create resumes that get 2X more interviews

Our SaaS product solves the different problems that job seekers face in the process of finding a new job - creating an efficient resume, writing a cover letter/email, updating their LinkedIn profile, applying to suitable jobs, interview preparation...
It's the 1st time in history that we've been able to create resumes using technology that surpass human made resumes.
This is a novel way to add active job seekers to our database of resumes as none of the incumbents are focussing on candidates' problems.
100k+ new active job seekers are added to our database every month, growing rapidly.
As the next step, we are launching a recruitment marketplace to close the loop.
