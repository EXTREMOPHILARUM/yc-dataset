# Global Belly

> Marketplace enabling social commerce for brands and content creators. 

Global Belly is a platform that helps creators and content companies add shoppable products to their content and monetize their audience. We drive social commerce for partnering brands, by matching their products to relevant content. 
We take care of technology, analytics and operations so that content creators can easily 
 create and start selling unique physical and virtual offerings in a matter of minutes. Global Belly has launched 100+ influencers in the food space and shipped thousands of products nationwide. Global Belly's co-founders are Ankita Sharma and Madhuri Sharma (unrelated). Ankita has a Masters in Computer Science (Carnegie Mellon) and MBA (NYU Stern) and experience working at Apple and Bloomberg. Madhuri brings the culinary and content marketing skills to the team, with a B.S in Film & Television (Boston University), a Culinary Arts degree (Institute of Culinary Education) and experience at Grey Global Group and The Food Network.
