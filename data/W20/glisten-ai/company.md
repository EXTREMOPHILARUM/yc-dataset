# Glisten AI

> Glisten AI automatically categorizes and tags e-commerce product data.

Glisten AI automatically categorizes and tags product data. With Glisten AI, e-commerce retailers and marketplaces can taxonomize millions of products with ease to enable better search, filtering, recommendations, and analytics.
