# BeGo

> BeGo Connects cargo shippers with truck suppliers in Mexico and US

In Mexico and US trucking demand outpaces capacity, yet 40% of the time trucks roll empty. BeGo uses Machine Learning to reduce empty returns, deadheads, and carbon emissions. 
BeGo is co-founded by two brothers, Jasiel, with 10+ years of experience in the industry, and  Ivan, ex-Tesla engineer with experience in product design and machine learning.
