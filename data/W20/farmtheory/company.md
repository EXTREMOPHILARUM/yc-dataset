# farmtheory

> India's first vegetable delivery service to restaurants with a hack

Farmtheory is a B2B agri-tech startup. We are bridging the gap between buyers and sellers of ugly produce by creating a farm-to-fork value chain. Ugly produce is freshly harvested fruitage that is cosmetically unappealing, hence left unsold at farms.
