# Benepass

> Fintech for corporate compensation

Benepass consolidates corporate benefits onto a single card, powered by our in-house fintech payments stack

We help companies take care of their employees through tax-advantaged benefits (e.g. FSA/HSA, dependent care, commuter) and company-funded stipends (e.g. wellness, WFH, professional development) all on a single card + mobile app.

With Benepass, HR teams can centralize all ancillary benefits and perks in the same place and make them a no-brainer for employees to use and engage with.
