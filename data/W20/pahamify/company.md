# Pahamify

> College entrance test preparation App for students in Indonesia

Pahamify is an online learning platform for K-12 students in Indonesia. We provide high quality educational videos which can be used to learn various concepts ranging from math, science, social sciences, and language based on the local curriculum. We also provide thousands of practice questions and modules which can be used for college entry test preparation.
