# Founders

## Armand Cognetta
**Founder**

Armand completed his PhD in Chemical Biology at the Scripps Research Institute under the supervision of Dr. Benjamin F. Cravatt. There he co-authored 19 scientific publications in prestigious journals such as Cell, Nature, and Science, as well as multiple patents, including a portion of the foundational IP for Abide Therapeutics (acq. Lundbeck) and Vividion Therapeutics (acq. Bayer). Prior to founding GP he was an early employee at the Flagship Pioneering start-up Inzen Tx (now Sonata).
