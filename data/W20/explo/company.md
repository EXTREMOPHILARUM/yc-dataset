# Explo

> Embedded Analytics and Data Sharing Platform

Explo allows you to build customer-facing dashboards and reporting, lightning-fast. With Explo, you can create custom white-labeled dashboards for your application, share custom reports, or embed an AI report builder.

- Dashboards: build fully white-labled dashboards for your application or enable your users to create their own dashboards from a library of charts you create

- Report Builder: embed a full self-service analytics experience into your application, allowing your users to create their own tables and charts from scratch

- Email: cadenced and one off emails to share analytics and data with your users

- Explo AI: embed an AI experience into your product to enable your users to gain insights into their data

Explo is built for product teams and developers. We power analytics of all sizes from YC companies to Fortune 500 companies.
