# Jamiphy

> TikTok for Musicians

Jamiphy is the destination for live music. All you have to do is watch, engage with what you like, skip what you don't, and you'll find an endless stream of live music that feels personalized just for you.

We make it easy for you to discover and create your own live music videos by providing easy-to-use tools to view and capture your greatest musical moments. Take your videos to the next level with Jamiphy!
