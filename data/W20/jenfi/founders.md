# Founders

## Jeffrey Liu
**Founder**

Jeff is the CEO and co-founder of Jenfi.

He founded GuavaPass, a fitness subscription service, which he ran as CEO from 2015-2018. The company expanded to 12 markets across Asia and the Middle East and was subsequently sold to ClassPass in 2018.

Jeff brings a strong operational and financial background from GuavaPass, BeachMint (corporate development), Victory Park Capital (credit hedge fund) and Lehman Brothers (investment banking). He has a MBA from Chicago Booth.
