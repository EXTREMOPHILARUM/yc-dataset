# QueryPie AI

> QueryPie AIP - Enterprise Ready Agentic AI Platform

QueryPie AI is an enterprise AI platform designed to securely operate AI agents in enterprise environments. Built on an MCP-based architecture, it connects internal data and diverse business systems while enforcing access control and centralized management. This enables organizations to execute AI Transformation (AX) with security as a foundation and extend AI adoption into practical automation and tangible business outcomes.
