# Founders

## Soham Sankaran
**Founder**

Soham Sankaran (https://soh.am) is the founder of Pashi. He was formerly a PhD student at Cornell working at the intersection of distributed systems and robotics, and a researcher at the Yale Institute for Network Science working on human agreement. Soham grew up in Bombay, India, where he built robots and nearly got expelled from both middle school and high school, and went to Yale for his BS in Computer Science. In his spare time, he writes for movies and television with his brother Sahaj.
