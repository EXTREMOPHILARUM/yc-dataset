## **Problem**

Let’s be honest - no one likes opening Quickbooks, yet it’s the place where your core financial data lives. This creates a disconnect between founders and their key financial metrics, leading to overspend, lack of confidence, and missed goals.

To get the answers you need, you’d typically export that data into a spreadsheet and then run some calculations.

However, the problem is that historically, your accounting software and spreadsheet have always been disconnected, relying on clunky extensions and periodic CSV dumps. This leads to broken, outdated spreadsheets and wasted time cleaning and managing data.

**Why do we still put up with this? We had to come up with a better solution.**

## Meet Modernbanc: If Quickbooks and Excel had a baby.

No more clunky sheets extensions or periodic CSV dumps. With Modernbanc you just connect your bank accounts and accounting software and point directly to live balances and transactions within a spreadsheet interface. It just works.
