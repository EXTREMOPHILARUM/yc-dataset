# Elemeno Health

> Just-in-time training and communication for frontline healthcare teams

Healthcare is delivered by teams – nurses, doctors, and staff, specific to each hospital, clinic, and department. They want to do their best, but cannot keep up with constantly changing information. Burnout >50%. Unprecedented turnover. Growing medical errors (the #3 cause of death in the US). $100B+ in waste.

Elemeno Health is a UCSF and Y Combinator-backed B2B SaaS for healthcare facilities– “a playbook in your pocket,” customized to support frontline teams with bite-sized training and communication at their fingertips, accessible 24/7 on any device. Our efficacy is demonstrated in our published results, passionate clients, and consistent renewals. 20x ROI. 75% decrease in medical errors.
