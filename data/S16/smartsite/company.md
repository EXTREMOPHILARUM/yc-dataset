# SmartSite

> Smartsite is a hardware device to protect workers from health risks…

Making construction sites safer for workers, and easier to manage for the firms that operate them. 

SmartSite makes active mitigation of health and safety risks automatic, and helps companies comply with regulation. SmartSite combines a rugged, site-deployed smart connected device, with a robust cloud infrastructure and accessible dashboards.
