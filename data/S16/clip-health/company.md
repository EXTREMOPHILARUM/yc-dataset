# Clip Health

> Testing & treatment at home, in minutes, for chronic & acute disease

At Clip Health, we are relentlessly executing on the vision to enable accessible, personalized, and actionable health testing, with immediate follow-up, across infectious, metabolic, endocrine, and chronic diseases and conditions. The Clip Platform brings our proprietary glow-in-the-dark nanochemistry in combination with connected easy-to-use hardware and delightful-to-use mobile apps to at-home, point-of-care, and enterprise settings.
