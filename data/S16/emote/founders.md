## Julian Golder
**Founder/CEO**

Julian Golder is the founder of Emote, a platform that uses real-time data analysis to help educators and clinicians identify students who need support and provide tailored guidance. He gets most excited working on problems that augment human capacity to build and sustain stronger relationships. Before Emote, he opened and ran a school focused on deep differentiation, developed a national curriculum, and was an early hire at Wyzant, helping scale differentiated instruction to millions of student
