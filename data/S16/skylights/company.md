# SkyLights

> Premium immersive cinematic VR entertainment for captive audiences

Skylights is a wearable immersive cinematic entertainment for captive audiences.  SkyLights started in the planes, as passenger inflight entertainment.

SkyLights enables patients and passengers to tune out of their environment and escape by enjoying premium cinematic entertainment using VR headsets.

Skylights integrates content licensing deals, secure software, headset hardware, and operations. All four are sold as a subscription service. Skylights is now developing in BtoC.

The company is incorporated in the US.
