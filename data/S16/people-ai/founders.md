# Founders

## Oleg Rogynskyy
**Founder/CEO**

Oleg is the CEO and founder of People.ai, an AI platform that drives intelligence and optimization for go-to-market teams. He is a native of Ukraine and moved to the US in the early 2000s, where he got his degree from Boston University. 
In early 2016 Oleg founded People.ai. Within 30 days of launching the company, they were accepted to Y Combinator,  and have raised over $100 million, with rounds led by Lightspeed Venture Partners, Andreessen Horowitz and ICONIQ Capital.
