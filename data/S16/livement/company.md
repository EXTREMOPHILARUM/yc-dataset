# Livement

> Livement makes selling tickets for events easy and safe for organizers

Livement (Boletomóvil in Latin America) transforms event ticketing with effortless and secure transactions. Our solution relies on controlling both the primary and secondary market, putting an end to ticket fraud and scalping. We empower event organizers with exclusive control over the secondary market, preventing ticket reselling on other web platforms. This prioritizes genuine fans and ensures fair revenue sharing among stakeholders.
