# Ready For Zero

> Personal debt and credit management. Acquired by Avant in 2015.

ReadyForZero creates online financial tools for managing debt automatically. ReadyForZero users create plans, make payments, monitor credit and track their progress via the web or mobile. 

As of 2015, customers paid down over $250 million in debt - on their own. ReadyForZero was acquired by Avant.
