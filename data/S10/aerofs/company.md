# AeroFS

> Secure Enterprise Collaboration.

AeroFS enterprise file sync and share & collaboration solution deploys on your infrastructure. Your files remain secured by your own security protocols, removing the risk associated with relying on your vendors to secure your data.

The service has a Dropbox like user experience.  Now, both the end user and the IT team can both get what they are looking for out of a file sharing tool.
