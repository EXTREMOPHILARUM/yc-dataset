# Leftronic

> Business dashboards

Leftronic is a Y Combinator-funded company that develops a web-based real-time dashboard application for monitoring a company's most important data.

Leftronic business dashboards retrieve a company's metrics from popular services such as Google Analytics, Twitter, Zendesk and others, as well as receive custom metrics through a super-simple API. The dashboards update in real-time and are designed for large screens. This makes it easy for teams to monitor their company's critical metrics.
