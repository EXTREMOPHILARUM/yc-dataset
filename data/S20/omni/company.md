# Omni

> Fast, up-to-date answers for Sales & Support

Omni is an intelligent chrome extension to help teams manage their internal knowledge and keep it 100% up to date. It fits in the natural workflow, integrates with current knowledge sources, and provides bite-sized answers when needed.
