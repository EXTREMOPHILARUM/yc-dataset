# Clover

> The all-in-one notebook for creatives to brainstorm, write, and plan…

We built a new style of text editor from the ground up to better support creative thinking. Clover lets you explode traditional, linear documents into an infinite "surface" for a more intuitive, visual way of note-taking, brainstorming, mind-mapping, and managing tasks.
