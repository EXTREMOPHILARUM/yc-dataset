# CoreCare

> Helping healthcare providers reduce administrative costs using AI

CoreCare is the leading AI-powered workflow platform helping Post-Acute and Long-Term Care (PALTC) providers achieve operational excellence. Trusted by 1,000+ locations nationwide, CoreCare increases admissions, optimizes reimbursement rates, improves collection metrics, and reduces administrative burdens—all on one intuitive platform.

At the heart of CoreCare is Collaborative Revenue Management, which unites essential data and teams on a shared platform. Our approach empowers employees—regardless of skill or tenure—to perform like experts from day one, collaborate with their peers on critical work, and give management unmatched visibility into performance across the organization.

CoreCare replaces error-prone spreadsheets and fragmented portals with streamlined workflows that drive measurable results. Trusted by leading providers, CoreCare redefines how teams work and sets a new standard for AI in long-term care.
