# QuestDB

> The fastest open source time series database

QuestDB is a high-performance, open-source database for time series data. It focuses on fast ingest, blazingly fast queries and superior developer experience with SQL and open formats.

QuestDB is designed from the ground up to operate as close to the hardware as possible -- eliminating ingestion bottlenecks and lowering overall hardware requirements.

QuestDB powers the infrastructure for companies in Financial Services, Crypto/Web3, Space Exploration, and Energy/Renewables.
