# Todos Comemos

> Taking from 1 to 100 the premade food industry in LATAM

With the mission of improving people's lives in LATAM by enabling pre-made food access to millions of people, TodosComemos is pushing forward the actual barriers of the industry in LATAM by: 

1. Developing pre-made and ready-to-eat healthy food above-edge technologies for the region, 

2. Enabling established products and brands of prepared food to go from 1 to 1000, offering them a competitive distribution one-stop solution to reach millions of new customers in a multichannel strategy

3. Building the first pre-made food subscription service including both pre-made and ready-to-eat meals for all needs.
