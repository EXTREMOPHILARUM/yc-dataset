# Founders

## Margaret Upshur
**Founder**

Margaret is the CEO and founder of Mobius Materials, a marketplace where manufacturers can buy and sell chips quickly, safely & at market prices. Previously, she led operations at Particle, a SF based electronics company, building a 40 person multinational team that launched 170 different products and shipped millions of units. She also worked at McKinsey where she consulted on manufacturing applications and helped open an office in Kenya. Margaret has an engineering degree from Duke.
