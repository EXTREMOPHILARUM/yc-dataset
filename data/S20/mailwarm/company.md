# Mailwarm

> Prevents marketing emails from landing in spam.

Mailwarm is an email warmup tool that improves your email deliverability and helps your emails land in the inbox, not the spam folder.

Mailwarm raises your sender reputation by automatically generating positive email interactions every day.

Mailwarm allows you to :
- get a perfect email activity 
- raise your sender reputation
- keep your email deliverability high
