# Founders

## Dileep Thazhmon
**Founder**

Prior to Jeeves, Dileep was the COO/Co-Founder at PowerInbox (www.powerinbox.com), an email ad server product used by 600+ brands including CNN, Disney etc. Dileep started and grew the company from 3 to 50+ employees, while maintaining a $35M+ARR and profitable business. PowerInbox  was recognized as #2 in Inc. Top 30 List, #3 Deloitte Fast50 List, #4 Crains Top50 List.

He previously worked in Venture Capital and graduated with honors from Stanford University Graduate School of Business.
