# Justo

> Helps restaurants take online orders directly from customers

At Justo, which means “fair” in Spanish, we aim to give restaurant owners access to technology that will help them increase their sales, at a more fair price. We provide restaurants their own online store, we teach them about advertising and marketing, and we automate the process for them. We also offer last-mile delivery service.

We are in Chile, Peru. Colombia, Mexico, Ecuador and Costa Rica

Our goal is to create a service that is profitable for restaurants, for Gigsters and for us.
