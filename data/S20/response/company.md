# Response

> Turn customer emails into ERP-ready orders.

Over 70% of B2B trade still happens through emails, PDFs, texts, and phone calls — not because companies prefer it, but because their customers do.

This is the default ordering workflow across industries, and despite decades of ERP software, it remains largely manual.

Response provides the infrastructure for this reality.
We turn unstructured customer messages into ERP-ready draft orders, so teams can review, approve, and move faster without changing how they work.

By automating the space between customer intent and systems of record, Response lets operators scale order volume without hiring more people, outsourcing labor, or retraining customers.

We’re building the missing action layer for how B2B trade actually works.
