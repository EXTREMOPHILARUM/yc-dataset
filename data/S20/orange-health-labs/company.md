# Orange Health Labs

> India's Fastest Diagnostics Service

Orange Health Labs is India’s leading on-demand diagnostics lab for urban consumers, offering 30-minute at-home sample collection and 6 hour reports across the top cities of India 

Launched in January 2021, Orange Health Labs is amongst the most loved healthcare brands in India and is backed by some of the world’s leading investors like Accel, General Catalyst, Y Combinator, Bertelsmann India, Amazon, and others.
