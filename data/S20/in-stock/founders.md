# Founders

## Ian McHenry
**Founder**

Founder and CEO of In Stock (S20).  Previously, cofounder and CEO of Beyond Pricing (exited), the first dynamic pricing software for the $180b vacation rental industry.  Beyond Pricing raised over $45m from top-tier investors including Bessemer Venture Partners.  Prior to Beyond Pricing, he advised large global airlines on dynamic pricing as a consultant at Oliver Wyman.  He holds a Bachelor's degree from Princeton University.
