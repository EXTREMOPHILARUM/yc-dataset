# Jeevam Health

> Digital holistic care for complex chronic conditions 

Jeevam Health (YC S20) is a healthcare app for holistic thyroid treatment.. Even after taking medication, 60% of women are having unresolved symptoms like fatigue, weight issues, and pregnancy challenges. 
Jeevam software assists doctors to diagnose the root cause of dysfunctions & recommend treatment protocol. The treatment revolves around nutrition, supplements, lifestyle changes, and medicine.
