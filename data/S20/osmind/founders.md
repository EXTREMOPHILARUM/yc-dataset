# Founders

## Lucia Huang
**Founder**

Lucia Huang is co-founder and CEO of Osmind, which provides software and data for precision mental health. Previously, she ran business and operations at Verge Genomics (S15) and helped scale the company from seed to A. She started her career in healthcare investment banking and investing. Lucia graduated with an MBA from Stanford and a B.S. Chemistry degree from Yale, where she first began to explore her passion for healthcare. She is a proud Californian and loves surfing, cycling, and hiking.
