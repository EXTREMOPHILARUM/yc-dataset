**Founder**

Seray Cicek is a medical technology innovator and synthetic biology enthusiast. She holds a MSc in Pharmaceutical Sciences and BASc in Engineering Science (Biomedical) from the University of Toronto. She worked at Sanofi Pasteur, Vaccine Bulk Manufacturing Department. Seray is the co-founder and CEO of LSK Technologies Inc.
