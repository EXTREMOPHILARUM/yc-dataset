# Parade

> Launch your company without hiring a designer

Through a series of simple questions, Parade’s machine learning-based software helps startup founders articulate the vision for their company, translate it into a design aesthetic, and launch it online in under 10 minutes. Parade makes it easy to create a website, marketing emails, social media posts, and more, all without a graphic designer.
