## ⭐️ 💖 Manifest AI - Introducing AI Shopping Pages

TL;DR eCommerce Brands, instead of getting their visitors to their static storefronts, can now how have a dedicated landing page with an AI shopping experience— trained on their eCommerce store + Sales data.

—

Hey Fam, we're Sonakshi and Ashutosh, and we're founders of [Manifest AI](https://getmanifest.ai) (it’s a product by [BIK](https://bik.ai)). We're on a mission to re-imagine how eCommerce is done today using Generative AI.

**❓The Problem**\
96% of online visitors drop off after visiting a Brand’s website because of multiple reasons, such as not getting what they wanted or not knowing where and how to find what they want.

**⭐️ Solution**

[Manifest AI](https://getmanifest.ai)’s shopping page allows brands to have a fully personalised AI shopping experience for each visitor where they can ask what they want, for example, “I have a date night, pls suggest something in black?” Manifest AI enables product recommendation, engagement & purchase depending upon the intent of the visitor.

**⚙️ How it works**

As soon as you sign up for [Manifest AI](https://getmanifest.ai), the LLMs gets trained on your eCommerce store & sales data, and generates an AI shopping page, the page can have different URLs depending upon the ads campaign making the AI shopping page to give a very personalised experience to each user. It takes care of search, product recommendation, and support on your storefront. It acts like a human sales agent on your eCommerce website.
