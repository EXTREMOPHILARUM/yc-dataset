# Founders

## Michael Simon
**Founder/CEO**

Michael Simon is Co-founder & CEO of Elucd, a technology company based in New York City. Previously, he established and managed the first-of-its-kind in-house analytics department for Barack Obama’s groundbreaking campaign for President of the United States, served in the Obama Administration, and founded a data science consulting firm.

He received his law degree and a BA in Political Science from the University of Michigan.
