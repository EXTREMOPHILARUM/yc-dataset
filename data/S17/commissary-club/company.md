# Commissary Club (formerly 70 Million Jobs)

> First agentic AI-powered community for 1 billion people with criminal…

Rising from the ashes of 70 Million Jobs, Commissary Club is a community built to address the most urgent needs of a billion+ people globally who have a record: employment, housing, access to medical and legal services, along with community, friendship and connections. 

Each user receives their own agentic agent who not only answers questions but actually solves problems: applies for jobs and housing, makes referrals, provides training, makes introductions, etc. 

We make money through advertising and sponsorships, sales of premium services to members, token transactions, events, etc.
