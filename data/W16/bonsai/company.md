# Bonsai

> The all-in-one solution for small businesses and self-employed.

Founded with the purpose of empowering anyone to work for themselves, Bonsai is the trusted back-office software solution for 500,000+ small businesses and self-employed around the world.

Bonsai helps individuals and teams manage their clients, deliver great work, get paid on time and track finances - all in the same place and integrated together to make running a business as seamless as possible.
