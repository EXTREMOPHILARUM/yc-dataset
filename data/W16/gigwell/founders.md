# Founders

## Jeremie Habib
**Founder/CEO**

Jeremie Habib is the Co-Founder and CEO of Gigwell, a digital booking platform that streamlines the booking process between live entertainers and event venues. The company has streamlined over $5B in gigs for DJs, musicians, comedians, and public speakers. Prior to launching Gigwell, Jeremie held senior Sales & BD positions at Cisco and several early-stage SaaS companies like OpenDNS where he helped grow the company from 0 - $100 m in revenue.
