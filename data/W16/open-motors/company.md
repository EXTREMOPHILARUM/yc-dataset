# Open Motors

> Open Motors (formerly OSVehicle) is a B2B company that is developing…

Open Motors (formerly OSVehicle): open, modular, electric vehicle platforms allow new and existing car manufacturers to develop their own models saving up to two years of R&D and millions of dollars in investments. At the same time, it is a reference platform for mobility startups to embed and develop their technology on.
Open Motors (formerly OSVehicle) is also developing “EDIT”, the first self-driving vehicle designed & engineered for services in white label, extremely modular & open. Fleets can last 10X more thanks to an easy repairability & upgradeability.
