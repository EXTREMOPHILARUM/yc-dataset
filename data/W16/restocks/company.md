# Restocks

> Buy sneakers and streetwear here

Restocks offers its users with instant notifications when the brands they like releases new items, restocks, and go on sale. The company offers its users with commerce and builds engaging shopping experiences on mobile platforms. It is funded by Y Combinator and angel investors. It helps its users stay updated with the brands they like, choose the updates they care about, view recent releases, restocks, and price drops, check stock and buy without leaving the app, and save profiles to check out.

Restocks is a U.S.-based company that was founded in 2015.
