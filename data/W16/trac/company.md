# TRAC

> An end to end timing solution for any scale athletic event. 

TRAC is a Chicago based startup that is focused on simplifying the track and field experience using innovative RFID technology, cellular data transmission, and unique application based software. Our integrated RFID timing system is the best in market for coaches and athletes in time based sports due to its precision, immediate feedback, and ease of use, thus saving time, effort, and money.
