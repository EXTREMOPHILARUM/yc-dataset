# Embark Trucks

> Self-driving semi trucks.

Embark Technology develops self-driving truck software. 

Embark was the first self-driving truck company to achieve an autonomous coast-to-coast drive, the first to reach 100,000 miles driven on public roads, and the first to open a network of transfer points.

Embark went public on NASDAQ in 2021 and was acquired by Applied Intuition in 2023.
