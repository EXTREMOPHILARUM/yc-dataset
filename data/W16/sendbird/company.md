# Sendbird

> The AI agent that doesn’t just support, it delights.

We believe in a future where AI unlocks human potential across all facets of life. We envision an A2A (Agent-to-Agent) world where AI agents connect businesses and individuals, empowering them to achieve more than ever before. It’s a future where the impossible becomes possible, and human achievement is no longer bound by time and cognitive load.
