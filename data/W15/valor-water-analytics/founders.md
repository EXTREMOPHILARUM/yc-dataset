# Founders

## PhD Christine E. Boyle
**Founder/CEO**

Dr. Christine Boyle is a partner at Burnt Island Ventures where she works with innovators to bring the next generation of water technologies to market. She was the CEO and founder of Valor Water Analytics, which sold to Xylem in 2018. She was the Vice President of Digital Product Development at Xylem for 5 years following acquisition.  Dr. Boyle is also Chair of the Ca-Nv American Water Works Association and a trustee of the American Water Works Association Management and Leadership Division.
