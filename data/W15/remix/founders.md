## Tiffany Chu
**Founder/COO**

Tiffany Chu is the former CEO & Co-founder of Remix. She has been named in Forbes' 30 Under 30, LinkedIn's Next Wave of Leaders Under 35, World Economic Forum's Tech Pioneers, and more. Tiffany served as Commissioner of the San Francisco Department of the Environment, and sat on the city’s Congestion Pricing Policy Advisory Committee. Previously, Tiffany was a Fellow at Code for America, the first UX hire at Zipcar, and has a background in architecture and urban planning from MIT.
