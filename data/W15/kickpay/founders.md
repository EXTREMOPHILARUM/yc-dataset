# Founders

## Andrew McCalister
**Founder/CEO**

Andrew is the co-founder of Kickpay (W15), which is focused on improving cash-flow for SMBs.

Prior to Kickpay, Andrew was Director of Business Development at Heyzap (W09), enabling advertisers to boost the discoverability of their titles and publishers to grow their monetization.

Andrew holds a Masters in Engineering and Management from the University of Cambridge (UK). For his Bachelors degree, Andrew completed a double major in Mechanical Engineering and Industrial Design.
