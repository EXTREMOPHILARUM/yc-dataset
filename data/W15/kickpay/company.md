# Kickpay

> A platform to finance inventory for e-commerce companies.

Kickpay is transforming how companies finance their inventory.

By leveraging a client's data, we enable them to access financing that was previously unavailable, allowing them to grow faster.

With Kickpay, you only pay for inventory after it sells. Not months beforehand.
