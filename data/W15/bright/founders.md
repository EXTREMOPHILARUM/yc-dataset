# Founders

## Jonah Greenberger
**Founder**

Jonah Greenberger is a co-founder of Bright, a Y-Combinator and First Round Capital company delivering solar subscriptions to homes in developing countries, first Mexico. He was most recently a Director at Chevron, where he helped build a renewable energy investment and construction group. Jonah received his MS in Thermodynamics and BS in Mechanical Engineering from Stanford University, where he was a Mayfield Fellow.
