# TeamNote

> Mobile Productivity(Slack for mobile workforces)

TeamNote as a company was conceived in 2014 after spinning off from AppTask, a Hong Kong mobile app development company specialized in enterprise mobile solution development. TeamNote’s mission is to help corporations unleash the productivity of their mobile workforce and maximize their working efficiency that mobile technology brings about.

TeamNote as a product started off its research and development in 2013 and was officially launched to the market in the first quarter of 2014. The first customer in Hong Kong is Cheung Kong (Holdings) Ltd.

In January 2015 TeamNote joined the renowned Silicon Valley accelerator Y Combinator as incubatee.
