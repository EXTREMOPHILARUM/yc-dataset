# Nextop

> Nextop optimizes the path between your app and backend. Client…

Nextop IO is a mobile content delivery network (CDN) that optimizes the path between mobile applications and back ends. It offers a client-side networking library that integrates with iOS and Android operating systems; network of proxies that speaks a custom protocol to optimize delivery times, throughput, and data and battery usage; and metrics to visually monitor and debug connection quality in its users’ applications.

Nextop IO was launched in 2014.
