# Outbound

> Create automated messages -- email, iOS/Android push, SMS and web…

Outbound organizes the messages you send around the actions your customers take. We allow non-technical marketers and product people to set up automated email, push notification, SMS or voice messages when you need your users to take an action, then test how well each message works.
