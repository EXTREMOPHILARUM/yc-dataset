# Bagaveev Corporation

> Launch small satellites

Bagaveev Corporation is a startup backed by Adam Draper's Boost accelerator (boost.vc)

and advised by Tim Draper (dfj.com) is designing, building and testing

3D printed aerospike rocket engines that will power both lower and upper stages

of a 3-ton, 35 foot rocket, designed to deliver 10-12 kg nanosatellites

to low Earth orbit and Sun-synchronous orbit.
