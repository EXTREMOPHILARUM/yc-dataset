# Wink Health

> The digital sleep clinic

Wink Health is a simple and effective way to get treatment for sleep apnea online. About 90% of people with sleep apnea go untreated. Our goal is to remove the barriers to getting diagnosed and receiving treatment.

We do this through a combination of a smartphone app that 'listens' for sleep apnea risk, telehealth consultations with doctors, and treatments shipped to people's homes.
