# Founders

## Varun Boriah
**Founder/CEO**

Varun leads Sales at Luminai, helping healthcare organizations drive operational efficiency through AI-enabled process automation. He was most recently SVP, Platform Solutions at Truepill where he managed the entire portfolio of solutions across the pharmacy, telehealth and diagnostics businesses. Prior to Truepill, he was VP, Business Operations & Expansion at Alto Pharmacy leading the nationwide expansion strategy and execution. Previously, co-founder and CEO of Lully, a YC backed company.

## Andy Rink
**Founder/CMO**

Andy is a Partner at Healthcare Foundry, where he’s been since 2022. A former surgeon, he’s focused his career on healthcare innovation and startups. He co-founded Lully, a YC-backed sleep health startup, and later launched and led Truepill’s nationwide telehealth business. Andy holds an MD from Indiana, a BE from Vanderbilt, and trained at Northwestern and Stanford Biodesign. He lives in Nashville with his wife, two kids, and dog, and enjoys golf, sports, music, food, and wine.
