# Dealyze

> Dealyze helps national retailers build exceptional customer and…

Dealyze is an in-store digital custom rewards and referral program with automated marketing tools for small businesses that is proven to bring back customers. Tailored to your specific needs, Dealyze ensures every transaction is a chance to build a relationship. Our mission is to bring a powerful digital marketing platform to any store. We hope to even the playing field by doing our part in building the next generation of business tools. Dealyze has the backing of top-tier investors including Y-Combinator. The company is headquartered in Silicon Valley.
