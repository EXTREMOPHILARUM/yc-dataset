# Dabble

> A synthetic photo studio for e-commerce

Dabble is a virtual photo studio for e-commerce powered by hyper-realistic CGI and computer vision. The demand for content in retail is skyrocketing due to the pandemic induced surge in online shopping, but product photography is still extremely slow, tedious and expensive. We’re building a platform to scale and automate product photography for every e-commerce brand.
