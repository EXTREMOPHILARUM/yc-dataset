# Kamcord

Our core thesis at Kamcord was that people do very interesting things on their phone screen but don't have a good way to capture and share those moments.

We've built 3 products in 5 years:
- A mobile gameplay recording SDK that was on 200M devices at its peak
- A live streaming community that reached 2M MAU and $2M ARR off virtual goods
- A mobile social app focused on editing and sharing screenshots and screen recordings
