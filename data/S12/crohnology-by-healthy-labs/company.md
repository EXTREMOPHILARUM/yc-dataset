# Crohnology (by Healthy Labs)

> We turn Patients into Researchers.

Crohnology is a global online symptom and health tracking network of over 27,000 patients with Crohn’s & Ulcerative Colitis. Performed research collaborations with University of North Carolina, the Crohn’s & Colitis Foundation of America, Genentech, and Qu Biologics among others.
