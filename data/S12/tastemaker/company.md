# Tastemaker

> Interior design marketplace and online retailer

Tastemaker aims to make interior decorating affordable and accessible to everyone.

We believe that everyone should have a beautiful home, and that a little help from a pro can make all the difference in the world. Our mission is to make hiring an interior decorator easy, fun, and affordable.
