# Kippt

> Kippt is a social bookmarking service

Kippt is a collaborative bookmarking system for professional networks that lets employees teach and share with each other on a dedicated platform.

When you collect something to Kippt, the page and the content will be searchable, organizable, readable on your devices and easily shared with other people.
