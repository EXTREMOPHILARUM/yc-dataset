# Founders

## Karri Saarinen
**Founder/CEO / Designer (Acquired by Coinbase)**

Karri Saarinen is Co-founder, designer and CEO of an issue tracking software Linear. Modern teams use Linear to streamline software development and to execute on their plans more effectively.

Prior to co-founding, Karri was the Principal Designer for Airbnb’s product design and the founding designer / Head of Design at Coinbase. Karri also co-founded a Y Combinator-backed startup Kippt, a non-profit Rails Girls and helped to bootstrap the startup community in Finland with ArcticStartup.
