# Flightfox

> A platform to plan, book and manage global and group travel.

Flightfox is a travel management platform for organizations with complex travel needs. We combine technology with human expertise to plan, search, book, monitor and manage global and group travel. We typically work with study abroad programs, humanitarian organizations, and other organizations with global reach.
