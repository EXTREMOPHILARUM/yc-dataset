# Tailor-ED

> AI-powered corporate learning for accelerated employee growth & skill…

Tailor-ED is a cutting edge AI-based learning bot calibrated to provide personalized learning experiences and recommend the best resources for learners. We are reshaping workplace training to meet the demands of a world where technology shifts rapidly and the race for new skills never ends. We help companies deliver workforce training faster, better and cheaper. We achieve this by identifying knowledge gaps and offering a learning assistant with personalized training paths, integrating smoothly into the daily workflow via tools like Slack and Teams.
