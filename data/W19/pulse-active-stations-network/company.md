# Pulse Active Stations Network

> We are a connected network of smart health machines in India.

We are Pulse Active Stations Network and are setting up a network of connected IOT-enabled “Make in India” Smart Health Machines known as the Pulse Active Stations Network.

We have conducted over 600,000 Health Check-ups and through our Pulse Active Station Smart Health Machines, we conduct mass screening for NCD risk factors such as Obesity, Hypertension and other factors to empower people to take informed decisions for theirs and their family's health.
