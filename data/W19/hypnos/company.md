# Hypnos

> A digital sleep journal that helps you get better sleep.

Hypnos is a digital sleep journal that helps you discover how your habits impact your sleep. It does this by taking in your mood, daily activities, and pairing them with data from your sleep tracker. Delivering insights on how to improve your sleep and habits.
