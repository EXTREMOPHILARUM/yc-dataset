🔴 **Problem** 🔴

* Sales managers don't have time to watch hours of recordings to find out what top performers are doing differently than the rest
* Coaching and training each team member individually is challenging and requires a lot of time and experience
* There is no one way to sell, and some things may work for some reps and not others

**🔷 Solution 🔷**

* **AI Sales Coach:** Delivers personalized, actionable sales coaching after every call to help reps improve their performance – without requiring team leaders to work overtime or have a PhD in data analysis
* **AI Assistant**: Automatically transcribes, summarizes, and extracts key insights from sales calls using battle-proven summary frameworks (e.g. BANT, MEDDIC, SPICED) and logs all relevant information into your Salesforce, HubSpot, or Pipedrive CRM - saving sellers time automating the tasks that they don’t like to do
* **AI Insights**: Provides X-ray vision across calls to quickly identify areas for improvement and gain insights that make a difference – without spending hours watching recordings, reviewing transcripts, or deciphering call stats
