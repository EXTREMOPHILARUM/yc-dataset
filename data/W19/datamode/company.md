# Datamode

> Datamode (datamode.com) is a tool for engineers to instantly diagnose…

We're building a tool to help data engineers diagnose problems in their data pipelines.  Datamode integrates with the technologies you use to provide a single place to start investigating problems and reduce the mean time to diagnosis.  This means higher data quality and improved reliability for the business.
