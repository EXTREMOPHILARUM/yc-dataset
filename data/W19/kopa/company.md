# Kopa

> Find furnished rentals and compatible housemates for 1-12 month stays 

Kopa is the marketplace for monthly rentals and compatible housemates. 

We believe that exploration and community make people whole. Kopa exists to inspire people to get out of their comfort zones, to form lasting relationships, and to live more fulfilled lives.

Kopa enables people to browse rentals, find roommates, and book and pay for spaces all online.
