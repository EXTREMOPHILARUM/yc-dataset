# Keynua

> E-signatures with identity verification for Latin America

Keynua is an E-sign platform that uses short video signatures which serve both to approve specific documents and provide the visual identity of the signatory, which is then confirmed using official records and social network profiles. All transactions are "notarized" on the Blockchain. Keynua stores encrypted versions of the identities of all signatories so that users may reuse them with other companies affiliated to the Keynua platform.
