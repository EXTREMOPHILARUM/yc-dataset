**The Problem: designing and developing fashion is very long, difficult, complex and expensive.** \
Designing and developing fashion is extremely difficult. Each step involves long and tedious work: sketching a design, creating a tech pack, drafting and fitting an initial pattern, making a sample, adjusting the pattern after fitting on a model (typically 2-3 fittings), modifying the pattern to fit specific fabrics, grading the pattern to fit all sizes, measuring each point of the pattern, making a pre-production tech pack, sewing a final sample, and finally taking pictures. The process is exceptionally complex, wasteful, expensive, and time-consuming, averaging over 92 hours per style. It costs brands $3,200 per new style on average. Some collections have more than 100 styles, costing brands hundreds of thousands of dollars and many months to complete.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=84021&key=user_uploads/275448/f68e3c78-590d-4a35-acb2-db94e30b7c01)

\
\*\*The Solution: AI that designs, develops, prepares for production and creates photos of new fashion styles in minutes, not months.\*\*\
PatternFast helps fashion companies and brands design and launch new collections in minutes instead of months. We save brands 90% of the time and 95% of the costs associated with the tedious, time-consuming, and complex process of garment design and development. Our AI software enables the instant design of over hundred billion of unique styles, complete with production-ready documentation, patterns, and automated sizing.\
\
