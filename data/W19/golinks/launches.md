## GoSearch Free: Personal Enterprise Search & AI Agents

### **TLDR**

GoSearch is agentic enterprise search that unifies knowledge across 100+ apps, lets you choose between federated or indexed search, and powers no-code AI agents that take action.

Today, we’re launching **GoSearch Free**, a free-forever plan for individuals to try semantic search, GoAI assistant, and custom agents.

### **❌ The Problem**

AI and enterprise search tools are exploding right now, but IT leaders are cautious.

* Most tools take risky approaches to data management
* Apps are increasingly locking down API access
* Centralized indexing struggles to keep up with modern enterprise needs

### **✅ The Solution: GoSearch**

GoSearch connects to your tools, understands context and roles, and takes action. Unlike most, it supports both **federated search** and **traditional indexing** for flexible data management.

What you can do:

* Semantic search across 100+ apps (Slack, Jira, Drive, etc.)
* Hybrid flexibility with full indexing controls + federated search
* Agentic AI that summarizes docs, extracts data, and answers questions
* No-code custom agents to automate workflows (file tickets, send invites, etc.)
