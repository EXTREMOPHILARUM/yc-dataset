# CareerTu

> Online school for Chinese to learn in-demand digital skills 

CareerTu is an online school for today’s most in-demand digital skills, starting with digital marketing, data analytics, and product design. We teach our students real-world business problems via capstone projects to make sure they are equipped with the must-have technical skills.

250+ top companies hired CareerTu students in 14 countries - including Google,  Facebook, Microsoft, Amazon, Spotify,  Pinterest, LVMH, TikTok, Expedia, Alibaba, Tencent, Meituan etc. 

CareerTu's mission is to teach students the industry’s most relevant and in-demand skills, making them the ideal hire at their dream companies, in return providing businesses with experienced and reliable talents to increase their efficiency.
