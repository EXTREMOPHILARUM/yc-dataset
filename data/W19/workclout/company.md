# WorkClout

> Safety & quality software for industrial sectors. 

WorkClout is enabling industrial labor to perform to their highest potential. 

WorkClout is a tool that helps industrial Operations & HR Teams make better decisions to improve production efficiency. WorkClout's SaaS tool centralizes on-the-job employee training, troubleshooting, safety/quality procedures, knowledge building, and performance tracking.
