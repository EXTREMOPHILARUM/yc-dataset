# Pengram

> Indoor navigation through augmented reality on your phone. 

Pengram is an indoor navigation application to allow anyone to map a building using just a phone and set up waypoints. Anyone can navigate in the building through Pengram to established waypoints. We primarily focus on helping fire protection, HVAC, and security technicians to locate devices scattered in a building during field work.
