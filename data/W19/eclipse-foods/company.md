# Eclipse Foods

> Eclipse Foods is building the future of dairy using micelles, the…

Eclipse Foods is building the future of dairy using micelles (the microscopic magic of milk.) Eclipse has developed the world's first plant-based dairy platform which makes plant-based products with the taste, texture, and functionality of conventional dairy. 

We are transforming the $500B dairy industry and have raised over $60M from leading investors such as Y Combinator, the chairman of Beyond Meat, the founder of Reddit, the creator of Gmail, Forerunner Ventures, Sozo Ventures, Initialized Capital, and more. 

Our mission is to create a more sustainable, healthy, and humane food system by making plant-based foods that require no sacrifice.
