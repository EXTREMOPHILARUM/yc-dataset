# Windsor

> AI Powered Personalized Videos

Windsor brings AI and deepfake tech to video personalization.

We create personalized videos at scale - where someone can record a single video of themselves talking to a camera, and then send millions of these to customers with unique names, at unique companies and different locations around the globe. Each video will look and sound like it was personally recorded.
