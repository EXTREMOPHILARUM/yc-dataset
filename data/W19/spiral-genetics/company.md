# Spiral Genetics

> Large Scale Genomic Data Mining Software

Spiral Genetics (YC W19) makes genomic data mining software to compare large populations of whole human genomes. Our customers are genetics testing companies, governments of countries, and pharma companies. We train our machine learning algorithms to enable large scale comparison of genomes to power novel discoveries, enabling new diagnostics and drugable targets.
