# Cuanto

> Building the operating system for developing world merchants

We are building the operating system to allow merchants in the developing world to sell online. We started by helping those in LatAm instantly launch online to monetize their audience and sell over social media.

We help merchants create a storefront, process payments, and manage fulfillment in one simple platform.
