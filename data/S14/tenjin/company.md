> Mobile marketing analytics and infrastructure

We track a bunch of metrics such as ad spend, ad revenue, in-app purchase revenue, subscription revenue, retention, etc. We also have a data warehousing product that lets our customers directly query the data we've collected.

Experts at mobile advertising, need help with handling all of this data and making a very technical front-end more user-friendly.
