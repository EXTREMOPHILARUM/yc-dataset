# HauteDay

> Gaming Meets Women's Shopping.

A community that finds the best new items in fashion.   Each day, HauteDay focuses on a specific fashion theme that our community then organizes into a shoppable collection.   

One of the most exciting aspects to HauteDay is that we are bringing attention and customers to some of the most innovative and up and coming fashion brands from around the world.
