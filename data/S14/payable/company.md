# Payable

> Simple payments and tax compliance for your contract workforce.

Payable is the easiest way to manage your external workforce. By simplifying and automating onboarding, payments, and 1099 compliance, Payable consolidates disparate and manual processes, while getting contractors paid faster! 

Payable is backed by Freestyle Capital, Redpoint Ventures, General Catalyst, Moment Ventures, Y Combinator, and others.
