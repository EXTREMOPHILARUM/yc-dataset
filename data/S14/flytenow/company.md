# Flytenow

> Ride sharing for small planes

Go flying with local pilots.

Thousands of private pilots fly every day with empty seats in their planes. We connect you with them so you can join in their same day, round trip adventures! Whether it be a common destination, lunch at an airport restaurant, or another day trip, Flytenow makes it possible to get off the ground and go on an adventure.
