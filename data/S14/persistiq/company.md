# PersistIQ

> PersistIQ is a free email automation platform to send a small batch…

PersistIQ is a free email automation platform to send a small batch of personalized emails.

We help hundreds of small teams (1-200 employees), particularly YC companies, send batches of personalized emails for sales, marketing, and other customer outreach. 

Our platform is simple to set up, allows custom sequence building, A/B testing, and analytics. Our integrations with Salesforce, Copper, and Zapier allow for a streamlined workflow.
