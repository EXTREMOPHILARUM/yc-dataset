# HeyDoctor

> HeyDoctor provides online primary care (like refilling birth control,…

Waiting rooms suck. Insurance and the paperwork are worse. We are a team of doctors, developers, lawyers, designers, and more working to try to fix it. Using our app, and soon the website, you can access medical care online right now and be done in 5 minutes. No insurance, video, or phone calls necessary and most visits are under $20. You can chat with your doctor anytime and anywhere.

Our services are currently available in Arizona, California, Connecticut, Florida, Georgia, Illinois, Montana, New York, Ohio, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, Virginia and Washington, but don't worry the rest of the country coming soon. Here are the medical services we offer so far:

-   Acne Diagnosis, Treatment, and Prevention
-   High Cholesterol (Lipid) Testing and Treatment
-   Start or Refill Birth Control
-   Urinary Tract Infection (UTI) Testing and Treatment
-   Vitamin D Screening and Treatment
-   Pre-Diabetes Screening and Referral
-   Hepatitis C Screening and Referral
-   Female Condom Prescription
-   Help Quitting Smoking
-   Find Out Your Blood Type

We are adding new services every week, so if you don't see what you need let us know. We want HeyDoctor and our partner health systems to be your first stop for healthcare and be your personal medical record.
