# numericcal, inc.

> Distributed AI for Industrial Process Monitoring and Optimization.

Turn-key solutions for anomaly detection, asset detection and tracking, and workforce management in automotive and manufacturing operations. We leverage modern Machine Learning to work with unstructured sensory data, like video and audio. By distributing the solution across the edge (on-premises) and multiple clouds (Azure + AWS/GCP) we lower the operating costs by 100x, while processing 1,000x more data than any cloud-based solution and provide better reliability and safety.
