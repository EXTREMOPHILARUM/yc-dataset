# Klarity

> Klarity: Your Always-On AI Analyst, Advisor & Coach

Klarity is Enterprise AI that helps companies like OpenAI, DoorDash, CrowdStrike and Zoom bend the G&A curve.

Klarity delivers two core capabilities:
(1) unlimited transformation; creating a digital twin of your business to identify gaps, risks and inefficiencies, and provide valuable insights into your business in weeks instead of quarters, and
(2) intelligent document processing that turns contracts, invoices, and POs into audit-ready data with 82%+ pass-through rates.
Klarity reduces OpEx, boosts accuracy, and empowers CFOs to deliver board-ready ROI.
