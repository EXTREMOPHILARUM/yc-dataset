# Xkit

> Universal CRM integration for B2B SaaS apps

Xkit helps B2B SaaS app developers to deliver deep integrations to all of their customers' CRMs with a single build. From allowing your users to interact with your app from inside the CRM to interacting with existing data including custom fields, objects, and workflows, Xkit gives you a standard API that works for every customer's setup, no matter how customized.
