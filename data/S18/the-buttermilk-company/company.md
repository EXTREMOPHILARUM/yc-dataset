# The Buttermilk Company

> Instant, fresh ethnic food that actually taste homemade. We're…

The Buttermilk Company is a direct-to-consumer business focused on bringing authentic and simple recipes from different cuisines to an American audience. We curate recipes from the local community and create packets filled with real, fresh ingredients and no preservatives. You can make a homemade meal in 5 minutes! We also give back a portion of our sales to the people who gave us the recipes.
