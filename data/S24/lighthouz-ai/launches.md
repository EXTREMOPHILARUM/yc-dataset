### **What Lighthouz AI does**

Lighthouz handles the entire freight billing workflow:

✅ Ingests **carrier bills, PODs, BOLs, lumpers, and invoices** from email and uploads

✅ Extracts and structures **all freight data** — rates, accessorials, remit-to, signatures, handwritten notes

✅ Verifies carrier bills against **rate confirmations / quotes**

✅ Detects exceptions like **duplicates, overbilling, missing PODs, wrong factors, and invalid accessorials**

✅ Auto-approves clean bills and routes **only true exceptions**

✅ Approves and submits **customer invoices within 24 hours of delivery**

✅ Sends automated **collections reminders** to customers pre- and post-due date 

✅ Creates a work queue for human collectors 

✅ Posts clean, validated data directly into the **TMS with full audit trails**
