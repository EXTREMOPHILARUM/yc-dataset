> ideate.xyz is an AI-powered 3D art suite on the web

ideate.xyz is an AI-powered 3D art suite on the web, taking your ideas from concept to production ready assets in record time.

Our powerful text to image to 3D pipeline rapidly generates model assets using the latest AI tech.

Rapidly generate and edit 3D model textures in your browser using our texture projection and inpainting editor.

Get your 3D assets production ready using our texture processing, upscaling and compression algorithms.
