### **The problem:**

Design tools are stuck at two extremes: powerful but complex, or simple yet limiting. This seemingly inevitable tradeoff affects millions of content creators trying to stand out with their thumbnail designs, the critical window to connect with their audience.

If you’ve had to design thumbnails for social channels, you've likely been here:

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=83411&key=user_uploads/620002/b533f0cd-3816-4699-b93b-a445528a046f)

You have a great idea, so you install the latest Adobe product. Suddenly, you're faced with a blank canvas and a dizzying array of options. Hours of YouTube tutorials later, you're ready to throw in the towel.

Time for Plan B. You load up Canva, pick a template that's "close enough," and tweak some colors. It doesn't really feel like yours, and it is so hard to stand out with a “Canva look” when there are over 3.7 million videos uploaded to YouTube alone every day!

### **Our story:**

As a design and engineer duo, we have spent years crafting creative tools in the WebGL and AR space. We've also built and mentored in the no-code space to help non-techies build out their online presence. Regardless of the domain, one challenge persists for creators — finding a design tool that’s both simple and powerful enough to bring a brand's vision to life. The reason why this problem persists in the market is simple - the inevitable tradeoff that every design software needs to make between power and ease of use. Typically, the more powerful a tool gets, the more complex it becomes. On the flip side, easy-to-use tools often limit creativity with rigid templates. 

But with AI, we have found the conviction to break this tradeoff. We are on a mission to create a design tool that actually puts your ideas in the driving seat, making the design process less about esoteric design language and more about the content team’s own creative impulses. Our goal is to empower the next generation of creators to build stunning brands and assets.
