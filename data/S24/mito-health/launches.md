**🤯 The Problem: Today’s healthcare is focused on sick care, not on keeping you healthy for longer**

* There is also no effective way to measure your current state of health beyond diseased or not-diseased
* Annual physicals with PCPs lack actionable steps on what to do next, leaving you confused with generic advice

**🎉 The Solution: Personalized insights and clear**, **actionable steps to keep you in your prime and stay away from disease**

* Personalized action plan with AI x Doctor co-piloted insights & health recommendations
* Organ system health status to know which are likely to fail first

**🩸 User Journey: Get your results in less than one week**

Schedule the blood draw at a Labcorp centre in 47 states across the US.

In a week, you will get your Mito biological age, detailed results, and a personalized action plan, including supplement, exercise, nutrition, and sleep strategies.
