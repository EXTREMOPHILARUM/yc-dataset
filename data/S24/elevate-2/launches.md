# **⚠️ Problem: Private equity-backed businesses struggle to use AI because of data challenges.**

📉 75% of M&A deals underperform due to **integration** challenges 

🔥 #1 cause of M&A integration problems is **data** fragmentation 

🤖 #1 hurdle blocking companies from using AI is **data** quality – #2 is **data** privacy & protection

# 🚀 **Solution:** **Elevate’s AI-powered data management platform acts as a translator enabling PE-owned businesses to use AI in their daily operations.**

Our platform helps PE-backed acquisitive companies (aka roll-ups) reach for data nirvana through:

1. **Migrations:** Transitioning data from legacy systems to the core tech stack,
2. **Warehousing:** Consolidating, cleaning, and enriching data across acquisitions and systems, providing a single source of truth, and
3. **AI Services:** Unlocking agentic AI automations built on top of clean warehouse data.
