# Keet

> Authentication for AI Agents

Keet provides authentication for AI agents. Developers use us to connect their customers accounts and perform actions on their behalf. 

Whether it be legacy software or consumer sites, Keet manages your users authenticated sessions and provides stable integrations on any web platform. Now your AI Agent can access healthcare data or send automated messages on LinkedIn via Keet’s API or SDK.
