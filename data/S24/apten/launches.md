## Apten: omni-channel AI agents for B2C sales/marketing

TLDR: Apten is an **omni-channel AI agent** that engages and nurtures leads across **text, call, and email, and webchat.**

Webchat specifically has been around for ages - but when a lead starts a conversation on the website and leaves halfway through, **they’re** **gone.**

Apten is not only able to start a conversation through webchat, but **proactively reach out via SMS/voice/email once the lead goes inactive.**

Today, Apten automates sales/marketing for hundreds of thousands of leads each month - we work with some of the largest enterprises in consumer services (industries including home services, healthcare, education, financial services, and insurance).

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=63At2HXNX4k

# **The Problem**

B2C services companies today **(insurance, home services, financial services, education, etc)** rely on a combination of call centers, dialers, email/sms marketing automation systems to convert new leads.

These systems fall short because:

* Human teams are required to scale up dialing/call efforts (leads slip through the cracks)
* SMS/email automations are typically one-way, static sequences without personalization

# **Our Solution**

Apten automates sales and marketing for B2C enterprises with:

* **Omni-channel capabilities**: set up a single conversational flow that works across SMS, voice, email, and webchat.
  * Apten maintains unified memory across SMS, voice, emai, and webchat (just like a human does) to **personalize conversations at scale**
  * Apten can also **switch between channels** at anytime to **meet a lead where they want to be met**
  * Move leads through different conversational flows depending on **where they are in the customer/lead journey**
* **Intelligent follow-up:** Apten will not only personalize follow-ups based on what it knows about a lead or a past conversation, but it will also dynamically adjust **when to follow-up based on a lead’s repsonse**
  * Personalized AI voicemails can be left on dial attempts that go to an answering machine
  * Apten is able to use local presence dialing to auto-match the number it dials from to one that best matches the lead’s area code
* **AI tools:** Apten can take actions with your systems (scheduling, dialers, quote APIs, etc)
* **Human in the loop:** Handles transfers to your human team at any time (cold or warm). Apten can also dynamically transfer to specific numbers depending on
* **Reliability:** setup custom guardrails that automatically detect non-compliant messages and proactively fix them before they’re sent to the lead
