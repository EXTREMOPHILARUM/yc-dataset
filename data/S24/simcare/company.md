# SimCare

> AI avatars to scale healthcare training.

SimCare is an all in one solution for training clinical skills in healthcare and behavioral health. From AI avatars that you can speak with, feedback systems trusted by the top programs in the country, to site placements tools that help you show your skills and find a job. SimCare is used across the board to train counselors, social workers, doctors and nurses.
