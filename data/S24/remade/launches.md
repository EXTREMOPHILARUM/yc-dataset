### **🧨 The Problem**

Traditional photography is labor-intensive, slow, and expensive. Enterprises spend thousands on photography, while smaller businesses can't afford it. Existing AI platforms struggle to capture the user's vision and maintain the consistency of input images.

### 🎉 **The Solution**

Remade replaces the entire photography pipeline with 3 steps.

1. Upload 10 photos of your item from different angles.
2. Direct the photoshoots through text prompts, or let Remade suggest prompts for you.
3. Receive studio-quality images in minutes.

![](https://t9014441523.p.clickup-attachments.com/t9014441523/1385a61e-b4fb-4535-af01-7dc92efa1706/image.png)

Remade reduces the costs of professional images by 100x and cuts delivery time from weeks to minutes. Work with us to amplify your marketing content across major social media platforms, including Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter, and more.
