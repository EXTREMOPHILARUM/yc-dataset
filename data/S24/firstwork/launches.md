# Launches

## 🚀 Firstwork - Rippling for frontline workers

**tl;dr**: [**Firstwork**](https://firstwork.com/) **is building Rippling for frontline workers.**

Hi everyone! We are Vardhan and Shubham, and we’re on a mission to build an all-in-one HR and compliance platform for frontline workers.

🧨 **The Problem:** Managing workforces in sectors like transport, delivery, and logistics is complex and inefficient. Many sectors have seasonal onboarding/offboarding and onerous credential validation that bottleneck HR Ops teams. Traditional HRIS solutions are scattered across multiple systems, which leads to delays, compliance issues, and high operational costs.

Working at Deliveroo and Remitly, where we spent a lot of time partnering with frontline workers across the gig economy, trucking, and logistics industries. Managing HR and compliance was a constant struggle.

🎉 **The Solution:** At **Firstwork**, we realize that worker onboarding, compliance, and management are not standalone problems but interconnected opportunities that require a unified approach. It’s with these insights that we’re building a compound product, with an initial focus on:

* **Smart Onboarding 📋:** Automates credential verification with Intelligent Process Automation and OCR, reducing admin burdens and speeding up time-to-productivity.
* **AI-Assisted Communication 🤖:** Enhances new hire activation for better engagement and faster integration.
* **Perpetual Compliance ✅:** Uses AI and OCR to ensure continuous compliance, tracking document expirations and updating records.

**Using Firstwork, Employees:**

✔️ Get onboarded faster and with less hassle\
✔️ Receive timely and relevant communication for better engagement\
✔️ Stay compliant with regulations effortlessly

**and Companies:**\
✔️ Avoid compliance issues and potential penalties\
✔️ Improve overall worker efficiency per hour \
✔️ Save time, costs, and reduce administrative burdens
