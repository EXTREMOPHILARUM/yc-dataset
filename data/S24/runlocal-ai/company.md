# RunLocal AI

> The first AI agent for optimizing ML model inference on edge hardware

RunLocal is the first AI-native platform for optimizing ML model inference on edge hardware (like Nvidia Orin and Qualcomm)

Our AI agent automates on-device model performance debugging and optimization. Our web platform replaces brittle validation scripts and manual experiment tracking.

With RunLocal, you deploy more capable/efficient models on your target hardware with a fraction of the engineering effort.

We’re working with market-leaders in autonomous vehicles, robotics, drones and smart cameras. We're backed by investors like Y Combinator and 468 Capital.
