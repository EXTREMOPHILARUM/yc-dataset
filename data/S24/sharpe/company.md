# Sharpe

> Agents for quantitative research and financial data science.

Sharpe is an AI data science agent purpose-built for quantitative finance, deployed on-premises at some of the world's most demanding trading firms and hedge funds. It absorbs your internal documentation, enforces your firm's methodology, and delivers completed, cited Jupyter notebooks at petabyte scale. Sharpe gives every analyst on your desk the institutional knowledge and output of your best.
