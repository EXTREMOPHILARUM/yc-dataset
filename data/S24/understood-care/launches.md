## Understood Care: AI Powered Patient Advocacy

### **What is Understood Care?**

[Understood Care](https://understoodcare.com/) is a virtual patient advocacy platform for patients with Medicare.  

Advocates are administrative assistants with healthcare expertise that unblock access to care for patients. Advocates can help patients find transportation, schedule appointments, apply for financial aid and much more. We use AI to help advocates deliver superior advocacy at scale. 

Patient advocacy became a new Medicare covered benefit in 2024. Most Understood Care patients pay $0 out of pocket.  

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=93210&key=user_uploads/1753723/334db23e-a29e-4633-bfb1-57c94259e184)

### **The Problem**

The American healthcare system is notoriously complex. 72% of insured adults lack confidence in managing their health insurance and benefits.

For the **68 million Medicare beneficiaries,** these challenges are even greater. Barriers in technology, finances, and health literacy often keep Medicare patients from accessing care, driving worse health outcomes and higher costs to the system.

The number of patients experiencing this problem is only growing. By 2030, the number of Medicare beneficiaries is projected to reach 80 million. 

### **How does Understood Care work?**

1️⃣ Patient signs up on website or by calling in.

2️⃣ We check the patient’s insurance eligibility and complete a patient intake with our clinical team.

3️⃣ We get the patient matched with the advocate that best fits their needs.

4️⃣ The advocate uses Understood Care’s clinical expertise and AI tools to resolve each patient’s unique care needs.

## 🍃 Ply Health: Get providers in network faster

### tl;dr

Providers need to get in network with insurance companies, but the process is time-consuming and an administrative headache. We ingest provider information and use AI to automate submissions, saving providers months of waiting.

### ❌ The Problem

Being in network with a health insurer is critical for both patients and providers:

* For patients, seeing in network providers means dramatically lower healthcare costs. This informs which providers they choose and whether they can even afford to visit one.
* For providers, being in network means a bigger pool of potential patients. It also simplifies billing and reimbursement.

However, the process of getting a provider in network is highly manual and fragmented. There are approximately **1,400** payers in the US, each with their own enrollment process. Some of these processes involve painstakingly filling out 50+ page forms.

On top of burning hours of administrator time, delays are costly. A single incorrect field can push back cases by months. With providers billing $3,000/week, this costs healthcare practices **tens of thousands of dollars per provider**.

### ⭐ Our Solution

We provide standardized questionnaires to collect all required provider information and documents. Our customers are then able to request a new network enrollment in just a few clicks instead of spending hours navigating insurance websites to find the correct forms.

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcMXSY2xKtBcO4T1bMrr_zU44eg-Shd1X_9JaM3loR6QgKdsuVHkOByIopdf09h2mBDLkIN563IS6LnLwhLnwQrvpoJKiG3qxYI6sAyIFgs6VCP0o-Eq-Y4niQc9koDRqzATtjI71T-pZFVZCBWP1jRbrE?key=qI7khypm2_NJ8SwqwOuqbw)

On the backend, we’re automating each part of the enrollment process. We also have an experienced ops team as humans-in-the-loop to make sure everything is processed correctly. This ensures that enrollments are completed quickly and accurately, our customers’ two most important KPIs.

Our product is currently optimized for behavioral health providers. We are working quickly to expand into other specialties.
