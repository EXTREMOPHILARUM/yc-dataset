# RentFlow

> The first 'Buy Now Pay Later' solution for Commercial Rent

RentFlow is the first ‘Buy Now Pay Later’ platform for commercial rent.

In the US alone, ~ 50% of small & medium businesses are behind on their rent. 
RentFlow is the first AI-powered platform to end the late rent cycle for businesses:
For landlords: Rent is paid in full and on time every month, at no extra cost. Cut the collections hassle.
For businesses: Rent is split into smaller, revenue-synced installments. Match fixed costs to real cash flow year-long.

No late rent. Just perfect timing on both sides.
