## Gauge - Show up in more AI answers

**Gauge helps your company show up more in AI answers.**

### ⚠️ Why should you care?

* [Google searches are falling for the first time ever](https://www.wsj.com/tech/ai/ais-threat-to-google-just-got-real-8280b4ee?utm_source=chatgpt.com) ([and Google itself is moving to AI answers](https://www.theverge.com/news/655930/google-q1-2025-earnings))
* [Gartner predicts AI chatbots will capture 25% of search volume by next year](https://www.gartner.com/en/newsroom/press-releases/2024-02-19-gartner-predicts-search-engine-volume-will-drop-25-percent-by-2026-due-to-ai-chatbots-and-other-virtual-agents)

With traffic leaving search, traditional SEO is dying. Our customers are seeing inbound AI traffic **double every quarter**.

### 💡How do we help?

We measure how often you and your competitors are referenced by AI, and derive insights from changes in the data over time.

We came across this problem through an Open Source Day hosted by YC. There, we heard from a huge range of speakers, including Vercel, Elastic, Resend, and Airbyte. A consistent theme across every speaker was the new inbound they were seeing from ChatGPT.

We decided to run an experiment: [productrank.ai](http://productrank.ai).

[productrank.ai](http://productrank.ai) lets you search for a topic, and see how the top models rank them. [Check it out here!](https://productrank.ai)

In just a few weeks, we reached the front page of HN, saw over 40,000 organic users, and found our first design partners.
