# Founders

## Joshua Ramette
**Founder**

Josh is the co-founder and CEO at Undermind. He has research experience in gravitational-wave astrophysics (LIGO), particle physics (CERN), atomic physics, and quantum information science. He did his PhD at MIT studying quantum computing architecture design and inventing techniques for generating entangled multi-particle states using atom-light interactions, with 3 patents pending and 6 peer-reviewed publications.
