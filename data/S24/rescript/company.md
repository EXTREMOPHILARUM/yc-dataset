# Rescript

> AI Regulatory Analyst for Enterprises

Rescript helps regulatory compliance and advocacy teams effortlessly track the constant stream of legal developments to always stay ahead and compliant. Rescript intelligently scans the web and analyzes regulatory changes and emerging issues, enabling teams to respond proactively and efficiently. Rescript helps Fortune 100 compliance officers juggling multi-state regulations, lobbyists tracking late-night amendments on the Hill, and trade association analysts scanning dozens of agencies at once. We’re profitable and backed by seed funding from YC, General Catalyst, and Floodgate.
