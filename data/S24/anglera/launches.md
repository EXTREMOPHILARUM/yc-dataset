## Anglera: AI Product Data Enrichment

**TLDR:** Anglera is the foundational AI layer that transforms messy product data into structured, searchable, enriched content at scale.

🎥 **See our AI agents in action**: https://youtu.be/zlLaT-Uv9Xs

---

### **🔥 The Problem**

While everyone's building AI apps, there's a massive infrastructure gap underneath: **product data is broken at scale.**

* **Poor Discoverability** - 20% of searches return zero results due to missing/inconsistent information
* **Low Conversion** - Data errors cause 23% loss in impressions and 14% drop in conversions
* **Slow Time-to-Market -** Weeks of delays getting new products live due to manual data entry

**Result:** Billions in lost revenue from broken infrastructure.

The tech stack looks like this today:

```
Beautiful frontend shopping UI/UX
↓
Intelligent search & discovery algorithms
↓
Messy, unstructured product data ← ✨ We fix this
```

### **🧠 Our Approach**

We've built AI agents that automatically transform any product data source into clean, structured, enriched content:

* **Input:** Messy spreadsheets, PDFs, images, brand websites, supplier feeds 
* **Output**: Complete, enriched product catalogs, continuously optimized for AI discoverability
* **Scale:** Process thousands of SKUs in seconds, not weeks.

---

### **🎯 Why Now?**

Two massive shifts are happening simultaneously:

1. **AI Search Explosion:** ChatGPT, Perplexity, and others are becoming primary product discovery channels
2. **Agentic Commerce:** AI agents are starting to make purchases autonomously

### **🔍 Problem**

Most rapidly growing e-commerce companies spend several hours each week managing their products across sales channels. Teams drown in spreadsheets, manually adding descriptions, images, and attributes for hundreds of items. This time-consuming process diverts focus away from actually growing their core business.

### **💡 Solution**

We’re tackling the $20B Product Information Management (PIM) industry by building the smartest platform for managing e-commerce product data.

<https://youtu.be/D3jKEOzbD-s>

Anglera empowers every e-commerce business with their own AI catalog ‘expert’ trained on thousands of top-converting product listings. We not only help our customers onboard new products faster, but also improve all of their listings to drive better sales.

**Key Features:**

* **✅ Faster Time-To-Market**: Instantly onboard products from any source (Website URL, Spreadsheet, PDF, etc.)
* **​✅ Better Sales**: Automatically enrich descriptions, images, & attributes to drive conversion
* **✅ Wider Reach**: Start selling on new channels with just one click (Shopify, Amazon, etc.)

We measure success for our customers with two simple metrics: 1) $ increase in sales, and 2) # of hours saved.
