# Spherecast

> AI Supply Chain Manager for CPG

Spherecast is building Agnes, the AI Supply Chain Manager for Consumer Goods. 

Agnes decides what to produce, where to make it, and how to move it through factories, warehouses, and channels – simulating thousands of options in seconds and executing the best plan.

We are building Agnes to shape the physical flow of goods in the world, turning it into something as programmable as code.

When we succeed, every consumer product you touch will run on the Spherecast stack – planned in the Spherecast OS, executed over Spherecast Rails, and optimized by the shared Spherecast Network.
