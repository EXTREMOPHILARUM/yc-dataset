### **Why does this matter?**

3 out of 4 businesses face payment delays, with the root cause beginning at the invoice creation stage.

**The Pain**: Most companies juggle contract softwares (ex: DocuSign/PDFs), spreadsheets, and a manual billing process riddled with errors, overdue payments, and hours spent data-entering.

**Why It Matters**: These inefficiencies lead to missed revenue, cash flow constraints, and wasted staff time. Founders often feel they need extra full-time staff just to manage invoicing.

### **How does Ari solve this?**

Signature to Revenue, _Automatically_

Ari takes over the moment a contract is signed:

1. Zero-Touch Contract Import: Signature lands → Contract processes → Revenue flows. All automatic, all instant. 
2. Intelligent Revenue Automation: Every signed agreement is automatically billed to its unique billing requirements
3. Real-Time Slack Updates: Approve the final output in Slack and ask questions as needed

While others offer yet another tool to learn, **your AI teammate takes over the moment a contract is signed.**
