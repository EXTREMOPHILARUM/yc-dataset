# Launches

## Paasa - Build wealth across borders

[Paasa](https://paasa.com/) gives high-net-worth individuals (HNIs) in emerging markets the same globally diversified portfolios as HNIs in America.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=83571&key=user_uploads/1349737/99f6cc47-f702-4c67-b7c8-9659ff814b35)

**What’s the problem?**

‘Pre-HNIs’ and HNIs in countries like India want to diversify their wealth internationally. A few cross-border brokerage apps exist, but most customers hesitate due to:

* Lack of guidance on foreign investments
* Remittance regulations that change every year
* Cross-border tax filing that their CFAs struggle with
* Slow outflows from local banks

These barriers keep global investing out of reach, reserved for the top 0.001% who can afford wealth managers and family offices. We’re changing that.

Our vision is to bring all the opportunities of the 0.001% to the pre-HNI without any hassle.
