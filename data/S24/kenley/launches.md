# Launches

## Kenley – AI for Consulting Excellence

### **TL;DR**

Kenley is the AI platform built for consulting firms. We help firms power critical workflows—from drafting proposal decks to scaling due diligence research—so consultants can focus on excellence.

[Demo Here](https://youtu.be/cCi7Uqu5WjU?feature=shared)

### **Problem**

Consulting firms sit on a wealth of insight, but it’s buried in decks, transcripts, and distributed tools. Most AI platforms ignore the unique workflows of services firms. The result? Hours wasted searching for precedent, rewriting the same slides, and missing what your firm already knows.

### **Solution**

Kenley WorkStreams connects your firm’s expertise directly to the work your teams deliver.

**Leverage your firm’s expertise**

Kenley connects siloed information across decks, meeting notes, and other operational systems into one unified search experience. Consultants can instantly surface precedent from past work, find relevant subject-matter experts, or reference similar deliverables. No more hunting through SharePoint or pinging colleagues for context. It’s the fastest way to apply what your firm already knows to the work in front of you.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90290&key=user_uploads/306562/7c6e4045-3142-4467-bd0a-afab99425e7b)

**Uncover conversational insights**

Every consulting project generates valuable insight through expert interviews, internal meetings, and client working sessions, but most of it goes unstructured and unused. Kenley captures and synthesizes these conversations to surface themes, recommendations, and supporting evidence. Results are traceable to source, so nothing is lost in translation. Teams can move from raw transcripts to cited insight in minutes, not hours.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90290&key=user_uploads/306562/d24e4fc6-ad08-48de-a731-4433ad8c8a1c)

**Generate high-quality decks**

Kenley helps consultants draft slides, summaries, and deliverables that reflect the firm’s voice and standards. Whether building a market landscape, summarizing client input, or structuring a SoW, WorkStreams saves time without sacrificing quality. It works with your templates and draws on your internal precedent to produce outputs that are 80% ready out of the box. Consultants spend less time formatting and more time thinking.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90290&key=user_uploads/306562/03d82e32-5c38-4301-8336-c82411ded7fa)

**Enterprise-Grade Data Security and Access Controls**

The Kenley platform is built with enterprise-grade data security at its foundation; robust access controls, role-based permissions, and strict data isolation. Kenley never uses your data for external training or 3rd-party storage. You stay in control of your information, with the assurance that every output respects firm policies and client confidentiality.
