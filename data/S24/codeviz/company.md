# CodeViz

> One living model of your software architecture

CodeViz creates interactive codebase maps to help engineers quickly understand and navigate code. 
 
Adopted by engineers at Amazon, Microsoft, and Roblox, CodeViz was founded by two ex-Tesla engineers to tackle the 75% of development time lost to reading code. Download today at https://codeviz.ai
