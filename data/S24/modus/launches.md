# 🤯 **The Problem: Headcount planning and reporting remains manual and painful**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=83088&key=user_uploads/1899616/0dd981e5-f59d-40ec-8ee8-53782bb12622)

Overhiring in tech has led to over 200,000 layoffs, costing companies billions. Workforce costs account for 70-80% of a company’s expenses, yet companies still make workforce decisions in error-prone, manual spreadsheets with incomplete data. Re-orgs, department changes add to the pain. Reconciliation is messy and time-consuming. Accuracy suffers. The cycle perpetuates. As Taylor Swift might say, _“I’ve seen this film before, and I didn’t like the ending.”_
