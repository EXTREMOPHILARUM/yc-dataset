# **The problem**

1. **No Visibility in Low-Network Zones** – When robots lose connectivity, **cloud-based diagnostics fail**, making it hard to track performance or troubleshoot failures in real time.
2. **Slow, Costly Root-Cause Analysis** – Debugging is painful, with teams sifting through **dense logs and complex graphs** instead of getting clear, actionable insights.
3. **Inefficient DIY Monitoring Stacks** – Robotics teams **waste months building custom diagnostics** because existing tools don’t work for their needs.
4. **Reactive Debugging Leads to System Failures** – Without **proactive monitoring**, small issues snowball into major downtime and expensive repairs.

# **Our solution**

### **🌐 Works Offline**

* **Edge-first diagnostics** to store and analyze data even when the network is down.
* **Smart caching & prioritized sync** ensure no critical insights are lost.

### **🔍 Faster, Clearer Troubleshooting**

* **Instant anomaly detection** surfaces failures before they escalate.
* **Real-time sensor & system health analytics** (cameras, IMU, LiDAR, CPU, memory).
* No more digging through logs—just **straightforward insights** when you need them.

### **⚡ Seamless Integrations**

* **Built-in Sensor Plugins & Robotics Middleware Support** – Off-the-shelf support for **Intel® RealSense™, Luxonis®, Orbbec®**, and more. Easy integrations for **ROS and e-Cal** —no extra setup required.
* **Deploy in minutes with standard Linux packages**—skip the custom integrations.
* **API-first design** lets you access diagnostics effortlessly and extend with your own **custom metrics plugins**.

### **📈 Scale up to Fleet-Wide Monitoring**

* Grow reliably from 1 to 10,000 robots with **consistent, structured data collection**.
* Identify **trends & patterns across groups of sensors and devices**.
* Works in **low-bandwidth environments**, ensuring continuous insights.

## Ember Robotics: Camera diagnostics for robots

# **tldr; Got camera problems? We’ve got solutions.**

[Ember](https://www.emberrobotics.com/) builds software to monitor and resolve robot camera issues - so you can reduce vision failures from the source.

Is robot camera reliability or degraded vision driving you nuts? Book a demo [here](https://www.emberrobotics.com/) or reach out to us at [founders@emberrobotics.com](mailto:founders@emberrobotics.com)!

### **The problem**

We’ve spent years working on vision systems for autonomous robots and found similar issues everywhere:

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=82673&key=user_uploads/1932951/335dd4a4-e0b6-4c6b-bb64-a4c9c0c60f7f)

☐ Robotics companies work on lean teams that have to do lots of things super fast - **hardware reliability is often secondary to core product features**

☐ **Camera failures cause large fires** because they float up the stack to critically damage perception, controls, and safety

☐ **Digging through layers of software / hardware to figure out what went wrong is painful** (ex. sometimes you spend hours goose-chasing a “perception issue” just to find a loose cable)

### **Our solution**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=82673&key=user_uploads/1932951/9c181ef2-e04a-498d-a839-35bfa75fd1dc)

**A camera diagnostics framework that ties in real-time data, logs, and reports to contextualize your robot’s vision health.** Support the full life-cycle of your robot with features like:

✅ Out-of-the-box tracking for common issues (disconnection, frame drops, time sync)

💬 Automated suggestions for error resolution

⏱️ Real-time alerts

🖌️ Image quality analysis tools to optimize computer vision

📈 Historical data analysis for predictive maintenance
