### **The Solution: AI that creates videos for you.**

VideoGen can automatically edit professional videos together in a few seconds, saving our customers time and the pain of hiring a video editor.

In just a few clicks, VideoGen can:

VideoGen does this all in over 100 languages and accents for a global user base.
