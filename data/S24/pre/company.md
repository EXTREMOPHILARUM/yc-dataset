# Pre

> The AI accountability agent for startup founders

Pre is an AI-powered execution system for startup founders who are serious about building real companies.
Set a 10-week goal, connect your tools, and get pushed to do your highest-impact work every week.
Pre doesn’t track effort… it tracks outcomes, and it holds you to a higher standard.
