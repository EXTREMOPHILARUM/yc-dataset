## 🤖 Affil: AI Compliance for Financial Affiliate Marketing

**TLDR;** Financial affiliate programs struggle to ensure affiliates are compliant with brand guidelines, leading to limited growth opportunities. [Affil](https://affil.ai/) monitors affiliate content, enabling companies to expand marketing efforts and maximize revenue.

_What is financial affiliate marketing?_ When you see content by The Points Guy or Forbes that talks about credit cards, checking accounts, etc, they get a kickback from the financial brand for anyone who successfully applies for the product.

### ⚠️ The Problem

Financial institutions (e.g., Capital One) or consumer fintech companies (e.g., Acorns) want to grow their affiliate programs but are completely blocked by compliance barriers. However, it’s expensive and time-intensive to do manual affiliate compliance & management.

On the flip side, affiliates are also dissatisfied with these financial affiliate programs. The majority have to work with disorganized & outdated middlemen hired by financial brands who take a significant cut of payouts.

### 🛠️ The Solution

Our AI compliance software monitors affiliate content so that financial companies know exactly what’s being said about them so that they can expand their marketing efforts:

* Track thousands of affiliate pages 
* Quickly identify compliance violations without needing human intervention
* Create support tickets for every issue, so affiliates & brands can keep track of errors

We’re also improving the affiliate experience:

* Offer higher payouts for conversions (since we have a lower cost structure)
* Provide a beautiful user experience & working APIs
* Proactively identify compliance risks & offer automated suggestions to minimize risk

For example (see below), the AMEX Gold Card underwent significant changes on July 27, 2024, but outdated affiliate content still exists on the internet (that’s not good). We caught this issue that both AMEX and Bankrate missed.

## Affil - AI Affiliate Marketer 🤝

**TLDR;** Our AI Affiliate Marketer helps companies launch & scale affiliate programs at a fraction of the cost.

**⛔️ The Problem**

Running a successful affiliate program is hard. Some common challenges brands face:

1. Competing with thousands of other brands on networks like Impact, CJ, or Awin
2. Reaching out to (many) content creators
3. Vetting affiliates to avoid spam and fraud
4. Ensuring affiliates follow brand guidelines, especially in regulated industries like financial services
5. Managing a growing number of affiliate relationships

These inefficiencies lead to poor experiences for content creators, who can struggle to monetize their work or feel ignored by brands. Currently, the most common solution is to hire an agency or full-time staff, but that’s expensive.

**🛠️ The Solution**

Our AI-powered affiliate marketer can integrate into any stage of a company’s affiliate marketing journey:

* Identify relevant content creators
* Handle affiliate outreach, contracting, & onboarding 
* Monitor affiliate content to ensure it meets brand & compliance guidelines
* Manage affiliate communications
