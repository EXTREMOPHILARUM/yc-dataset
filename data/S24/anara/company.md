# Anara

> AI for researchers

Anara is AI for scientific advancement. We help researchers find, understand and write scientific documents, together.

We started working on Anara two years ago. In that time:
- we’ve grown to 3M users spanning hundreds of major colleges and research-led companies,
- we are fast approaching $10M in yearly revenue,
- 600M+ people have seen our content across Instagram, TikTok and YouTube,
- and we raised $3M from YC and the founders of Github and Reddit.

We're a small, tight-knit team with huge ambitions. As a founding member, you'll be instrumental in helping us scale revenue from $10M to $100M and then $100M to $1B+.

If you’re excited by the idea of being a part of that, definitely reach out.
