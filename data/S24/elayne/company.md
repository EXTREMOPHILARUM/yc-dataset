# Elayne

> AI copilot for growing, organizing, and transferring family estates

Elayne an AI-powered estate settlement platform that helps families settle an estate with clarity and speed using AI plus expert review. We turn scattered accounts, paperwork, and deadlines into a structured workflow: asset discovery, document intake, next steps, and status tracking.

Elayne generates a verified step-by-step action plan based on institution and jurisdiction requirements. Families track progress in one place, share a single source of truth with executors and advisors, and move cases forward with fewer calls, forms, and surprises.
