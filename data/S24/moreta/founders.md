## Sam Yu
**Founder/CTO**

Sam is the co-founder and CTO of Moreta.

He studied Computer Science at the University of Waterloo and has extensive experience building and shipping consumer products.

As the Head of Research & Development at Stems, he led the development of their first mobile app and generative AI product. During his internships at Apple, Google, Meta, and Uber, Sam worked on high-impact projects, including developing the instagram.com search feature used by over 50 million people.
