## Dataleap - Perplexity for Consultants

**_TL;DR -_** [_Dataleap_](https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/dataleap) is a GenAI-powered market research platform for management consulting firms and corporate strategy departments.\
**Think Perplexity for Consultants.**

_Our secret sauce is combining the natural language interface from Perplexity with trusted, verifiable market data from best-in-class market research firms and the company’s internal resources. We already work with two of the three tier 1 consultancies (MBB) and industry giants like Uniper and Siemens Energy._ \
[_Try it out here!_](https://dataleap.ai/)

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=83457&key=user_uploads/1018869/02fe7c8f-50ea-4c20-8212-61614e1015b4)

# **Problem:**

* AI search tools like **Perplexity or ChatGPT are not cutting it for professionals analyzing markets**. The answers are either hallucinated or using low-quality sources.
* Consultants & corporate strategy departments need to work with **paywalled data** curated from expert market research firms like WoodMackenzie, Rystad, or S&P Global.
* They do not want to interact with 15 different platforms or badly implemented AI chats but with one platform that is close to them and aggregates everything.

# **Solution:**

1. Dataleap provides a **powerful AI Copilot** that aggregates and searches through all the relevant market data that strategy teams need to look at.

   ![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=83457&key=user_uploads/1018869/9632454d-b1c0-4d26-9a72-f646fa636a2f)

2. We always **highlight the exact sentence** in the original data where the information is coming from so that you can trust it and make real business decisions based on it.

   ![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=83457&key=user_uploads/1018869/328dfc41-53ae-42d2-8629-73e16cb1d72d)

3. You can also **upload your proprietary information** to have the full picture of all your available information within Dataleap.

   ![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=83457&key=user_uploads/1018869/124c9538-8375-4b79-88ca-6ec967a84530)

4. You can not just search through the documents but also conduct meta-studies of reports or extract your own datasets from documents with our **document matrix**

   ![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=83457&key=user_uploads/1018869/c44448c3-527d-4caf-a92b-984c7a1ace83)
