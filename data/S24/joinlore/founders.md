# Founders

## Terrell Marshall
**Founder**

Terrell Marshall is the co-founder and CEO of Lore.

Before Lore, Terrell worked on AI products with over 1 billion users at Google, like Google Photos, Google Payments, and Hotels Search, and as a software engineer at Workday. Before that, Terrell studied computer science where he met his co-founder Aditya Nadkarni and created a patent for an AI Physical Therapy Platform

## Aditya Nadkarni
**Founder**

Aditya Nadkarni is the co-founder and CTO of Lore. Together with his co-founder, Terrell Marshall, they help users transform their iMessage conversations into shareable stories. Aditya spearheads the development of their platform, drawing from his experience at Flexport's AI and document processing team, and Artera, a start-up streamlining doctor-patient communication.
