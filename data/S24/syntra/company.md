# Syntra

> AI chart review for speciality medical clinics.

Syntra provides real-time chart review for specialty medical clinics to support revenue cycle management, patient identification, and compliance. It replaces slow, retrospective workflows from insurance companies, patient recruitment teams, and third-party auditors. Syntra supports hundreds of specialty practices nationwide and processes $100Ms in medical claim charges annually.
