# Assembly HOA

> AI-enabled HOA Management Company

Assembly is an AI-native HOA management company on a mission to fix homeowner associations. Traditional HOA management is often reactive, confusing, and inefficient. Assembly takes a different approach by combining professional expertise with intelligent software to make communities run smoothly and decisions easier.

We exist to build trust, create transparency, and ensure well-run communities that protect and enhance property values.

Assembly simplifies day-to-day operations, automates routine tasks, and delivers the right knowledge at the right time. Our platform gives boards and homeowners clarity to make smarter, more confident decisions. 

We manage HOAs in San Francisco Bay Area and Greater Los Angeles.
