# **🚀 Introducing Emergent 🚀**

Emergent is your on-demand CTO and founding engineer, reinventing product development. Our state-of-the-art (SOTA) coding agents deliver production-ready, full-stack applications with stunning UI and real backend, transforming ideas into market-ready products. No developers are required.

We go beyond just prototypes and mockups. Emergent ships fully functional apps with real backend, databases, and integrations -- built and deployed without ever leaving the platform.

https://youtu.be/lT2dRng4fc0

### **😖 The Problem:**

Shipping products is hard for non-technical builders, due to expensive engineering talent, inflexible no-code platforms, or AI tools that produce half-baked prototypes. As a result, most ideas never make it past the whiteboard or overcome the barriers to real-world execution.

### **🤓 Our Solution:**

Emergent redefines how a product gets built. At its core, Emergent is more than a coding assistant. It's a fully integrated platform that autonomously builds, tests, and ships software. What Emergent stands for: 

* Conversation-to-code transformation: Describe your product in plain language — Emergent handles architecture, logic, and implementation.
* Fully Autonomous End-to-End Development: Our agents plan, build, test, and debug apps fully autonomously to produce shippable apps.
* Built-in deployment: Ship fast and learn faster. Emergent manages the entire product lifecycle, from concept to customers.

### **👷🏽 Why We're Building This**

Emergent aims to democratize access to advanced software creation, empowering new builders to turn their visions into reality without traditional barriers. We’ve watched great ideas get stuck on whiteboards because building them was too hard, expensive, or slow. As engineers and builders, we saw that AI could change this by writing code, truly understanding what’s needed, and building complete solutions.
