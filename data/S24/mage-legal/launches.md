## Mage: Legal Diligence for M&A and Financings. Minutes, Not Days.

Hi All!

**TL;DR:** We automate legal diligence for ECVC and M&A attorneys. Upload a data room, get flagged issues, and client-ready reports in minutes instead of days.

---

## What Mage Does

Mage is AI legal diligence software for attorneys. We scan entire data rooms, flag issues, and generate diligence reports in minutes instead of days.

**Document Processing**: Automatically classify hundreds of documents by type (NDA, employment agreement, IP assignment, option grant, etc.) and route for analysis.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97352&key=user_uploads/688868/b261058b-62cd-4988-a24d-2502fdad4753)

**Tabular Extraction**: Extract key provisions from every contract into a structured grid. Red and yellow flags highlight issues requiring attention: liability caps, change of control triggers, assignment restrictions, IP ownership gaps.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97352&key=user_uploads/688868/4143a0a6-391f-4943-8edf-3333f97ca3b2)

**Issue Flagging**: Identify problems before they delay closing. Cap table discrepancies, missing contracts, and non-standard terms.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97352&key=user_uploads/688868/c9c16c14-099a-480d-9aa0-c43dfb726bb5)

and disclose schedules (see below). Full coverage. No spot-checking. Better results for your clients.

## For M&A Attorneys

Larger data rooms. Compressed timelines. Mage enables your team to handle more deals without adding headcount.

**Memo Generation**: Generate client-ready diligence memos organized by practice group. Includes executive summaries, flagged issues, and document references.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97352&key=user_uploads/688868/8975c025-50b4-49e7-82ea-fc374ba8ccd4)

**Redline Comparison**: Track changes across negotiation rounds. Identify exactly what moved between versions.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=97352&key=user_uploads/688868/40ac5584-ca4d-4bdd-8e11-43e3b037ee97)

**Disclosure Schedules**: Auto-populate disclosure schedules from the merger agreement and data room. AI provides reasoning for each classification. Attorneys review and finalize.

Service more clients with your current team. Handle capacity spikes without scrambling for resources.
