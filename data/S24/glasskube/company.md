# Glasskube

> Software Distribution Platform for On-Prem, Kubernetes, Docker & BYOC

Managing enterprise software deployments shouldn’t feel like a balancing act. At Glasskube, we make deploying, managing, and updating software in on-premises, VPC, and air-gapped environments easier than ever.

Our Mission:
We empower software vendors to deliver exceptional customer experiences with less complexity. Whether you’re onboarding new clients, rolling out updates, or troubleshooting issues, our Software Distribution Platform streamlines every step.

The founders have been best friends for over 15 years, lived together in college, and even had a startup together. Philip was the CTO of a European Kubernetes-based company that was acquired in 2022.
