## Luc Succès
**Founder**

Luc is a 2 time VC backed founder. His first startup, Often, launched a keyboard app that enabled you to text your friends with Drake lyrics. Upon launch, Often blew up to #1 on the App Store and got over 400k downloads. He scaled his second startup Seasons, a rental service for high end menswear to $1mm in ARR.

Luc is a seasoned tech leader, having led teams at Artsy & Spotify. He is also an avid traveler, having lived and worked in over 50 countries worldwide.
