# Founders

## Ray Morgan
**CEO / Co-Founder**

Ray is the CEO of Vibe, making everyone a software engineer

Before Vibe, Ray spent 10 years at Stripe leading and scaling the core payments engineering teams (<$10B payments volume to well over $100B a year), launching new products (Stripe Issuing, new payment methods), and worked on Stripe’s enterprise readiness efforts to enable enterprise accounts like Amazon. Prior to Stripe, Ray started a company that got acquired by Ebay and led the frontend engineering team at Zappos.
