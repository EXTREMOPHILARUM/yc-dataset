## Zuni - AI Copilot For Your Browser

Hey, we’re Will and George from [Zuni.](https://zuni.app?utm_source=launchyc)

Zuni is an **AI assistant that works inside Chrome** as you write email, draft messages, work inside web apps and browse.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=89612&key=user_uploads/860429/8b303072-d7a3-49b1-a4c2-bd799b2f6083)

# what does it do?

As you browse the web Gmail UI in ‘Zuni Agent’ mode, Zuni can see which emails you’re reading, proactively analyse them to **provide summaries, identify action items and suggest _actually sensible_ reply options.** Finally it can **help you draft on-point replies.** You can prompt for edits, discuss the content of emails and even war-game how you should respond, treating Zuni like a thought-partner.
