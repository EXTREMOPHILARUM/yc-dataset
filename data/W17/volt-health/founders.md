# Founders

## Scott Wolf, MD
**Founder/Founding CEO**

Scott founded Aerin Medical to create non-surgical therapies to treat nasal airway problems, including nasal obstruction. Scott was also a founder of Zeltiq Aesthetics, the maker of CoolSculpting. Scott was previously a partner at Prospect Venture Partners and a vice-president at Frazier Healthcare Ventures, both leading life science venture capital firms. Scott received his M.D. from George Washington University and his B.A. from the University of Pennsylvania.
