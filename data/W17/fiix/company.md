# Fiix

> Fiix sends expert auto mechanics to your home, so you don’t have to…

Fiix offers auto repair services for its clients.

The company offers on-demand auto repair services in terms of battery, brakes, diagnostics, inspections, doors, windows, engines, exhaust systems, filters, fluids, fuel systems, heating, lights, sensors, suspension, steering, tires, and transmission. The company offers auto repair services at its clients' homes or offices.

Fiix was founded in 2015 by Zain Manji and Arif Bhanji and is based in Ontario.
