# Hivy

> An end-to-end platform for office management. We enable office…

Hivy is a task management tool for office operations teams and helpdesk system for employees. The platform enables office managers to collect employee requests, manage projects, assign tasks and notify employees when their requests have been completed.

Hivy was acquired by Managed by Q in 2017.
