# Neema

> Neema is an app for unbanked workers, which provides a checking…

Neema is an app that helps people manage their money as well as send money to other people. Neema is a financial solution for 70 million underbanked Americans. Users download their app, which is a digital wallet, where they can cash their own pay check, send money internationally to their families in 90 countries, and they also provide them with a debit card, which they can use in any store or ATM.
