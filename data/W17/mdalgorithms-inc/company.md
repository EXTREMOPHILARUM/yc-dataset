# MDalgorithms Inc.

> Democratizing Dermatology with AI 

MDalgorithms is revolutionizing dermatology by delivering convenient, effective AI-powered solutions for skin and hair care. Since pioneering machine-learning models for acne treatment in 2017, we’ve advanced to develop the world’s first AI-powered scalp analysis for hair regrowth.

Our proprietary AI technology, built entirely in-house, analyzes blemishes, lesions, and hair conditions with unmatched precision, utilizing the largest global dataset of acne and scalp images. Clinically validated and tested by dermatologists, our personalized treatment kits—MDacne for acne and MDhair for hair regrowth—are customized to each individual’s unique needs, combining over 100 medical and botanical ingredients for optimal results.

We empower users to track their progress, adjust treatments as needed, and access real-time support from our team of dermatologists and nurses. By doing so, we make personalized dermatological care accessible to all.
