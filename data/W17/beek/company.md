# Beek

> Leading audio subscription platform for Spanish content and creators

Beek is the app where latinos listen to the best audio content in Spanish from their favorite creators (audiobooks, meditations, audio series, and original content).  Users pay a subscription to access all the content, and creators make money per minute listened. 

There will be an audio platform for the 480M native Spanish speakers and Beek is the team building it.
