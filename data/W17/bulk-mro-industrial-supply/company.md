# Bulk MRO Industrial Supply

> Full Stack Fintech and Supply Chain Platform for Global Trade

Bulk MRO is a Full Stack Fintech and Supply Chain Platform for Global Trade. 

We provide our customers and suppliers turnkey solutions for procurement, sales and financing of long-tail industrial products (MRO items) as well as high value bulk commodities like agriculture, petroleum, and chemical products. 

We work with hundreds of Large Corporations and Government customers across North America, Europe, India, Middle East, and Africa as a one-stop shop for millions of products from thousands of manufacturers.

We are backed by the world’s best investors like Y Combinator, Bain Capital Ventures, Ace & Co., Rebright Partners, Dream Incubator, Refactor Capital, AAF, and FJ Labs.
