# SimpleHash

> Multi-chain token data platform

SimpleHash allows developers to query all blockchain data from a single platform. We index multiple blockchains, take care of edge cases, and can be integrated in a few lines of code. We make the lives of developers working with token data easier by abstracting away all the complexity.

We serve some of the leading names in crypto, including Coinbase, Phantom and Uniswap. We're growing quickly, are well-funded, and have an ambitious vision to organize the world of digital assets.

Join us if you're excited by a high impact role on a lean, fast paced team at the forefront of the revolution in digital assets.
