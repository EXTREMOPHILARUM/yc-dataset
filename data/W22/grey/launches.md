# Launches

## Grey - US banking services for remote workers, freelancers, nomads, and creators in emerging markets

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=82277&key=user_uploads/556140/a5ad7448-63b6-42a8-934d-3a149c352ec1)

When we started Grey in 2020, we just wanted to make it easy for people like us to receive payments for work without any stress. Over the last four years, we’ve enabled global banking access to digital nomads across the US, UK, Europe, Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and Nigeria.

Today, I’m excited to announce that Grey is now available to remote workers, digital nomads, and creatives in **Brazil** 🇧🇷, **Ghana** 🇬🇭, **Indonesia** 🇮🇩, **Mexico** 🇲🇽, the **Philippines** 🇵🇭, and **South Africa** 🇿🇦! Get started at [grey.co](http://grey.co).

In the next few weeks, we’ll roll out to more Latin America (LATAM) and Southeast Asia (SEA) countries. If you’re a digital nomad, freelancer, or remote worker in these countries, you can enjoy the following:

* Get a free USD, EUR, or GBP account to receive your salary and payments.
* Convert to other currencies (MXN, BRL, PHP, IDR, ZAR, NGN, KES, TZS, GHS, etc) and send to your local bank account or mobile money wallet.
* Get a virtual card to spend.
* Send Stablecoins (USDC) to your external wallet addresses.

We’re on a mission to make globalisation work for everyone by providing inclusive banking solutions regardless of location, and this expansion takes us six steps closer today.

If you know any communities we should speak to or any person in these markets we should connect to, please let us know at [founders@grey.co](mailto:founders@grey.co).
