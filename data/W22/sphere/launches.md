### **The problem**

When I ran [my last business](https://www.businessinsider.com/y-combinator-edtech-startup-sphere-seed-round-workplace-training-2022-8), we sold our product globally from day 1 and had a number of challenges with domestic and international tax compliance.

Specifically, we didn’t even know where/when we needed to pay sales tax, VAT, or GST, and even when we did, dealing with archaic government portals and filing on a regular basis was a royal pain in the behind. 

After talking to hundreds of finance leaders, we found similar pain points, and when it comes to indirect tax, companies either:

* **Ignore it,** but their tax liabilities accrue, and eventually, they have to pay the liability out of pocket, which can block future fundraises. Additionally, it is becoming increasingly difficult to ignore as tax authorities pass new regulations to clamp down on indirect tax evasion, especially for tech companies. 
* **Use an incumbent tax automation tool** (e.g. Avalara), but they don’t provide functionality for international coverage, are old/clunky, very expensive, and offer terrible support
* **Use local tax advisors,** which becomes incredibly expensive as you scale
