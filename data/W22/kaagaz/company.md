# Kaagaz

> 'Office' Suite for Mobile-first Businesses in India

Kaagaz is an 'Office' Suite for Mobile-first Businesses in India. With Kaagaz, these users can manage all the document requirements for their business using their smartphone itself, without needing a Desktop.

We have an active user base of 3Mn+ users and paid users north of 60,000+ - growing every month.

With over 200M such businesses in India, at $15/year subscription price, this a $3B opportunity in India & much larger globally.

We are a team of 2nd time entrepreneurs having scaled the pervious startup to $1M in GMV. We are backed by YCombinator, Goodwater Capital, Pravega Venture, Better Capital & other investors including Alan Rutledge (TrendMD) and Farid Ahsan (Sharechat)
