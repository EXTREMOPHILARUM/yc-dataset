# Onboard.io

> Manage & automate customer onboarding and implementation

Onboard is a SaaS which automates & streamlines the customer onboarding & implementation process. Easily create dynamic launch plans (customized per customer), which are collaborative with clients and visible to all stakeholders.  Onboard is built for onboarding by a team that spent a better part of the last decade onboarding some of the worlds largest brands.
