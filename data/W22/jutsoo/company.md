# Jutsoo

> All your corporate bank accounts connected under one platform

Jutsoo is a modern treasury management platform that helps finance teams work smarter by having efficient, reliable, real-time visibility and control over their financial data across their different banks and bank accounts.

We do this by unifying data from internal systems, integrating directly with banks for your money movements, and reconcile them automatically.

Money movements, reconciliations, cash forecasting, and identifying liquidity trends will be a breeze using us.
