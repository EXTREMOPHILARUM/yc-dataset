# CrediBook

> Faire for Indonesia

CrediBook digitizes the MSMEs' supply chain, from wholesale to retail, through online wholesale and bookkeeping operation system.

Our online wholesale platform CrediMart aims to create an efficient inventory-sourcing. We provide online-ordering, Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) payment method, and logistic support. We help our wholesale partners earn an additional daily income stream by 50%.

Our bookkeeping app CrediBook also enables MSMEs generate financial report in less than 5 minutes and proven to speed up users’ micro-loans process from financial institutions.
CrediBook strives to encourage more MSMEs to benefit digitalization from easy and efficient platforms that meet their needs.
