## Viva Labs: AI Agents for Social Media Video Ads

**Hi! Excited to be helping brands and marketing agencies turbocharge their video ad creatives with AI!**

1. **Research Agent**: Figure out messaging in your ad creatives
2. **Creation Agent**: Create UGC/Broll ads or repurpose existing video content
3. **Analyst Agent:** Identifies opportunities to create more new ads

**The problem?**

Most brands (mobile/gaming apps, ecom/d2c brands, b2c/b2smb) we’ve talked to want to create engaging short-form video ads that convert.

They usually don’t have the time or budget to:

* Hire expensive creators and video editors
* A/B test video creatives
* Figure out market positioning

They’ve been burned by expensive ad agencies.

They associate creating winning ads as a cumbersome process.\
\
\
**The solution? Viva Labs Ad Agents**

1. **Research Agent:** uses AI to quickly & cheaply create in-depth creative research reports about your social media ad accounts. We research your audience, as well as other winning ads in your space, to help you develop compelling scripts and storyboards.
