# **The Problem**

Every business has mission-critical processes that eat up hours of manual work: employee onboarding, contract approvals, lead qualification, vendor management. You know automation could help, but it faces three major issues:

1. **Existing business systems are rigid,** and systems of record like CRMs and ERPs make customization frustrating, complex, and expensive, often requiring external consultants or their own dedicated developers 
2. **No-code tools still have a significant learning curve,** and teams still spend weeks figuring out how to configure and build custom workflows properly before they can deliver value
3. **Pure no-code solutions struggle to handle edge cases** or highly custom business logic, requiring complex and clunky workarounds.
4. **Existing tools struggle to fully capture real-world business processes,** which involve complex processes that require a mixture of automation and human-in-the-loop, involve multiple teams and stakeholders, and extend across days, weeks, or even months.

   

## **Our Solution**

Workflow86 is an AI workflow engineer that takes natural language descriptions of business processes and automatically creates fully configured automations with forms, tasks, AI steps, integrations, and more. 

### **Design and deploy automations in minutes**

This allows anyone to build and deploy custom business processes and workflow automations by chatting to their own on-demand workflow automation expert. 

1. **Describe your process** in natural language or even upload an image, flowchart, or process diagram
2. **AI asks clarifying questions** and develops an implementation plan just like a business analyst would
3. **AI builds and implements everything** using a combination of forms, human-in-the-loop tasks, database queries, AI steps, API integrations, and even custom JavaScript/Python code
4. **Deploy immediately** or iterate. You can manually edit the workflow yourself or with more AI assistance

### Use cases and examples

Here are some real-world examples you can try:

> Create an employee onboarding workflow that sends welcome emails, creates IT tickets for equipment, schedules meetings with the team, and tracks completion of training modules

> Build a contract approval process that routes based on contract value, requires legal review for contracts over $50k, collects digital signatures, and notifies stakeholders

> I need a lead scoring system that enriches leads from our website, scores them based on company size and engagement, assigns to sales reps by territory, and creates follow-up tasks

> Set up a customer refund process that validates the order, checks refund eligibility, gets manager approval for amounts over $500, processes the refund, and updates our CRM

> Create an expense approval workflow where employees submit receipts, AI categorizes expenses, routes to managers based on amount, and syncs approved expenses to QuickBooks
