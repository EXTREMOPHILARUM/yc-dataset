# Founders

## Eduardo Ortiz de Lanzagorta Gonzalez
**Founder**

Eduardo started his first VC-backed fintech while finishing his engineering degree. After that, he co-founded what became the most active seed fund in Spain, backing over 40 companies. Throughout these years, he suffered the pain of managing and controlling company expenses and decided to build Fuell: the corporate card with expense management software built-in he wanted for himself.
