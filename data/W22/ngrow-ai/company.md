# ngrow.ai

> Low-code SaaS for marketing in push notifications

AI-driven push marketing tool for mobile apps, that can be integrated in hours, without any code writing.

nGrow connects to behavioral data from analytical systems like Amplitude, Google Firebase, Mixpanel etc. and allows to setup auto push campaigns with an hours.

It's as simple as running  Google or Facebook but in push notifications channel. 
nGrow.ai customers usually got their up to 30% incremental Revenue lift.

We got Amplitude COO and ex-Google advisors on board
