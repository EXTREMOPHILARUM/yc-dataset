## 💪 Our 3 Core Strengths

### **1. ⚡ Speed: 10x Faster Testing**

* **Smart test selection:** Our AI analyzes PR diffs and runs only affected tests—when you change checkout, we test checkout, not your entire suite
* **Automated test creation:** Tests that once took hours of hand-coding in Playwright or Cypress now generate automatically in under 10 minutes.
* **Intelligent caching:** Tests run in cached Playwright until your app changes, then auto-heal

### 2. 🎯 **Accuracy: AI That Actually Understands Your Product**

* **99.7% assertion accuracy** by learning from your Notion docs, Google Drive specs, PostHog recordings, and Git history
* **Context-aware testing:** Tell Stably "test checkout with expired coupons" in plain English—it knows what checkout means in YOUR app
* **Catches real bugs:** Not just UI changes, but actual business logic failures

### 3. 🧩 **Easy Integration: Works for Everyone, Everywhere**

* **No-code for all teams:** PMs describe tests in English, QAs point-and-click to show flows, and engineers get PR-ready tests
* **One-click GitHub app** or use our API for GitLab, local CLI, and Cursor MCP (upcoming)
* **Human-in-the-loop:** When AI needs help, anyone can step in and show it what to do—no coding required
