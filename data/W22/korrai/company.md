# KorrAI

> AI-native workspace for end-to-end desktop studies

KorrAI provides an AI-native workspace for remote desktop risk studies across construction, infrastructure, and mining projects.
Our platform, TRAIL, enables insurers, asset owners, and builders to evaluate site and construction risk in hours instead of weeks by unifying geospatial data, geotechnical reports, catastrophe model outputs, and engineering context into one traceable system.
