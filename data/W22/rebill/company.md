# Rebill

> Global payments infrastructure in LATAM and US

Rebill helps global companies process payments, payouts, and financial operations in LATAM and the US without local entities.
‍
With one integration, companies can accept payments, subscriptions, and installments across markets without setting up fragmented payment stacks country by country. Rebill combines local acquiring, 100+ payment methods, smart routing, retries, recovery, unified settlement, and a developer-first product in one platform.

We help merchants launch faster, increase conversion, and operate across underserved markets with the simplicity global companies expect.
