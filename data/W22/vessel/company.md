# Vessel

> Developer-first, native integration platform for GTM tools

Vessel is the developer-first, native integration platform for GTM tools. We make it easy for developers to integrate with your customer’s CRMs, Sales Engagement, Marketing Automation, Chat, Dialers, and more. Integrate in different ways customized for your use cases: 

- Speed: Use our Unified APIs to roll out many integrations quickly through a common data model and unified interface.
- Depth: Use our actions API to build deeper integrations when working with entities custom to each app.
- Ease: Use ETL to let us handle pulling and pushing customer data to your DB
- Extensible: Support any integrations through our open-source integrations library

Breadth and depth, make your integrations limitless.
