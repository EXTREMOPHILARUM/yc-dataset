# **Problem:**

Most medicines being launched are missing their revenue potential by a large margin due to messy pharma data causing expensive inefficiencies. When preparing a drug launch, pharmaceutical manufacturers have to choose between three options:

* **Manual research:** time-consuming, human errors, language barriers
* **Databases:** $300k / year, poor data quality, limited scope
* **Consulting:** $300k / project, answers in weeks, data snapshots

# **Solution:**

* Cellbyte consists of 3 main layers:
  * **High-quality data:** comprehensive, globally interconnected, up-to-date
  * **Analytics:** instant due to integrated databases, personalized to each user
  * **Workflows:** built on deep user understanding, human-in-the-loop-interactions, consulting-level outputs
* Cellbyte’s AI workforce replaces Legacy Providers
* We’re live in **12 key markets,** adding new ones every week
