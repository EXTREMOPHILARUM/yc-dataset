# Plumter

> Financial tools for cross-border trade

We're providing a global platform for cross-border trade between importers and exporters. Leveraging powerful multi currency and stablecoin infrastructure, multilingual invoicing powered by AI, and deeply embedded apps that feel natural to traders around the world, we are making cross border trade as easy as texting.
