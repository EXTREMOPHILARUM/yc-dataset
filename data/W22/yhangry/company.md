# yhangry

> Marketplace for private chefs

Yhangry is a private chef marketplace, where you can book a chef whenever you want to socialize at home.

For $40pp, you can book a 3-course feast in just a few clicks. On the supply side, we help chefs earn 3 times as much as working in a restaurant.
