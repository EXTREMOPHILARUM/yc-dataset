# Rehook.ai

> Run, Track and manage any and all user incentive campaigns.

rehook.ai is an all-in-one promotion engine. A complete stack which lets you run referral, loyalty, rewards, discounts, coupons, leaderboards, streaks, tiers etc and is flexible, trackable, scalable and secure.
 
With an intuitive rule engine, marketers can simply add/edit/change rules to generate coupons/rewards/cashbacks or any other incentives. rehook.ai lets you experiment and personalize campaigns around user segments, geographies, demographics, journeys and any other attribute- without ever going back to tech.
