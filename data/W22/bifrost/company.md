# Bifrost

> Wills and Estate Management for Crypto

We write smart contracts that can automatically transfer assets during end-of-life events.
When you hold your own keys to your own wallet, your funds are lost in worst-case-scenarios. We make an on-chain dead-mans-switch to help your loved ones claim your holdings.
