## Andhika Sudarman
**Founder**

Andhika is one of the 24 people who ever attended Harvard Law School from Indonesia, and is the first-ever from South East Asia to have delivered a commencement speech. In just 14 months, he built SejutaCita, a community of ambitious local and international graduates that generated over $300K in annual revenue and achieved profitability with more than 1.4M QAU. SejutaCita also won the Best App of 2021 award in the Indonesia category on Google Play.
