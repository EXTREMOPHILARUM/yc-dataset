# Proper

> Reconciliation and financial operations platform

Proper provides software & APIs to help companies manage their payments data across providers. We connect banks, API providers, and networks in a single platform and provide automated reconciliation and accounting, so companies can maintain financial accuracy at scale.

Our integrated financial operating system gives companies a unified back-office data platform across their services and providers. With Proper, companies can spend less time on low-level financial system infrastructure and more time focused on building for their end-users.
