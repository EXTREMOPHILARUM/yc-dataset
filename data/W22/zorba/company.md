# Zorba

> AI copilot for real-estate sales teams, powered by proprietary data

Zorba is a real estate sales AI copilot, powered by proprietary vertical data, designed to help sales and customer success teams grow their client base and improve retention. It's used and trusted by both Fortune 500 and mid-market firms. With a multi-layered, LLM-driven engine, we’ve built a proprietary dataset of consolidated real estate entities, delivering real-time insights tailored specifically for the industry. Think Apollo or ZoomInfo, but AI-first and driven by real estate data
