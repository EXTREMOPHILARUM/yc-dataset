# PolicyFly

> Modernizing Specialty Insurance

PolicyFly empowers insurers to sell and manage specialty insurance in minutes instead of in weeks.

Every year, $200B of specialty insurance like Cyber, Flood or Trucking is processed over email between agents and insurers – PolicyFly connects both parties to automate these workflows and to consolidate all data. 

We bring growth, efficiency and transparency to otherwise hard to insure markets.
