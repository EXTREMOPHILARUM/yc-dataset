# Brainboard

> Visually design and manage cloud infrastructures.

Brainboard is a collaborative and end-to-end solution for engineers to visually design & manage their Cloud infrastructures.
We allow companies to lower the learning curve while moving to IaC, build a self serve model, have a service catalog to not reinvent the wheel.

Both technical profiles with 25+ years of combined expertise and after working together at a major French Cloud provider, Chafik and Jeremy launched Brainboard in 2020.
