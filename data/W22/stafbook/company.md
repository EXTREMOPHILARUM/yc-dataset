# StafBook

> Rippling for Southeast Asia

StafBook help SMEs  to simplify HR & expense management.

With StafBook, business managers can manage the People administration starting from HR (onboarding, leave & attendance, payroll, until off-boarding) and also expense management (reimbursement, cash advance). Thus, people can save their time on administrative task, and focus on core business
