## Blaze 2.0 - Use AI to find high intent leads + trigger relevant outreach

Hey everyone! 👋 Today we are thrilled to announce a number of huge updates to Blaze, including AI personalization, DM Sequences across Twitter and LinkedIn, and AI Replies from our unified inbox.

**The Problem** Email is saturated and it’s hard to stand out from the noise: Reaching the right audience through outbound email is becoming increasingly challenging. The proliferation of lead databases and ever changing spam filters has reduced the effectiveness of email campaigns.

 

**The Solution** Blaze helps you:

 

1. Generate leads with AI-powered signals: Identify qualified B2B leads on LinkedIn and X with a higher likelihood of conversion. Create lists from unique signals, such as users liking your competitors’ social posts, following relevant interest groups or influencers, or mentioning certain topics or initiative at a company or individual level.
2. Send personalized DM sequences on X or LinkedIn and auto-respond with AI: Target leads with more compelling messages taking into account the lead context on social channels where you are more likely to cut through the noise. Add in optional steps like following someone first to improve your response rate further.
3. Engage with trending topics and buyers posts posts to build trust and awareness: Create feeds that surface relevant posts so you can engage and foster closer relationships with top prospects alongside building your personal brand.
