## TeamOut | The offsite revolution for small groups

**TL;DR** 

TeamOut's latest update makes it easy for teams under 20 people to plan a team offsite. Choose among a selection of thousands of villas and hotels, add food, activities, and transportation in one click. Don’t delay your next offsite; make it happen today.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=76274&key=user_uploads/707520/27bfaff0-7165-436e-8b54-630a0605f1b5)

**Problem**

All team offsites require the same ingredients: accommodation, food, activity, and transportation. Unfortunately, today no platform exists to do everything in one place, and you end up spending hours looking for an affordable house or a hotel close to the airport. Then you have to make 50 phone calls to find a good catering company, activity, and so on. You're basically reinventing the wheel every single time, and as a result, your team is not meeting enough.

\
**Solution**

* Thousands of houses and hotels close to airports, curated for team offsites.
* Food, activity, and transportation options available for each venue.
* A team of dedicated trip designers to help you find the perfect match for your team.
* Optional event planning services to put your planning on autopilot and remove the stress of planning an event.
* Tools and resources to manage RSVPs, suggest team-building activities, icebreakers, and templated itineraries.
* A solution used by companies like Netflix, Brex, Kayak, Cloudflare, and more.
