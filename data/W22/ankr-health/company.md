# Ankr Health

> Virtualizing brick-mortar infrastructures of clinics w/ generative AI 

Adoption of Telehealth 1.0 has dropped to <5% because it's inefficient and unintelligent. Ankr uses generative AI to recreate functions of clinic staff including front desk, medical assistants and scribe. This allows Doctors to "spin-up" virtual clinics on-demand, anytime, anywhere. 

An average clinic using Ankr generates $1M in new revenue/year.  We integrate with all top EHRs and work with all types of clinics in mid- to large-sized healthcare organizations.
