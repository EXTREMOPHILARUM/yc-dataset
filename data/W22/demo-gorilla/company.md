# Demo Gorilla

> Make all your live SaaS demos impactful: close more ARR faster

Demo Gorilla is a browser extension for live SaaS demos that tells your sales team exactly what to say to sell your product.

Keep everyone on the same page with the best customer stores, competitive talking points and feature explanations. Collect insights across product, engineering, marketing, and sales experts to ensure your sales team always knows exactly what will close this deal.

- Automatically get relevant talking points for the part of the product you're on and who you're talking to
- Track notes and metrics back in to your CRM
- Share slides and images in line while demoing to set the stage
- Clean up your UI to keep attendees focused
