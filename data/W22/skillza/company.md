# Skillza

> Skillza builds simple tools for teachers and schools in MEA

Skillza works with schools, teachers, students, and their parents in the Middle East and Africa to build tools to maximize learning outcomes.

We help today's teachers to integrate new technologies into their classrooms to improve the learner-centeredness of their classrooms.

Our flagship product Quest is a tool that helps teachers and schools to simplify the process of creating and managing assessments. Teachers create and distribute assessments as well as measure and manage student performances in seconds saving them hours everyday to allow for them to focus on what they do best - teaching.
