# Dojah Inc

> Prevent Fraud and Onboard Users Faster

Dojah is the anti-fraud infrastructure for the internet, helping businesses verify identities, detect fraud, and stay compliant with powerful APIs and no-code tools. Fintechs, banks, and digital businesses rely on Dojah’s solutions for seamless user onboarding, AI-driven fraud detection, KYC & KYB verification, and transaction monitoring.

By leveraging government data lookups, biometric authentication, and real-time risk analysis, Dojah ensures businesses can mitigate fraud risks, improve user trust, and scale efficiently while staying compliant with regulatory standards.
