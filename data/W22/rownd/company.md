# Rownd

> Build powerful AI solutions. Deploy anywhere.

Rownd helps enterprises in regulated industries build and deploy AI applications with our open-core model, providing production-ready components for RAG systems, model fine-tuning, and prompt engineering that run entirely on-premise while maintaining complete data sovereignty. 

With a powerful CLI and YAML configs, teams can create differentiated AI products without vendor lock-in or ML expertise, ensuring compliance and cost control that cloud-only solutions can't match.
