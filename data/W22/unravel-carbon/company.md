# Unravel Carbon

> Enterprise software for companies to track & reduce their carbon…

Unravel Carbon is a leading sustainability data management platform, used in 48+ countries. It uses AI/ML to help enterprises transform any operational data into full carbon supply chain data, helping them measure, track and report on their sustainability performance. It is already serving some of the world’s largest companies and is backed by Sequoia and Y Combinator.
