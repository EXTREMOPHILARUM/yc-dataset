# Heptabase

> An intelligent, visual knowledge base for learning complex topics.

Heptabase is an intelligent, visual knowledge base built for students, researchers, and lifelong learners. Our product helps you master anything you learn and do your best research with AI.

Heptabase is backed by Y Combinator, HOF Capital, Kleiner Perkins, Moving Capital, Tonic Fund, and other investors, including the founders of Socialcam, Triplebyte, Codementor, and more.
