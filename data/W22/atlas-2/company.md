# Atlas

> Modern customer support platform replacement for Intercom and Zendesk

Atlas is a modern, all-in-one customer support platform that is built with AI first. We help you solve your customers problems faster by understanding them more completely with a suite of customer tools like session recording, bug tracking, and event feeds. You can think of us as the next generation Zendesk / Intercom combined with Log Rocket and Linear.

Learn More: https://atlas.so/

Products:
- Chat Widget
- Ticketing
- Omni channel Support (chat, email, WhatsApp, Slack Connect, and SMS)
- Customer Timeline
- Session Recording
- Help Center
- AI Chatbot
- Customer Insights
