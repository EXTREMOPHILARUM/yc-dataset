# Moots

> Helping Event Teams Vet the Room Before and Convert It After

Moots is the guest intelligence platform for corporate events. We help event teams vet guest lists, prepare their hosts, and convert conversations into measurable pipeline — all from one collaborative workspace.

Before the event, AI agents evaluate and score every guest candidate against your objectives, enriching profiles across dozens of intelligence sources in seconds. PR partners, sponsors, and internal stakeholders collaborate on curated guest lists directly in the platform — each seeing only what they need to.

At the event, every team member walks in with personalized briefing packets: who to meet, what to discuss, and shared connections to reference.
After the event, automated follow-ups go out within hou
rs — not weeks — while every interaction syncs to your CRM. Leadership gets a clear ROI dashboard tying each guest to pipeline outcomes.
From branded houses at Cannes Lions and Art Basel to satellite dinners, client retreats, and donor cultivation events, Moots turns every room you curate into a compounding intelligence network.

Trusted by corporate event teams, PR agencies, financial institutions, and advisory firms.
