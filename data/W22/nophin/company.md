# Nophin

> AI Deal Screening Analyst for Commercial Real Estate Acquisitions

Nophin helps commercial real estate professionals screen deals faster with Cresa, an AI Analyst that automates multifamily deal screening and diligence processes, freeing up time for what truly matters—closing the best opportunities.
 
Acquisition teams are overwhelmed with manual tasks, from screening countless deals that don't meet buying criteria to analyzing complex documents and conducting market research. These inefficiencies lead to missed opportunities and slowed decision-making, making it difficult to focus on high-potential opportunities.

Cresa is the first fine-tuned multimodal Agent capable of handling various workflows for acquisition teams around the clock so they can focus on high-potential deals that align with their buying criteria. Cresa can screen deals, analyze documents, and generate comprehensive reports.
