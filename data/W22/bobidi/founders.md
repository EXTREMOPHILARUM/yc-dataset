# Founders

## Jeong-Suh Choi
**Founder**

Jeong-Suh Choi is the co-founder, CEO of  Bobidi.  Choi drives Bobidi's vision to build the world where every AI in the world is helpful for everybody, and sets the strategy to accomplish the mission of making every data bit meaningful for people.  Choi dedicated the first half of his career working on multiple startups (sold the last one), and the second half in big tech companies (eBay and Facebook) as Product Manager.  Originally from Seoul, Korea, living in the Bay Area.
