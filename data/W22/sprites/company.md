# Sprites

> The AI marketing agent for entrepreneurs, agencies and growth teams

Sprites AI automates customer acquisition for entrepreneurs. Our AI agent handles managing SEO, running paid ad campaigns, content marketing, influencer outreach—tasks that normally require hiring multiple people or agencies. 

Entrepreneurs using our platform acquire customers much faster and scale their growth funnel without the overhead of building marketing teams or hiring expensive agencies. 

The result: SMBs and solo founders can now scale revenue like they have a full marketing department.

We're arming entrepreneurs and small businesses globally with systematic customer acquisition run by AI- so they can focus on the important things that make their offering unique.
