# Kular

> AI Lead Generator

Kular is an AI Lead Generator, generating leads for busy professionals and business owners and only charging for results. Today, Kular generates leads for over 1000 companies via email and LinkedIn. 

Kular is being built by a team of MIT, IIT, Cambridge and Oxford grads, backed by some of the best investors in the world, including Y-Combinator, Entrepreneur First, Acequia Capital and angel investors behind companies like Ironclad, OpenSea and Stripe.
