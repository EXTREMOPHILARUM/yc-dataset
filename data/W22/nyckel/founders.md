# Founders

## Oscar Beijbom
**Founder**

Oscar is the co-founder of Nyckel. He is a ML engineer and researcher with 9k citations and 20+ patents. He spent 5 years as a leader in the AV industry, been a researcher at UC Berkeley, and developed the AI behind the Hovding airbag bicycle helmet. He also developed CoralNet - the largest AI platform for coral reef image analysis. 

Oscar has a M.S. in Eng. Physics from Lund, and a PhD in Computer Science from UCSD.

## George Mathew
**Founder**

George is the co-founder of Nyckel. Before Nyckel, George spent 5 years at Oracle Cloud, where he led teams building Functions-as-a-Service and Application Performance Management services. He started his career at Microsoft, after which he was an early engineer at CareEvolution, where he led the development of one of the largest health information exchanges in the US.

George is passionate about distributed systems, data systems, and building high-performing engineering organizations.
