# Emobi

> A single point integration for secure and automated EV charging.

Emobi is a unified legal, financial, and technical infrastructure that delivers seamless, secure, and reliable EV charging across networks—powering apps, fleets, and automakers while maximizing charger utilization. Our secure, fully automated charging technology, JustPlug, works instantly with no special hardware, software, or setup, making EV charging effortless.

With 122,000+ EV chargers integrated across the U.S. and Canada, Emobi is recognized as the largest EV charging ecosystem in North America. Backed by two provisional patents and 15+ enterprise customers, Emobi's technology is also trusted by the Department of Energy (DoE) and the Department of Transportation (DoT). To learn more, visit www.emobi.ai.
