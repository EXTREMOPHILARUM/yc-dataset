## Lin Sun Fa
**Founder/CEO**

Lin Sun Fa, CEO at Emobi, is an EV enthusiast with a deep knowledge in cybersecurity. Lin plays a leading role in the climate tech and energy space, garnered trust from organizations and leaders in the industry. He earned his Cybersecurity degree at Purdue University and was previously the global director of operations at Solar Chapter, a World-bank affiliated non-profit. In his free time, he is an avid traveler and a loving corgi-dad.
