# Compra Rápida

> One-click checkout and AI sales assistant for LatAm.

Compra Rápida is checkout made easy. Our platform offers a headless checkout experience for e-commerce stores, enabling retailers to increase conversions, retain abandonment, and boost sales. Registered users can buy at any e-commerce site from our network without requiring lengthy registration forms or complicated payment processes. Stores can automate their sales and recovery flows with our AI sales assistant. We aim to empower retailers to sell more while providing shoppers with a superior, humanized, and effortless buying experience.
