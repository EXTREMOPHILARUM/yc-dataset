# Olympian Motors

> Next-generation, modular electric vehicles

⚡️Olympian manufactures timeless, art-deco electric vehicles. We challenge today’s mundane, over-standardized car designs produced by the traditional automotive industry.

► Olympian EVs are powered by an open, modular EV architecture. We are pioneering a new modular production model in the U.S., delivering 85–90% reductions in tooling and machinery costs, and achieved 60–80% faster production-lead-times compared to legacy automakers in the U.S.

💸 Revenue Backlog: $61M+
🚗 Number of Orders: 790+
🏭 Production Capacity: 2,400 units in 2026
✅ Backed by: YC, NVIDIA, Climate Capital, Mobility Vision, Amazon AWS, Soma & Collaborative.

--
Olympian has two pillars:
1) Art-deco, aesthetic, but simple electric cars
2) Modular EV architecture that enables rapid, cost-efficient production.

Olympian rejects today's mundane and over-standardized car experience.   We challenge the 40-year status-quo in automotive design, and bring aesthetic, simplicity and color back to the cars. 

We are manufacturing electric vehicles with our innovative 'modular' vehicle & drivetrain architecture (MVDS), providing Olympian with strong cost and lead-time advantages compared to legacy automakers like Tesla and Ford. Olympian is disrupting 120+ years old automotive industry and targeting to capture 7% market share in the U.S. automotive market by 2030.

Olympian developed a fully-decoupled, modular, EV drivetrain system (eMVDS) that provides a 'Lego-like' vehicle architecture. Built on 4-core-hardware and 2-core software modules, Olympus eMVDS platform unlocks 80% reduction in tooling & machinery costs and built ~60% faster production lead time.
