# Founders

## Mason Chilmonczyk
**Founder**

Mason is the co-founder and CEO of Andson Biotech, an academic spinout based on Mason's research at the Georgia Institute of Technology. At Andson, Mason is driving the company's mission to develop novel methodologies for advanced biomanufacturing discovery and monitoring. Mason is formally trained as a mechanical engineer with a BS from the University of South Florida ('14) and an MS and PhD from Georgia Tech ('17, '20).
