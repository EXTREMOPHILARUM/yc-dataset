# Founders

## Bobby Pinckney
**Founder**

Bobby is the co-founder and CEO of multiple consumer apps including Verse, one of the fastest social platforms of all time to reach over 10M users, and Discz, which Rolling Stone called "one of the hottest music apps in the world."

He is a Forbes 30 under 30 lister, Y Combinator alumni, and his ventures have been backed by multiple of the top venture capital firms.
