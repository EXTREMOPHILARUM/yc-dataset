# Heyfood

> Doordash for Africa

Heyfood is building the “Doordash for Africa”.  But unlike Doordash in America, we make a profit on every single delivery, from day 1. 

We are targeting Millennials and Gen Z urban professionals in Africa’s top 70 cities, withing 10 years there will be 140m number of these people, we assume they will want to order food once a week, we make $1 per delivery, this represents a $51B market.
