## Edvin Fernqvist
**Founder**

During the onset of the Corona pandemic I looked for ways to help out and started to source and produce PPE. Coming in contact with the hard pressed nurses and doctors working in ICUs and other facilities I soon found there were other more pressing issues to be solved. When I met my co-founders we decided to band together to create Bemlo and reverse the deteriorating situation we saw in healthcare staffing and recruiting.
