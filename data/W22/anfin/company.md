# Anfin

> A multi-asset trading platform dedicated to retail investors

Anfin is a modern trading platform that aims to democratize access to financial services and wealth management for Vietnam’s retail investors.

Our mission is to help the underserved retail investor navigate the complexities of investments in Vietnam, from stock market to the underutilized commodity trading.

We are backed by international renowned investors such as YC, Goodwater Capital, Global Founders Capital, and many more.
