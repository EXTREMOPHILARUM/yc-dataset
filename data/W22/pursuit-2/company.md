# Pursuit

> Turnkey co-ownership & management of properties

Pursuit (also dba Hearth) offers you the opportunity to own and enjoy properties via turnkey co-ownership and ongoing property management. 

- We specialize in top-tier recreational properties throughout the world that possess scarce natural resources. 

- We coordinate small ownership groups (ten or fewer) that cooperatively steward the land, enjoy it together, and benefit from investment growth. 

- We manage the property on your behalf with your enjoyment and investment goals in mind.
