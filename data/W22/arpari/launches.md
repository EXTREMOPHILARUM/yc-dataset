# **❌ The Problem**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=89175&key=user_uploads/859217/57361321-c472-4677-955d-e6feecc21f04)

Finance teams who are responsible for balance reporting and transaction reconciliation often scramble at month end, don’t report at the frequency they’d like, or sacrifice reporting accuracy altogether.   

1. **ACCOUNT INVENTORY**: Using multiple banks can mean liquidity is an unknown, especially for teams who work with many accounts. Bank accounts tracked in Excel—many unnamed or vaguely labeled—makes effective management nearly impossible. Often accounts are missed, and cash misplaced. 
2. **MANAGING ACCESS**: Financial operations are severely hindered by fragmented bank access scattered across multiple team members. Accountants regularly need separate logins, deal with constant lockouts, and navigate multiple security code requests, reducing productivity. Add in payment approval workflows, and you have duplicate, complex permissions to manage across multiple portals. 
3. **DATA NORMALIZATION**: Beyond data access, when teams finally gather transaction data across banks, the data comes through in various formats. Many banks only offer PDF downloads, some banks don’t even provide export.  
4. **TRANSFER MONITORING**: Teams need to log into multiple portals to monitor pending transactions and approvals. Additionally, when vendor or investor bank details change, they need to update the information across multiple portals.

 

Teams who have tried to solve this problem have found treasury software that doesn’t work for multibank users. Incumbent solutions price with bank-by-bank implementation, which doesn’t work for users who have hundreds of accounts across 10+ banks, with their account and bank list constantly in flux. 

  

# 👩‍💻  **Our Solution: One login for all finance teams needs**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=89175&key=user_uploads/859217/7bf3d96a-04c3-4687-b9a6-6d369a929170)

  

We’ve built a dashboard for finance teams to interact in one place. When teams are doing the same manual tasks 10+ times, why not do them once? 

### STABLE CONNECTIONS

We leverage various connection methods to banks to provide stable, usable data daily to our customers. The account, balance, and activity information is the foundation to all workflows for our customers; unifying this information into a single place gives them back time for month-end reporting, peace of mind, and full transparency of their true cash flow.  

### DASHBOARD CUSTOMIZATION

We offer added customization to help organize account, balance, and activity data. Our dashboard is built around teams with complex fund and entity structures and prioritizes easy navigation according to how our users think about their bank information. 

### SINGLE PORTAL ACCESS

With payment approval workflows and read-only access, finance teams no longer need to permission non-account signers into bank portals. Finance teams can conduct their day-to-day operations without needing to log into 10+ portals to make payments or pull activity. 

  

Whether you are the controller sending investor distributions and paying vendors, an executive who wants a headline snapshot of cash, or an accountant reconciling transactions, Arpari saves you time.
