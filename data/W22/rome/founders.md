## Craig Weiss
**Founder**

Co-Founder & CEO of Rome. Craig was an early engineer at Scale AI (engineer #20). While at Scale, Craig was a founding member on the Sensor Fusion team and was a key player in securing multi-million dollar contracts with Scale’s largest autonomous vehicle customers. His past experience includes engineering at Google, Facebook, Snapchat, Lyft and NASA.
