# Cotera

> The fastest way to build an AI agent

Cotera is an AI agent platform that connects directly to your data warehouse and APIs, enabling businesses to build agents that take actions across their systems. With built-in tools, a visual workflow editor, and fine-grained control, teams can deploy agents at scale for use cases like intelligent ticket routing, voice-of-customer analysis, and proactive customer health monitoring.
