## Cotera: The easiest way to put reliable AI agents into production

Cotera is building the easiest way to put reliable AI agents into production.

Most AI agent platforms force teams to choose: either deal with complex frameworks that require months of engineering work, or use simple no-code tools that give you zero visibility and control.

Cotera eliminates this trade-off.

In one place you can define all your context, access all of your data, connect to all of your apps and APIs, create guard rails, monitor agents in production, and manage how much they're spending.

The best bit is that it's all incredibly easy. All your important context lives in the heads of your domain experts, so we've made building an agent as simple as writing a document and having a conversation.

For some time we've been working with great teams like Bilt, Coterie and Peloton to automate customer support, generate marketing assets, automate sales follow ups, and run back office processes. Finally, we're ready to open this powerful platform to the public.
