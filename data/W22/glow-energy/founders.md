# Founders

## Greg Sirotek
**Founder**

Greg is the co-founder and CEO of OpenCoreEMR, launched in June 2025 with Open Core Ventures to commercialize OpenEMR, the world’s most widely used open-source electronic health records platform. Previously, he was co-founder and CEO of Glow Energy, acquired by Ivy Energy in 2024. Earlier, Greg co-founded a proptech startup that led to his role at Airbnb, where he helped reaccelerate host growth and later served as Head of Home Experience, Luxe.

## Ross Gruber
**Founder**

Ross is the co-founder and CTO of Glow Energy. Ross was previously a lead data engineer and engineering manager who helped scale engineering teams at YC and VC-backed startups like Dia&Co and Upkeep. Ross is passionate about sustainability and is focused on building decarbonization as a crucial building block on our way to a net-zero future.
