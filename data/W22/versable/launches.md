## Versable - AI-powered product data enhancement for the auto parts space

Hi everyone! My name is [Christina](https://www.linkedin.com/in/christinaseong/), and I’m building [Versable](https://www.versable.ai/). We use AI to help auto parts manufacturers, retailers, and distributors generate market-ready product listings at scale.

**The data problem in auto parts**

Auto parts retailers get product data from hundreds of manufacturers, often riddled with inaccuracies, inconsistencies, and missing key values. This forces companies today to manually process and enhance this data line by line, causing a weeks to months long lag between receiving product data and actually being able to start generating revenue from those products.

This bottleneck means companies are slower to market and unable to onboard new products at the scale they want. Manufacturers also face challenges with data requirements, which limits their ability to sell across multiple retailers or even consider listing their own products on online marketplaces like Amazon or eBay, which have even stricter platform-specific content guidelines.

**How Versable solves this 10x better than the status quo**

Versable leverages AI to scan the web for tens of millions of auto parts listings and uses a fine-tuned LLM with RAG to instantly process, enhance, and transform product data. With just a part number, Versable generates market-ready titles, product descriptions, and specs in any required format. This means our customers are able to sell more, faster, and across more platforms than ever before.

By incorporating brand-specific voices and relevant SEO keywords, Versable ensures that in a market filled with near-perfectly competitive products, our customers can come out on top.
