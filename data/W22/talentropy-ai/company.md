# Talentropy.ai

> AI solution that streamlines candidate screening and recruitment

Think of us as your dedicated AI recruiting team, available 24/7 to engage with every single candidate in their preferred language. We help companies to evaluate 100% of their candidates while reducing the workload on their recruiting teams. 

Our technical co-founders, Rubén, Vladimir, and Sebastián, bring vast HR industry experience. HoyTrabajas is supported by Rebel Fund, Alan Rutledge, Kube Ventures, Flucas VC, Magic Fund, New Ventures, Y Combinator (w22), and more.
