## Trace 👾 Route repetitive tasks to AI agents

Hey everyone 👋

We’re [Tim](https://www.linkedin.com/in/timcherkasov/) and [Artur](https://www.linkedin.com/in/artur-romanov/) – co-founders of [Trace](https://www.trace.so/).

**📙 TL;DR**

Trace is a workflow automation platform that routes tasks to the right agent – human or AI.

By connecting to tools like Slack, Jira, and Notion, Trace breaks down existing workflows, spots automation opportunities, and embeds AI agents to handle repetitive tasks – without disrupting how your team works. More than 30 companies already use Trace to automate gruntwork.

**❌ The Problem**

Most teams lose ≈ 40% of their productivity on repetitive manual tasks. 

Routine work like chasing updates, filling docs, coordinating teams, and handling support drains hours, breaks focus, and slows execution.

Meanwhile, employees aren’t sure which tasks can be automated, which agents to use, or which tools to trust – problems made worse by fragmented data and lost context.

Even worse, most AI tools work alone, disconnected from both teams and the people using them.

✅ **Our Solution** 

Trace is an AI-native workspace that breaks down existing workflows, assigns repetitive tasks to AI agents, and routes the rest to the right people.

With Trace, AI works across your entire team – passing tasks between agents and people without disrupting operations.

It respects permissions, learns from past and ongoing work, tracks progress with a full audit trail, and lets you review, edit, or reroute tasks in a simple interface.

**🦄 How It Works**

How? – Through a company wide context engine!

By integrating with your systems, our context engine analyzes your internal wiki, activity logs, and people data to understand your team’s work.

Trace then builds a unified index of your company and provides rich context for every new workflow, making it easy to route tasks between people and AI agents.

We’re on a mission to build CompanyOS – a cognitive layer that ingests internal signals and drives intelligent action across every team.
