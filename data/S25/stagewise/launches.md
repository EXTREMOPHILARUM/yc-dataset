# **THE PROBLEM**

**AI-powered app builders work great - until they don’t.**

Once you move into a production-grade project setup on your local machine, you lose the awesome experience of visually building your apps frontend with simple prompts.

You move from tools like **v0 or Lovable** to tools like **Cursor and Claude Code**, but there’s a catch.

**AI code editors are** **not good at building frontends**, because:\
→ They don’t understand your apps user experience\
→ They don’t see your browser content, but only the codebase\
→ You have to switch between browser and IDE all the time

‎

# **THE SOLUTION**

With stagewise, we’ve developed an **open-source** and **framework-independent** toolbar that brings our frontend coding agent **right into your web app on localhost**.\
\
Here’s how it works:\
→ You prompt changes by clicking and commenting on elements in your app\
→ We automatically collect browser and app metadata\
→ The agent makes precise changes to your local codebase

The toolbar has already surpassed **4,800 GitHub-stars** ⭐️ and **130,000 downloads 📈**
