## Riff – Cursor for music production

Hey YC!

We’re Adith and Kenny — the founders of Riff!

# **TL;DR**

[Riff](https://goriff.com) is a **music editor with superpowers** where experienced producers can be 10x more productive and hobbyists can delight in making music without needing technical chops.

[**https://youtu.be/bjyjtr8-yz8**](https://youtu.be/bjyjtr8-yz8)

# **Problem**

**Slow**

🖥️ ❌ Legacy music production software is clunky and weighed down by archaic UX.

**Repetitive**

🎧 ❌ Musicians sometimes spend hours making changes that should realistically happen in minutes. With legacy software, there is no high-signal way of getting your creativity down.

**High skill barrier**

🎸❌ The difficulty of existing tools blocks people with musical interest but no patience to learn complex audio engineering.

# **Solution**

Riff is an AI music editor meant to make music creation easier, yet still keeps the artist in charge.

# **Who’s it for?**

In the short run, we’re targeting aspiring musicians who are between entry level music software like GarageBand and BandLab. Very soon, we’ll have enough features that we can directly compete with FL/Ableton/Logic/Pro Tools and get experienced producers onboard.
