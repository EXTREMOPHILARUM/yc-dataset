**The problem**

Voice AI is exploding: in the past three YC batches, 60+ companies have depended on voice models. But voice models live or die on the quality of their training data.

Labs need datasets that reflect:

* Languages (global coverage, not just English)
* Accents (real-world variety)
* Emotions & expressiveness (not monotone samples)
* Demographics (ages, genders, cultural nuance)

Right now, most labs struggle to source enough realistic, diverse, high-quality conversational audio.

**Our solution**

Panels delivers exactly that.

* We have a network of 10,000+ vetted voice contributors
* Coverage across 20+ languages and 100+ countries
* Expressive, conversational audio datasets designed for training state-of-the-art voice models.

This makes Panels the reliable data provider for the labs building the future of Voice AI.

**Who this is for**

* Voice labs scaling foundational models
* Research institutions working on speech, linguistics, and AI
* Conversational AI startups wanting to finetune their agents
