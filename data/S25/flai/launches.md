❌ **The Problem**

Dealerships are trapped in a never-ending cycle that bleeds revenue:

* **60% of dealership leads arrive after business hours** and most dealers can't answer
* **70% who hit voicemail contact a competitor within 30 minutes**, losing the sale
* **Each store loses $1M annually** from those missed calls alone
* **Staffed coverage is costly, inconsistent, and impossible to scale**

✨ **Our Solution**

Flai's Dealership-Tailored AI Assistants gives every dealership a tireless voice agent with three core capabilities:

* **Always-On Coverage:** Our AI agents answer or dial every single customer 24/7, so no inquiry or follow-up slips through the cracks
* **Multi-Channel Communications:** Calls, SMS, and email work together to book test-drives, service slots, and recall visits the moment customers are ready
* **Dealer-Ready & Secure:** Fast DMS/CRM integration, full call logs, and SOC 2 Type II compliance, built for automotive retail from day one

Dealers switch it on in an afternoon and instantly reach **100% call coverage** with zero wait time.
