# Perspectives Health

> Automating away admin for behavioral health clinics

America is facing an addiction and mental health crisis. We're building a suite of AI agents to automate away the tasks that occupy half of the time and resources of behavioral health clinics—documentation, compliance, audits, and more.

We save time, money, and mental bandwidth so that clinicians can stop chasing insurance reimbursements and go back to saving lives.
