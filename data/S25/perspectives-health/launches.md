# Launches

## Perspectives Health: AI Agents for behavioral health admin

**Perspectives Health is the AI assistant automating the admin work that slows down behavioral health clinics.**

[https://youtu.be/xL-XqY41L10](https://youtu.be/xL-XqY41L10%5C)

Hey everyone! We're Eshan and Kyle, founders of Perspectives Health.

We help the millions of clinicians and clinical directors finally use AI inside the outdated EMR systems they've been stuck in.

Most clinical record software works like Microsoft 95, has no modern APIs, and can't integrate with new tools. That leaves clinicians stuck doing endless manual computer work. 

Believe it or not, most behavioral health clinicians will spend 5 years of their lives just typing notes!

**The Problem**

* 60%+ of clinicians work outside of major hospital systems.
* Most of them use software that nobody can connect to.
* This means they can't access life-changing AI software, which could automate notes, billing, scheduling, and more.

**Our Solution**

* Connects to any web-based EMR instantly. Yes, we mean any EMR.
* Perspectives listens to sessions, writes notes better than a clinician, and populates them straight into any EMR. 
* No copy/paste. And we don't rely on browser agents, so our tool fills out any form in seconds, not minutes.

AI is rapidly improving, but most of healthcare is locked out due to legacy systems. Perspectives bridges that gap, making AI usable immediately. Clinicians want to help people. Not to be drowned in manual work. We're building toward a future where clinicians never touch a keyboard for documentation again—where intelligent systems handle every workflow outside of patient care. 

The first step is integrating seamlessly with outdated EMRs and filling in the critical workflows they lack. From there, we'll expand across specialties—behavioral health, speech therapy, physical therapy, veterinary care, and beyond—unlocking AI for every provider outside the hospital system.

**Our Traction**

* We're growing 25% every week.
* Once users try our product 5 times, the habit is built, and they never write notes without it.
* We have onboarded 9 clinics for our pilot program since YC started, and each is on track to onboard all our pilot clinicians by September (180 clinicians).
