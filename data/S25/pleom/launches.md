# Launches

## Pleom ☂️ - Talk to your AI

**TL;DR**

> Today, Pleom is a website. Ask about anything by talking, showing, or sharing. Tomorrow, it will be integrated into our family of hardware devices.

**What does Pleom do?**

https://youtu.be/uH-W9BKGKT0

At Pleom, we are building AI hardware devices that everyday consumers can talk to for real-world tasks that understands your context. We’re going head-to-head with OpenAI, moving faster to market and capturing mindshare first.

**The Problem**

The current form factor for how people interact with AI hasn’t changed in 5 years.

Texting is also a slower mode of communication than speaking.

Moreover, Artificial Intelligence lacks the context to properly respond to your questions. Products like ChatGPT fail to understand your complete digital context and have zero knowledge of the physical world context.

**The Solution**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=93152&key=user_uploads/2354623/b50422f7-89d9-4b71-bc77-1f86a0e664eb)

A real-life “Jarvis” which seamlessly connects your phones, laptops, car, home appliances, and more to build context to help consumers _ask and do anything_.

Our current live solution on [pleom.com](http://pleom.com) allows anyone to talk to, share their camera or screen and ask questions and receive answers instantly.

But hardware is our focus and we are transitioning from prototyping into production to launch our first version of pocket AI wearable device with a camera, microphone, and speaker that anyone can carry around and interact with faster than using any current form factor today.
