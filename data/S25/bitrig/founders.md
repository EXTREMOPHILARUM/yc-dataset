# Founders

## Kyle Macomber
**Founder**

Kyle is the co-founder and CEO of Bitrig.

Before Bitrig, Kyle spent over a decade at Apple where he co-created and led the development of SwiftUI. He also managed teams behind Swift Charts, WidgetKit, and the Swift Standard Library. Kyle holds a degree in Computer Science from Stanford University.

## Jacob Xiao
**Founder**

Jacob is a co-founder of bitrig.
