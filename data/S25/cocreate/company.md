# cocreate

> video production software

Cocreate was built alongside our friends and colleagues — editors and their assistants, DITs, post supervisors, and UPMs — to design something that will keep individual professionals competitive in a landscape rapidly overrun by needless automation.

We've spent the past few years collecting as many grumbling stories of production and post production inefficiency; lost footage, wasted time. Shots un-slated, timecodes un-jammed, script supervisor notes incoherent; days wasted on menial tasks.

What if there was a tool to take care of all that?
