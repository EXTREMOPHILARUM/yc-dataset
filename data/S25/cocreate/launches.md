# Launches

## CoCreate — AI-Native video editing tool

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=93051&key=user_uploads/1742167/37a5da06-50ca-423f-9bcb-f0ca21e16254)

**TL;DR**

Before editing even starts, professional video-production teams spend days just preparing footage — copying files, syncing audio, creating proxies, and organizing footage. For big shoots, this can eat nearly half the project’s time and budget.

CoCreate is an AI-native tool that does all of that in the background while you back up your footage, so by the time your card is copied, it’s already synced, proxied, organized, and ready to edit in Premiere, Avid, Final Cut, or DaVinci Resolve.

If you work with large volumes of footage, we’d love to onboard you as an early user, connect with assistant editors, post supervisors, or DITs you know, and get your feedback on our demo.

**Problem**

Media prep is the slowest part of post-production. Professional video teams juggle multiple tools to back up, sync, proxy, and organize — a process that’s repetitive, error-prone, and can consume 40% of a project’s time and budget. Existing tools fail at solving this core issue.

**Solution**

\
CoCreate is an AI-native video editing and media organization tool that gets professional video editors from SD card to edit as fast as possible. CoCreate works in parallel during backup: we checksum your files for safety, automatically sync audio with video, create proxies, and organize footage by scene, take, date, camera, and person. The result: **weeks** of prep work done in **minutes**, with everything searchable, and exportable in your preferred format.
