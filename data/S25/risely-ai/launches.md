## Risely: AI agents that automate administrative tasks across college campuses

Hi! We’re Sadia, Shahryar, and Danial, co-founders of **Risely AI**.

**TL;DR** \
Risely’s AI agents automate administrative tasks across college campuses. Our first product, the AI Advisor, improves retention by connecting to university systems (SIS, LMS, and CRM) and unifying student data to flag at-risk students, send personalized outreach, and draft success plans, all in natural language.

### **The Problem**

Colleges spend millions of dollars recruiting students, only to have 1 in 3 students drop out because of poor student service.

Academic advisors – the front line of student support – spend more time digging through clunky systems to manually find at-risk students than helping them succeed.

By the time they realize a student is struggling, students have already slipped through the cracks. 

### **Our Solution**

Risely’s AI Advisor improves retention and expands a university’s capacity to deliver personalized support to every student. 

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92561&key=user_uploads/2145090/6a0960dc-5aad-4498-98cd-bd72892d089d)

**Our AI Advisor works across the SIS, LMS, and CRM to:** 

📍  Flag students showing signs of risk before it’s too late\
✍🏽 Generate personalized success plans in seconds\
🧠 Answer questions about students, courses, and caseloads in natural language\
🗂️ Send personalized outreach and follow-ups

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92561&key=user_uploads/2145090/ae6c7627-5ade-4447-bb02-0fe79637ba02)

**And this is just the beginning.**

Risely’s AI agents will form the new operating system for universities, improving retention, staff productivity, and efficiency. Next, we’re building an  AI Sidekick for every student, an AI Registrar, and an AI Research Admin - all deeply connected to campus workflows and knowledge.

Risely’s early customers include private colleges, large public systems, and online universities - all delivering personalized support at scale without increasing headcount.
