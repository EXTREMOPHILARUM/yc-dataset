**The Problem:**

* Millions of people every year join class action and mass tort lawsuits, hoping for justice and compensation. 
* For the law firm, this means verifying proof of usage and liability for each person—information often buried across thousands of pages of medical, pharmaceutical, and product records. 
* This compounds to several months of work for law firms just to determine who qualifies and who doesn’t.

**Our Solution:**

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EaPmwze4G18

Kalinda…

* **Extracts** proof of use, proof of injury, and other critical data points
* **Aggregates** and correlates this data against lawsuit criteria
* **Generates** a single report so plaintiff attorneys instantly know if a case is valid to file

All while doing this for each case, _working tirelessly and in parallel_. 

When scaled across an entire litigation this turns a process that once took months into just a few hours.

As the lawsuit progresses and criteria change, we also help attorneys filter, categorize, and score to surface their strongest cases.

**Where we’re at:**  To date, we’ve processed 600k pages of records and are live with 2 of the largest plaintiff law firms in the U.S!

**The Why & The Vision:**

The plaintiff side of class actions and mass torts have struggled for decades now with what’s often called the [“Field of Dreams” problem](https://texaslawreview.org/multidistrict-litigation-and-the-field-of-dreams/). Thousands of clients sign up for a lawsuit looking for restitution - only to leave the law firm buried in records, missing deadlines, and filing bad cases. When courts throw out these bad cases, it weakens the plaintiff side’s leverage in settlement negotiations.

With Kalinda, class action & mass tort law firms can now file valid cases faster and file more of them — fundamentally changing for the first time in 172 years not only how many people can get justice against corporate negligence, but how fast it’s delivered.
