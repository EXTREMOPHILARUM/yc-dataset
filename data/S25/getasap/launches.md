# **❌ The Problem**

Legacy distributors _don’t believe in tech_.

• Systems don’t talk to each other, so information is stuck in separate tools.

• Lead times are long and freight isn’t optimized.

• Forecasting is guesswork, which causes stockouts during peak season and costs stores significant revenue.

• Labor costs are high, much of the work is manual, and prices are managed by hand, so margins shrink.

# [**👷**](https://emojipedia.org/construction-worker)**The Solution**

GETASAP ASIA is a tech-enabled distributor for retailers in Southeast Asia, operating a network of optimized fulfillment centers that restock stores in under 8 hours at the lowest landed price.

Our enterprise-grade demand intelligence powers our 8-hour fulfillment model and enables real time stock tracking, automated reordering for stores, and forecasting that positions inventory close to demand—so orders move from purchase to shelf within hours and stockouts approach zero.
