# CareSwift

> AI scribe for Ambulance Reports

CareSwift is a real-time AI assistant built directly into ambulance reporting systems, guiding medics as they document care and instantly flagging errors. Crews complete accurate, compliant reports in under three minutes, cutting documentation time by 80 percent and helping agencies avoid insurance claim denials. Ambulance crews lose valuable time completing reports after each call. Even minor mistakes cause insurance claims to be rejected.
