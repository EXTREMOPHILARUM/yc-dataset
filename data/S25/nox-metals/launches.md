## 🛑 The Problem

Every part, from aerospace components to automotive tooling, starts with raw metal. But the supply chain that delivers it hasn’t changed in decades:

* **3–5 days** to get a quote from a broker.
* **Multiple markups** between the mill and your shop.
* **Slow fulfillment** with no visibility once you order.
* **High overhead** baked into every part because of manual, fragmented workflows.

This slows down factories, raises costs, and makes American manufacturing less competitive.

## ⚡ The Nox Metals Model

* **Orders in → Machines moving:** Your quote instantly becomes a job on our automated cutting line.

Lower overhead means better prices. Faster cycle times mean more throughput for your factory.
