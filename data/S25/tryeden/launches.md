# Launches

## Eden – Email marketing your customers actually want

**TL;DR** - Eden helps e-commerce brands convert hesitant shoppers by automatically creating personalized email campaigns based on individual shopping behavior and customer data.

**Demo:** [**https://youtu.be/bpdzF5rWnTs**](https://youtu.be/bpdzF5rWnTs) 

**The Problem:** 

Your customer data is a mess. It's spread across your CRM, data warehouse, product analytics, customer support tickets, and dozens of other tools. Your marketing team is left guessing. A prospect keeps looking at an expensive piece of furniture but never buys, so you assume it's the price. Your emails are shots in the dark.

**How Eden Works:** 

We connect all your data sources to build a complete story for each prospect’s behavior. We tell you _why_ they hesitate and automatically generate highly targeted emails that give them the info they need to pull the trigger.

* **Before Eden:** 5 visits to a sofa's product page = "Abandoned cart flow + send them a discount"
* **With Eden:** 5 product page visits + downloaded the dimensions guide + read reviews mentioning "apartment" + **a live chat question about assembly services** = "This customer is worried about logistics, not price. Let's email them a video of the easy assembly and proactively offer our white-glove delivery service."

**Why We're Building This:**.

As two conscious consumers ourselves, we started Eden to help the brands we love to buy from. 

While building partnerships with 100+ e-commerce brands at Phia, Alex realized the most valuable insights that drive conversions didn’t come from what customers did, but _why_ they did it. 

Jason learned the same lesson working with advertisers while at Reddit: the biggest wins didn't come from a clever ad, but from providing customers the information they needed to trust the purchase.

**Who we are best for:**

We work best with large DTC/e-commerce brands that have a lot of customer data and want to use it to create emails that drive more sales.
