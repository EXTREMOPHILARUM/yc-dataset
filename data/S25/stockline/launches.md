# **THE PROBLEM**

Most food wholesalers still rely on software from the 90s.

One symptom among many: **Orders come in through WhatsApp, SMS, email, or phone calls — and get retyped manually into their ERP.**

Admin work takes over.\
Headcount grows just to keep up with repetitive tasks.\
From quoting to delivery, everything is **manual, error-prone, and slow**.

They barely use data. **Prices are set by instinct**, with no visibility into stock or margins. And most tools out there just don’t match how they actually work.

This leads to:\
→ Mistakes\
→ Tight margins\
→ Food waste

---

# **THE SOLUTION**

**Stockline replaces outdated ERPs with AI at the core.** The game-changer? **No more manual data entry**.

Our AI captures orders from every channel — **WhatsApp, SMS, email, even phone calls** — and feeds them straight into one unified system.

We centralize:\
• Communication (WhatsApp, emails, SMS…)\
• Operations (billing, inventory, CRM…)

**All in one platform. Admin disappears.**

Once you have all the data in one interface, the sky’s the limit! And the broader vision is to rethink ERPs entirely so that wholesalers finally get to focus on what really matters:\
→ Their products\
→ Their customers\
→ Their margins
