# Stockline

> AI-native ERP for food wholesalers

Stockline is an AI-native ERP for food wholesalers. Our agents eliminate human labor for repetitive tasks, starting with order entry. 

Our goal is to replace clunky software with the next generation of ERPs, built with AI employees at their core.
