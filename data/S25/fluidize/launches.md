❌ **The Problem:** \
**Today’s tools are still slow and disjointed.** In our physics research at Harvard, we wasted weeks navigating incomplete setup instructions, fixing broken dependencies, and duct-taping legacy tools together. That time should have been dedicated towards **actual** science.\
\
🌟 **Our Solution:**\
Fluidize uses AI to accelerate R&D while keeping scientists and engineers in control of critical decisions. Our platform offers:

* **Simplicity**: Turn any sequence (e.g. mesh → solver → validate) into a clear visual pipeline.
* **Automation:** Use natural language to guide AI agents to set up, parameterize, execute, and validate simulations or experiments.
* **Reproducibility:** Save your pipelines and easily share it to your team or the world.
* **Scalability:** Prototype locally, then scale seamlessly to cloud or cluster environments.
* **Transparency:** View and edit all underlying source code for full control.
