### How does it work?

Type a brief; Stormy handles the pipeline:

1. **Describe your ideal creators**\
   “US-based AI YouTubers talking about agents, 10–100k subs” → Stormy searches YouTube & TikTok, podcasts, etc., and verifies each match.
2. **One-click contacts**\
   Grab real emails in bulk (bio, site, press pages) and enrich profiles.
3. **AI Negotiator**\
   Connect your Gmail and TikTok account to personalize outreach, automatic follow-ups, price extraction, and counter-offers within your budget guardrails—logged to a lightweight CRM.
4. **Close & track**\
   Mark deals, export assets, and keep everything in one place (performance tracking integrations coming).

### Who’s it for?

* **Founders/In-house Growth** at AI & consumer apps who want predictable creator acquisition
* **Agencies** running lots of small collabs (micro/nano) and are sick of manual workflows
* **Creators/brands** who need a simple way to scale outreach without scaling headcount

### Why now?

Creator supply is exploding; attention is fragmented. Winning brands are those that can operationalize many small deals, fast. Stormy turns the “thousand tiny tasks” of influencer ops into a clean, automated flow.
