# Launches

## Design Arena - #1 Benchmark for AI Design

**📣 TLDR**

[**Design Arena**](https://www.designarena.ai/) **is the first crowdsourced benchmark for AI-generated design.**

We evaluate how models perform on real-world design tasks - frontend, audio, image, video and more - with live, organic users. It’s the first step of our broader **mission at [Arcada Labs](http://Arcada.dev) to** **distill and understand the human experience.**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92609&key=user_uploads/876530/8919d5ba-9d0e-47e0-9cb9-83ed83b21630)

**❌ Problem**

LLMs can pass bar exams and win IMO gold, but they still generate UIs with white text on a white background. 

There’s no standard to measure taste, usability, or aesthetics, and no benchmark means no pressure to improve.

**🏆 Solution**

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ay89pA_wUHM

We’re building the definitive benchmark for AI-generated design.
