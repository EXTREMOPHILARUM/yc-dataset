## TL;DR

Synthetic Society deploys AI-driven synthetic users to proactively catch bugs, UX flaws, and broken flows before real users experience them. Eliminate manual testing, accelerate your release cycles, and ship confidently.

## Who We Are

We’re Aaron and Kavan, competitive programmers who met in high school, bonded over building, and never stopped. After a few years as product engineers, we dropped out of Columbia and MIT to build Synthetic Society. 

## The Problem

* With AI codegen, teams are pushing features faster than ever. Unfortunately, they’re also pushing bugs, bad design, and broken flows just as fast.
* Traditional testing practices are slow, resource intensive, and error prone. 
* These inefficiencies lead to frustrated end-users, decreased user retention, increased churn, and ultimately, loss of competitive advantage and revenue.

## Our Solution
