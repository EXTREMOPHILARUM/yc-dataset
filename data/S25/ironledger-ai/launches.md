# **Today, property accounting is a massive headache**

For every 1,000 multifamily units, someone on your accounting team is stuck analyzing 400+ transactions. They’re flipping between Yardi, Excel and Outlook, while texting property managers for more detail. It’s not uncommon for this process to take a full week of every month. Accounting teams also have to balance billbacks, bank reconciliations and dozens of other workflows. As an owner or manager, the stress of maintaining accurate financials and finding quality accountants, takes energy away from growing your business. 

Traditional thinking is to throw more headcount at the problem or outsource accounting. But these are expensive, time consuming, and low quality options.
