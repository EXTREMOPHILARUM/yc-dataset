# Palace

> Palace lets commercial truck fleet optimize load plans in less time

Today, commercial trucking fleets make planning and dispatch decisions on gut feel and legacy tooling. Palace lets planning and dispatch teams unify their driver, operations, and freight data so they can optimize their load plans in less time.

Leeds and Derek have been building together as close friends for over six years. The team have been early PMs/engineers at exited startups and decacorns and worked on the algorithms that power Amazon’s distribution centers.
