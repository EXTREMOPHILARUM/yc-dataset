# Founders

## Anmol Tukrel
**Founder**

Anmol is the Co-Founder & CEO of Closera. 

He studied CS at Stanford and was a PM at Google where he monetized Gemini, NotebookLM, and Flow. Previously, he was an APM on Gmail and Photos.

In high school, Anmol founded an app that had users in 120+ countries and was named one of TechCrunch's top AI stories of the year. He was also featured in a Google commercial alongside a Nobel prize winner, Turing award winner, and the Prime Minister of Canada.

## Chinmay Patel
**Founder**

Chinmay is the Co-Founder of Closera.

He holds a BS/MS in CS from Stanford and pursued a joint master’s program at Harvard & MIT.

Previously, he advised F500 companies on AI automation at BCG, built AI to analyze drone footage for investment insights at Point72, and shipped creator products to millions at Roblox. 

He also worked with John Hennessy (Chairman of Alphabet, fmr Stanford President) on ReadRite, a platform to curb online misinformation.
