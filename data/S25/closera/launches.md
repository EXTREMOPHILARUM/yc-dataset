**Problem**\
Commercial real estate (CRE) is packed with boring, repetitive, and tedious work across tasks like creating marketing materials and performing diligence on property listings.

We were inspired to work on this idea after a friend (who is an analyst at a top CRE firm) vented at dinner that she couldn’t hang out later that week because of all the tedious work piling up. A few weeks later, we sat down with her to see what AI could take off her plate. Turns out, a lot! 

We estimate commercial brokers work 60 hours a week, and 35 of those hours could be automated away with AI. This is especially true for the deal process: generating client deliverables, verifying listing details, and valuing properties. These three tasks represent about 60% of time and money spent by CRE brokers today.\
\
**What We’re Building**\
Closera is the AI platform for commercial real estate brokerages. We automate their most repetitive workflows across sales, marketing, and property valuation, saving them hours each day. We envision a future where people in the industry are free from making decks and doing grunt work, so they can focus on the parts of the job they love (or just have more free time for pickleball! 🏓 ).

We’re starting by automating the entire process of creating deal materials like offering memorandums and broker opinions of value, even doing all the research needed as inputs to these documents. We’re able to take a process that usually takes 4 weeks and $5,000, and do it in 5 minutes. 

Closera is building the AI native operating system for the $37T commercial real estate industry.
