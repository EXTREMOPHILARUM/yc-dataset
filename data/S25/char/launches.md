# Launches

## Hyprnote⚡️ Open-source AI notetaker for enterprises

### **TL;DR**

[**Hyprnote**](http://hyprnote.com/?utm_source=yc&utm_medium=launch&utm_campaign=launch_yc) is an [open-source](https://github.com/fastrepl/hyprnote) **AI notetaker for enterprises**. It transcribes and summarizes meetings while keeping 100% of data on-device or on-prem — so companies stay in control.

### **Why Existing AI Notetakers Fail**

Every other tool ships your conversations off to 3rd party clouds. Enterprises banned them because they violated data sovereignty.

### **How Hyprnote Flips the Script**

[**Hyprnote**](http://hyprnote.com/?utm_source=yc&utm_medium=launch&utm_campaign=launch_yc) runs entirely on your laptop, server, or air-gapped network:

* **Private by default**: not a single byte leaves your system, ever.
* **AI-powered notes**: clear, actionable summaries.
* **Enterprise-ready**: HIPAA, SOC2, ITAR, FedRAMP-friendly.
* [**Open source**](https://github.com/fastrepl/hyprnote): transparent and extensible.
* **Flexible deployment**: run it where you want.
