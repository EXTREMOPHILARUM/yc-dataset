# Pally

> Intelligent Unified Inbox + Personal CRM

We spend our lives in messaging apps. Slack. iMessage. WhatsApp. We face a constant stream of information, introductions, and things we need to do.

What if you could bring together every message, from every platform, in a Superhuman-like experience, with AI agents that automatically complete your action items?

That’s exactly what we’re building at Pally; a future where you never need to check another inbox again.
