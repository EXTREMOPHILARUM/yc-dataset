**The Problem:**

* **Certification are critical, non-core work** - Teams (often the Chief Engineers) spend days parsing standards
* **It’s not clear what you actually need** - Compliance can be opaque, often requiring expensive consultants to decipher
* **Labs take long to book** - Third party labs can be unresponsive without insider connections
* **You can’t sell without them** - Not having the right certifications is gating

**What We’re Building:**

* **Certification roadmap to sell:** Clear, market‑specific path (what to test, in what order, and why)
* **Automated lab bookings & logistics:** We handle scheduling, sample coordination, and paperwork end‑to‑end
* **Live test tracking:** Visibility into progress, blockers, and results
* **Technical file generation:** Auto‑assembled technical files and audit packs ready for submission
