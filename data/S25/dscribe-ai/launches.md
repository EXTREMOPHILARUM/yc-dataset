**Problem**

Bulk inventory tracking is broken. Companies either rely on guesstimates or pay for expensive manual surveys which are still off by 20 to 30 percent. Without accurate numbers, it’s hard to plan logistics, like how many truckloads are needed. And it creates accounting issues by misreporting inventory value, hurting financial planning and leading to missed opportunities.

**Solution**

dScribe AI figures out what your inventory is, turns it into a 3D reconstruction, and tells you exactly how much you have, in both volume and weight. If your piles are smaller and indoors, like in a shed, we’ll use a fixed camera. If they’re bigger and outside, we’ll fly a drone to map them. And we don’t just stop at giving you the numbers. We plug into your system so the data helps you make real decisions, not just sit in a spreadsheet.
