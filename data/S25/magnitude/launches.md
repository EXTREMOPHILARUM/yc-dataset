## Sidekick - Automation for the rest of us

Hey! We’re[ Tom](https://www.linkedin.com/in/t-greenwald/) and[ Anders](https://www.linkedin.com/in/anders-lie/), founders of[ Sidekick](https://joinsidekick.com).

**TL;DR:**

Teams in sales, marketing, and ops often turn to platforms like Zapier to automate their busywork. But these platforms are too complex and time-consuming for them. Sidekick replaces the steep learning curve with a simple chat interface, making powerful automation accessible to everyone.

https://youtu.be/K3nd9Ybxux4

**Problem:** Automation platform interfaces are outdated, drowning technical and non-technical users alike in complexity

For anyone in sales, marketing, or ops who wants to build an automation, the options are frustrating:

1. **Struggle for hours** to build a watered-down version of what you actually need
2. **Lose days (or weeks) you don’t have** trying to master a complex platform
3. **Wait for a technical teammate** to find time in their packed schedule to build it for you
4. **Hire an outside agency** that lacks the internal context to build it right

Even those with technical know-how end up wasting hours wrestling with these platforms, often just to hit a dead end.

**Solution:** Sidekick enables anyone on your team to build powerful automations just by chatting

Instead of tangled flowcharts and endless dropdowns, you build in a simple side-by-side interface. The chat on the left is your input, and on the right, a live visual of your workflow is generated as the output. You build and modify the entire automation just by typing in the chat.

The result: Automation is finally accessible to the team members who need it most, while also giving technical pros a dramatically faster way to build.
