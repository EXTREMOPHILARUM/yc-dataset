## Mimos - Answer Engine Optimization for regulated companies

Hey everyone! We’re Rohit and Michael, co-founders of Mimos. We’ve been building together since sixth grade—everything from mobile apps to algorithmic trading systems—and now we’re turning compliance into an advantage in the age of AI.

https://youtu.be/McIKklZ0xHI

**The Problem:**

AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) are replacing Google for high-value questions on health, wealth, and legal. Whoever they cite becomes the voice customers trust.

Regulated firms should dominate this shift — they already have **compliance** as verifiable proof of authority. But that advantage is trapped behind a unique structural dynamic:

* **Marketing** needs to move quickly to publish relevant, expert answers.
* **Compliance** must carefully review every word to ensure regulatory safety.

This friction results in a drawn out publishing cycle, resulting in missed opportunity to rank in AI search.

**Our Solution:**

Mimos makes it easy for regulated firms to get cited in AI answers by streamlining the path from insight to compliant content.

* **Define your audience** — Capture your brand expertise and ICPs to guide tone, examples, and disclosures.
* **Find the right questions** — Track the exact high-intent prompts your prospects ask AI using trend and citation data.
* **See where you stand** — Monitor visibility by engine, track competitor citations, and measure share of voice.
* **Act on opportunities** — Get prioritized recommendations for content, SEO, and distribution.
* **Create faster** — Generate first drafts aligned to your brand and compliance needs.
* **Speed up review** — Package answers with disclosures and claim checks so compliance can approve faster.

**Result:** Insight → content → review → AI visibility — in minutes, not weeks.
