# **📉 The Problem**

I saw firsthand as an operator that growth in distribution is capped almost entirely by hiring. It’s hard to find good sales talent, and even harder to retain.

* Sales teams spend most of their day quoting, entering orders, and chasing product data instead of talking to customers.
* Training new reps takes months, and **more than 50% of the workforce will retire this decade**.
* Critical knowledge lives in inboxes or people’s heads, and disappears when they leave.

The result: companies can’t scale beyond the speed of hiring, while service slows and relationships suffer.

# **📈 Our Solution**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=93001&key=user_uploads/1006692/0ab6182e-5b8b-4bf9-9d33-3a0a0668e079)

Avent watch every channel where customer requests arrive—email, fax, phone:

* **Quoting:** When a customer asks for a price, Avent finds the correct product and pricing, generates the quote, and prepares it to send.
* **Order Entry:** When the customer follows up with a purchase order (PO), Avent matches it to the earlier quote, creates the sales order, and inputs it into the company’s ledger
* **Procurement:** Never lose an order. If a customer needs something that’s not in stock, Avent will find the best cost from in-network distributors and quote

Avent generates over **$200,000 worth of quotes every day** for distributors across North America and the UK.
