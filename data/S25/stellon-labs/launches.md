# Launches

## Stellon Labs: Building Tiny AI Models for Edge Devices

[Launch Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aUoLxgi5hQA)\
\
**The problem:**\
Existing foundation models are so large that they need an insane amount of compute and memory, making them inaccessible for edge devices like smartphones, laptops, robots and other embedded systems. \
\
**What we do:**\
We are training state-of-the-art tiny models designed specially to run on these devices without compromising on quality. Our first model is KittenTTS 😻 , which is a tiny open-source text-to-speech model that is under 25MB in size. It got 8K Github stars and 45K model downloads within 2 weeks of launch. At Stellon Labs, we are building tiny frontier models for Speech, Language and Video intelligence to power every single device in the world.\
\
**Our ask:**\
We would like to connect with developers or companies( both software and hardware) that are interested in running AI models locally. We are happy to provide support for custom models, training and deployments across different applications.\
