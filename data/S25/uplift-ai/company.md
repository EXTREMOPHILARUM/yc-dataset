# Uplift AI

> Foundational Voice Models for regional languages

We build Voice Models for regional languages (like Urdu, Bengali, Greek etc) and make them available through simple APIs and SDKs. 
Developers use us to create Voice-first digital experiences (eg. Voice eCommerce, Voice Banking, Voice AI Tutors etc).

We gather our own data, develop our own labelling tools, train our own models, and design our own voice infra. This fully integrated approach enables us to offer the best models and the best developer experience for regional languages.

---

People who would previously never use certain digital services (like online ordering), actually use the service when they are given an intuitive Voice based interface.
We believe that Voice Interfaces will onboard 100s of millions of new users onto digital services; and double the addressable market for technology companies. 
Uplift AI will provide the best models to create these Voice Interfaces for 60% of the world's population that only speaks their regional language and represents 42 trillion dollars in GDP.
