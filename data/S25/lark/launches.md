## Lark: Stripe without the workarounds

**Problem** 🔴

Too often, customers tell us they use Stripe for billing but when I ask how they manage their credits or track usage, I always hear the same answer: “we built it in-house”. These pricing models are not edge-cases. They’re the new normal in the age of AI.

**Solution** ⚒️

[uselark.ai](https://uselark.ai) is a modern billing platform with the flexibility to support any pricing model (credit-based, usage-based, hybrid, dimensional, token-based, etc…) at any scale (**10x more** than Stripe).

Whether you’re building an AI powered API or a B2B SaaS product, Lark provides everything (including checkout + customer-facing portal) you need to implement complex billing logic without the hassle.

A few nice things about us -

* You don't have to manage any webhooks or store custom billing state (like stripe customer id) in your database
* Our APIs are simple. You don't have to integrate with a separate checkout API. We also provide [frontend SDKs](https://docs.uselark.ai/guides/subscriptions/feature_access/) to check billing state (like remaining credits, feature access)
* Seamless pricing changes that automatically apply to your customers without involving engineers
* We also help you track margins & LLM costs per customer for AI applications

## Lark: AI Agents for Billing

**TL;DR** 

Lark helps subscription companies automate customer billing support inquiries, manage chargebacks, and prevent fraud.

https://youtu.be/gmg8HxjaS1Q

**Problem**

Billing your customers is hard.

Teams spend tens of hours every week attending to billing support inquiries and customers often wait days to receive a response. You also have to worry about fraud and chargebacks.

All of this is distracting for your team and no one likes working on this. Ignoring the issue leads to frustrated customers, risk of being banned from your payment processor, and lost revenue.

**Solution**

Lark provides AI agents that automate all of this for you.

Our agents integrate with your billing provider, support system (e.g. zendesk), and data sources to gather context about the customer and take actions on your behalf. The agents’ decisions are evaluated against strong guardrails to ensure their actions are safe and follow your policies.
