# Launches

## Doe: A new productivity platform

Hey everyone, we're [Adrian](https://x.com/AdrianBarbir) and [Richard](https://x.com/YuhuangOu). We've been building Doe for the past few months.

**TL;DR**

Doe is an action engine for work. You connect your tools, tell it what you need, and agents go do it. Not chat. Actual execution: pull data, update records, send messages, run workflows across your systems. When you do things yourself, like modifying spreadsheets or writing an email, agents work alongside you.

**What we're building toward**

Knowledge working is changing as you read this post. Not "AI answers your questions" change. More like: you wake up and agents have already reconciled your expenses, flagged at-risk deals, and drafted the weekly update. You review, adjust, and move on. The tedious stuff happened while you slept.

When you do want to dig in or do stuff yourself, you're augmented with sets of tools. A good example is cursor’s tab/autocomplete. Your work platform has context and every action you take is faster because of what's running in the background.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=96000&key=user_uploads/1867926/23d9a617-3216-4243-8198-3f6a8ae8e52d)

That's the vision. A workspace where agents do work and humans get augmented.\
\
**What you can do today**

1. **Ask anything about your data.** No SQL. No dashboards. Just ask.
2. **Get boring work done.** Agents update your CRM, send Slack messages, create Linear tickets, pull reports, and create financial models. You watch in the preview pane and step in when you want.
3. **Set up Loops.** Agents that run on a schedule. Daily pipeline reviews. Weekly reconciliation. Continuous monitoring. Set it once.\
   \
   We like having Doe go through our linear + github to create a performance review each day :)

**Enterprise stuff**

SOC 2 Type II. HIPAA-ready. Zero data retention with LLM providers. SSO, SCIM, audit logging.
