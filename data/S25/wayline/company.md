# Wayline

> AI voice for property managers

Wayline is an AI front desk assistant purpose-built for real estate to answer calls and messages 24/7. It’s like an always-on property manager that can convert leads and resolve issues better than any human, day or night. Our AI can be setup in as little as 24 hours and works seamlessly with existing phone/management software. Uniquely, our AI knowledge base is domain-specific to learn and handle the most complex real estate workflows. For property operators, Wayline instantly replaces robo-menus and call centers, while centralizing and scaling back-office teams.
