## Wayline: AI voice and text for property managers

**TL;DR**\
Wayline is voice AI and text for property managers that automates all tasks traditionally handled by receptionists, leasing agents, and maintenance coordinators. Imagine staffing an always-on property assistant that can convert leads and resolve issues instantly, day or night. Instead of robo-menus or call centers, every call and text is answered instantly by AI that knows the property better than the onsite teams.

We bring a decade of property tech and real estate operations experience, having bootstrapped a management company to $1M ARR, run WeLive’s global operations, and oversaw WeWork’s $110M All-Access product. We’re also multi-time founders, having raised $25M+ across three YC-backed companies, and built platforms at LinkedIn and Workday. Together, we combine deep real estate expertise with repeat founder experience in AI and enterprise software.

**The Problem**

Property management is human-intensive, low-margin, and full of repetitive communication. Operators juggle endless leasing calls, maintenance triage, and support inquiries - all while managing constant staff turnover and fragmented tools like AppFolio, Yardi, and spreadsheets. The result: overworked teams, missed opportunities, and reactive service.

**The Solution**

Wayline is the new front desk for real estate. Property management is largely administrative work - phone calls, coordination, and repetitive workflows - and we automate all of it with AI and a property-specific knowledge base. We pull in data from hundreds of public and proprietary sources to resolve issues in real-time and automate follow-up workflows. For tenants and prospects, calling or texting the building _is_ the interface. For operators, Wayline centralizes and scales what used to take teams of on-site managers and assistants.

**Why Now**

Real estate has resisted disruption for decades with fragmented platforms, duct-taped point solutions, and thin margins. However, AI changes the labor equation: for the first time, the administrative side of property management can be run largely by software. And unlike human teams, AI never sleeps or churns.
