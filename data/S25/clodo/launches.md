## Clodo – The AI Assistant for Real Estate Agents 🏡

# **TL;DR**: Clodo is the AI Assistant for real estate agents. It helps agents remember and follow up with all their leads.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WDXtM2T6NwA

Book a demo at https://calendly.com/clodoai/30min

Clodo is an invisible, hands-free CRM that enables agents to effortlessly record and recall every detail about their leads just by speaking in natural language. With this information, Clodo automatically figures out who to follow up with, why and when. Clodo is currently used by over 60 real estate agents across the country.

**Our Ask: If you are a real estate agent (or know one), we would love to get in touch**. **We’re at** [founders@clodo.ai](https://mailto:founders@clodo.ai)

### **The Problem**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92815&key=user_uploads/353032/e6def58f-d8eb-4fca-95d6-6c616ee16c8f)

Real estate agents are in the business of constantly doing things that don’t scale, whether that’s making note of every little detail about a lead or scouring the MLS for the perfect property for a client. **When leads are forgotten or not followed up with correctly, that directly translates to missed deals**. The factors that drive this are:

↳ **Terrible I/O**: Simply remembering a lead’s interest in a bigger yard could be the difference-maker in getting their business. Making a durable note of preferences as well as looking them up requires logging onto a CRM (don’t forget 2FA) and navigating a rocket-cockpit dashboard.

↳ **Follow Up is Difficult**: National data indicates that 80% of sales happen between the 5th and 12th contact, but despite this, \~50% of agents do not follow up after first contact.

↳ **Too Many Tabs**: Extremely manual functions like finding properties to send to a particular lead or client requires switching between multiple tools such as the CRM, notes app, MLS, email client, and nowadays, ChatGPT.

**RESULT: Poor quantity and quality of follow up = less conversion and deals for any agent, from rookie to market specialist.**

### **THE SOLUTION**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92815&key=user_uploads/353032/e39f24e0-27a0-43ac-91fc-788a44da4532)

**Just speak to your CRM** — Natural language is the new I/O medium for the real estate CRM. Quickly make note of new leads and recall information about clients on the go, such as when you’re door knocking or driving.

**Take the guesswork out of follow up** — Clodo automatically figures out who to follow up with, what to say to them, and why.

**Personalized property search** — Clodo connects to MLS IDX feeds to find and send hyper-personalized sets of properties to clients that go beyond the traditional search criteria of # of bedrooms, # of bathrooms.

Real estate agents that obsess over their clients — find properties that are a perfect fit for them, remember their kids’ birthdays, send them coupons for their favorite restaurant long after a transaction — are the ones who dominate their market. **Clodo makes it easy to obsess over leads and clients, and thereby win more deals.**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92815&key=user_uploads/353032/5ef2ebcc-0e30-41d5-b537-21f5ef7cebf8)

For too long, complicated tools for real estate agents have been justified by the mantra: “\_the effort you put into it is what you get out of it\_”. We humbly disagree. Tools should be effortless, even for real estate.
