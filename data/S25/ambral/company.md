# Ambral

> AI Account Managers

Ambral synthesizes signals from all customer activity and interactions into AI-powered models of every account. It pinpoints who needs attention and why, and autonomously takes the best next action to drive expansions, prevent churn, and ensure no customer slips through the cracks. 

Team:

Jack (CTO): Ex-SpaceX Flight Software Special Projects, where he developed telemetry routing and analysis systems for the most complex machines ever created. He’s now doing the same for the complexities of customer relationships.
