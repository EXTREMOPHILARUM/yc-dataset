# 🛑 **The Problem:**

Staffing a dedicated human Account Manager for every mid-market and SMB customer has never been economically feasible. Companies are forced to focus only on their largest accounts while the rest are underserved or ignored, resulting in millions in expansion revenue left on the table and preventable churn.

# 🔥 **Our Solution:**

Cortex eliminates this tradeoff and makes it possible to bring an enterprise-grade experience to every customer. It uses AI to find hidden revenue signals, predict what each customer needs next, and autonomously executes the right revenue-driving or strategic action. It can handle everything from conversational onboarding calls, to manual reporting work, to driving expansions and securing renewals. It's like cloning your best Account Manager, giving them a degree in data science, and assigning one to each of your customers.
