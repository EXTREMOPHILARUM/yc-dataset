# Launches

## Flywheel AI: Waymo for excavators

Hey everyone! We’re[ Jash](https://linkedin.com/in/jashmota) and[ Mahimana](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mahimanabhatt/) - co-founders of Flywheel AI - we enable remote control for excavators on construction sites.

We do this by enabling remote teleoperation. We then use the teleoperated excavators to collect the datasets needed to automate the machines.

https://youtu.be/Kpbor3M6Mr8

**🚧Problem**

Construction industry has some troubling trends:

* **1 million worker shortage** in the US alone
* **1000+ deaths annually** from safety incidents (and rising)
* **Hundreds of thousands in penalties** + multi-week shutdowns per incident
* **40% operational downtime** due to blind spots and inefficiencies

It’s **Dull, Dirty, Dangerous**, dialed to the max!

The problem is global and getting worse as the workforce ages and recruitment plummets.

**👷The Flywheel Solution**

**🚜Retrofit**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92796&key=user_uploads/367031/4e9829e3-a90f-4d93-b08f-d17cb961ea7f)

We engineered a new way to retrofit heavy equipment, which is machine-agnostic and compatible with pretty much any hydraulic equipment. These can be installed in few hours and don’t block manual operations.

**🕹️Remote Teleop**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92796&key=user_uploads/367031/cb9452e8-c8eb-4b0f-9a2f-37cfb58a1314)

We built a low-latency remote teleop + dataset collection platform with the ability to remotely turn on/off the machines and let supervisors take over at any time. This enables controlling the fleet even from a minimal setup of laptop and joysticks.

**⚡Workflow Autonomy**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92796&key=user_uploads/367031/a75da0c3-ee2b-4b86-9f6f-5108e7a5423b)

We use data from actual operations and robot learning to train models which does various tasks like dig and dump, trenching, grading and more. This will enable a single operator to manage more machines and sites at the same time.
