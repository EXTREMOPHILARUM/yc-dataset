# Pingo AI

> AI companion that helps you learn languages

Pingo AI is an AI language learning app that helps users build fluency through speaking. It immerses learners in realistic dialogues and personalized lessons with an AI tutor who sounds like a native speaker. Our goal is to make learning languages as simple as daily conversations with Pingo — a companion who understands your goals, remembers your progress, and keeps you motivated.
