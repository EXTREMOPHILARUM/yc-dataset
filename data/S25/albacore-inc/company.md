# Albacore Inc.

> Autonomous undersea vehicles to deter maritime invasions.

Albacore is building 6ft-long drone submarines to deter littoral conflict. Our first product, Ghostfin can be launched from land in minutes, and carry precision effects or conduct sensing missions up to 1,000 nautical miles. Our team consists of multiple serial entrepreneurs in defense and hardware who have worked alongside top DoD leadership on autonomous platforms.
