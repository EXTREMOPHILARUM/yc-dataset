## Pond: Superhuman for iMessage

Hi everyone! We’re Aagam and Brian — the founders of Pond.

**tl;dr**

We’re building an AI-native text messaging app. Salespeople, real estate agents, recruiters and others use Pond to read and send texts at lightning speed.

**Problem**

_Does your messages app look like this? It shouldn’t have to._

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92163&key=user_uploads/1249430/94cc5e7c-d0bd-4676-956f-f77089f248f7)

Busy people get hundreds of work and personal messages a week, some of which are job-critical. The messages app on your computer isn’t built to sift through this information, making texting more time intensive than it should be.

**Solution**

Pond is a desktop app that connects to your iMessage (WhatsApp and others coming soon):

* **Sorts your messages** - using AI to tag conversations and show you the most important ones
* **Lets you fly through messages faster** - with better search and shortcuts
* **Connects AI to your messages** - with an assistant to draft and summarize messages

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92163&key=user_uploads/1249430/7fee7357-7a48-48fa-ac23-09be3b6f751a)

At Pond, our mission is to unlock your personal context trapped in messages and other data while still keeping your personal information safe.
