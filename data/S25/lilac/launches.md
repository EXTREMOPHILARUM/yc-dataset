## Lilac - We find you GPUs!

💬 **TL;DR Lilac is an open-source tool that ensures your data scientists always have enough GPUs for their work. We seamlessly connect compute from any source, on-prem or cloud. [Check out our GitHub!](https://github.com/getlilac/lilac)**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=92724&key=user_uploads/2382454/b1a41fba-535e-4392-9526-f97e72771387)

❗**The Problem: The GPU Scramble is Slowing Down AI Innovation**

Ask any data scientist and they will likely tell you their biggest frustration is finding GPUs. One team we talked to is spending $7 million dollars a year with a service provider to guarantee GPUs, yet they were still running out. This forces data science and infrastructure teams into a difficult position:

* **Wasted Time:** Data scientists waste hours hunting for available GPUs across different clouds and on-premise servers, distracting them from building models.
* **Spiraling Costs & Security Risks:** Infrastructure teams lose visibility as desperate data scientists use personal keys on unmanaged instances, leading to security gaps and unpredictable cloud bills.
* **Slowed Innovation:** The result is a direct trade-off between innovation speed and engineering best practices. Companies either accept a slow model development lifecycle or pay millions for dedicated GPU contracts in a single cloud, losing flexibility.

🏆 **The Solution: Unify Your Compute, Unleash Your Data Scientists**

Lilac is an open-source platform that transforms your scattered, multi-cloud, and on-premise GPU resources into a single, unified compute fabric.

Instead of hunting for servers, your data scientists simply submit their training jobs to Lilac. Our intelligent scheduler handles the rest—finding the first available GPU that meets the job's requirements, allocating it, and running the job.

**The Result:**

* **For Data Scientists:** Maximum productivity. They can focus on building models, not managing infrastructure.
* **For Infrastructure Teams:** Centralized control. Gain full visibility, enforce security policies, and manage costs across your entire fleet of compute resources.

Lilac bridges the gap between R&D and infrastructure, turning GPU scarcity into a streamlined, efficient workflow.
