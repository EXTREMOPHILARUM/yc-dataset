# Autosana

> AI Agents for Mobile & Web QA

Autosana uses AI agents to save countless engineering hours and help teams ship with more confidence. 

Turns natural language into E2E tests, eradicating manual testing and the need to write/maintain UX scripts. Seamlessly integrates into your CI, supports both iOS, Android, and Web apps, and is framework-agnostic (React Native, Flutter, Swift, etc).

Catching bugs before users do.
