## Dash: AI That Actually Gets Work Done

**We built an AI agent that connects to your Gmail, Slack, Notion, and more to complete work autonomously.**

# [usedash.ai](http://usedash.ai) | [founders@usedash.ai](mailto:founders@usedash.ai)

## The Problem

Knowledge workers spend 90% of their time on manual, repetitive tasks that could be automated. Current AI tools like ChatGPT operate in isolation; they can't update Notion databases, search across Slack, or send emails.

## Our Solution

Dash is an AI agent that connects to your tools (Gmail, Slack, Notion, Linear, Google Docs, HubSpot, calendar, and more) to complete entire tasks.

https://youtu.be/kFPAxXAUlq8

## Real Examples from Beta Users

**"Weekly team update"**

* Reads Slack channels for key discussions
* Checks Linear tickets for sprint progress
* Pulls metrics from HubSpot and Notion
* Compiles and emails formatted report

**"New enterprise lead follow-up"**

* Adds leads to Hubspot with enriched data
* Creates qualification tasks in Linear
* Schedules follow-up calls in calendar
* Notifies team in Slack with context

**"Daily standup agenda"**

* Pulls yesterday's completed Linear tickets and today's planned work for team members
* Generates standup summary in Notion
