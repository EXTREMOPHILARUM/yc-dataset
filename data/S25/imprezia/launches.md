# Launches

## 🚀 Imprezia - World’s First AI Ad Network

# **TL;DR:** MIT & Ex-Meta founder building the first ad network for AI chatbots. Brands pay to get mentioned and linked inside AI responses, and developers can monetize their AI apps.

# **The Problem: Subscription only gets you so far…**

Most AI apps today try to monetize with subscriptions, but just can’t find enough users who are willing to pay. 

→ **Inference bills are crushing** - AI compute costs 10-30x more than traditional apps\
→ **Subscription gates kill growth** - Only 3% of users typically convert

→ **Can't scale to billions** - Free tiers = bankruptcy\
\
Meanwhile, Brands are dying to get mentioned organically inside of AI chat results.

It’s time for a new model.

# **Our solution:** Imprezia brings contextual advertising directly into AI conversations.

\
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wI7k7Byftks

### **How It Works:**

1. **One-line integration** - Drop in our SDK with an API key
2. **Contextual ads** - We subtly include relevant brand mentions in your AI chat
3. **Revenue sharing** - Developers get paid per impression or per click
