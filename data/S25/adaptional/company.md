# Adaptional

> AI underwriting for insurance

Adaptional is the first system of intelligence for insurance. Existing insurance systems organize work. Adaptional AI actually *does* the work.

Our first product Underwriter AI pre processes every submission for an underwriter. AI extracts data from submission files, validates each data point using public sources, and summarizes risk using your underwriting guidelines.
