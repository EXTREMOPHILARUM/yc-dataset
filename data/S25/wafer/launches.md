### **🧙 The Problem**

Getting AI models to run fast on GPUs requires a rare breed of engineer. 

These GPU wizards:

* Command $300k+ base salaries (if you can even find them)
* Spend **months** hand-writing custom kernels for each model
* Need to rewrite every kernel for new hardware

Even worse: If you want to run on alternative GPUs or other accelerators, you need **different** **experts** to **rewrite all your kernels**. Most teams are stuck choosing between:

* Paying $$$ for GPU talent
* Accepting &lt;50% hardware utilization
* Locked to one chip longer than they want

---

—

### **🐄 How Wafer solves it**

Wafer does what those GPU engineering wizards do, automatically. Feed it your PyTorch code, and it generates the optimized kernels that would normally take months of manual work.

**What you get:**

* **Auto optimization** - Your PyTorch goes in, optimized GPU code comes out. Same model, **1.5 to 5x faster**. The kernels Wafer writes are what a senior CUDA engineer would write after weeks of profiling and tuning.
* **Hardware portability** - NVIDIA can’t give you enough capacity? Want to try out AMD’s new MI350 series? Wafer makes your code fast on both in seconds. No rewrites, no performance penalties.
* **Production monitoring** - Wafer watches your models in production, spots inefficiencies, and fixes them. Think of it as having a GPU expert on-call 24/7.

You write PyTorch. Wafer makes it run like it was hand-tuned by the best in the business. Wafer then monitors prod to track performance. No CUDA expertise needed on your team.
