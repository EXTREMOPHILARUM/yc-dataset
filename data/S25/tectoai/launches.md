# **The Problem**

💸 Most companies are eager to **unlock the $B in efficiency** promised by AI, but unsure which use cases to start with. Should they bring on an AI email manager? Or a task manager that syncs with email? Or an all-in-one AI coordinator? 

📋 Even after choosing, the **approval process**—from vendor risk assessment to infosec, GRC, and legal—is slow and convoluted. And by the time it's complete, the tool or regulations may have already changed.

🐒 Moreover, agentic AI tools **aren’t static like traditional SaaS**. They continuously evolve with new models, multimodal capabilities, and dynamic behaviors. **Much like humans**, their responses can vary depending on context, drift to favor shortcuts, or adapt tone _([Chen et al., 2025](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2506.06366))_

📊 Most importantly, relying solely on **model benchmarks falls short**. Agent behavior is shaped by capability, context, and adaptation, not just the underlying model. For example:

* A single missed medical term in a transcription can lead to a hallucinated diagnosis in the summarization layer (error propagation) ☠️
* In a back-office copilot, the assistant began favoring shortcuts over accuracy after users consistently chose the fastest suggestions, optimizing for user approval instead of policy (favoring shortcuts)  💾

# **Our Solution**

**Tecto platform provides the evaluations and qualifications needed to employ and monitor agentic AI, like gusto for AI employees.**

* Tecto helps you pinpoint where AI fits within your workflow and matches you with a curated list of vetted AI employees aligned with your organization’s expectations, risk tolerance, and compliance requirements. 
* Once deployed, we continuously monitor their behavior—using output testing to detect drift, edge case failures, and other unexpected actions as they arise. 
* We also keep you ahead of rapidly evolving AI regulations worldwide, alerting you to public incidents or legal actions involving tools in your stack, so you can respond before issues escalate.

🕹️ With Tecto, onboarding AI employees is fast and oversight doesn’t stop after deployment. Your AI adoption is structured, controlled, and safe from day one.
