### **The Problem**

Hiring globally is still: 

* **Expensive:** Typically charged at **$50**/month per contractor, **$599‑$699** per employee (plus hidden fees and 12‑month lock‑ins)
* **Slow:** Onboarding can take days or weeks, especially for employees
* **Clunky:** Portal logins, support tickets, and back‑and‑forth for basic changes
* **Delayed payouts:** Contractors wait days (sometimes weeks) for bank transfers

This results in many teams manually managing the process or skipping hiring global talent altogether.

**Our Solution**

Shor is the fastest, simplest, and most affordable way to hire globally, without outdated portals, 12 month lock-ins, or slow bank transfers.

From one Slack or WhatsApp thread, you can:

* **Hire contractors or employees anywhere in the world** with no local entity required.
* **Generate country-specific contracts instantly**
* **Onboard new hires in minutes** while we collect documents, verify compliance, and get them ready to work in the same conversation.
* **Run payroll in seconds** with payouts landing in a matter of minutes rather than days
* **Stay compliant automatically** while we handle local labor laws, taxes, and filings in the background
* **Scale without friction** adding or removing team members with no 12-month lock-in and no hidden fees.

Contractors are **$20/month** flat. 

Employees (EOR) are **$99/month**, rolling out corridor-by-corridor starting with **India, the Philippines, and Nigeria**.
