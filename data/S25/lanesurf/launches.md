### **❌ THE PROBLEM:**

**Freight still runs on phone calls. That’s where brokers lose deals, margin, and time.**

To cover a single load, brokers often take 30–50 inbound calls from carriers. \
**On each call**, they repeat the same load info, manually negotiate rates, and check compliance for each.

Brokers either use in-house reps or offshore teams but even then:

* **Missed calls from carriers offering better rates**
* **Reps settle too quickly in negotiations just to close**
* **High costs and churn**

This is **slow, expensive**, and repeated **100s of times daily**.

### **✅ THE SOLUTION:**

Lanesurf is Voice AI for carrier sales that:

* **Answers every call**
* **Shares load details and vets carrier compliance**
* **Negotiates rates using fuel prices, lane preferences & market trends**
* **Makes outbound calls if no inbound match is found**

* **Book more margin per load**
* **4x lower costs to book a load**
* **Even win more shipper loads by quoting reliably**

Our **AI gets smarter** with **every call** learning patterns across lanes, carriers, and seasons - creating proprietary pricing signals brokers can act on.
