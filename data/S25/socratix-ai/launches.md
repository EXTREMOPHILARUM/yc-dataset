# ❌ **The Problem:**

Today, fraud and risk teams at fintechs and banks are overwhelmed. They're reviewing **hundreds of alerts per week** - jumping between tools, manually gathering context, and making tough judgment calls under pressure.

The work is **repetitive, slow, and prone to error**. Hiring more analysts is slow and expensive. Meanwhile, fraudsters are creative, constantly evolving their tactics to bypass static rules and outdated ML models.

The result: overwhelmed teams, blocked good users, and missed real threats.

# ✅ **The Solution:**

Socratix gives fraud and risk teams AI coworkers - **agents that think like human analysts but operate 24/7 and scale effortlessly.**

Our agents plug into existing tools and workflows to:

* Ingest alerts
* Pull rich context (accounts, devices, behaviors, history)
* Assess risk using past cases and SOPs
* Auto-close false positives
* Escalate high-risk cases with clear rationale and next steps

The result is fewer fraud losses, reduced false positives, and better customer experiences - all without scaling headcount.
