## ❌ The Problem

Referrals are one of the most critical, yet most broken, parts of the US healthcare system. Health centers are required to track specialist referrals to ensure continuity of patient care and meet quality standards. **But with limited staff and overwhelming administrative demands, health centers struggle to keep up, leading many to outsource the work.**

**Consequences:**

* High error rates in referrals
* Denied authorizations and delays in specialist appointments
* Wasted time manually copying data between various systems

**Impact:**

These operational inefficiencies create major **financial risks** for health centers. On top of the resources centers already deploy to manage referrals, failure to effectively close the loop puts quality standards in jeopardy, carrying even greater financial implications.

## [👷](https://emojipedia.org/construction-worker) Our Solution

Locata uses AI to automate the referral management process from end-to-end. Imagine having a team of the world’s best referral coordinators that operate with higher accuracy, lower costs, and faster delivery, giving health centers the capacity to scale on demand and close more referral loops.

* **AI auth submission**: Our AI extracts patient data directly from EHRs, intelligently maps it to the correct authorization forms across different payor portals, and submits requests automatically.
* **Automated status updates:** We monitor authorization status continuously, with automatic notifications sent to staff for cases requiring urgent attention.
* **Voice AI patient follow-up:** Our Voice AI calls patients on customizable cadences to monitor whether they’ve made a specialist appointment. Our system alerts staff when they need to step in to offer additional support.
* **Consult note retrieval**: Once a patient confirms an appointment, our AI automatically contacts specialists, requesting consult notes to effortlessly close the loop.

Within two weeks of building our product, we signed a major regional health center and are taking them live to automate their entire referral workflow.
