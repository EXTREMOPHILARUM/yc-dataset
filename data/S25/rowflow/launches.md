## RowFlow: Turn any form into an AI conversation

**Hey all -** we’re Jack and Alec, co-founders of **RowFlow**.

**TL;DR**

RowFlow can take any existing static form and instantly convert it into a dynamic AI-powered conversation. New flows launch in minutes and reach respondents over text, Slack, embedded chat, or wherever your users are. We guide the exchange, follow up automatically, and return clean structured data without extra work from your team. Compared to traditional webforms, our AI-native approach delivers higher completions, less overhead, and a better user experience.

**Problem**\
Forms suck. They power power billions of business interactions yearly _(think: lead qualification, customer onboarding, client intake, feedback forms, surveys, event RSVPs, registration forms, compliance, healthcare…the list goes on),_ but most have:

* **Low conversion:** too many users provide half-answers or drop off before completing.
* **Time drain:** teams waste hours building complex logic, chasing non-responders, hand-holding, and cleaning messy data.
* **Poor user experience:** even when forms do work, they frustrate users and hurt your brand.

Teams have been forced to tolerate these pain points as there's been no scalable alternative for collecting structured information from people - until now.

**Solution**\
With RowFlow, we abstract away from the static form structure altogether. It works like this:

1. Say what data you need ([or try our Google Form converter](https://rowflow.ai/)).
2. RowFlow runs guided AI conversations over text, Slack, email, or embedded chat with automated follow-ups.
3. You get clean, structured data delivered into your systems.

Our goal is for RowFlow to become the new default way for how organizations collect needed user data in an AI-first world.
