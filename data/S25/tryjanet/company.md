# Janet AI

> The AI-Native Jira

Janet AI (janet.ai) is the first AI-native ticket management system, built for modern engineering teams and tool stacks. Janet doesn’t wait for your manual work.

- Ideas, bug reports, and feature requests get tracked directly from meetings/Slack/Discord/SMS/email.
- Tickets are auto-moved when GitHub PRs resolving them are opened and merged.
- You can reply to issue reporters in Slack/Discord directly from Janet AI when a ticket is resolved.
- Janet auto-tags issues, clusters related issues, suggests assignees and priorities for issues, and evaluates ticket quality.
- Your Cursor/Claude Code's context is synced with full knowledge of your Janet AI workspace, so coding agents can create, understand, update, prioritize, and resolve your issues.
- And more!

Founders, product leaders, customer success, and engineers all love Janet: cross-team communication, collaboration, and execution are made seamless.

Check out janet.ai to get started today!
