# The Problem

Videos are incredibly time-consuming and tedious to make. You need to be an expert in video editing, sourcing, storyboarding etc…  

But videos are the most engaging format to communicate information. A video can change someone’s mind in a way almost no other medium can. 

Today, AI video models, like Sora and Veo 3, are not practical for communicating real information. They are great at making cinematic videos but cannot explain a 10-step onboarding flow, walk through a Github repo, train your team, or teach multivariable calculus. If you need to actually _communicate_ something, no AI video tool would be useful for you.

# Our Solution:

We have built a proprietary video generation engine to generate explainer videos from your documents. 

Golpo is built specifically for use cases on explaining, learning, and reporting, and feels authentic and engaging in a way no other AI video generator does. 

After Golpo generates a video, you can fully customize its animations frame by frame. Also you can make a video anywhere form 15 seconds to 30 minutes
