# Founders

## Roman Rothaermel
**Founder**

Roman is a neuroscientist who has conducted several years of research on memory encoding in the brain, neuropharmacology and drug target characterization. During his PhD at Oxford University he uncovered a new mechanism causing brain-wide synchronization which is necessary for correct retrieval of complex memories. He also has an MSc in Neuroscience (Oxford, UK) and MSc in Biology (Tubingen, Germany).
