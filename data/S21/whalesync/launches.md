## Whalesync: Give your spreadsheet edit access to data in apps

Hi everyone! We’re Matthew and Curtis, founders of [Whalesync](https://www.whalesync.com).

**Whalesync gives your spreadsheet edit access to data in apps like HubSpot, Webflow, and Supabase.** This way, you can bulk edit, collaborate, or build AI workflows directly from Airtable, Notion, or Google Sheets. It's a little scary... but also super powerful ;)

### **Why spreadsheets?**

Spreadsheets like Google Sheets, Airtable, and Notion are super flexible, require practically no learning curve, and let you view and update records in bulk.

When you connect these tools with your business apps (like your CRM, CMS, or database), you basically get spreadsheet superpowers ✨.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=85696&key=user_uploads/689259/a269b120-351c-41f1-ae49-576fd317a397)

### **Common use cases**

Hundreds of companies, including Webflow, Descript, and Clay, use Whalesync to work faster by:

* **Enriching CRM leads with AI** from a spreadsheet (HubSpot + Notion)
* Creating **thousands of programmatic SEO pages** from a spreadsheet (Webflow + Airtable)
* **Creating internal tools** without any engineers (Supabase + Airtable)
* **Updating deal statuses** from a spreadsheet (Affinity + Google Sheets)
* Giving clients an **easy way to update projects** (Notion + Google Sheets)

### **How it works**

1. Connect your app and your spreadsheet.
2. Map the tables and fields you want to sync.
3. Turn it on, and your data syncs into your spreadsheet. Then you can start editing/creating.

### **The problem: connecting tools is hard**

Connecting apps like Airtable & Webflow enables amazing use cases, but is actually really hard to do without code!

We started Whalesync because we repeatedly saw people spending weeks architecting multi-step automations only to find them brittle and unreliable.

While we love automation tools, they’re really not built to handle true data syncing where you connect deeply and bi-directionally.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=68930&key=user_uploads/689259/f59ab897-31e7-4717-8c1d-5353077f4f28)

### **The solution: simple, no-code data sync**

Whalesync is the no-code tool built to handle data syncing. In a few minutes, you can hook up your apps and have them 2-way syncing in real-time.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=68930&key=user_uploads/689259/05a48b3c-e3a4-49cc-870c-9b8564835d98)

### **Use cases: pSEO, internal tools, + much more**

Whalesync works by connecting different tools, so there are a TON of different things you can do. A few of the more popular use cases:

* 🔎 Launch thousands of **programmatic SEO pages** (Airtable x Webflow)
* ⚒️ Build an **internal tool** on top of your production database (Postgres x Notion)
* 💻 Create a scalable **no-code app** (Bubble x Airtable)
* 🧑 Personalize hundreds of tier-1 **ABM landing pages** (Airtable x Webflow)
* 📝 Manage your **blog** (Notion x Webflow)

### **How it works**

1. Pick the tools you want to sync
2. Tell Whalesync how the data maps
