# Sully.ai

> Autonomous team of AI agents that runs the entire hospital operations

Sully.ai is printing labor for health systems by building a team of autonomous AI agents to run the entire hospital operations. The autonomous agents run like ClawdBot in the background (Receptionists, Medical Consultant, Triage Nurse, Scribes, Coders) collaborate to execute entire workflows—replacing the $800B labor doing all the manual work at hospitals.
