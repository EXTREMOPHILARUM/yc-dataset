# Lariat Data

> Observability for Data Engineering Teams

Lariat is a Continuous Data Quality monitoring platform to discover data bugs before your consumers do. Ensure data products don’t break even as business logic, input data and infrastructure change.

Use Lariat to define and then automatically extract, store and visualize data quality metrics on raw event-level data through to delivered data products.
