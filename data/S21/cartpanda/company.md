# Cartpanda

> Global online sales platform

As an ecommerce platform, we provide a complete ecosystem for creators and entrepreneurs to sell and promote their products and services, including tools for product management, affiliate management, payment processing and much more. Users can sell various types of products, including digital goods, services, events, and physical items.

Cartpanda help entrepreneurs scale their global sales by providing a high converting checkout page and payment solution, capable to convert to 180+ currencies and languages.
