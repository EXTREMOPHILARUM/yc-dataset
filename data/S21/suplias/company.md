# Suplias

> We enable FMCG distributors in Africa buy inventory on credit

Suplias helps FMCG manufacturers in Africa receive payments and finance inventory purchases of their distributors using a mobile app called Obtainly.

We launched in Nigeria, where we have been growing revenue by 32% month over month for the last 11 months

Our founding team has a combined 25+ years of experience in supply chain and ecommerce having worked for Procter & Gamble, Pepsi Co, Gap and Jumia, the #1 ecommerce company in Africa.
