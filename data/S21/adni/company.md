# Adni

> AI career platform for healthcare workers

Healthcare workers use Adni's AI career assistant, Allie, to autonomously find jobs, buy gear, connect with peers, manage credentials, and more. Leading organizations, like Penn Medicine, MedPro, and PRN Healthcare, use Adni’s clinician network and AI voice agents to source, screen, and hire healthcare talent faster and cheaper.

Backed by Y Combinator, Flare Capital, Liquid 2 Ventures, Fresco Capital, Augment Ventures, Crista Galli Ventures, and more.
