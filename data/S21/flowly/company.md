# Flowly

> Our mobile app reduces chronic pain for 100 million Americans. 

Flowly’s mobile app is the VR version of Calm with a focus on reducing chronic pain for 100 million Americans. Flowly subscribers receive a $5 VR headset and Heart Rate sensor that collects a user's biometric data like HR and HRV, and converts it to visualizations in virtual experiences that reduce pain by 46%. 
Flowly is backed by the National Institutes of Health.
