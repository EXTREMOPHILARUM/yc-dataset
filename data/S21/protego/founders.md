# Founders

## Pablo Rojas
**Founder**

Pablo Rojas, co-founder and CEO. Prior to founding Protego, Pablo led Rappi Bank's Onboarding and Know Your Customer (KYC) for Latam and helped structure the company's chargeback reconciliation process. He also co-founded a non-profit organization that improves the living conditions of vulnerable social sectors, victims of extreme poverty and armed conflict in Colombia.
