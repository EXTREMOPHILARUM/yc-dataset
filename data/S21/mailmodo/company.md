# Mailmodo

> No-code email marketing platform to send forms & widgets within emails

Mailmodo is an email marketing software that helps marketers create app-like experiences in email by adding forms, shopping cart, calendar, NPS and more widgets within the email. Our interactive emails reduce the number of steps for users, thereby increasing email conversions. You can use it to collect feedback from your users, book meetings with prospects, recover abandon carts, generate leads, and more all within emails.
