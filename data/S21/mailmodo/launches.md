## **We identified a problem.**

In 2025, you can build an entire MVP in 3 days with AI tools like Lovable and Bolt. But setting up basic email flows? That still takes 2 weeks.

I've watched this happen countless times. Founders ship their product, then get stuck navigating 15 screens, learning complex automation builders, and spending hours on segmentation logic. Meanwhile, every other business tool has gone conversational.

Email marketing, however, remained stuck in 2015:  painfully manual despite being the most important channel for early-stage growth.

## **So, we built a solution**

For years, Mailmodo has been propelling emails into the future. We made them interactive through our drag-and-drop editor, turning inboxes into actionable playgrounds. But we weren't solving the real bottleneck: managing multiple workflows, juggling between tools, and wearing too many hats.

So we rebuilt email marketing from scratch around one idea: **What if you could run your entire email strategy from a single conversation?**

You describe what you want: _"Create a 5-part onboarding series for SaaS trial users."_ Mailmodo AI handles strategy, copy, design, automation, segmentation, and analysis, all from one prompt, in one place.

What used to take days now happens in minutes.

## **How It Works**

A founder types: _"Create a re-engagement campaign for users who haven't opened emails in 30 days."_

Mailmodo AI instantly:

* Segments the audience based on your data
* Generates ready-to-send email designs
* Builds the multi-email automation flow with strategic timing
* Tracks performance and suggests improvements
