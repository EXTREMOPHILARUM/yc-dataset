# Titipku

> Super Hyperlocal Marketplace for SMEs in Indonesia

Local market is a traditional wet market that consist with 200 - 1000 merchants who sell groceries located in every residential area. We  digitize all these merchants into our apps and provide personal shoppers. So every household can buy groceries from multiple merchants at one local market in one single order. There are 16.000 local markets in Indonesia with 8.000.000 merchants inside.
