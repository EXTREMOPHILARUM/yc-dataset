# hotglue

> Native user-facing SaaS integrations for your customers

hotglue is a tool that helps developers embed user-facing native SaaS integrations into their product with minimal development work, all while allowing the developers to remain in control of the data. hotglue also features a configurable Python-powered transformation layer for you to control how your product receives your customers' data.
