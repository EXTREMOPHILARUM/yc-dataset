# Matidor.com

> Project management on a live map

Matidor.com is a project management software for projects where you need to see what's happening live on a map. Natural resources companies such as energy operators and consultants manage their environmental activities across thousands of locations. Instead of switching between spreadsheets, they need to visualize their projects on a map and zoom into each one of them for details such as site drawings, tasks, costs and communications.
