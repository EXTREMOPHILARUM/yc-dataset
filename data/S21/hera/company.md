# Hera

> Calendar app to prepare, join and follow-up on virtual meetings

Google Calendar is built on hypotheses that are now deprecated. Now that most of our meetings are virtual, there is a massive opportunity to remove frictions at each step of the lifecycle of these meetings, and finally make calendars actionable.

From Hera, users can:
- Schedule a meeting or share availabilities
- Prepare a meeting with the right context on attendees and past discussions
- Join a meeting in one-click
- Snooze a meeting to make sure you don't forget to follow-up

All of that without leaving the keyboard.
