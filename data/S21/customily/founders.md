# Founders

## Martina Möring
**Founder**

Martina Möring is the Co-Founder of Customily, a design tool that makes it easy for e-commerce stores to sell personalized products. Originally from Uruguay, she moved to St. Louis in 2020. Martina has a background in architecture and design, and she previously co-founded Sur3D, focusing on 3D printing technology for product personalization. She has a dynamic and adventurous spirit, loves exploring new places with her family.
