# Akudo

> India's first learning-focused neobank for teenagers

Akudo is a digital bank for teenagers in India that gets them their own sleek, personalized prepaid card that they can use independently, helps them save money and gives them exciting rewards under a gamified setup when they're good at their personal finance.  
We are crafting the concept of "Learning-first Banking" where financial learning is seamlessly intertwined with full scale banking.
