# Pluggy

> Plaid for Brazil: we allow developers to access users' financial data 

Pluggy is a B2B Fintech that allows any developer to connect to the data from their users' bank and broker accounts, essentially Plaid for Brazil. 

We are 100% focused in Brazil. One of the largest markets in the world. Its complexity, risks and challenges require its own solution.

Local focus and our deep experience in banking tech engineering allowed Pluggy to create a product that is considered by our customers as the only one to provide reliable data at the right time to market.

We currently cover 90% of the bank accounts in Brazil a market of more than 7 billion USD. 

We are Pluggy!
