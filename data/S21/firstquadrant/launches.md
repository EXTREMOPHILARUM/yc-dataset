## FirstQuadrant: Maximize B2B sales with human-centered AI

Hey YC Community, Carlo here. Today we’re making FirstQuadrant available for the public! This one’s been a long time in the making.

Anand and I started working on this over 2.5 years ago (before ChatGPT was even released). And to be honest, it took a lot of fucking around and finding out. We spoke to over 300+ founders and heads of sales, rebuilt the product from scratch multiple times, and only after countless conversations did it finally click.

So today, we’re excited (and a bit nervous) to finally release FirstQuadrant to the public: An AI sales execution platform that helps founders and revenue teams move faster, stay organized, and close more deals, by streamlining the behind-the-scenes sales-work so nothing ever slips through the cracks.

https://youtu.be/UG5RfdbKPfI

**The Problem:** 

If you’ve ever looked inside a CRM, you know how chaotic things can get. Deals are scattered, updates are missed, and there’s always this underlying anxiety: “Did I forget to follow up?” “Am I waiting on someone to respond?” “Did I send the right documents?”

Most sales reps are great at conversations and closing — but not at the repetitive, behind-the-scenes tasks before and after meetings. That’s where deals fall through the cracks.

**The Solution:**

FirstQuadrant solves these exact problems. Our platform analyzes all your sales conversations and uses advanced AI reasoning to understand where each deal stands and what should happen next.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=90182&key=user_uploads/569815/03bcb01a-7e6c-4c01-bb08-0a37d57a6a3f)

It automatically manages tasks like:

\- Following up with leads

\- Rescheduling missed meetings

\- Qualifying new inbound leads

\- Staying in touch with old or inactive contacts

\- Sending resources or summaries post-call

All of this is handled in the background, giving your sales team the mental space to focus on what really matters: selling, meeting with customers, and building relationships.

As a result, your deals move faster, your pipeline gets cleaner, and your reps aren’t constantly playing catch-up.

They don’t have to keep everything in their heads anymore. FirstQuadrant tracks it all and nudges them at just the right moments.

And beyond just managing active deals, it also helps uncover hidden revenue by reactivating older, inactive business relationships (the ones reps never quite get around to following up with). And on the top-of-funnel side, it qualifies new leads that come in, lets you build custom-qualified audiences, and even runs AI-personalized outbound email campaigns at scale. So you’re filling your pipeline with high-quality leads and keeping everything organized and moving forward — all in one place.
