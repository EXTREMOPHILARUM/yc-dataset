**❌ The Problem**

**Generalist artificial Intelligence fails most dramatically in finance-specific use cases**:

* Multi-document analysis
* Guidance identification
* Numeric accuracy and precision

As a result, AI solutions have **failed to deliver the consistency, reliability and usability required by institutional investors**.

Traditional financial service software providers, like Bloomberg or FactSet, provide coverage of standardized data e.g. net income. But less structured information e.g. soft guidance or product-level results, can often only be found manually. As a result, many of the most sophisticated investors in the world, have **painfully manual workflows**.

AI solves this problem but only when it meets the industry’s standards of consistency, reliability and relevance.
