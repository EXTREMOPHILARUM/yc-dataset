# Founders

## James Vuong
**Founder/CEO**

James is a serial tech entrepreneur, angel investor, and former VC.  He has broad experience ranging from engineering to building products, from investing to founding startups, and from Silicon Valley to SE Asia.  Prior to Infina, James was CEO of Lana group, a digital media startup acquired by Japan's LINE Corp.  Prior to that, James was a VC at IDG Ventures, Vietnam’s first VC fund with $100M USD.  James was a Kauffman Fellow selected by the Kauffman Foundation.
