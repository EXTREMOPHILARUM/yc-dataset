# AlgoUniversity

> Building Job driven Virtual Tech University for India 🇮🇳

We are building live teaching infrastructure that enables handful of top educators (from Google, Facebook, etc) to coach a large number of students.
We train college students in a year-long live cohorts and they pay us a portion of their salary as fees once they get a tech job (Income Sharing Agreement)
