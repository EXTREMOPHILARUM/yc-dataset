# Founders

## Cathy Han
**Founder**

Cathy Han is the Co-founder & CEO of Levro, a multicurrency neobank for businesses. Levro provides US accounts that hold up to 34 currencies to help companies pay teams, vendors, and receive revenue from customers internationally.

Prior to Levro, Cathy co-founded 42 Technologies (YC W14), a big data company with retail customers around the globe. Cathy holds a Finance degree from the University of British Columbia & MBA from Harvard Business School.
