# Adravision

> Automation for dental clinics and insurance companies.

We build automation solutions powered by AI to improve the efficiency of diagnostics, documentation, and patient education for dental clinics. Our technology is also used by dental insurance companies to process large volumes of claims by analyzing unstructured data like X-rays, narratives, and documents.

We're a dentist and clinic owner, a computer vision expert, and a bioscientist.
