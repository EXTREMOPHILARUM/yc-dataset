## Mustafa Al-Adhami
**Founder**

Mustafa Al-Adhami Ph.D., CEO, Astek Diagnostics. He has long experience in customer discovery and diagnostics. He envisioned the technology behind Eugris while completing his PhD. As a doctoral student, he won the national Three Minute Thesis (3mt) competition, Baylor Business Plan, elevator pitch competition, and VentureWell’s BMEidea competition. Mustafa has raised over $5M for Astek to date to revolutionize antibiotic prescription.
