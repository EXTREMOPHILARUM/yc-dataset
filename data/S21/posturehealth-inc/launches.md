## Zen AirPods - Track and Correct your Posture with your AirPods 🧘🏽‍♀️🎧

### **Problem:**

Posture impacts the way we think, look, feel, and act. Unfortunately, most of us spend the majority of our workdays hunched over a computer, leading to back and joint pain, headaches, and even long-term heart disease.

### **How Zen AirPods Can Help:**

![uploaded image](https://bookface.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=66567&key=user_uploads/819571/c1ef6098-6893-4b54-92ba-eb3ba129248b)

_Real-time Slouch Alerts -_ Zen uses the motion sensor in your AirPods (3rd generation, AirPods Pro, AirPods Max, Beats Fit Pro, and other headphones that can send motion data to your iPhone) to mirror your posture in real-time and gently alerts you (via phone vibration, audio alert, and/or a message notification), whenever you’re slouching for an extended period of time.

![uploaded image](https://bookface.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=66567&key=user_uploads/819571/7e971e8b-1da3-4fb1-a81c-26e3c1441ebf)

_Background Mode_ - Zen runs completely in the background. With Background Mode you can get slouch alerts even when your phone is locked or if you’re using another app.\
\
Zen can still send you gentle slouch alerts (via phone vibration, audio alerts, and/or a message notification). You can completely customize your Zen experience.
