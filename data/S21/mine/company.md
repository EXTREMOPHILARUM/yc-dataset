# Mine

> The AI personal finance company. 

Mine is the personal finance app designed to help you get ahead.

Founded on the idea that financial independence begins with ownership — of your money, your momentum, and your decisions.

We make the credit-builder thousands know and love, with budgeting and spend tracking features meant to help your money be the best version of itself. We’re also the makers of MoneyGPT, the money assistant that’s got your back.

Young people today are stepping into a financial world built for someone else’s life.

We give young adults the confidence and control to take charge of their financial journey with simple tools, smart guidance, and a system that works for the path you choose.

Let’s build your future together. Get started at usemine.com
