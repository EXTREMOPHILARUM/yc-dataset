# Affogato AI

> Affogato AI — Your Agent for instant TikToks, Reels, and ads.

Affogato AI is your all-in-one AI Video Agent, designed to help businesses, eCommerce sellers, agencies, and creators produce professional short-form videos instantly. From TikTok ads and Instagram Reels to YouTube Shorts, UGC content, and branded campaigns, Affogato makes high-quality video creation simple, fast, and scalable. No editing skills required — just type an idea and get campaign-ready videos that look studio-made, in minutes.
