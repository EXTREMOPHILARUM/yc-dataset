### The problem

AI image generation is a powerful storytelling tool for social media, marketing, education, and entertainment. Yet, a recurring challenge lies in _preserving continuity across a series of images._

Let's say you _generate 5 images featuring the same fictional character_ - each one could render them with a completely different face, ethnicity, clothing, and overall appearance.

This _lack of consistency_ makes it extremely difficult to use those AI-generated images together cohesively for narratives, marketing campaigns, or any project requiring a unified character identity throughout.

### The solution

[RenderNet](https://rendernet.ai) is a new AI image generation tool with a focus on consistency and control via its features - _Facelock_ and _ControlNet_.

**Facelock** enables a consistent face across all your creations. Whether you start with an AI-generated image or upload a photo, FaceLock maintains that face's integrity in every new image prompted. This ensures unparalleled continuity in your visual narratives.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=79116&key=user_uploads/218500/fe70e107-5813-4708-bcd7-0c63766b0ef1)

**ControlNet** lets you upload a reference image to guide the pose, composition, and style within your AI-generated visuals.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=79116&key=user_uploads/218500/68211b19-352c-4315-9a7b-ba5b286e1967)

Combining Facelock and ControlNet transforms your AI image generation process, offering unparalleled consistency and control. This dynamic duo ensures your characters remain true to your vision, from their facial features to their poses and styles.

Whether you're crafting a comic series, designing an ebook, or creating a [virtual influencer](https://twitter.com/CharaspowerAI/status/1754183001066410266) akin to [Aitana Lopez](https://www.euronews.com/next/2024/01/20/meet-the-first-spanish-ai-model-earning-up-to-10000-per-month), Rendernet becomes an indispensable tool in your creative arsenal.
