# Pillar

> A white-label care enablement platform

Pillar is designed to solve healthcare’s longitudinal engagement problem through a combination of our SOC 2-certified, HIPAA-compliant platform and health coach workforce. Our white-label infrastructure enables companies to guide patients from outreach and activation through to flexible microsites and automated digital pathways including content, services, vendors and forms. Together with our in-house network of 1,200+ health coaches, Pillar partners drive positive outcomes through scalable digital care programs focused on health education and care navigation to chronic condition management.
