# Pipekit

> Pipekit scales data & CI pipelines for enterprises

Our app manages Argo Workflows for data teams, enabling complex data &
 CI pipelines in half the time while saving companies hundreds of thousands of dollars annually. We maintain Argo Workflows, an open-source pipeline framework for Kubernetes that’s used in production by Bloomberg, Intuit, Adobe, New Relic, NVIDIA, and many other open-source early adopters.
