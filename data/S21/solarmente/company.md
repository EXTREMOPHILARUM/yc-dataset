# SolarMente

> Home electrification at $0 upfront cost

SolarMente helps homeowners in Southern Europe install solar, storage, and heat pumps with zero upfront cost, allowing them to pay through predictable monthly savings. 
Based in Barcelona, we’ve raised $5M from YC, Climate Capital, Soma Capital, Plug & Play, Leonardo DiCaprio, and others.
