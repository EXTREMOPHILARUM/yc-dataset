## Pippa: a Pelvic Floor Fitness Booster for Women

# **Meet Pippa**

# The world's first workout product designed to boost pelvic floor fitness—just by wearing it while you move.

[https://youtu.be/OP0vhTb6n8U?si=ZOPh69qU3PaznicV](https://youtu.be/OP0vhTb6n8U?si=ZOPh69qU3PaznicV%5C)

Pippa is worn internally (like a tampon). The soft silicone spring gently conforms to the body, activating pelvic floor muscles with each step, stretch, or squat. No batteries, no routines—just natural, effortless, on-the-go exercise.

**✅**  Up to 35% more lift during a contraction\
✅  Fits seamlessly into a daily workout or walk\
✅  Two models for a comfy, personalized fit

**The Problem**

78M American women worry about pelvic floor weakness, including college and pro athletes, new moms, and older women. Pelvic floor weakness can lead to a range of problems, including urinary and fecal incontinence, pelvic organ prolapse, and more, significantly impacting quality of life. Most solutions are high-effort, expensive, inconvenient — or all of the above.

# **The Solution**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=91753&key=user_uploads/583225/4f74514a-9282-4194-b496-f5a8f8bb9a9e)

Pippa is the world’s first _conforming resistance spring._ It comes in two sizes and levels of resistance: light (42mm) and medium (50mm).

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=91753&key=user_uploads/583225/b5b7eaf1-5356-4c53-a2f9-deb7d4cb441e)

It works like a resistance band, but for hard-to-reach inner core.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=91753&key=user_uploads/583225/0d8c4d39-8f5e-41f3-ad68-1bd2736df857)

Pippa passively responds to core muscle movement by expanding and compressing, thereby activating the muscles and increasing effective function by up to 35%.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=91753&key=user_uploads/583225/483ecf52-ea8b-4f74-9b7f-11f453d8460e)

The reusable applicator makes it easy to put Pippa into place. The large finger loop makes anytime removal a breeze.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=91753&key=user_uploads/583225/c47281f3-861d-4a89-baef-c1226c1e9092)

She conceived Pippa after trying different solutions for kegel exercise and bladder support, none of which met her comfort and convenience expectations. \
\
Cofounder and CEO, [Melody Roberts](https://www.linkedin.com/in/melodyannroberts/) (R), incubated service models and self-ordering solutions for McDonald’s and Panera. \
\
We worked with a wide range of experts–including urogynecologists, physiotherapists, and other health professionals–to ensure Pippa would be comfortable and fully safe.
