# Blixo

> Accounts receivable automation to help companies get paid faster 

Blixo is an all-in-one replacement for things like subscription management, invoicing, collections, and cash application software.

Blixo improves conversion rates, increases average order size, drives subscriber loyalty, collects unpaid bills, speeds up cashflow, and is the single source of truth for reconciled real time revenue data.
