# Founders

## Vrinda Gupta
**Founder**

Vrinda Gupta is the CEO & Co-Founder of Sequin, building AI voice agents for consumer lenders and servicers to automate collections efforts. 

Previously, she founded a women-focused neobank being rejected from the very credit card she helped launch (the Chase Sapphire Reserve) as a PM at Visa Inc. Vrinda holds an M.B.A from Berkeley Haas and a B.A. from UCLA. She is a proud first-generation Indian immigrant and currently lives in San Francisco, California.
