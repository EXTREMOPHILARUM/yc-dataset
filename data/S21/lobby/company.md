# Lobby

> Local onboarding for the $200B health tourism industry

Selling healthcare products online is hard. You need accurate health data to serve customers. Getting this data is extremely difficult. 

We are building a network of locations that companies can use to collect this health data and onboard their customers. 

Today our focus is the $270B health tourism industry. We help health tourism clinics onboard customers coming from the UK.  Our licensed guides help them collect dental scans and other important health data. 

They use this data to create personalised treatment plans.
