# Buzzle

> Automatic voice-of-customer trends & metrics from sales conversations.

Buzzle help product marketing teams identify competitive moats and weaknesses, marketing teams generate & understanding voice of customer and product teams gauge demand in feature requests based on customer pain points, all automatically from a company’s library of recorded sales and success calls. Buzzle is founded by alumni from the University of Michigan. Adithya operated as the NLP modeling lead at Capital One's Center for Machine Learning. Michael was the lead developer of Capital One's privacy portal, serving millions of customers. Bhairav dropped out of his PhD at MIT to found Buzzle, after publishing research on deep learning and robotics at NVIDIA Research, NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, and the Montreal Institute for Learning algorithms.
	
Buzzle was formerly named q&ai!
