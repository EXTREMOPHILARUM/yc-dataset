# Varos

> AI Business Analysts to gather requirements and optimize processes

Varos AI delivers the work of business analysts 10x faster, better & cheaper with AI agents built for discovery.

Arthur, our AI agent, acts as a Senior Business Analysts and works for you 24/7 in any language. He conducts intelligent 10-15 minutes voice or text-based interviews with your teams to rapidly capture the institutional knowledge you need to execute on projects. He learns from each conversation, cross-references information, and analyzes all the findings to deliver actionable insights. 

Arthur is an expert at capturing mapping & optimizing complex processes, gathering knowledge from employees' heads and documenting it, supporting automation projects, uncovering root causes to significant problems and more. 

All this information gathered is turned into a private, secure knowledge base that can be used by the org → turning tribal knowledge into institutional knowledge.

For example, Arthur unblocked a critical system migration for a large financial system in 3 days by interviewing 100+ operators in the org, replacing a $1m & 6 month engagement of a consulting firm.

Backed by Y Combinator, Mosaic General Partnership, and leading financial institutions ($8.5m in funding).
