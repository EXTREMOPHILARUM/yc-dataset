# Orderli

> Square for Europe

Orderli is tableside ordering for bars and pubs in Europe. Orderli works without downloading an app, guests can scan a QR code on the table, browser the menu and order what they want. 

For the staff we provide a simple app which gives them a better overview who is ordering and control of the orders that are sent to the kitchen.

In the backend we use predictions to recommend restaurant owners what to purchase and make changes to the menu in real-time.
