# Infiuss Health

> 2 sided Marketplace for accelerating medical research

We are building the world's first community of patients eager to contribute to and advance  medical research. Our patient community platform offers health management, patient support and patient health data aggregation. We offer researcher's and life science companies with the opportunity to connect with these patients for their medical research needs. They recruit real patients for clinical trials, for bio specimens sourcing and to obtaining real world patient data.
