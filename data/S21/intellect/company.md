# Intellect

> A modern-day mental healthcare company for global workforces.

Intellect is the global leader in hyperlocalised mental healthcare, serving 4 million lives across enterprise employers, insurers, healthcare systems, and governments in over 50 languages and 100 countries.

With a comprehensive, AI-powered care ecosystem, Intellect seamlessly integrates digital therapeutics, virtual therapy, high-acuity crisis support, and specialty clinical treatment - delivered through its proprietary digital platforms and directly owned mental health clinics.

Headquartered in Singapore with nine global offices, Intellect is backed by top investors including Tiger Global, Y Combinator, Insignia Ventures, and HOF Capital. It is trusted by hundreds of Fortune 500 companies and industry leading organisations.
