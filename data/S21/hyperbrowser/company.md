# Hyperbrowser

> Web infra for AI agents

Hyperbrowser.ai is the internet infrastructure for AI agents. We provide instant, scalable browser infra with built-in CAPTCHA solving, proxy management, and anti-bot detection. 

Developers use us to power their AI agents for data collection, testing, and web automation at scale. We're backed by YC, Accel, SV Angel, and others.
