# SnapCalorie

> Single photo nutrition tracking.

SnapCalorie is the world’s first app where you can take a photo of ANY meal and get an accurate calorie count.  It takes seconds to use and is more accurate than a trained nutritionist. The team previously co-founded Google Lens, Cloud Vision API, and published a CVPR paper demonstrating the first algorithm to outperform a professional nutritionist at calorie counting.
