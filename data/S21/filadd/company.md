# Filadd

> We help students in Latin America graduate from College.

We provide premium, personalized courses for college entrance exams (like Chile’s PAES and Brazil’s ENEM) combining engaging video lessons, live tutoring, AI-powered study plans, and downloadable resources.
Today we serve over 25,000 students annually across Chile, Brazil, Argentina, and Colombia, with a 90% YoY growth and industry-leading margins.
