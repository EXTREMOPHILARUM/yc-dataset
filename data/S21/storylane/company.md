# Storylane

> Build Interactive Product Demo in 10 mins for SaaS companies

Storylane enables marketing and sales teams to create and share interactive product demos in 10 min. Prospective buyers can self-experience the demos without talking to sales. Our no-code platform allows teams (without any engineering help) to capture, personalize, add guides and publish a demo. These Demos can be embedded on websites, email campaigns or sales followups.
