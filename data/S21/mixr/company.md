# Mixr

> Team building through online game tournaments. 

Mixr is a social platform for remote and hybrid teams; we host virtual game tournaments and an ongoing office competition.  Mixr tournaments have built-in audio/video chat, a library of fun games, ice breakers, and automatic breakout rooms - all built with the goal of being easy for managers and employees to use.  By participating in tournaments, players earn high scores and trophies which places them on the company-wide leaderboards.  Our goal is to bring companies together, boost employee engagement and help companies bolster a collaborative corporate culture.
