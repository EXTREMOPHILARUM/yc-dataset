# Founders

## Rachita Navara
**Founder**

Dr. Navara is an EP cardiologist and bioengineer internationally recognized for her contributions to heart rhythm research and medical innovation. While caring for patients in the hospital, she identified the need to expand access to patients starting heart drugs. To solve this problem, she assembled a multi-disciplinary team including heart rhythm physicians, data scientists, AI engineers, clinical trialists, and regulatory experts. Her passion is to use engineering to transform heart health.
