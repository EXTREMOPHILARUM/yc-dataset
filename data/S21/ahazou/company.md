# Ahazou

> Podium for local businesses in Latam

Ahazou is a SaaS app that helps offline local businesses to connect online with their customers to promote their brand, to build their reputation and to sell more. We've reached USD100k MRR with 16k+ subscribers. Our vision is to build the next all-in-one platform for 50 million local businesses to grow online in Latam. The company is founded by Rui Miadaira - 10+ years building local businesses, 3x entrepreneur, tech background with a Harvard MBA - and has built a team from iFood, Movile, PicPay. Ahazou is backed by GFC, Monashees, Y Combinator (S21) and more.
