# Fingo Africa

> A neobank for Africa's 500 million youth

Fingo is building a pan-African digital bank.

Today, most Africans pay 5-15% fees to transfer money to friends, family, or businesses - with Fingo these fees will be 90% cheaper. 

In 240 seconds, any young person will be able to access a full suite of financial services including personal, business, investment and savings accounts.
