# **The Problem**

 Email is the glue that holds our lives together. Calendar invites, receipts, notifications, newsletters, (and more) all end up in our inbox. But, it's like a digital junk drawer that other people put things in and then we have to sift through them every day.\
\
And it never ends! Even if we get to the bottom today, it just fills back up again tomorrow.

# **What is Forage Mail?**

Forage is not a new email app you need to learn. It’s an AI that plugs right into your Gmail account and filters out your low-priority mail. Then, it sends you a clean daily summary with everything it filtered out—including a TLDR of every newsletter you received.\
\
Forage AI auto-labels emails, learns from your preferences, and gives you full control with custom rules. You can continue using any email app you prefer while Forage works in the background to streamline your inbox.

\
**Works with every mail app**\
Forage doesn’t replace your mail app. It connects to your Gmail account and runs in the background. So you can continue checking email as usual.

**A beautiful daily summary**\
Forage sends you a daily summary email with everything it filtered out of your inbox. It groups emails by type and summarizes your newsletters—this makes it easy to skim, scan, and move on.

\
**A TLDR for every newsletter**

Ever find it hard to keep up with your favorite newsletters. Now, you can get the key points in your daily summary email and decide which articles you want to read in-full. Forage even gives you an estimated read time for each article.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=88022&key=user_uploads/77398/2321939a-c461-4a89-8993-b51a305de075)

\
**Protects your inbox from unwanted senders**

Forage makes it easy to unsubscribe from any sender with one click. You can also mute entire categories of email so they never show up in your inbox. Sometimes, senders don’t respect your unsubscribe request, but Forage is able to hide them anyway.

\
**You’re always in control**

Forage AI makes intelligent decisions so it works well out-of-the-box. It learns from your preferences so it gets smarter the longer you use it. You can also create custom rules so you can decide exactly how your email is handled.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=88022&key=user_uploads/77398/418dd868-14e5-4f10-ae8d-b1f39fc27ef1)

# **How to get started**

Forage takes 30-seconds to set up. All you need to do is connect your Gmail account and Forage will create your first summary with the last 24 hours of email in your inbox. Then it will continue monitoring for new emails going forward.\
