# Locale

> Organic and gourmet high-protein meals, designed for longevity.

We create organic high-protein meals with high standards, backed by leading longevity research.

Our standards:
- 40-60g protein per meal
- Nutrient-dense meat (grass-fed, pasture-raised, wild-caught)
- No seed oils (cooked in extra virgin olive oil)
- 8g+ fiber per meal
- Organic, pesticide-free produce
- No processed ingredients. No refined sugars
- Microplastic-free, reusable glass jars
- High in Anti-inflammatories, and Omega-3s

Since starting in 2020, we’ve delivered 100K+ orders and are backed by a16z, Y-Combinator, Goodwater, Decent Capital, and other top investors.
