# Dot

> Chat with your Data Warehouse

Our mission is to help everybody in an organization to take data-driven decisions, by democratizing access to trusted high-quality data and metrics. 

**Chat with your Data Warehouse**

Answer most business questions instantly 24/7. Data teams can focus on deep work, not on answering easy questions about dashboards.

Dot understands your existing data stack: tables, SQL queries, documentation, DBT metrics or LookML are its learning material.

Users can interact via Slack, Teams or our native web app.
