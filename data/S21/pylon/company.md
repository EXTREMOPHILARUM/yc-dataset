# Pylon

> Infrastructure management software for water & electricity companies

Pylon provides water and electricity distribution companies, in emerging markets, with a platform for reducing their losses as well as opening new revenue streams. These losses are coming from leakages, consumption thefts, and uncollected bills, amongst others.
We have signed 11 utilities onto our platform across 2 countries and connected our systems to over 560k endpoints.
Today our average client has reduced their aggregate revenue losses by 45%
Expanding in Africa, LATAM, and SEA.
