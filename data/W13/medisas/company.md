# Medisas

> Medisas builds workflow & collaboration software for hospitals.

Healthcare is broken and underserved by technology.

Hospital software is too complicated, too convoluted, and too disconnected. Doctors and nurses spend more time clicking through prompts than checking in on patients, and the quality of care suffers as a result. We're going to change that, and have been able to do so for the care teams of half a million patients and counting.
