# Swapbox

> Stop missing deliveries. Ship to Swapbox and pick up at our kiosks.

Send and receive packages with Swapbox

# Receive packages with Swapbox #

Prevent missed deliveries with Swapbox. We give you control over your packages — no more missed delivery notices or stolen packages. Shop online and ship packages to a Swapbox. We'll notify you via email and/or text message with the pickup details when they arrive.

Learn more: swapbox.com/faq 
Receiving deliveries video: https://vimeo.com/76379214

# Send packages with Swapbox #

Take anything that you want to ship to your local Swapbox, no planning ahead necessary. Enter a few details about where your item is being shipped and how fast you want it there. Place your item in the Swapbox (It doesn't even need to be packaged!), and Swapbox will take care of the rest. Swapbox ships via UPS, USPS or FedEx. Swapbox is the fastest, most convenient way to ship anything.

Learn more: swapbox.com/shipping 
Shipping video: https://vimeo.com/97379917
