# RoverRide

> A universal urban transit app. Given all transportation options…

Real-time transit directions in San Francisco. Rover uses real-time public transit data to give you the best route, right now, to your destination. Always get the best route to your destination. Rover tracks all MUNI, BART, and Caltrain lines and gives you directions that make sense right now, so you don't have to wait or worry about missing a connection.

Currently supporting SF MUNI, BART, and Caltrain in the SF Bay Area.
