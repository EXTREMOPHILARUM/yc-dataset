# Meldium

> Simple password & identity management for teams. Acquired by LogMeIn…

The easiest way for companies to connect their empoyees to the apps they need.

Meldium is a YC-backed startup helping teams & companies manage accounts & passwords for cloud apps. From shared passwords to user provisioning, Meldium is a central dashboard that connects to every cloud app and makes it simple to create, edit and remove user accounts across all of them. All without IT.
