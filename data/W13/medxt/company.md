# MedXT

> Radiology in the cloud.  X-rays, CAT scans, MRI's, and…

MedXT is the easiest way to manage medical images online.  We make it easy for medical professionals to access and share their medical data safely and securely.  We provide specialty tools such as RIS, PACS, and HL7 connectivity. The MedXT platform will power your imaging center, hospital radiology department, or radiology group.
