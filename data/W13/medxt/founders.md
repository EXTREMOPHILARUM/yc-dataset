## Reshma Khilnani
**Founder**

Reshma is CEO of Medplum. In the recent past she was a Visiting Group Partner at YC. Reshma Khilnani was co-founded of MedXT (YC W13 acquired by Box) and Droplet (now Kit.com). Early in her career she held roles at Facebook and Microsoft. Reshma has a BS and MEng from MIT in EECS. She lives in San Francisco and owns reshma.com
