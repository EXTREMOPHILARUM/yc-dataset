# BeatDeck

> Music, Metrics... Meritocracy. 
(Upload Music, Connect your Social…

Beatdeck is a big data music analytics platform. It is used to evaluate artist's KPM, analyze social signals, and provide a clean extensive dashboard into the music industry. Beatdeck was founded in January 2013 at Y Combinator in Mountain View, CA by Josh Mangel and Avi Zolty.
