## Cedar 🌲 In-product AI copilot & PMF analytics for any app

Hi everyone! It’s Jesse & Isabelle from Cedar :)

### How do we productise conversion optimisation?

In business terms, we do two things:

1. We let you understand your user’s stories at scale. What do they want from your solution? What are they specifically trying to do on your software? Who are they?
   1. We do this by pulling your existing analytics and adding a thin layer of our own. You can re-target churned users when a relevant feature comes out, inform product roadmaps, and most importantly use it to find PMF.

      ![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=87967&key=user_uploads/561766/7f7d6dc9-b759-454c-a897-e94b5dd76197)

2. We use those analytics to increase conversion & retention, from pre-sale to activation/onboarding and beyond.
   1. Create individualised sales materials and activation flows to bring more users to their magic moment.
   2. Instead of not knowing how to do X and being blocked until customer support replies (or worse, churning), Cedar instantly responds.

      ![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=87967&key=user_uploads/561766/aa9b2e93-ed62-469a-9f49-c1e4a1de8596)


Think of a general-use product like Notion. Imagine if every user saw a landing page that explains **exactly** how Notion helps them reach their goals. When they sign up, an activation flow walks them through solving a relevant problem they face every day. Then, as they use the app, an AI copilot makes them a power user, without the need to leave the app.

### Our story

In more personal terms, we find it deeply wrong that people make awesome, valuable software that doesn’t reach people simply because of things like UI/UX or messaging. We turn every user into a power user - with the retention and promoter scores that come with it.

While AI is scaling engineering teams, we’re going to be the growth and customer success team in one product, with one centralized source of user knowledge.

Jesse worked at Intuit, learning the science and fun of making boring software (don’t tell TurboTax) that converts and serves customers of all tech literacy levels.

Isabelle worked at Figma and Notion, learning the struggles of getting users to effectively use powerful yet complex products and the GTM strategies to reach them.
