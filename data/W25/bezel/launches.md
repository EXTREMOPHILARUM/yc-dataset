# **The Problem**

Apparel brands invest over $100k annually on photo and video shoots to produce marketing content. Yet, even after this significant spend, marketing teams often lack a sufficient variety of high-quality imagery and video to develop fresh, high-performing ads and creatives at scale.

Due to a constant need to remix visuals, teams quickly exhaust their original shoot assets, creating a bottleneck that limits growth and stifles ad performance.

This gap between content needs and available assets slows experimentation, increases acquisition costs.

# **Our Solution**

Bezel makes it easy for e-commerce teams (small and large) to create realistic, studio-quality images and videos of their apparel on AI-generated human avatars.

# **Key Features**

* **Preset Human Models:** Choose from a wide variety of realistic male and female AI models in multiple ethnicities.
* **Instant Studio Shoots:** Generate studio-quality, static images of any apparel in seconds—no photo shoots needed.
* **Effortless AI Video Shoots:** Instantly create photorealistic videos of your apparel on human avatars, perfect for ads and social channels.
* **No AI Prompting Required:** Just upload your clothing image and Bezel handles the rest, with no technical skills needed.
* **Flexible Formats:** Download assets in both vertical and horizontal aspect ratios for websites, ads, and social use.
* **Self-Serve Platform:** Designed for fast, easy content creation meaning no waiting for bulk processing or outside support.
