# Cuckoo Labs

> Real-time AI translator for global sales and marketing

Cuckoo is a real-time AI translator for global sales, marketing, and support. Companies like Snowflake and PagerDuty use Cuckoo in Zoom and in-person meetings for even the most technical discussions.

Cuckoo instantly learns the technical details of your conversation from your keywords and documents and interprets into over 20 languages—customized for your specific industry and organization.
