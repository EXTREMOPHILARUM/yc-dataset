## Swerve - Snap AI Videos with Friends

Hey! We're Jason and Soroush, co-founders of Swerve. Swerve lets you snap AI videos with friends:\
https://www.youtube.com/shorts/qJuJOQm9SWM

📷 Capture a quick video and choose what you want to see next. Swerve makes it happen! You can also follow friends and discover trending videos from our community. Our users have gotten super creative with Swerve, and we’d love to see what you’ll create.

👇 Check out our iOS app here: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/swerve/id6746973177

🎉 If you like the app, we’d love it if you:

* invite your friends 
* share your favorite clips on X / Instagram / TikTok
* drop us a rating on the App Store
