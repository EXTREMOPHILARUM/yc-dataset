# Cenote

> AI agents that convert and retain patients for digital clinics

Cenote builds AI sales reps for online health clinics and deploys them for abandoned-cart and win-back campaigns.

Today, digital and telehealth clinics rely on generic text blasts or human follow-up for speed-to-lead and customer reactivation. Since the lead has often gone cold and the outreach isn’t personalized, conversion is frequently below 1%.

Cenote’s AI agents reach out immediately, communicate on the patient’s preferred schedule, and operate across channels—dramatically increasing conversion rates and unlocking revenue that otherwise goes unrealized.
