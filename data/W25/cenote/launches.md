# Launches

## CENOTE - Sales Agents For Digital Health Clinics

**TL;DR**

Telehealth clinics spend hundreds of thousands on ads but convert only a fraction of the patients who visit their site.

Buying GLP-1s, TRT, or other treatments is a high-stakes decision, yet most patients are left alone with a website.

Cenote provides AI sales agents that instantly reach out, answer questions, and guide patients through the purchase, helping clinics grow revenue and patients buy with confidence.

**Problem**

Telehealth customer acquisition is extremely expensive.

Clinics often pay hundreds, sometimes thousands, of dollars per lead, yet most visitors never convert.

After spending heavily to acquire demand, clinics rely on two imperfect tools:

* marketing automations with low engagement
* human reps who can’t scale coverage or response time

The result is lost revenue for clinics and confusing shopping experiences for patients making complex medical decisions.

**Insight**

High-consideration healthcare purchases shouldn’t be self-serve websites, they should be guided experiences.

Patients don’t abandon because they aren’t interested, they abandon because they have questions, uncertainty, or timing issues.

In the offline world, these purchases would involve a knowledgeable guide.\
Online, that layer has been missing.

We believe a new default is emerging: instead of navigating complex decisions alone, every buyer will have an intelligent concierge helping them evaluate, decide, and act.

**Solution**

Cenote gives every lead a personalized, persistent sales experience.

Our agents:

* reach out instantly via call and text
* answer questions and qualify needs
* follow up over days or weeks as patients decide
* support use cases like abandoned carts, failed payments, and win-backs

Every lead gets the experience of a dedicated care guide.

**The Impact**

Clinics using Cenote see:

* significantly higher conversion on existing traffic - for one clinic our agent converted at 2x the rate of a human rep
* better patient experiences

Instead of losing demand, they capture it.

**Why now**

AI can now hold natural, helpful conversations at scale.

For the first time, it’s possible to give every patient a guided buying experience, something previously only available to the highest-touch businesses.

**The ask**

We’re looking to speak with:

• Telehealth operators spending heavily on acquisition\
• Patients who recently considered an online health purchase

If that’s you (or someone you know), we’d love to talk.

We’d love your help!

## Cenote - Back office automation for specialist medical clinics

### **tl;dr**

We work with specialist clinics to identify their biggest administrative blockers and deploy custom **AI** to resolve them. Right now, we’re automating **referral intake**, reducing the time it takes for clinics to process new patients and accelerate revenue collection.

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u403AnLMSNE>

### **Problem**

Specialist clinics struggle with **slow, manual referral processing**, leading to revenue delays, administrative overload, and patient drop offs. The biggest bottlenecks we've uncovered:

* **Referral processing is manual and slow.** Clinics receive **thousands of referrals per month**, requiring extensive manual effort to extract and enter data.
* **Medical necessity verification is tedious.** Clinics must determine if a referral meets insurance criteria, often requiring **days of back-and-forth with primary care providers** for missing information.
* **Insurance approvals are a major roadblock.** Clinics waste **days negotiating with insurers** to confirm reimbursement eligibility before scheduling treatment.

These inefficiencies cause **40-60% of referrals to drop off and** costs the U.S. healthcare system **over $150 billion annually**. The administrative burden is also immense, with an average of **4.3 administrative staff per doctor** in the American healthcare system.

### **Solution**

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXduwoVo9LiyuYd4ldqDHoJxHu2uaKzKbOzQnI4MZ0vlWDvKTY-nE2iokzaFTCR7-7PeqhwdI9FIqtRbpP1FkZ3ivWq6TdtuyftWnKoMCyzw-smhtrM7tZacSCTnDkJ45wF92PRK7w?key=9LANEIwzbMw6NQWGiMH6ii1e)

Cenote eliminates the **referral-to-treatment bottleneck**, allowing clinics to focus on patient care while accelerating revenue collection.

* **Automated data extraction:** We categorize and extract key referral details the moment they hit the clinic’s inbox.
* **Seamless medical necessity verification:** If critical referral data is missing, our system **auto-requests** the necessary information via the preferred form of digital communication.
* **AI-powered insurance authorization:** Our **AI callers** handle insurance approvals, freeing clinic staff to focus on higher-value tasks.
