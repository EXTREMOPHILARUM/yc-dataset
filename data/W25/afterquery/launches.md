## **TL;DR**

AI models need high-quality training data to effectively automate complex professional tasks - we provide that data. If you are building an AI model or need fine-tuning data for your agent, we’d love to help. Reach out to us [here](https://calendly.com/spencer-afterquery/30min).

## **Why We’re Building AfterQuery**

There is a dearth of high-quality training data.

Current AI models are trained to be smart but are not trained on the work of actual professionals in many fields. 

For example, current models are largely unusable to professionals working in finance (private equity, hedge funds, investment banks, etc). See our [paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2501.18062), where LLMs fail 60% of realistic daily tasks – but with some fine-tuning on high-quality training data, significantly improve.

And the lack of high-quality training data is everywhere:

* Domain-specific AI (legal, consulting, healthcare, etc.)
* Multimodal data (audio, multilingual, etc.)
* Foundational models looking to push the boundary on reasoning and grading

## **Solution**

We are building a platform to onboard experts to work on custom datasets. Think working professionals at top firms and the smartest students in various subjects (PhDs). Then, whenever you have a request for a certain type of data, we will construct and lead a team to produce:

* Post-training / fine-tuning data
* Human-Led Reinforcement Learning (RLHF) data
* Custom benchmarks

You will know the people making your dataset and their qualifications while we coordinate with you to ensure quality standards. We save you time and energy, so you can focus on the model and not the data.

## **Example Datasets We Provide**

_Foundational Model Advancing Coding Capabilities:_

* New data structures and algorithms problems with test cases and step-by-step answer reasoning

_Enterprise SaaS Company Building Internal AI Developer Tool:_

* Codebase-wide code snippets with corresponding refactoring suggestions and explanations

_Startup Building an AI Agent for Law:_

* Answered legal questions with expert reasoning

**If AI is one day to replace jobs entirely, it can’t just be good, it needs to be near perfect. This transformation will require an immense amount of high-quality training data.**
