### **❌ The problem**

* Dental practices **waste 800 million hours** and **$13.2 billion** per year on time-consuming and repetitive back-office tasks. On top of that, dental practices are facing an unprecedented labor shortage and **90**% of them find it extremely or very challenging to fill vacant positions.
* The largest, most time-consuming, and error-prone part of a dental practice’s back office is revenue cycle management (RCM)—the process of getting paid. **80%** of dental practices face financial concerns due to RCM challenges. Tasks like insurance verification, claim submission, and payment posting are labor-intensive and prone to errors. Frequent policy changes make it difficult to verify coverage, leading to billing discrepancies and delayed payments.

### **✅ The solution**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=87923&key=user_uploads/2386492/97bc335c-1d5b-4c4a-ab85-e45f800ca7b2)

* Toothy’s AI agents automate the entire RCM process, **saving practices 160+ hours per month and increasing revenue by 5-10%**. Our AI agents handle everything from insurance verification to payment posting, eliminating the largest source of administrative burden.
* How do our agents work?
  1. **☎️ Verify Insurance** – Calls payers and check online portals to confirm coverage.
  2. 📝 **Input data** - Fills out and uploads a comprehensive breakdown form with 50+ codes into your practice management system.
  3. 📬 **Create and submit claims** - Accesses imaging software, clinical remarks, and plan details to create & submit comprehensive claims.
  4. 🛠️ **Correct denials** - Understands denial reasons, updates the claim, and resubmits to payers. Can even draft appeal letters.
  5. **💰 Payment posting** - Collects, scans, and deposits insurance checks into your bank account.
* Toothy becomes your full-time RCM team, reducing staff workload and ensuring faster, higher reimbursements.
