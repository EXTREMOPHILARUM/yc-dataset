# Bystreet

> Find your next hospitality deal.

Bystreet is a hospitality deal sourcing platform for development teams going after the Caribbean and Latin American hotel market. It offers a database of properties and hotels, allowing users to filter by country, asset type, and ownership details. The platform also provides contact information for owners and developers, streamlining the deal-sourcing process.
