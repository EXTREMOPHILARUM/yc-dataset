**What is Quote-to-Cash (QTC)?** Quote to Cash is the full business process from receiving customer orders through fulfillment, invoicing, payment collection, and reconciliation.

**Why this matters:** Slow or inefficient QTC processes directly delay cash flow, add operational overhead, and limit business growth.

**The Problem:** Companies dealing with high order volumes typically struggle with:

* **Incomplete Orders:** Missing details cause delays.
* **Mismatched Data:** Manual errors create reconciliation headaches.
* **Delayed Payments:** Inefficient processes slow down cash collections.

These issues often extend QTC from days to weeks, impacting revenue and productivity.

**Our Solution:** Agentin AI’s Agents connect with existing enterprise CRM and ERP systems to:

* **Automate Data Entry:** Remove manual input, reducing errors.
* **Ensure Data Consistency:** Detect and fix data mismatches automatically.
* **Accelerate Payments:** Speed up invoicing and reconciliation, cutting QTC time from weeks to minutes.
