## Brogan McPartland
**Founder**

Brogan is the co-founder and CEO of Vocality Health. Previously, he co-founded Fulltrack AI, a viral computer vision sports app, with Vivek. He was the CTO overseeing the app reaching 4 million downloads and becoming the #1 sports app in India, surpassing the Olympics app (during the Olympics). He scaled Fulltrack to mid-six-figure MAUs and holds three patents. He studied computer science at Harvard and speaks fluent Hindi.

**Founder/CTO**

Vivek is the co-founder and CTO of Vocality Health. He is a Harvard CS graduate and PhD in generative models. He previously led the implementation of computer vision algorithms at Second Spectrum (acq for 200M) and then founded a computer vision sports app that reached 4M users.
