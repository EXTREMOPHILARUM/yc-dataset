# Launches

## Olive 🫒 Build internal tools with natural language

**Olive connects to your database and 3rd-party services and builds internal tools for you in minutes.** Just prompt it with what you need.

[Introducing Olive:](https://youtu.be/UVM4ty9nUDI)

# **Problem**

Internal tools are incredibly valuable for companies and startups of every size—they give you valuable insights on what’s going on inside your product and speed up complex workflows. Unfortunately, building them is still a pain.

Companies either:

1. Take time to build these from scratch
2. Use existing providers and sometimes spend hours building a dashboard (it should take minutes).

Often times, they **know** these tools are needed, but it feels wrong to move engineering time away from what matters most: the actual product. Thus, the solution is to either put it in the backlog, or dedicate time building and forever maintaining them.

# **🫒 Our Solution**

With Olive, building these internal tools will take minutes.

You can connect your data sources during onboarding, and select which one Olive should read and generate around for each tool. **Olive will examine your database schema, write appropriate queries, and generate + deploy a full-stack internal tool.**

We’ve been hard at work to ensure the results are consistently high-quality, so that we can use this product ourselves. We’re excited to accept users off our waitlist in the coming weeks, as we support more database integrations and features.
