## G LNK - Simplifying collaboration between Pharma & Healthcare professionals

**TL;DR**

G LNK is an AI-powered platform that connects Pharma companies with doctors & researchers. Rayan worked on this as a consultant, where Pharma companies spent billions annually just to determine how much to pay doctors while staying within regulatory guidelines. Using LLMs, we’re automating the end-to-end process—from identifying candidates to compensation and compliance.\
<https://youtu.be/16oP6roNnZk>

**Problem**

Pharma companies spend over **$70 billion annually** on engaging doctors & researchers, yet a lot of that money is wasted on outdated and manual processes:

* **Outsourcing is expensive and slow**: Companies spend millions on third parties (consulting, recruiting, and regulatory firms)  just to find the right experts and to know how much to pay them compliantly.
* **Compliance risks are high**: Payment miscalculations can lead to regulatory violations and hefty fines
* **Data is scattered**: Contracts, payments and compliance tracking are spread across disconnected systems, making transparency and oversight difficult

<https://youtu.be/qDdFbMWO6eU>

**Solution**

**G LNK automates Pharma companies' entire healthcare professional (HCP) engagement process**—from identifying suitable doctors to engaging them. Here’s how it works:

1. **Identify HCPs:**
   * Identify a doctor from our database of over 3.2 million user profiles (growing 250% MoM).
   * Alternatively, if you know who you want to engage, simply upload the doctor’s CV and we will take care of the rest.
2. **Calculate Compensation:**
   * Instantly determine their compensation rates.
   * Adjust the rates dynamically based on factors such as service type and travel requirements.
3. **Engage Directly:**
   * Contact the selected doctors directly through our platform.
4. **Ensure Compliance:**
   * Maintain regulatory compliance and prevent compliance violations before they happen.
   * Leverage real-time data from trusted sources like NIH, PubMed, CBS, CMS, and more.

This step-by-step solution replaces multiple vendors with a single AI-powered platform, ensuring efficient, compliant, and cost-effective HCP engagement.

**Additional Features**

* **Built-in CRM** to track all HCP engagements
* **Comprehensive financial tracking**, including:
  * All Transfer of Value payments made to HCPs (date, purpose, associated drug)
  * Drug coverage breakdown
  * Monthly payment summaries
  * Payment analysis by Therapeutic Area
* **Tailored recommendations** for grants, conferences, and publications for HCPs
* **Centralized database** of regulations and Codes of Conduct across 150+ countries
