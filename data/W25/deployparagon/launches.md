## Sift: The Anti-Logging Platform

**Sift** transforms your logs from overwhelming chaos to clear, powerful insights. Our platform profiles, analyzes, and routes your logging data, intelligently eliminating noise and highlighting critical data. Continuously learning and adapting, Sift evolves with your data to keep you ahead of every challenge.

## **How We Got Here**

While seeing band aid logging products launched at both Datadog and Splunk, we witnessed firsthand how traditional logging tools overwhelm developers with data—burying critical insights under mountains of noise. Teams waste countless hours sifting through logs, missing the signals that matter most. Meanwhile, costs escalate as volumes grow, and early decisions limit vendor flexibility. We realized the problem wasn't just about collecting logs but in really understanding them.

Frustrated by these limitations, our mission for Sift became clear: create an anti-logging platform that gives developers control over their data while unlocking its true potential.

## **Sift through your data, intelligently**

<https://youtu.be/uQ-TTdiu3fc>

Sift is designed to flip the script on traditional logging. We’re not here to simply collect logs; we’re here to transform them into actionable intelligence that empowers developers and drives smarter decision-making.

1. **Profile, analyze, and continuously optimize** your logging data to find meaningful patterns without digging through mountains of information.
2. **Send your logs** exactly where they need to go, integrating right into your existing tooling
3. **Format and manage** your log volume precisely to focus on the information that matters most.

## **How Sift Works**

**Quick Setup:** Launch Sift with minimal configuration—start sending data in minutes.

**Seamless Integration:** Connect effortlessly with your existing logging infrastructure, with support for over 40 different integrations
