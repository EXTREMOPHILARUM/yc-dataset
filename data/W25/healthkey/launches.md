### **The Problem**

Pharma/Biotech/Medical Device companies (a.k.a. Sponsors) rely on doctors’ offices (a.k.a. Research Sites) to find patients for clinical trials. **Each enrolled patient can bring a site anywhere from $20,000 to $100,000**. The problem is finding qualified patients. **Doctors have to manually search through dense medical records** looking for extremely specific criteria, for [**example**](https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT05683691#participation-criteria): “Prostate cancer in T2c stage, 20-80 cc in size determined via MRI.” **Existing search tools rely on simple data fields**, so they can’t handle these nuanced requirements. As a result, doctors manually sift through lists of 1000 patients with Prostate cancer only to discover \~5 patients match all eligibility criteria. The result is **wasted time, missed revenue, and fewer patients** getting access to potentially life-changing trials.

### **The Solution**

Our solution works in two steps:

**1. AI-Driven Prescreening**

We connect with clinics’ EHR system\* directly to pull all medical records, including faxed PDFs. Then we use AI to compare each patient’s medical history against a trial’s specific eligibility criteria. For example: prostate cancer’s status, stage, size, and impression source. We repeat this for every criterion, across all trials, creating a carefully vetted list of potential candidates ranked by how likely they are to qualify.

**2. Doctor Review**

Doctors log in to our dashboard to view the prescreened patients. For each candidate, HealthKey highlights the key pieces of evidence—such as MRI details or encounter notes—so doctors can verify if a patient qualifies. This feedback loop improves our system, making it fast and easy to identify, qualify and enroll patients into clinical trials.

Doctors can onboard in under a week with our existing integrations\*, or we’ll integrate with any certified EHR for free. Clinics only pay when they successfully identify qualified patients!

\* Current Electronic Health Record (EHR) integrations include Athena, Veradigm Allscripts, Epic, NextGen, and MedConnect.

### **Case Study: $1.4M in Missed Revenue at Urology Clinic**

One of our current partners is a +10 location urology practice. They have 4 research coordinators who each spend 3 hours every morning searching for trial candidates manually in their EHR. This manual screening is the most time-consuming part of their day.

Last year, they actively recruited for 11 clinical trials. They missed their enrollment targets because they couldn't identify enough eligible patients, resulting in $1.4M in missed revenue.

HealthKey has freed up countless hours of staff time, and has already identified 100s of eligible patients. With HealthKey's AI-powered Patient Identification, they're now positioned to meet enrollment targets and capture their full revenue opportunity.
