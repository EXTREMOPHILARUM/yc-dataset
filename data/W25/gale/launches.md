# Launches

## Gale - Fast and Data-Driven Business Immigration

Gale is a tech platform that automates the entire H-1B application and compliance process for US employers, with independent licensed attorneys ensuring accuracy at all steps.

https://youtu.be/tmMnGLIa3fs

### **The Problem**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=88070&key=user_uploads/1751117/82978c7b-fae9-48b4-8b08-a909d1852a7c)

_An email from a founder in W25 to their immigration law firm (name redacted to protect the guilty)_

We’ve talked to enough employers to know dealing with immigration sucks. Here’s why:

* Slow response times from lawyers (1-3 business days at least)
* No help on complex compliance requirements after visa approval
* Knowledge transfer and communication done via email or janky web forms
* Confusing questionnaires or document checklists

### **The Solution**

We help US employers spend as little time as possible worrying about H-1Bs and how to be compliant. Here’s how:

* Fast online data intake process with AI-powered document intake that auto-fills your application
* Easy to follow tooltips and comprehensive FAQ 
* Automated H-1B compliance for you and your team even after H-1B approval
* An **experienced immigration lawyer** handles and reviews each individual case with the same level of scrutiny as a law firm
* Fast turnaround time for legal questions
* We use english, no legal jargon

### **Moving forward**

H-1Bs are just the beginning. We’ve already started working on expanding our offering to help both consumers and businesses with other types of visas such as O-1s, Fs, Ks and TNs. The US immigration service market isn’t built for the 21st century, and we believe by using a mix of AI and professionals in the loop we can change that.
