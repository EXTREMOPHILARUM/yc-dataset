# ❌ The Challenge

Hardware development is a high-stakes game. A single overlooked error in a PCB schematic can derail timelines, inflate budgets, and frustrate teams. Here’s what’s holding things back:

* **Endless Reviews:** Manual schematic checks drag on for days—or even weeks—on complex boards, draining time and focus.
* **Expensive Fixes:** A tiny mistake, like a mismatched pin or voltage error, can trigger re-spins that delay production by months and cost thousands.
* **Data Overload:** Critical details are buried across hundreds of pages in schematics and datasheets, making it easy to miss something big.

Traditional approaches just aren’t keeping up with today’s fast-paced hardware demands. But we’ve got a better way…
