# Launches

## Conntour: Ask Security Cameras Anything

**TL;DR**: **Conntour lets governments and corporates query their security cameras.**

Users can search past footage (“Find a man with a blue backpack”), set alerts (“Notify me if someone climbs over the wall”), and extract data (“How many vehicles entered yesterday?”).

In just a few months, Conntour secured the Singapore government as a paying customer.

https://www.tella.tv/video/cm6k221g4000f0bl1csynd0p1/view

# **Who we’re building it for:**

Conntour targets organizations with thousands of security cameras. That includes governments (military, police, intelligence) and businesses (corporates, retail, industrial companies). These teams can’t manage so many cameras and it leads to huge losses - money, legal violations, and even human lives.

# **The Problem with existing solutions:**

They offer low customization, as they’re limited to pre-defined parameters (like shirt color or license plate number). This **lack of flexibility** causes missed critical events, too many false alarms, and countless hours wasted on manual footage review. Conntour solves this by offering **unlimited flexibility** through natural language queries - just ask anything.
