## Imogen Low
**Founder**

At 17, Imogen joined SAP’s Innovation team as a MLE after teaching herself to code and earning international recognition for her indoor navigation app. She later led NLP and ML initiatives across Asia Pacific at SAP before co-founding NWO.ai in New York at 21. As CTO, she helped build a pioneering platform that analyzes unstructured data to forecast global consumer trends, securing enterprise clients like LVMH, L'Oréal, P&G, and Colgate. Later, NWO.ai was acquired by Growth Protocol.
