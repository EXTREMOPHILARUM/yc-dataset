# Launches

## Optifye.ai - AI Performance monitoring for factory workers

Hi everyone, we’re Kushal & Vivaan - co-founders of Optifye.

We’re CS grads from Duke and because our families run manufacturing companies, we’ve seen more assembly lines than most industrial engineers!

**Tl;dr;** We place cameras in manufacturing lines and use computer vision to help supervisors solve shop-floor inefficiencies in real-time.

# **The Shop floor is a black-box.**

There has never been a way to accurately measure shop-floor performance. It is also understaffed; each supervisor, on average, is responsible for 50+ workers. 

Companies struggle to boost efficiency because they cannot determine what caused the problem. 

Ask any manufacturing company owner about the last time they reached their productivity targets and you will get a very long, frustrated answer.

# **For the first time ever, Optifye makes it… not a blackbox!**

We place cameras in manufacturing lines and use computer vision to count the number of pieces each workspace makes in real-time.

We then relay this information on a dashboard.

We believe that if you can measure it, you can improve it.

# **What this means for the ____**

**Owner**: Gets accurate real-time factory, line, and workspace productivity metrics - no need to rely on your production head (who never has the incentive to say production looks terrible)

**Production Head**: Gets line-wise and workspace-wise metrics to identify areas for improvement

**Shopfloor supervisor**: Can finally identify what is causing inefficiency in the line and fix the problem on the go

We align every stakeholder with manufacturing companies’ primary KPI – efficiency.
