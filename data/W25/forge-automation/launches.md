Forge Automation runs software enabled factories that deliver quickturn on-demand CNC parts.  For customers who prioritize consistent and fast lead times, Forge offers the option to reserve dedicated CNC machines on their floor, giving you a guaranteed SLA on production time. Alternatively, customers can order parts on-demand at a higher price. We’ve shipped thousands of parts to engineers all over North America ranging from Fortune 10 companies to start-ups.

**Problem:**

Hardware development is hard because it takes 2-3 weeks to receive prototype parts after placing an order with a supplier. For those in the software world, imagine having to wait 2 weeks to compile your code - you would hate your life and nothing would get done! Engineers just want a CNC service that is quick, affordable, and reliable! 

**Introducing Forge Automation:**

We are a North American based machine shop that makes custom, low volume CNC parts. Over the past two years, we’ve shipped thousands of parts to engineers all over North America ranging from Fortune 10 companies to brand new start-ups.

Along with the high quality and fast lead times, customers love our service because it’s the easiest way to order parts online. **Engineers can ditch the drawing and add manufacturing intent directly onto their CAD model within our platform.** Here's a quick demo showing how easy it is to submit an order.

[**Quoting Demo:**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bkJ73dUJ1gs)

We are excited to launch a new product – **Forge Reserve Machines**. Customers can get dedicated access to their own CNC machine on Forge's shop floor. Forge handles everything - purchasing and maintaining a 3 axis mill, plus providing one of their skilled machinist to make your parts. Forge Reserved Machines represent an SLA guaranteeing you 4 day production times so your parts are never late!
