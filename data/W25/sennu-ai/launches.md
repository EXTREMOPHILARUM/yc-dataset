## Sennu AI - AI Sales Agent for Tech Consulting Firms

**Hey everyone! We’re Sukhjit and Sriman, co-founders of Sennu AI (W25).**

## The Problem

Tech consulting firms offer a suite of deeply technical services (SAP S/4HANA migrations, AWS Redshift implementation) with large contract sizes ($100,000-$1,000,000+). Sales representatives at these firms struggle due to a lack of technical knowledge leading to ineffective outbound, inaccurate estimates on pricing, and lost deals. AI has only made this problem worse as SDRs face an increasing number of new unfamiliar services to sell (Agentforce, Microsoft Copilot Studio) and rapid changes in recent years have rendered historical pricing unreliable for many technical services.

## The Solution

**Sennu AI** is our answer to this problem: we provide an **AI SDR** purpose-built for tech consulting companies. Instead of juggling multiple tools and outdated workflows, Sennu AI autonomously:

* **Creates targeted outbound campaigns** based on your specific offerings
* **Sources leads** from across the web, qualifying them through extensive research
* **Crafts personalized emails** with a deep understanding of each prospect’s business needs
* **Syncs seamlessly** with your existing CRM or workflow

All with just one click. By leveraging context on all your previous projects combined with comprehensive business & technical expertise, we’re ensuring that our AI agent sends the most compelling outbound possible to every lead at scale.

Growing up in a tech consulting family, I (Sriman) experienced firsthand the challenges of running a project-based business, especially a technical one.
