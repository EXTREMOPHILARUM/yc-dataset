## BlindPay - Stablecoin API for Global Payments

### **TL;DR**

BlindPay is a stablecoin API that helps companies navigate LatAm's complex financial regulations while enabling fast and affordable global payments. Watch our demo [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N9hHISlGp9Y).

### **The Problem**

Businesses face significant hurdles when sending and receiving global payments:

* Traditional methods rely on correspondent banks, adding fees (1.5-6% for B2B) and delays of 2-5 business days or more. Bank transfers typically require 2-4 intermediaries between smaller countries, with costs reaching hundreds of dollars. 
* Frequent fluctuations in Latin American currencies, coupled with extreme inflation rates (over 50% annually in some countries), make international payments unpredictable and risky. 
* Complex regulations and strict compliance rules across jurisdictions require different documentation for each institution. 
* Fragmented payment landscapes across countries require integration with multiple local payment systems (like SPEI, Pix, PSE), making implementation resource-intensive

### **Our Solution**

Our API integrates seamlessly with your existing workflows. With BlindPay you can:

* Send and receive payments using fiat money and stablecoins
* Get competitive FX quotes and low transaction fees
* Connect directly to local instant payment systems across Latin America
* 24/7 payments with instant settlement
* Ensure compliance through blockchain screening, tiered KYC/KYB levels, and continuous transaction monitoring

Expand your global reach while reducing costs and transaction times by up to 80%. Powered by stablecoins and blockchain, our platform eliminates the delays, high fees, and regulatory barriers of traditional payment systems.
