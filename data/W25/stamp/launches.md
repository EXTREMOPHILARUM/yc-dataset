# Launches

## Stamp: Cursor for Email

# **TL;DR**

[Stamp](https://stampmail.ai) is the first AI Native Email Client. It replies to emails in your voice, automatically prioritizes emails, and tracks key action items for every email. 

It’s more than an email client. It’s like your second brain.

Watch our demo [here](https://youtu.be/pLrj4Gtgg6Y)

# **The Problem**

We’ve all dealt with managing hundreds of daily emails, and it's a massive headache. 

Trying to stay on top of your emails is practically a second job. The average employee spends 25% of their time at work buried in their inbox, and the average person receives 100+ emails daily that require manual action.

# **The Solution**

This is why we built Stamp, the inbox that tailors itself to you.

**Tab, Tab, Tab**

Stamp gives you Cursor Tab, right in your inbox. It automatically generates contextualized sentence completions and inline diffs, right as you type your email

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=87653&key=user_uploads/974285/99a30113-256c-4483-9b01-8f3ba4551924)

**Write Emails at the Speed of Thought**

Stamp writes emails in your voice with global context from your inbox. The tone and context for every reply are intelligently tailored to the recipient, exactly like the emails you’d write yourself.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=87653&key=user_uploads/974285/71ebc832-0d0c-4424-bb75-0fc78a30bba0)

And just like Cursor CMD+K, you can edit portions of your email with a single highlight.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=87653&key=user_uploads/974285/ab444480-64be-4467-a31a-0177190f61ff)

**Filter Out the Noise**

Stamp allows you to write email filters in plain English, so you can automatically prioritize the emails that matter most and filter out junk. Emails from the same categories are automatically grouped together, so that you can focus on the emails that truly matter most.

You’ll never have to read a junk email again.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=87653&key=user_uploads/974285/63ad1ba9-afa1-4608-9514-59a82e56aff2)

**Stay On Top of Your Inbox**

Stamp automatically generates summaries and extracts key action items for every email you receive, so that you always remember to follow up on critical tasks.

Never worry about missing a deadline again.

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=87653&key=user_uploads/974285/797ff8f1-4db9-4c3a-b7e0-9239f53a3f00)
