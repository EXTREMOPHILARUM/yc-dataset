# Launches

## Vantel - AI Software for Commercial Insurance Brokers

**TL;DR**

Commercial insurance brokers spend 80% of their time on manual, repetitive tasks like policy analysis, manual data entry, documentation, billing and administration. Vantel builds AI software that automates these workflows, **currently helping brokers save 10+ hours per week**. Our first product lets brokers compare five 100-page insurance policies across 50 factors in 5 minutes instead of 5 hours.

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9ybGwHNyi90>

**The Problem: Commercial Insurance is Extremely Manual**

Almost every company needs insurance. Most companies do not have the competency in-house to know exactly what coverage they need. Because of this, they often hire a commercial insurance broker to advise them on insurance-related matters.

After interviewing 100 commercial insurance professionals, we discovered some of the largest challenges in the industry: documents and workflows are unstandardized, there are multiple parties involved, and high-quality data is hard to collect. This results in **commercial insurance brokers spending the majority of their time on highly manual processes**:

* Comparing policy documents
* Validating insurance requirements in contracts versus policies
* Regulatory documentation of advisory work
* Compiling claims data from multiple sources into a common format
* Translating complex material from carriers to a digestible format for the client

**Our Solution: AI-Powered Workflow Automation for Brokers**

Vantel is building an AI platform that automates the most time-consuming tasks for commercial insurance brokers. We have started with:

**1. Policy Comparison**

* Compare 5 policies in 5 minutes (versus 5 hours if done manually)
* Works with any carrier and policy type, instantly recognizing differences and key terms
* Ranks policies based on coverage quality

**2. Contract Validation**

* Cross-check insurance requirements in contracts versus policies
* Identifies gaps in current coverage, to avoid hidden risks
* Enables more extensive client service – this is currently a high-touch activity
