## CopyCat - Browser Agents for Businesses

Hey, we’re the [Copycat](https://www.runcopycat.com/) team - Zyad, Abhi, and Graham. 

**Tl;dr 🐈‍⬛**

Copycat helps businesses automate repetitive & manual browser tasks. Simply describe the process, and Copycat generates a reliable browser agent that runs seamlessly saving time, reducing costs, and eliminating manual work.

**What’s the problem? 🚨**

At our previous company, we spent hours each week on manual tasks like filling out app store release forms because APIs weren’t available. We saw firsthand how businesses rely on fragile browser scripts when APIs aren’t an option. These processes are critical to operations but quickly become time-consuming and difficult to manage.

**The Product 🤖**

Copycat’s interface lets teams create browser automation agents easily. You can schedule a Copycat, monitor its progress, and integrate seamlessly with tools like Google Sheets, Slack, and more. 

[Demo Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LcIy1-Xx4CU&ab_channel=ZyadCodes)

**The Use Cases 👥**

Copycat’s use cases are primarily for businesses with manual tasks that are core to their company. For example, one company is using CopyCat to automate moving data from legacy medical EHR systems into their own product. Another company is using CopyCat to automatically place purchase orders from various vendors based on their customer’s demands.
