# Launches

## 🦾 Proception.ai - World's most dexterous humanoid

Our mission is to revolutionize humanoid robotics by developing robots capable of performing complex tasks, transforming how we interact with technology in industries like healthcare, manufacturing, and beyond.

### **What We Do**

At Proception, we are developing advanced humanoid robotic hands and robots, starting with a high-precision hand for research. We’ve successfully built our first functional prototype in just 4 months—an incredible milestone that sets us on the path to shipping to researchers soon. Our unique approach to **data collection**—where we collect data through human interaction rather than teleoperating robots—has significantly increased the efficiency of our data gathering. This enables us to train more effective AI models for manipulation and task execution.

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cEo4WlCanMc>

### **The Journey Ahead**

Our robotic hands are just the beginning. In the next few years, we’ll expand from hands to fully autonomous humanoid robots capable of performing a wide range of tasks across industries. From healthcare and elder care to manufacturing and logistics, Proception’s robots will drive automation and unlock new efficiencies.

### **What’s Next**

In the coming 9 months, we will continue refining our robotic hand and prepare for the first shipments to research customers. We’ll also focus on further developing our AI software and preparing for the next phase: scaling to humanoid robots capable of performing real-world tasks. By years 2–3, we plan to deploy humanoid robots into industries that can benefit from automation and complex task execution.

### **Backstory**

My journey into humanoid robotics began during my two years at Tesla, where I worked on the Tesla Optimus project. I developed a deep emotional connection to the potential of humanoid robots, which inspired me to pursue a path where I could bring that vision to life. Before Tesla, I worked at two startups, both of which successfully IPO’d. Those experiences taught me that the most challenging problems, especially in robotics, can only be solved through the agility and innovation of startups. That’s why I founded Proception—to take on these hard problems and push the boundaries of what’s possible with humanoid robots.
