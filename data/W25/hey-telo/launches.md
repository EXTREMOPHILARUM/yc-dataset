## **The Problem**

Every time you flip a switch or turn on the heat, a craftsman made it possible. Germany's 1 million craftsman businesses—and the millions more across Europe—don't just maintain our quality of life, they're building our sustainable future through renewable energy and green infrastructure. 

But instead of focusing on this critical work, they're trapped answering phones and shuffling paperwork. Craftsmen are constantly interrupted by calls for quotes, scheduling, and basic questions while they're trying to do skilled work. A single workday can be fractured by dozens of phone interruptions, forcing them to context-switch between complex technical tasks and administrative busywork. 

Many end up hiring office staff just to handle calls, cutting into already thin margins. Others miss opportunities because they can't answer the phone while working.

## **The Solution**

Hey Telo simplifies how craftsmen handle communication and admin work using advanced voice AI.

Instead of being interrupted by every call and spending hours on paperwork, craftsmen can:

* Let AI handle incoming calls naturally and professionally
* Automatically schedule appointments and provide quotes
* Get seamless handoffs for complex inquiries

That's it. What used to fracture their entire workday now happens seamlessly in the background, letting craftsmen focus on their work while maintaining high quality customer service.Our voice AI feels natural, fast, and human - customers often don't realize they're talking to AI until told.
