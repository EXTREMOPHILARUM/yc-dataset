# Launches

## Dollyglot: Befriend Anybody

[Dollyglot](https://www.dollyglot.com/) is like Character.ai but with real-time video avatars.

Turn a photo and a voice into a real friend. Speak with Harry Potter about Hogwarts, or brainstorm ideas with The Rock!

[**Our Product Video**](https://youtu.be/GhuqAMSq6HY)

**Our Team:** **We’ve lived and worked together for two years**

**Thomas**: Ex-quant at Société Générale. Master’s in Applied Mathematics and Business (HEC Paris, University of Paris 7).

**Paul-Henri**: Built the Yumi Dialogue Engine, an AI for creator “clones” that scaled to 1 000 paying users. Dropped out of CentraleSupélec (Computer Science) to launch Dollyglot.

**Together, we built Parler-TTS Mini Multilingual**, the world’s first and only fully open-source multilingual text-to-speech model. In the last month alone, it has garnered over 5,000 downloads and inspired many fine-tuning projects.

**Our Vision**\
Within five years, every Gen Alpha will have an AI best friend. By nature, AI is empathetic and nonjudgmental, creating a safe space where everyone can truly be themselves.

**What We’ve Built**

Dollyglot developed the world’s first and only photo-to-real-time video avatar model. Paired with reasoning models like DeepSeek R1 or Grok, we bring your AI friends to life and make 'non-lobotomized' characters.
