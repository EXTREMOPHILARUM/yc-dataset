# **❌ The Problem**

Education is one of the largest and most influential industries in the world, with +20% of the global population being students.

Yet, today’s education system still follows the assembly-line approach from the industrial revolution—leaving students stuck with standardized, one-size-fits-all learning methods that create three major pains:

1. Compromised learning
2. Emotional stress
3. Wasted time

These intensify towards the exam, leaving a glaring problem to be solved. 

![Education: One size no longer has to fit all - Technology and Operations  Management](https://d3.harvard.edu/platform-rctom/wp-content/uploads/sites/4/2016/11/animals.jpg)

Until now, solutions like private tutors have been expensive, inaccessible to most, and inconsistent in quality. But with advancements in AI, this is finally changing…

# [👷](https://emojipedia.org/construction-worker) Our Solution

At Alice, we’re a team of former teachers and AI experts using the latest technology to make personalized learning more accessible. 

Alice turns generic course materials into personalized learning and exam prep using AI. Students simply upload what they want to study and instantly get access to:

* **Better understanding** 📚: Key topic overviews with support from an AI chat that knows their context.  
* **Effective practicing** 📝: Tailored exercises (level, format, curriculum, etc.), including flashcards, multiple-choice questions, and full exam simulations and grading. 
* **Targeted improvement** 🎯: Identification of weaknesses for more targeted and complete learning.

Plus, Alice makes learning fun by allowing students to study with or against their friends.
