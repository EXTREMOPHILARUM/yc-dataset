### **TL;DR**

When banks grant loans, they make decisions based on their complex, proprietary credit rules. These rules are maintained by humans and, given their complexity, even the smallest changes can take up to six months to implement. This lag alone costs banks billions of dollars each year.

At Tejas AI, we enable banks to harness the power of AI to update their credit rules and other policy documents. Our platform allows banks to update credit rules faster, more frequently, and on a hyper-local level.

## **💥 The Problem**

Imagine you’re a risk manager leading a 200-person team at a bank, dealing with:

✅ Millions of loan records scattered across fragmented systems\
✅ Constant regulatory changes that impact risk policies\
✅ Thousands of credit rules requiring manual interpretation

Your team spends weeks—sometimes months—trying to answer:

> Where are the pockets high risk of loan defaults?”\
> “Where all should the bank expand next?

After all this effort, you get a handful of incomplete recommendations, leaving you guessing instead of making data-driven decisions.

💸 Banks **lose billions** in missed opportunities, defaults, and penalties.

---

## **🚀 The Solution – Tejas AI**

Tejas AI is an AI-powered decision automation platform that gives banks full control over AI-driven risk decisioning—enabling faster decisions, smarter risk management, and scalable growth with confidence.

### **🔹 How It Works**

✅ **Data Ingestion & Policy Input**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=88236&key=user_uploads/992401/4452a7c7-f0f1-4aee-bbf3-16e64e598383)

* Banks upload loan records, risk models, and existing policies.
* Teams set business goals—whether they want stricter lending criteria or aggressive expansion.

✅ **Real-Time Impact Analysis & Recommendations**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=88236&key=user_uploads/992401/b9330bb8-7c48-4ad6-b1cb-798c753e4e21)

* AI identifies optimal lending strategies and highlights risk areas—all with full explainability.
* Teams see the projected impact on key business metrics before making a decision on each of the provided strategy.

✅ **AI-Powered Policy Exploration & Simulation**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=88236&key=user_uploads/992401/9de730e3-88aa-49d5-94c1-a5bd0d16df5b)

* Teams run "What-If" simulations—testing how changes to risk models will impact loan approvals, risk, and profitability.
* Users input policy queries in plain English, and AI processes them instantly.
* The system analyzes borrower behavior, risk factors, and trends to generate optimized policies.

✅ **Automated Policy Execution**

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=88236&key=user_uploads/992401/226c1b55-3622-43b5-b6a8-fc4815445f51)

* Once a policy is finalized, Tejas AI converts it into executable code for their BREs (Business Rule Engines)
* No manual coding, no integration delays—just instant deployment.

✅ **Continuous Learning & Optimization**

* AI learns from loan performance and market trends to improve recommendations.
* Banks receive ongoing insights and alerts, keeping risk models adaptive and up-to-date.
