# Launches

## Vetnio - Automating admin work for veterinarians with AI 🐾

**TL;DR:**

* ⏰ Veterinarians spend \~40% of their time on admin work, with note-taking alone making up \~20% of their time.
* 🎤 By recording patient meetings, Vetnio automatically generates clinical notes and emails in seconds.
* ✨ Additionally, Vetnio helps out with diagnostics, follow-ups, invoice generation, and more, reducing the risk of missed revenue for clinics while ensuring optimal care for animals.

[Introducing Vetnio](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z6KIg5SJUUk)

We are [Arman](https://www.linkedin.com/in/armankaregar/) and [Max](https://www.linkedin.com/in/max-henry-xie/) from [Vetnio](https://vetnio.com). It all started when Arman brought his 🐶, Knut  (picture below), to a stressful veterinary visit. **Problem**\
With a rising patient load, veterinarians are overworked and stressed, leading to mistakes, burnout, and lost revenue. Admin work takes up \~40% of their time, and that's where Vetnio comes in.

**Solution**\
Vetnio has built an AI Copilot that records meetings and writes clinical notes, thereby saving veterinarians and clinics time, money, and headaches. Additionally, Vetnio helps out with diagnostics, follow-ups, invoice generation, and more, reducing the risk of missed revenue for clinics while ensuring optimal care for animals.

[Demo video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5PeoKr2yXSw)

**Case studies and traction**\
Among others, we work closely with the global leader in veterinary care, IVC Evidensia, and are currently conducting pilots with several prominent veterinary chains. Recently, the largest veterinary magazine in the Nordics published an independent interview with Evidensia, highlighting how our solution was saving them up to [15 minutes per 45-minute consultation](https://www.svenskveterinartidning.se/effektivare-journalforing-med-ai/), significantly reducing their administrative workload.
