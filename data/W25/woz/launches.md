# Launches

## Woz - Your AI Technical Cofounder

Hey everyone - we’re [Brad Eckert](https://www.linkedin.com/in/chefeckert/) and [Ben Collins](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bentcollins/), cofounders of Woz. 👋

Woz is an AI platform that empowers non-technical entrepreneurs to build and scale real software businesses–no technical cofounder or engineering team required. To start, we’re helping entrepreneurs build high quality mobile apps and launch them directly to the app store.

<https://youtu.be/DFZ5NCKrV58?feature=shared>

### **Problem:**

For most people, finding a technical cofounder is a frustrating and often insurmountable challenge. AI tools for generating prototypes and toy apps only exacerbate the struggle, leaving non-technical users stranded with no way to get from prototype to customer-ready product. 

### **Solution:**

With a proprietary system of AI agents, **Woz enables you to go from idea to production-ready product, eliminating the need to find a technical cofounder or hire developers.** Simply explain your business idea as you would to a human developer, and Woz handles the rest - product, payments, security, infrastructure, scalability, and more. Once you're up and running, Woz provides the tools you need to reach customers, grow sales, and scale your business, all in one place. Think of Woz as the command center for your business. 

### **What Woz is NOT:**

Woz is **NOT** another no-code tool for generating prototypes, demos, or “toy apps” (e.g. surveys, to do lists, landing pages, etc). Maybe you’ve tried tools like Bubble, Lovable, or Bolt–these tools are fun and easy to use, but they're not engineered for business building. At Woz, we take the opposite approach–starting from battle-tested backend infrastructure and then customizing it to your business. That way, everything built on Woz is ready for customers on day one.
