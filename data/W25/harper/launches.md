## Harper: AI-native commercial insurance brokerage

Hey YC! It’s [Tushar](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tushar-nair-100787144) and [Dakotah](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dakotahrice), and we're building Harper, an AI-powered commercial insurance brokerage transforming how businesses get the coverage they need. Since acquiring our first customers in October, we’ve already grown to write over $6M in annualized premiums across 35 states.

### The Problem

Commercial insurance is drowning in operational inefficiency:

* Manual processes that limit broker productivity
* Complex, redundant applications
* Information asymmetry and limited market access

These antiquated systems create terrible customer experiences and prevent traditional brokerages from scaling.

### Our Solution

We built an insurance brokerage with a suite of AI solutions that we use internally to enhance our sales, operational, and service interactions. Harper Hub, our flagship tool can:

* Complete forms automatically
* Find optimal carriers and underwriters
* Manage client communications seamlessly

This creates an [infinitely scalable brokerage](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KvD1NyGJq3k) that delivers superior service with significantly higher margins.

### Our Vision

At Harper, we believe that every legacy professional services business will have an AI-Native counterpart that will operate more efficiently, generate more revenue, and grow faster than incumbents. We want to become the largest insurance brokerage in the world with the best NPS. The commercial insurance brokerage market represents over $100B in annual commissions.
