# Lopus AI

> Analytics built for Growth

Growth and product teams are drowning in disconnected data—HubSpot, Salesforce, Stripe, PostHog, QuickBooks. The result: leaders can’t trust dashboards, data teams get buried in ad-hoc requests, and deal teams burn days stitching context instead of driving revenue.

We have built Lopus, an AI-powered grwith analytics platform, that unifies structured and unstructured data into one governed workspace. Powered by an agentic semantic layer, it understands your company definitions, joins marketing + CRM + billing + product, and delivers fast, explainable answers with lineage back to the source—turning every question into a lever for growth, so leaders know what is actually driving pipeline and converting to revenue.

Made for Founders, GTM, and Product leaders.
