# Launches

## TripleZip - AI for Commercial Real Estate Accounting 📊

**TLDR:** TripleZip automates complex accounting tasks for Commercial Real Estate firms, cutting admin costs and lowering legal risk.

**Demo:** <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=epLN4pXpcTU>

**❌ The Problem: Expensive and error-prone accounting**

* CRE firms pay $$$ for lawyers to manually extract data from their leases
* Then they pay $$$ for accountants to reconcile tenant accounts
* The whole process takes 3+ months & often has costly errors.

We learned about the problem from our parents, who own commercial real estate in San Antonio, TX. After trying existing CRE accounting software, we found that the entire process relied on error-prone manual entry and expensive consulting services.

**✅ Our Solution: Replace manual entry with AI**

* TripleZip extracts data from leases with AI (more accurately than humans)
* TripleZip feeds that data into an accounting engine which reconciles accounts instantly
* Done in <1 day, 10X cheaper than hiring a consultant or service firm

We’ve started by automating the most complex part of CRE accounting: Triple-net (NNN) reconciliation. This process accounts for up to 1/3 of the accounting effort for a large CRE firm and often results in expensive human errors and audits.

By reducing the overhead costs of accounting and avoiding costly audits related to NNN reconciliations, we aim to improve our customer’s margins by as much as 25% percent.
