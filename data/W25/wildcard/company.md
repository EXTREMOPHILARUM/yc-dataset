# Wildcard

> Sell Products in ChatGPT Shopping

Wildcard is the infrastructure platform for e-commerce brands to succeed in ChatGPT and AI shopping. We give brands SKU-level visibility into how their products appear and perform inside AI assistants—showing when they surface, why they don’t, and how to fix product attributes to improve discovery and conversion.

As AI shopping standardizes, Wildcard will help merchants activate next-generation experiences through the Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP).
