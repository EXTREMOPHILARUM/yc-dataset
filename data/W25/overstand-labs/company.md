# Overstand Labs

> Navigate any set of data - no matter how large - in minutes.

Overstand is a data lab that allows our customers to navigate any set of data in just a few minutes.

For enterprises, we we unify Slack, email, calls, and operational data, then surface the signals that matter — customer needs, risks, and revenue opportunities hidden in everyday conversations.

For legal firms, we help them move through their discovery dumps with ease.

Instead of waiting on reports or manual analysis, teams get immediate, evidence-backed answers from the data they already have. Overstand delivers clarity and leverage as your business scales.
