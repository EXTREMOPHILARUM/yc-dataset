Hey everyone! We’re [Sandra](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sandra-lifshits/) and [Maddy](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mjyip/), cofounders of [Retrofit](https://www.retrofit.shop/)! 👋

**TL;DR: **[**Retrofit**](https://www.retrofit.shop/)** is a vintage marketplace powered by AI. We use AI agents to curate and personalize in-demand, trending inventory. We analyze social media trends, sales data, and your personal style to find the best vintage pieces for you—so you don’t have to dig through thousands of listings to find vintage gems.**

<https://youtu.be/-tw1LSSL91k>

# 🚨**The Problem:**

The secondhand apparel market is booming — it’s estimated at $43 billion in 2024 in the US and expected to grow to over $125 billion by 2034. However the existing players have not kept up with this demand.

Shoppers want to buy more vintage online, but many don’t because the selection is **overwhelming**, inventory is **low quality**, and online secondhand shopping **feels like work**. Shopping secondhand often means sifting through garbage inventory and being an expert to find that incredible one-of-a-kind vintage gem. But this can finally change… 

# ⚒️**The Solution:**

We are building a new curated vintage marketplace powered by AI.

Our AI agents sift through hundreds of thousands of listings from vintage sellers across the country, to find the **best inventory.** We rely on sales and trends data to determine what type of items people are looking for and then add only the best inventory based on factors such as brand, price, materials, and condition. 

Our inventory is **personalized to your style and needs**. Give us your style pinterest board or other inspo photos and we will personalize your recommendations. If we don’t have exactly what you are looking for right now, you can tell us what you are looking for and our AI agents will find it for you.
