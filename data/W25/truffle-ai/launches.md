### **The Problem**

While AI models have become more accessible, getting AI agents to production remains painfully complex. Developers don’t just need a model—they need to handle:

* Managing state, memory, and vector databases
* Manually integrating external tools and APIs
* Customizing agents for specific business logic
* Setting up and maintaining deployment infrastructure

Despite existing frameworks, **developers still spend 60% of their time on "glue code" instead of building actual AI solutions**. Companies burn **$100K–$200K per year** on DevOps overhead, just to manage AI agent infrastructure. AI should be easier to integrate than this.

### **The Solution**

Truffle AI eliminates infrastructure headaches by turning AI agents into plug-and-play APIs:

* **No infrastructure setup** - Our managed service handles the heavy lifting, eliminating deployment and scaling concerns
* **Seamless integration** - Add AI agents to any application with just a few lines of code
* **Full customization** - Build agents tailored to your specific business challenges
* **Reusability** - Deploy the same agents across multiple applications

Most AI adoption today is being held back by engineering complexity—Truffle AI removes that bottleneck, making AI agents as simple to integrate as calling an API.
