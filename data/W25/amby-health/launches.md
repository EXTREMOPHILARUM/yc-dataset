# **The Problem**

EMS quality reviews and billing are still manual and inefficient, leading to:

* **Missed Errors & Compliance Risks** – Incomplete patient reports (PCRs) slip through, leading to under-documented care, protocol violations, and legal exposure.
* **Lost Revenue & Billing Delays** – EMS teams spend hours fixing reports, chasing down medics, and resubmitting claims. Agencies miss out on millions due to denials, coding errors, and charge capture gaps.
* **Slow, Labor-Intensive Process** – Quality review and billing teams are bogged down by manual chart reviews and corrections, delaying reimbursements and patient care improvements.

 

# **Our Solution: AI-Powered EMS Quality Review & Billing**

✅ **Automated Quality Audits** – Identifies errors, missing documentation, and compliance risks instantly.\
✅ **Real-Time Medic Feedback** – Helps providers improve documentation and protocol adherence with instant insights.\
✅ **Billing Optimization** – Ensures complete PCRs, flags missed charges and reduces denials to speed up payments.

The result? EMS agencies recover lost revenue, cut review times, and improve patient care—while reducing manual work. 

One fire department **increased EMS revenue by 33%** (from $350K to $500K) simply by improving its review process.
