### **Problem**

**Slow**\
Legacy CAD software is weighed down by antiquated UX and outdated geometry rendering from the early 90s.

**Repetitive** \
Mechanical engineers and design professionals spend hours everyday restarting projects due to rigid workflows. The current tools are clunky and slow to iterate in meaning the iteration process is greatly slowed down.

**Demands significant expertise**\
The complexity of CAD tools hinders rapid prototyping and wider adoption.

### 

### **Solution**

Design professionals spend hours every week converting ideas into CAD design and its complexity often creates barriers for both professionals and newcomers. The Adam web-app provides a frictionless experience to start rapidly prototyping and shipping out great ideas.
