### Story

Lightbox came out of our own frustration of duct taping AI tools together. The GenAI space for image/video models is a complete s\*\*t show, always chaining together different models and jumping across tools.

We had had enough of it, so we went to the drawing board, and we asked ourselves one question: What would an AI native creative tool look like, if built from the ground up?

Today, we’re launching our first experiment — Pipelines. All batteries included workflow builder for generative AI. The goal is _an **infinitely flexible tool for your creative process**_.

If you think that’s all we’re building…you would be wrong. We’re too naive to stop here. There are two more experiments cooking in order to complete our vision for the creative suite.

## emojis.com - AI Copilot for graphic design

# 

### TL;DR

Our mission is to give small businesses an AI Graphic Designer to help them create a brand and identity without having to pay 💰💰💰 of dollars hire someone.

### Backstory

Back in October, 2023, we’ve tweeted about a little side project for creating emojis with AI that went on to be called [**“_best app of 2023”_**](https://x.com/rauchg/status/1709318615911317621).

Over the course of the following year, we’ve turned this idea into a full fledged consumer app:

* launched [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ai-emojis-keyboard/id6468916301) app and became the [**#1 app in Graphic and Design**](https://x.com/pondorasti/status/1855326867605340553)
* deeply integrated with messaging platforms like iMessage and WhatsApp
* and grew to 2.5M users that generated over 5M emojis

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=88060&key=user_uploads/688130/13e428c7-74f3-4ba8-8d9b-024c79aa381e)

### Learnings / The Problem

What we’ve learned from our consumer app is that majority of our power users are actually closer to business use cases:

* **small businesses** and **indie hackers** using ai emojis for website icons
* **influencers** and **creators** using ai emojis for social media posts and thumbnails
* **teachers** and **students** using ai emojis for courses and assignments

![uploaded image](https://www.ycombinator.com/media/?type=post&id=88060&key=user_uploads/688130/3f699bb3-9d89-4774-b5fd-ed81d43570be)

### What’s next? / The Solution

We’re doubling down on providing value for small businesses and expanding to a suite of models that can become your one stop shop for graphic design needs:

* ai **illustrations** for websites and blogs
* ai **stickers** for your business
* consistent ai **characters** and **mascots** for your brand

Every company should be able to have their own “octocat” brand identity, and we’re making that a reality with AI.
