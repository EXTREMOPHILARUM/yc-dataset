## Axiom - The Gateway to DeFi

**TL;DR:** Axiom is building the first platform that lets anyone trade anything in DeFi.

**The Problem:**

* People in crypto focus too much on the tech rather than the user experience.
* There are too many protocols and chains.
* For every new chain and asset, you need to sign up and manage a different account/app.

**The Solution:**

Axiom is building the first app that lets you trade any asset on any chain, with all the technicalities abstracted away. No longer do you have to bridge, swap, manage all your assets through 5 different apps.
