**The Problem** \
Technical companies face four major challenges with Reddit marketing:

1. **Finding the Right Discussions** is like searching for needles in a haystack - manually scanning for relevant conversations is incredibly time-intensive
2. **Creating Authentic Responses** requires deep technical knowledge and platform understanding - especially challenging for marketing teams
3. **Measuring Impact** is nearly impossible without proper analytics tied to specific engagements
4. **Maintaining Consistency** demands significant resources, leading to sporadic engagement that hurts brand presence

**Our Solution** \
<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zzby3_YHbCs>\
Casixty is a digital marketing platform that:

* Indexes your company's knowledge (blogs, docs, etc.) to identify perfect engagement opportunities
* Generates draft responses that match your brand voice and technical expertise
* Delivers notifications via Slack for quick action
* Takes just 3 minutes to onboard and minutes per day to maintain
* Creates evergreen content (Reddit discussions index well in Google!)

**Customer Success Story** \
We recently worked with a cybersecurity company targeting infosec and MSP audiences. In just 10 weeks, we helped them:

* Generate over 5,000 karma in technical discussions
* Create thought leadership content that resonated with their audience posted by C levels (CEO, CTO, CMO, CPO, CISO) and SMM team (10 accounts total)
* Build brand awareness that amplified their Reddit ad campaigns
