> AI Healthcare Provider Credentialing Software

Harbera helps clinic keep their doctors credentialed and compliant. Our AI software continuously monitors and proactively helps admin teams re-credential doctors, preventing denied claims, disruptions in patient care, and lost revenue caused by doctors falling out-of-network.
