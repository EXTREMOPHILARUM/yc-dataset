# **❌ The Problem**

**Small firms don’t know how to deal with clients**.\
_Or at least, that’s what Ethan saw through visiting \~80 firms in his previous legal startup._ They couldn’t effectively:

* intake new clients
* do follow-ups to close clients
* keep clients updated throughout their cases

**Lawyers are stuck**—if they answer calls, they lose billable hours and time. If they don’t, they lose potential clients.

**Currently**, receptionists, intake specialists, case managers, and personal assistants eliminate those issues, but cost $60k+ per year each and it’s very difficult to find and train good ones (we met one lawyer who went through 3 receptionists in 6 weeks)—and outsourcing is unreliable and still expensive.

**Vicious cycle**: law firms want to spend more on advertising to get more clients, but getting more clients requires a larger support staff, and having a larger support staff means they can’t spend more on advertising. This causes stagnation.

# ✅ **Our Solution: The AI Support Staff For Law Firms**

We created the all-in-one system that intakes, closes, updates, and manages clients with AI agents that are 5X better and 10X cheaper when benchmarked against humans. It’s like you’re running your law firm, but on easy mode.

**Intake**: Luna, our multilingual AI intake specialist:

* empathetically intakes clients
* convinces callers to book consultations
* answers questions
* forwards callers to the appropriate attorney

Her multimodal features let her take payments, retrieve client data, send information through texts during the call, and simultaneously communicate with other attorneys about what’s happening in her call. She is the most advanced law firm intake system ever created.

**Client communication**: Jess, our client manager:

* interacts with clients via SMS
* does personalized lead follow-up to get leads to sign a retainer
* keeps clients updated throughout their case
* schedules and reschedules meetings

She does this by integrating into law firm CRMs and case management software. She increases conversion rates with lead follow-up, saves attorneys dozens of hours per week—allowing them to handle more cases—and heavily decreases their operational human costs.

**5X better, 10X cheaper** than human staff—helping law firms reach peak performance.
