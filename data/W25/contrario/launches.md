**❌ The Problem**

Recruiting is one of the largest and most influential industries in the world, worth over $2T and employing 60M+ workers per year.

Yet, today’s recruiting market still follows outdated, manual processes from 20+ years ago – leaving candidates stuck with a poor job search experience. There are three major pain points: 

1. Endless job applications with zero transparency
2. Low recruiter response rates leaving applicants in the dark
3. Failing behavioral screens with recruiters

These challenges compound over time, widening a critical gap in the recruiting market.

Until now, people relied on referrals and network connections to have a better chance at getting more interviews. But with advancements in AI, this is finally changing … 

**✅ Our Solution**

At Contrario, we’re a team of former candidates, recruiters, and AI experts using the latest technology to build the best candidate experience possible.

Companies can import their job from an ATS or upload their info, and our system immediately:

* Defines **top recruiters** to work on your role based on past expertise and placement history
* Uses **proprietary AI models** to dynamically generate **talent scorecards** that capture their top skills.
* Leverages inbuilt integrations with **ATS systems** like Ashby/Lever and Slack/Calendly for seamless management.

With Contrario, companies conduct an intake call + upload their most important info for a job, and we handle the rest.
