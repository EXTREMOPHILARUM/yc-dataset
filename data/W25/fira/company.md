# Fira

> Financial research platform for investment firms

Fira accelerates financial research for investment analysts by instantly searching and analyzing thousands of pages of company filings and earnings reports, delivering source-cited answers and verifiable calculations.

Our mission is to build a comprehensive research platform that integrates seamlessly with internal and external data sources, handles research and analysis, generates investment documents, and automates repetitive workflows. This frees investment professionals to focus on what matters most: building relationships and making strategic decisions.
