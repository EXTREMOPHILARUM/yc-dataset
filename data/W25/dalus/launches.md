## Dalus - Hardware system design software powered by AI

# **TL;DR**

Dalus is building the next generation of hardware system design software powered by AI. Our goal is to make building hardware as fast and iterative as software development.

🔧 Use Dalus to model complex hardware systems with ease\
🛠 Collaborate in real-time with a single source of truth for your system designs\

We started working together on Dalus in spring 2024 after Eliot quit his job at Aerojet Rocketdyne (L3Harris) where he worked as a test/systems engineer. He experienced firsthand how silos and poor communication in hardware development reduced the productivity and efficiencies of the teams he worked at.

# **Our Hypothesis:**

Hardware development in industries like aerospace, defense, and automotive is slow, siloed, and error-prone due to poorly maintained documentation. 

Traditional document-based approaches to systems engineering are outdated and fail to keep up with today’s agile hardware teams. Dalus provides an **intuitive model-based systems engineering (MBSE) platform** that centralizes all system requirements, functions, architectures, and constraints into one living digital model.

## **The Need for Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE)**

Traditional systems engineering relies heavily on scattered documents and manual processes, leading to inefficiencies and costly mistakes. MBSE addresses these issues by:

1. Creating a single source of truth
2. Enabling faster design-test-iterate cycles
3. Catching errors early in the development process
4. Supporting rapid changes in hardware designs
