# Founders

## Jason Madeano
**Founder**

Co-founder and CEO of Nextbyte

Prior to Nextbyte, Jason was a Machine Learning Engineer at Pinterest leading ML recommendations for modules on the Homefeed Relevance team. He holds a Master’s and Bachelor’s degree from MIT in Computer Science and Brain & Cognitive Science. His research on Cultural Transmission and Reinforcement Learning received a best paper award at NeurIPS.

## Matt Stallone
**Founder**

Co-founder of NextByte. 

Before tackling agents, Matt was leading the post-training of Foundation Language Models at IBM Research, working on major releases including the Granite model series. During his career, he published multiple first author papers on his LLM research. He is classically trained in ML and Distributed Systems and holds Master's and Bachelor's degrees from MIT in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering.
