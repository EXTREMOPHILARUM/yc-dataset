# Launches

## Peppr AI: The world's first AI-powered knowledge capture platform

### **TL;DR**

Lost knowledge is lost productivity.

Peppr AI turns scattered company data into a living, self-improving knowledge hub to fuel faster decisions across your organization. Through this, Peppr ensures that **no matter** **how much your team scales in size, it can still operate with the same speed as the leanest startups.** 

**The Problem**

As companies scale, their teams inevitably grow—and so does their information. Projects multiply, contacts proliferate, and goals stack up. Critical details sink into buried Slack threads, outdated Confluence docs, and a myriad of PM tools. This not only leads to incessant handoff meetings and endless email threads, but **90% of critical knowledge remains undocumented (in large U.S. businesses) due to inefficient knowledge-sharing.**

### **Our Solution**

Peppr AI is built to solve these deep-seated knowledge bottlenecks.

**Unlike tools that rely on employees manually documenting what they do (which we all know they don’t ;)**, Peppr autonomously captures critical insights hidden in engineering workflows—surfacing the knowledge that typically goes undocumented.

[Peppr AI Demo](https://youtu.be/toz3hokheXs)

By **autonomously ingesting data** from key platforms, Peppr:

1. **Auto-Generates Internal Documentation**
   * Creates synthesized summaries of daily activity from Slack, Notion, Jira, etc., keeping everyone up to speed.
2. **Offers Intelligent, Real-Time Insights**
   * Pinpoints blockers in projects and recommends intelligent workflows or POCs (Points of Contact) to resolve them.
3. **Develops “Virtual Employee Minds”**
   * Retains institutional knowledge, so when employees leave or are away, their expertise doesn’t have to be waited on and doesn’t walk out the door with them.
4. **Supercharges Existing AI Tools**
   * Empowers AI tools like Cursor and Glean with the most advanced, continuously updating knowledge base, enabling them to operate with deeper context and greater accuracy.

### **Why It Matters**

In the perfect information system, no one wastes time hunting for answers, waiting for replies, or sitting through redundant meetings. **Unfortunately, for most organizations today, it's the opposite.** 

Engineers get blocked searching for past solutions instead of shipping faster. Managers struggle with visibility. Teams waste hours trying to align.

**Peppr changes this.**

Unlike other AI-powered tools, which only surface information that employees have manually written down, **Peppr AI captures and records the knowledge that never makes it into documentation** – so work moves forward instead of in circles.

And because every productivity tool, AI agent, and search system is only as powerful as the data it relies on, Peppr doesn’t just unify knowledge—it supercharges all your existing systems. **Like an internal Scale AI**, it continuously organizes, refines, and contextualizes your data, ensuring every one of your organization’s systems operates at peak efficiency. **No bottlenecks. No blind spots. Just pure execution at startup speed—no matter how big you grow.**
