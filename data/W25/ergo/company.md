# Ergo

> AI Agents for Revenue Teams

Ergo is AI for sales teams, monitoring customer communication across tools like Zoom, Email, and Slack to keep CRMs such as HubSpot and Salesforce up to date. Ergo’s AI agents execute revenue-generating activities, such as automating mid-funnel follow-ups based on external market signals and past customer interactions. By eliminating manual operational tasks, Ergo helps sales teams maintain active pipelines, prevent deals from stalling, and cut sales cycles in half.
