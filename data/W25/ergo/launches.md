### **📉 The Problem**

Manual CRM updates are the bane of every sales team’s existence. Here’s why:

* **Incomplete records** make forecasting and reporting unreliable.
* **Missed updates** can lead to $100k+ in lost revenue.
* **Superficial data** creates friction across sales, onboarding, and customer success.

For sales managers, founders, and account executives, this means wasted hours and missed opportunities.

### **📈 The Solution**

Ergo automates CRM updates & completes tasks pending human approval (e.g., follow-up emails, personalized nurturing).

 Here’s what we do for you:

* **For Sales Managers**: Gain real-time pipeline visibility without chasing your team for updates. Reporting and forecasting has never been more clear.
* **For Founders**: Save 10+ hours a week and prevent costly pipeline bleed.
* **For AEs and SDRs**: Focus on building relationships while Ergo handles CRM updates.
* **For Onboarding and Customer Success Teams**: Access complete customer history without chasing upstream details.

### **🎬 Our Backstory**

Two months ago, Ishan and I were at Georgia Tech working on medical software when we went to Delve (YC W24) for advice. What began as a feedback call turned into an invitation to spend four days in San Francisco with them and join the team. While shadowing them, we saw how 15–20 daily sales calls led to missed follow-ups and poor record-keeping in HubSpot.

Inspired, we built an initial version of Ergo using a 72-step Zapier workflow that retroactively updated their CRM and unlocked and closed $75k in contracts. Encouraged by the results, we expanded the solution to integrate with more communication channels like email and Slack. Back in Atlanta, we tested Ergo with other founders and saw the same widespread problem—CRM management was universally painful.
