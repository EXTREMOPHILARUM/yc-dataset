## Edexia: AI Teaching Assistant for Grading Papers

# TL;DR

Edexia is an AI teaching assistant that grades papers for teachers. It learns the style of each individual teacher, so it can grade just like they do, saving up to 100% of grading time.

Edexia can grade assignments from any curriculum, year level, subject, or format. Learning like a teacher, it visually breaks down its understanding of the rubric, and then updates this, as a teacher corrects its grading on the fly.
