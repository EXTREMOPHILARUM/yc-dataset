## Oki AI - Automated Project Tracking for Engineering Leaders

Hey everyone! Luofei and Aayush here from Oki.

**TL;DR**\
Oki unifies data from Slack, GitHub, Jira, and more into one AI-powered dashboard, so Engineering leaders can automatically track progress, better allocate resources, and boost org productivity. No more endless hunts for ground truth or time-consuming status updates!

**Demo**\
<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zUKDdNCNeK0>

**The Problem**\
CTOs, VPs, and Directors of Engineering can’t truly see what’s happening across their teams. Even with teams of direct reports and countless status meetings, updates get missed, priorities shift, and some projects get overlooked. There’s too much context for any one person to track, leading to incomplete or inaccurate reporting.

**The Solution**\
Oki aggregates all your project data - no setup or manual input required - and surfaces behind-schedule items, emerging blockers, and under-resourced teams. You can drill down into any issue to see exactly what’s happening, so instead of spending meeting time asking, “What’s the status?” you can focus on, “How do we fix it?”
