# **THE PROBLEM:**

**Electrical system design tools have lagged behind modern-day needs:** 

1. Manually reading datasheets takes too long and is error-prone
2. Visio, [draw.io](http://draw.io), or Excel don’t have built-in electrical capability
3. Linking low and high-fidelity designs is manual and error-prone
4. Manufacturing drawings are manually drawn and fall out of sync

# **THE SOLUTION:**

**We’re building a single-source-of-truth electrical tool with AI hooks:**

1. Use our AI datasheet parser to generate pinouts
2. Embed electrical capabilities (e.g. parts libraries & design checks)
3. Link Low-fidelity block diagrams and high-fidelity details
4. Autogenerate manufacturing drawings from system definition
